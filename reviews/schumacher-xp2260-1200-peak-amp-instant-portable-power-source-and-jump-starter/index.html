I own 3 of these units and use them in the most difficult of situations. We own an RV and go camping regularly. We have also used the unit to give a booster charge to the RV house batteries to get the generator going.Additionally the unit has literally been a life saver. My husband must sleep with a positive airway machine that needs to be plugged in to AC while he sleeps. It is in essence a car battery with an handle, plus a power inverter, with an air compressor and work light thrown in for good measure.
