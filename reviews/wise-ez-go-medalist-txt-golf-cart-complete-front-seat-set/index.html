Product Specifications

  • Complete set includes fully assembled back rest and seat bottom cushion
  • Easy install using existing hardware and components from your golf cart
  • Heavy duty 68 gauge vacuum-formed vinyl covering, high compression foam padding, marine grade plywood substrate
  • Mildew / UV ray resistant / low temperature resistant to -36° f
  • Seat back substrate covered in black vinyl and hidem welt trim to mask and protect plywood base

  • Seat Back: 10.25”H x 36”W / Seat Bottom: 20.5”D x 40”W

    Product Description

Revitalize your golf cart seats with the Wise EZGO Medalist/TXT Golf Cart Front Seat Complete Set. All sets include a fully assembled back rest cushion and seat bottom cushion, ready to mount to your cart with a simple install using existing hardware and components. Each OEM style cushion assembly consist of heavy duty, vacuum formed vinyl covers stuffed with premium, high-compression foam affixed to pressure-treated, exterior grade plywood. Choose from Fairway Buff, the flagship DS 2000 color or add contrast your cart with Avalon White, or Black. Also available in Max 4 Cordura Camouflage.
