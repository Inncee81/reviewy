REVISED OPINION

I have come to love this device, which I bought primarily to photograph my new dog. I think it takes terrific pictures (I’ll attach one of them), but my initial reaction was tainted when I tried to get a users manual. I went to the website of JK Imaging LTD, which actually manufactures the item for Kodak, and found user manuals for numerous cameras, but not the FZ51. The website listed quite a few customer service phone numbers, but none in the United States. The box contained an ominous-sounding note saying “Stop–do not return product before contacting JK Imaging LTD,,” so I emailed them and while their reply did include a link to download the manual, it also included the disclaimer: “We are regret to inform you that Amazon does not belong to our official retailers, therefore the one-year warranty does not cover any repair of your device, I am afraid.” Once I downloaded the manual, however, it did include a phone number for Kodak USA, and a customer service rep said that is not true, that the standard one-year warranty does apply. So for the time being, at least, I’m very happy; it’s a remarkable camera for the price, and I haven’t had any of the problems some other reviewers have had with blurry edges or logos that have to be deleted. Definitely well worth the money.
