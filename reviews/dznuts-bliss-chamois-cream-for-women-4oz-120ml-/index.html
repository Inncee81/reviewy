Good after a ride, not effective during

I like this chamois cream, but I don’t love it. DZNuts Bliss does not prevent saddle sores for me, but it does help alleviate existing ones. This only works for me post-ride. I do like the thickness and how moisturizing the cream is. I just wish it was effective during rides. Some other reviewers have reported a tingling sensation when using this product - I do not experience that. It just feels like lotion. I will probably try other brands when this runs out.
