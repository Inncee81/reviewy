Great stand for additional height and stability

Great stand, does what we needed! We wanted out dresser top TV high enough to continue using the space under the TV for jewelry boxes, coin banks, and decor as well as higher for better viewing laying down. Basically, we went from a 32in TV to a 46 and wanted more than JUST the TV on the dresser. At its highest setting, This stand allowed for ~13in under our 46in TV to the table top, enough for for a moderate jewelry box and a 1.75l Jack Daniels bottle used for coins. Heights will vary depending on the mount locations and TV “border” thickness. Stand looks good and is sturdier than the factory one, must be to get a TV up that high.Cons for removing 1 star: Hardware isn’t the highest quality… nuts tended to wobble as you screwed them down (But hold tight). One leg had a wobble to it, which flattened out once cranked tight against the base.TV has no adjustment for leveling the tilt side to side… you just have to pull on it so hardware shifts a bit. There’s no need for a level adjustment since the legs themselves are adjustable, it just needs some “tweaking” to square up everything once assembled.TV “sags” just a bit… pointing towards the ground in front of it. This could be fixed used a washer/spacer on the bottom of the TV mounts…. for us, it was fine. The TV is so high on a dresser that the SLIGHT downward is better than perfectly vertical.These are minor issues and haven’t affected my use of it at all… but worth mentioning.
