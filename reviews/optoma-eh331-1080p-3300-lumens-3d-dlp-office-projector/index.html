Style:3300 Lumens 1080P The Optoma EH331 projector delivers an amazing 3300 lumens along with a class-leading 22,000:1 contrast ratio for bright readable presentations and colorful videos with rich, vivid colors and astonishing detail. The EH331 is well equipped with HDMI and VGA ports,10,000-hour lamp life, and brilliant color which allows fine tuning of color, contrast, and detail. Srgb color profile enables rich accurate color, based on primaries of rec.709 producing images aligned with HDTV specification when used with a PC, Mac, or any compatible source. Wall color adjustment is designed to compensate for the loss of brightness when projecting on non-white projection surfaces in various environments. This feature is extremely useful in classrooms, conference rooms, training rooms or anywhere the presenter may be using a colored wall, blackboard, chalkboard, or whiteboard for the projection surface. And the EH331 small form factor is robust and flexible, meeting the portability needs of today professionals from road Warriors to guest lecturers to educators Hoping to turn their classroom into an interactive learning presentations for their students.

Awesome for the price

I bought this for use on my back deck which has a covered pergola. For the price this thing is awesome! I bought the projector, a 100in screen and an amazon sound bar for less than I could get a large flatscreen tv for. In the daytime the picture is a little faint but you can still see it as long as your screen is shaded. No problem in the evening while the sun is still out but starting to go down. I bought this one because it has an audio out which makes it nice because I ran a 3.5 cable from the out on the projector to the sound bars 3.5 aux in. No issues with sound. I plugged an amazon fire stick right into the back of the hdmi port on the projector and I use DIRECTVNOW to stream tv through my internet with no satellite required. Hope this helps answer some questions about functionality. Be careful to select the language of your choice upon setup because it took me forever to find out how to switch it back to english.

Great projector for all types of video!

However, these delays are not a big deal.Our living room is on a side of the house that gets hit with direct sunlight all day long. We can use this projector with the curtains open on a sunny day with surprisingly little wash out. It is bright enough that watching is during the day with curtains open is not an issue.Sure there are projectors with more features and better options but at this price, this projector offers everything I would expect and more.We use the projector for everyday TV viewing. It is used for Sports almost every day and movies at least 4 times a week. This projector handles still and high motion video very well.
