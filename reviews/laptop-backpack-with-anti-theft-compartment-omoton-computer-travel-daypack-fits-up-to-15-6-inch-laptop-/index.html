On the upside, I don’t think thieves are going to target you in a crowd for having it.As someone else noted, the zippers feel a little less premium than the rest of the bag, so you’ll probably want to open and close them with some care, but again, this isn’t a hiking bag. Barring any zipper disasters, looks like a good purchase.
