A solid first offering from VAVA in the true wireless market. If you want TWS earbuds with bass, then these are for you.

 If you are a fan of bass heavy music and you want true wireless earbuds, then the Vava Moov 20’s could be the right earbuds for you to purchase. If you are looking for true wireless earbuds that will stay in your ears during a workout (I am speaking from a runner’s perspective), then I would probably recommend a different pair of true wireless earbuds. I’ve been using these earbuds for around a week now, and here are my thoughts on them so far:Pro’s:+ I personally really like the sound quality. They are definitely bass heavy earbuds, but I personally prefer that. Of all of the true wireless earbuds (TWS) that I’ve tried out (I’ve reviewed a lot of them on my YouTube Channel), these are definitely the most bass heavy.+ They are super comfortable to wear. I can easily wear these for 3+ hours and forget their even there.+ The case they come with is classy and quality. While I would prefer it to be a charging case (like a number of other TWS earbuds), the case is undoubtedly a nice one.+ The connection process is super simple. If you have any trouble check out my video.Cons:- The battery life, like many other TWS earbuds is lacking. 3 hours is pretty much standard operating procedure for many of them, but I’d like to see more TWS earbuds that get closer to 5 or 6 hours of battery.- They have to be plugged in a dual micro usb for charging, so don’t lose that cable.- These will not stay in my ears if I’m working out. They would be fine for weight lifting, but any sort of running or similar movements will cause them to fall out of my ears. Check out my reviews on my YouTube Channel (in my profile) if you want TWS earbuds that are good for working out with (specifically running).

Exceeded Original Expectations Greatly with Sound and Convenience from VAVA

These true wireless earbuds from VAVA boast quality sound, design, and controls. For those who are not familiar with the technology, true wireless earbuds are simply two separate earbuds that create stereo sound but do not have any physical connection. Since there is no physical connection between the earbuds, it is much more freeing to use true wireless earbuds rather than traditional Bluetooth earbuds for working out. This is because most people using Bluetooth for getting rid of the annoying, bouncy, cable, but most Bluetooth headphones still have some sort of cable which still can bother you while running or cycling.At first, I was a bit hesitant in this purchase because of the mixed reviews, and was preparing myself for the worst. However, I am very happy I made the decision to purchase these earbuds. I was concerned about the fit, as there are no ear hooks provided. By twisting the earbud into my ear, I found that you actually can get a secure fit for walking, although I would not recommend these for running or cross-fit.These earbuds are also inconspicuous and have an attractive design. The black finish with the VAVA logo makes the earbuds stylish and fashionable, which is a perfect look for both exercise and office or formal use. The VAVA branded unique in-ear soft gel design for the ear tips makes them comfortable to wear for long periods of time.The sound quality on these is also much better than expected. Although I would have preferred a bit more bass, especially for running, the audio is clear with a wide range of sounds. The connectivity is not the best, but considering the convenience of having no wires, the rare cut-out of the left earbud is completely expected and acceptable.The battery life is about three hours of continuous music playback and phone calls. This is also decent for the true wireless earbuds category. I do wish the included case doubled as a charging case. However, I do like the look of the case as it is elegant and well-designed, compared to other cheaper plastic cases.Overall, I would definitely recommend giving these a try!
