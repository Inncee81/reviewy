    Product Description

These Floating Glass TV Accessory Shelves are designed to support your entertainment accessories for DVD Players/Cable Boxes/Games Consoles/TV Accessories.

The Jeronic Floating Wall Mounted Shelf are designed to accompany a wall mounted television. When you have a TV mounted to the wall you will often find that you do not have a place to put your other devices. These shelves are the ideal solution.

These shelves are universal and are designed to suit any type of entertainment device. One Large Strengthened Tempered Glass Shelves(Approx 380 x 280mm x 5mm each shelf) are made using High Standard Glass (5mm thick). These Floating Glass Shelves will look fantastic in your home and compliment all entertainment equipment. Also with a cable Management System to hide all your messy cables. Brand:Jeronic.
