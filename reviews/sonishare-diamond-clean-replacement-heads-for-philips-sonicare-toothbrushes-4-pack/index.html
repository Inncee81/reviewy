A+ alternative to the pricey originals!

I have tried many different SoniCare replacement tooth brush heads, mainly because the brand name tooth brush heads are exorbitantly expensive, and it doesn’t seem that this simple product should cost that much. Unfortunately, what I have found is that most of the replacement heads suffer from one of two problems: (1) poor bristle attachment, ie. bristles falling out - often times into your mouth - after just one or two days, and (2) longevity, ie. the tooth brush head has a noticeable difference in cleaning performance after just 2-3 weeks, as the bristles begin to fail and you can feel particles on your teeth even right after you have brushed.I was pleasantly surprised by the SoniShare brand tooth brush heads. The price is extremely reasonable and they left my teeth feeling very clean. Here are the pros and cons that I have found so far in using them:Pro’s:- stiff bristles clean very well, but are not uncomfortable- very reasonable price- the tooth brush head is quiet - ie. not vibrating against the tooth brush base - as many other replacement tooth brush heads doCon’s:- no cap or travel case; I travel a lot and liked the fact that other replacement tooth brush heads came with these. However, this is pretty minor in comparison to the effectiveness and longevity of the tooth brush head - plus I saved the travel caps that I received with other brandsLastly, I have only been using these tooth brush heads for 2 days, so it is still too early to tell if these will have a similar durability as the original Phillips Sonic Care brand replacement tooth brush heads - but rest assured that I will certainly come back to revise my review if I notice a substantial difference.
