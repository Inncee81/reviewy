    Product Description

Make your air mattress extra cozy for you or your guest when you throw on the Intex Queen Airbed Cover. This quilted polyester cover adds a comfortable layer to your airbed, making it feel a little bit more like home. This cover fits mid-rise to double high queen-sized air mattresses, up to 22 inches tall. It has a thread count of 200 and is 100% polyester. The elegant quilted pattern looks like your mattress’ cover. This cover fits your airbed snugly when strapped on and doesn’t slide off in the middle of the night.
