Product Reviews

Works great, strong magnet for hanging

I bought these to replace identical looking work-lights by an unknown maker that finally bit the dust after many years of use. The lights we received are bright, and the two lights, main and top, allow for multiple uses. The magnet hangs one to our fridge and the hook hangs one in the garage. The magnet is much stronger on these as it stays where I put it whereas with the older, unknown brand the light would slide until something blocked it. My only complaint, and boy is it a small one, is that the battery compartment requires a screwdriver to open whereas our old ones had the sliding back that allowed for easier battery changes. I had read other reviews that said the battery compartment had to be opened and a plastic piece removed before it started working (saves the batteries) so I was prepared for them to not work out of the box. Overall, I am very happy with these lights, and since the batteries were included, I went ahead and ordered some for a friend who admired ours.
