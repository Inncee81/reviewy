Product Specifications

  • Precision engineered to match size, style and color of SONOS Play 1 speaker
  • Sturdy stand (8 lbs each); made from superior quality steel
  • Neat cable routing on back of stand to hide power cord
  • Comes with all necessary installation hardware, including rubber feet and adjustable carpet spikes Includes 2 speaker floor stands (speakers not included)

  • Stand sits 32” high without the speaker (fixed height)

    Product Description

Color:Black Flexson floorstands put your SONOS PLAY:1 speakers at the ideal listening height. Whether you’re streaming music to a single speaker, or using a pair of PLAY:1 speakers in stereo, or in a 5.1 PLAYBAR set-up for your movies, TV and more, your system will look - and sound - great. Flexson’s custom floorstand is precision engineered in England to perfectly match the size, style and colour of the SONOS PLAY:1 speaker. The Flexson floorstand for PLAY:1 is a sturdy, metal design - with a stable base that can slip under sofas or chairs if needed. Ehanced cable routing, with a cable channel at the rear of the upright column, makes it easier to neatly hide away the speaker’s power cable. Like all Flexson accessories for SONOS, the floorstand is easy to assemble and use. It comes with your choice of spikes (for carpeted floors) or gel pads (for hard floors). This product is sold as a pair (2 stands). Speakers not included.
