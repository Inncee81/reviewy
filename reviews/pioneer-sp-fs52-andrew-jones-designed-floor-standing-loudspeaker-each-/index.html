Excellent quality for the price.

I am not an audiophile by any means but i have owned many speakers in the past. These speakers are excellent for the price. Sure you could get better quality speakers but IMO you would need to shell out a lot more money for them. These speakers sound excellent. I was hesitant buying them offline especially if I didn’t like them but I am happy to say I couldn’t be happier with them. I went ahead and bought the entire Andrew Jones pioneer set and its phenomenal. The sound quality is excellent. This set which cost me a little over $500 almost performs on par with my father’s home theater system and he spent around $4000 on his. My father even was quite surprised how well these sounded and said they sound just as good as his home theater system. I don’t think you can go wrong with these speakers at this price range.

Amazing value

Very clean

Before buying the FS52, I had a pair of Dayton B652, then a pair of 3 way sony bookshelf speakers from Goodwill. After switching to the pioneers floor standing speakers, the major difference was mostly in the mids and highs. The pair of old sony had a 7 inch woofer, so the bass was decent, but the mids and highs were not as good as the pioneers. The dayton b652 was no match what so ever. The pioneers aren’t as tiring on your ears as my old sonys. I can listen to them all day no problem. The bass is pretty nice, not boomy, just there and enough for you to enjoy. Of course a 10 inch subwoofer would be nice also further down the road. The pioneer is definitely a great choice if you listen to a lot of music that includes singing. Definitely recommend you to buy it. I bought it when it dropped to $65 or so.

An excellent sounding speaker for music or movies

I love these speakers. They have good bass by themselves, but a subwoofer definitely helps them get deeper for movie special effects. Their overall sound for music is very balanced and smooth, not harsh or too subdued . A definite buy for a small intimate room. I have speakers that cost me 5X as much, and they don’t sound greatly better unless you are playing really loud, which I rarely do. These are the best inexpensive speakers I have ever owned, or listened to anywhere. They sound even better when placed on a speaker stand , to get the speaker closer to ear level when you are seated, along with a good sub woofer.
