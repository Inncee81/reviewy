but this bag is perfect for me

I thought it was bigger.

I gave it three stars because I honestly thought the backpack was much larger. I guess I should have paid more attention to the details when purchasing it. I am able to fit my Nikon D3200 with a 55mm kit lens pretty easily. I also am able to fit a full sized flash and a 200mm lens in it. Most of the cleaning equipment, extra batteries, chargers, and stuff fit in it pretty well. I like the top zipper - because it is roomy for odd-ball items that you wouldn’t have any other place for. I am disappointed that I can not fit my tablet with the case in the back zipper. I am sure that i could fit the tablet in there by itself, but I have to have the keyboard on it to be able to use it efficiently.All in all, I wish that it was larger so that I could carry additional lenses and equipment when I go walking. I am not exactly sure how you could attach it to an additional backpack and keep it safe enough not to be stolen or keep something from breaking. I think that it would be easier to keep the backpack on hikes if there was a way to attach it to a larger backpack.I would recommend this backpack for people who need the extra room more than one of those small cases, but not for a professional photographer who carries a lot of extra prime lenses and things that need the extra room.

Good Backpack

We were planning a long trip and I wanted a lighter weight, easy access backpack for tours when I found this Case Logic bag. It is smaller than my other bags but will still take my d7000 with a 300 lens although it is tight. I could also fit a few other things like my point and shoot camera in the lower back section with the dslr (case comes with some velco shaping pads but I modified with some other velco pads I had too) and misc. small items in the top zippered section. When I flipped it on the side to get the camera out during a tour, someone commented on what a nice feature that was. Also liked that it had a section for ipads. We carried a Fire tablet in it but it fit the ipad too. Both my husband and I used it and the size was a relief after a long day. It did not look out of place with its smaller size.
