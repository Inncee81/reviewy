Great camera, some extra junk and poor customer service

I am happy with this package overall, the price is fair and the camera is great, however, I am only giving it three stars for two reasons (1) most of the accessories are worthless and (2) the telephoto lens is not Auto Focus as the camera body does not have a motor in it and the seller did not assist me with this in a satisfactory way. I contacted them to see if I could return the telephoto lens and pay the difference to get the Auto Focus lens and they wrote back asking for more information, which I promptly did. After that, they did not respond to my emails. While I can manually focus the lens on still life shots and some portrait situations, catching anything that’s moving with it is impossible. I am going to be selling the lens and investing more money in another telephoto lens.To note, what they don’t adequately tell you, is this lens would be auto focus with a more expensive camera that has a motor in the camera body, but this is the entry level DSLR and it does not have a motor in it to auto focus. There is another version of this lens with a motor in it, but it is not a part of this bundle.
