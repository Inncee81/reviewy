Good quality and great looks for casual play

The description very clearly states that this is for beginner and for casual entertainment. Keep this in mind before you purchase.I am not a professional player. I play casually for fun and also as a light workout for 20-30 mins outdoors. This set met and exceeded my expectations in terms of quality and looks. Even for casual play, I won’t suggest getting a discounted $8 racket from a local retail store. Without a decent racket, not only will it spoil the fun, the racket threads will start coming off in days or weeks. Kids may lose the interest to learn or play. (If you have small kids wanting to learn, I would suggest getting a separate training (starter) racket. It makes a big difference initially.)Unlike Tennis or some other games that require decent amount of set space, proper net and drawn boundaries, Badminton is a game that can be played in limited space – near home – with minimum requirements – for casual players and beginners. Even setting up the net and small cones or markers for boundaries is a simple thing that most people can manage. Casual play outdoors for fun mins can be done without a net or markers. Just with the things that come in this pack.There are 2 nylon shuttlecocks that come with this set. Personally I am not a big fan of the nylon shuttlecocks. I like the feather ones and I think this makes a huge difference. They are not expensive, the only downside is the feather ones needs to be replaced after certain number of games. If you are satisfied with the nylon ones, great. If you have not tried the feather shuttlecock, I would suggest to give it a try. They are not expensive to replace.
