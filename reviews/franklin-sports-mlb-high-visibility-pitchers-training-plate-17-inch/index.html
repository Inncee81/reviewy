Great plate to practice pitching!

Very good colors that stand out compared to the other models that are painted green, yellow, and red. When those fade it looks terrible. I personally only advise to use this plate when once in a while during practices and practice warm-ups/progressions and not pre-game. Use a standard “blank” plate pre-game always. I had an 8u girl step on this plate a few times (she was catching for another 8u pitcher) and I did notice that her rubber cleats did take off some of the black paint rather easily. I keep another smooth plate over this one when I travel so it hopefully prevents more scratches and I wouldn’t pack this with other equipment that can scratch/scrape paint off this.I also like the way this looks compared to the other green, yellow, red plate - everyone has that plate and everyone stares at mine like, “Ooooh, where’d you get that????” Plus the MLB logo makes it look even more “pro”.Nice, good, weight to it and it’s thick rubber so don’t expect to fold or roll this up. It’s raised about half-inch so I like that I can plop on some grass and it sticks out just enough and weight is just enough to hold it down. Seen lots of floppy plates out there but not this one.
