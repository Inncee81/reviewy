Great screen protector

Literally a perfect fit for the Hero 4 Session back LCD screen. Matte finish improves visibility during bright light, and the added scratch proof protection makes this a must-have. With all that said, when I say a perfect fit for the Hero 4 screen, I mean LITERALLY a perfect fit. Usually pretty adept with attaching screen protectors without subsequent readjustments, I had to readjust the screen quite a few times before getting the corners flush with the screen. The pros of this design is complete coverage and protection for your screen, but the cons include difficulty with aligning the screen protector without a lot of wiggle room for error. If you do mess it up, you have 2 other attempts for a total of 3 screen protectors. Good news is this screen protector doesn’t play around once it is attached.
