Product Reviews

Great buy!

This is a surprisingly good buy. Bought this for daily web browsing and media consumption and I must say it’s great for the price. The touch screen is responsive, the built quality is good and it has all the port’s you’ll need. Not going to be carrying it around much, but it doesn’t feel heavy so I’m guessing it would be great for a student, and 1 TB is plenty storage for its intended use. The one thing that blew me away was the speaker quality, I’m no audiophile but the sound quality was the best I’ve heard from any laptop I’ve owned and used over the years! The one small gripe I have (and this is something with all HP Laptops I’ve come across) is the sub-par touch pad. There aren’t any dedicated left and right click keys, you have to hit the touch pad just right to register a left click. This could be avoided by using the multi-finger touch pad gestures or the touch screen, so it isn’t really a huge deal breaker. If you’re looking a laptop that is priced reasonably for the specs and intend to use it for media consumption and regular daily tasks, then this is definitely worth it.
