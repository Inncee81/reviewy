Died after about an hour and a half of continuous use. It’s nice you can recharge it with the same cord as my galaxy! You get in fan speed what you lose out in on clipping ability. To be honest though I’d probably buy a fan that is less powerful but clips appropriately. This is unable to be used like I’d like it to.
