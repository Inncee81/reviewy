Nice Little Uke

For the price and the size this little uke is amazing! It has a nice bright tone to it, stays in tune nicely and is pretty decently made! I would definitely suggest getting new strings. I’m assuming the ones they come with are just cheap cheesy strings. Also, I had to file the nut where the C string fits in just a tad as it was too narrow for the string. It also comes with a nice little gig bag that fits perfectly. It has some protection to it, enough for a drop or a rainstorm. Overall this is a very nice product and I would definitely recommend it to either a beginner or someone who wants a travel uke.
