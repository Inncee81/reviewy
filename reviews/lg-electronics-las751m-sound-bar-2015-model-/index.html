A good sound bar, worth the money.

Not great, but a very good sound bar. This is my second LG and once it was placed and configured properly it sounds pretty good (I was an audio engineer). If you research a lot you may find a sound bar that is a little bit better, but this one works fine for me.You’re never (imo) going to get good high-quality professional component sound and audio presence out of ANY sound bar, but I have a small place / limited space, my “ear” isn’t what it used to be, so this one does a decent job for the money.

For the price, this is not a good buy.

I bought this to do surround sound with two H3 wireless speakers. The soundbar regularly loses connection to the remote speakers and requires re syncing. I have this optical cabled to my LG smart tv and it still disconnects from the tv on occasion. Sound sync from LG is flawed in that it disconnects or worse, sends a delayed signal to the remote speakers that results in an echo. I often wind up turning the remote speakers down or off to use this system.Sound is good when it works but it doesn’t work often enough to make it a bad buy. I would return it but waited past the time as I gave it as a gift to my wife. Very unhappy with this purchase. If it had been a cheap speaker set I would be less disappointed, but this was a premium price and I expected a premium product. It does turn on and off with the LG tv remote but occasionally gets wonky and even that has to be reset.I haven’t tried streaming music to the speakers, but the app itself continues to lose connection to the speakers even with strong wifi signal.
