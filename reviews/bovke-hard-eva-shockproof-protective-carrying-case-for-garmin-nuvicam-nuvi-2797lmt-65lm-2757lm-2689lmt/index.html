Best Carrying Case I’ve Found Available for Garmin 770 dezl Navigation and Accessories

Finally found a proper carrying and protective case for my Garmin 770 dezl Navigation. Even though the description doesn’t specifically speak about this particular navigation, it fits it and accessories, unlike many others that are in fact marketed for the 770 dezl. This ability to carry charging/ power cord, windshield suction piece, and navigation head was the most important reason I bought and kept this Bovke case.Pros:1) Fits navigation head and all accessories2) Nice handle to carry navigation and accessories around safely3) Tough outer shell that will protect navigation and accessories from falls and bumpsCon:1) There is a velcro tab that allows you to secure the navigation head inside of the carrying case, which is a bit short in my opinion. As a result, you have to pull it tight to velcro and fully secure your navigation head in its compartment. This is not dissimilar from other protective carrying cases, and as such, I’m not critiquing this feature too harshly. I’ve made it work as is, although I do think they should improve this feature.

Perfect fit for larger GPSs

Ample space for my 7” Garmin GPS and for a dashboard mount, windshield mount, and charging cable. Be aware though, this case has a strong chemical odor when you first open it but it eventually airs out over time. My only thought is that it is related to the materials used for its construction. As far as I know, it’s not harmful if you touch it but I caution against kids or pets that may lick this or put it in their mouths.

Great medium-sized case!

This is a great medium sized case. Although specified for a GPS unit, it is handy for a great many things. It’s body is rigid, yet not a hard case. It’s exterior has a durable, seamless “fabric like” appearance and construction. There were some mild superficial markings/damage to the woven exterior, and some small spots where excess glue/adhesives looks to have marred its appearance (one star deduction). The BOVKE logo is a plastic panel glued to the top, and molded as part of the rubberized handle on the back. Neither of them bothered me, but either could easily be muted with permanent marker. Visitors appear to be good quality and have been jam free for months now. This case is well built, durable and practical. It’s advertised proportional dimensions are accurate. Despite my one star deduction for anesthetics (may have just been my unit) I highly recommend it.
