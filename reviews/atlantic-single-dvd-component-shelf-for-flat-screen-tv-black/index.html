    Product Description

Color:Black The Wall Mount Adjustable DVD Component Shelf allows you to mount your DVD player or Cable/Dish Box to the wall, saving you space. This Component shelf features heavy-gauge steel construction with durable product coated finish for years of enjoyment. The 6mm tempered glass shelf can support up to 22 lbs. Based on your room decor, you can fix this shelf to a desired height. Easy to install, this DVD wall mount shelf makes cable management quite simple with the TV and components close together. Comes with instruction manual and mounting screws.

This is a beautiful shelf. After hanging TV in bedroom, It hit me I had no place for our cable box. Wife was giving me the stink eye because of all the ugly wires it took to connect the cable box to tv from table below tv. Got this shelf, easy to install and wammm! 0 to hero in 20 minutes. the shelf could very easily hold pretty much any size DVD or Blu Ray …

For $10, including the free 2-day Prime delivery, I was not expecting much. I was blown away by the looks, quality, and ease-of-installation of this item. You really can’t go wrong buying this.I’ve included pictures of it. I mounted it in the same stud on which I mounted my fully articulating TV bracket, and thought it would then be centered with the TV, but because of how the TV mount works, I was wrong. I probably could have mounted it with drywall anchors in a place where it would be centered, since only a light weight, compact DVD player is being placed on the shelf, but I didn’t want to risk it, and didn’t want to start over (and have to fill in the big holes I drilled through the stud). As you can see, the shelf could very easily hold pretty much any size DVD or Blu Ray player, and possibly even multiple components. It’s a tempered glass shelf, and holds something like 22 lbs. The black color goes with most modern TVs and mounting brackets.
