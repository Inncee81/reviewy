Product Reviews

Fintie fit and finish does not disappoint!

This is my second Fintie case (first having no keyboard) and have been very pleased with their fit and finish. These new revisions have openings on both sides that allow easier gesture swipes in Windows 10. The keyboard is tiny… but has surprisingly good tactile feedback! I only wish the ESC key was dedicated and didn’t need the Fn key to be held. Small nuisance, redeemed by the overall quality of the case and keyboard.I got the Navy blue (same as my first case) and it looks better in person. At just under $30, paired with the NuVision 8” tablet, this may very well be the most versatile Windows 10 tablet+keyboard device for under $150 currently in the market!

I like the magnetic attachment so I can move the keyboard …

Occasionally Windows loses the pairing but I think that is a Windows issue. I like the magnetic attachment so I can move the keyboard around. The only thing I would like to see improved is the stand on the back. If it was full width I could use it on my lap without having to place a wide book first to form a “desk”. It would also be nice if there was a way to make the stand latch in the deployed position in case you have to move or re position. It immediately closes now and to prevent this I have to clip my stylus on the inside.

Real good case

Actually impressed by this case. Fits my NuVision 8 tablet very well and has notches that allow you to swipe from all sides of the screen. Case works well with or without the keyboard.Keyboard is small, cramped (which was expected), Bluetooth connection syncs in a few seconds, and the keyboard is held in place with strong magnets.Overall, for the price I paid and how well it works, I’m very pleased. Wouldn’t want to type a college term paper with this but it does work for what I need.

The overall quality is good, I am satisfied and happy with the purchase

The battery is not replaceable, and neither is it replaceable in the NuVision tablet… very disappointing. Insertion of the tablet is to this case is very difficult and seemed impossible without tearing or damaging the case… for a while. BUT, it will go in without damage if you are persistent and careful. The overall quality is good, I am satisfied and happy with the purchase. Too bad the tablet and the keyboard are ‘throwaway’ products once the batteries die, I’d like to keep them a long time..

Worked fine for about a month

Worked fine for about a month. Now it likes to randomly begin inserting the single quote/apostrophe symbol. That is a character I would have to hit the function key + o to pull up manually, so I don’t think it is a case of the o key getting stuck. Happens even when I haven’t pressed that key… or any key, really. Sad, because the case is cute and I liked the keyboard until it went wonky.

Wonderful case at a nice price. Excellent features.

I love how the case holds the keyboard magnetically. That makes it easy to detach and use anywhere. The kick stand on the case is also a very nice touch. This is a great little case for a nice price.

Nice case & keyboard combo for 8” tablets.

Very good keyboard and case for my NuVision 8” Win 10 tablet. The keyboard lasts many days on a charge and shuts down to save the battery all by itself. The case is well made and keeps the tablet securely in place. Glad I purchased it.

Great case and keyboard.

The case fits the tablet like a glove and is very secure. The keyboard paired up without a problem. The keys feel like a regular keyboard. Very happy with this purchase
