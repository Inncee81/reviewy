    Product Description

A Smart Way to Expand MacBook Storage Capacity Time to turn your unused microSD card into extra storage MacBook storage! The xDrive L03 microSD card adapter is designed for Apple users to instantly expand MacBook storage capacity with microSD cards. No hassle installation, it is the easiest and most flexible way to increase your MacBook’s storage space. About the L03 MacBook Adapter Support Micro SD Memory Cards up to 256GBThe L03 adapter can support the capacity of memory cards up to 256GB. Easier Operation with the Groove Design The special groove design on the edge of the card is made for easy removal with just a tip of your finger. About the High Endurance microSD Card Ideal for Write-intensive Applications The High Endurance microSD card is specially designed for write-intensive applications. It can be perfectly used on your 4K/Full HD video cameras, sports cameras, dash cameras, and home security cameras. High Endurance and Stable Performance Built with high quality MLC NAND flash chips, the micro SD card can withstand up to 12,000 hours of Full HD video recording, offering high endurance performance. To ensure the stability and reliability, it is also equipped with an automatic error correction code (ECC) function. Meets the UHS-I (U3) spec to enable fast data transmission Featuring the UHS-I U3 technology, the microSD card delivers fast read/write speed, ideal for continuous shooting and full HD and 4K video recording.To reach the read/write speed of UHS-I (U3) speed class, a UHS-I enabled and USB 3.0 reader or host device would be recommended. Built for Harsh Environments The High Endurance microSD card can survive from water, shock, X-rays, and wide-temperatures storage ranging from-40ºC to 85ºC.
