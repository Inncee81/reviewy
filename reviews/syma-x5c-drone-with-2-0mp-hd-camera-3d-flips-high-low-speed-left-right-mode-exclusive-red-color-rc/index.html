Product Reviews

Used as an awesome dog toy

 Quite possibly the best drone I have ever flown. I bought this as a toy to play with my pitbull with. I wanted something with a lot of agility so I could play chase with my dog. This drone does the trick! It is super light and amazingly agile. My dog has pulled it out of the sky and the blades do not cut him which is necessary. With the propeller guards and landing skids the dog has something to bite on without messing up the drone and so far has been awesome. The fact that all the parts are replaceable is great because eventually the dog will bite something too hard. I’ve replaced the propellers once already and it has a couple of bite marks in the frame but still flies perfect. I am glad the camera was removable and it was unnecessary for what I wanted to use it for. I would recommend this to all pilots, new and old, this thing is too good to pass up!I tried to take a video, it’s hard to hold a phone to film and use both hands to fly the drone!

Lots of fun!

Purchased for my 8 year old for Christmas. Fairly easy to learn and has held up for a day of flying on Christmas. Def pick up the spare batteries/charger and spare parts because the guards for the blades do break fairly easily. I think its an exellent price for the drone…it can go sky high and my son loves it. It synced fine upon turning it on. Haven’t tried using the camera yet. This is a great beginner drone for your kiddo…and for an adult…I’m having fun with it too!

You get what you pay for.

The drone functions more or less as advertised.The overall product just doesn’t feel like it is very high quality. There are a number of purely decorative buttons on the remote.The low quality is most evident in the included instruction manual which is somewhat confusing, appears to have been written for a different drone, and is very poorly translated from whatever language it was originally written in. I guess I should have expected as much when I purchased the cheapest available drone on amazon, but still was slightly disappointing.

RC

It’s amazing how stable it is. I’ve tried to master control of a quad-copter and found myself frustrated with how easily they slide off in some direction. While this one still exhibits momentum, it does at least level itself, which is incredibly helpful in keeping it in control.

Not what I expected

Was a little smaller than I expected and also only flies for 7 mins but takes An hour and 45 mins to charge back up. I wouldn’t have bought this had I known that. My husband had problems with it taking pictures as well.

Third time is a charm.

The second drone we got was used for sure and missing parts off the drone. Again returned item… Reordered for the 3rd time, this time the drone works. Not sure what is up with Syma.
