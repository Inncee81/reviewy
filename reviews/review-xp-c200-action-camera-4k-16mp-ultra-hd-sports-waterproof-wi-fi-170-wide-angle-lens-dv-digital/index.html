UPDATE - The supplier is awesome

I received this camera on July 14, 2017. I’ve since written to the vendor for assistance, but…… zero response. There are multiple issues.1. The camera periodically hangs when booting. The only way to clear it is to remove the battery.2. The time and date reset whenever the battery is removed or swapped out.3. There is no image stabilization, so using it while in motion produces less than desirable results.4. Attempting to take photos with the remote fails just about 4 out of 5 times, even though the indicator lights on the remote flash.5. The remote also fails frequently with video capture. In addition, if you aren’t looking at the screen(i.e. it’s on your helmet), you can’t tell if the video is recording. The lights on the remote work fine, but the camera fails to respond.6. Overall, when the video does record, the quality has been excellent. But if you move it too quickly, it will produce pixelation, especially when recording at 4K. Yes, I have a 64GB Samsung high speed micro SD card.The good? Lots of attachment goodies.Bottom line: I should have bought a higher quality product.UPDATE: The vendor had since responded and determined that I had received a defective unit. They offered to send me a replacement, free of charge. They also offered a 10% discount on my next purchase. Because I was having significant issues with image stabilization, I inquired about their 600 series camera. They offered the 10% off on the upgrade and fully refunded the original camera. Fantastic customer service. I’m looking forward to testing the new camera.
