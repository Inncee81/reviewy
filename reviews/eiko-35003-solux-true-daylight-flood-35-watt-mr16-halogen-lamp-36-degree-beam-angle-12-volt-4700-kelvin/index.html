    Product Description

The EiKO 35003 SoLux true daylight spot halogen incandescent light bulb with a 36-Degree beam angle is a 12-Volt, 35-Watt, 4700 Kelvin light bulb with a clear glass face. This MR16 bulb has a GU5.3 base, a C-8 filament, a candlepower of 3,998 and an average life of about 4000 hours. SoLux is the world’s only lighting technology that mimics natural daylight and allows products’ colors to be seen the way they naturally appear. This bulb will enhance the appeal of art, photography and jewelry and decrease product returns based on inaccurate color reproduction. The low IR and UV emission does not cause fading over time from IR or UV lighting exposure. Since 1978 EiKO has been providing only the best lighting in what has grown to include the automotive, industrial, electrical, electronics, audio/video, photographic and health care industries.
