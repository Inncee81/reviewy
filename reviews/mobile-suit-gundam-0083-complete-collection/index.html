Product Description

Universal Century 0083. Having triumphed in the One-Year War, the Earth Federation has grown complacent, while the last remnants of Zeon forces have been planning one final stand. It all hinges on ace Zeon pilot Anavel Gato stealing one of the new prototype Gundams out from under the Federation’s nose. With a nuclear-equipped Gundam missing and their pride wounded, the Federation ship Albion and rookie pilot Kou Uraki set out in pursuit of the Zeon thieves and the stolen Gundam GP02A.

Contains all 13 episodes Mobile Suit Gundam 0083: Stardust Memory plus the “Afterglow of Zeon” compilation film.

Mechanical Styling by Shoji Kawamori (Blue Sub. No.6, Cowboy Bebop, The Vision of Escaflowne). Character Designs by Toshihiro Kawamoto (Cowboy Bebop, Escaflowne: The Movie, Wolf’s Rain, Witch Hunter Robin).

Special Features: The animation shorts “The Mayfly of Space 1” and “The Mayfly of Space 2”, Clean Openings, Clean Closings, and Japanese Promos.

Audio: (TV episodes) English and Japanese LPCM Surround, English and Japanese LPCM Stereo; (Movie) Japanese LPCM Stereo. Subtitles: English.

Video: 1080p 4 x 3 native HD.

The Bridge Between Gundam and Zeta Gundam

0083 serves to fill in the gaps between the original Mobile Suit Gundam and it’s sequel Zeta Gundam. 3 years after the end of the One Year War, Zeon holdouts steal a prototype gundam armed with a nuclear warhead as part of Operation Stardust. The ultimate goal of this operation is to damage earth’s food supply by dropping another colony onto it. This series of events is taken advantage of by extremists within the the Federation who use the political and military fallout to seize power and eventually form the Titans, the brutal and ruthless antagonists of Zeta Gundam. While two antagonists from Zeta do make cameo appearances, the series does not require you to be familiar with Zeta to follow what transpires. It also gives a satisfying explanation for how the Federation so drastically changed between the events of Gundam and Zeta Gundam.
