Product Reviews

Great Baby/Toddler Bowl

These are amazing bowls. They hold 1 cup of liquid and are the perfect size for my toddler. These bowls are shallow and wide, making it easy for him to self-feed. The lids are great and fit on very securely. I use these bowls to make meals and snacks for him and then put them in the fridge for later. When I need to feed him, I just grab one and pop it open and microwave it if needed or throw the whole thing in my purse to take with us. I usually hand wash them so can’t say how they hold up in the dishwasher. These are definitely the best bowls I have used.

They are the perfect size for toddler meals (at least so far - …

We have two sets of these and use them everyday. They are the perfect size for toddler meals (at least so far - son is 21 months and a big eater). The lids snap on very tight and we’ve never had an issue with leaking, even though they sometimes get turned upside down due to enthusiastic shaking of our daycare bag. A couple of ours have stains from pasta sauce that I haven’t been able to get off, but that doesn’t bother me. At this price, you can always recycle the stained ones and order replacements. We do not put ours in the microwave (though they might at daycare), but we do wash them in the dishwasher on the top shelf.

I’ve gotten several of these because I love them so much

We use these every day to store my daughter’s snacks in the fridge and pack for her to go over to family’s house while they watch her 1-2 days a week. They are the perfect size for a small meal for a toddler or to pack several different snacks into one lunch bag. The see through tops are great too so you can tell what is inside without opening. The lids seal very well but aren’t tough to get off when you actually want to remove them. The colors are great too as it is easy to “color code” snacks ie, blueberries in the blue, mango in orange, etc if you want to.

Not for the dishwasher

These say dishwasher safe and microwaveable on the bottom, but after running them thru the dishwasher a handful of times, they all have that white damage to the plastic… I have tried scrubbing and it doesn’t come off. I’m not sure if it’s bad for the kids to eat out of them, but I’m not willing to find out. So, these will now be used for the toy kitchen.

Smaller, thinner than expected

The lids are convenient and the colors are bright. However, these bowls are thinner and have a smaller footprint than I anticipated. I quickly measured bowls I already owned to compare the sizes before ordering but was still a little disappointed. The rims reach the same diameter but the bottom is more tapered than I expected. Regardless, they are a good size for small toddler snacks but I still prefer larger bowls for meals. They do not warp in the dishwasher but I don’t expect them to last as long as more expensive bowls.
