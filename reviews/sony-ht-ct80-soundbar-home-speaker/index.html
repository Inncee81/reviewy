Product Reviews

Don’t let the score scare you!

I don’t understand the mixed reviews at all. I bought mine new from Walmart, it’s a great 2.1 soundbar miles above TV audio. I keep mine on the surround setting, sub up and it’s awesome. The optical port is what I use. Straight to TV and all my devices come through it. It has some nice thump. The optical cable was aittle thin and short but works great. If you need a longer one I’d say get one.Set up was fool proof, no worries about batteries and sub linked up for me out of the box without any input from me. This one might not bounce you off the floor and blow you back but it is an amazing 2.1 system for me. Bluetooth worked from my phone too.If you’re looking for a noticeable upgrade from you’re puny TV speakers this mid range 2.1 soundbar will be great.These reviews really confuse me. I’m glad I didn’t bother researching because I would’ve been put off by the 3.8 score.
