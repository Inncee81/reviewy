    Product Description

The Neatfreak garage/sport utility storage rack is a simple yet ideal vertical solution to control the chaos of storing your sports gear, Hockey sticks, Golf clubs, Baseball Bats, umbrellas, lacrosse sticks, cricket Bats, gardening equipment & tools . Tall items find a home in one of the 12 sections and stay vertical for easy access and quick grab. Storing Vertically protects your equipment and tools too. It stacks with the 3400 3 tier bin for increased vertical storage without taking up valuable floor space. You’ll have it assembled quickly and your gear will be organized in no time. Neatfreak is a trusted brand who develops innovative, attractive storage solutions for every room in your home. Garage and sports gear storage doesn’t have to be big, clunky and oversized. Our garage/sport utility storage rack can be used in your mudroom, basement, garage, front hall, equipment room or anywhere where you need a special place for all those tall pieces of equipment and tools.
