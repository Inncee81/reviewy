Product Reviews

Great product quality but I did not have the correct model.

I can tell Newmowa makes and delivers great quality products and I really value that. During my first time ever to buy and expand on my battery inventory for my Xiaomi Yi, it was difficult to understand if I had the international version versus the US version. I read the questions and comments section within the product listing to see what the community has pointed out on. After doing some reading, I was convinced that I had bought the correct set of batteries for my camera but didn’t turn out to be the case. It seems that the US version is the AZ13-2 model with a thicker size compared the international version is the AZ13-1 with a thinner size respectively. In my case, I misunderstood the model numbers by the difference in the 1 to 2 as potential revision numbers and maybe the actual model numbers differed in the first 2 characters (e.g. US13-1, AZ13-1, etc). It was not clear to me as I checked and compared the details of the international and US versions. Take note: the AZ13-2 battery is only compatible in US models and AZ13-1 is only compatible in international models. I hope future customers don’t get confused as I did.

I bought this charger with the two batteries for my new YI Action camera (U.S. model). I really like it. It’s hard to tell from the pictures but the charger is double sided with each side looking identical. It’s easy to use and allows for charging of two batteries at once.
