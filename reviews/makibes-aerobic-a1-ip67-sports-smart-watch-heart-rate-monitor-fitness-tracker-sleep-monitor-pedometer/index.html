Product Reviews

Cannot beat this smartwatch!

This little watch has become my favorite new workout companion. I work out 4-5 times a week and run each morning. I like to keep an idea on my heart rate during my workouts so I know when I am in the proper fat burning zone. This watch does just that and accurately. It sits well on the wrist and the rubber band is adjustable and comfortable. Even when sweating the watch does not feel as a distraction and breathes well. The pedometer functions is also accurate. I have tested it against the gps app on my phone and it is spot on. Notifications work well and the watch pairs easily with my Galaxy S7 Edge. The notification feature is great for me because I set me phone down during my workout and it is not on me so I am now informed if I have a call or message. I have worn the watch in the shower and swam with in the pool and it still works as should. No sign of any moisture collecting inside the unit. Great watch at a great price that performs just as well as the name brands
