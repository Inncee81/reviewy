    Product Description

Color:Red / Black  |  Style:Base Meet Bebop 2, a second-generation Parrot Quad copter Drone. First in its weight class to surpass 20 minutes of autonomous flight time, this sleek and durable drone doesn’t just travel far, it travels well. The 3-axis digital stabilization offers pilots a fixed angle of the view, regardless of altitude or turbulence. The 14mpx, 1080p camera captures the moment via live streaming video or recorded clips. Take in the horizon with a stunning 186° field of view and keep track of your location with the new, powerful rear flash LED. Fly for up to 25 minutes at speeds of up to 38 mph

Very bad quality control but very good drone

I feel like I should write two reviews. As received, in factory sealed box, the unit was very dusty. Other reviewers have mentioned this as well. More importantly, the left control stick was pushed down into the housing. The mounting was broken inside. I’m handy with tools, so I dismantled the sky controller and repaired it rather than go through the hassle of returning it. The box was undamaged, so I have to chalk this broken controller up to amazingly poor quality control and inspection. It shouldn’t be up to the buyer to do inspection and repair. Now for the second part of the review. The Bebop 2 is incredibly easy to fly. I have had great fun with it.
