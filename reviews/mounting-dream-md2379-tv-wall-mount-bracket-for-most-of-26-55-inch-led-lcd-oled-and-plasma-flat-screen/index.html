    Product Description

Fits 26-55” TVs

This mount fits most of 26-55” TVs sold today. It fits TVs with mounting holes as close as 3”X3” or as wide as 16”x16” (in TV terms - VESA 45x45mm to 400x400mm). Specifically, it fits VESA 75X75mm, 100X100mm, 200X100mm, 200X150mm, 200X200mm, 300X200mm, 300X300mm, 400X200mm, 400X300mm, 400X400mm.

Retracts 3.2” low profile, Extends 15.5” from the wall With articulating arm, this mount retracts to 3.2” (to wall) to save space, and extends up to 15.4” from the wall.

Tilt, Swivel and Rotate function +5°/-15° Tilt, +/-45°Swivel and +/-3° rotation for maximum viewing flexibility, provide smooth adjustment for all directions, making it easy to view your TV from multi-angles.

Heavy duty steel construction and automatic robot welding technology Constructed with heavy duty steel materials with durable powder coated finish, this mount is 100% welded by automatic robot. This advanced welding technology makes the mount sturdy and ensures mounted TV stable and secure, supporting TV weight up to 99 lbs.

6’ high speed HDMI cable 6’ HDMI cable supports 3D, Ethernet, 1440P and Audio Return conforming to latest HDMI 1.4 Standard.

Includes all necessary mounting hardware This mount ships with hardware for both wall and TV mounting. Anchor included is ONLY for concrete wall installation! This mount fits solid concrete wall/single wood stud installation. Caution:This mount is NOT for dry wall alone installatoin!

RoHS certified This product is RoHS certified (European Union directive standard of Restriction of Hazardous Substance), aiming to protect human health and environment.
