Product Reviews

Soft liner, nice stuff

Used this to line a big ol’ HDX tool chest’s drawers. It was easy to cut and feels great. I don’t put it to the test very often because I grab tools from the chest only about once a week, and my tool collection is paltry so there’s plenty of space. Thus far, however, it is holding all the tools, looking good, protecting the drawers from scratches and generally being a great liner.Bottom line for me: I’ll buy more of this exact JEGS liner should the need arise again.Note: I did NOT notice a smell of gasoline on the liner, like a handful of other reviews have mentioned. I’m a pretty weird dude that frequently smells stuff he buys from Amazon so I’d have noticed. Might’ve been lucky, or perhaps the fact that I only bathe once a decade has something to do with it, but seriously the liner smells only faintly of plastic. Nothing out of the ordinary.

Excellent drawer liner

This is exactly what I wanted, a solid liner that is easy to cut to fit, lays flat and doesn’t slide around when opening/closing drawers and moving tools around, and doesn’t let dirt/debris get down to the drawer surface. I don’t care for the open grid style as it can let dirt and traces of oil from tools fall through the liner.

Great Mat, Great Price

Great mat for the price! Generally the same price as the cheaper foam mats with the holes and is far easier to cut to size. Surprisingly it also came with next day since JEGS is local. Will definitely be buying more to do my other tool chests. May need double sided tape in the corners in drawers that see a lot of tool movement so it doesn’t bunch or shift.
