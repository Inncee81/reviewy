Much better than headstrap mounts

I had previously purchased a head lamp style holder for the go pro but I really hate how that feels on my head. So I saw this product and thought i’d much rather wear a hat instead of a head lamp. I had a bit of a challenge hooking it on. I think I just had the connector upside down. Being new to GoPro, I wasn’t used to the connectors or how to use them on various attachments and accessories.Once I got it hooked up, it was pretty cool. I could walk around and get the benefits of a hat (which I always wear) blocking out the sun, not leaving my head exposed for sunburn, etc. The headstrap mounts are useless in the sun in my opinion.Overall I though this was one of my better accessories for the Gopro. I’m glad someone was very creative here in coming up with a cool way to attach a gopro instead of the countless headstraps.

Why didn’t GoPro think of this…

Made well has velcro adjustment and is comfortable. The GoPro mount is a little crooked. Seller sent me another and it’s a little crooked too. So you can buy from these guys they are legit. The manufacturer may have dished out a bad batch. Overall this is pretty close to a must buy if you own an action cam and want to be hands free casually wearing a hat. The brim does not get in the shot even in super view unless you point it down quite a bit. I put my session on it and it’s an awesome combo. I also put my Cube and it is fantastic. It’s the lightest of them all. Having a GoPro with a frame case is not bad. When you have a GoPro in the WP housing you can feel the weight just a tad but it’s not uncomfortable. I don’t know why GoPro hasn’t thought of this. They would probably charge an arm and a leg like they do all their accessories LOL. This is solid product and I recommend it. You will especially enjoy this with the Session or Cube and even the Mobius.
