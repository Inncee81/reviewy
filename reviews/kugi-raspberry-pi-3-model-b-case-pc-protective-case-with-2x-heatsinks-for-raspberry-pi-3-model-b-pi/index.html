It’s not much, so not much to say about …

It’s not much, so not much to say about it. The mounting posts for the board and all the openings are positioned correctly, everything fits well. I would have appreciated some kind of blank inserts or a closed, break-away design for the often unused connector openings (GPIO, display, camera), but it’s just a cosmetic consideration.Tip: the mounting posts for the four small screws to hold the board are not pre-threaded. The assembly will be much easier if you first screw them in without the board, to cut some thread. The board makes it awkward and you also wouldn’t want the screwdriver to slip and damage it.

Good for protecting against dirt and debris, while still leaving accessibility of ports.

Does the trick. This clear case has the exposed feel that seems to fit with a Pi but also manages to keep dust and debris from falling on the board. All of the port holes align nicely and even the SD card slot is accessible if you have various OS or builds that you swap out. The heat sink fits in the case as well.The SDI (camera) slot is only accessible if you open the case, but there is a hole so you can feed the ribbon and camera through and mount it externally. Same goes for the GPIO pins, must be open to easily connect/disconnect but once done you can feed wires through the hole.Screw bosses make it possible to firmly secure the board but the fit of the case is such that it is not entirely necessary.

Great Fitment

Great fit. I haven’t had any issues with any part of this case. Seems to be pretty well designed and produced.Edit: To provide a bit more information about the KuGi Raspberry Pi 3 Model B Case - My Raspberry Pi has been in this case for a few months now. I have yet to run into any issues with overheating or port-blocking. The little rubber feet on the bottom are a nice addition to prevent the case from sliding around on hard surfaces. The kit includes screws to secure the board to the case which is nice because you don’t have to worry about it coming loose and getting jostled around

Great Value.

Great value for what it is. You can definitely find a better case for the Raspberry Pi but probably not at this price. It doesn’t hold the unit in tight and is a little to quick to pop apart. But for the price it is a great case for the Raspberry Pi 3 board and works as it is intended to, to ensure that the Raspberry Pi 3 board does not get accidentally damaged. If you are looking for a really tight case with some additional features you will need to spend a bit more money.

Great product that shows off the lights and board.

Great case for raspberry pi model B+

Awesome case, with included mounting screws, heatsinks and rubber feet.
