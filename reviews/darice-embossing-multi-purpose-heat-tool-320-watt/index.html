Highly recommended

I use mine to fast dry finish I put on my wood turnings, especially when I apply wood dye. It drys it off much quicker than the old hair dryer I was using. I plan to use it for other uses as they come to me. I love the quiet operation. It doesn’t blow like a hair dryer so, if you’re working with something that you don’t want blown all over the place, this is heating what you want to heat without forced air movement.

Does the job of larger heat guns at 1⁄3 the size.

I use this tool primarily for heat shrink tubing and unlike my larger heat gun, its a lot easier to control thanks to its compact size and light weight. Best of all the outside plastic nozzle stays cool to the touch so no more burnt fingers like with the larger gun I have with its metal nozzle.

Perfect for electronics

I got this for heat-shrink tubing for electronics projects. It is perfect for this use - it is small, fits easily on my desk and heats up fast. It delivers much more concentrated heat than a traditional heat-gun so it wont affect other surrounding parts as much. It is easy to hold and maneuver around parts. Highly recommended for people doing electronics.
