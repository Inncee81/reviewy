    Product Description

Wilson & Miller knows how important it is to have the right bag for your expensive tools, and the Heavy Duty Tool Bag, Desert Camo, is designed with plenty of features to make storing and transporting your gear easy and convenient. A large main compartment unzips with a wide opening to give you easy access to all of your tools at a glance, and outer pockets let you store smaller accessories close at hand for when you need them most. Two drop-in pockets give you easy access to your tape measure or the tool you use the most, depending on your needs, and an exterior pocket with flap closure helps you secure smaller tools safely and securely while keeping them close at hand.

You have options for transport with the Wilson & Miller Heavy Duty Tool Bag. A removable shoulder strap lets you sling the bag on your shoulder when it’s time to go, or the sturdy handles make it easy to grab the bag and run when time is of the essence. And a reinforced bottom keeps the Heavy Duty Tool Bag from getting scuffed and torn, and provides a stable base when you need to get at your tools.

Whether you need a toolkit in the back of your truck for repairs on the road or just want a handy tool bag for organizing all of your hunting gear, the Wilson & Miller Heavy Duty Tool Bag gets the job done.
