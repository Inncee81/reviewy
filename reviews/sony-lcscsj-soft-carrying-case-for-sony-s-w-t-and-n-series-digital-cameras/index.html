    Product Description

Keep your digital camera within easy reach while protecting it from bumps and scratches with this soft carrying case. Store extra memory cards and small accessories in the exterior pouch. Constructed from a durable nylon material, the LCSCSJ carrying case is perfect for toting your camera everywhere you go while protecting it from harm. With a casual look and detachable shoulder strap, this case is the ideal solution for those who like to travel with their gear. A convenient front pocket holds additional memory cards and other small accessories. This compact, lightweight case is designed for use with your Cyber-shot S, W, T, and N Series digital cameras.

You can travel with confidence knowing your precious expensive top-line point and shoot camera will be protected for almost anything as its safely residing inside this case.It comes with a neck strap so you can hang it around your neck for easy access, or you can utilize the belt buckle case and attach it to your hip there. The case also comes with an assortment of small pockets, perfect for the extra memory card or usb cable that you might need for whatever occasion.The only problem, not indicative of the case, is that it doesn’t fit in a pocket nicely. But you already knew that if you have a Sony RX100 camera (any mk). As long as you’re aware of that, this is the perfect case for your camera.

A Case That Fits

It works for me!!! My Panasonic Lumix ZS60 fits snugly into this case. There is a larger front zippered section that holds one battery sized 1 5⁄8” X 1.5” X 1⁄2” in a mesh pocket and another front smaller zippered pocket that will hold a memory card in its case. There is rigid padding on both the front and back of the case in the section that holds the camera. The top and bottom of the case feels like it has some foam padding. This carrying case is for only use when you’re out and about. It won’t carry any extra batteries nor will it hold a battery charger. It includes a shoulder strap and on the back of the case there is strap that you can slide your belt into to hold the case at your waist. Reminder: the case fits snugly as my camera measures approximately 4 1⁄4” wide X 2 5⁄8” high X 1.5” deep. This case is stain resistant but on the back of a card that accompanies the camera it states that “the effect of water repellency processing is not continued permanently”. Hope this helps anyone trying to find a case for their camera
