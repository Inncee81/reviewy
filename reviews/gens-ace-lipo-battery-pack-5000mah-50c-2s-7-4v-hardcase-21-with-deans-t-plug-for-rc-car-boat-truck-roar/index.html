Batteries work well for us …but check the length

These batteries come at a great price and work well for us.While it is no fault of the manufacturer I prefer the batteries with cable leads that plug into the top side of the battery in lieu of these with the cables coming out the end. Something to keep in mind for your own cars. I cannot use these in one of my cars because there is not room to tuck the cables in with the battery. The cables add to the overall length you need to set the battery in place. If your batteries fit really tight along the length then these might not work for you. Still 5 stars because they perform well in all our other cars and it is not their fault. I will just need to buy the hard case batteries for one car.
