Product Reviews

Great except for the mounting template

I purchased this mount to hold a 32” TV perpendicular to the wall. It articulates great, it holds the TV (not with the included screws, none of which were the correct size) solidly. I took off 1 star because the cardboard template they give you to mark the holes in the drywall into the stud was backwards, which I realized before drilling. I reversed the template, and all was well. It took me a little longer to do the install because of the confusion of the template, but it was still only about 30 minutes total (including reading the directions several times).

Solid TV Wall Mount w/adjustments

I really like this mount. It is very sturdy and adjustable. I was going to knock it down to 4 stars because the VESA mount screws I needed for my 32” Emerson aren’t one of the sized provided in the packaging. If you have this type of TV it uses M4 16 mounting screws which cost me about a $1 at the local hardware store. I also like how you can adjust the leveling of the unit to make sure the TV remains level. I like this mount so much I plan on buying another one for a different TV.

Great fit, Great price.

I bought this item because it was on sale, couldn’t be more happy with it! Very solid product, Easy install. Product came with extra brackets for mounting different size tv’s but didn’t need them for the size of tv I used. Will definitely recommend and buy again for another bedroom tv install.

well built and easy to install

This is our 2nd WALI and it is strong, well built and easy to install. We mounted a 40” LED TV in our office/exercise room. The WALI adjusts easily and the TV can be swung to view from the desk or from the treadmill. WALI bolts to a stud with 4 lag bolts. The mounting plate and pivot arms are steel and feel like truck parts. Adapters are included to fit most any monitor. A great product at a great price.

Good, low cost wall mount

I found it easy to install and it works as advertised. I read where others didn’t like it because it didn’t hold their TV up - I have had no problems at all. I expect their TV might have exceeded the weight limits which are clearly indicated in their description, and on the box/instructions when you receive the product.

Good quality, great value

Less expensive option than other mounts out there and is really solid and well constructed. Used for a 40”+ TV in my workshop and it worked great.

Very solid assembly

Great wall mount assembly - everything is included with the kit. Very solid material used. Only fault I could find is even after tightening the two knobs that secure the bracket attached to the TV to the actual wall mount - every time I’m move the TV it rotates. The manufacturer uses a vertical saddle slot on top and horizontal slot on bottom - this allows the rotation to occur. Not to worry the Manufacturer includes a micro level. I will try adding neoprene washers to saddle and slot to see if that helps with the TV slipping.

Overall a great purchase that I’m happy I made.
