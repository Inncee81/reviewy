Excellent Overall

Excellent quality open frame housing for situations wherein the camera won’t be getting wet. It fits perfectly for my GoPro Hero 3 Silver. It comes with the tripod socket mount, regular mount, and lens cap. It is thoughtfully designed, so you have access to the HDMI socket, mini USB socket, and the micro SD card. There are holes for the internal microphone, WiFi button, power button, and beeper. The overall profile of the GoPro camera is noticeably smaller and lighter in weight with this open frame mount, compared to the waterproof dive housing. The price beats any others out there, too.
