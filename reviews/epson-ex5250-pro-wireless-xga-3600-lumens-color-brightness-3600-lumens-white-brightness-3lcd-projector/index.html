This will be one of several reviews. I’ll write more as I use the product. I bought this projector because of the positive reviews and glad that I did. This projector has an amazing picture and is very easy to use. I have it at ten feet and have about a 94 inch picture. Currently using the living room wall, which is egg shell, works just fine though ,and like I said, it’s an awesome picture.I’ve heard some comment about poor sound and that the fan is loud. Well, my wife has asked me on several occasions if I could turn it down a little. I have purchased surround sound speakers for this, haven’t used them yet, really don’t need to…. As far as the fan goes, I can’t hear it when I’m watching a movie, and when viewing pictures, I can barley hear it.. I have mine connected to my lap top via an HDMI cable and have been watching Netflix. Actually when using my lap top now I just use the projector as my monitor. It really is an awesome picture. I have had several friends and family over to either watch a movie or look at my daughter’s weeding pictures, and they all agree, the picture is truly HD….I use to have a 60 inch television and this picture is better, and much easier on your eyes…I’m feeling a little guilty though, and almost regret buying it now, because I’m spending more time watching movies and neglecting some of my chores..LOL
