Using the SX-500 for workouts on my Jaybird Bluebuds X Sport earbuds

I was debating either a 4 or 5 star on this review simply due to the price. I ended up giving a 5 though after trying a couple cheap knock-offs that were terrible.I use these on my Jaybird Bluebuds X Sport. The first time I went to put these on, I was not sure they were going to fit (as some other reviewers have mentioned), but they did indeed stretch enough to fit and they stay on very nicely. I use the headphones strictly for my workouts, which is ~ 90% lifting weights with a little cardio added in.A few things I was looking for when purchasing these:1) Better isolation than the stock Jaybird tips, but less isolation than the Comply Isolation tips. These are the perfect in-between! With my music on (fairly loud), it eliminates almost all noise around me. But if I turn my music down or off, I can easily have a conversation and hear what is going on around me.2) Being able to stay in my ears - I sweat quite a bit in the summer while working out and I’m always annoyed when my earbuds start slipping out of my ears. While I can’t say these are 100% foolproof in this area, they are definitely some of the best ones I’ve used.3) Comfort - it wouldn’t matter how well they performed if they hurt. I have absolutely no complaints about comfort with these.4) Price - this is where I was debating knocking off a star. They’re expensive! But after trying some cheaper options and wasting more money, I have come to the conclusion that they are worth the money.5) Sound - I do feel these help the sound with the isolation and being able to keep the earbuds in my ears where I want them.Overall I highly recommend these to anyone that is active and keep in mind that the 500 series does fit on the Jaybird Bluebuds…just be careful putting them on and they’ll stretch right over.

Game Changing Earbud Tips

I use these for my powerbeats which I use when I workout at the gym. The silicone tips that come with the powerbeats and most other earbuds do not seal properly thus letting much of the lower frequencies escape. Also, the silicone tips often fall out of your ears as your activity increases and you sweat making it merely impossible to enjoy your music properly. The Comply Foam Tips change everything. The specialized foam conforms precisely to your ears and make a secure fit that will not easily fall out with sweat or movement. You get the full range of frequencies broadcast by the speakers giving you a much fuller sound. Similar to foam ear plugs used for hearing protection, they block out outside noise allowing you to exclusively listen to your music. These foam earphone tips make even cheap earbuds sound excellent. The only drawback is that they deteriorate over time and will need to be replaced. You will need be sure they have a strong bond and and remain intact after several uses. Although they do last a pretty long time, the last thing you want is a piece of foam lodged in your ear canal. So just be careful. Overall, these are game changers. I wont listen to any earbuds without them.
