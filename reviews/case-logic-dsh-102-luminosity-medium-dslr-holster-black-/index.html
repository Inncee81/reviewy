Product Reviews

it will accommodate a pretty long lens

A very practical camera bag for a DSLR! Extended, it will accommodate a pretty long lens. Alternatively, you can use the space for a second lens. Works really well with my Canon 6D. I’m sure it will take almost any full frame camera, but not with a battery grip. I haven’t tried it, but it doesn’t seem to have enough room. It’s good for APS-C cameras also, for which I first bought it. Materials and construction seem very good.There are internal and external pockets, which are limited, but very useful. They’ll easily hold filters, cables, data cards, and spare batteries. Some setups might allow for a small flash, or a charger, but that’s a stretch. (Yeah, I said it.)No camera bag is good for everyone, and on every day, but this bag accommodates a wide niche of people, who have formidable cameras that need protection when they (the people, but applies to the cameras, too) are not shooting.

Little clunky, like the rain cover.

I was hoping for a compact camera bag for my Canon T6. I like things small and I thought this holster would do the trick. It’s quite a bit clunkier than I thought it would be. This is good because it provides padding, but it’s larger than necessary and, because of the shape, you can’t really store anything other than a camera in it. This amounts to some wasted space. The zip pockets are good for storing extra batteries and SD cards. The big one in the back is pretty flat and so far I’ve just stored my polarizing lens in there, other than that, they’re not good for much.The zipper isn’t really quick to undo for a fast draw, I think I would have preferred some sort of snap mechanism. I do like how it comes with a rain cover. That’s a nice feature.I guess I need to be a little more picky when shopping for bags. Not really what I was looking for maybe. Not really their fault though.

Just go for it.

Fulfills the purpose very well. Great product for the price. I’m using this for my Canon Rebel T3i which has the 18-55mm lens. There’s place for a spare battery, cables, charger. The camera fits very well and it feels snug too. The cushioning is great and offers great confidence that the camera is safe inside. The zips are smooth and function well. The bag is reasonably light too. The materials used for this bag are amazing. I’m just concerned about the latches that keep the strap hooked to bag. I’m sure they’ll last long as the bag itself isn’t heavy.
