Excellent protection for your camera’s LCD screen

This is a replacement for the first one I installed 2 years ago, which cracked when I dropped my Nikon D300. It did its job by protecting the LCD, so I decided to buy another. The original never scratched and was clear to the point of not affecting the image on the LCD, so these screen protectors do what they claim. If I had a complaint it would be they are not easy to remove, but that is probably a benefit, as they stay put. I tip for anyone that has to remove theirs: use tape, preferably clear packing tape, to cover the entire piece before removing. This will keep it from coming off in many pieces that can potentially cut you. It is glass, after all. I decided to use clear tape so I could see what I was doing. Beyond that, follow the directions for removal on the package, if you are replacing a broken one. Most of all, take your time! These are easy to install, just keep your fingers from touching the surface before applying, because they are not easy to remove without breaking. I would highly recommend these for LCD protection.
