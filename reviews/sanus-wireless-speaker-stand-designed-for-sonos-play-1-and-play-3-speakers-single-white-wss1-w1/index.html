Product Reviews

Almost 100% perfect

Excellent quality. Easy assembly. The product is very sturdy a well design. I am using it for the Play 1 speakers and it looks a bit bulky, however, for Play 3 or 5 speakers this product will be perfect. The only thing I am questioning is the unfinished (unpainted) “L” shape bracket. If the speaker is located in the corner and against the wall, it does not matter. But if it is away from the wall, you will have to look at a raw grey sheet metal bracket and that is not pretty. I will end up spray painting it, but for the cost of $100 each, I shouldn’t have to do that.

The price is right on these stands but the plastic …

The price is right on these stands but the plastic cups that hide the cords on top and bottom are awful. I got three stands and on one of the three I literally had to cut off the plastic cups. I would not buy again due to the that inconvenience.

These speaker stands are terrific. The base of the stand is of sufficient …

These speaker stands are terrific. The base of the stand is of sufficient weight to prevent accidental tip-over. Designed so my SONOS play 1 speakers could be mounted quick and easy. Highly recommend.

These stands are great for Sonos Play 1 and Play 3

These stands are great for Sonos Play 1 and Play 3. Easy to put together and brackets included for Play 1, and 2 positions (horizontal and vertical) for Play 3. Mounted both Play 1 and Play 3 speakers and liked them so much I bought another pair to permanently mount both sets. Highly recommended.

Pretty good! The assembly directions were remarkably clear but …

Pretty good! The assembly directions were remarkably clear but remember to run the plug though the tube before tightening screws. This product is solid, stable, and good looking although a tad bulky. I’m pleased and glad I got them.

The stand is very sturdy because the base is has some weight to it

The stand is very sturdy because the base is has some weight to it. The stand was easy to assemble. I bought the white stand. It is hardly noticeable against the wall. I really like that the cord can be fed down the tube of the stand. The only cord you can see is the small section that plugs into the wall outlet. I would recommend this stand for any SONOS Play 1, 3, or 5 speakers.

Does job but for cost should have better, sleeker design

It does the simple job it is advertised to do, but I am very unimpressed with the design, size, and cost of it. It has a nice wide heavy base to keep it stable, however the post is large and looks silly when you place a Play1 on it. We have a Play3 on it and given it’s noticeable size it is unseemly. It is wide so you can hide wires in it, but look us I assume most Sonos customers use wirelessly and only have power cord. Wish we purchased a different brand that offers a smaller post that is not as noticeable.
