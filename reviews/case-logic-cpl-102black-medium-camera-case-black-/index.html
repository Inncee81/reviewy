Product Reviews

Perfect for Panasonic ZS50

I have three versions of this case for different cameras. They all protect the camera well, especially the moderately delicate automatic lens cover.This comes with a strap, has a belt loop that will handle 2 3⁄4” and has an open internal pocket.I bought this for a Panasonic ZS50. You can also stuff the ZS50 in a slightly smaller Case Logic DCB-302, but it’s tight.

If you have a Canon Powershot G7x, this is the case for you

I was looking for a small case to protect my Canon Powershot G7x when I put it in a bag or am not immediately using it. I was having a hard time finding a case with reviews specifically from people who owned the G7x. The G7x is a uniquely sized Point & Shoot as it’s a little larger than most, yet still quite compact. I also wanted a small pocket inside the case to store an extra battery and memory card. This case fits the bill perfectly, it is literally the perfect size for the G7x. There is also a small pocket on the inside where I was able to fit two extra batteries (and of course a memory card if you want). There’s not too much to the case, it looks nice and will protect from scratches. My only knock on this case is that it has no hard shell to actually provide any type of drop protection. For what it is, it’s worth the price and will do the job I need it to. Great case for any Canon Powershot G7x owners!

Works well for a Sony RX100

Bought it for a Sony RX100 M2 and it works fairly well - fits the camera snugly, with an internal pocket for an additional battery and other accessories. Also looks pretty nice.I can’t imagine who would use this with the shoulder straps, given it’s so tiny. I just cut the strap loops off, and use it with the belt loop, or just as padding with the camera in the bag.Would be nicer if the accessory pocket was on the outside rather than the inside, as it could probably hold more. Case Logic makes other bags like that, although they are smaller overall.

Great for golf rangefinder

I bought this for a golf rangefinder, it works perfectly. There has been no scratching or other damage to my rangefinder after 10+ rounds with this protecting it between shots. Highly recommend for golfers. (I’d imagine it would do the job it was designed for and protect small cameras too)
