Totally solid for my gaming and office needs.

This mouse arrived yesterday. After one day of use, I’m very pleased by how the shape feels in my hand (very similar to Dynex’s cheapest mouse, which I am replacing). Upon plugging it in, I noticed immediately that the pointer was really sensitive (since it is a 4000dpi mouse), which I didn’t like. I don’t have a CD drive on my computer, so I couldn’t use that to install the drivers, but the XM6 driver is available on Joteck’s poorly-translated website. With the drivers installed, you can switch between mouse sensitivities, which is very nice for gaming. Have to have lower mouse sensitivity when Mercy has to pull out her pistol! ;) The clicks are very satisfying, the scroll wheel gives some decent tactile feedback, the totally unnecessary LED lights are cool, and the buttons are all nicely placed.

Meets all my expectations

This is my first gaming mouse, so I can’t provide much judgement in comparison to other gaming mice, but this has perfectly suited my needs. All buttons are comfortable to reach and both easy and satisfying to click without any chance of being hit accidentally. I have not used the triple-click feature so I cannot vouch for that, though I wish I could set it as an additional mouse button rather than setting it to a key on my keyboard and then setting the function of that key in-game. Also it should be noted that, no, it’s not actually possible to make a mouse that will fit any grip style on any hand size - for me it forced me more into a palm grip than what I’m used to, but it’s very comfortable and I don’t mind the change. I’m also slightly disappointed that it’s impossible to turn the LED completely off, but you can turn it down very low to where it’s not bothersome.Certainly overall very happy with the mouse, and I hope to keep using it for a long time.
