Product Specifications

  • Model: Tactical Vest; Size: One Size; Material: Nylon.
  • 7 rows of 1” straps on front side of the vest; 8 rows of 1” straps on back side of the vest.
  • 1” molle webbing cross straps for attaching molle pouches; 1 triple magazine pouch.
  • 1 map and flashlight pouch; 1 medic pouch

  • Color: As seen in the photo

    Product Description

A lightweight camouflage combat vest. Has 3 lower pockets, with a velcro fasten, one smaller upper pocket, also with a velcro fasten, and a side molle pouch with a zipper. It is fully adjustable. One size fits most. It is fastened at both sides with velcro and at the shoulder. Can be used for airsoft or costume wear. Not suitable for military soldier or law enforcement professional.

Product Reviews

Great starter and moderate airsoft vest

I am 5’10”. 150 lbs. and this vest feels good. It is lightweight with decent padding inside and stitching is pretty good. Adjusting the vest isn’t difficult. It took 5 minutes max. It may feel a little loose but if you put on a sweatshirt, jacket, ect. then it is a good, real combat fit. it is tidy and doesn’t prevent any arm movements. There is room for more pouches if need be but so far ive been in 5 wars with it as is and it is holding up great. I’d recommend just getting 2 pistol mag pouches. However, all depends on how much you use your sidearm. I use mine frequently. This vest is definitely worth the money for someone looking for a decent quality and priced vest. This would be ideal for an airsoft starter.
