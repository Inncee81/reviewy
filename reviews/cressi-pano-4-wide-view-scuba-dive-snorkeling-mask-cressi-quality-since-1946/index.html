Nice small fitting dive mask

I had bought this thing as a replacement for a much larger dive mask I bought from my SCUBA instructor that had a bad habit of getting caught on my widows peak.It’s only pro is that it did it’s main job of getting the skirt off my widows peak, but that’s probably the nicest thing I have to say about it.This one feels tight on the face but not around the head, and I can’t find a replacement head band to try to fix it.Through the whole first two dives with it, it slowly and continuously began pushing up against the septal cartilage of my nose. Which was extremely uncomfortable.The lense was clear and didn’t fog up through the whole dive, but the angled glass on the sides heavily distorted anything in the corner of my eyes. A couple times I thought I was ready to bump should with my dive buddies, only to turn my head and see them several feet away.I’m not saying it’s a terrible mask, just doesn’t really fit well on the head.

Looks better than it works

Really liked the look of these but the panoramic side windows actually create like a reflection or ghost image that is very distracting. I found myself jerking my head to see something that wasn’t actually there, just a reflected image. Unfortunately I will probably not be using them again.

I also purchased the dry snorkel with it that matches as well and it makes for a nice set! I will be purchasing another one for my son as well!
