    Product Description

Product Description Constructed with an ergonomic design the Black & Decker Smart Select Drill Driver offers improved comfort and control for hassle-free use. The easy to operate driver’s smart select clutch allows user to choose project drill then automatically adjusts to correct power and speed for optimal results every time. Features a two speed switch, 0-400⁄0-1200 RPM, 3⁄8 chuck, high or low setting indicator, high torque motor delivering 130 in-lbs., metal chuck providing durability, and a slide pack battery offering quick removal and installation. Driver includes 12V battery, charger and a double ended screwdriver bit tip.

From the Manufacturer The Black & Decker SS12C 12-Volt Cordless Drill/Driver incorporate the SmartSelect Clutch system; choose your project icon and the drill automatically adjusts the power and speed for optimal results. The gear Indicator has 2 Speeds-indicates high or low setting. It has a high torque motor that delivers 130-Inch-pounds of force. It also has a metal chuck for durability and an ergonomic design for improved comfort and control. The slide pack battery is easy to remove and install.
