Perfect in Early 2015 Retina MacBook Pro 13”

Fits perfectly in my Early 2015 Retina MacBook Pro 13”. I paired this with a 200GB MicroSD card also sold here on Amazon (speed class UHS1), it was seen right away and I setup FileVault and moved my Documents and Photos folders off to the card and turned off journaling to reduce write wear on the MicroSD card. You will want to keep things that change infrequently or not at all on the card. While it is still a magnitude of order slower than the internal PCIe SSD, it is still plenty zippy.

Great product, though once inserted it will be more challenging to remove

Overall, this is a great product, and it does what it says it does. I take off a star because it isn’t easy to remove the item. While there is enough of a indent where you’d think you can use your fingernail, you’ll likely break it as it needs a bit more strength. This is fine as most aren’t removing this frequently (I’m not), but there should likely be some tool provided as I do fear jamming things in there and breaking the device or my SD card.

Works great in an Aspire

Used it to add another 64 gigabytes to the 32 onboard my Acer Aspire ES1 that has no SATA header. It’s much shorter than the card slot, so I had to shove it in far enough so that it won’t ever come back out. I’m not planning on taking it out though, and it works perfectly fine so I’m not complaining. The aluminum frame felt durable and should hold up well if you frequently remove it.If you’re planning on buying this for something other than a macbook, you may not be able to get it back out as even the shallower readers in windows laptops tend to be deeper than the macbooks. It’s just a card reader, so as long as it physically fits, it should work with any computer. Make sure your drivers work too, as my win 10 ones give me trouble often. If you are upgrading the storage on a windows laptop with those annoying eMMC drives, have a header and don’t need the cheapest solution, an SSD would be a better option. But for those that can’t have one (like me) these little devices are the way to go!The measurements are .98” (width) x .63” (depth) x .07” (height). 25 x 16 x 2 in millimeters.
