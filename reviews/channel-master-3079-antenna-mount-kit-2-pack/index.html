Channel Master 3079 Antenna Mount Kit - 2Pack

I have bought many items made by Channel Master and all of them are excellent in quality.I needed the mounting bracket to mount an antenna mast for our Davis Weather Station on our garage roof. Since I had to drill into the stucco, the screws provided were too long, and loose in the stucco since I could not find any wood behind the stucco.I got a shorter screw with a plastic insert to put into the stucco hole that I drilled, and it made for a better and more secure mounting of the two brackets into the stucco wall.With the antenna mast that I bought via Amazon, and the two brackets I now have a solid and secure mount for my weather station that is 25 feet above ground level for an unobstructed view to measure wind speed and other measurements.You will not be disappointed with the Channel Master brackets, and the price was cheaper than at my local hardware stores. The package on Amazon says it is from ACE Hardware, but it is a Channel Master product.

Some details

These are decent. Not intended for a 30 foot pole but good for a light to medium weight antenna. The included screws provided to attach the mounts are not adequate. Also, the mounting holes for attaching the bracket to the building are on 6.5” centers. Just about all wall studs are on 16” centers so I would recommend stopping by the local hardware store for some toggle bolts for hollow walls and lag bolts for studs. Good mount and I would recommend it.
