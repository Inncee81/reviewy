Product Specifications

  • COMPATIBLE FIT FOR MOST 22”-52” FLAT-PANEL TVs: Designed to accommodate almost all 22”-52” and many 55” LED, LCD, Plasma and other flat-screen displays, this heavy-duty TV mount is fully rated to support up to 80 pounds.
  • VERSATILE, LOW-PROFILE DESIGN: Multi-purpose, sleek design offers a stylish look to suit any décor. The mount compresses to just 1.75” and extends up to 19.25” from the wall. The articulating arm allows for offset mounting, where the panel (when extended out on the arm) can be shifted horizontally up to 17.5” left or right.
  • MAXIMUM TILT & SWIVEL CAPACITY: Enjoy comfortable, optimum viewing from nearly any angle with a +10°/-2° tilt capacity, along with a 180° swivel capability.
  • VESA COMPLIANT: Supports VESA mounting standards including 100mm x 100mm, 100mm x 200mm, 200mm x 200mm. Included extensions that are easily added with two screws each supports 200mm x 400mm, 300mm x 300mm, and 400mm x 400mm.

  • INCLUDES COMPLETE MOUNT HARDWARE: Everything needed to mount is conveniently packed in this set, including HDMI cable, magnetic level, installation wrench, full hardware package, and instructions.

    Product Description

Upgrade your TV viewing experience and save valuable floor space with the Mount Factory TV mount, offering over 19” inches of extension, 180° swivel, and +10°/-2° tilt capacity to reach the best angle possible. Solid, heavy-gauge steel construction mount fits virtually all 22”-52” LED, LCD, Plasma and similar flat-screen display TVs.

Smooth articulation from the wall makes it easy to point the TV exactly where you want it for the best view or to reduce glare, while a slim, low-profile design allows it to compactly compress to just 1.75” when not in use.

To ensure fit of the mounting pattern, check the VESA pattern for your panel, either online or by measuring between the screw holes on the rear of the TV or panel (100mm is about 4”).

Specifications: Fully rated for up to 80 pound displays. RoHS, CE, and ISO-9001 certified.

Complete mount package includes: pre-assembled, 12-gauge powder-coated, heavy-duty steel mount, a magnetic level for even mounting, optional mounting extensions for use with larger TVs, installation instructions, and a full hardware package complete with an installation wrench, stud wall screws, masonry anchors, mounting screws, washers, and spacers.

It’s not perfect, but close enough. The TV wall mount came as advertised and thanks to Prime I had in on my doorstep in 2 days. This is a beefy (and heavy) mount. I can’t review the Sheetrock anchor system that is included because I screwed into a stud, or the mount extenders as my little Sammy fit the 4 holes in the base plate so didn’t need to add the wings. The only “bad” thing I ran into was the mounting screws that attach the TV to the mounting plate. The included screws were too short to also use the spacers, but were about 1⁄8” too long to screw directly into the TV. I really don’t blame anyone for this and was prepared to drive to the HW store to buy the correct screws, but found that a couple of thick washers on each screw worked so I saved myself a drive. The mount folds nicely and is very stable in all positions. One thing I did that helped me, but I’m really not sure if it was worth the extra time was to unbolt the wall base from the arm so I didn’t have to hold the whole thing in place while I bolted it to the wall.

This is a very heavy/strong TV mount & does have a ~ 5” offset from the stud mounting center hole to the center of the TV. But this was fine for me and it holds my Samsung UN32J4000AF TV just great. The Mount Factory TV mount was perfect for the VESA 200x200 (M8) pattern on the back of my TV - I used the four M8 screws that came with the mount; however I did need to go to Lowes to get a bag of M8 washers ( 2 washers were use under the head of each screw to meet the TV mount guidelines). If you are placing this in a corner or near furniture, draw a detailed diagram first to make sure the TV sides/top/bottom have clearance when the TV is moved in its different positions
