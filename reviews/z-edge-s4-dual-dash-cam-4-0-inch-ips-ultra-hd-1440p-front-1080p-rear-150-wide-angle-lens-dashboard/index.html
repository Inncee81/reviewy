A lot of WOW for a really good price! I am impressed and I actually feel a sense of relief now that I have one of these things!

I am giving this system 5 stars but not because I think it is the best you can get but because of its overall value, performance and really impressive customer service. The best compliment I can think to give a company is to buy from them again, and I did. I just bought a second unit for our other vehicle!First I need to say that the quality of video for both cameras is very good for something this size and in this price range. You aren’t going to zoom in to a tight area and magically clear up a license plate recorded at a good distance but if its not too far away the cameras will see it fine. I read quite a few reviews on other cameras citing poor quality in the rear camera. I wanted the best in both directions. We were rear ended at a complete stop last week making this 3 times in 8 years so I decided to pull the trigger on this. It was on a lightning deal but with no reviews so I figured why not its Amazon I can return it if its not good.I bench tested it right away and was impressed with the video. It may be 1080 resolution but its not like a $50,000 HD TV camera so there will be artifacts due to compression. Still, I am impressed.Pros:1. Battery - I bench tested it and it ran for an hour only making a couple of 5 minute looped videos before going dead. Given the documentation says 30 minutes I am making this a plus!6. Power Adapter - There are two USB ports on the power adapter. One listed as 2 amps so you can still run another USB device while occupying the power port in your car.7. Audio - Both cameras can and by default do record audio. Extra mount and adhesive for front camera so yes, you could move it between cars if you used both mounts and had power and another rear cam for the other car.11. Very easy to install. You will have to pull out some snap on plastic covers (pillar etc..) for the most discrete install otherwise tucking it in here and there will get you by. I would caution that when running from front to back of the car if you have side curtain airbags you don’t want to obstruct their deployment path with the wire!12. Piece of mind. Updated review after Customer Service

This camera will get the job done, BUT there are some things you need to know before buying…#1. This Dash Cam is NOT compatible with SanDisk memory cards. It won’t work, I tried. The camera does come with a 32gb micro SD card that IS compatible but if you want something bigger you will have to go spend more money.-#2. Event Marking: When you mark an event using the “down arrow” button a very, VERY small key icon appears in the center of the screen. This icon is nearly impossible to see which makes it difficult to determine whether the function has engaged correctly, especially while driving. It should be more easily visible. It would be better slightly enlarged and moved to the top of the screen next to the other icons where it is much more easily visible.#3. The Cord Positioning is Terrible: Having the plug for the power and rear camera on the top of the main camera prevents you from being able to mount it close to the headliner. You have to mount it about 1 inch down on the windshield because the plugs stick out so much on the top. Move the plugs to the side.#4. SD slot location: The slot for the SD card is located on the right hand side of the camera. When you mount the camera on the windshield, the rear-view-mirror blocks access to the slot making it difficult to access the SD card. This is where the cable inputs should be, the SD slot should be on the left where it is easily accessible.#5. Rear Camera Cord: On the plus side the cord is EXTREMELY LONG. However, the rear camera cord is attached to the “bottom” of the rear camera. When mounting the rear camera, you want the cord to protrude from the top of the camera so that it can be hidden in the headliner. The way it is now, I have to mount the camera upside down in order to prevent the cord from dangling down. This is really dumb. If you want to be able to mount this rear camera properly and still be able to use the video that comes from it you should be prepared to edit the video to flip it right side up.#6. Screen brightness: The screen brightness on the main camera is far too dim and is not adjustable. Difficult to see during the day.#7. Screen polarized lens effect: When wearing polarized sunglasses the screen is oriented in such a way that it appears to be off when you are wearing polarized sunglasses. This is very annoying. This is the only screen I’ve ever seen do this.- Most Rexing 1080p dash cams will give you a slightly better picture than this one will but this picture is totally usable. Although it’s definitely not “Super HD” or “Ultra HD” (which is technically 4K which this camera is definitely not capable of) as the product description claims.- The audio quality of this camera is OUTSTANDING and is the best of any camera I have ever used.- The file and event saving works perfectly, splitting loop and event files into separate folders making them easy to access.- The big LCD screen is nice to have but I would rather have the smaller dash cam overall. This thing is HUGE!!!Overall this is a decent camera. Most dash cameras on the market are COMPLETE trash and this definitely stands out from those. I’ve had over 15 dash cameras and this seems to be one of the better ones.
