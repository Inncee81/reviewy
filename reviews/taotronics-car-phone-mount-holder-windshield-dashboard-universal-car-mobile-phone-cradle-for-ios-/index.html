Strong suction, holds iPhone 6 great

I am constantly using Google Maps on my iPhone for directions while driving and wanted to stop holding my phone in my hand. I bought this holder and have mounted it on my dashboard. I have had it for 3 months, and it has been a good investment. The suction is sufficient enough to hold my iPhone 6 without me having any fear of it falling over. Moreover, the grip to hold the iPhone is strong. You just slide the arms, allowing them to click and lock into place. Then to release, you just click the button on the bottom. This holder can swivel a full 180 degrees and can be angled up or down by about 45 degrees or so. I have not tried to attach it to my windshield so I cannot review its ability to stick to glass. However, I can say that I leave the holder mounted to the dash even in 100 degree weather and it has held up fine. For people with smaller phones, I tried it with my iPod 4th generation and it still held the iPod securely. Bottom line: this is a strong holder and a great way to avoid looking down at your phone to look at directions while driving.
