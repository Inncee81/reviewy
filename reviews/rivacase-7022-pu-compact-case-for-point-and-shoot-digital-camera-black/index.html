Purchased as a present for a Canon 940 IS. Specs advertise that it is compatible. The camera does technically fit, but just barely. The zipper and material have to stretch and it squeezes tight on the camera body. I’m hoping after it breaks in, it will fit better. Nice case, but had I been able to give it a test drive, I would have purchased a different/larger case for the Canon 940 IS.

Good case for a micro camera like the Sony Cybershot.

This holds my Sony Cybershot, just barely though. It’s the length. The width/height of the case allows it to be put at an angle so that it fits. This also allows you to store an extra battery and memory card in the case too. So this works for a very small camera. There is a small net/mesh pocket inside to hold these items. All in all a very good case for the money!
