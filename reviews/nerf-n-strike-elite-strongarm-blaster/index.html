The “slam fire” option is really cool, and I love that this gun can shoot …

This is the first Nerf gun I’ve bought since the Vulcan was brand new, and I’m very impressed with the change. The “slam fire” option is really cool, and I love that this gun can shoot these darts, Vulcan darts, Velcro OR the suction ones. For Slam Fire you just hold down the trigger and pull back the lever repeatedly and it’ll launch one dart per pull. For anyone who had the Maverick, this is a new and improved version that’s worked out the kinks. The chamber doesn’t rotate when you pull the trigger unless the gun is loaded, and misfiring happens much less often, meaning that each shot has full power.As far as the darts themselves, they fly MUCH farther than regular darts, but be warned - they can curve unexpectedly. Shooting a velcro dart out of this gun it’ll go a standard distance in a straight line. Shooting one of these elite darts will easily go twice as far, but land anywhere within 8 feet of the target on either side. Still, it’s a toy. Having a 30% chance of hitting your target 50 feet away is better than a 0% chance with a regular dart that can’t even go that far.READ MORE
