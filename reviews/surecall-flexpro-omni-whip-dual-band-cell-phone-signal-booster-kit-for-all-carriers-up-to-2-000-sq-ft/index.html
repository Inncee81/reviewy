The FlexPro Omni/Whip delivers unrivaled performance with cutting-edge technology, reducing dropped and missed calls and extending battery life for multiple simultaneous users on all cellular networks. Cellular reception weakens due to topography, building materials, structural design and distance from the cell tower. The FlexPro Omni/Whip captures this outside cellular signal, boosts it and re-broadcasts that signal inside to your cellular device. The FlexPro booster is bi-directional meaning signals are amplified as they travel back and forth from your device to the cell tower ensuring that you stay connected.
