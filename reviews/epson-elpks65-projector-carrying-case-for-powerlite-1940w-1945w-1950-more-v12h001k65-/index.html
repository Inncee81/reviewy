Decent Case However Low on Storage Capacity

Decent case however for the price leaves much to be desired.I ordered this case for our company to transport an Epson VS230. The case fits the projector well, although the case is taller then the projector, and the case we received did not have the ability to move the velcro inside to create a better fit for different sized projectors. The case also does not offer a whole lot of storage for cables, the remote etc. as well as it doesn’t offer much protection in the way of padding.Pros:Case That Holds a ProjectorMessenger Style BagTwo Separate CompartmentsBuild QualityCons:Doesn’t Hold Enough (may be okay for some)Not Enough Velcro to Secure Top FlapCannot Move Velcro to Properly Secure ProjectorNot Enough PaddingAll in all for what the case is it works, some things may be desired but it isn’t enough to knock off another star. The build quality is decent and we wanted something compact and this is just that. You can’t have both a compact case and a lot of storage so this will suffice for our needs.

Delivery was quick and satisfactory

The product arrived before the schedule date. I was impress with the speed with which it was delivered.This bag is too big for the Epson VS230 projector. I think that something should be done to find a more suitable bag. This one is so large that the projector keeps moving when being transported. The quality of the bag however is fairly good. I have a previous projector and the bag was perfect for the machine.

Too Big

This carrying case is not padded well at all and there’s too much room in it for the Epson projector I bought ( Epson VS230, SVGA, 2800 Lumens Color Brightness.). Even with the cable wires, the plug and the HDMI wire there’s still too much room in the bag. I would rather have a snugger fit for the projector so that it doesn’t slide around. It should indicate in the description of the bag suggested projectors for a better fit.
