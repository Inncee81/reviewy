Size guidelines

Great racket for a beginner tennis player. Nice and light and easy for kids to handle. Here are the age guidelines for the Wilson kids line:19” up to 5 years old21” 5-6 years old23” 7-8 years old25” 9-10 years old

How to know which size- measure fingertips to ground = size of racket you should buy your kid.

The size of the racket you should buy your child is determined by the distance from his/her fingertips, when standing straight, to the ground. My son is 7 and this racket is a great size for him, however his sister, also 7, is 2 inches taller so I would choose the next size up. It might say an age on these rackets but it’s really based on your child’s height.

Great First Kid’s Racquet; Recommend Ordering a Replacement Grip

Great first racquet for kids. For my eight year old daughter, I purchased the 25”. It says that size is for 9-10 year olds, but my child is tall and I figured she will grow into it. I would suggest ordering a replacement grip because after a week of use it was filthy and lost it’s “gripiness.”
