Very nice, fubnctional, good looking case.

I bought a case made by MoKo for a Microsoft Surface. It was pretty good quality so I tried them again when I moved to the Nokia 2520. The case is good looking, solid, durable, and functional. It is a well thought out item.The case fits the Nokia like a glove. There are 4 little “cups,” that fit around the corners of the tablet. Two are slightly shorter than the other two which makes removing the tablet fairly simple. The design of the case is pretty cool. Instead of having some sort of stand or fitting behind the device or a part of the case jutting out to hold the Nokia at an angle, you simply pull the tablet out of the two bottom claws. It then has several positions where the tablet fits snugly holding it at a desired angle. Even though you remove the bottom of the tablet from the claws, the Lumia still feels very secure.The look of the case is fine. It is a faux leather look and is kind of nondescript. It looks professional and solid. I was not looking for a case to draw attention to the unit and this one looks just fine.The case fits snugly around the tablet both when the cover is open or closed. There are no moving parts, hinges, etc with the case. It uses sturdy “leather,” clasps to hold everything where it should be. The inside it a suede-like material that is soft and easy to clean.The durability of the case is good. It is not going to help the Lumia if you drop it off of the roof, but it will keep scratches off of the screen and save you if you happen to bump it on the corner of the table. It is also water resistant so if you spill a little coffee on the front of the case, it will quickly and easily wipe off. The case is surprisingly light for its durability. It does not bulk up the tablet really at all.Lastly, the case is a spot on match with all of the 2520’s characteristics and features. You can use all of the tablet’s functions; camera, ear buds, volume control, etc without having to remove the tablet from the case. It is well designed for the Lumia. A lot of other cases give you problems when you want to change out the memory card, take a picture, etc. this one fits like a glove.I am very happy with the case. I would certainly recommend it.
