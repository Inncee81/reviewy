Product Specifications

  • Compatible with VESA (mounting hole pattern) 75x75mm(3”x3”) or 100x100mm(4”x4”), it also works for VESA 200x200 (8 “x 8 “),200x100mm (8 “x 4 “) by using attached 4 additional plate adapters to fit most 13” 15” 17” 19” 20” 22” 23” 24” 26” 27” 30” 32” 37” 39” LED TV Flat Panel Screen.

    Product Description

Size:13-39” WALI articulating wall mount for LED LCD TV Monitors, makes it easy to view your TV or Monitor from different angles. Fits TV or Monitor with VESA 75x75mm, 100x100mm, 200x100mm, and 200x200 mm mounting hole pattern (these are square or rectangular patterns between 3 and 4 inches per side on the rear of your display), using attached 4 additional plate adapters can make it fit screen up to 39”, maximum loading capacity is 33 lbs. This dual link arm wall mount gives you 14” extension from the wall with maximum flexibility. You can also fold it close to your wall for a low-profile appearance. Removable TV plate designed for single-person installation. Post installation level adjustment to ensure perfect level. Tilt, swivel, extend function provide smooth adjustment for all directions. Durable steel construction with black powder coated finish. Common mounting and screen attachment hardware included.

I am mounting a small TV, probably 24”, but it is a 200x200, so I have to use the adapter.All you have to do is find out the distance between the mounting holes on the back of your TV before you order and make sure it is compatible - NOT the screen size. The screen size matters, too, but just because your TV is the right screen size doesn’t mean it will have compatible mount holes.Just measure the distance (8in in my case, aka 200x200) and see if it will work with your TV before you order.
