Good but

Sounds good. Nice enough price. The only problem is there is a port labelled on the back COAX/OPTI1, and the manual states it takes a Coaxial cable into this port. It does not. As you can see from the picture, and as I confirmed after I bought the device, Coaxial cable does not fit. A coaxial end has a collar but the port on the device has no holder for the collar. Solid value and great performer

A really midrange-premium feel to this little guy, and the Bluetooth functionality is a huge plus. Sound is excellent, practically the same as the Q5 Pro which this is replacing, but be warned as these digital amps sound very transparent and cold at first listen. The endemic “warmth” from more traditional DAC->amp setups is noticeably absent here, and it’s a little jarring at first. But to my ears, the sound is brutally accurate and becomes very agreeable once I’m adjusted. Minus one star because the remote doesn’t come with a battery out of the box, and my upsampling settings killed the USB audio and had to be disabled, which was not the case for the Q5 Pro (might have something to do with this one displaying the sample rate).
