I tried the bag out at Best Buy, and I was about ready to purchase it on Amazon, until this bag came up as a recommended choice. It looks identical to the Canon, but without the giant ‘steal me’ Canon logo. Reviews for this bag backed up my choice, so I figured I would try this baby out, and if I din’t like it, just return it for the Canon.Little did I know, this bag exceeded my expectations with features much better than the 200EG. In the meantime, I keep my chargers, minidv tapes, and remotes in the open spot. That is until I invest in a flash unit. Which is nice considering there is no laptop slot.All in all, this bag is highly recommended in my opinion. Keep it in mind in your searches, and don’t click away because it is Amazon branded.
