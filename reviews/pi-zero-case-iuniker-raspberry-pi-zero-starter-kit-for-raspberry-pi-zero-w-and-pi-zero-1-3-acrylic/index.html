Excellent case with a great list of accessories.

Excellent case with a great list of accessories. The board fit properly, the screws fit and were nice and secure. It even has a little hanging hole on the back for putting it on a wall or holding it in place with a nail.The only complaint I have is that the two micro-b USB ports on the Pi Zero are NOT the same. (You cannot use the power one as another USB port, and you cannot use the USB port to power the board.) For this reason, the case really should have a little marker to indicate which is which. (May I suggest a little lightning bolt over the power one, perhaps?) This isn’t enough to make it not a 5-star case, but it is a bit inconvenient to either swap plugs or open the case to remember which is which. I ended up scratching a lightning bolt over my power plug with the screwdriver included in the kit.
