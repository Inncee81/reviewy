    Product Description

Nothing says that you’re a cyclist like Giro’s Classic Cotton Cap. On or off the bike, this cap offers a bit of shade from the sun, a cover from wind and rain, and a layer of insulation when it’s cool. It also wicks sweat when the heat rises. The Giro Classic Cotton Cap is one-size-fits-all and is available in the colors Black/grey, Black/white, White/black dot, and White/black/grey.
