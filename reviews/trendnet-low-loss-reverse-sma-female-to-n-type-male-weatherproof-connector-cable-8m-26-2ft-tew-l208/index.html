Good quality coax with correct connectors

LMR-200 eqivalent coax with correct reverse female SMA connector that will fit all Chinese routers or wifi adapters with antenna inputs and a N-Male on the other end, which will fit the vast majority of external wifi antennas. Since you’re dealing with 1.8 to 2.4 GHz signals, even small losses in the coax will drastically affect your signal. This is good quality coax, and the connectors are well made and also good quality. I bought the 26 foot long version because that’s how much I needed to reach the antenna, but keep the coax run as short as possible. You will lose more signal in the coax over about 30 feet than you will gain from increased antenna height unless you’re willing to pay for much more expensive LMR-400 or hardline type coax.
