A VERY USEFUL AND REASONABLY PRICED TOOL

I combine this with a D Addario string winder bit and it makes changing strings on my guitars and basses much easier, more fun and I get a better tighter wrap around the machine heads.I love giving these as gifts, people think it’s a great tool and it feels more expensive than it is.The only thing I don’t like is they no longer ship with a double ended screwdriver bit (one phillips and one straight end). They now include two separate bits which are much easier to lose or misplace.A set of batteries lasts a long time too. I have several of these screwdrivers around the house, we use them all the time. In my recording studio I use this for racking up equipment, it’s a great way to put in rack screws, not as inclined to strip threads as a powerful electric drill driving a screwdriver bit.Highly recommended!

A HUGE Time-Saver & Life-Saver – Wish I’d Gotten This Cordless Electric Screwdriver YEARS Ago!!

l got this Black & Decker Cordless Screwdriver last year, after my mom had a stroke in August, and I needed to do some small repairs/fix-its/installations & other “honey-do list” type stuff (like putting in a closet organizer/shelves, etc.) around the house to make things safer and easier for my mom to manage with decreased mobility & other issues.Until last year, I’d always used only manual screwdrivers whenever a screwdriver was needed for any tasks. I don’t know why it never occurred to me to get an electric screwdriver, but it just didn’t. I’d been using manual screwdrivers for years, and though I hadn’t really had any problem with them, per sé, using them was pretty time-consuming – and since I had several tasks around the house to complete preferably before my mom came home from the hospital (actually the rehab center she’d been admitted to, after she was discharged from the hospital) I was in a major “time crunch” and thought that using a manual screwdriver for everything that needed to be done, would take way too long.So, in order to save the time, energy & effort of manually screwing in countless number of screws (for all the various tasks necessitating use of a screwdriver) I got this Black & Decker Cordless Screwdriver…………….and I am GLAD that I did!This battery-operated cordless electric screwdriver is pretty simple & easy to use. It has two buttons (one on either side of the handle) to easily switch between installing screws and removing them with forward and reverse buttons. This screwdriver comes with 2 “bits” – one of them for flat-head screws, and the other for Phillips-head screws and they pretty much just snap into place in the specific slot.All in all this is a great screwdriver, and saved/saves a LOT of time & frustration when installing shelves, and doing other small home improvements/repairs. The only reason I gave this Black & Decker Cordless Screwdriver 4 stars instead of 5 is because rather than putting both the flat-head screwdriver and the Phillips-head screwdriver on either side of one “bit” (so that both types would remain in the drill at all times) instead they put it on two separate “bits”. So only one of the “bits” can be in the drill at a particular time, and since they are so small they’re much harder to keep track of.Other than that, this is a GREAT cordless electric screwdriver – especially for such a low price!★☆★☆ 4 STARS!!!!!

Handy device for driving in a screw without stripping or slipping off the screw.

I like this little guy. I have several cordless power drills. Not with this little guy. It has low gears to give it the torque to drive in a screw but at a consistence slower speed that helps keep the bit on the screw with ease.
