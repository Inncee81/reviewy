Product Specifications

  • Set includes: (1) Super Scoop Bath Toy Organizer, (8) Ocean bath squirts and (8) Lake bath squirts
  • Super Scoop features a super-strong Push-Lock suction cup that securely mounts to tile and fiberglass tub walls
  • Super Scoop designed with mesh fabric to increase air flow and dry toys quickly, reducing mildew risk
  • Bath toys squirt water, float and are perfectly sized for little hands to hold and squeeze

  • Suitable for children 6+ months

    Product Description

Has your little one turned your bathtub into a toy box? You’ll love how simple it is to keep your bathroom clutter-free with the Super Scoop Bath Toy Organizer from Munchkin. The wide mouth and handle allows for easy toy scooping after bath time, and the fish-shaped hook hangs securely on the bathroom wall to drain and dry, providing convenient bath toy storage, too. Just unhook the scooper from the wall and glide it across the water for quick, convenient cleanup. With plenty of room to pack all your Ocean and Lake Bath Squirts, you’ll no longer spend extra time cleaning up and will get to enjoy extra bath time fun.
