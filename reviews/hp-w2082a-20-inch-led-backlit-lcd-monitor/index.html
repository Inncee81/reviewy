A Solid Brand Name Monitor That Fits The Bill

I needed a solid computer monitor for a backup PC currently residing in my garage, but it didn’t need the resolution (1920 x 1200) of my new PC. As it turns out, 1600 x 900 was more than adequate for this backup, and it also contained built-in speakers, a real bonus. The price was more than right, and HP was certainly a brand name. This HP monitor fit the bill more than adequately, and I got what I paid for: An inexpensive but solid monitor. Recommended for anyone who doesn’t need the latest and greatest.
