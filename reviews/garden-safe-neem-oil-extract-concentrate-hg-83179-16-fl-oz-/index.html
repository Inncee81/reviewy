Neem Oil in my sprayer is an important part of my organic gardening plan! Organic gardening in the South is challenging (I live in Atlanta). We don’t get good hard freezes that can help balance the insect population that may attack our food crops. I mix Neem Oil Extract in my sprayer with water and go after a wide variety of pests that appear on my garden plants. I don’t hurt the birds, earthworms, or the pollinators that I want to encourage in my garden. Take a look at the link below if you want to see how Neem Oil affects bees and other pollinators. A good rule of thumb is to apply Neem Oil in the early morning or evening to be absolutely sure it won’t hurt bees and other “good guys.” Overall, you can be assured that it won’t hurt the good guys unless you absolutely saturate your plants in the hot sunny heat of the day when bees are active on the plants!
