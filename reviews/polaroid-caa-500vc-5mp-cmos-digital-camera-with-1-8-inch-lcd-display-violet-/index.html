From the Manufacturer

Polaroid a500 Start shooting pictures the minute you pull the a500 digital camera by Polaroid out of the box. That’s how easy it is to use the five megapixels enhanced resolution camera. It comes complete with point-and-shoot features like a 1.8-inch LCD bright color LCD display so you can preview images before you shoot. It even captures VGA video with audio at 30 frames per second. Snap and store up to 120 images using the 8MB of built-in memory, or insert a SD/SDHC card to expand its memory capabilities and snap many more pictures. Use the electronic self-timer so you can finally be in that family portrait and print your favorite pictures once you’re done using the built-in PictBridge technology. The a500 includes a USB cable for PC or Mac connections and photo editing software on CD-ROM.

Polaroid PoGo™ Compatibility Polaroid digital cameras make printing to the Polaroid PoGo™ Instant Mobile Printer easy. A customized user interface makes printing as effortless as connect, select, and print.
