Very Well Made. Now appears to be Shipping with the Right Screws

This is a very well made product an well worth the money. An issue that was discussed in a previous review: It was indicated that the included speaker-to-bracket mounting screws were metric and could strip the threads in the speaker housing (US 1⁄4”-20 threads per inch). In the box that I received (December 2015), I received the correct screws; so the issue appears to be fixed by the manufacturer of the brackets. However, since some retailers could have the older versions in stock, be sure to check the screws. If you have a question, your local hardware store should be able to help you out.

WRONG SET SCREW - be careful not to strip the mounting thread on your speaker!

This is a well engineered product - but receives a 2 star review because the wrong set screw is shipped with the device! Be careful, or you will strip the Thread Mount in the speaker. The Sonos directions call for a 1⁄4 screw with 20 thread. The Brits shipped with an M5 screw - METRIC! So be prepared to go to the hardware store to get the right screw (about $.30 for stainless).
