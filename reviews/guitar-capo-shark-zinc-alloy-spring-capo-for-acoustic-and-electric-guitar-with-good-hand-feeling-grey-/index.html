Looks cool, but !

It looks cool, it’s easy to operate…but the spring could be a little stronger. I don’t own cheap guitars I use a Fender LaBrea, and a Martin A-31 so I don’t have any issues with excessive travel between the strings and the frets. “Jaws” Needed A Dentist!

As an avid guitarist, I enjoy an abnormal and unique piece of gear! This capo fit the bill. Finish is plated zinc in a very bright gold-tone and is top notch. I’ve played with this over 2 months now and the pistol grip lever action has remained very stiff. My only complaint is the “teeth” on the capo came loose on the bottom jaw after a few sessions. This was remedied by a drop of super glue. Do not buy this if you have weak hands or if you prefer a screw type tensioner on your capo as it requires some strength to open it. Do buy if you want a very hefty, weighty capo that will last until the end of time, with superglue! 4 stars for having to repair it so early on in its life with a consumer, but it remains bullettproof as long as you have some super glue around.
