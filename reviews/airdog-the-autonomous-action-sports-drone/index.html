AirDog - The Autonomous Action Sports Drone

by people ...

  • Fully Autonomous Drone with sports specific flight modes
  • AirDog autonomously follows you without a bulky remote control, so you can focus on the action!
  • Control AirDog & make inflight adjustments with AirLeash - the wearable, waterproof control & tracking device
  • Future-proof. New flight modes & performance enhancements through free software updates
  • Foldable, easily fits in your backpack
  • Ground Collision Avoidance with LiDAR
  • Compatible cameras: GoPro Hero3, 3+ Black, Silver, White (in slim housing) and Hero4 Silver, Black (in Standard Housing), Adapter for GoPro Hero5 available as an accessory

It’s not about flying, it’s about getting the shot without taking anything away from what you love most - the action itself. AirDog is a self-flying action sports drone. Motion prediction technology and dedicated action sport flight modes is what enables users to capture themselves in action effortlessly in the most demanding conditions. Use AirLeash, a wearable, waterproof controller to select your sport, launch AirDog with the push of a button and go about business as usual; your every move will be captured no matter how fast or high you go.

Amazing auto-follow with dual GPS for no guess tracking

I got this drone to help me shoot my Kitesurfing action, although they don’t have yet a dedicated mode for that. the Windsurf one does just fine. I did have issues with the battery, arrived DOA. I wrote to customer support, Eldars in particular was SUPER nice and responsive. They asked me to send a video with the battery not charging and dispatched me at no cost a new battery via DHL. As I found later, prior to the battery patch they put out few weeks ago, the smart battery would continue to self discharge while stored. That led to LiPOs getting below the recovery level and bricking them. The new battery I tested is perfect. It will self discharge down to 3.8V per cell for safe storage, that’s about 60% and it stays there. As for flying, the Airdog is impressive, just read read read the manuals, it’s not your average drone. The autonomous flight part needs understanding from you so as you run downhill you won’t push the drone into the trees ahead of you. You need to understand that, the drone does an AMAZING job at repositioning and keeping you in the center of your footage. the Gimbal is decent, not super high end but decent. During re-positioning for example the drone will fly from the back to front and during this, since the gimbal doesn’t move left-right axis but only on 2 axes, you might have some jitter. I think other drones have similar issues w/o a true 3 axes gimbal. Out of the rest of the drones, the fact it packs down and you can put it in your backpack is a REAL nice thing. I considered the cheaper $1000 auto-fllow drones that rely on video to identify you in the picture and follow. they do NOT work at all, losing tracking on the subject while you go downhill or on the water flying a kite and seeing the drone hovering and not reconnecting to you sucks. So AirDog was really attached to the leash it followed so to speak. Dual GPS in the drone and leash makes this coordination super nice.

Kickstarter Backer here. It was worth the wait.

The airdog is exactly what I expected. Having purchased it during the Kickstarter campaign made it an even better deal.The biggest issue I have is the battery time. It only lasts about 15 mins. You really have to prepare ahead. Place the drone closer to the action and be ready to start as soon as it gets in place. I plan to get some replacement batteries but they are expensive because they are not a standard LiPo battery.On the other hand the Airdog has a lot of positives. All of the parts that experience the most wear and tear can be replaced. The battery is quick and easy to swap. The body is sturdy AF and will survive a beating however it is not waterproof.
