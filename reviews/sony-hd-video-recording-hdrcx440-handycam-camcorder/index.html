A budget HD camcorder you can really like, but not one you’ll love

Bought this to replace an earlier Sony HD digital camcorder. Love the small size. Accepts microSD cards to record. Does have a built-in memory (8 GB), which is enough for a little over an hour of HD recording, but I recommend getting a card (32GB or 64GB) so you don’t worry about space. Microphone is surprisingly good in a camera this small, but if you really care about sound you will need a different way to record it as this camera does not have a microphone jack.There are a lot of good things about this camera. The picture is good, the USB cable is attached so you don’t lose it, and it’s very easy to carry and use. However, I just have a couple gripes that prevented a full five stars. First, night shots are decent but not great. Recording outside is great, and recording inside in well-lighted conditions is also great, but inside with low light is not. I know this isn’t a high-end camera, but that feature has been around for years, so it could easily have been included. Also, you cannot make video recording adjustments on the fly. That is, if the circumstances change (say, from bright daylight to overcast or from outdoors to indoors), you have to stop recording to make any changes in the settings. Maybe it’s a “safety” feature, but I don’t like it. Also, the camera defaults to internal memory for recording even with a card inserted. The camera will switch over to the card while recording automatically, but your video will suffer.Great HD camcorder on a budget, but people who need great sound or low-light video should look elsewhere.
