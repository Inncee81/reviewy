Eliminate those red LED reflections with this blackout case

I bought this because I noticed when taking pictures through a piece of glass or plastic I would get 1 to 3 reflections of the red LED lights. The blackout housing covers all of the LEDs (and also comes with a set of stickers to cover the front LCD screen if you really want to). It comes with a solid back door, a clear panel back door, and a skeleton back door. (But not the larger battery back door, apparently - not being the owner of an actual larger battery, I cant say for sure) It has the same standard GoPro mount tabs on the bottom, and comes with one of the slide-on action clip mounts too. Finally, the front rectangular lens mount is identical to the clear case, so if you bought a polarizing filter you can use it on this case as well. This case has allowed me to get video clips and static shots without the LED light reflections that the clear case can allow.
