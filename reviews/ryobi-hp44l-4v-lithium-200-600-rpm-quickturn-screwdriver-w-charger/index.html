    Product Description

Save your wrist and joints by switching from a manual screwdriver to the Ryobi Quickturn HP44L. With a built in 4V Lithium battery, this power tool can tackle most jobs around the home. Assemble furniture, install panelling, or open up machinery with ease. Its versatility outmatches the features found on basic manual tools. Its head can snap from the standard pistol-grip style of drill / drivers to a straight line. This lets you work in tight spaces or above you without putting excess strain on your body. It has two speeds, 200 RPM and 600 RPM, that let you work on both delicate and hardy jobs. Its ergonomically designed grip with a rubber overmold allows you to retain maximum comfort for as long as possible, taking even more stress off of your person. With all of these features and comforts, you can be sure to focus more on the job at hand to get it done right. Working with Ryobi means working with reliability.
