    Product Description

The Dremel 8-Volt Lithium-ion Cordless rotary tool is the tool for people who are passionate about hands-on projects around the home such as finishing, intricate sanding, polishing, cleaning, engraving and more. The tool’s variable speed control allows you to set the speed of the tool to match a particular accessory to the task at hand and it is designed to be used with all Dremel accessories and most threaded attachments.

Product Reviews

bad over the long term

Dremel makes a good product, really the only acceptable rotary tool…This 8v unit is about the right power to do dogs nails , has a variable speed so you can start out slow to not scare them…. contrary to another reviewers opinion, I don’t find it too powerful to do small dogs with.I previously had a 7.8 volt Dremel NiCd and had battery issues a lot, they would wear out quickly, discharge quickly, when I bought Dremel branded replacements they might or might not fit correctly, this new Li Ion design solves all that.Pro:- Li Ion means no battery memory, longer life, shorter recharge times- new battery plug in design is far superior to the previous NiCd design which rattled, made noise, was hard to latch…. this one fixes all of that- good size considering the power- a case and plenty of bits included- variable speed so you can slowly work up speed without scaring your dogCon- none yetenjoy!—————————————————————————————–2 years later, warranty just ran out, have about 2 total hours on the tool… and the front bearing is out and the power slide doesn’t work, Dremel isn’t allowing a little warranty extension, so I’m outta luck… the 8100 has developed a terrible repuation over the last couple of years and you should avoid it and go with the 8220

Defective Switch Design

Stay away from this model! The switch went bad on me after barely using it for 3 months. Sent it in to Dremel and found out that their customer service was superb. They have acknowledge that the switch design is not sealed and that performing a dusty project can clog up the switch contacts causing to overheat or cut off. They are sending me a different model in replace to this one. Although I’m giving this product a one star and as rightfully so, I’d like to give Dremel’s customer service a 5 star. So in the end, do not buy this model. Spend an extra $20-30 and get a 8220 model which is far superior in almost every way.
