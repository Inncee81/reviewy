The controllers fit securely into the dock so you can rest assured that your PS4 controllers will not fall or bend the charging port. That was a huge problem for me with using a USB wire to charge the controllers. Any movement to the controller or wire while connected, run the risk of bending the metal connection and ruining the wire. No worries about that with this charging station.The charging times are pretty good. Great, actually. I had one controller charge up from dead to 100% in about 30 minutes. I don’t know how much of that is because of the desktop charging station that I am using to power (connected directly to the wall outlet) or if the PS4 charging station is just that good. When you play as much as I do, thumb pads get switched out quite frequently!So there you have it. A great charging station that simultaneously charges 2 controllers at once. I strongly recommend getting a charging station for any set of PS4 controllers you may own… and at under $10, you can’t go wrong with this great Pecham DualShock 4 Dual Charging Station!
