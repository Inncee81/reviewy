    Product Description

Dome Z-Wave Door/Window Sensor Dome now officially Works with Wink! Install it on garage doors, closet doors, medicine cabinet doors, and all the windows in your home and get a reminder or sound an alarm when you’ve left anything open. Protect yourself from danger or an invasion of privacy, and find out when someone is snooping around. The Dome Automation Smart Home Door/Window Sensor can automate the lights to your doors and windows, and it turns them off after you’re closed and locked up. Sleek, modern design, and battery operation, means you can install the long-range Dome Door/Window sensor anywhere. Use with most any Z-Wave Hub including: Wink, Home Seer, SmartThings, Vera, Harmony and more. 3-Year battery life and range of over 200 feet!
