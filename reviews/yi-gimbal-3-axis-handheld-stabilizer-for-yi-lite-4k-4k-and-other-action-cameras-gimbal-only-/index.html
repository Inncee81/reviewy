Product Specifications

  • 3-Axis Pan / Tilt / Zoom Gimbal with 320 Degree Pan / Tilt rotation helps you to capture smooth footage even on the roughest roads.
  • 2-4.5 hours of battery life: Two 18350 lithium-ion batteries, each 850mAh.
  • Universal ¼” thread attachment allows you to use the YI Handheld Gimbal with other professional mounts.
  • Compatible with YI Selfie Stick&Bluetooth Remote - features a simple twist-and-lock function and rotates 360° horizontally, 180° vertically and is adjustable from 7” to 28”.

  • PACKAGE ONLY include Gimbal. 12-Month Warranty service only for the item sold by Yi. It’s important to calibrate the unit before use.

    Product Description

Developed for professional shooting the YI Handheld Gimbal features a 3-Axis gimbal head with 3 operating modes, combined with a built-in high precision sensor and brushless motor, you won’t have to worry about rocky roads and shaky hands when shooting.

A flexible 3-axis pan/tilt/zoom with 320 degree pan/tilt rotation allows you to capture stable shots of fast moving objects.
