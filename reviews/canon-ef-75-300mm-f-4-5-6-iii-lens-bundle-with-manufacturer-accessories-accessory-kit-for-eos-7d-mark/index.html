Lens bundle

First off, the zoom lens is very nice. No image stabilization but it does have auto-focus and the glass seems to be good quality. The pictures I get from it are very good (for a newbie). But the rest of the package, not so much. I tried the 2.2x telephoto lens and the picture quality is awful. I went from a nice clean photo to one that was washed out and unable to focus. The other items in the package are OK, the filters seem OK but I really don’t know if I will use anything else. The “Tulip Lens Hood” fits and seems OK for what it is but does not allow the use of a lens cap if you have the hood installed. I already have lots of memory card/USB adapters so that is of no use to me. Overall this could have been a nice bundle if the lens adapters were good quality, but I would strongly recommend that if you want the zoom lens, just get the lens by itself and maybe pop for a couple of UV filters separately.
