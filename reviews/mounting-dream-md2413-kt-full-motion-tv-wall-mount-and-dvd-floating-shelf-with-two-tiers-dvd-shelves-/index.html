Product Specifications

  • TV Wall Mount: for most of 26-55” TVs up to 60 LBS with max VESA (mounting hole pattern) of 400x400mm (including100X100, 200X100, 200X200, 300X300, 400X200, 400X400mm). Please check TV’s VESA, possible blocked cable/input, TV weight and stud location to ensure compatibility. Caution: This mount fits concrete wall/single wood stud application, cannot fit dry wall alone installation!
  • DVD Floating Shelves: holds 22 LBS per shelf, for DVD player, and other AV equipment size up to 15.3” x 11.8”.
  • Full motion (TV mount): +5°/-15° Tilt, +/-90° Swivel and +/-5° rotate for maximum TV viewing flexibility. 19.4” extension, 2.9” depth to wall. VESA plate can be taken off for easy installation. Post-installation level adjustment allows the TV to perfectly level.
  • Single Wood Stud installation: Both TV wall mount and DVD shelf are for concrete wall and single wood stud installation. Please check the stud centering prior to installation.

  • 2-in-1 kit design: DVD shelf and TV wall mounts in one kit offer ideal solution to organize TV and video devices completely just in one purchase. Shelf’s 5mm strengthened tempered glasses, 4 sets holes for height adjustment holds video devices in stable and adjustable manner.

    Product Description

TV Mount Fits most of 26-55” TVs

TV wall mount fits most of 26-55” TVs up to 60 LBS. It fits TVs with mounting holes/VESA as close as 4”x4” or as wide as 16”x16”. Specifically, it fits VESA 100X100mm, 200X100mm, 200X200mm, 300X300mm, 400X200mm, 400X400mm.

TV Wall Mount-Full motion with articulating arm Articulating arm allows TV retracts to 2.9”(to wall) to save space, and extends up to 19.4” from the wall.+5°/-15° Tilt, +/-90°Swivel (Maximum swivel angle depends on TV size) and +/-5° rotate for maximum viewing flexibility, providing smooth adjustment for all directions.

DVD Floating Shelves - 5mm Tempered Glasses 5mm two layer strengthened tempered glasses hold video devices in 15.7” x 11.8” (L x W), up to 44 LBS totally (22 LBS per shelf).

DVD Floating Shelves- Multi-levels Height Adjustment This bracket has 4 sets installing holes to locate shelves for height adjustment, accommodating different height of AV equipment.

DVD Floating Shelves- Cable Management Cables run behind the wall plate, contributing to good appearance.

1 HDMI, 2 Bubble Level and 5 Velcro Cable Ties 6’ HDMI cable supports 3D, Ethernet, 1440P and Audio Return conforming to latest HDMI 1.4 Standard. Magnetic bubble levels free your hands for other mounting work, making leveling work easy and convenient. Velcro cable ties manage cables from messy cables into neat look.

Heavy duty with All Necessary Mounting Hardware Constructed with heavy duty steel materials and with durable powder coated finish. Advanced welding technology ensures stable, sturdy and safe use. Finish of the mount and all hardware is RoHS (Restriction of Hazardous Substance) certified. Hardware kit includes all necessary hardware for concrete wall/single stud mounting.
