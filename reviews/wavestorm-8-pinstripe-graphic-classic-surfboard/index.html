Product Reviews

Sweet soft top

Overall it’s a great beginner surfboard. Any body who can listen to instructions from a surf instructor can ride this board. The reason for 4 stars instead of 5 is because costco sells the same board for 100$. I have taught many people to surf on this board.

heavy and awkward, but paddles well

Paddles really easy and fast. More maneuverable than it looks. Unfortunately, it’s really heavy for a Styrofoam board. The holes where the fins plug in are not sealed, so if any water leaks in there, the board will get waterlogged. Mine has a bunch of excess glue around the tail pad - it’s really sticky and annoying, but it will eventual get covered in sand anyway.The leash tangles easily. You will need to put in some sort of marine swivel to fix this.

Price
