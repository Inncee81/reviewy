Simple, useful, effective, but not for power users

Got this for a Dell Latitude 10 tablet to make it a mini-computer for some simple tasks. Pairing was simple. The magnet for the case is solid. My only nitpicks are that one of the keys popped off on me (it was my fault, and the key popped right back on, but it still seemed a hair too fragile for what I had done) and the keys are small and close (again, this is for a mini-computer/tablet and not a laptop, so this is just something to be aware of and not a real problem). The tablet is held securely and the keyboard worked as expected. Again, be aware that this is a keyboard for a small tablet-type computer, so it’s not a full-sized keyboard. That being said, once you get used to it, you can type fairly quickly on it and the keyboard keeps up. No power typing and no long-use typing, but simple document changes, emails, web searches, and other small tasks are adequately performed. And the case itself looks nice without adding more than expected bulk. Very pleased.

The keyboard connects magnetically to the case and comes on and off easily, but doesn’t fall off

I got what I expected to receive. Perfectly sized for my Microsoft Surface 3, but anything larger would not fit. The keyboard charge lasts a long time, I’ve had it for three months now and have charged it twice. I wasn’t even sure if it needed to be charged the second time, I just did just in case. The keyboard connects magnetically to the case and comes on and off easily, but doesn’t fall off. The grips that hold the corners are attached to elastic to the center which allows it to expand as needed to device size. The grips are made of plastic and covered with little pieces of rubber, I would assume those are to protect the corners of the device from being scratched by the plastic, and also to keep it from slipping. It holds snugly. My one complaint is that the rubber bits are already starting to tear a bit. The keyboard is smaller than a standard sized keyboard, but it’s not so small that it’s difficult to use. That being said, I have tiny lady fingers. I have been experiencing an issue with keys not working occasionally. It happen more with the numbers and upper letters of the keyboard mostly. Sometimes they’ll stop working for a few seconds and you have to press the key several times before it will work, other times, which is why I’m giving four stars, they won’t work for several hours. This was a problem for me as I was filling out financial paperwork and only the numbers five and six worked. The other numbers did start working again several hours later though. It should be noted that the back is solid which means that you will not be able to use a rear facing camera. All of that being said, I think it is well worth the price I paid for it.
