    Product Description

Style:7’6” Light Stand Kit - HTC Vive Edition Product Overview

The StudioPRO Set of Two 7’6” Photo Video Light Stands w/Ball Head Mount is the perfect kit for setting up your HTC Vive. The Vive Base Stations need to be positioned at least 6 feet high, face each other, and stand pointed toward your “play area”. With this kit, you will not have to mount the base stations directly onto your wall. Instead, you can easily set them up and move them around with the two light stands. The mini ball head mount operates with an adjustable swivel bracket, enabling you to pivot and turn the base stations any direction. 

The ball head mount can also attach to your DSLR camera, and be mounted onto tripods and monopods for photography or videography. This kit also includes a light weight carrying bag for traveling or storage when not in use. The foam padded walls help protect your equipment from becoming damaged. This bag makes it possible to pack up your play area or studio anytime, anywhere. 

Note: VR electronics sold separately.

Even with the slight vibration these boxes generate. Coupled with the tripod ball head bracket and it’s a complete solution for your VR space.

Great small stand for ringlight or other options. Quite durable.

I thought I reviewed this previously, but I guess I didn’t. I needed something to hold my ring light on a table as my larger stand was just too tall. I have an 18 inch ring light so it can be quite top heavy, but I position the legs of this stand so that it keeps it balanced and I haven’t had any issues with toppling over. This thing is sturdy, I’m very impressed with how well it is made and how durable it is. I use it several times a week for hours on end and it doesn’t have any issues. If you are looking for a small table top stand, I highly suggest this one as it is affordable and really holds up well. I’m impressed for sure. I do suggest that you not put it on a slippery surface. I place it on a table top that is not slippery and it stays, but on my table that is slightly slippery, it can slide. Otherwise, it works really well.
