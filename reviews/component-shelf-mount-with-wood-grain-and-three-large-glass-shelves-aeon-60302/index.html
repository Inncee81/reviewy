    Product Description

Component Shelf Mount with beautiful wood grain finish and smoked glass - Sleek and stylish.

This black wood grain three tier DVD component wall mount shelf and bracket holds your entertainment audio / video component to the wall. Perfect to compliment your wall mounted TV. Gain back valuable floor space, eliminate wires and cables, make your entertainment center into a work of art rather than an eye-sore. Unlike other component shelves, this three tier component shelf has over sized glass shelves to fit even the largest of components . Built from a beautiful black wood grain finish and extra heavy duty glass shelves to compliment your TV. The large channel behind the shelves can hide and manage many power and av cables. The extra heavy duty shelves are safety tempered and safe for even children- rated to hold 35 pounds per shelf.

Can be used for any of the following audio video components: - AV receiver- UP to 14.5” deep -DVD Player -BluRay Player -Cable / Satellite Box -PS3, Playstation -XBox -Wii -All other Audio Video Components

It looks great in our living room and I have no fear of it falling.Right now it’s holding an Home Theater PC, DVD’s, a cable box, a router, a clock, and a framed photo.We have probably 10 cables running through the cable management section. All my components fit perfect!!

I ordered this shelf because of the with on the back side and the large shelfs. All my components fit perfect!!! I only needed to fish a few wires through the back of the unit. I actually cut a hole in the wall directly behind the middle shelf and feed all the wires though and back though another hole I made behind the tv. The wires on the “bedroom” side were covered with a painting, that way if I ever need to access the wires all I have to do is grab the painting and i’m there!!!Unit is pretty heavy and would need to be stud mounted. I was able to handle it myself, but I am a big man. Most people would probably need two people for this install.

Nice - No Issues At All With Weight

Before purchasing this unit I saw some displeased customers due to the fact that they had wall damage from where this unit was too heavy for the wall and allegedly couldn’t take the weight it claims: This unit is heavy just by itself. Do not place on just any spot on the wall, ensure you mount it into a stud. If you do that, you’re golden. Absolutely no issues with this unit, works as advertised if mounted properly. Looks nice. Good quality.
