Cheap looking and mislabeled, but functional

This monocular has some obvious cosmetic issues. As some have pointed out, the compass and Panda brand name are cheap little things glued onto the rubbery casing, and look like they’ll fall off pretty quickly. Also, mine says “35x50” above the eyepiece, which would normally mean 35x magnification and a 50mm objective lens, though it gives 10x magnification, and the lens measures 40mm across. That is the magnification I was expecting, and wanted, but the lens having less than 2⁄3 the expected size and light grasp bothers me, and it being mislabeled like that is a little annoying.Other than that, it’s fine. The 10x40 optics aren’t bad, and the hardware for taking pics with a cellphone seems very handy. If I’d known that it was 40mm I’d have bought something else, but most buyers probably won’t care.
