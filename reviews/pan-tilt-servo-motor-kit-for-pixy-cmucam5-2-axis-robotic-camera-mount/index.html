Pan/Tilt Servo Motor Kit for Pixy (CMUcam5) - 2 Axis Robotic Camera Mount

by people ...

Product Specifications

  • Works with Pixy CMUcam5 (not included)
  • Good “Hello world” demo for Pixy
  • Powered from USB port (no batteries required)

  • Does not work with Pixy LEGO

    Product Description

This is a kit for a pan/tilt mechanism designed specifically for Pixy CMUcam5. It includes two laser-cut acrylic pieces for the base, two servos for the pan and tilt axes, and all the mounting hardware and cable ties you need to assemble. Assembly instructions are located on our wiki.
