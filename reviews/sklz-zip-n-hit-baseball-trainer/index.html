    Product Description

The SKLZ Zip-N-Hit Baseball Trainer helps teach batters ‘top-half’ hitting, while reinforcing a level swing. Practice hitting a variety of pitches at different speeds, without having an actual pitcher on the mound. With no fear of getting pegged by the ball, you can focus on the batter’s stance, swing, eye tracking, and overall technique. Set your players up for success before they step up to the plate, improving their batting average and giving them more confidence for the game. The Zip-N-Hit is a fun way to work with real skills. The long line rope system is easy to use; simply pull the cord apart to “zip” the pitch down the line. You can either attach it to a stable post, chain link fence, or tree with the attachment clip and strap, or you can use a teammate or coach to “pitch” to you. The easily portable, convenient Zip-N-Hit teaches confidence, power, and overall better hitting technique for youth baseball and softball players. Have fun and improve your baseball skills with the Zip-N-Hit baseball trainer.

Product Reviews

Not very durable

A great concept… unfortunately the plastic-type washer inside the ball (nearest the handles) wore away with deep slices in it after our first use. So the cord finds these slices (made by the cord) with every pull… therefore the ball just stops mid-way down the cord. I will try to get some replacements and perhaps the item was damaged already. The other washer on the bat side broke with the first few swings of the bat but I don’t think that affected the movement of the ball. However, the washer closest to the handles certainly will stop the balls progress down the cord.

Piece of crap.

The plastic grommet in the middle of the ball should be metal. The nylon rope cut through it after about 10 of use and the ball wouldn’t go more than 5 feet after it started cutting through it. The idea behind it is perfect. But the execution of the engineering is garbage. They cut a corner to save a few cents and it made the product not worth a single dollar.

Works, but no the quality I had hoped.

The SKLZ Zip-N-Hit could be great tool to teach young ballplayers how develop a good swing. The fact that they dont need to adust to varying pitch locations allows them to focus on the path of the ball and making contact in the heart of their swing as well as develop repition in their swing to encourage peoper mechanics. I also like the fact that you can change speeds so they really have to watch the ball instead of just loading up and putting the bat out there like some do with pitchiing machines.The reason I gave it three stars is due to the quality. After the first time we used it (10,8, and 6 year olds swinging at it) one of the black pieces that is attached to the ball to let the string glide through it came off. It looks like it was glued on. While this is not affect its performace, it was disheartening to see it start to come apart after the first use! The second time we used it, the small bumpers on the hand grips that stop the ball when it is driven down the line came off. It also looked like it was glued on. The other bumper came off of the handle as well. Again, this does not affect the performace of it. I just have to (after almost every hit) readjust those bumpers back into place before we launch the next ball. It is a hassle.I rated it three stars because although it did have some asthetic problems with the pieces coming off, it is a neat concept for a hitting tool.
