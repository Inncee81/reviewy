    Product Description

When you hear the Proscan name, immediately you think of quality built Tablets at amazing prices that are packed with the features you want and need. Well the folks at Proscan know this and are excited to deliver the PLT1090-K, with a reversible screen AND a detachable hard case and keyboard! The most recent Windows 10.1 operating system is efficient and quick. The gorgeous 10-Inch IPS touch screen is elegant, easy to view and easy on the eyes. It is easier than ever to connect to the internet and all of your favorite on-line activities including, email, social networking, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube and the list goes on! This tablet also has built in Wi-Fi (802.11 B/G/N) so you can connect to the internet via any Wi-Fi network in the world. Along with all of this, the PLT1090-K comes with a rechargeable battery for hours of fun. Proscan has provided not only a front facing 2mp camera but a 2nd, rear facing 2mp camera! Take all the pictures, video and selfies your heart desires in perfect quality to share with your friends, family and your favorite social network! This feature packed tablet includes and SD slot, Bluetooth, 2 USB inputs along with inputs for your mouse (not included), mini HDMI and a headphone connection for presentations and audio! The tablet is also loaded with Microsoft’s, “Groove Music”. Play ad-free music from one of the biggest catalogs on the planet, Listen on PC, tablet, Xbox, web or smartphone (Android, iPhone and Windows Phone) and Create playlists and custom radio stations based on artists you love. Of course the Microsoft “store” is pre-loaded as well which allows you to shop for Apps, Games, TV, Movies and MORE! So go on, the wide world of the internet is just a click away. Facebook, YouTube, Gmail, Pandora to name a few. This all comes from the makers at Proscan, the leader in high powered Tablets.
