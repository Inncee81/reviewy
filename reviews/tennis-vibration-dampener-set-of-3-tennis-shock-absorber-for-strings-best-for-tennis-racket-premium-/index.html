They work great on my Head Graphene Radical MP

I’ve been using these dampeners for about 3-weeks now. They are fantastic. I use them on my Head Graphene Radical MP. They fit nicely on the bottom of the racket face outside of the last cross string. I also use one outside of the last cross string on the top of the racket face. USTA permits multiple devices so long as they are located inside the outside cross strings. The reason I use two is that this particular racket is dead or vibration prone near the top portion of the string bed. I used to use conventional dampeners but they would slide or come off. So far this has not happened with the 21Ksports ones. Note that the outermost portion may occasionally pop off the top string bed. It doesn’t effect my play and I just periodically adjust the dampener at the top between points. I’m a 4.0 player and string my racket with 17G poly at 38 lbs. This string combination and tension with the 21Ksports dampeners works great. Very satisfied.
