    Product Description

The Optoma EH300 data projector delivers a powerful 3500 ANSI lumens image combined with an industry-leading 15,000:1 contrast ratio to produce stunning widescreen high-definition 1080p presentations with amazingly rich, accurate colors and incredibly sharp detail. The Optoma EH300 is remarkably feature rich, easy to operate and has one of the most comprehensive input connector panels in its class. Designed to deliver outstanding performance and maximum reliability, the Optoma EH300 offers whisper quiet operation, full 3D compatibility, superior lamp life to minimize cost of operation and, best of all, the dependability that Optoma is known for.
