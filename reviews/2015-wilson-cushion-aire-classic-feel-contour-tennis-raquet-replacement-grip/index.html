    Product Description

The Wilson Cushion-Aire Classic Contour Replacement Grip has been a staple in the world of racquet grips for years. A ridge throughout allows for increased grip sensation, while tiny perforations promote increased absorption and decreased drying time. Made to replace the base grip on your racquet, the Cushion-Aire features the comfortable feel its name would suggest along with a bit of tackiness to promote confidence in your hand positioning as you swing.
