What a big horrible disappointment!!!! So much potential too. :(

Wow, I am really upset to have to write this review. I bought this because I have to leave my kids along in the morning on occasion for school and wanted to be able to keep an eye on them in the 30 to 45 min time frame between when I leave and they get picked up to go to school. I ordered 3 cameras, and one of them arrived defective! I had to fight them to not have to pay the shipping cost to return their defective camera. AND the process took 3 weeks involving them not following up, sending emails asking for information I had already given by phone 2 days earlier, and getting their customer service representatives CONTACTING ME AT 3AM and not even apologizing!!!!! The system has a HUGE delay in recording for motion detection often completely missing the activity. The audio is so sensitive that my front camera records the passing cars so i end up with like 80 recordings draining my space and battery. Their “geofencing” stopped working when I added the3rd camera once it finally arrived almost a month later. It kept recording even when I was home, sending me like 30 email alerts saying motion detected, BUT I HAVE NOTIFICATIONS TURNED OFF, SO WHY AM I GETTING ALERTS!?!? When you select the type of mode you want the system to work in (Armed, disarmed, schedule, or geofencing, or create your own), you have to do it for ALL cameras. So even if I want the outside cameras on at night, but not the indoor cameras on, it’s all or nothing. You can’t individually select each camera’s mode. I also wanted to turn the audio off for the front door camera to not get every loud car driving by, but the only audio you can turn off is the speaker audio or ability to hear. the camera still DETECTS the audio even with audio set to off, so, what’s the point?? The system is so faulty I really wish I had not spent so much money on it. If they could fix all those problems, it could potentially be a great system. The picture quality is good and the speaker and alarm feature are also great, hence the added star (instead of giving 1 star). Please Arlo, fix all your glitches and you will do great. As of now I will be contacting you to trouble shoot these issues. If they can’t be fixed, you can have your cameras back, I will need to get something I can really on.
