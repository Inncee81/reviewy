EXCELLENT Light in all respects … except for one …

This little light works great. Battery life is excellent. I use it lighting music score in live Broadway Musicals and it gives a great amount of light that is really constrained to where it needs to be and doesn’t light anything else. It is super flexible so directing the light to exactly the right spot is a cinch. The battery charges very fast with is a plus. The spring is solid so the light stays put. I would have given this light five stars except that the USB charger cable is NOT A STANDARD micro or mini USB connector. It is a special cable that has a USB A connector on one end and the other end that plugs into the light is a co-axial connector, like the typical 9v and 5v round connectors with the hole in the center and the sleeve the other connector. If you lose this cable or break it you’re toast. You can’t just grab any old USB cable - you have to have this special cable. I have dozens of cables at home but no other cable like this one. Great light, really, but a poor decision to have a non-standard USB cable.
