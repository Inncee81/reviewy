Product Specifications

  • 16 MP Maximum Still Image Resolution and 720p HD Video Resolution

    Product Description

Color:Sky Blue The Bell+Howell WP7 Splash camera is a 12 Mega Pixel waterproof digital camera that is easy to use, compact and handheld. It is waterproof to 10 feet so you can take it with you to the beach, in the pool, surfing, skiing, boating and standard every day use. Pictures come out clear and vivid with vibrant colors.
