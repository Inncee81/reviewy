38 Inch Model

I purchased the 38” model this past weekend and I am extremely satisfied with my purchase. It compliments my Vizio E series very nicely and the additional bass was worth the step up from the 29” model. The sound bar turns on with my set and shuts off by itself after the set is turned off. Bluetooth connectivity is simple and the bass/treble adjustment via the included remote are a breeze. All necessary cables are included in the box along with mounting brackets and screws. This isn’t going to give you the sound that a 5.1 Bose system would but it is leaps and bounds better than built-in speakers and it produces rich, clear sound with a surprising amount of bass. For $100 and about 10 minutes of setup it’s hard to go wrong here.

Fantastic value

Music was muddy. Vocals were indistinct. In scenes crowded with background noise and music, it was nearly impossible to hear dialog because everything washed together. I finally decided I could not take it anymore and started searching for sound bars. I needed something affordable that delivered good sound for the money, and this product has fit the bill. Music and sound effects are crisp, vocals are distinct, and there is just enough bass for a bedroom. It is the perfect sound bar for the space. If you are looking for a product upgrade your main living room TV, you might want something higher end with a dedicated subwoofer, but for bedroom applications, you can’t go wrong with this.
