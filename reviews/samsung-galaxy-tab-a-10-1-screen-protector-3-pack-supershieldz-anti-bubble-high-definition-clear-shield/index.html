Supershieldz works good. Worth the money.

The screen protector is a flexible plastic of some sort and, once I got it on, works fine. As usual, the problem is with the instructions. The actual film sheet that protects is a little bit thicker than the two pieces of plastic film that cover it on both sides. The trick is to pull off the correct plastic sheet first while applying the protector layer to the tablet. The protector has a sticky side that adheres to the glass on the tablet so you pull down the cover little by little as you ‘iron’ out the bubbles.The tablet has to be scrupulously clean of ALL dust, airborne debris, fingerprints, etc. There is a little square of treated cloth fabric to do that.As you apply the protector the best tool to use to get the bubbles out is the cloth, not the little card included. With the cloth, you can slip the air bubbles out to the side without scratching or abraiding the protector.I like it and would buy it again.

I was skeptical of the price compared to known brands like Zagg, but for my tablet pickings were slim and this came through. I really have no complaints thus far!
