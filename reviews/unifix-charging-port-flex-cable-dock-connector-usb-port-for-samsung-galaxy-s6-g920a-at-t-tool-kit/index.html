Created more problems than what I was trying to fix

I needed this charge port board for a Samsung Galaxy S6 AT&T phone to fix a badly damaged USB charge port. After installing this part, the folder and back buttons would not work or light up. The phone won’t charge now because the display says “Unable to charge. Charging paused. Battery temperature too low”. It doesn’t make a difference whether charging by the USB connector or Qi inductive. I read on the internet that the temperature sensor is located on this same board. When I compared this board with the original board, I can see that the original board has two spring contact tabs on the back side and the new board does not. I don’t know if this is the cause of the button and charging failures.The tools seem decent with the exception of the tweezers. The tweezers are thin metal and flex too much to get a good grip on small parts. There was some casting flashing on one of the blue prying tools but a quick trim with an x-acto knife cleaned it up.
