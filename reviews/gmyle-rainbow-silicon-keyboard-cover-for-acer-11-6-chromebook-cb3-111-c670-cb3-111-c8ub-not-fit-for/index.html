It is so thin and flexible and actually makes typing feel better. It stays in place

I recently purchased as Acer Chromebook 11 and wanted to protect the keyboard. I bought the GMYLE silicon keyboard cover. I was skeptical and worried that it would be too thick ant flexible enough. I was pleasantly surprised when it arrived yesterday. It is so thin and flexible and actually makes typing feel better. Durable and Useful Product that Makes Typing Comfortable and Quiet! 4.5 Stars.

I have to say that I really like this keyboard protector and find it very useful. I originally purchased it to make my typing more quiet, as I have a tendency to “bang” on the keys. While it does not make my typing completely silent (I don’t think any protector can), the noise is significantly reduced. I am able to bang away on my Chromebook even in the “quiet study” areas of the library without disturbing others.It takes some time getting used to typing with the protector on the keyboard – maybe a couple of hours. But after that, that is very comfortable. I find typing to be more enjoyable and the keys to be more “springy” with the protector on. The material feels rubbery but soft to the touch. It becomes smoother the more often you use it as it absorbs the oils from your fingers. It is certainly a better (thicker) quality than the protectors I first tried on Ebay for $1-$5.It has been nearly 10 months since my purchase and it is still going strong. The silicone rubber has not torn or gone out of shape, despite being washed 6 or 7 times. It still adheres very well to the keyboard and does not fall off unless I turn the Chromebook upside down. Once on, the protector does not shift or slide underneath your fingers even when typing fast.However, the bright gold/champagne color of the protector has worn off significantly – it is only half as bright and vivid as it was when I first received it. The black color of the keys underneath is showing through, but when I first used it, you could not tell what color the keys underneath were (see picture for reference).The white lettering has also held up well, but is starting to wear off on some of the most used keys (see the pictures): E, S, N, I, A. This is with constant, daily use (3-4 hours a day). If you are touch typist, however, this will not matter to you. Though aesthetically it might not look so pretty.I cannot say anything regarding the protective qualities of the product. I haven’t spilled anything on the Chromebook. But it has kept my keyboard underneath very clean and spotless.Overall, a great product, that has held up well under constant daily use and will probably last you as long as your Chromebook, or longer. There are not a lot of products out there which you can say that about. I only wish the price were lower so that I can purchase another one in a different color. 4.5 stars for with points docked off for color fading and wear on the lettering.
