    Product Description

The Waterpik Water Flosser For Kids is the easy, more effective, and more fun way for kids to floss. The Water Flosser For Kids is a custom model for children ages 6+, with a small and simple design that makes water flossing easy. It’s perfect for any kid who has braces or has trouble flossing. The Waterpik Water Flosser For Kids features an extra safe, kid-proof electrical design, easy fingertip controls on the On/Off switch and the Pressure Control System, and a reservoir that can be used as a cover to keep the unit clean. The Water Flosser For Kids includes one Orthodontic Tip for braces and one Classic Jet Tip for general cleaning, and each unit has a two-year warranty.

Little did I know that somewhere in that 2 year period the waterpik started gathering black mold/gunk inside the hose and handle. It was actually packed with it. I have since seen on line a way to keep it from forming and I will buy this Waterpik again, as I had to throw the other one out. I would recommend this to anyone needing a good waterpik at a very reasonable price, but only if you go online and see the maintenance needed to keep the gunk from forming inside the pik. This company should make these instructions a necessary part of the packaging for this waterpic. I have only given 3 stars because of that, otherwise I would have given at least 4.
