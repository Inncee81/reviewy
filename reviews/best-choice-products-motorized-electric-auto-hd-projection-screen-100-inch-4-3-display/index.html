The motor is super quiet albeit a little slow to raise/lower, but its for my “Man Cave” so it’s not a big deal to me. The remote and its wall mount are a nice touch. I did notice the infamous “smell” on the screen but it’s not that noticeable and it goes away after a couple of hours. All in all, I’m very happy with the purchase.
