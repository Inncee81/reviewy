Cheap and best.

After my backup hard drive crashed recently (costing me a few thousands of pictures and memories) I decided to backup my pictures and videos on my GoFlex network hard drive AND start an album of SD cards. One SD card for every long trip. This SD card is my favorite. It is super fast (overkill even for my Nikon D7000 DSLR), plenty of space, and the best part, ITS PRICE!!! I bought a couple of these SD cards and a SD card pouch in this link ( http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000ZD0CV4?psc=1&redirect=true&ref_=ox_sc_sfl_title_3&smid=A3QJ4LGSRS09NU ), Now I have a second line of defense for my pictures.

Great upgrade for a multi-track recorder!

I bought this to replace a Class 10 SDHC card in a multi-track recorder. They were the same capacity but this card is most definitely faster. The track recorder can record 8 tracks simultaneously and if I was using the old SDHC card, there would be a pause to wait for the device to finish writing to the card. There was only about a three second pause to wait with the old card (not too bad) but with the new Samsung card it’s only about one second (much better). Definitely worth the money. I gave the old card to my mom for her point and shoot camera (The old card was plenty fast enough for that). Not all Class 10 SD cards are created equally; Samsung really knows how to make good flash memory.
