Product Specifications

  • Capture 12MP Crystal Clear Still Images
  • Waterproof to Depths of 49ft (15 meters)
  • 8 Point G-Sensor Stabilization

  • With 2.5 Hours of Continuous Shooting, Wi-Fi Enabled and FREE 10GB Of Cloud Storage

    Product Description

The Air Pro 3 HD Video Camera is designed for professionals and consumers alike. Waterproof without the need for a case down to 49 feet and free cloud storage. It also records HD video up to 60 frames per second at 1080p, and up to 120 frames per second at 720p. Dual file recording allows for simultaneous ‘shoot and share’ capability.

Will be great when the software is fully refined

I wanted a camera that was standalone waterproof, without any enclosure, and the iON fit that bill. It provides a good picture and was easy to learn. However, it has at least one significant hitch. On two occasions, when using the wifi feature (which is nice) to frame my shot with my iPhone, I noticed that when the wifi was turned off, the camera turned off. When the camera was turned on with the slide switch to record the event for which it was set-up, the camera came on (at least the light) but did not enter recording mode, and on at least one of the occasions had to be reset to get it to respond. Needless to say, the record of the event was lost. Only one, as I was primed to correct it on the second. On at least two occasions where I staged a test to replicate the occurrence, all went as normal, so this appears to be unpredictable. This is a major handicap, as the reason for wanting the wifi capability was to allow me to properly frame a recording where the camera is mounted in a remote and/or high noise location where I cannot detect the vibes.The software is up to date. I am keeping the camera, but will have to live with the unknowns, unless and until they are corrected.NOTE: Minimizing wind noise with this camera is a bit of a challenge, as the entire outer surface of the camera seems to conduct sound to the mic. I have been able to compensate for this by enclosing the camera (all but the lens that is 8^) is a 3⁄16” foam casing with a slit in the bottom for the mount. The rubber skins have this effect to a small extent, but not near as effectively as the foam. The operation of the button and the slide on the top is still relatively normal through the foam layer. The foam must come as far forward as possible to maximize effectiveness.
