KitchenAid K5GB 5-Qt. Tilt-Head Glass Bowl with Measurement Markings & Lid

by people ...

Product Specifications

  • 5-quart glass bowl fits all KitchenAid tilt head stand mixers except Accolade
  • Pour spout, wide handle, and measuring lines for easy use,Fits models KSM150PS, KSM152PS and KSM155GB.
  • Microwave- and freezer-safe for added functionality
  • Tight-sealing lid is dishwasher-safe and stores in bowl

  • Measures 7.5 inches by 9.1 inches by 11.8 inches

    Product Description

Color:Clear Elegant transparency, meets superior quality with the new 5-Quart glass bowl designed exclusively for your KitchenAid stand mixer. Whether you are melting chocolate or chilling butter, this glass bowl can be conveniently used in the microwave or freezer, allowing you to do more with less clean up. This timeless design incorporates a pouring spout, handle, and measuring lines for ease of use, and includes a specially designed lid that is dishwasher safe and can be stored on the bowl when not in use. The Glass Bowl will fit all tilt head models with the exception of the Accolade Model. Does not fit Bowl-Lift models.

If beater hits the side of the bowl, try rotating the bowl 180 degrees

The bowl feels solid, and the lid fits well. I attached the bowl to my KitchenAid Artisan stand mixer. At first, the flat beater lightly hit the side of the bowl when mixing. I detached the bowl, rotated it 180 degrees, and reattached it; in this new position, the flat beater did not hit the side of the bowl.
