Good, solid, inexpensive…. just watch out for your plug in the back

Seems like a good solid product. I bought this for a smaller size 26” tv. All the necessary hardware for several methods of attaching it to a wall are included. I fastened it to a wall stud, by far, the best method. I gave it 4 stars instead of 5 because the bracket used to attach it to the back of the tv overlapped the receptacle for the power plug. I used a hacksaw to make a few small cuts and bent the offending part out of the way. No big deal, but not something my mom, for instance, could do, so just be alert. Other than that, it’s a great deal for the price!
