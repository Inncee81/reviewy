Product Reviews

Cute Brushes, FYI Brushes Not Replacable, As Product Description States, Characters May Vary

This is a product that initially, I bought one for our son, who has Autism among other things, because I thought this would be a good item to work on getting him used to the tooth cleaner they use at dentists visits. I know that visually there are definitely some differences, but the noise, and the circular shape of the top part of the brush I figured would give us a good starting place to work on desensitizing him to it.When it arrived, my daughter thought it was so cute, she wanted one. And she’s been using it for a couple days now, and she says she loves it and prefers it to a traditional toothbrush. So I may get her a permanent electric toothbrush with replaceable brushes after this one dies.As other reviewers have noted, characters you get will vary. This is actually in the top line of the product description under Amazon, so I personally don’t feel like I can find any fault with that. It was right there for me to read. We personally got 1 with the princesses, and one with Olaf. So our daughter wanted the princess one, and I let her take that one, because our son prefers Olaf anyways, but quite honestly, he’s still in the totally hating it stage because it makes a noise he doesn’t like. But I feel pretty confident this is going to be useful in his therapy modules, so I’m pleased with the noise.And it does make a fairly loud noise, which is something to be aware of if your kiddo does have sensory issues and it’s something you want to avoid. Also, and this isn’t mentioned on the product description on-line, be aware that you can’t replace the brush heads on these. So if you are wanting a permanent electric toothbrush for your kiddos, this isn’t the product for you. For us, I just wanted something temporary to start transitioning our son into tolerating the cleaner used at the dentist, so I’m totally pleased with what I got. And given the price, even if it dies within a few months, I’m not going to be all that upset because it can still be used as a traditional toothbrush. So I think this is a cute product, as long as you don’t care which character you get, you understand you can’t replace out the brush heads, and I think it’s going to be wicked useful in our son’s therapy modules.
