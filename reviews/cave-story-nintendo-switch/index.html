Product Specifications

  • Upgraded “HD” graphics
  • 7 play modes (including Wind Fortress, Boss Rush and more)
  • Over 20 epic boss battles through Mimiga Island
  • 15 levels to explore in the vast world

  • 10 unique weapons to find and upgrade

    Product Description

Arguably the most well-known indie game of all time, Cave Story+ features a completely original storyline wrapped with personality, mystery, and hours of fast-paced fun. Cave Story+ is an action-adventure game from the critically acclaimed independent designer, Daisuke Amaya–or Pixel to his fans. Overflowing with unmatched charm and character, Cave Story+ takes you into a rare world where a curious race of innocent rabbit-like creatures, called Mimigas, run free. Run, jump, shoot, fly, and explore your way through a massive action-adventure reminiscent of classic 8- and 16-bit games. Take control and learn the origins of this world’s power, stop the delusional villain and save the Mimiga!

I decided to get this for the great cover art but was surprised to find some extras

I first played this game many years ago when it was a free, PC download. I decided to get this for the great cover art but was surprised to find some extras. Notably, a mini CD that contains the game’s soundtrack and a full-color, illustrated game manual with tips and instructions that made me reminiscent of NES game manuals. I think this is well worth the price and if you’ve never played Cave Story–now’s the time!

Still a great game

Cave Story + is seriously a good game. It’s side scrolling platforming at its best. This version should be the definitive version of Cave Story. The new visuals for Switch are outstanding. It has different styles of the soundtrack to choose from. The game controls like a dream. My only reason for a 4 star review is that this is my 3 version of the game on Nintendo systems and there really isn’t anything new in the content. Players who have played before might want to pass even though I would recommend giving it one more go. If you have never played before I would say it’s a definite pick up. I also recommend Cartridge over a digital download as they cost the same anyway. You can get some trade in value for your cartridge or save it as a collectable.

This is new nostalgia!

At first I was a little frustrated by the controls for Cave Story+, but after consulting the included instruction manual, I was much more comfortable after just a few minutes. The gameplay truly is like a combination of classic Metroid and Castlevania with a little bit of classic mMega Man for good measure! And even playing on easy this game is challenging. But overall I like Cave Story+, and I look forward to paying it for a good while. So if you’re looking for a step back to a more classic side scrolling platformer, then look no further! This is what you’re looking for!
