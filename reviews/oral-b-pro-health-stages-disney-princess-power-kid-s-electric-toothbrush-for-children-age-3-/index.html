Turned Brushing Teeth Time into the Best Part of the Day!

This was the best purchase!!! Brushing teeth used to be a huge battle with my daughters. We skipped way too many mornings because I would forget to remind them when I was busy getting ready for work. And we skipped too many nights because my daughter would be overtired and need to go bed ASAP and it just wasn’t worth the battle. I only purchased this toothbrush because it was on sale for very cheap and I needed a prize for my daughters for completing their rewards chart and I thought they would like anything with princesses on it. What I didn’t know is that there is an app that comes with this toothbrush that times them for 2 minutes while they watch a Disney picture get uncovered. My daughter (5 years old) looks forward to brushing her teeth, never forgets morning or night, and brushes for the whole 2 minutes. On top of that, she is so excited by it that she encourages her sister (3 years old) to brush her teeth and helps out! Definitely recommend this toothbrush!
