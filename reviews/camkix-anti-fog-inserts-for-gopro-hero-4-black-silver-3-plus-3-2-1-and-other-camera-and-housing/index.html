Keep Your GoPro Safe and Fog Free

CamKix Anti-Fog Inserts Will Absorb Moisture and Keep Your Lens and Housing Free of Fog

When traveling through cold or humid climates with your GoPro, moisture can easily build-up inside the housing and on your lens. This can damage your camera and make it difficult to capture clear images and video.

By simply placing CamKix anti-fog inserts inside your GoPro housing you can prevent this problem. Made from high quality silica gel on cellulose fiber, the inserts absorb moisture and keep it away from your camera.

The inserts can be used multiple times. When you want to remove the moisture from the inserts, simply bake them in the oven at 300F for 5 minutes.

Cut them ahead of time: they will get wet while handling them and you should regenerate them after cutting and before use.
