    Product Description

Navy Classic Crest Covers are the perfect way to display any award, degree, or achievement! These prestigious certificate covers have a beautiful Gold Foil design on the front. Inside are slots for mounting a certificate. This cover is made from 80lb Cover card stock. The size of the cover is 12” x 9 3⁄8”, and each cover will fit a standard size certificate measuring 8 1⁄2” x 11”
