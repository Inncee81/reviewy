    Product Description

Size:18-55 Black The new Nikon D3400 makes it easier than ever to take vibrant, sharp pictures and videos and share them with people instantly. The introduction on smartphones changed the way most people take and share pictures and video. The convenience of having a camera phone with an internet connection meant that people could take a photo or video and instantly share it with friends and family via text messages or social networking sites. However, the trade-off for this convenience is a huge drop in image quality. Using a smartphone camera in low-light environments frequently produces blurry and grainy images while trying to capture an action shot is largely a game of chance thanks to the phone’s slower performance. The Nikon D3400 changes all this by giving you the best of both worlds: You get beautiful 24MP images and HD 1080p videos that can be wirelessly transferred to your phone or tablet for instant sharing. The D3400’s compact design means it’s easy to take anywhere and the swappable NIKKOR lens system allows users to tailor the camera to the shooting environment. This particular camera includes a versatile 18-55mm NIKKOR lens that’s great for shooting portraits as well as landscapes.

Great camera

Great package to get started with. All items that were advertised were included when it arrived. The lenses are obviously top quality, as was the bag. Everything else was a more off-brand name, but all seem to function as they should, except the external flash. I cannot get the external flash to work on the camera and, in fact, cannot even get it to fully seat on the camera as it should. It’s like the base plate is to thick to fit in the slots on the camera.Have used the camera on several trips now and am extremely pleased with the photos the camera takes as well as how easy to use the camera is, even for a beginner like myself. Great value and excellent quality

Great camera, great kit. I bought this camera prior to a trip to China, and it didn’t disappoint. I got a lot of compliments on the camera while there, as well as the quality of pictures now that I have returned.
