    Product Description

This is an innovation of battery charger.

You can use AA Batteries the moment you need them. The fully charged batteries will be in the battery storage. Just take them out whenever you need it.

Put on the drained batteries without polarity. Drained batteries can be placed in the stand-by room  without the polarity. The stand-by room can fit 20 batteries with ease. With a click of a button, the batteries in the stand-by room will be quick-charged in order.   Automatic charging system

When the charging starts, the batteries in the waiting stand by-room will channel to the recharging slots to be quick-charged. About 4 batteries will be charged at once and it will take and an hour. The charging will be continued until all batteries in the battery storage are completely charged. The fully charged batteries will be carried to the battery stroage automatically. Quick Charge

There are 4 machinery parts that charges batteries. When the button is pushed, 4 AA batteries will be quick-charged. When Eneroid is recharging the batteries the lamp will show a red light and after completing the charge, it will glow blue light.

Refresh Mode The refresh mode is a technology that exhausts the reaming energy left in the battery than charges the battery. There is a switch to turn on refresh mode at the back of Eneroid. The life of Ni-Cd batteries can be prolonged when charged after being refreshed.   Smart Interface

There is no battery contact/spring making it very easy to charge the battery. You also do not have to worry about plus and minus making it very convenient. There is over charging with the protection system built in Eneroid. If Eneroid is not being used, it will automatically go into sleep mode minimizing the usage of electricity. The lamp will automatically change the brightness of the light based on the surroundings condition. This automatic system will not bother people when sleeping.

Battery Storage The fully charged battery will be automatically channeled to the battery storage. The lamp or the display of Eneroid will show a signal when the Battery storage is full. The surface of battery storage is made up of translucent material which makes the batteries visible when closed. Simply slide open the Battery storage whenever batteries are needed.

3 USB charging ports There are one 2A and two 1A USB Charging port on the rear side of the deluxe models.These ports can charge and product that uses USB port for charging such as smartphones and tablet PC.

Finally, it only takes AA format so you can charge AA batteries but only with adapters (it came with 4 adapters).On the plus side, it is convenient if you have a lot of batteries to charge..which is the main point of the device in the first place.
