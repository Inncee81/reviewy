The included 3” tube was perfect for our installation and the HDMI and power cord were easily routed through the tube. One side to side adjustment and tilting the projector down a little was quickly done.One note: be sure and wipe down/clean the included allen tool before using otherwise you will be cleaning everything afterBasically, if I ever have to mount a projector into a suspended ceiling, I will use this mount again
