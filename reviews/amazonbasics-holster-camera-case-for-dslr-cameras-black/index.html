An excellent value and extremely well-built case for your DSLR

UPDATE: I’ve used this case for just over 3 years now and it is holding up perfectly. I really like this case since it is minimalistic and has proven to be very durable. It is just as good today in every way as it was 3 years ago when I first acquired it.—This is a very nicely made case for your DSLR. I’m completely happy with the quality of construction and padded protection this provides my Nikon D300. It’s the perfect size when I want to grab my DSLR and not be burdened with extra lenses and all the other stuff that I normally would have with me using a larger camera bag.The depth of the case is 7.5” enabling me to fit my D300, and attached battery pack with my 17-55 f2.8 lens. It’s a tad tight closing the top zippered lid, but it does fit and that’s very nice. If I were to remove the battery pack, it would fit very comfortably with a little wiggle room.The case has an extremely durable bottom piece that is about 1 inch tall and is a solid plastic base. This cushions and protects the DSLR case from water infiltration if you were to set your case on the ground.There are 3 outside pockets. The left and right zippered pockets are marginally useful. They do not expand at all and have little volume but are suitable for lens cleaning cloths, lens filters or something thin. The front pocket is way more useful and has enough room to put in several smaller items. It has a elasticized back nylon mesh panel that you could throw a couple memory cards into as well as two dedicated memory card (SD) pockets with velcro closure on the front inside panel.A comfortably padded and suitably wide shoulder strap is included.What a great case this is. Very functional, comfortable to carry and just the right amount of space to easily carry a DSLR, decent sized lens plus a few odds and ends.It’s a great price and seems to be of the highest quality. I expect this will provide years of good service.If you’re looking for something that will hold more gear but is still very easy to carry check out the AmazonBasics Backpack for SLR Cameras and Accessories-Black that I have reviewed here http://www.amazon.com/review/R168VTS3JFPB86

Side pockets a little too small

I received this as a bundled package (including a 16GB SD card) with my D3200. The case is well made, with two side pockets and two front pockets. There is a handle on top, as well as a nice shoulder strap. There are two straps inside the bag that velco to the sides to hold the camera aloft so it’s not sitting on the lens. There is a little room under the camera, but I wouldn’t count on putting anything besides a camera with the attached lens in the main compartment. The case is really for limited carrying; there’s not room for lots of things. The front pockets have some smaller plush velco pockets (for SD cards) and other tiny implements.Two things I wish of this case:An extra way to carry the case, possibly with a belt strap on the backside.Slightly larger side pockets; they are only really useful with thin items.Overall, it will serve me well protecting my camera. If necessary, I can place this bag into a larger bag to carry around.
