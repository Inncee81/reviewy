Product is just ok. Customer service leaves much to be desired.

The case broke within one day of receipt. The magnet that holds the stand door closed broke 1⁄4 of the way down by simply closing it, with no abuse or misuse. The stand did not close flush into the back of the case and leaves a very noticeable lip from day one. This, combined with the magnet defect, prevents this case from being used further without the stand flying out and making the device awkward to handle and more susceptible to dropping. I attempted to reach out to the company Gumdrop, and they requested pictures of the damage and have not heard back. Customer service isn’t great, though I will say that aside from these issues the case is very rugged and will most certainly protect the device. Just hope you do not get a defective unit, as your experience could be similar. I will be shopping around for another case.
