Product Reviews

Completely worth it!

This stuff is fantastic! I just got back from a 4 day backpacking trip and one of my biggest worries was that my inner thighs would become chaffed and I would be miserable. I took 3 of the small containers with me on the trip, including the one I opened on the day that I left. I put the open one in a small ziploc because I didn’t use all of it on the first application. I ended up using the same container for all 4 days and it still didn’t run out. I applied the cream once every morning and then I may have put a little more on my inner thighs one time in the evening. I was also wearing clothing that would help prevent chaffing, but either way the cream worked great and I had no chaffing! I was worried that I would go through multiple containers of it very quickly, but I can probably still get 2-3 more days out of the original one that I opened. A little goes a long way. I was not applying it to the entire length of my inner thigh, only on the upper part of my thighs in a relatively small area. I’m not so sure that the “female variety” of this makes a difference, but I bought it just incase. I have used the regular version of this stuff on week long camping/hiking trips and it seemed I had to apply it more often but I was literally up moving around for more than 12-15 hours of the day while leading a group of students through the wilderness. I also wasn’t wearing the type of clothes that would help prevent chaffing. I think if I hadn’t worn clothes that help prevent the chaffing on this last trip I may have applied the cream more often. I will definitely buy this product over and over again.
