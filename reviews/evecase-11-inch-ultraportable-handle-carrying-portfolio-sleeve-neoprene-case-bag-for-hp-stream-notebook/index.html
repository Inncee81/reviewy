Great Protection, Great Durability and Great Color Options!

I purchased this for my HP Stream that is 11in. The case itself it soft and has two pockets one for your laptop and the other for things like your charger( you could also probably fit a wireless mouse in at the same time like I do). The two handles make it easy to carry and it provides pretty good protection for your laptop. I like the fact that it is not too bulky which allows me to store it or take it with me with ease. You can’t beat this price and the color options, it has kept my laptop safe for a while now. I also usually like to give my reviews after having a product for a little bit to test the durability and this case definitely passed.

Perfect size to securely hold my HP Stream without excess …

Perfect size to securely hold my HP Stream without excess space for “rattling around”. Intergal padding is augmented with an insert which can be removed - I like to leave it in for extra protection - although it is only for one side. Handle will stay folded flat if you want to carry it without the handles, and then neatly fold up and connect via a velcro strip if you want to carry it with the handle. I like to carry it WITH the handle, but like that the handle fold flat so I can slide the whole thing in another bag if I want. THE SIDE POCKET has a seperate zipper and is big enough to easily carry the charger/adapter, and a mouse if you use one.Bottom Line: Provides good protection from scratches, dirt, spilt liquids, minor bumps..etc. Is not going to do much if you drop it onto the concrete. If you need HEAVY protection, buy a full size bag…BUT for me, I do NOT like to carry a huge laptop bag - I mean I got the HP Stream becasue it was thin, light and handy - why would I undo all that by lugging it around in a full size bag…FOR ME this neoprene case WITH handle is exactly what I was looking for.Delivery was timely, and precisely uneventful!! which is a GOOD thing!

No Shock Absorbing Interior

I really like this sleeve, I picked this one among many other sleeves because of the following features:- A handle- An external zippered pocket- Shock absorbing interior (a real plus)The sleeve fits my Acer 11.6 inch perfectly, and I can store the wireless mouse and power cord in the zippered pocket; the handle is useful although I will probably carry it in a different way so the weight of its content won’t put too much stress on the stiches and make it tear off the sleeve. However, there is NO shock absorbing interior in this sleeve, the interior looks just like the exterior, same material, which doesn’t offer much protection to the laptop. I was really disappointed when I found that out, because that feature would had made this sleeve just perfect, I even emailed the seller asking if I have gotten the wrong item, but no, the description just doesn’t match the item itself, and for that reason I don’t give it a 5 star. I considered returning the sleeve for a refund but I figured I just would keep it since I still like the other features, just don’t like the false advertisement.I have not owned this sleeve long enough to be able to evaluate it in terms of durability, but hopefully it will last since I won’t be carrying the laptop around (inside the sleeve) too often.

Fits HP Chromebook 14” perfectly

Will update later after it has been used awhile. We also have a iPearl hard cover on the Chromebook and it still fits fine in this carrying sleeve.
