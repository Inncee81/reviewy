Best product ever- crisp linen is the only scent that won’t leave you tasting it long after using.

Love this product- I use it everywhere and on everything! It’s a supper cleaning disinfectant- so selecting the right scent is key; otherwise it will knock you out. Crisp linen scent is the way to go- it’s a nice pleasant smell when applying and isn’t too strong after it drys. I have gotten other scents like clean breeze and nearly choked from the smell- I could taste it in my mouth and it wasn’t pleasant either way. I tend to spray more- to ensure that its soaked and actually disinfects- if you don’t use enough to wet the item it won’t work. Don’t get me wrong this is a great air freshener spray, but I love the added bonus of killing germs- especially on couches, chairs and curtains- to disinfect these you must spray enough to soak the item for at least a minute but 90 seconds is better. Otherwise your just getting the surface and leaving a scent. I cannot stress enough how much scent matters- this is the only scent to get- I’ve used others and know when someone used it- you can still taste it when you walk in the room 5 minutes later- this scent isn’t as overwhelming as the others but it still gets the job done and smells good.
