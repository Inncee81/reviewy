Product Reviews

Good if you brush too hard

Used these for a couple weeks now and they seem good. I’m often told by dentists that I brush too hard, and these brush heads are very gentle. They seem to clean well but I’ll update after more time (that’s why I gave 4⁄5 stars). I was concerned about the size because I prefer compact. These are larger than Sonicare compact but smaller than standard brush heads (photo left to right is Sonicare Sensitive, Gum Health, and Compact). I will buy the Gum Health brush heads again.

Better cleaning than the regular brush heads.

I have a lower-end Sonicare and a higher-end Sonicare. The lower-end Sonicare came with the regular (Simply Clean?) stock brush heads, and the higher-end one came with these, which is how I discovered them. These are so much better than the stock and the sensitive brush heads if you’re looking for more scrubbing. Who knows what these are doing to the enamel or my gumline, but my teeth feel cleaner.If all you need is a basic Sonicare and don’t use the fancy settings on the handle (sensitive, clean and white, etc…), get the cheapest handle, then upgrade the brush refills that are mostly interchangeable.

These Brush Heads are Very Effective in Improving Gum Health

These work wonderfully. I purchased a Sonicare toothbrush this summer with a recommendation and coupon from my periodontist. I had some seriously deep pockets in my gums and my periodontist told me I’d have to do my ‘homework’ in addition to getting root planing and scaling. After undergoing all that work I wanted to do everything I could to maintain a clean mouth and help improve the condition of my gums. I now brush as well as rinse with mouthwash three times a day and floss twice.When I went back in for my last check up my pocket numbers had drastically improved. I’m convinced that the Philips Sonicare toothbrush with these Pro Results Gum Health brush heads helped immensely.I will definitely continue to purchase and use these.

Highly recommend to anyone thinking about switching to Sonicare!

I recently started using these in place of the Plaque Control bush heads. I LOVE them. They’re much softer than I had expected & have an overall different feel when I’m brushing my teeth. As always, the sonicare brushes leave my teeth feeling as clean as they do when I leave the dentist after a pro cleaning. I recommend Philips Sonicare to anyone looking to take better care of their teeth.I started using Sonicare electric toothbrush about a year ago now & at my 6 month check up with my dental hygienist she was able to see a noticeable difference. I have a permanent retainer on the back side of my front bottom teeth. It’s very hard to clean around & I was experiencing a lot of plaque build up before I switched to Sonicare. Before Sonicare I was using the disposable Oral B electric toothbrushes which is usually around $20 for 2. So I feel like the trade off was a well decision. I don’t feel like I’m spending much more money than I usually would & my teeth feel 10x cleaner than they ever did with Oral B.

My favorite kind of toothbrush head
