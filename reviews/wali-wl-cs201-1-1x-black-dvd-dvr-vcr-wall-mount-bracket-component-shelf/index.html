Product Reviews

Surprisingly helpful instructions, easy to install

The instruction manual is actually helpful! Step 1 clearly says to use 1⁄8” drill bit to make long enough hole. I wish every set of instructions specified the size of the drill bit. It’s so nice not have to guess at the instructions. The 3 shelves can actually be installed as 2 shelf and 1 shelf if needed, and it’s pretty clear to do so without the instructions. Once all the screws are tightened, the shelves are snug, no wobble. I haven’t put anything too heavy, just cable box, modem, router, and a laptop, across all three shelves.Only thing the instructions could improve on are the translations (sometimes tells to tighten four screws when there are three shelves), but that is minor. It seems the front page was carefully edited since it deals with putting holes in the wall, while the back page has more mistakes, since it is all with adjustments and cosmetic parts.

Straight Forward and Good Value

It is EXACTLY what it says it is in the description. It’s easy to hang and assemble. It does everything it is supposed to and nothing that it doesn’t. Arrived in good order, instructions were clear, packaging simple. Done. Wife loves it.

I kind of hate it

So I needed something to hang my Samsung 4K player, Xbox One and my Denon home theater. When I got it, it looked a lot smaller than what I anticipated.I installed it, which was fairly easy and once I started putting everything on I realized it’s just not what I needed. First, the home theater is a little wider but WALI is not going to hold it. The glass shelves started to tilt to the side, and I thought maybe I needed to screw it tighter and it was as tight as it can be.So I took it off, and went to Samsung 4K player which is also wide but not as wide as the Denon and most definitely not as heavy. I plugged in the my Monster Black Platinum HDMI and it pushes it back from the wall. The HDMI is a lot thicker and won’t go into the slots dedicated for HDMI and other wires.This isn’t made for higher quality items, it’s made for much smaller DVD players, and other small electronics. In my opinion, not worth $29.99.I now have to look for a replacement, something much wider and hold heavier electronics.
