Zircon MultiScanner i520-FFP Center Finding Stud Finder with Metal and Live AC Wire Detection

Product Specifications

  • Locates the edges and center of wood or metal studs up to 1 1⁄2-inches (38 mm) deep
  • Scans for metal and live AC wires
  • Easy-to-read LCD display, audio tone, and the patented spotlite pointing system indicate stud center

  • Wirewarning detection indicates the presence of live, unshielded electrical wiring up to 2” deep

    Product Description

Product Packaging:Frustration-Free Packaging The multiscanner I520 is one of the most advanced wall scanners and stud finders that Zircon makes. It has 4 scanning modes: 1. Stud scan mode locates the edges and center of wood or metal studs in normal walls up to 3⁄4 inch (19 mm) deep 2. Deepscan mode doubles the scanning depth for walls up to 1 1⁄2 inches (38 mm) deep, and enables scanning for joists in some ceilings and floors 3. Metal scan mode detects non-ferrous metal, such as copper pipes, up to 1 1⁄2 inches (38 mm) deep, and ferrous metal, such as rebar, up to 3 inches (76 mm) deep 4. AC scan mode finds and traces live, unshielded electrical wiring up to two inches deep in addition to the scanning modes, the I520 is packed with other original and patented features including: 1. Wirewarning detection which indicates the presence of live, unshielded electrical wire up to 2 inches (51 mm) deep in stud and metal scanning modes. Most wires in homes are run along wall studs, causing potential safety hazards. 2. Act (auto correcting technology) automatically corrects common user errors, such as when scanning begins over a stud. The tool constantly scans its surroundings and recalibrates itself if necessary. 3. An ultra-bright LCD screen indicates direction, edges and center of studs. Makes the screen easier to see in low-light the display screen, spotlite pointing system, and audio tone all indicate the location of the target. 4. A signal strength indicator lets you distinguish between Shallow and deep targets. Weak signal strength can also imply other non-stud objects, like wires and pipes. The I520 is a perfect choice for serious users, such as home improvement hobbyists and professional contractors.

Does what it says

This i520 works as advertised in my experience. Accurately detects studs in normal walls with ease. It does not do so well on some of my walls which are made of plaster and lathing. I doubt much of anything would do extremely well on those type of walls.One aspect of performance that I was looking for and did not find about this i520 was how it does on the electrical and metal detection. It seems it works as advertised in these areas but I was hoping for a little more depth when detecting electrical. I think the instructions say it can detect up to 1.5 inches in depth and it will definitely not give you any more than that. I guess that is typically a safe depth when driving nails and screws but don’t expect to be able to trace your wires throughout your walls. It did come it handy for checking if exposed wires were live or not without pulling out the multimeter.Overall, I’m glad I put up the extra cost for this model.
