    Product Description

Take your training to the next level with Electric Athletics Lightning Balls! These incredibly durable training baseballs are packed with features to improve your game. They collapse on impact to provide players with a feel for where contact is made, but will never dent or crack. Their indestructible long-lasting design makes them a perfect choice for intense, team use and strong, powerful batters. The limited-flight design makes Lightning Balls a space-friendly choice, and means you can spend more time practicing and less time retrieving balls. Available in a 12-pack of easy-to-see bright white or neon green balls. Electric Athletics Lightning Balls can be used with metal or wooden bats and are great for baseball players of all ages.
