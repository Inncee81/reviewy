Inexpensive fan that works!

I use this fan to cool just me, not a small room or anything else. Just me. It works well, has 2 speeds and I never have to go further than the first speed. I can use the stand and place it on a bookcase near my chair when I watch TV. It also has a clip that is pretty sturdy and I like the fact that the clip is build into the fan chassis itself. It clips onto the base, so I don’t have to go looking for the base when I want to use it. My only complaint is that the base is made from Very light weight plastic, so the fan itself is top heavy. As a result, I have to hold onto the fan blades’ cage to turn the fan on and off, so it won’t tip over. I have several other Holmes products (fans and heaters) and they all have worked well. The fan costs very little, so I don’t consider having to hold on to the fan any big deal. If you buy it knowing these things, I think you will like it.
