DON’T WASTE YOUR MONEY

I wish you could provide a ZERO star rating, just know that’s how I would rate this.The shelf itself is fine, but the attachment hardware is horrible. M6 lag screw with a hex head and a philips. For some unknown reason, the hole you have to insert the fastener through isn’t large enough to put a socket through. So, the only way to attach this is with a screwdriver. Well, it’s nearly impossible to drive a lag screw 2” into a stud with just a screw driver, even with the proper pre-drilled hole. Before I attempted to install the bracket, I pre-drilled, then drove the lag screw with my socket in hopes of making it easier to drive. I should have stopped right there, because at that point the hex head had already started to deform (yes I was using the proper 10mm socket). I attempted the install, started the top screw, then started the bottom, and I drove them as far as I could until the socket reached the outer hole. From there I attempted by hand, again as far as I could. I eventually had to resort to a drill driver on the lowest torque setting I could get to still drive the lag screw. Well… prior to getting the lag screw tight, the philips head stripped out. This happened on both bolts, leaving me with a shelf support that isn’t secure to the wall and no real way to remove the bolts now.I attempted at first to drill out the larger hole, but even with a step bit, you don’t have enough depth to work the bit through to open up the hole. I then attempted opening up by using a smaller bit and working it around the hole. I gave up on that, and went to the hardware store to purchase an extractor bit (which cost me as much as this shelf). That worked like a CHARM, and I got this piece of crap off my wall.Time to order something different, please don’t waste your money on this. Even if you started with good fasteners, you have no way to drive them tight. The outer hole isn’t large enough to get a socket through, and you can’t buy wood screws large enough to properly secure this. Other seem to install just fine, but the hole diameter is 1⁄16” smaller than the outer diameter of a 10mm socket.
