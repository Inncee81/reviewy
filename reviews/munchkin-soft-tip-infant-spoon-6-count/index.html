Product Reviews

Great spoon, but I wish it was more shallow.

I really like these spoons. They’re a good width for my baby’s mouth, they’ve held up well in the dish washer, and I do like the bright colors as well. My little girl is starting solids (6 months), and doesn’t feed herself yet, but she will grab onto these and put the business end into her mouth. I do wish they were slightly more shallow, because she can’t really rake the food off of the whole spoon with her lips. We have some other spoons I like slightly better to use at this point for that reason. When she is a little bigger, I think I will like these better. I will still use them some now when the others are in the wash, but they will be a second choice at this point :)

SUPER PRICE & QUALITY SPOONS!

These spoons have a soft tip that will prevent any damage to my grandson’s baby teeth, which is nice because sometimes he moves his head too quickly when he gets excited taking a bite. The handles are a little thicker than normal to be easily gripped (for him and for me). Five star rating! I am very pleaseed with these spoons and the cost!

Good enough

I’ve had a couple of these melt slightly in the dishwasher and ended up tossing them. I also wish the spoons were ever so slightly shallower, but that just a personal preference. The color supposedly changes if the temperature of the food is too hot, but I’ve never used that feature and it’s probably just a gimmicky thing to market Other than that, the function and price are good enough for me to have purchased this item twice.

Perfect Size for Baby and Easy to Use

I love these spoons and actually ordered two packs of them. Others reviews mention the spoons falling out of or knocking over bowls, but it really just depends on the type of container you’re trying to put them in. They’re pretty long, so yeah, they’re going to fall out of or knock over a small, child-sized bowl. The only tiny complaint I do have is that the spoons have gotten slightly misshapen/become malleable from the heat of the dishwasher - though not enough for me to want to get different spoons. My baby girl loves the bright colors, which makes her want to hold the spoon herself.

Comfortable, but stain easily

These are nice spoons that are comfortable for parents and baby. The colors are bright and fun. Four stars because I found that if you are feeding some sort of darker color foods (i.e. carrots, sweet potatoes, etc.) and you don’t wash right away, they stain easily. But the price was right and they are good spoons that are gentle on baby’s mouth and gums. (My daughter is 6 mos old.)
