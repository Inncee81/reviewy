    Product Description

Size:16” Wood stud Fits 42-70” TVs

This mount passed test of 4 times claimed loading weight capacity. This mount fits most of 42-70” TVs sold today. It fits TVs with mounting holes as close as 4”x4” or as wide as 24”x16” (in TV terms - VESA 100x100mm to 600x400mm). Specifically, it fits for VESA 100X100, 200X100, 200X150, 200X200, 300X200, 300X300, 400X200, 400X300, 400X400, 500X300, 600X200, 600X400mm.

Retracts 3” low profile, Extends 16” from the wall. With dual articulating arm design, this mount retracts to 3”(to wall) to save space, and extends up to 16” from the wall.

Tilt, Swivel and Rotate function +5°/-15° Tilt, +/-5° rotate and swivel side to side for comfortable viewing flexibility, provide smooth adjustment for all directions, make it easy to view your TV from multi-angles.

Easy tilting angle adjustment handle This mount is equipped with tool free spring-loaded handle for easy tilting angle adjustment.

Heavy duty steel construction and automatic robot welding technology This mount is constructed with heavy duty steel materials and with durable powder coated finish, and 100% welded by automatic robot, this advanced welding technology makes the mount sturdy and ensures mounted TV stable and secure, supports TV weight up to 100 lbs.

Torpedo level and 6’ high speed HDMI cable It comes with torpedo level and 6’ HDMI cable. HDMI cable supports 3D, Ethernet, 1440P and Audio Return conforming to latest HDMI 1.4 Standard.

Includes all necessary mounting hardware This mount ships with hardware for both wall and TV mounting. Anchor included is only for concrete wall installation NOT for dry wall installation!

Quality, Strength, & Ease of Installation

Well built. Very strong. Super easy to install. Took me all of 10 minutes to install this unit to a wall with a 49” tv. Not joking. The tv I mounted had a 300 x 300 Vesa pattern, so I was able to use x-plate with out the vertical and horizontal cross members. I am sure that saved time, but maybe and extra 15 minutes max. My Vizio M492i-B2 tv came with M6 x 8mm screws, so I used them instead of the ones supplied by Mounting Dream. However, there a multiple sizes and lengths of screws along with spacers to accommodate most Tv’s in its range.I have installed roughly 5 different types of TV mounts from 32” tv to 65” tv and the mounting dream was by far the absolute easiest that I have installed to date. I would buy this again before any other mount and highly recommend it.Note to Mounting Dream: Since this unit is so well constructed of heavy guage steel, it did make holding the unit to the wall to mark the drill holes somewhat of a challenge. I strongly recommend that you include a paper template that can be taped to the wall and used from drilling the mount holes, in the case that the installer does not have a second set of hands to hold the mount securely while marking.

Simple, Durable, Versatile

This device has duel swing arms. This is an advantage for some people and advantage for others. It is strong, durable, and simple. This TV mount would appear quite capable of exceeding by double its stated carrying capacity. Even if it broke all of the repair parts would be readily available at the local hardware store. The depth of travel that the TV can achieve (being pulled away or pushed back to the wall) is the major selling point here. It allows the TV to come out far enough from the wall that a 65 - 70 inch TV can complete a full 45 degree swing each direction if nothing is in its path of travel. Bonus: it comes with a free HDMI Cable.Here are quick setup instructions. If the TV or monitor being mounted fits the base place already equipped, then you are good. Undo the two Allen bolts at the back of the plate, mount to device with included combinations and bolts that fit your device, then mount stand base to studs and re-attach plate & monitor. Being sure to put the Allen bolts back in after hanging the plate. Make sure not to mount plate on TV upside down, this is a gravity based rig. If your TV is too big ( TV mounted in my case a 100lb 65in) then make a square mount using the B & C steel arms. Attach to TV. The mounting plate will bolt to the square that is bolted to the TV. I mounted this TV by myself, but wished for help. At this point the TV is very heavy with so much extra steel and very hard to maneuver onto the swing arms properly. Get help, do it with 1 or 2 other people. Falling TV’s kill more people than sharks, or so i have been told.
