Product Reviews

Great value for hobbyist

The strength of this sanding kit is price - no way about it. These are good enough arbors, wheels and sand drums for hobby use (I wouldn’t recommend for commercial applications or production environments) with a Dremel (I use a Proxxon, b.t.w.). However, these are not Foredom quality so if you’re into professional wood carving you would be well served to investigate further. However, I’m a hobbyist (wooden puzzles, intarsia, compound cutting, light sculpting,etc. ) so i don’t challenge my Proxxon much. For me, these are great. And at ~$7 for the entire set, I’m entirely satisfied.The only weakness is that the sand sleeves tend to slide off the rubber mandrels with use which is a minor hassle. But given the price, that’s to be expected. I mitigate against this by rubbing the rubber with denatured alcohol once in awhile. There is also a micro flap sander drum that I use a lot - also on Amazon. 10 sleeves and mandrel are about $7 or so as I recall. I use these for most of the heavier material removal because they are more sturdy than these are, and then use these for the finishing work because of the convenience of the 3 sizes. These smaller drums are indispensable for finish sanding on fine scroll saw work like fretwork.

Tip - I also learned that it is better to loosen the screw on the sanding tube when …

The first sanding drum I used on plastic to remove a small amount of material and within 20 seconds of sanding the little sanding drum came apart at the seems. I’m guessing either the drum was cheaply made, or the plastic heated up and caused the glue to fail on the sanding drum causing the paper to come apart. It did what I needed it to do but a bit disappointing to see a drum come apart so quickly.Tip - I also learned that it is better to loosen the screw on the sanding tube when you are finished with it and put your tools away. If you leave it tightened it can cause the rubber to stay squished causing it to be difficult later on when you want to change sanding paper.
