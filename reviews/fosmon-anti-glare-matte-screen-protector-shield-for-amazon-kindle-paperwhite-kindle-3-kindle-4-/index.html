Product Reviews

On a smart phone or tablet

Bought these screen protectors after noticing my screen getting a little grimy when I was reading on the bus to and from work. My concern was that wiping the screen on my Kindle Paperwhite would cause small scratches eventually so I bought these to protect it.If you’re familiar with putting on a screen protector on a phone or tablet, these aren’t much different. Included in the package are three screen protectors and a microfiber cloth.When placed on the screen, there is a very small gap around the edges. On a smart phone or tablet, this would have been a bad thing, but with the Kindle’s slightly recessed screen, the small gap allows a fingernail or other non-abrasive object to take the screen protector off, if you decide to do that. Without the gap, these protectors would have been near impossible to remove.The gap isn’t a big deal. Maybe a few millimeters of space on each side. The margins of the text on the kindle don’t reach that far anyway so eventually you become blind to it.Good price and worth the piece of mind.

It works - if you have a Kindle, get it

It works well enough - it keeps scratches off of the screen and the flat finish works well when I’m reading in a well-lit situation and not relying on the backlight. The only problem I had in installing it was that I tried to do it in a somewhat dusty environment - I have furry pets (and I’m not such a great housekeeper anyway). No matter how clean I got the screen and protector, a couple of tiny bits of something would get between them and stick by static electricity. I took in to my office and, with care, got the protector on cleanly - no dust and only a few air bubbles which worked out with the plastic card provided for the purpose. It appears to have no effect on touch screen function and protects it from minor scratches.
