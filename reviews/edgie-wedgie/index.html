    Product Description

The Edgie Wedgie will hold the ski tips together and aid the beginning skier in maintaining a balanced and controlled wedge position. It will help to speed up the learning process while providing a more enjoyable introduction to skiing.

If you’re paying for a ski trip, spend a few extra bucks on these…. Great teaching aid!

I worked at a ski school in Vt for several years and we always kept these in our pockets as an aid. Overall they are great, and fit most types of skis. Sometimes they can leave a mark on skis from the wing nut that tightens them, but anyone with kids will tell you they’d trade that for a kid learning to stop.Now ten years later, with my own kids I bought these to help my kids learn. Both my 7 and 5 year old only used them for half an hour before they could stop on their own, and i’m sure it made the learning process much faster than it otherwise would have been.Couple of tips:Tie a knot in the middle to make it a little shorter and keep the skis a little closer together.And tighten them down as much as possible by hand, and maybe consider the use of small plyers if you don’t have strong hands to keep them from falling off.As the kid is moving, simply tell them to open their legs as far apart as they can, and they will automatically be in a perfect wedge.Can’t recommend these enough if you are teaching a kid!READ MORE

These are easy to take on and off, are interchangeable with other skis and do not fall off when your child is tackling Blues and Greens and falls down. Excellent product that gave my son a lot of confidence on the slopes. At first he didn’t want to put it on because he thought he could manage without it. After we put it on, he was able to make it down from top to bottom, including on some milder intermediate runs, without falling. And he’s now able to turn and avoid people and other obstacles. These also stay in place very well. In contrast, we also had a set of the Lucky Bums ski connectors, and they did not stay on the skis.
