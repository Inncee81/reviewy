    Product Description

Size:Head & Backpack Mount Bundle Three Options for Hands Free GoPro Mounting

The Mount Bundle for Head and Backpack by CamKix offers three different accessories that allow you to shoot hands free(compatible with frame and waterproof housing). Perfect for use during action sports such as cycling, climbing, skiing, snowboarding, surfing, diving, kayaking, go carting, equestrian sports and other (non-impact) activities.

All items are made from quality materials.

The Head Strap Mount will fit comfortably on your head or you can also wear it over a helmet. It features extra grip inside the band to ensure that it stays securely in place.

The Backpack Clip features a strong clip mount that can be rotated 360 degrees for optimal camera positioning. Easily attach your GoPro to your backpack strap, belt and so on. The mount also can be used as a small stand.

The Hat Quick Clip allows you to attach your GoPro to a backwards baseball cap or other 3mm to 10mm thick object like a belt. The clip can also be added to the head strap for mounting an extra GoPro.
