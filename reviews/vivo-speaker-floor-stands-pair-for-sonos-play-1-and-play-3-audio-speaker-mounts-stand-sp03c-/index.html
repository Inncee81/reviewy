    Product Description

VIVO premium speaker floor stand (two stands) for SONOS PLAY 1 and PLAY 3 audio speakers. Designed to elevate your speakers and enhance audio sound, STAND-SP03C’s sleek appearance is perfect for any home theater space. A heavy duty steel base with bottom protective padding supports each stand to ensure safety and stability. The center pole features internal cable management to keep power and audio cords clean and organized. We include three interchangeable mounting brackets that allow you to mount your SONOS speakers in vertical or horizontal position. Total height from the floor to the top shelf measures 39”. The base measures 15.75” x 11.75”. These stands come with necessary hardware for assembly and mounting.
