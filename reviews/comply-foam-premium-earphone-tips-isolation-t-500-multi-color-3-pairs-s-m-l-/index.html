The Tips Make Everything Better

I have these tips for so many of my earphones. They work great and will last you a while if you take care of them.They really help with fit and helping sound signatures to come through loud and clear.Also, the multi-color ones are great for earphones that have small left/right markings. WOW. Best thing to happen to headphones since we stopped using jagged rocks as earpieces!

WOW. These are literally the best thing to happen to headphones since we stopped using jagged rocks as cushioning. Hyperbole? Maybe. However, these have completely transformed my listening experience. I’m using them with the Jaybird BlueBuds X. The silicone tips that came with the headphones kept falling off, often inside my ear canal. The isolation and bass response were not good, and they would become uncomfortable after about 30 minutes. Then I switched to the weird “sound-isolating” stepped tips. Those were even less comfortable and sounded even worse. In a final act of desperation I ordered the Comply Premium Replacement Foam Earphone Earbud Tips - Isolation T-500 (Multi-Color, 3-Pairs, Large). My first impression was that they were quite ugly. My second impression was that these were in no danger of ever coming off of my headphones, considering how much force I had to use to get them on. Then I stuck them in my ears…..Pure bliss. It’s like having fluffy clouds in your ears. The noise isolation is superb; I can’t hear ANYTHING but the music now. And, my headphones have BASS. I’m not actually a big fan of bass, especially in headphones, but they were seriously lacking before, and now I might have to get an equalizer app to tone it down a bit. All in all these would have been a bargain at twice the price; without them I would have basically stopped using my headphones, and now I’m going to use them all the time!

Comfortable, but not sure the price is worth the longevity…

I was a bit worried purchasing these after reading reviews on their longevity, and it appears that people weren’t far off on saying they were not very tough. I ordered my set on 12 Feb and received them shortly after ordering. I use my earbuds at least 4 times a week for an hour+ each use and today I experienced my first failure.Overall I was very pleased with the comfort of these tips. They fit solid and were initially very firm. Over the past two months the memory foam gave out and especially in the past two weeks they are less memory foam and more really soft comfortable tips! Today (5/4/16) I got my last wear out of the first pair I’ve put on my ear buds where one of the red tips I’m using has split and starting falling apart. I didn’t realize the tear at first, but I could tell that my right bud kept slowly falling out of position as I walked.To be honest I’m not sure how fair my rating of three stars is; but I feel that I should have gotten more than two and a half months out of them. The majority of wear has been during weight lifting and I have not been extremely sweaty during/after my workouts. I don’t run either, so walking or doing the elliptical has been at a pace where I had a raised heart rate, but not sweating much. I’d imagine if I were a runner and constantly sweaty that these probably would have got a month’s worth of wear.To conclude, you do get three “pairs” of tips; however, from my experience you’ll probably get a little over 8 months of life from all three pairs. If you’re running more/sweating more than you may want to consider a different type of tip like silicone. I may be switching myself after these have all failed.
