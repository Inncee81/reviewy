Great Bracket and Simple Portability

Purchased item for Samsung 32” LED TV that I plan to move between indoor workout room and outdoor patio. Mounting instructions and hardware very easy to follow and packaging contained all the right hardware. I like the fact that the pivot arm remains on the TV while only the mounting bracket remains on the wall. Quick and easy setup with excellent portability! I initially thought the pivot joint required more lubrication so I added some silicone. I advise you to wait until you hang your TV, as you want to maintain the friction to support the weight of your TV. Fortunately, the pivot joint allows for additional adjustments to restrict or loosen the range of motion. I would highly recommend this bracket!
