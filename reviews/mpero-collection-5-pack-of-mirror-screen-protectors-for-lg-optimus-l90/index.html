Product Reviews

Better than decent, Great for the price.

There are not too many cases and screen protectors for our LG Optimus 90L phone. These screen protectors were quite inexpensive and we got 5. We have tempered glass on our iPad minis and that is unbeatable for clarity and feel. These are plastic and have sort of a rainbowish look when viewed from an angle. Clarity is pretty good. Contrast is better than expected for something this cheap. Plastic screen protectors usually mean a noticeable loss in touch sensitivity. With fingertips, it is at least as good as the bare screen. The slight texture may be helping. There is slightly more drag with a stylus.They go on easy. I put 4 on without incident. There were some slight marks on some on the bottom about 1⁄8 inch from the edge. No biggie considering the cost.

Good enough…

I got these to protect a two-pack of phones in the family. One or two of these shipped with a weird swirl near the bottom, where the pull-tabs attach. It hasn’t affected my use but it does show up on the phone. If I wanted to, I could switch it out for one of the others in the pack, but it really hasn’t bothered me that much. They do hold up pretty well and stay on nicely. I’d get them again but with the quantity I got, they’ll outlive the phones. Be sure you want the mirror finish; it’s not anti-glare (it’s more like maxi-glare) but it DOES keep the screen from washing out, that’s the entire point.
