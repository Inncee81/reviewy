Product Reviews

So far, I am very happy with this SD carrying case

I am very happy with this memory card holder. It holds 12 SD card securely, and has a sturdy latch to keep it closed. Since it is a hard case, it provides plenty of protection for my SD cards. I also like that the inside form that holds the cards is not a squishy foam. It is more solid, and holds. the SD cards securely. I try to be critical in my reviews, but so far, I can’t think of anything negative on SD case.

Loose SD cards

Use for keeping SD card together for Trail camera changes. Case seems very nice & the snap closure keeps case closed with no problems. The SD cards themselves are not held very secure and tend to come out of their assigned placement and are quite often found loose when opening the case after a good hike. Ratting would be 5 stars if case held the SD cards in place better.

Beware: Carabiner NOT included (even though description states otherwise)

I like the case. The issue I have is that the carabiner was not included when the description of the product clearly states:“Integrated strap loop allowing the case to be looped onto most camera straps for safe keeping or attached to camera bags/equipment bags etc ( carabiner is included).“Would have given a 5-star review if all the product came as described.

An outstanding case with good construction

A WONDERFUL CASE! This is extremely well constructed with very good seals around the perimeter of the case. The cards fit snugly inside the inserts but not so snugly to make it difficult to get out. The waterproofing is outstanding. The first thing I did was submerge this case since that was my primary reason for purchasing it in the first place. NO LEAKAGE AT ALL! The external clip to seal the case is also sturdy and easy to operate fitting snugly over the rim to seal the case up tight. I would highly recommend this product for anyone looking for a case to weather/water-proof their SDHC/SDXC memory cards.
