Product Reviews

Good value for price

This was a gift for a young family member. It is easy to use, and takes very good photos. My only minor complaint is that it’s tricky to learn how to use only a slight pressure on the shutter button to depress it halfway in order to focus before depressing all the way to release the shutter. It’s mostly a learning problem, but the key is for the user to wait for the on-screen focusing aid to change to green before depressing the shutter all the way down.

So much better than the old camera

My old camera was wearing out, not taking clear picture, so I got this Nikon to replace it. Cost, battery type and reviewers’ comments were big factors in my choice. I am thrilled with this little dynamo! Long battery life and sharp pictures. I create textile art, and this camera shows every stitch in crisp detail. Highly recommended.
