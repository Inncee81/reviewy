    Product Description

Color:Graphite Gray Zipper, a small fan with big attitude! the Vornado zippy personal fan features a powerful motor driving soft nylon blades that quickly and quietly propel the air and are safe to touch while the fan is in motion. That feels so much better

I got this fan for work. I love it, and so does everyone I work with. I work in a garden center, so I put it up at one of the front registers, and whenever co-workers come in from the heat, they like to spend a little quality time with the fan. It’s really quiet, and makes working in that environment a lot more tolerable. When I end up at that register, the fan is probably 2-3 feet away, so it doesn’t feel as strong as if I was sitting right next to it at a desk, but it definitely makes it feel a few degrees cooler up there.It’s also cool that the nylon blades can’t injure anyone. Definitely a good thing, since EVERYONE sticks their fingers in it once they realize that.

Great Fan and Cats LOVE It!

I read so many good yet BAD reviews I was honestly afraid to try this. Turns out this has quickly become my favorite of all the fans I have. The reason is simple. Even on a hot day of 90 degrees, if I place this next to me at my recliner, I can usually skip turning on my large Vornado fans entirely. And this is on the LOW setting. The fan at that speed is virtually silent and moves all the air I want or need. On high, this little fan move far more air than I expected given some reviews. The fact it also has a mail back 3 year warranty, I feel I got a LOT for the money. If you need a fan for personal use and in a small space I recommend this 100%. Given I use it in a large living room, next to my chair and have such amazing results, I actually recommend this for anywhere as long as it is within 4 feet of you or so. Remember it will NOT cool any room or large space but will cool you if close by. My cats (Lucy & Ethel give it 10 stars each.
