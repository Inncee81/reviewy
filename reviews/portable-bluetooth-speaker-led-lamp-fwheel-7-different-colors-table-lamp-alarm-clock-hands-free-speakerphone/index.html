Perfect nightlight, less than perfect alarm clock.

I bought these for my girls’ bedrooms, for nightlight and alarm. As a nightlight, they are perfect. Love the adjustable brightness, because it means they can use it as a reading light as well. HOWEVER, it leaves a little to be desired in functionality, especially for kids (mine are 10 and 13). First, unless I’m missing something, there’s no way to switch from military/Euro time to the 12 hour system we tend to use here in the US. Second, changing alarm time is NOT intuitive, at all, so I keep having to go in their rooms and do it myself. Third, I don’t see a way to adjust the volume for the alarm. It makes a lovely sound, but my daughters sleep through it a good 30% of the time. We’re gonna keep working on it, but those are things I would definitely change if I could.

Didn’t work the way I thought it would

I wanted to love this, but really didn’t work for what I needed it to. I thought I could pick a color for my daughter to fall asleep by but it is always changing and the color setting is kind of bright. The clock is always in military time which is fine but its for our daughters room so I would prefer a regular clock. I did pair it with the bluetooth to try to change the settings but it didn’t change the clock. The bluetooth paired easily, but it auto plays anytime you are near it, even if my phone was not playing music, it would just turn it on. so again, no useful for it to start playing when my child is asleep in the next room and it suddenly sensed my phone. I also did not like that it didn’t have a normal plug. The battery only lasts about 24 hours, so you have to constantly charge it if you use it often, but it only comes with the USB cord not an outlet plug. For my own use, it probably would have been fine, but definitely not perfect. The instructions are very vague.
