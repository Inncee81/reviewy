    Product Description

Color:Black Gardman R350 Rose Arch. This arch provides an attractive 6’3” archway for climbing plants to create a beautiful entry into your garden. Gently curving structure forms a perfect arch at the top. Horizontal bars connect vertical sides to each other producing a sturdy structure. Horizontal bars in the top portion serve as an ideal place to hang lightweight, potted plants. This arch is perfect for supporting lightweight climbing plants and vines or for displaying small hanging accents. Train your plants to grow through the arbor by weaving them in and out of the structure as they grow. Sturdy tubular steel frame construction with durable powder-coated black polyester epoxy coating. Black coloration will blend with any surrounding. Easy to assemble with complete instructions included. Dimensions: 1’ 7” Long x 3’ 3” Wide x 6’ 3” High. Gardman “Bring your garden to life”

As advertised. Works well with light fencing and runner beans in a garden.

I bought this for my rear garden so red runner green beans could climb. With a little care to pound guide holes in the ground then get a level to even things out, I had no problem with this. I covered it with a little roll of light american-like fence held on by twist ties. It all worked well. The biggest issue is the sturdiness of the individual metal tubes but only a little care is needed for this and the thinness of their walls keeps the weight down. Put into the ground carefully with a rubber mallet, this resisted even 50+ MPH wind storms with ease. When I got it out for the winter and moved it to the front walkway, I did bend one tube slightly as the unit fell partially over while that was still in the ground and that was my fault. The bend was not severe enough to worry about and it resides in front of our house now for winter decorations and hopefully some summer trumpet vines (I hope deer don’t eat these). I’ll be ordering another shortly.
