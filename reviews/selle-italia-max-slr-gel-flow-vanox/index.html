Love this saddle

Saddles are a very personal item, what works perfectly for one, feels terrible to another. This is my second SLR Max Gel Flow saddle and it works for me. I have last year’s model on my road bike and have put many miles on it without any discomfort. I built a gravel bike this fall and put this saddle on it and so far it’s been great. Comparing the two, the new one feels a bit stiffer, however the old one currently has over 1000miles on it, so I expect the new one to break-in over time. build quality is great as well. Selle Italia has a saddle fit calculator on their website which is worth looking at as well. It tries to match you to a saddle from the data you provide.
