    Product Description

The SPF Ultralight Skull Cap gets rid of the brim, hooks you up with some spF 30 protection, and keeps your head cool and dry with X-static performance jersey mesh and power mesh side panels. Designed for a thin, barely there fit so it doesn’t bunch up and distract you from the ride when worn under a helmet. Product Reviews

Wicks sweat and doesn’t feel hot!

Fits great! Wicks sweat, but doesn’t feel hot. Wore it Memorial Day on 47 mile road bike ride. I normally sweat through headbands and have to change them out. Bought one of these and tried it, and it never started dripping sweat down my face. It wicked the sweat all out. Unfortunately, I am a shiny cue ball head–probably God’s joke for the shoulder length hair of my youth! I bought this thing because my head was chafing against the helmet on rides and making friction hot spots and even sores on my head. This protected and worked fantastic! I am a fan!

Good quality under helmet liner

Good quality, comfortable, wicks sweat, fits easily under helmet. The lack of a drawstring or toggle like some under helmet caps makes this fit easier under my helmet. The light color is good for reflecting sunshine. I have a large head and this cap fits snugly - any more and it would be too tight. I imagine the elastic loosens up over time, so the fit is just right.

Great but smells

I really like it, it stops my bald head from getting burnt and mops up sweat. Mine smells a bit. The instructions say hand wash only. I wrote to Giro and asked if I can use hot water or detergent. They never replied. So other than it smelling a little and Giro not offering washing information, it is great.
