Very clever, simple product that works well, if you get a big enough size for your screen weight

This gizmo is fantastic. I never would have believed it would work so well, if I hadn’t seen the positive reviews. The one piece of advice I’d give is don’t “cheap out” and get too small a size if you have a moderate size screen. I got this one for a Garmin with a 6 inch screen, and it really stays in place well, even if I drive in “sport mode” and take country road curves HARD, accelerate hard, etc.It does get a bit stiff in cold weather, which I guess is to be expected, but it becomes flexible as the car warms up, and I don’t notice excessive slipping for normal driving even when it’s stiff.Between all the complaints about the suction cups and so many states having laws against attaching a GPS to the windshield, this seems like a really great concept. Just attach the Garmin screen, and toss it onto the dash. I wiped my dusty dash off a bit, but that’s the only prep I did, and I suspect even that wasn’t necessary, but can’t be sure now.
