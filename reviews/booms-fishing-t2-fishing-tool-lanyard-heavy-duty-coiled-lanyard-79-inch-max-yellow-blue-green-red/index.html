Product Reviews

sturdy, does the job and being stainless steel should …

Strong, sturdy, does the job and being stainless steel should last a long time on my boat. My only issue is the small size of the clips. It’s not easy to tell from the photo’s on the product page that they are so small with an opening barely a 1⁄4 inch wide. They won’t fit through the hole in the handle of my Bite Fishing Tackle pliers so I had to add a small key ring and then clip them to that. It works but not the ideal. Had I known this I would’ve looked for something with a larger opening clip. Still I’m happy with them and don’t feel the need to replace or return them.

Too Short

Good quality, but a little short for using for tools snaps are small for attaching to kayak hull rings. I added screw together rings to be more secure to connect to fishing tools. Snaps are too small to go thru fishing pliers and lip grabber.
