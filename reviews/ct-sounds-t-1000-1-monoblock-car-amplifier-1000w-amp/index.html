Size:T-1000.1D Product description:

CT Sound’s T-1000.1D is one of the best powerful monoblock amplifiers, which gives you perfection into your subwoofers/speakers with no doubt. This T-series amplifier from CT Sounds comes to you with a set of fixed features that helps you to get decided prior to buying. This amplifier cannot be bridged; therefore, giving you fixed option for connecting subwoofers and providing them with high efficient gain on the way it is designed.

 

Along with the T-1000.1D amplifier- you can get mounting kits and screws, external amplification module with knob. The chassis is steel made that helps the amplifier to work as a heat sink and work better at any environment!

 

Features:

1.      Class-D amplifier Technology

2.      T-1000.1D single channel car amplifier ratings-

a.      1 ohm @ 14.4v, 1000W (rms), 2000W (max)

b.      2 ohm @ 14.4v, 700W (rms), 1400W (max)

c.       4 ohm @ 14.4v, 400W (rms), 800W (max)

 

Technical specifications:
