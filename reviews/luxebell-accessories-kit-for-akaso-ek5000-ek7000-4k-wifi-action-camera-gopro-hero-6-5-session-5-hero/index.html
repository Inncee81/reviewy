Great starter kit for my GP Hero 4 Session.

This set proved very useful on our last trip, especially the flotie and monopod during snorkeling, and the head strap when kayaking. I haven’t used the bike handle clamp and suction cup. The screws got rusty easily, but I think it’s because I forgot to rinse them after using in seawater. So DO rinse them after saltwater use. Read the list of actual contents in the details section of the page. Mine did not come with the white plastic case attached to the headstrap in the picture. They explained that that was not included. That’s when I checked the details page. But overall a good set for beginners.

Good storage case…has some minor but important issues, but still works

I wanted a case to put my cameras in and all the extra mounts and gear. This case does the trick for a reasonable price. It seems well made and accomplishes what I wanted, so I gave it the 4 stars/I like it rating. However, there are two things that would make this even better product and give it a full 5 stars.Unfortunately, as others have pointed out, the handle was placed on the hinge side of the case. If you use the handle, all the items you placed in the net area will fall out of it. Simply moving the handle to the other side of the case would insure the contents of the net area stay in the net. I simply cut the handle off so I wouldn’t make that mistake. The case is small enough to be carried without using the handle at all.This leads to my suggestion that you don’t bother with the extra manufacturing steps for a handle at all and instead put a positive closure like a zipper or Velcro closures on the net area which makes that more secure and usable. I’m going to try to do that for myself down the line.Until then, I will carry it zippered side up and store it in the same fashion.Overall, it’s a nicely made unit for holding all your equipment in one place…I have 2 cameras and associated gear with plenty of room in it. It’s not a case that will take a tremendous amount of abuse if you’re going off trekking somewhere, but it’s compact enough to fit in a bag or backpack to keep all your video gear together so you can find what you need when you want it. I may have rated it a little higher than it deserves, but it seems to be well made and does what I wanted it to at a reasonable price.
