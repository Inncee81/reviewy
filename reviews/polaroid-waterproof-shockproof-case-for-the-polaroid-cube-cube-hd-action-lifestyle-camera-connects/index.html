Works perfectly

Works perfectly for the cube, and is so easy to use. Used this for some snorkeling, scuba and pool time in Mexico, and not a drop of moisture got in!The very well polished screen scratches easily, so you may wish to get a piece of screen protector for it, or keep it in a soft carrier.

Functional. Mostly.

This case has a bit of trouble on two fronts. The first is that the OEM cube mounts seem to lack an adjustable base, you know, so you can change the direction it’s pointing. The second is that the button mechanism is a pain in the butt. I struggled for quite some time to get this thing to work properly, and I still ended up losing a fair amount of underwater reef footage because the waterproof case button didn’t work so well. On the upside, it didn’t leak and what video I did get was fantastic.

It is %100 water proof and the picture is super clear. I used it while snorkeling in Mexico and the image looks exactly how I saw it. The sound isn’t as clear but that’s expected. I only wish there was a monopod that could be attached to it since the case alone is super awkward to hold.

Excellent waterproof case

Used this in Hawaii while snorkeling in Hanauma Bay and the case itself worked great! Made of a hard plastic, I had no leaks or any problems at all while I used it in the water. Deepest I had the camera down was probably only about three or four feet, so I can’t speak as to how deep you can take this before water seeping in would become a problem. I did not have a snorkel equipped to hook the case onto, so I had to hold it in my hand, which somewhat worried me since a rogue wave or something easily could have knocked this out of my hand and then bye bye camera! But it worked out well and I had easy access to the Record/Stop button. Now if I would only have taken some quality photos or video, but that’s on me, not the equipment. I’ll try and upload what I did take later (I’m doing this review on a computer that I don’t have access to what I took). But the case itself is a good deal and does work well.
