Used for GoPro Hero Black 4 to mount to backstops for softball games

Bought this for Hero 4 Black to mount to backstop so I can record softball games. Everything included will allow you to mount your GoPro to this jaws clamp. I can’t tell the difference from the branded GoPro jaw clamp. This has all the same features: strong jaw clamp, internal adjustable rubber strap inside of jaws for increasing grip strength on unusual objects (like metal backstop fencing), and stable flexible gooseneck. Product is great and you save a little money over the GP brand. My only gripe is that I have to use a small bungee cord to secure the jaw clamp from not moving around in the wind.I am using this setup just to keep GoPro clutter down to a min. I was trying to avoid buying a lynxspider ([…]). But, I will probably just buy a lynxspider in the near future as an alternative.Good product for non GoPro, quick shipping.
