Five stars with some reservations

With every new mobile gadget, there comes the need for a new screen protector. I carry my phone in a pocket full of coins and keys and I can not imagine using it without a screen protector. Since there are plenty of different brands but not a single brand makes screen protectors for all new devices, every time I buy these protectors it is from a different company. But almost all are the same with very similar package contents, installation guides and the level of protection they offer. This one is not much different either.First of all it works okay and is easy to install. Therefore I rate it five stars, but after in depth analysis and comparing it with the previous protectors I had, I have to say that I have some reservations on this rating:Pros:-Fits well (not covers the entire screen but covers 99.9% which is a good thing since the protectors that aim to cover everywhere are very difficult to fit and the edges are real problem.) This one got on in a simple one time try.-Bubbles are not nightmare. Again, some models get bubbles in and are almost impossible to remove. This is not one of them. With the little tool they send it took me less than 30 seconds to remove every single bubble.-Anti glare is working fine. Most anti-glare claims are not translating to reality in the world of screen protectors but this is really working okay.-It is not a finger print magnet. The texture is not the smoothest and I guess that is why it does not show fingerprints on. A real plus on my side.-Relatively okay value for money. Not the cheapest but not the most expensive either. I think it is OK priced.Cons:-Lacks an instruction manual. Actually there is an instruction manual but it is not about how to install but about how to write a review on amazon. Installation is left to a website link (which is okay but still not very handy)-The screen cleaner, they sent is like a joke. Before installation, you need to remove dust and grease and make your screen look like just out of factory. There you need a microfiber cloth which is normally provided in the package. This one has something like that too but the least quality disposable one and it did not help me clean anything. I had to use one of the existing lens cleaner clothes I had.-It is a little on the matte side in terms of texture and clarity. I have seen crystal clear ones which you don’t notice at all, this is not one of them. I don’t want to be bold on this claim but there may be a very slight decrease in the image quality and may be some grainy appearance added but again it is very minimal and besides that it probably why it does not get fingerprint smears as much as the other one so both a pro and a cons.-The additional dust remover sticker is too strong and should be used as the last option since it may leave some marks behind on the screen which can be more troublesome to remove. A microfiber cloth in a stable indoor environment (not much air circulation) is all you need for the dusts.In conclusion, I liked the fact that it worked flawlessly and was not a pain to get it working. I still have two more in case the one I put gets scratched over time. There were times I had to send back screen protectors, compared to those experiences this is a smooth one and therefore I rate them with 5 stars. But as I said I have some reservations and I hope the manufacturers are reading this review and take it as constructive feedback towards better products in the future

A Superior Product

Absolutely the best screen protector system I have ever used. I have always had problems with dust under the screen protector in the past, and just did the best I could and learned to live with it (which always drove me nuts). Mr Shield gives you the ability to remove any dust trapped during installation. Love that! Comes with an anti-static screen wipe, dust collectors (to help clean screen before installing), removing tapes (used to lift the edge of the screen protector and to slide under the protector to remove dust), and a smoothing card (to remove bubbles).The matte finish cuts down on screen glare and protector does not interfere with responsiveness of the touch screen, nor the brightness of the screen. It is supposed to be scratch resistant, but only time will tell about that. Since you get 3 in the package, these should last a good long time. And nobody can complain about a lifetime replacement warranty!! If I had to nit-pick about anything, it’s that installation instructions are not in the package, but online. The instructions are a little video that explains everything you need to do to have a flawless installation. While this is a minor inconvenience, the benefit is step by step instruction on getting it right the first time. With a large selection for almost any device, I’ll never use anything else!
