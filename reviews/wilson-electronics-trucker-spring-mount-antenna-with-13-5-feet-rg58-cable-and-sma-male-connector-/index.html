    Product Description

The RV Trucker Spring-Mount Antenna with 13.5ft RG58 Cable & SMA Male Connector from Wilson Electronics is the most powerful high-gain cellular antenna Wilson makes. It accommodates 4G frequencies and is dual band, operating on 800MHz and 1,900MHz. The Antenna gain is 5.12dBi at 800MHz and 6.12dbi at 1,900MHz. This kit is designed specifically for use with RVs, trailers, campers and other nonmetallic-bodied vehicles. The kit includes the spring-mount antenna, 13.5ft RG58 cable and SMA connector and a 3-way so the antenna can easily attach to a vertical or horizontal pole.
