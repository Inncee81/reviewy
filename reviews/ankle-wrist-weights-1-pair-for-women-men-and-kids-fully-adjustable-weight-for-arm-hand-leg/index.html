These are great for use during work outs but also just wearing …

These are great for use during work outs but also just wearing at home or hiking. It is only 6 pounds of extra weight but it feels like 20 after a couple of hours! I use them specifically to target an ankle injury and the extra weight actually helps balance and support. What I don’t like about the design of this ankle weight and most other ankle weights is, the actual cushioned weight part goes only partially around even a small ankle. Which leaves a good 3 or 4 inches of your ankle exposed to the constant scraping of the velcro strap. Ouch. There are other companies that make ankle weights that are about 4 inches longer that do not do this, but these are overall okay for the price.
