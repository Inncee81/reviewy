Product Specifications

  • 32 Inch Screen Size: A 32-inch class (31.5” measured diagonally) 16:9 monitor offers more than a 70% larger screen area vs. a 24-inch class monitor*. That’s plenty of room for work on spreadsheets, editing documents, watching videos, viewing photos and more.
  • Input Versatility No matter what connections the job requires, it likely has it covered, for supreme workplace versatility. Display Port, HDMI, D-Sub, USB 2.0 (2), and USB 2.0 Up are all supported.
  • On-Screen Control Optimize your personal viewing preferences for a variety of tasks with convenient On-Screen Control. Within a handy window, quickly and easily change preset display options and adjust an array of picture settings including brightness and color levels
  • Screen Split 2.0 The applications you need most -spreadsheets, email, documents, and web browsing–can all be viewable in separate windows at the same time for more efficient multitasking. Up to four different windows can be displayed at once, with 14 possible configurations including four different picture-in-picture choices.

  • Wall Mount This VESA-compatible monitor gives you the freedom to take it off the desk and hang it on the wall. Liberate your viewing options and save desktop space with this wall-mountable LG monitor. (Mounting hardware not included.) Reader Mode The monitor’s Reader Mode is designed to help alleviate eye fatigue by reducing blue light. It’s the ideal companion for those long stretches in front of the monitor.
