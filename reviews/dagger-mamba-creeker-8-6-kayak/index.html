    Product Description

The Dagger Mamba Creeker 8. 6 Kayak will take you from rolling in the high school pool to charging through endless drops on some of the biggest class V rapids. This larger-sized kayak is designed with bigger paddlers in mind, but maintains all the performance of its more moderately-sized cousins. Built with the same profile and design elements as its fraternal twin, the Dagger Mamba 8. 6, the Creeker only differs in the outfitting. The change in set-up throws in a bit more structural integrity if you’re considering high-risk rapids, all while saving weight.Linear polyethylene is roto-molded to give you a planing hull with sharp edge transitions and a rockered, peak-deck bow. Learning won’t leave lasting scars and hunting rapids will leave you with a huge grin on your face. Room in the rear lets you stow gear if you’re planning on being river-bound for several days. Added volume in the cockpit puts your legs in front of you, making your assertive paddling efficient and effective. Charging hard across vicious chops towards roaring falls is sweet and easy with this Creeker.Both the Creeker Mamba and the Mamba feature Contour Ergo Outfitting, including the reinforced bulkhead footbrace system. However, the Creeker’s seat and step-out pillar are what distinguish it. Due to roto-molded, tank-style construction, the Creeker seat is stiffer and more durable than the seat found in the Mamba. A step-out pillar makes escaping tight situations easier and adds to the overall stability of the boat if below-water boulders try to crunch your bow.
