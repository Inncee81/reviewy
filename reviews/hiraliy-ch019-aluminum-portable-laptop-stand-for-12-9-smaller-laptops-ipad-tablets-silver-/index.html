Good product, even if it is a little bit flimsy

First of all, I am not being paid for this, and was not given the product with any agreement to provide a review.The stand works well, and is highly adjustable to many different positions. It’s solid overall and holds your laptop and stays locked in place. I will say that it is a little bit flimsy, so if you have a heavy laptop in it (like my Macbook Pro) and bump it or bump the desk that it is sitting on, it will shake a little bit. It shakes a bit with my iPad in it too, but not too badly, and only if I bump the desk. I work on a really heavy, solid desk, and my typing on my wireless keyboard doesn’t vibrate the desk enough to make it shake too badly (just a slight movement that is barely detectable). If you’re working on a flimsy desk or table, it might shake a bit more.I have a heavier stand for my iPad that doesn’t vibrate at all, but that one is really heavy and pretty expensive. This one isn’t as sturdy, but it’s much lighter and easier to travel with, and costs much less. So if you want an inexpensive and highly portable laptop/tablet stand that is pretty solid and works well, I recommend it.
