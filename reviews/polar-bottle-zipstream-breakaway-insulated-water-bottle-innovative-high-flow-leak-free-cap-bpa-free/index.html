    Product Description

The Zipstream cap features a self-sealing valve. The valve’s design prevents the bottle from leaking even in the open position, and allows pressure to build inside the bottle when squeezed releasing the powerful burst of water you need to push you to the front. The patented Polar Bottle water bottle features an insulating layer between two walls of plastic, so it keeps liquids cool twice as long as other water bottles. The Made in America bottle fits standard bike water bottle cages and features a convenient, removable carrying strap. All Polar Bottle products are, and always have been, BPA-free and Phthalate-free.

Good sport and bike bottle

This is a nice water bottle for biking or sport use. The insulation is just a reflective foil between two layers of plastic. Mouthpiece is comfortable and self sealing, you can either suck on it, or squeeze the bottle. The handle loop is more comfortable if you reverse it so it doesn’t hit you in the nose. Very slight plastic odor when new. Water tastes good. Not as good as a vacuum insulated bottle, but will keep cold water cold on a 2 hour ride.
