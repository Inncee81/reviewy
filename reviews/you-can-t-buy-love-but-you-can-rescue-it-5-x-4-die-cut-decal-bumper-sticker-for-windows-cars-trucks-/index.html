I love my rescue dog and now my rescue decal!

I love the decal and the message it gives. It arrived within a weeks time and came with instructions on how to apply. I cleaned my back glass with a 50⁄50 mixture of water and rubbing alcohol, then wiped it clean with a cloth. I’d strongly suggest cleaning your surface before applying any decal. The decal went on easy and then I used a credit card and started from the center, working my way outward, to remove any bubbles. The backing was easily removed by slowly pulling if off at a 45 degree angle. If you still notice bubbles, I’d suggest using an X-Acto knife or something really thin and sharp. You can kind of poke the bubble and work it out.SIZING: Mine measures 5.5” wide and 4” high.Overall, I’m really satisfied with this decal. I’ve had several people ask me already where I purchased it from. Highly recommend!!
