Great construction. The installation kit brings down the rating

I installed this for my 2013 Samsung UN60F6300 (60”).The bracket itself is great: strong, sturdy construction and very secure. My problem was with the installation kit.Previously I bought the same brand cheaper HANGING mount system (MI-104) for another Samsung TV (40”) and works great. And I love how close it is to the wall. So I wanted to buy the same mount for this new TV. But that system was not recommended because 1) this new tv is 60” (in the limit), 2) my studs are 24” apart, 3) my TV has many cables in the back so it can’t be to close to the wall and 4) I needed the tilt which is not possible with the hang system - hence I decided to play safe and buy the same brand but in bracket format.First, my house has metal studs, and this kit comes with only 2 screws for this type. So I needed to buy 2 extra screws (only sold in packets of 10).Then, when I was installing the screws to the wall with my drill, since the heads are much smaller than the bracket holes you need washers and the kits comes with them, but these are small and poor quality, they BENT while tightening the screws!! So I had to hurry to the store again to buy bigger stronger washers.Other than that, the bracket works ok. The level worked ok for me even though I have my own one which just confirmed that the installation was leveled ok.Once you adjust it in place and tighten the screws it is not possible to change the angle without using a very long screwdriver, but hey… this bracket is not intended to be ‘movable’ so its ok for me to have it fixed and I’m happy with it.Oh, the box and the manual mention that it comes with HDMI cable, however it was missing in my package. I didn’t care since I already have mine.So, I didn’t like the problems with the screws and washers and the missing content, however I really like the bracket… it is much stronger than other more expensive ones I’ve seen at the store.I just bought a new 65” TV and I am ordering the same bracket again.
