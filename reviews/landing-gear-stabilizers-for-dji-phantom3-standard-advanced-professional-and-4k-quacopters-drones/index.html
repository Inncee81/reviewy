      Product Description

Style:P3 Landing Stabilizer TELESIN Phantom 3 Landing Gear for the Phantom 3 series increases stability of the Phantom 3 by widening the stance. TELESIN Phantom 3 Landing Gears provide the Phantom 3 with a wider stance for landing and taking off on uneven terrain. The landing gear easily snaps on/ off for quick and easy storage. The Landing gear securely clips onto the legs for easy installation and removal. By widening the legs the Phantom 3 is less prone to tipping over during rough takeoff’s and landings. The landing gear also enables the Phantom 3 to take off from uneven surfaces. In addition to the wider stance, the legs also raise the Phantom 3 up by 1 inch to keep the camera further away from the dirt. The Landing Gear is a great addition to help you protect your DJI Phantom 3.

Package Content: 1 Pair Landing Gear Legs for Phantom 3
