Purchased my first GPS watch a couple of weeks ago. I absolutely love this. Very easy setup, took only a couple of minutes. Recognizes my home course quickly. Yardage to front/middle/back are accurate versus my son’s Bushnell laser. Watch band is very comfortable and no problem with it affecting my swing or glove. Battery is excellent got over 3 eighteen hole rounds and still had battery left.. Charging was quick and easy. Very happy with this purchase.

Three Cheers for Bushnell

This is a great watch. I used Skycaddie products in the past and decided to give this unit a try after a friend’s recommendation. Could not be happier. This watch is relatively easy to use, provides quick access to hazards and is very accurate. Also, I do not have to recharge the unit after each use which I was forced to do with my Skycaddie. Be patient at start up. It does take a bit to locate a satellite at the first tee but is great after that. Also there is no required subscription

Great Watch

This watch is great. I had it about 3 months now and I am very happy with its proformance. I know it’s accurate because when I first started using it I would stand on the yard markers to check the yardage. Right on every time. It automatically senses what hole your on. Once you pick the course you don’t need to hit another button. Good if your not good with tech. If you are you can pull up hazards and check your shot yardage. I have not have any problems with the band as others had posted. I played with it about 15 times now. It is very light weight and does not bother me while playing.
