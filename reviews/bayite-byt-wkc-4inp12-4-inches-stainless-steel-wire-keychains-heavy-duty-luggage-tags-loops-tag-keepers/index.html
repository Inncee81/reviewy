Good buy, worth the money

I bought these as a key chain because you can’t fit anything else in today’s new car key fobs. Seriously! I bought this pack to replace the one I’ve been using for the past 2-3 years. It’s a good sized pack that is well worth the money. You can get colors (to tell things apart) or plain. The screw part seems pretty tight, though I’ve made it a habit to always tighten it when I pick them up after the old one let loose my keys all over the place one day. Some mentioned the other end, the non-screw end, coming out. My old one did that but it’s a simple dollop of crazy glue to fix that. So far so good with these - there’s not much to break or go wrong. It’s a simple piece of wire with a connector. At this price you can’t really lose with these.
