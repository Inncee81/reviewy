    Product Description

This is the BBTac Government .45 Spring Pistol BT-P169. The scale is 1⁄1 and it’s an high performance assembled plastic model gun. Includes airsoft spring pistol and sample bag of 6 mm BBs. This is a Spring Airsoft Pistol, which means it’s powered by a spring and must be cocked for each shot. It’s fast, clean, inexpensive and easily maintained; it requires no gas or batteries to operate. Also, Spring Airsoft Guns are the toughest, most durable of all Airsoft Guns, because they’re made with few breakable pieces. If you’re looking for a fun, reliable Airsoft Pistol that’ll look great and perform even better, you’ve come to the right place. Features 1⁄1 Scale High Performance Assembled Plastic Model Gun with a 15 round capacity in each magazine.

it turned out good enough. If you are getting this as a …

For $1.26 (+ 4.99 shipping) how can you complain about this gun?I bought it as a prop, so after filling in “made in China” with putty and re-painting it, it turned out good enough. If you are getting this as a prop, the hammer doesn’t do anything, but the scales for the hand grip can be pried off with a little care. I just sanded them to take off some of the plastic “shine” and then popped them back on after painting the gun…. also there are two screws under the right sides scales so if you are taking the gun apart, you will have to remove that. There was a slight gap between the body and upper slide, I took off the front spring and glued a piece of painted art-board to the barrel then made sure there was enough clearance for the slide to work.Of course I had to play with it to see how it shoots, At about 20 feet some of the plastic BB’s were right on target, but some hit up and to the left.All in all, for such an inexpensive gun, I was happy with my purchase.
