    Product Description

The Umarex 2279040 Hecker and Koch MP7 AEG Airsoft Air Gun Pistol is an electric gun that shoots BBs at 240-FPS. With a 180-round capacity and a built-in hop-up BB system you can shoot for long periods of time without reloading. The Flip-up sights are a great accessory as well as the integrated accessory rail and detachable grip. Powered by 7.2V NiMH rechargeable battery and charger included.
