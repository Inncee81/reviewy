Great controller, weak goggles, high price

I bought this pack for the controller, and was not as interested in the goggles. The controller works perfectly with my Bebop 1. I considered a Skycontroller 1, but I am new to the Bebop and have been enjoying it so much that I can see purchasing a Bebop 2 at some point, so I wanted the newest controller. I also like the reduced size of this one over the first Skycontroller, and the functionality of a wired connection to a phone or tablet.The controller feels of high quality and is very responsive. Setup was pretty easy and it basically worked right out of the box, although you do have to follow the specific startup sequence when using a phone. My only criticism of the controller is that the large wifi antenna adds a lot of weight to that end of it, and makes it feel a little unbalanced in my hands.The goggles are not great. They have a cartridge that slides out, in which you have to fit the phone. This makes getting the phone in and out much more difficult than other smartphone goggles. Additionally, you cannot put the phone into the cartridge while it is plugged into the controller. Therefore, if you were using normal viewing and want to switch to FPV, you have to unplug the phone, put into the goggles and plug it back in. The optics are not great, and the lack of adjustment is also pretty disappointing. They are comfortable, but the cartridge system is not well designed, and I don’t see ever using them. I have an actual cardboard “Google Cardboard” set that I would be more likely to use, which only cost $12. I also have a Firefly set, which is far superior to these (although it doesn’t have adjustment either). For the price, I kind of hoped for a really nice set of goggles.Overall, the controller is great and the goggles are not. The very high price for this set makes it just ok. However, in using one of the Parrot drones, this controller is the best control option if you did not get one in a bundle with your drone.
