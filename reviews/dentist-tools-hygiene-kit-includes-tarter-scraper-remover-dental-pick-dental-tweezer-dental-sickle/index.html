Directions:

The plaque scraper has two ends, use whatever end that comforts you in removing the plaque more efficiently. Go from one side of the mouth and work around the mouth. First, work on the flat surfaces on the teeth of the cheek side. Apply the blade of the plaque scraper on back of the flat surface. Make sure to place tip of the plaque scraper toward the gum line, then pull the plaque scraper gently, after each motion. Wipe the plaque off the blade with a tissue, repeat until there is no more plaque on the blade, then work between the teeth. To remove plaque between the teeth, place the blade near the gum line between teeth, then pull the blade away from the gum line. Go to the next tooth and finally around the mouth, then work on the tooth surfaces of the tongue side. Dental Pick: Start at the gum line between the teeth and gently pull the pick away from the gum line to remove the impacted food.

Safety Warnings:

Keep away from children. Do not swallow material removed. If bleeding of the gums occurs, stop and consult your dentist.
