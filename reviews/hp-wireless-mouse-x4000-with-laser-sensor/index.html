    Product Description

Color:Black The high-end design of the HP Wireless Mouse X4000 mimics the motion of a fast sports car. But the fun doesn’t stop there. This comfortable mouse also features wireless connectivity, 30-month battery life and a highly-precise laser sensor. The HP Wireless Mouse X4000 with Laser Sensor in deep Black features a Link 5 USB receiver that can be shared among five HP accessories. Connect your laptop or desktop to keyboards and other mice without using software or taking up all of your PC’s USB ports. With 2.4GHz wireless technology, you can use your mouse from farther away with less interference. The sculpted and striking lines of this beautifully designed wireless mouse feature smooth edges and sweeping contours for exquisite comfort. Its high-precision laser sensor (1600 dpi) works on almost every surface. And you’ll be alerted when the battery runs low by an LED indicator light. The HP Wireless Mobile Mouse uses two AA batteries.

We originally purchased wireless mouses for our department 5 years ago which everyone was happy with. However my wireless mouse started consuming batteries faster and faster as time went on. This mouse was purchased as a replacement because it boasts a 30 month battery life. WRONG!!!! I got about 5 months out of the first set of batteries and thought maybe it was because they were the non-name-brand batteries that ship with small electronics, that does not seem to be the case. Now that the mouse is about a year old, I am lucky to get two months out of a set of batteries with this mouse and normally have to replace them after 3-5 weeks. I have tried turning the mouse off each afternoon, three different mouse pads, and several different brands of batteries; none of that seems to make a difference. The rest of my department is still using the older mouses and replace the batteries once or twice per year…..guess I’m going mouse shopping again. I am very disappointed in this HP product as the HP mouse I use at home has been phenomenal.
