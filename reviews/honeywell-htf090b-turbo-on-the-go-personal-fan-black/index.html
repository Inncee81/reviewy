Great fan for eliminating bike-commuting-clothing stench and saving the world from evil fungus in small, enclosed spaces

I bought this fan to use in my work locker to dry out my bike commuting clothes. Previously, you could smell my stinky clothes from across the room and I’m sure that some evil fungus was setting up shop in my locker. I bought this fan and had a spare 13000 mA USB battery pack for power, and it drives this little fan for 8-10 hours on the 1A output. I have used this little fan every day for ~6 months and it does exactly what I need. The stench is gone, and I no longer fear that I am I unleashing a foul fungus on the world.

Solid little travel fan!

I recently travelled from Detroit to Oakland and wanted a small, portable fan that I could take with me. I’m one of those people who has to have a fan running in order to fall asleep. Also, as this was a business trip, I also wanted something that I could use in the office. This little fan from Honeywell did the trick. It features one speed only (which is sufficient), can be powered via a proprietary (boo!) USB cable or two AA batteries, and comes with a handle that can be used to hang the fan or as a stand. I used the handle as a stand and found that I could adjust it exactly how I wanted. For being such a small fan it still managed to produce a decent amount of air flow. Also, while not the loudest thing around, it still made enough noise for me to fall asleep every night. My only real gripe is the cheap, proprietary USB cable used to power the fan. A standard micro-USB would have been a far better choice. Overall, this is a solid travel fan that I can recommened.Pros:- Lays flat for easy storage- Produces a decent amount of air- Super lightweightCons:- Proprietary cable
