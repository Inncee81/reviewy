Blue Bebop - Refurbished

I have used one of these before and was pretty set on getting a Bebop. With the release of the Bebop 2, I was looking to get one a bit cheaper. I grabbed this refurbished unit during a Lightening Deal on Amazon, and have been very pleased.The drone speaks for itself, and is a pretty big jump over most of the toy level models that are available. The camera, app, brushless motors, and GPS are among the many features that make it a good choice for the money.I was a bit nervous about the refurbished drone, but Amazon seems to be careful about selling refurbished items and they have never disappointed me in the past. This came with all of the expected accessories, and the batteries seem to be holding charge as expected. Aside from the packaging, this looks like a new unit.I would have really liked to have gotten a Skycontroller too, but it seems awfully expensive for what it is. I have been using this with a Samsung Galaxy S5 and a generic PS3 controller and it controls very well and with no noticeable lag. I haven’t pushed the range, but I have no intention in ever flying this thing 2km away.If you are interested in a drone and can get this for under $200, you won’t be disappointed. Expect to get a controller of some sort though. It can be flown with the app on a touch screen, but it is quite difficult and no fun at all.
