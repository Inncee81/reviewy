Nice Camera, 4K Content Is Decent

Have you always wondered how a cheaper 4K action camera works? Because lots of people don’t have the money to buy a goPro. Well, I can say it doesn’t work too bad. Cheaper Chinese cameras are getting better. They still have a lot of room to improve but they are getting there. So first off with this camera if you are expecting crystal clear 4K then you might want to say away from this camera. If you want 4K with some blurriness then this might not be a bad camera for you. If you were to put this cameras 4K content on TV you would have to stand real close to the TV to notice the blurriness. So, it’s not too bad but again it’s not crystal clear. Which I wasn’t really expecting that in the first place. If you were to bump down the content to 2.7K then you will you get close to crystal clear. So, this leads me to the next part. I’m pretty sure this camera can only shoot 4K because it up scales it from 1080p. That explains why you can shoot 1080p just fine and its clear, but when you shout in 4K they content is a little blurry. Which again like I said, if you have the content displayed someplace and you were standing away you wouldn’t notice it that bad. After you can get pass the video quality then the camera is pretty awesome. They give you all the mounts you would possibly need. Also, this camera has Wi-Fi so you can stream the camera to your phone and manage the camera with your phone. The app works pretty well, I was really shocked that it worked that good. Overall, this camera is pretty good for being as cheap as it is. Yes, it has its issues but it still works good and the content it still good, even at the higher resolution. If you have proper lighting then you should be just fine with this camera. So, who would I recommend this camera too? Well, anyone that is wanting to experiment with a sport camera and someone that needs a sport camera that doesn’t want to spend a lot on one.
