smarter soundbar mount with depth adjustment

Well what took so long for a smart soundbar mount to be made? I initially bought two hockey stick shape metal pieces for my soundbar to mount it to my TV which is on a wall mount. Once I got those sticks on the wall mount, the soundbar sat over 6 inches behind the TV. My TV is not the thinnest flat screen. It looked stupid and I said to myself, why didn’t someone think of being able to adjust the depth of the bars to make the soundbar get at least close to the front edge of the TV. I imagine a real thin TV could have the soundbar stick too far in front as well. I came to amazon and found the solution. This mount from Aeon did exactly what I wanted. It not only pushed the soundbar to the front of the TV, I was able to get it perfectly flush with the front of the TV. Now it looks like it should. As if it was a part of the TV. Easy enough to assemble. I did have to take the TV off my wall mount to do it (the hardest part of the install). What Aeon calls it vertical bars, ended up going in between the wall mount and the TV. I used the longer screws that came with my wall mount to go through the wall mount and the soundbar mount into the TV screw holes (glad I kept them). The vertical bars were long too, so no issue getting the soundbar to the right height for my TV, snug against the bottom edge. This is a well thought out design and I only wish I found this sooner. I spent $50 on the hockey sticks and was going to live with that. Since I have had the sticks for too long, I cannot return them. Not sure what to do with them now. Suggestions? I thought about the old table hockey game. Remember that? OK, very pleased with this product, how it works, and how it makes the soundbar look on my TV.
