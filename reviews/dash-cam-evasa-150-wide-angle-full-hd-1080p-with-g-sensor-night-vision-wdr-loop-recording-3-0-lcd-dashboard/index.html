Great Camera for the Price!!!!

 This was exactly what I was looking for, “just in case” of an accident, or capture something cool. Quality of the camera is excellent, as well as great video quality. Price is a key factor as I did not want to spend several hundred dollars. The only drawback is the instructions. The ones that come with it are pretty much useless, however they send a PDF file via E-mail the same day you order it, or the next. It is better.One other thing is that when the camera is plugged into a cigarette lighter for power, the camera will turn on automatically and shut off automatically as long as power to your cigarette lighter port shuts down after turning the car off. Now if you have a vehicle that always has a power supply active you can manually shut off the camera.. You can shut the camera on and off with the power button on the lower right side.• Unit must be in “pause” mode to change settings. When the unit is recording the top right of the LCD display shows the record time for that segment in RED.• Once in pause mode, push “M” button once to enter mode to change Resolution, Cycle Record (loop times) WDR, Motion Detection, Audio, Date stamp, Gravity Sensing.• Push “M” button twice to enter mode to change Date/Time, Beep Sound, Language, Frequency, Screen savers, Light Set, Parking Guard, License Number Set, Format, Default Setting, and Version.• Once you enter one of the modes, use the up and down buttons to scroll to the criteria you want to change, Date/ Time as an example. It will be highlighted in light blue.• Then press the “OK” button to enter the window to actually change those settings. The up and down arrows can then be used to select the correct date and time. Press the “OK” button to move to the next segment of date or time criteria.• When finished with any of the setting adjustments, press the “M” button to exit.• The “Up Arrow button can also be used to mute the audio recording, when in recording mode. The mic symbol will show a red “X” through it, indicating it is in mute mode.The only issue I found was because of the wide angle fish eye lens the left and right sides of the video are a bit blurry when played back. Also sometimes on playback the video sometimes for a split second speeds up then returns to normal. Not a very big deal and is hardly noticeable.I hope this review helped in some way.
