MegaGear Neoprene Camera Cases

Cameras are essential devices and carrying them around makes them vulnerable to damages. However, there is an easy and simple solution to protecting your camera completely: the MegaGear Neoprene Camera Cases. These lightweight yet high quality camera cases will easily cover your camera and make sure no harm comes to it.

The MegaGear Neoprene camera cases come in many types and styles to cater to different camera models and keep them safe. They are designed to look attractive, protect the device, and remain small and compact at the same time. The best feature about all the MegaGear Neoprene camera cases is that they are made up of the extremely light weight material of Neoprene. This also makes them soft so that the digital camera they encase is completely protected. No more having to cringe or damage your camera if it accidentally slips from your hand.

The design of the MegaGear Neoprene camera cases is compact and stylish. They are handcrafted and all the types are precisely manufactured to fit the designated models they are made for. The case features a camera-like shape with big fashionable handmade stitches to give it a unique look. A flap is used to open and close the case and a keychain holder is attached to the side for easy carrying. The MegaGear logo stylishly sits at the back in rubber. It is a very small product but designed just for your digital camera. The entire case has a foam padding of Neoprene inside for extra safety. This camera case can easily protect your digital camera from moisture, hits, scratches, dust and dirt.

The cases come in many colors for the customers to pick their favorite. Whether you prefer black, gray, magenta, light blue, bright red, teal, dark blue, hot pink, purple, yellow, or maroon, you can have it.

Product Reviews

Too Big for the G9X

This pouch is a bit too big for the Canon G9X (the camera it is marketed as being designed for), in my opinion. That’s odd, because I have the G7X, too, and the MegaGear pouch for that camera, which is anything but loose. In fact, the G7X pouch is quite snug. I would not buy this again.UPDATE: I compared the two cases I have (the one I just got for the G9X Mark II and another for the larger G7X). While they are sold via separate pages on Amazon and have different item numbers, they are basically identical. I feel this is a bit dishonest. They’re marketing these as if they’re two different cases, each tailored to a different camera, when they actually seem to be identical. So the case is snug (some would say a bit too snug) on the G7X and too big for the G9X. Annoying.

Nice size, a bit expensive

This is a nice size that works both for my camera (Canon G9X) and for my handheld GPS (Garmin Oregon 450). In fact, I can’t decide which to use it for. There’s a bit of a lip on top, which gives it more coverage but also catches the camera’s protruding lens ring slightly, so I think the GPS unit will win out. It’s somewhat pricey, but it’s getting harder and harder to find what I want – a neoprene case with a velcro closure. A zipper takes too long to open when I’m fumbling for my camera, and I don’t like zipper teeth scraping my devices. So, if that’s what you’re shopping for, this is a good one.
