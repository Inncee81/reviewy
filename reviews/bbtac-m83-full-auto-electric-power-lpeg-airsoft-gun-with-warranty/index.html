The woodpecker hunter

This here gun is awesome! I got it to combat an insurgency of woodpeckers. I loaded all 7 mags with this cool speed-loader straw contraption. I went out back to sight it in. The gun shoots consistently. The bb’s curve a little, but you can see where they go, and they all go to the same spot. The book talks about how to adjust for that, but who cares? It’s not like it’s a real rifle and I’m fighting off Al Qaeda. They’re Al Qaeda Woodpeckers! I couldn’t wait for them to attack once I was all checked out on my new weapon. I dug a little fox hole in my back yard and grabbed my pith helmet. Okay, it was a lawn chair and a 6-pack. Don’t judge me! Sure enough, at 0500 hours, the little sons of B*#$&es showed up. Pounding their tiny beaks on the metal flashing of my dryer exhaust. I emptied the first mag on full auto and slapped in a new one before they knew what was happening. I didn’t want to kill them mind you, I wanted them to go back to the rock from under which they’d crawled and report to their C.O. that the humans had the upper hand. BB’s zigged and zagged all over the place. The second mag hit the ground and I loaded a third as he fell back to his position on a telephone pole, about 30 yards across the drainage ditch behind my house. Bliiiiiiiiip, another mag empty as the bb’s walked their way toward the enemy combatant. Small shards of telephone pole wood shrapnel ricocheted dangerously close and I think it finally dawned on the little creep. He flew the coop down and out of range as I loaded the fifth mag into place. Imaginary smoke wafted from my white-hot barrel (that was actually cool plastic). Mission accomplished. The enemy has made no further incursions into my territory since this encounter. The gun lies loaded and fully charged, in the ready room. (My lanai). Waiting for the day I pray will never come. Until then, I’ll keep sharp and practice on my friend Billy Bob. He gets drunk and likes to take off his shirt. A few 250-fps reminders help him put it back on. Single shot selector of course.
