The device gets 4 Stars, Wilson Electronics gets 0 Stars for blowing me off…

I have mixed feelings about this device. When it’s set up properly, the enhancement to the quality of service (QoS) is pretty stellar. My negative feelings are that the device is extremely sensitive to direction and that the seller would not send a “free” patch antenna when asked.To be fair, I’m in a fringe area for both ATTWS and VZW, the latter is a native USCC region but line-of-sight to the nearest VZW antenna (I know where the ATTWS and USCC and VZW antennas are; I’m supposedly “roaming” on USCC since I’m in a different state (WA) but the VZW antenna is in OR, across the Columbia River on microwave backhaul so my iPhone “thinks” it’s 20 miles away in Astoria OR…). My ATTWS iPhone connects to the antenna near the Bradley Wayside Park in OR, with little issue. This booster helps with both networks, with a caveat.The Booster is very sensitive to direction. Only because I know exactly where the tower with the installed antenna array is (I worked on the installation teams years ago), I can align the Booster antenna in the relevant direction. “Tweaking” the antenna can result in much better QoS, or kill service completely - a couple of degrees in rotation can provide solid LTE service or No Service.I’d like to try an external antenna with this unit, however, a call to the company led to my being told “No”. This, IMO, is to me a frustration in that I am in a fringe area, with pretty good external service that isn’t great inside, but the company did offer a free/no-cost external antenna as an option (I’m surmising the antenna is offered to enhance the device’s capabilities, but I’m likely not going to find out as I’ve been denied this option and the manual/web site do not specify which external antenna options exist.4 Stars on my experience with this device and some time invested to make this device work, 0 Stars for poor customer support. The eqo costs “only” $350, I’m surmising that because I’m not spending more on their other products I’ve been blown off. I like the eqo, just be prepared to be left to your own devices to make this work.
