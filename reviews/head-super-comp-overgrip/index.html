Product Reviews

This product is a great deal

I’ll start off by saying I am definitely not a top rated tennis player, I would barely qualify as a competitive tennis player. This over grip has the nicest feel to any I have used, just the right combination of grip and comfort. The product was easy to install, but this is true of nearly every over grip, wrap around handle and secure. The securing band has been in place for about a month now, used every day that it doesn’t rain for maybe a total of 20 to 25 hours, and is still holding strong, the over grip itself it showing only slight wear. Some people have complained that this gets slippery when your hands get sweaty, I have not noticed this and the grip has been consistent for me from the time I start playing until the time I stop. Every once in a while someone else will play with one of my rackets and everyone who ever has eventually asks about the grip, they are always surprised to find out it is one of the cheaper products available and I have started noticing this product wrapped around more and more of my friends rackets, even those who don’t use a head racket.

Head Super Comp 3-pack is OK but not the best

I much prefer the Wilson Pro overgrip to this Head overgrip. The Head is OK but the Wilson is tacky which gives me a better grip. The Head also got a wrinkle when I started the wrap which I didn’t get with the Wilson.

Great overgrip, but lacks instructions

For the price, the grip is of great quality. It was a bit difficult to figure out how to put it on however, as there were no directions for it. After a few tries, I managed to wrap the overgrip around the racket sufficiently well to keep it on. I recommend trying it with the wrap on first to gauge the approximate angle you’ll need to unravel the overgrip at, and then go about the wrapping slowly and tightly. It took me a while, but I managed to get it to work on the first try.
