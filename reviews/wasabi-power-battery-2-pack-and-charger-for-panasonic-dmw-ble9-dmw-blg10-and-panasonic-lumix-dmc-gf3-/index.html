Not OE battery life. Not recommended for 4k video

I’ve purchased many different wasabi power batteries and I’ve always had good luck with them being close to OE battery life and quality. For some reason I am very disappointed by this set for the GX85. Shooting in 4k is demanding. But the battery randomly cuts out and shuts the camera off before the camera flashes a battery level warning. It does not offer the same performance as OE and the battery level bar doesn’t seem to be consistent with the actual capacity of the battery. Just overall disappointed but livable if only using the camera for stills

Good bang for the buck.

Batteries are good - I get approx. 90 min. per battery recording time in my Panasonic Lumix DMC-LX100 camera, shooting at 1080p HD. Have used other Wasabi batteries, and they also performed well. Charger contact pins may need slight adjustment from time to time to be sure they are making proper contact with the battery, which you’ll know if the red light comes on when you begin charging and turns green when charging is complete. I also own the Panasonic brand battery charger for this size battery. Performance is identical, in terms of charging time and fullness of charge. Customer service and communication from this company has been excellent.

Wasabi batteries fall out of the Wasabi charger?

The 2 Wasabi batteries that came with the charger keep falling out of it, yet my original Panasonic ZS100 battery charges and stays fine in the Wasabi charger. The Wasabi batteries must be a very tiny amount smaller so they fall out of it, although they look identical to the OEM battery with the naked eye. Normally not a big deal I could return it but I left for Spain for a month right after purchasing these products. So, I give the charger a 1 or even a zero. I’ve had to charge the batteries in the camera so far and they have worked.Update 9-20-17. Emailed with Wasabi and they replaced the battery charger and batteries. Things should work better now. Changed rating to 5 stars.

The charger is great as I prefer not to charge the battery in-camera

The Wasabi feels a tiny bit looser in the charger than the Panasonic, but still charges properly. A big savings compared to the cost of a Panasonic battery.
