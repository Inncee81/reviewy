    Product Description

Product Description 5 pack, plastic, cut off wheel, specific for cutting plastic, for use with the DREMEL EZ lock Mandrel model EZ402, compatible with all DREMEL rotary tools.

From the Manufacturer Dremel EZ Lock is the fastest and easiest way to change mandrel-mounted accessories! There is no more screw to lose and no more wrench needed to tighten the screw. The new EZ Lock Cut-Off Wheels last two times longer than current cut-off wheels. The new system even makes to easy to change wheels while you’re wearing work gloves. Dremel EZ Lock Cut-off Wheels are labeled for easy identification. The patented one-piece mandrel design means that no screw is needed to hold the cut-off wheels in place. EZ Lock Cut-off Wheels can be used to cut metal conduit, PVC piping, reslotting screw heads, removing rusty nail heads.
