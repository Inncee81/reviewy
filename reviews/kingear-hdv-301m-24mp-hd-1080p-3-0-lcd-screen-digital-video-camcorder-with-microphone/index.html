Product Reviews

Camera comes with everything and has tons of features.

 This camera comes with everything to get started including the camera, lens cover, attachable microphone, charging cable and block, protective storage case, and hdmi cable. The only thing it doesn’t come with is an SD card, but most cameras like this don’t include a memory card. The camera is well built, heavy and sturdy. The swinging screen door snaps open and closed and the lens cap also snaps on securely. The microphone is removable and screws on and does not move once fastened. The camera is basically the same size as a sony handycam and has many of the same features.The screen is bright and easy to view and the camera is so simple to operate. Just charge the battery, insert a memory card, open the door and hit record. The included hdmi wire can be used to connect to a TV to view recordings. Recording quality is pretty good with high resolution and pretty bright colors and good contrast. Darker lighting environments does create some challenges just like it does with other similar models. My wife used to have a Sony Handycam which had a similar issue with some graying. These camcorders don’t have as large a sensor as professional cameras but they perform just fine for recording family videos, sporting events, and other everyday activities.The microphone is excellent because you can pick up sounds from longer distances. The db level can be adjusted depending on the distance from the audio source which is a very nice feature.Overall, for just over $100, there is a lot included in this package. It will be great for family videos and does record quality audio and video.
