    Product Description

LifeBox is dedicated to protecting your mobile phone. Our premium tempered Onkyo DP-X1 glass screen protector is built with 9H hardness and is only 0.33mm thin.

This provides a super shock absorbant protection, high screen clarity, anti oil, anti scratch protection for your device. If broken, the glass breaks up into small pieces for safety, exactly the same as a car windshield. This remarkable protection is a high quality screen protector that even sharp objects such as a knife and key will not scratch the glass.

Flawless Screen Protector in terms of application but had one small issue…

I’m a little torn with this review. It comes with 2 protectors. One of the two was scratched already and I almost returned this. But I wanted to try using the other protector to see how it works. I’ve done a lot of these installations and I have to say that this was one of the most easiest to apply. Because the adhesive isn’t so sticky, after you put it down, you can adjust the screen to exactly how you want it and then press down to make it stick. Because of this, it fits almost perfectly on my DP-X1a, exactly in the middle of the screen with a only a few mm around the edges.Contrary to other reviews, you don’t need instructions to apply this to your unit. My goodness, just make sure there’s no dust on your screen, put it on, adjust carefully, and then press down.So, VERY RECOMMENDED (but only 4 stars) because the product applies very easily so I will not return this product as I don’t actually need two protectors. No biggie.

Great layer of protection

Was a breeze to install. Clean the display well with the alcohol pad provided, then clean it some more with your own pads, then clean it again. Dry it with the satin cloth provided and have a can of compressed air standing by to blow off any dust or lint that tries to settle back. This glass doesn’t flex like other screen protectors so be prepared to do a few test runs before actually cleaning and installing so you know what to expect and aren’t too nervous. The glass is slightly smaller then the screen so take care to align it in the center properly. It does raise the display area a bit but the protective glass edges run in the borders of the display so you won’t see them when using the DP-X1. Adds a level of relief against accidental damage, scratches while in pockets or bags, etc.. Nice to have a spare as well. Once applied I realized just how well it works and how it is worth the $20.
