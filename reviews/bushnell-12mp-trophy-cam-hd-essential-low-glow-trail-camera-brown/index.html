    Product Description

Compact game camera with a multitude of photo and video settings. Adjustable resolution can be set at 3, 5, or 12 MP. Features a 0.3 second trigger speed and runs up to 1 year on a set of batteries. Records 720p video clips up to 60 seconds in length. Runs on (8) AA batteries (not included).

Great value

 Great price. Well made. Takes good day time and night time photos. Setup was straight forward. Just read the directions.Haven’t tried the video much yet, but, I notice it takes bright sunlight to get color video.UPDATE: I used it for the first time to get videos after dark. I have raccoons coming out of a storm drain pipe. The trigger (response time) time for still shots is quite short, listed as 0.3 secs in the users manual and 0.17 secs from an independent tester (trailcampro), The trigger time for videos is much longer, tested by tailcampro as 1.77 secs. This was too slow to catch most of the action with my raccoons last night. See the attached video and png. I have at least two raccoons coming out of that pipe and going back into the pipe each night. The camera only caught one raccoon leaving the pipe last night, and the camera didn’t trigger until it was almost out of the field of view. A returning raccoon was only caught already back in the pipe with just the tail showing. But, still a great camera and a great bargain.

I would not buy this camera under any circumstances unless I wanted a doorstop or paperweight. I only gave it one star because zero was not an option.
