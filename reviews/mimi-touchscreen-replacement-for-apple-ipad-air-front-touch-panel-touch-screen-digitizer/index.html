Second ones a charm!

My first attempt destroyed this digitizer, their website had no step by step instructions for the Air. In the end I had ghosting issues which made the iPad totally useless and when I tried to remove the glass to fix it I cracked it. $40 down the toilet so I was really dissapointed. through some online research I discovered that even people who professionally repair the Air digitizers were having a problem and only a few manufacturers didn’t ghost, It turns out the issue is the sensetivity at the edge of the glass and requires a special tape put on before you install it. Since I followed the instructions online my second Mimi digitizer has worked perfectly. The tape is called kapton tape and Amazon sells it cheap, just do a google search and you will find good examples of how to apply it to the edge and along with this digitizer you should have no issues. 1 Mil Kapton Tape (Polyimide) - 1⁄4” or 1⁄8” will work. Other than that just take your time and let the heat gun loosen the edges, never force the glass, remove the inner film before you adhere it in place, oh and try to put all the screws back where you found them.
