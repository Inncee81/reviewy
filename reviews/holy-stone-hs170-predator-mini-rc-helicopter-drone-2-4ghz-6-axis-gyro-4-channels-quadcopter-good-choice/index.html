Great drone, would recommend

My 6 year old son got this for Christmas and is his first Drone. First impression was great. The product was packaged well and instructions were clear to get us started.Having never flown one we started inside and had him fly it on his own. As expected we did a fair bit of crashing around in the house, nothing serious but learned quickly how sharp those little props can be on fingers. We ended up breaking a propeller and as it turns out, not only are there different color props for front and rear, there are also letters on each prop which have to go in a specific place or the drone will flip over repeatedly.Got that figured out and we were back in business. We then went outside and the drone worked great. Easy to hover, easy to maneuver and great practice for fine motor skills (for us and him). Flight time felt longer than advertised and we were having the drone doing flips in no time.Only reason for the 4 stars instead of 5 is that the manual could have been more inclusive regarding prop replacement specifics and the illustrations on how to adjust trim on the remote could have been a bit clearer.Other than that I would fully recommend this as a first drone or if you want an indoor/outdoor or smallish drone just for fun. Great deal and a ton of fun.
