Product Reviews

It’s All You Really Need

I’ve had larger cases than this one, but after seeing the quality of it in some store, I ordered it from Amazon. I’ve been VERY pleased. It holds my MacBook Pro snugly and my MacBook Air comfortably even when it’s wearing its padded sleeve. Some of the inside pockets might be a little small for whatever hand-held calculator you have, but no big deal. The capacity of this case is just right for 95% of your every day use. I realized that when I had larger cases, I had stuff in them that I really didn’t need to carry around. This case will impose some discipline on you, so you don’t become a hoarder.

Good for what it is…

I like this bag, but I’m not going to sugarcoat it: it’s a $20 bag. It’s padded, but don’t expect the thick layer of protective padding you’d get in a much more expensive bag. The fabric handles are fine, but aren’t nearly as robust as a more expensive bag. The shoulder strap feels kind of cheap, and connects to the bag via plastic (not metal) clips. So I just wanna make it clear: for a $20 laptop bag, this is fine. But don’t have high expectations for it, OK? It has plenty of space for the power adapter, a Bluetooth mouse and my Amazon tablet (so much space that these accessories won’t cause the front pocket to bulge). You could probably fit a notebook or magazine in the bag, too… but not much more.

Cheaply manufactured; a durability downer; faulty zippers

I purchased this case based on previous reviews promising long lasting satisfaction and multi-device flexibility. It had sky-high ratings and the company manufacturing it was a brand I recognized. I thought there would not be too many risks. Welp, I was dead wrong. I have not yet owned this bag for two years and every zipper but the front one is broken. I literally cannot zip the top of my bag (with either of the two available) or the second compartment. I am forced to use my dilapidated case until the end of the school year (I am a schoolteacher). But I almost can’t wait to toss this piece of garbage, and simply buy another case from a company that makes a more reliable product. I guess this is the price I pay for buying something cheaply made and falsely advertised. Caveat emptor? No, that surely shouldn’t be the answer. More like take this caveat: don’t waste your time or money on this breakable bag.

ample storage, good design

Ample storage. I can fit my 15” macbook pro retina, Ipad, Iphone, all cords and a few files. Easy to organize and access all contents. I hate the inside. I ended up getting a caselogic sleeve for my macbook pro, which slips into this laptop case. Reduces friction and provides a little more padding. A lot of users complained about the padding. The padding is adequate for me because I’m moderately careful with electronics. If you’re clumsy, forfeit the slim design of this case and go with a more heavily padded case. Overall, I’m very happy.
