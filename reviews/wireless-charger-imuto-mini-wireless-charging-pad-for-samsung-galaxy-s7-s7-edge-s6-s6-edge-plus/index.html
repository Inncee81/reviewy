Product Reviews

Doesn’t include power adapter

The charger works but it didn’t come with a power adapter. Every other charger I ever bought included one, so was surprised this didn’t, especially at the price and since it requires a 2.1A adapter, which not everyone has one lying around. Another issue is the charging indicator light is very faint, it looks too much like the constant light. It also doesn’t charge when connected to my laptop. Other than all that, it’s OK.

Wireless wonder

Works as expected. It charges a little slower compared to when the SG7 is plugged directly but that is to be expected as well. Only complaint is the blue light being too intense especially in a pitch-black room. Hoping that the light actually turns off after some time or that there’s a switch that can turn it off manually.

Average charging but convenient

The phone I have is a Samsung S6 active. It’s not listed specifically on the compatible devices but it does work with the phone. I have to put the phone just right on it for it to work. I guess find just the right spot. My husband has a Samsung S7 and it works just fine with his as well. The built in USB cord is very short. I can see its use only right next to a laptop, about 6 inches or less away. It’s light and definitely portable. It charges at an average rate, not a fast charger. But it is convenient not fumbling to plug the charging cord in, especially when it’s dark. We all know those USB ports on phones can break. Would be a good option to charge a phone in which the USB port has already been broken.
