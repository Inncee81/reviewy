Product Specifications

  • 4 packs, each with 10 exposures for 40 exposures total
  • Instant color film for Fujifilm instax 200 and 210 cameras
  • High-speed ISO 800
  • 3.4 x 4.25”

  • Color Temperature: 5500 K (daylight balance)

    Product Description

The four-pack of Fujifilm instax Wide Instant Film contains 40 total sheets of instant color film that is compatible for use with Fujifilm instax 200 and 210 cameras. This film features vivid color reproduction with natural skin tones when used under daylight (5500K) or electronic flash lighting conditions. A refined grain structure provides high image sharpness and clarity as well as a versatile ISO 800 film speed for use with a wide variety of subjects. Each sheet of film produces a 2.4 x 3.9” glossy image area that is surrounded by a white border with a notation area beneath the image. Quick, instant developing is possible when working in temperatures between 41°-104°F.

This Wide film measures 3 1⁄4 x 4 1⁄4 print size with the actual image being 2 1⁄2 x 3 7⁄8”. More like a standard instant print than the mini camera prints (2x3?). I used Polaroid film years ago and ever since Fuji entered the game I’d pick Fuji for print quality every time. Less contrast, better sharpness, more accurate natural color. According to Fuji these prints are fully developed in 90 seconds, but I think the full color saturation requires more like 2-2 1⁄2 min. which is worth waiting for. Overall great film to go with a their Instax Wide 300 Camera.
