It ran my a/c but I believe that’s only because plug I used has it’s own 30amp breaker (I ran it straight off breaker box so it was only thing plugged in). I don’t think it’ll run and a/c with 20amp breaker.

perfect for plugging into a 110 outside outlet

this thing is heavy duty works great, I have an outdoor outlet that has the doors on it, my RV plug would not fit between the door and the plug flush with the adapter plug into it, this plug is not as wide as the one from my RV and fits perfect. The handle also worked out so I could tie up a small piece of paracord to make a drip loop. The cheap plugs you buy that plug into your RV plug always seem to burn up, I have gone through 3 of them, I am wondering if it was arching because the plug would not sit flush and would wiggle? Anyway its not an issue anymore. If you plug into a 110 I would suggest you get one of these. Price is not too bad and you know how RV stuff is.
