Takes a little messing around, but works well

Works as expected (after reading the reviews). The usb power doesn’t fit well into the box, but it is powered by the hdmi coords anyway; so it really doesn’t matter. Also, there is no instruction manual, but really it is plug and play so you really don’t need instructions. Other than that this seems to work pretty well. The remote switches inputs correctly and it does sense what ports have power coming to the switch. I had some minor problems due to the devices I hooked up (which have nothing to do with the item): my sony bluray player showed no signal (soln: set the video output to 1080i instead of 1080p in bluray player settings), and my apple tv is usually selected over other inputs since it continues to produce power even while in sleep mode (soln: use the remote to change inputs and the switch will stay at the desired input).
