This cover is a flexible rubber material and it’s thick enough that I think it’ll last a while. The camera lens cover is a hard plastic and fits snuggly on the actual lens if you aren’t using the housing. The part labeled “battery cover” is actually a side cover replacement for the Hero 4 Black. It’s a hard plastic and snuggly covers your HDMI, USB, and SD ports. There are inserts on the inside of the cover that fit inside the HDMI and USB ports. This helps it stay on tight but you have to be careful when you pull it off because it’s on there pretty tight and you don’t want to damage the pins inside. Finally the part labeled “camera side cover” is of no use for owners of the Hero 4.
