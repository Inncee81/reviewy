The lightweight Halo Cycling Cap has quickly become our favorite mountain and road bike riding gear. Keeps salty sweat and rain from getting into eyes

I am into randonneuring and, therefore, have found after riding long distances, my helmet pads are crusted with salt. When it rains, the salt washes off, down my forehead and into my eyes. It burns so much that I couldn’t keep my eyes open. Not good when you’re cycling on city streets. Before randonneuring, I hadn’t ridden in a lot of rain, so it was never an issue. I bought this cap to address this issue and it works great.I don’t wear it when it is not raining as I found that it does heat up your head a bit. I also bought the cap that has a flap down the back and I use that one for full sunny days to shield my neck from the sun.When it rains the headband and little rubber band in the front keep the sweat from running into my eyes. The brim of the cap helps with rain getting into my eyes somewhat. I now never go on any rando ride without it packed somewhere.
