Generally sturdy but with a few faults

My first impression of this GripTight GorillaPod is that’s really small, with the total height (including the head) being roughly 6.5”. The tripod is extremely light and felt a bit on the cheap side since the legs are made of plastic; but the joints on the individual sections have a decent amount of tension. The top of the tripod features a small quick release mount where you can attach the GripTight mount or most any other smaller smartphone mount for that matter. Just underneath the quick release mount is a ring which you turn to lock the quick release so that you don’t accidentally press the button to release the mount. One thing to mention is that after locking everything in place, there is a small amount of play at the quick release mount which may cause noticeable movement if you’re doing videos, long exposures, or time-lapses.If you have an iPhone 6 Plus and/or a Reti-Cam mount, they do work on this. I purchased the regular sized mount (not the XL) and if you angle the phone you can get it to fit. I was also able to mount my phone with a very slim (0.4mm) case. In either case, they were an extremely tight fit. I would recommend the XL version if you don’t have another mount. If you have the Reti-Cam, rest assured, the GripTight supports the weight of the Reti-Cam mount and iPhone 6 Plus (with slim case). Overall, it seems sturdy enough while gripped onto something until you get to extreme angles, at which point the top quick release joint may tend to sag slightly. If you use it on level ground, it’s best to make sure you extend the legs since it can feel top-heavy.Overall, my only complaints are that I wish the top had an actual screw-down lock for the ballhead instead of the round joints since I feel like it’ll eventually wear down and become loose. Also, the top quick release mount does show movement when everything is locked down and I would have liked a bit more quality at such an important joint. But honestly, for around $15 it’s quite a decent product to have for on-the-go stabilization which is easy to carry around.
