Perfect Mouse For Fun and Work

I work from home on a MacBook Air and I wanted a mouse that would not only get the job done, but brighten up my desk as well. After looking at what seemed like a hundred mouses, I settled on this one, and I’m so glad I did.The packaging was great and even included an easy open tab in back so that I didn’t have to cut through all that plastic to free the mouse. Once I got it out, I literally followed two steps to get the mouse up and running - pull tab to activate the battery and plug in the receiver.I literally was up and running in seconds! I didn’t need anything fancy (trackball, etc.), so this mouse works perfectly. It does run a little on the small side, so if you want your entire hand to rest on the mouse, then you’ll want to look for a bigger one. For me, I like the smaller style and find that it works perfectly with my MacBook Air.The mouse also has an on/off switch on the bottom so that you don’t waste the battery, though the reported battery life is really good.One important note: The receiver is NOT unifying. If you are looking for a unifying receiver, don’t get this mouse (or make sure you already have a unifying receiver - one that connects your Logitech devices). I didn’t need one so this didn’t impact my purchase decision, but definitely keep it in mind if that’s something you want.Overall, I’m really happy with this mouse and love the fox design! The colors are vibrant and it actually makes me want to use the computer more.
