Amazing for the price

This backpack is as nice as other backpacks that literally cost twice as much. I use it for college, and it holds everything I need. It holds my laptop, notebooks, calculator, pens/pencils, glasses, umbrella, textbooks (including my insanely large anatomy textbook), and it even holds my Nintendo Switch. All at once!The only complaint I have is a minor one. It’s about the charging port. I knew going into it that it didn’t include a charger, but I am kinda bummed out that there isn’t a dedicated pocket for one. The charging cord used to connect to the battery is pretty unruly and creates a bit of a mess. I’d like a more organized approach. But other than that, I have no other complaints!UPDATE: I do have another minor complaint. I wish the back was a bit more breathable, as when I wear this on a hot day, my back gets drenched in sweat. Still a great backpack though.
