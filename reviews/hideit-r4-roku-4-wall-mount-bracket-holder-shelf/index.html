Product Reviews

Perfect fit, ideal for wall mount, only one (small) complaint

I bought this to mount my Roku 4 unit behind my wall-mounted TV.The bracket is made from heavy gauge steel and it is designed to last forever, barring all-out nuclear annihilation. Your Roku will be safe and it will fit ideally within the bracket provided the Roku is properly oriented in the bracket so as to not impede any of the ports.The only complaint I have, and it is a small one, is that I would have preferred to be able to secure the Roku player in the mount, to allow for orienting the bracket in any configuration: upside-down would have been ideal for me to provide the best cable routing, but this unit does not allow for securing the unit into place other than upright.

May Not Be Aesthetically The Best….

It fits the original 4 nicely however be aware that it CANNOT be mounted so that the cables connect at bottom. It’s designed (can’t figure out why) so that the cables point out the top side. If you try from the bottom - the HDMI cable won’t fit. I did manage to have the cables come out the side instead. See my pic. I think most folks would have wanted the cables exiting at the bottom. (I may be wrong)But I’ve noticed improved interface speed since mounting it on the wall.Update: Please note that the genius software of Amazon turned my pic sideways and gives me no option to edit it (even though I uploaded it with the correct orientation) - so the cables are actually coming out the right side. Not up like the photo suggests.
