Product Packaging:Charging Dock and Battery 2PK

​SHARKK is proud to present our GoPro batteries and dual dock charging station to ensure your adventures will always be captured on camera. Designed for GoPro 2 and 3, each lithium-ion battery features 1050mAh for a long lasting charge. The dual dock charger can simultaneously charge two batteries, ensuring that you never run out of power to keep capturing. This includes a USB charging cable that works with any standard USB wall or car outlet and dual dock charger. Simply charge and walk away!

Dual Dock Charging You can charge both battery packs at the same time or use one to take capture those important moments while  the other recharges. Whichever you choose, you’ll always be ready for continuous picture taking. 

Convenient & Compatible Both battery and charger are compatible with GoPro HERO2, HERO3, and HERO3+ for added flexibility and convenience.
