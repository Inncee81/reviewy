My First Lab Mini Duo Scope Toy, Blue GreenDon’t be put off by the plastic design or the low price, the “My First Lab Mini Duo Scope” is an excellent “first” microscope for kids and can even be fun for older children and adults (if they’ll overlook the toy-like appearance). In fact, let me get right to it – this is an incredible bargain and Amazon currently has the lowest price I could find anywhere ($24.99).This thing is solid, very well designed, and most importantly, it works very well with a clear image and many nice features. Dual lighting (above and below the stage), and good lenses (a first, in my experience, for plastic lenses). The unit is very light and easy to carry around (you’ll need to find your own case, though, as none is included, but that’s OK with me at this low price).I love that this thing looks like a real microscope. Back when I was a kid, I got a first “student” microscope that was of very good quality and I had years and years of fun with it and feel that it helped spark my interest in nature, science, and the fascinating world around us. This scope may do the same for your young, budding scientist.The microscope is easy to use and has good lighting, much better than many others I’ve seen. I also found this one much easier to focus than some. Again, the image is sharp and clear and a delight at this very low price. It comes with some accessories and a few prepared slides, but I would advise purchasing a small set of good prepared slides so your young one has a better variety of interesting things to look at right away (kids are often impatient as we know).
