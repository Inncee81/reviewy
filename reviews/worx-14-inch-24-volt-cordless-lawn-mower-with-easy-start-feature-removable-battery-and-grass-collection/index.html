    Product Description

The WORX WG775 is a greener, quieter, cleaner and more convenient lawn mower. No gas, no oil, no smelly exhaust – and no cord. This 14-inch cordless mower features a powerful 24-volt battery capable of cutting up to 6,000 square feet of lawn on a single charge. Its compact, lightweight design makes it perfect for small or hard-to-maneuver yards. A simple push of a button gets you on your way, so no more pull strings. A battery power level indicator takes the guesswork out of knowing how much charge is remaining on the battery. The small compact battery is removable and has a carry handle so it can easily be charged in a separate location from the mower. This mower offers the flexibility of three different cutting options with three adjustable cut heights ranging from 2 to 3.5 inches. The mulching feature chops and distributes clippings evenly to help the lawn hold moisture and nutrients. The rear discharge allows clippings to be swept quickly from the cutting chamber. And the included grass-catching bag allows grass and leaves to be conveniently collected and removed via the bag’s oversized handle. There’s even an indicator valve that shows when the bag is completely full.

I call him SQUIRT

This little lawnmower has given me my life back! I can now mow my lawn with ease. It is actually FUN to do. I was paying $20 a week to have someone mow my lawn because my big gas powered machine hurt me. I have arthritis and have good and bad bone days. I decided to buy a cordless, and did a lot of online research, watched videos, etc. Amazon offered a great deal on this little machine, so I decided to take a chance. I love this little guy. Piece of cake to put together! I even named him SQUIRT. He does an amazing job. I power off, sit and rest, hit the key and get back to work. I use the mulch mode, and it discharges from the back, so there is no mess to clean up after. I also don’t have to use my weed whacker, because of the small size of this mower, I can get as close to the edge as possible, so nothing left to trim! Also, it is so quiet I mowed at 7:00 a.m. when my yard was completely shaded. My neighbors didn’t even know! I couldn’t be happier and would highly recommend this little gem for anyone who cannot handle pushing a regular gas powered lawn mower. My gas powered machine is self-propelled, but that doesn’t help when you have to go backwards, which happens a lot in my yard. I love SQUIRT! Can’t wait to use him again!
