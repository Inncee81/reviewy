    Product Description

Elite’s self-adjusting Custom Race Water Bottle Cage lets you focus on the road rather than fighting a poor bottle/cage interface. Constructed from fiber-reinforced polyamide makes for a light and durable bottle cage. The key part, however is an elastomer rubber that allows the Custom Race to not only expand, adapting to different bottle shapes, but it also absorbs vibration too. The Custom Race Water Bottle Cage comes in White/USA Graphics, White/Italian, Gloss White/Blue, Chrome White, and Chrome Black.

Fit too tight for standard camlebacks

My standard camelback bottles don’t fit- the cage is way too tight and you have to put real effort to get the bottles in there (and out). Maybe they will loosen up over time, but I’d like to be able to hydrate in the meantime, so returned to amazon it goes

I love all the options of colors and am very happy …

This is a solid and lightweight cage. I was a little skeptical at first because I have always had metal ones, but this one seems to been quality enough that it will hold up with a lot of use. I love all the options of colors and am very happy with how this item looks in person. The design of it makes it very easy to get the water bottle in and out easily. It fits around a water bottle tightly so I am not worried about it flying out. It does not come with the screws to secure it to your bike frame. Fortunately I had some laying around from an old bike so it wasn’t too much of an inconvenience. Besides not coming with screws, the only real issue (and it’s not even that huge) is the pointy edges on the top of the part that actually holds your water bottle. I have a cruiser bike to the water bottle sits more in front of me a few inches below my handle bars, between my knees when I am peddling. Every once in awhile the inside of my leg would brush the pointy part of the holder. My husband has one of these bottle cages also, which is located in the “normal” location, and he does not have any issues. As of now, I don’t hit the holder enough for me to warrant getting a different one, but just be aware that it might be a little bit of an issue depending on where the location of your cage is.
