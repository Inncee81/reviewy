Surprisingly good!

I bought this solar panel, my first purchase of any solar panel, to use on a boat to keep the battery up. A couple weeks after the purchase and installation a storm blew through and the boat filled up with water. The solar panel was now sitting face up, on a seat, under 4” to 8” of water. The same storm devastated the trees around my home so I was involved with that for quite a while, leaving the boat to languish in a floating-but-swamped condition. After I got around to pumping it out, I went back to the tree issues and the boat again filled up with water in a second storm, covering the panel once again. Now, about two months after the first storm, I repaired the boat’s trailer and pulled the boat out of the water. To my great surprise the panel was still generating electricity, and the battery (which was under water whenever the panel was under water) was fully charged. I was shocked - something that rarely happens.Note that I had not installed the charge controller, so this review says nothing about that item, just the panel.
