Does not fit C135 Need to edit out waterproof description

The only reason I am giving this a two star rating is because it was automatically advertised along with the underwater camera (Kodak C135) I purchased and in it’s description they state it is ultra-waterproof.Have to say it is a decently constructed camera case, but it does not fit the C135 model very well at all (camera has to be inserted in a specific direction and you have to really work to get the zips to close all the way). Thus not easy to get the camera in and out quickly.As well, as you can see in the product picture, the zippers only come up to and stop at the edges of the black, fold-over Velcro flap, thus if someone is purchasing as an “ultra-waterproof” camera case they are going to be disappointed. Water would easily ingress into that area. In my case, the camera is an underwater model, so it didn’t matter that the advertising is off. Wouldn’t even call it waterproof to be honest.I only paid $6, so not a major loss, but just wanted to state that it does not fit the C135, and do not purchase if you need a waterproof case!

Very poor quality

Should have known considering the price, but it was suggested to be paired with the Kodak C135 underwater digital camera. Upon opening this HDE case from the loose, plastic wrapper, I could already tell it was poor quality. Mind you, I wasn’t expecting much for $6, but this is ridiculous. I attempted to fit the camera inside the case…which barely fit…when I removed the camera the entire liner on the inside of the case came out with it. This liner is of such poor quality that I would worry about it scratching my screen or lens anyway. Lastly, once the wrist lanyard was on the camera, it would not fit at all.I would love to return this item, strictly because of quality issues, but it would cost me as much as the case did just to return it (not to mention the “damage” of the liner pulling out). Very disappointed even if it was only $6.

A little snug, but otherwise good for the price

Good case, nice color. Purchased for my dad to go along with his Kodak Easyshare. Minus 1 star because the case is actually a little tight for this camera and I think it was meant to be a case for something else. Also as far as I know there is no wrist strap or anything like that. However, 4 stars because my dad is hard to please, and he always thinks most colors other than black are “feminine,” and it’s really hard to buy him things that he will actually use. And for some reason, he really likes and actually uses this case. Great price, but if you have a little more money you might want to get something officially licensed for this camera so the size isn’t so snug.

You always get what you pay for, this one worked out!

This product is very basic, and for the CHEAP cost, it was exactly what I expected. I am happy with it. It looks nice and I am confident taht it is good enough to protect the camera.If you want something super high quality, you can easily pay TEN TIMES OR MORE, but again, for a few bucks this did the job just fine!

Does not fit Kodak Easyshare Sport C123

Despite being offered as part of a package with the Kodak Easysport C123 camera, the camera does not fit in the one I received. It’s too small and tight, and cannot be zipped closed with the camera inside, especially with the wrist strap attached. A small pocket inside would hold a CD card, but again, useless with this camera.A close reading of the cameras it fits in the product description does not mention the C123. So beware.Still, it’s a nice case, a bargin, so I’ll keep if for future use if/when I ever get a camera small enought to fit.
