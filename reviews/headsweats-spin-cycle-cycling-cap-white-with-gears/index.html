This thing is huge!

This cap is supposedly one size fits all, and technically it fits me, but it just sort of falls around my head. It is elastic all the way around, so I would expect it to be smaller and stretchy, like other cycling caps I have. I primarily wear it under a helmet, so I can set it on my head and put my helmet over the top, and it works fine. It is just a bit of a hassle to get situated. So, if you have a large head, this thing is great. If not, it isn’t bad, but you may want to look for something else.
