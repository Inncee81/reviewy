Product Reviews

Does the job and was easy to install.

Installation was easy… I did NOT remove my TV from it’s current wall mount (I just didn’t want to mess with moving the whole TV).I removed one of the upper screws (near the top of the TV) attaching the wall mount to the TV, added a spacer from the soundbar mount and reinstalled the screw. Then I moved to the upper screw on the other side and added the spacer there and replaced the screw.Next I removed one of the lower screws that attached the TV to the wall mount and pushed the soundbar mount between the back of the TV and the wall mount. I lined up the screw with the hole on the soundbar mount I thought would work best and then replaced the screw. I then repeated the process on the other side’s lower screw attachment making sure to attach the soundbar mount in the same hole on both sides.Next I attached the other pieces of the soundbar mount to the soundbar and then attached them to the pieces already mounted to the TV. I just had to do a little adjusting to get it evenly spaced (I did NOT have to remove the mount and put the screws into a different hole) and then tighten down all the screws. Works perfect!I have a 55” Samsung TV and a Klipsch soundbar in case that helps anyone.UPDATED – well, I don’t know if I’m just not strong enough to tighten bolts or what BUT the sound bar has shifted a little so I had to readjust it and tighten the screws down again… hope that doesn’t happen again or I’ll have to take my review down a star!
