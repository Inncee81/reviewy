Proffessional Grade Quality, Very Impressed

I installed 2 of these brackets today. One in my bathroom and one in my basement. They are made of steel, which is heavy. You can tell you have a quality product when you pick it up. The bracket comes with 2 anchors, 2 mounting screws, and a mounting bolt that is hand screwed into the back of the SONOS: Play 1 speaker, which hooks onto the bracket once it’s mounted on the wall. It also comes with nice little screw covers which go over the screw heads once the bracket is mounted so you can’t see the screws. You will need a 1⁄4” drill bit, a cordless drill, and a hammer to install the anchors. The screws are phillips head so you’ll need a phillips head screwdriver to screw in the screws and make adjustments. It’s very simple.I originally had the SONOS: Play 1 speaker downstairs sitting on a table next to my workout bench. I have a finished basement btw, with carpet and a finished ceiling. I mention that because it may affect the quality of sound if you don’t have drywall or carpet in your basement and plan to put one of these down there you may get some echo. Mounting this speaker to the wall not only clears up space and allows me to get rid of that table, but also seems to do a better job of filling the room with sound since the speaker is mounted a bit higher, and points down.The bracket swivels up, down, left and right. You can point the speaker in any direction you want. The nice thing about it is that you can then tighten the bracket with a phillips head screwdriver so that whichever position you want the speaker in it will stay that way. This bracket has also allowed me to mount this speaker in my bathroom close to the ceiling, clear of any water splashes during vigorous showers :), and I LOVE to listen to music in the shower. Since the SONOS:Play1 is moisture resistant, I can shower stress free with super hot water like I like it knowing the speaker is going to be just fine mounted there on my wall.I would highly recommend this bracket for anyone looking to clear up some space with their sonos system or even if you’re looking for a more tidy look. I can see myself purchasing several more of these in the future.
