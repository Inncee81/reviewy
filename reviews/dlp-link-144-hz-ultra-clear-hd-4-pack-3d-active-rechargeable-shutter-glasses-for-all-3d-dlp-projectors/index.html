Great price and great quality

These 3D glasses are perfect. They don’t need to be charged very often, and I have yet to have one issue with them. They sync up instantly and work just as I would expect. Reviews of other brands complained that the glasses were too dark, but I don’t find that to be the case with these at all. They are not perfectly clear, but the tint is barely noticeable. I increased the brightness just a few ticks up on my projectors 3D profile setting and it’s really hard to tell the difference. I just wish that Amazon carried a kids size in these. I have been using a clip on strap to help them fit my 4 year olds head, which isn’t the best solution, but works for now. Every pair comes with a bag, a cleaning cloth, and a charging cable. the cleaning cloths work pretty well, as my son likes to put fingerprints and popcorn butter all over the lenses. So far no issues. One thing I might consider doing is getting a multi-port charger so I don’t have these all hanging off of one of my laptops when they need charging.
