GREAT fit for Epson 99W sized projectors!

We ordered another bag made by Epson for their 99W projector – it was loose, cheap, fit poorly and didn’t have any pocket. Returned it and ordered this one to try. It will fit any projector 10” x 12” x 3.5-4” perfectly! Nicely padded (not thick) and the extra pocket is perfect for cords & remote. Note the entire projector compartment zips open (2 zippers) but generally you’ll only unzip the top flap to store the projector.

Fits My BenQ HT1075 Projector Well

I have the BenQ HT1075 projector and this case fits snuggle. The added side zip pouch holds the power cord and my 25 foot HDMI cable. The build is good and the handle and strap work well. My only complaint, I wish it had more padding. It has some, but it seem a little shy of what I feel is needed.

Probably a 3.5, but needs more padding

This is actually a very good projector carry case for what it is designed for. It is padded and probably appropriately padded. However, this is not padded enough for me. This is a small back pack size or a large purse. It is padded and fits the designed BenQ projector perfectly, but I can’t add anything else except what it came with and the padding is appropriate but not designed for a fall from a height or off of something tall. Otherwise, it’s good.

BenQ 5J.J3T09.001 Projector Soft Carrying Case fits Mitsubishi HC 1500

Just received this carrying case and it fits my Mitsubishi HC1500 very nicely, snug and not too tight. The outside pocket is enough for my power cord, remote, manual and MiniDVI-to-HDMI cord; it might be possible to get some other small items in. This a soft case and the padding is sufficient. I gave it a “4” rating because I would prefer a bit more padding, but I am satisfied with it as it is. With everything in, it is nice and compact. I would recommend this case.
