    Product Description

Preserve all of life’s special moments and get the most out of your digital camera or camcorder with Samsung’s EVO 16 GB SD Memory Card. This high-performance memory card features read speeds of up to 48 MB/s for quickly transferring high-resolution photos and videos and is UHS-1 enabled to capture Full HD video. Waterproof, temperature proof, shockproof, magnet proof, and X-ray proof, this reliable memory card ensures that memories of your vacation, wedding, or child’s graduation remain safe.

Designed for the Latest Digital Cameras and Camcorders This ultra-high-speed memory card is ideal for your DSLR camera or full HD camcorder. It offers support for photographers and anyone who needs to capture split-second action shots and quickly transfer them to their PCs or other devices.

Shoot Seamless Full HD Video Capture special moments as vividly as you experience them. This memory card is Class 10 and UHS Speed Class 1 rated, which means it records smooth Full HD video.

Fast Read and Write Speeds for Better Performance Fast read speeds of up to 48 MB/s make transferring high-resolution images from your DSLR or camcorder to your laptop or tablet PC efficient. The memory card’s write speeds let you capture shots twice as fast as conventional SD cards, making the card ideal for fast-action photography. When shooting in continuous burst mode, you can capture up to 360 photos (8 MB) per minute.

Large Storage Capacity Holds More Memories The card’s generous 16 GB capacity holds up to 9,310 photos (8-megapixel) or 270 minutes of HD video. This means you can shoot longer without stopping to download or having to switch cards.

Built to Withstand Extreme Conditions This waterproof and temperature-proof card can withstand up to 24 hours in seawater and in temperatures ranging from -13 to 185 degrees F. It also protects your data from damage caused by airport X-ray machines, resists up to 13 times the magnetic force of home theater speakers, and can survive being run over by a 1.6-ton vehicle.

Great SD card for cameras!

I have a Nikon D60 and was getting tired of having to swap out my cards every hundred shots or so. With this 16GB SD card, that problem is history. I don’t have to worry about carrying multiple cards with me, I get everything on one card and then transfer it all on my computer daily.Speaking of transfers - FAST FAST FAST! I use a multicard reader on my computer and this Samsung 16GB SD card flies through transfers with ease! I used to slap one of my old cards (1gb) in, go to transfer, grab a coffee and come back to find them almost finished. Now, with 16x the size, it’s done before I get my coffee! WOW!I love the performance, the size and most of all, I love Samsung for making some seriously reliable flash drive storage options. I’ve been a Kingston fan for a long time, but recently (past couple of years) their longevity has taken a hit. They work great, but they die after a year or two. These Samsung products have been going strong for years and they’ve even made me a convert with their SSD line - they’re just that good.They may not be the fastest on the market (There’s one or two out there that can beat them on transfer speeds) but for quality AND speed? You’re not going to touch them - especially at these prices!

It feels heavier and it appears thicker. Here’s hoping I’ll never have to test that.2. It records 1080p video without any hiccups or issues. I’ll update my review if it dies and adjust my rating accordingly but if you’re reading this and it’s 5 stars, it’s still alive and kicking. To test it, I’ll be leaving it in my camera and giving my Transcend a break.Cons1. Totally thought this was a MicroSD. They look freaking identical. That is, of course, my bad so no stars off…but still, maybe like a different color or something.2. Didn’t come with a carrying case or any peripherals. I know the read/writes don’t match up to others and that sucks if you’re really into numbers (and I’m a tech nerd so you’d think it would bug me..but it doesn’t) but it writes fast enough to record the largest files I can produce and that means it’s good enough for me and for most people.
