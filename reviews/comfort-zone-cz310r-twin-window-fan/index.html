Quietly does its job

This fan is surprisingly quiet on the two lower speeds and moves a good bit of air. It’s lightweight, but seems sturdy enough. Despite the manufacturer’s claims, it will not fit a 36-inch window. Even in my 34-inch window, there are small gaps at each end. The maximum opening it will actually span is about 33 inches. The remote is easier to use than the push buttons, which are the same color as the surrounding plastic and hard to see. With such a reasonable price, it seems like a bargain to me.

… 2016 for our window as a means of pulling cool air into our room beyond just having the window …

We bought this fan in May 2016 for our window as a means of pulling cool air into our room beyond just having the window cracked whether we were pulling the cool air in from outside during the colder May and June nights in the California desert or just aiding our evaporative (swamp) cooler in cooling our room off. This fan really helped a significant amount to draw the cool air from our evaporative (swamp) cooler into our room by means of venting the air out all summer long even when the outside temp was reaching or beyond 115⁄120 f all summer. Kept room at least 15 to 25 degrees cooler than the previous years, where normally we’d be a little stifling because of the room being a mid day to afternoon sun facing room, this room is nearly always hotter than the rest of the house.Sadly the lower fan (the one that changes directions on circulate) stopped working today exactly 4 months after purchase. Great product, perhaps not meant to deal with the heavy dust that the desert is inclined to have.
