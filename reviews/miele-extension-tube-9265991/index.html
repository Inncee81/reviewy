Bad design. Fails with age and use. Cloggs on exposed internal cord.

Horrible design. they changed it from the original. In the original the internal cord, for driving the power head, was enclosed. This new one has the internal cord, coiled and exposed within the tube. As it ages the coil loosens up and starts to extend out into the vacuumed tube air space. It collects dirt and dust and ends up clogging. It also makes it impossible to use in all but the most extended position. The original that came with the vacuum broke at the head and I purchased this replacement. It started failing just after a year of use. This is obviously the weak link in an otherwise fine vacuum cleaner system. As there is no alternative, that I can find, that will supply the power needed for the head you are pretty much stuck with this. For that reason I would not recommend a Miele canister vacuum cleaner to anyone looking for long life use.Hard to get a good photo with my cell phone but you can see, in the uploaded photo, the crap stuck in the tube that you cannot be remove as it is stuck to the internal cord.
