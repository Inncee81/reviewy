A nice case that gets the job done.

Well made case for the Galaxy View which has a very odd shape. I feel so much better now that I have an actual place to store my Galaxy View when not in use. However, you will not be able to completely close the case if you put the charger in the front pocket.When inserting your tablet in the case, I recommend putting your charger at the bottom first. Then hold the tablet by the handle and begin to lower it into the case. Once the bottom of the tablet enters the case, switch the tablet position (to the standing position). This way, the charger will be comfortably between the legs of the tablet.I hope that wasn’t confusing, but I feel that’s the best way to put both the charger and the tablet in the case. I would only use the pocket for small items such as pens, calculators, or maybe a smartphone.The only thing that could make this better was if it had a handle on the top rather than the one on the back. You could possibly add one if you wanted to. If you have a Galaxy View and in need of some way to store it or carry it, I recommend giving this a try.
