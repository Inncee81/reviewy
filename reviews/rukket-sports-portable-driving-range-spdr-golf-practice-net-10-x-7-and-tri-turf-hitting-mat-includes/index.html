It is about 10 ft wide by 7.5 ft tall, seems a little to short but we have hit it only once or twice close to the top, nothing ever goes over. Two problems are that the wings are attached by nots, which do hold it up but their are some gaps between the main net and wings. I recommend buying another mat that is non slip and also can hold a tee.All in all this is a great product recommend it to everyone!
