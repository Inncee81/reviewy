    Product Description

Down and dirty. A simple, durable glove for riding trails, jumps, berms and anything else you can find. The DVD (Down And Dirty) is all about the essentials - fit, durability and control. Built to be affordable and long-wearing with a supple feel thanks to the Super Fit engineered AX Suede palm that eliminates excess material and bunching. The upper breathes well, and offers 4-way stretch more maximum comfort day-in and day-out.

I usually wear a size L glove, but gave my original pair of large DNDs to my girlfriend, who usually wears a M. I also considered returning my XL’s for a XXL, but ended up keeping them since crits are pretty short. Hopefully they’ll also stretch a bit once they’re broken in.Update: After about a season of use, these gloves started to rip along the seam of the pull tab on the palm.
