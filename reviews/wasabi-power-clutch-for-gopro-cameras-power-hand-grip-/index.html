    Product Description

Get the power you need in the palm of your hand with the Wasabi Power CLUTCH power hand grip. Featuring 5200mAh of extra battery storage, the CLUTCH functions as a portable power bank, ready to go when you need it. It’s the perfect companion for all-day adventures when you’d rather be focused on filming instead of swapping batteries.

Compatible with all GoPro cameras with a skeleton or frame housing to provide USB access (housing not included).

Not for use in wet conditions.

Specifications: Weight: 6.4 oz Length: 8 inches Input: Micro USB, 5V, 1A Output: USB, 5V, 1A Capacity: 5200mAh

Includes: 1 x CLUTCH Power Hand Grip 1 x Wrist Strap – Adjustable and Removable 1 x Thumb Screw 1 x Charging Cable – USB to Micro-USB 1 x Charging Cable – USB to Mini-USB

Product Reviews

A must for GoPro HERO Session

This thing is awesome. Seriously. You don’t even need a selfie stick when you have a battery pack and selfie stick in one. I have a GoPro Hero 5 Session, and I’m always worried about the battery capacity for such a small camera. I don’t worry anymore because this charger can charge the GoPro about five times. The only thing I would recommend is to buy a short USB cable that matches your GoPro camera; then you will be able to charge and record at the same time no problem. The grip isn’t too big for my hands, but people with smaller hands may feel uncomfortable holding it. This unit doesn’t turn on automatically, you have to push the button on the side, which turns on the small LEDs on the side, then you will see the charging LED on your camera light up. You do have to be careful that this unit does not get too wet because the flap leave the unit vulnerable to water when you are using the USB port. Anyway, this thing is great and a must-have for GoPro owners who are worried about their battery life. I’ve even used this thing to charge my phone! Highly recommend!
