Not bad for the price

If you are one of those people who likes high-quality sound and digital resolution, this TV may not be for you. But if you are someone looking for a less expensive way to have a decent-sized TV/DVD player, then this will probably fit your needs. I happen to be someone who wasn’t necessarily looking for great sound and a perfect picture. The sound level goes up sufficiently for my needs on this unit and the picture is acceptable. It’s a great buy on an extra TV. I’m using it as a DVD player and am happy with my purchase so far. NOTE: The neck of stand is actually kept inside a recess in the stryofoam packing. Don’t throw the styrofoam packing away until you have carefully looked for the neck piece of the stand. (The bottom piece is easily found, as it is packaged with the instructions.)

Affordable the best 24” DVD combo TV

Bought this Sceptre 720p DVD combo TV for $119.99 wall-mounted for my bedroom, no complaints so far.Prime shipping with 2 day delivery well double boxed by Amazon and Sceptre.Pictures are great, built-in DVD player pretty easy to use with simple DVD insertion/ejection. Sound is more than good enough for my bedroom where I usually watch DVD, Netflix and play some console games.I see some comments mentioning not being able to assemble the base piece. It won’t just accept it like other players

A lot of people have said that the DVD player doesn’t work. Well, I thought that too until I tried the source button and chose DVD. Your disc WILL NOT go in unless you choose DVD first. It won’t just accept it like other players. It plays just fine and the tv screen is crystal clear. I’m happy with it so far.

Love this TV

I bought this tv with the built in dvd player for our RV. I knew when we went camping we wouldn’t always have very good tv reception so I knew we’d need the dvd player. The speakers are great, the dvd player works great, and we were able to get many channels in the middle of nowhere. All I had to buy was the HD hook up cord that cost $2 whole dollars. The sound is clear and I was very impressed. This was a great investment.
