    Product Description

A daytrip with friends? A day full of quick snapshots? This Day Holster mimics your compact system camera’s shape and size to provide barely-there but ample protection. Designed to attach to your camera’s neck strap, the Day Holster dangles weightlessly from its own tether when you’re ready to start your photo shoot.

Nice small case, fits Nikon L820

I bought this case for my Nikon L820. I was worried it would be a little small, but it’s a perfect fit.The case has an external pocket, but with no closure so I would be reluctant to use it for anything more than perhaps a microfiber cleaning cloth. Inside, there’s a zipper pocket in the top flap, and a small side compartment which is a suitable size for a four-pack of AA batteries.The reasons for only four stars are that for the price, it could be a little more plush; more padding on the sides, and perhaps metal D-rings for the neck strap.

Fits my new Nikon L-340 perfectly

Fits like a soft, comfortable glove for my new Nikon L-340 camera, which was shipped in the same order. My recent history with cameras has been checkered the past few years. I dropped a less pricey camera and broke it. Another time I got a camera wet, so another wasted $80.I wanted to protect this investment right from the start. So far so good, with no pilot error to report so far. The very soft case fits like it’s made specifically for this camera. If the dimensions of your camera are very close to the dimensions of my L-340, then you should be fine.

Just The Right Size

You’d think it would be easy to find a small case that houses a Canon PowerShot G15 on a cord, a couple of batteries, and a spare memory card. I searched high and low, but with this singular exception, the cases were either too large or too small. And this case is designed so that stuff can be compartmentalized and thus protected. I also find the quality of materials and construction to be superior. Heartily recommended.
