Better than the included Complys, in certain situations.

For $7, if you’ve lost or destroyed your previous (included) set of these tips, and you’re just tired of the added hassle of using the Comply tips (which are pretty great, so long as you don’t mind the whole squeeze-roll-insert-hold technique of getting them properly seated), these are great. There are 6 sets of varying style and size, so you’re sure to find something that fits and seals properly. If you’re getting weaker-than-normal bass response - like I was at first - try other sizes. Heck, I found out I really should be using the large single-flange in my right ear, and the medium single-flange in my left!These definitely offer pros and cons vs. the Complys:Pros:* Easier to install, as it’s just stick-em-in-and-go* Comfy enough to wear for extended periods of time, so long as you have the right size on* Lighter weight* More resilient (those Complys just love to tear!)* Easier to cleanCons:* Don’t seal out sound as well. They still do a great job with the right size, but it’s not quite as good as the Complys, especially in louder environments.* Bass is subdued a bit, again due to the not-quite-as-tight seal these form with the inside of your ear canalI lost my original set in a move about a year ago. Since then, and even before, I’ve been rocking the Complys, because the M6 Pros came with a pair that fit beautifully, and I went ahead and bought another set of 3 pairs at some point (for like $12!). Thing about the Complys is 1) they’re more of a PITA to get seated properly in your ear, 2) they gunk up a LOT faster than these silicone ones (and are a lot harder to clean), and 3) when I got sweaty with the Complys, no amount of removing, drying, and re-inserting got them to sit quite right again. With these silicone ones, if you’re out mowing the lawn, or riding a bike, or going for a run, or whatever sweaty activity you like to do with IEMs in, a quick wipe-down and a reinsertion is all that’s needed for a decent, fairly secure seat.For me, having the options was the real reason I bought these, and I’ve been very happy with them for the past few days. Doesn’t mean I won’t switch back to the Complys, but for now, they were well worth the price of admission.
