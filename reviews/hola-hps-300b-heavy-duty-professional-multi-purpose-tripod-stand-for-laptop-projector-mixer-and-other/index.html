SOLID is an understatement.

This stand is well constructed with thick tubing (1 3⁄8” - 1 1⁄2” thick). Top plate is removable & everything locks together nicely. Weighs in at 11 pounds! If only it had a locking pin for the plate, you could stand on it (not recommended), but otherwise without a doubt stand should hold just about anything you can put on it. Stand is no joke and makes me chuckle when I see it. Love it!

Absolutely perfect!

We have an old Panasonic data projector, the kind you would have normally mounted from the ceiling or something. It does not have the female threaded receptacle for camera tripods, and would probably be too heavy for those anyway.This stand is perfect! It’s heavy duty, easy to set up and take down, portable…and its platform for the projector is just the right size. This is a very well-made product!

Really sturdy, well-built stand for 40 dollars

This is a surprisingly solid stand. 40mm diameter steel lower members and a 35mm diameter upper section. The tray is really well built - steel sheet measures out slightly thicker than 16ga with a steel clamp. The two lower clamps are plastic. The lower section is similar to the Pyle speaker stand. On my order, the tether which retains the lock pin was torn and the pin was rolling around in the packaging. I couldn’t see shipping this back to Amazon, so I bought a section of steel chain at Home Depot for $0.75. I use this stand to hold my 15.6” laptop while I train on my indoor trainer - it works great.
