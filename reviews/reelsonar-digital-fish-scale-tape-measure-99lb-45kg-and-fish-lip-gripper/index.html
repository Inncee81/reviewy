    Product Description

The ReelSonar digital scale with measuring tape is compact and accurate. This digital scale offers high end features at a value price. Measures in both pounds and kilograms up to 99lb / 45kg. Bright, backlit LCD screen is easy to read in bright light and at night and features a gentle fish lip grabber that prevents harm to your catch. Ideal for tournament situations and for just making sure that record catch is really a record! Works for all species including bass, catfish, panfish/crappie, walleye, musky, salmon, trout and saltwater fish and for all types of anglers including fly fishermen, trolling, bass boat, kayak, canoe, shore, dock or pier.
