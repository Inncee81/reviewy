Easy to use & Fast

I purchased this pump to inflate our pool floats during the summer. It’s small and compact, but it fills the floats fast. The pump includes a few nozzle sizes for different plugs. The deflate feature also comes in handy at the end of the pool season. The floats deflated almost as fast as they inflated. I’m happy with this pump and would definitely recommend it.

Does what it is supposed to; works pretty well for a small pump at a reasonable price

The advertised pump is an Intex pump which I purchased to inflate a fairly large Intex pool (see pictures). I received a similar pump by Coleman. Although it is not the same as the picture, they are essentially the same type of product, a small electric pump, and I cannot comment on whether one brand is better than the other.I found the pump to be effective for blowing up my pool. It comes with 3 different attachments: a large round one, a medium sized with pointed tip, and a smaller one with pointed tip. The pool I was blowing up also had 3 different sized valves for various parts of the pool. The main part was wide and open, about 1 inch+ across and the other 2 sizes were small like you would find on normal sized floats and a slightly bigger one like you would find on larger sized floats. The attachments fit perfectly in the largest hole and the bigger valve, however, there was not a good fit for the smaller valve. I was able to make it work, but in the future I will be purchasing a smaller attachment for more efficient and better results.It also took a very long time to inflate. I expected it to take some time considering it is a small pump, but it took much longer than I thought it would. If you have a large inflatable and do not have patience, you may want to look for a more powerful pump.Overall, it does what it is supposed to do and works pretty well for a small pump at a reasonable price.

Quick, handy inflater

Bought and used this handy pump recently to inflate a sofa/bed item. It inflated the sofa which is in 3 sections, very quickly! Only took about 7 minutes to inflate! I was impressed! It’s a bit loud but I don’t think there are any quiet air pumps and it does the job fast so not a big deal. Haven’t figured out how to use it to deflate though. There are no specific instructions regarding that. The top part of the pump is the “deflater” but try as I might with that and nozzle, the sofa would not deflate. The first time deflating I ended up just using the nozzle part in the valve of the sofa without turning on the pump! The second time my hubby found out the thing can be deflated simply by unscrewing the valve - the air comes out “by itself”! Who knew? LOL So, anyway, I recommend because it’s really handy and I like it doesn’t need batteries like our previous one!
