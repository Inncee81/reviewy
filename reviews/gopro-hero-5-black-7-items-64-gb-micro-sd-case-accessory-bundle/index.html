Buy a waterproof case before using in water

Do yourself a favor and buy a waterproof case before taking this camera to any depth in water. Mine happened to have a defective seal somewhere and began to leak in shallow water (no deeper than 4ft) and the back LCD screen got ruined. These type of leaks are common for this camera based on what I’ve read elsewhere. If I ever get a replacement I will not use it in water without a waterproof case.Other than the leaking issue I liked the camera’s picture and video quality. The memory card that it came with was a Sandisk 64GB MicroSD card which is more than sufficient for what I use the camera for.
