    Product Description

Color:Black Many of us want to protect our business& home and are looking for a cost effective solution to monitor them while we are not there. It can notify us when someone enters the area they shouldn’t, capture images of events that take place while we are away, give us a view of what is going on , and keep an eye on pets, children,and the elderly.

We are familiar with traditional CCTV surveillance attached to a VCR or DVR,but many of the modern DVR do not allow you to do what we mentioned above. What’s worse ,it requires expensive wiring and takes too much electricity without HD quality images on our televisions,computers and cell phones.

Now,the “CCTV” (Closed Circuit Television) camera, while still in use, is gradually being replaced by another more efficient digital product—wireless IP camera.Wireless IP cameras provide an easy-to-install and easy-to-control solution for video surveillance. 

Now,I would like to recommend KeeKoon’s IP camera for everyone!

Benefits of KeeKoon IP camera include:

Remote administration from any location.

2.0 Megapixel/1080P HD Wired/Wireless IP Camera

IR Cut-off Filter for true color (no color cast)

Night Vision (up to 8 meters) via auto IR-LED illumination

Two-way communication.

90°wide-angle advanced glass lens combined with 340° horizontal & 90° vertical rotation range creates a complete 360° coverage.

The ability to send alerts if any suspicious activity is detected.

Support PC (Windows, Mac), Tablet (iPad, Android), Smartphone (iOS, Android) to monitor and control from anywhere at anytime.
