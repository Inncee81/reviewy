    Product Description

Resistant Unibody Design Let broken cables be a thing of the past with this sturdy RAVPower charging cable. Both the Type-C and USB-A connectors sport a resistant one-cast metal construction that allows for seamless insertions without fearing that the connector might tear apart from the TPE housing. Military-Grade Durability While securely hosting the cable’s high-strength, durable internal wires, the TPE soft-touch insulation features some reinforced stress points that provide a long-lasting 8000+ bend lifespan. The reversible Type-C connector conveniently inserts any which way for no more fumbling around. Safe Charging for Your Devices Unlike other cables that’ll convey just as much voltage as the output provides – despite this being excessive and hazardous for your device, the built-in resistor of value 56kΩ prevents overvoltage during USB-A – USB Type-C connections for safer charging for both you and your tech. Speedy 480Mb/s Data Transfer Spend less time waiting and more time doing with the USB 2.0 standard transfer speed. This provides a high bandwidth up to 480Mb/s so that you can seamlessly import / export all files at your smartphone’s maximum transfer speed.
