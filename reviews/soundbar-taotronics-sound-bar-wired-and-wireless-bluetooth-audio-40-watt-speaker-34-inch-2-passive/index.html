    Product Description

Color:Black Connect It Your Way What do traditional devices like TVs, PCs and DVDs have in common with in-demand Bluetooth gadgetry? Both can connect to the sound bar for unparalleled convenience and usability. The sound bar allows wired connections included LINE IN, Optical, Coaxial, and RCA IN so practically every device can connect; while your favorite smartphones, tablets, and other Bluetooth devices have the option to connect wirelessly.

Touch Controls Misplaced the remote? Never fear because the sound bar is fully operational from the unit itself. Adorning the side is a high grade metal control unit that responds to the slightest touch allowing you to adjust the volume, select tracks, or play and pause music. And when you do find the remote, it can also do the same.

Place In Any Space As versatile as it powerful, the TaoTronics sound bar can be placed directly under your television or mounted to the accompanying wall to reduce clutter and save space. No matter how you choose to place it, rich clear sound will reach all around the room.

Contemporary Design A sound bar adds a modern and exciting touch to any home, elevating your standard living room into a personal theatre system so you’ll never watch a film anywhere else. Sitting low and stretching long, the sound bar uses tough ABS plastic and is covered by a soft touch cloth ensuring you’ll have great sound for years to come.

The Taotronics TV Sound Bar Experience Review

Hi,This is my review for the product listed on Amazon as:TaoTronics Sound Bar Bluetooth AudioI have taken some photos and done a video demo for anyone who is interested.Massive product packaging for a long, slender, quality 20W sound bar.The packaging was well thought out and professional. The appearance of the boxing on the outside is similar to what you’d find at a Fry’s or Best Buy Electronics Store in your area. It’s a sturdy cardboard that keeps the inside components well protected. Even further to that protection, inside, the sound bar is cushioned for shock absorption with foam inserts on the sides and center of the sound bar. So even with the evil delivery folks who may toss around a product or drop the box from standing position to your front door, you’ll be OK that the sound bar doesn’t get affected in any way in terms of damage. The sound bar is wrapped in a plastic bag to cover it from dust and there’s removal adhesives on the sides of the sound bar for covering up from scratches or dust.For this product, I went through the 8 page English section of the Multilanguage user manual to understand the contents and setup. The user manual confirms the design specs to validate what you receive as well.In looking at the package, I was happy to see a small remote with easy to understand buttons labelled to operate the sound bar. There’s also a provided RCA cable for those who have old television models to plug in as wel. But most modern televisions have optical inputs or coaxial. Unfortunately, Taotronics only provided in the package at the time the RCA cable. My TV used for demo had an optical input which is a better quality option and that along with the coaxial and AUX inputs were not provided cables for each in the package. I would like for them to provide these accessories in future model releases. Fortunately, I found an optical cable in my storage for use and it worked fine for this sound bar. You’ll need to remove the protective dust cap from that input to plug into the sound bar.In terms of looking around at the sound bar, it’s long, but it isn’t very wide, so it won’t take up a lot of space should you do what I do, which is leave it on top of a shelf space to not deal with mounting it on the wall and poking holes on your dry walls.It’s also lightweight. I can grip and hold from the center and lift up with one hand and I’m not a very strong guy. So this makes it easier to manage and transporting around.There’s a touch panel on the top of the sound bar that controls the power and volume control options if you’re not wanting to use the remote or if the remote battery gets low.The front panel of the sound bar has LED indicators to let you know when the sound bar is in a specific mode of operation, powered on, or on standby. When you control the volume, there’s an Audio LED indicator that flashes so that you know that it’s responsive and triggered a response. The downside that I had about the sound bar is that there’s not a visible volume meter to know how high you’ve cranked up the volume on the sound bar or if it’s reached the max output of volume. I suggest they include that somehow, possibly as a digital display.The backside of the sound bar has all the inputs and mounting sections that you’ll need to get this up and running. One side has the optical and coaxial inputs. The other side has the power plug in input.The bottom of the sound bar has nice and thick rubber stoppers to prevent the sound bar from sliding around easily if you put this on a flat smooth surface. There’s rectangular slices of rubber on both ends of the sound bar.For the sound quality, there weren’t any sports on at the moment to test the crowd noise perception and commentary with sound effects. But I had a DVD of a foreign concert and played it with this sound bar enabled while disabling my TV speaker. Turns out the music quality was great. Clear crisp audio. Bass levels noticeable. Social commentary and music during the listening and testing experience was what I expected for a TV sound bar. No hissing. Using the Taotronics remote, I was able to crank up the volume and sit at the dinner table to enjoy myself around my family watching some performances.Definitely an improvement in sound quality with this sound bar than without it. In fact, everyone needs a TV sound bar for the modern TV’s they buy because TV makers have slimed down the design of the TV and sacrificed audio quality by placing it on the backside where you rely on having the sound bounce against the wall to hear it back at you. Cranking up the TV volume controller used to be a real pain and not enjoyable to watch sports or TV shows. With this TV sound bar, there’s a much better experience with the sound projected towards the front of you like how old TV’s used to be.If there’s anything else to see improve for this sound bar, it’s to allow it to sync up with the TV when you power it ON and power it OFF. There is a standby power saving mode on the speakers, but if I turn on the TV 30 minutes later, the sound bar will not automatically come on to project out the audio. I’ll have to use the Taotronics remote to power on the sound bar which causes some delays and not a quick performance reaction to catch something on TV going on really quick.I hope this helps.
