The mount is very stable and has never fallen off the glass, IF YOU KEEP THE SUCTION CUP SURFACE CLEAN (run it under water for a few seconds, then wipe off any debris). When used in hot weather, it can sometimes be more difficult to remove since the adhesion seems to increase with temperature. The key to removal in hot weather is to carefully peel away from the glass by pulling on the tab on the side of the adhesion/suction disk.I have also found it to be helpful to stick the paper disk packaged with the mount on the suction surface when not installed; it helps keep the disk clean.
