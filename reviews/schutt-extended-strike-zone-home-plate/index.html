Cheap paint!!!

Though this is a very good visual aid for pitchers it is very cheaply made. The paint is peeling off after just three uses. Every time a ball skips off this plate paint gets removed. I sat my batting tee on it during storage for approximately a week. When I pulled the equipment out for use paint from the plate was removed and stuck to the bottom of the hitting tee. I suggest making your own with perhaps cardboard and paint.

Nice product

My daughter is a fastpitch softball pitcher and I had just put a pitching rubber and home plate in the back yard. One of the things I wanted her to work on was hitting her corners and saw this product on Amazon. Th product arrived in no time and did just as it was advertised. I like having the black and brown areas on the outside of the plate because as I am catching it allows me to better describe the location of her pitches. My only complaint about the product would be that if you leave it outside the colors do fade and peel in a relatively short time frame (month and a half).
