This one does not protrude like the other one and that seems better.I do just like the disclaimer about having to test the product out before actually using it. How ever I believe that if your product was manufactured with high standards that there should not be any need to test it out ahead of time.I will update my review if I hear from the seller about the tempered glass versus plastic and if testing it out turns out to be unsuccessful.
