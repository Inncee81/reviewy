Product Reviews

Look Ma, no cords!!

This is such a clever gadget! I love my Dots, but music just sounds sad over her little speaker. This speaker has the quality of a good bluetooth speaker, plus a battery so Alexa can roam free! Setup couldn’t be simpler - drop the Dot into the top and plug it in, that’s it! The speaker connects to the Dot via the 3.5mm aux output so no fussing with Bluetooth or wifi, and you get full quality audio rather than compressed over Bluetooth. The battery connects to the Dot’s micro-USB port, and the speaker unit has a separate micro-USB port for charging. You use the cord you were already using to power your Dot. The sound quality is good. Not Bose-quality, the bass is definitely lacking, but substantially better than the built I speakers. It’s equivalent quality (maybe a little better) than the JBL GO I generally pair to my Dot. It’s also LOUD, so definitely turn your Dot down to sound level 3-4 before you plug it in, or you’ll get a sonic barrage. That could be a down side, as the only volume control is via Alexa - the speaker volume isn’t controllable separately. If you’re looking for the softer end of the spectrum you may want to unplug the speaker for that purpose. One interesting thing I noted was that with the speaker plugged in, Alexa doesn’t stop playing music to answer questions or respond to her name. She decreases the volume slightly, but the music is still clearly audible. Overall, I’m thrilled with this purchase. It makes my Dot so much more functional! I feel like I scored an Echo at half the price!!

Dot/VAUX combo is the best “Echo” device so far

Very impressed by the quality of this speaker. I have a Bose SoundLink Mini II hooked up to a Dot in another part of the house and while the Bose speaker has better fidelity (it better since it cost more than 4 times as much) it’s not that far off. The speaker’s design and aesthetic is excellent. A second generation Dot sits almost flush with the top of the speaker making it look like it’s part of the speaker rather than something that doesn’t belong. The ability to grab the speaker and take it with you is huge. Whether you’re taking it along while you clean the house or sit with it on the patio sipping iced tea, it’s the perfect digital companion.I personally haven’t had any issues with the speaker so far. It sits on my kitchen island connected to the original Dot charger. It hasn’t turned off, stopped working or any other issues I’ve seen in other reviews. I’ve been able to disconnect it and take it around the house with no issues. This is a high quality and thoughtfully designed piece of equipment that, in a way, makes the Dot/VAUX combo better than any other “Echo” product due to its ability to be a small stand alone or a portable powerful speaker depending on your needs.This is by far the best peripheral for the Dot to date. If you have a Dot, or are thinking about picking one up, I highly recommend this product.
