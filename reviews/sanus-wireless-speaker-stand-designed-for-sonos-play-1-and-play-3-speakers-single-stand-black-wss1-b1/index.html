Securely supporting the speaker for optimum bass response. Positioning your SONOS speaker at the ideal angle for the best sound quality. Product Reviews

Great Product

Good product, using with my Play:1 speakers. Hides the cords well and looks very nice. Only one complaint. The stand comes with 3 mounting brackets (one for Play:1, one for Play:3 horizontal and one for Play:3 vertical). I’m planning to save and upgrade to Play:3 speakers for the rear of my surround setup. Unfortunately the stand only comes with one felt pad for attaching between the mounting brackets and the speaker, so it’s a one and done and then you’ll have shop for more parts if you want to switch to a different speaker or orientation. With the two extra felt pads this would be indisputably complete and in my opinion the best option for pillar mounting Sonos speakers.Update: Sanus reached out to provide me with the felt pads I needed. The pads arrived very quickly and are exactly what I needed. Thanks so much, excellent product and service.

Excellent Stand but Better Directions Needed

This is a nice product and fits the SONOS Play 1 well. It would have rated 5 stars, but the directions need to be a little better. Another review references the plastic collars and the danger of sliding them into place too soon instead of leaving them in the middle of the extension pole until you’re ready. That is advice well heeded, and had I not seen that review, I would have had a similarly frustrating experience. (I’d also recommend putting the rubber floor skids on the base before doing ANYTHING if you’re working on or above a wood or laminate floor. The base is heavy by necessity and will scratch a pliable surface without the skids in place.) Other than the directions needing a little tweaking, the Sanus Speaker Stand is a quality piece of equipment.

A solid, well-built stand for a PLAY:3 speaker

This speaker stand does what it claims to do and does it well. It is well-constructed out of solid materials, and the design pays attention to isolation of the speaker so it does not transmit vibrations to the stand or the floor. I used a pair of these stands with PLAY:3 speakers in vertical orientation, and assembly was straight-forward. As others have noted, the plastic collars fit rather tightly on the shaft – a little lubrication makes them easier to position. The assembly instructions, though wordless, are quite clear, and the hardware for each step of the assembly is in its own plastic pouch with a number keyed to the instructions.I expect to buy these again when I extend my audio system into another room.

Saves shelf/table space, but requires floor space

I ordered these for my bedroom because I wanted to reclaim the surface area my two Sonos 3 “rear channel” speakers were taking up on the dual nightstands. The speakers are now mounted vertically on each stand and sitting in opposite corners against the back wall angling toward the bed. The stands are pretty large actually, particularly at the base. So be mindful of the dimensions when considering. Common sense. Also be aware that the power cables that are shipped with Sonos speakers are not terribly long. Of course that, and the location of the outlets, will limit your placement options unless you use an extension cord. Again, common sense. They work fine. And look pretty good, too. Very stable (won’t be easy to tip over) and sturdy.
