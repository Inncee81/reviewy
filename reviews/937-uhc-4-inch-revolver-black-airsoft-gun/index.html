Good as a sidearm.

I bought this gun for $25 plus shipping. It is definitely worth the money. First of all the size; it is a LOT bigger than I expected, and I later found out it’s full scale. It’s pretty light though, much lighter than my TSD m9. Range; it will go about 40 feet before dropping, but curves after about 25-30. Accuracy; the accuracy varies, usually 5 of the six shots should be able to hit the small target it comes with from about 20 feet away, but one of the shots will sometimes shoot out at an angle. Power; it has roughly the fps they say it has, it shoots like an average spring pistol. Looks; its ABS plastic with metal internals and some metal external parts (like the ejecting rod). It is large and extremely cool looking, and it feels awesome to eject the empty shells, although you can load the gun with the shells still in it. Overall, I would recommend this revolver as a sidearm but not as a primary weapon.
