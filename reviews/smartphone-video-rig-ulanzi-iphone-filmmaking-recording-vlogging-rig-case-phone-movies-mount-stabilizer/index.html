Wonderful Product, Great Price

I use the ulanzi Smartphone Video Rig with an iPhone 7. I also an iOgrapher product that perform the same function for my iPad Air 2. The ulanzi compares well to the iOgrapher and is a better fit for the iPhone 7 at this time. The product is well made, sturdy and easy to adjust. The moving components look like they’ll hold up to constant use. I really like the fact that it has a metal threaded tripod mount on three sides so my phone can be used horizontally or vertically. It also has metal reinforcement on the two accessory shoe mounts. I wish there were three shoe mounts on top. The clamp held my precious iPhone securely and I didn’t worry about it falling out. Also, I didn’t have to take off my iPhone case to mount it in the ulanzi. There was some side to side movement when I touched my phone, but it was stable during shoots. There is plenty of room to use a lens mount adapter for wider angle, telephoto or macro shoots. Overall, I’m really delighted with the quality and functionality of the product. It’s a great buy at about 1⁄3 the price of the competitor’s product. I highly recommend.

This rig does what I bought it to do - bring together my phone, mic, and light - but I live in constant fear that my phone will fall out of the rig. It wobbles and shifts incessantly when it’s by itself but it really becomes unstable when I attach my Moondog Anamorphic Lens. I basically set it up on a tripod, gingerly press record, and back away for fear even a gesture’s whisper breeze will cause it to move or fall out. It works for now, but I’m definitely on the hunt for something more secure.

Good product but larger phones pose issues

I got this as a simple way of doing easy, on-the-go vlogs without having to bring my camera. I especially liked it because of the two cold shoe mounts on the top for a microphone and anything else (product pictures show lighting) and the fact that it could be mounted on a tripod. My phone is a Galaxy Note 5, which makes for some issues. Larger phones will have an issue fitting onto the rig because they can get in the way of the handles. My phone also has a case on it, meaning it has struggles fitting into the holder itself. That is fixed upon me removing the thick case (which I have for protection). Basically, if you have a regular sized phone this is perfect for your phone.
