Not just the GoPro

I got this mount to use with a Nabi Square HD action camera. I found the part the makes up the hinge on the parts that came with the Nabi was a bit to fat to fit into the hinge side of this mount ( where the top screw is on the picture) but a little sanding on the Nabi part I was good to go. I used this to mount to the windshield of our tour bus on a trip to Israel. The suction cup held firm and after getting the camera settings right the result was some excellent footage through the streets of Jerusalem. I can’t say how well it would hold on the hood or outside surface of your car or truck door but the “through the windshield” mounting was easy and allowed for a variety of angles.

Attention all OtterBox owners!

When I got my first OtterBox back in the day I knew I wasn’t going to use the belt clip phone holder and since I used my phone for GPS instead of my TomTom I Zapp screwed the belt clip to my old TomTom windshield mount. Well the suction cup finally went so flat that it wouldn’t suction anymore so I bought this to zap screw to my OtterBox belt clip. To my glorious surprise the GoPro Mount clipped on to the OtterBox belt clip perfectly! It’s very solid and a well-made suction mount.

Bigger than expected but solid and good features

Documentation inside shows through Hero 3 but works perfectly with my Hero 4, as expected. (The product description is accurate, just the note inside the box doesn’t say Hero 4) This unit is slightly larger than I was expecting but it’s solid. I was pleased to find that the main pivot next to the suction cup has detents that click slightly when the screw is loosened and the arm is moved. This is important for me because I’m using this mount for time lapse and want the camera to have the exact same position should I need to rotate it away from the glass to remove the microSD or swap the battery. With this mount and the open frame mount I use with the camera, I know exactly how many clicks to rotate out then back into position later. No guessing and I can leave the suction mount in place and still have the exact same position. Thanks.

Does not adjust in every direction.

This mount has a fixed rotation under the camera mount, where some units will not only tilt, but also rotate there. This limits the usefulness for me, where I am mounting the camera to a side wall in the pool and I need to adjust the mount in a direction it cannot be adjusted.
