Product Specifications

  • Almost every dish you prepare in an electric, natural gas or propane oven can be made in the Omnia. It holds about as much volume as an 8” baking pan.
  • Aluminum cooking & baking pot and lid – steel base and stainless steel baking rack. Inside cooking dimensions: 9-1⁄4” diameter by 2-1⁄4” height. Unique heat distribution chimney cone is 3-3⁄4” diameter by 2-1⁄4” high.
  • Additional custom-fit parchment rounds for non-stick baking (up to 425 degrees Fahrenheit) will be available on Amazon by the end of February. Twelve are included with your purchase. Also available the end of February will be a full-coverage silicone mould.
  • It is especially pleasant to use at home for small baking and cooking recipes because it heats up the kitchen far less than conventional ovens.

    Product Description

OMNIA OVEN + BAKING RACK The Omnia Oven performs beautifully on gas burners because you can control heat intensity and duration so well. Omnia cooking & baking on electric burners works well enough – but not great – when you do not require precise heat control. The Omnia oven does not work on an induction cooktop (even when using an induction interface disk). An excellent thermometer is available on Amazon: Maverick OT-3 BBQ Kitchen Thermometer. Custom-fit parchment paper rounds for non-stick baking (up to 425 degrees Fahrenheit) on the rack will be available on Amazon by the end of August. Silicone round and rectangular baking cups – both mini- and full-size – work very well, too. Starter recipe book included. Facebook : Omnia Stovetop Cooking.
