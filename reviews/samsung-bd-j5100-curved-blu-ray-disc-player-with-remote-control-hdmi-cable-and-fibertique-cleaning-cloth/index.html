Experience the best picture and sound quality available combined with advanced special features on the Samsung BD-J5100 Blu-Ray Disc Player. Featuring full HD 1080p Playback via HDMI, 1080p Resolution Upscaling, Dolby TrueHD, and DTS-HD Master Audio; the BD-J5100, brings your home theatre system to a whole new level providing higher quality video and audio for your entertainment.
