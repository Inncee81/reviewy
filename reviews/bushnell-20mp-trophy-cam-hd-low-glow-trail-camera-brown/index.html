Product Reviews

Super Trail Camera

 I use this for wildlife and bird watching purposes only. It has been GREAT for me. I capture stills and video up to 50 seconds. The daylight image quality is excellent. The nighttime (IR) resolution is lower, but that is entirely expected. The color reproduction is excellent. I uploaded one day light example (Amazon only allows the reviewer to upload one file). PLEASE NOTE: Amazon plays the video in LOWER RESOLUTION than it was recorded. On Amazon, it looks fuzzy, but in reality the video is crisp. With a 32GB SDHC card (costs less than $30 most places) it can capture thousands of stills or videos. A 10 second video is about 11MB. A still (at max resolution) is about 1MB. I have used the camera 24⁄7 for the two weeks I have had it and have not replaced the batteries yet. If I had a complaint, it would be that the programming interface is clunky and awkward. It should be easier to program, but once you get used to it, the programming is acceptable. Would I buy this unit again? Yes.
