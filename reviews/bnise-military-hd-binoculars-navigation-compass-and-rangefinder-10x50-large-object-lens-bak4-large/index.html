Great pair of binoculars

These binoculars are really easy to grip and are super sturdy and outdoor ready. The camouflage for these are the brand real tree camouflage. The binoculars are nitrogen filled so no fogging or distortion happens. Has a lifetime warranty that if the seal leaks the nitrogen out you can get it fixed or replaced. Because they are sealed they can float if dropped in water. The vertical and horizontal compass reticle works really well and it lights up to red with a push of a button in low light conditions and it also has an azimuth. To estimate distance is a little difficult I still haven’t gotten the hang of it yet. You can see really good in low light conditions like at dusk and dawn and even after dark with the moon you can see relatively good. The binoculars are super clear and you can see quite a distance. The distance is not adjustable but the eye pieces are adjustable to each individual eye for the best clarity and vision. Lens caps and the eyepiece cap do not fit right and just falls off with a bump. You can take the lens caps off and install the binoculars on a tripod if you want to use them that way. The binoculars came with a carrying case, a chest harness and a neck strap to carry the binoculars with. Overall besides the eyepiece cap and the lenses cap falling off it’s a great pair of binoculars for the money.
