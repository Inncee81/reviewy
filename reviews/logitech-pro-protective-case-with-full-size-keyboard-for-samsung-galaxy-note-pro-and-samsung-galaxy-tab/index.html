I’ve had this keyboard a few months and it has been a 4-star experience overall. But no deal breakers in my view–it’s still been very good and my primary case.HOWEVER, with the Samsung upgrade to Lollipop 5.0.2 this keyboard NO LONGER WORKS. It will pair but will not operate. So, I strongly recommend that you DO NOT BUY this unless you are sure to be/stay pre-Lollipop or until a fix has been implemented. We need some urgent help here from Samsung and Logitech!

Beware of the Bluetooth connectivity issue!

Before you buy this keyboard and you are running Lollipop 5.0+, this keyboard will not work. The keyboard will pair with the tablet but you are not able to type with it. Samsung and Logitech have been notified of this issue so just be warned. A couple of forums confirmed it is not just a select few but almost all of the keyboards will not work. Some go as far to say no non Samsung branded mice or keyboards will work. There may or may not be a fix in the future. This review is in no way, shape, or form directed to the seller or associated merchants who sells this keyboard. It’s just to inform consumers of the Bluetooth issue there is with connecting the tablet and keyboard.
