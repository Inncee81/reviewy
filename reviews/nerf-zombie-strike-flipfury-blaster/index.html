A fun, large pistol that takes some getting used to.

This blaster is kind of bulky for a pistol, but you do get 12 shots. Slide action priming may not be everyone’s cup of tea, but this pistol is too big for one-handed hammer cocking. Some sort of pump action would be odd for a pistol, and would just move your “off” hand from the top of the blaster to the bottom. I have heard of people dual-wielding this sort of pistol, utilizing a strap behind their back or neck that hooks into the slide and you extend your arms a bit to prime the blasters. Slam-fire was not reliable on the one I have tried, and I have heard reports it is problematic in this particular blaster. All in all, a fun and large pistol with some quirkiness.READ MORE
