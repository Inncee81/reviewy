Product Specifications

  • N Male to SMA Male Jumper Cable
  • Connect from SMA Equipment to N-Style Antennas
  • 25 ft Low-Loss LMR240 Size Coax
  • Heat Shrink on Both Ends

  • Compatible with all SMA-based equipment including modems and routers from Cisco, Cradlepoint, Digi, Pepwave, Proxicast, Sierra Wireless, Sixnet/Red Lion and many others.

    Product Description

Color:25 ft Coaxial extension cable made of low loss LMR 240 sized coax cable.

Use to connect SMA radio sources such as modem, routers, etc. to the female N connector found on many external fixed mount antennas (e.g. Yagi, directional panel, sector, fiberglass omni-directional).

Thin, flexible, light-weight 240-sized coax makes installation easier, especially for tight bends.
