Slow leaks with patches

The tools are handy but the glueless patches are not very good quality and don’t hold for long. I’ve ended up repairing the same puncture three times only to replace the tube because air managed to leak out anyway. I’ve never had this problem with the tradition glued-on patches, but I can’t seem to find those anymore.UPDATE: The patches in this kit are really not worth anything. They are completely useless. I tried to patch the same puncture three times and ended up replacing the tube because the air kept escaping. Then I had a puncture on another tube, “fixed” it with one of these glueless patches, only to find the tube flat again. Thankfully it was a slow leak, so I was able to get home before the tube went completely flat. I finally got some proper patches at my local bike stores, you know, the ones that require glue, and I fixed the puncture for real. I am so sick of repeatedly trying to fix punctures with these useless patches. If you want the tools, fine, but if you’re thinking you’ll need to use the patches to repair your tube, forget about. They do not work.
