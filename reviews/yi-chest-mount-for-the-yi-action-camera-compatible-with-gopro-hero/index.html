Product Specifications

  • One size fits all – Adult Size. Chest strap adjusts from 25 inches (slack) to 55 inches (full-stretch). Shoulder straps adjust from 18 inches (slack) to 54 inches (full-stretch).
  • Hands-free, first-person POV, and liberating single shoulder strap
  • Handcrafted, environmentally friendly, breathable neoprene. High-quality 304 stainless steel used to prevent rust.
  • Compatible with YI Action Camera and all GoPro cameras including HERO5 Black, HERO5 Session, HERO4 Black, HERO4 Silver, and HERO Session, meets RoHS International Requirements

  • One year warranty

    Product Description

Color:Black  |  Style:Chest Mount The sleek and comfortable chest mount attaches the camera in the upper left shoulder. It has top quality breathable neoprene to make it versatile and waterproof including the anti-corrosion stainless steel bolt. YI’s fully adjustable chest mount was designed to fit all sizes including over large jackets.
