Brush heads are soft but are big for kids and mine were BOTH blue

These kid brush heads are just ok. For years, I would just buy the ProResults Compact for my kids since it is small and soft for them, however it seems that they have been discontinued. So I bought these and I was not too thrilled with it.Even though the item description and photo says there is a red and blue brush head, mine came with two blue brush heads. This was not clear when I purchased this that it will be both blue. There are no differences between the brush heads meaning my kids have no way to tell it apart, so I have to use stickers or mark it somehow. Other Sonicare brush heads I’ve had in the past can change the color at the bottom, but these do not have that option. The description should be updated to state when it’s the same color.In addition, the brush head is big for my 9 and 7 year old kids. It’s about the same size as their manual toothbrush, perhaps a bit bigger. It’s the same size as my DiamondClean. When using these brush heads, they tend to spray toothpaste more since it is bigger. They liked the Pro Results compact size better. I don’t know why they got rid of this line, this was a popular line.The only good thing is that the brush heads are soft and are good quality. I will look for other options for my next purchase.

Makes a big difference

It might seem early for a kid of 8 (now 9) to use an electric toothbrush of the quality and sophistication provided by Sonicare. Not at all. We are very glad we tried it. The difference between regular toothbrushes and the Sonicare is entirely visible. Our son’s teeth are a gorgeous white, and proof of what made the difference is that when, without realizing it, the toothbrush had been accidentally reset to the shorter, entry-level cycle, we wondered after a few days why his teeth were not looking so great. I checked the brush and found the error, reset and within a couple of days his teeth were looking fabulous again. Like we adults, after using the Sonicare for a while it feels yucky to him to use a regular toothbrush again.We travel a bit and the toothbrush holds its charge for up to about two and a half weeks. The charger is easy to transport. The subscription service we use with Amazon is great.
