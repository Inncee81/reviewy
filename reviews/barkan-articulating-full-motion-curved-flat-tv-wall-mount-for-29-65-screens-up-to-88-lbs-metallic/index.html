Product Specifications

    Product Description

The Barkan 3300 is a three-movement TV mount with rotate, swivel and tilt options. The mount fits both flat and curved screens 29 to 65 inches and weighing up to 88 lbs. The minimum distance between the mount and the wall is only 2.4 in. And the maximum distance is 10.3 in. It fits TVs with VESA standard (hole pattern on the back of the TV) 100x100, 100x200, 200x100, 200x200, 300x300, 300x400, 400x300, 400x400 mm and non VESA up to 400x400 mm. In order to offer a better point of view, the mount has two Hinge pivots: rotate near by the wall 180 degrees, screen connection plate swivel 180 degrees & also tool free tilting from 0 till 15 degrees. The mount has a lifetime warranty and its color is Metallic black. The package includes connecting screws for the majority of TVs. Part of Samsung Televisions and a small Part of other TVs need long connection or different threaded screws. In this case, please contact US at sales.Usaatbarkanmounts.Com and we will send you screws free of charge.
