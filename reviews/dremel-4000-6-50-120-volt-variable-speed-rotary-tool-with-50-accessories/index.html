    Product Description

Style:Rotary tool The 4000 variable speed rotary tool offers the highest performance and most versatility of all Dremel rotary tools. The increased strength of its motor plus electronic feedback circuitry delivers consistent performance at all speeds. The slim ergonomic body provides a 360-degree grip zone for control in any position and the separate on/off switch and variable-speed dial allows convenient speed adjustment and maintains a selected speed. It uses all Dremel accessories.

Great when used as intended.

As another reviewer mentioned, this tool is ideal when used as was intended. The tool doesn’t have a lot of torque, so you need to let it operate at high speeds and let the rotational speed do the work. If you force the tool into the medium, it will bog down and obviously work less efficiently.All in all, I think this is a very nice kit. While I’ve only used it for one back splash installation as of now, I was satisfied with the performance and anticipate good performance in future work. The versatility of the tool made minced meat of the tile I put it up against, making outlet cuts, etc, very easy. I would recommend this tool, just make sure your expectations are in line with the intended usage patterns.

When you just can’t stop Dremeling

I got this kit to go with the drill press stand and was really happy with the tool’s performance. Very smooth even at max rpm. The kit includes several useful attachments and a nice assortment of bits to get you started. Several mandrels and collets were included that added to the kit’s versatility. It has plenty of power and the adjustable speed lets you tune the Dremel to the task at hand. The case is well made and designed to hold the tool and attachments in place. there is even an icon of each component marked in the proper location to store it. One drawback was the plastic flashing left over from molding the motor vents was left on instead of trimming off during assembly. This could lead to overheating. I can’t cut the flashing off while it’s on the tool as pieces could end up damaging the motor during use. I’ll have to disassemble it. That’s a big hit on quality control. Otherwise a great tool if used for its intended purpose.
