Product Reviews

Oral B – The B stands for Best

I have been searching for a new toothbrush for my daughter and the Oral B Pro-Health Power Rechargeable Toothbrush is the treasure I was searching for. My daughter has lost a number of her baby teeth (as five year olds tend to do) and I’d recently noticed that she wasn’t brushing her teeth as well. I guess it is hard to get excited about brushing the three teeth she has left :o) She had a Crest Spinbrush before and the gaps in her teeth seemed to make it less effective. I was interested in something that would allow her to focus more on each individual tooth and in comes Oral-B. I received this toothbrush as a gift in exchange for my honest review and the timing couldn’t have been more perfect. I am familiar with Oral-B products as I have an Oral-B Precision 5000 for my own use so I was excited to see what the kid version was capable of. My daughter was immediately drawn in by the Finding Dory Theme as I received the toothbrush right around the time this movie was being heavily advertised and she already loved Finding Nemo. She enjoys the music it plays when removing the toothbrush from the base and the tune it plays to tell her to switch from the top to the bottom. There was a small learning curve getting her to brush each tooth versus the sweeping motion she was accustomed to with the SpinBrush and other more traditional toothbrush heads. Luckily, she had it down after the first couple brushes. I was a bit worried that the toothbrush head might be too aggressive for her mouth but seems to provide just the right amount of scrubbing to her teeth and gums. Plus the toothbrush heads are not too hard and not too soft for their more sensitive mouths. The toothbrush is extremely kid friendly and seems to be fairly ergonomic for their small hands. I think the similarities to the more mature designed adult version adds to the appeal for kids; especially if their parents have a similar toothbrush, which I do. She definitely has more independence and seems more mature about her oral care now that she has a “big girl” toothbrush that’s like Daddy’s. Let’s not forget there was an immediate improvement in my daughter’s brushing over the last few weeks. The mild buildup I was noticing while using the SpinBrush was no more and she was actually excited about brushing again. So much so that there were a number of days where she brushed “just because” without having had a meal or snack. The initial charging time was reasonable and the charge seems to hold most of the week if left off the base. I can’t say enough good things about this toothbrush.
