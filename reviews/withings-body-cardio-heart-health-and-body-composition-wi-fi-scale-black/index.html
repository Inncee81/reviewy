Withings Body Cardio - Heart Health and Body Composition Wi-Fi Scale, Black

by people ...

Product Specifications

  • Full Body Composition Analysis: Get high-accuracy weight, BMI, body fat, muscle, bone mass and water measurements thanks to Position Control technology
  • Heart Health Management: Assesses heart rate and Pulse Wave Velocity, a medically-accepted metric of arterial stiffness, for a complete picture of cardiovascular health
  • Automatic Synchronization: Data from every weigh-in appears in the Health Mate app automatically via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth sync; Works with iOS 7+ and Android 4.3.3+
  • Daily Report: Customize the scale to display a local weather forecast and, if you have a Withings activity tracker or use in-app step tracking, your previous day’s step count

  • Multi-User: Up to 8 users can weigh themselves and access their personal weight history. Plus Withings Body Cardio automatically recognizes who’s who

    Product Description

Color:Black The first and only Wi-Fi scale in the world that gives a medically-accepted assessment of cardiovascular health thanks to cutting-edge Pulse Wave Velocity measurements. This ultra-thin scale also tracks body composition (weight, body fat, muscle, bone mass and water) and synchronizes with your smartphone to display your trends and help you reach your health goals.

Product Reviews

This scale is freaking awesome. It arrived faster than expected

This scale is freaking awesome. It arrived faster than expected. Mine is black, it is a little heavy. The screen is nice and easy to read. Setup is super simple. You can transfer data to your phone with Bluetooth or Wifi. It tells you weight, fat and water percentage, weather, and heart rate. When you stand on it, the screen shows you where you are placing most of your weight. So if you are leaning too much on your toes or heels, the scale will indicate that with a set of arrows. Until you adjust, your weight won’t be calculated. There is an interesting feature called Pulse Wave Velocity. You have to weigh yourself 5 times in a row to get your wave number. In a graph it show the health of your heart compared to others in your age group. When you lose weight it show that as well. I’m satisfied.

Measures Weight fin… but sometimes is unable to measure heart rate… what is the point of paying extra for this feature!?!

Seems to measure weight well. However, from time-to-time, it is unable to measure my heart rate, etc. That is the entire reason I bought this in the first place. If I didn’t want the heart rate, then I would have spent half the price on something else.Added 1 star for the integration with the Withings app. It is nice seeing the weather before I get dressed in the morning so I know what to wear. It also auto-updates my weight which then gets synced to other apps like Google, etc. This seems to work well.
