Product Reviews

Works great with the Polaroid Zip

I’ve been using this paper with my Polaroid Zip Instant Mobile Printer and photos come out great with it. Photos in outdoor lighting have the best results. Photos taken in indoor lighting still print well, but the more natural lighting of outdoor images come out so rich and with the most realistic colors and details. Inside this box of 50 sheets is actually 5 ten packs of paper. And each ten pack has a blue Smartsheet card with it. You are supposed to use this card at a minimum of once before running a pack of Zink paper through your printer. The purpose of the blue Smartsheet is that it cleans printer’s head and calibrates colors. So to make sure you’re getting the very best photo results from your printer, remember to run the blue Zink Smartsheet through your printer first, with each new 10 pack you use!Sample pack provided for review

My best gadget ever!

I give this little gadget a 5 star because, for what it IS, it’s brilliant. I’ve been a photographer from childhood and have always loved to photograph people. I’ve traveled far & wide and have never had any problem cajoling subjects into letting me snap them, from all over Europe to Tunisia, Mongolia, Mexico, Beijing, etc. I have always regretted not being able to give them a photo. To those in the “modern” world, I’ve managed to forward a print, but now this little thingy just hits the spot! It’s “old fashioned” in that it’s just a dinky format with not really the digital quality we have come to expect on paper, but people LOVE it, and I’ve realized this why they love it. As with the old Polaroids, your are feet-on-the-ground among your subjects and in hands-on contact. You have to touch them to give them a photo. My sophisticated niece and her roommates were over the moon when I could give them each a copy right then & there, for example.The best part is you can achieve the digital quality “real” photographers “need” because it is digital, can be downloaded & never lost. It may be the “back up” to my regular gear, but it goes with me everywhere.

Warm tones for authentic summertime pictures

Worked great with our polaroid printer (as it should, since it’s made for it!) but I mostly like this for the warm tone this particular brand brings. I live in a tropical climate with a light haze, and the pictures come out truer to the memory of a warm glow rather than cool tones that other models deliver.

Love!

I love this little printer and the paper. It’s just so fun to use. I’m a scrapbooker and use them in my journals. But, they photos print dark. You definitely need to lighten them either in an app or in a program. I lighten them where I think to myself that’s too much, and then I print and the picture is fine. Outdoor photos turn out better, but if they’re in low light you will still need to adjust. Otherwise I love it.

I tried an off brand paper but they didn’t work as well, so I’m back to getting the real thing. I admit I bought the little printer without really knowing what I was going to do with it, but now I use it all the time. When we go on vacations, I send postcards with a selfie in front of something cool. For birthdays, I send a card with a little photo that reminds me of the person. I also have stickers with photos of loved ones in my home and work offices. Even on the back of my iphone, I have a picture of my family…it reminds me of them and if i lose my phone, anyone in the office knows its mine. I use it more often than I thought I would and these little stickers are great.
