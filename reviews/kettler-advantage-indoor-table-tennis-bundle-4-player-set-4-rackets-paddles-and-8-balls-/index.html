Product Reviews

Peeling Paddles in Only Three Weeks

After only three weeks of light recreational use, the rubber is peeling off every single paddle. I realize these get great reviews; that’s why I bought them. Maybe they just changed manufacturers. These are terrible. Moreover, there’s nowhere on Amazon’s website that allows contacting the seller. Very disappointing. (I purchased these paddles in December 2016.)

Hidden damage

The actual paddles and balls seem to be fine. We are just using them for play at home in our rec room. What I was very disappointed with was that the product was damaged. One of the balls was completely bent in…like half of the ball was bent. What annoyed me even more was that it looked like someone knew that had happened and put it in the case with the good side of the ball showing, so you couldn’t tell until you actually open the package and take the ball out. I’m assuming it was returned by someone and then sent to us because two of the paddles were wrapped in plastic and two were not. My kids really wanted to use them right away and not wait for me to send this back and order a new one, so we’re keeping it. Just disappointed and annoyed.

OMG!

I’ve used these so many times since coming to college. For the price the value is absolutely amazing! Would buy again if need be.

Handles are a bit rough

Decent quality for the price. Wood handles are a bit too rough and need a bit of sanding. Orange balls don’t bounce as well as the white ones. Both are 1 Star balls. Nice that the rubber seems to be well-glued to the paddles.

Nice set for the price!

This is a nice table tennis set! Good quality and the balls respond well off the paddle. Much better than the old cheap pips out style. Smooth face on these is nice.

Arrived with damaged balls

Two of the balls were dented upon arrival. Packaging should be better to avoid damage to the balls. The paddles seem to be of good quality however.

Satisfied customers!

All we were expecting! My grandsons and I played recently, for a couple days actually, and really enjoyed the equipment. I would have preferred to receive some three-star balls as well, perhaps 4 one-star and 4 three-star, so they could experience the difference between the two. Note: if they were truly beginners it wouldn’t be an issue but they are already pretty good players. The equipment is excellent for the price and I anticipate many good years with these paddles.
