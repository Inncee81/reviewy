Great for learning to take Macro Photos.

So my husband bought me the camera because I have been really getting into Macro Photos and my cell phone just wasn’t cutting it anymore. I needed a beginners Digital camera but one that had zoom lens built in and I could adjust the settings. This camera does all of that for me. The camera has its on automatic “scenes” set up when you just want to point and shoot,, but personally I took about a day and watched several Youtube videos and learned how to do lots of personalized adjusting..including how much light I wanted in or out.I have MS and my hands are very week so I needed a lighter camera and one with the anti-shake. While the camera is pretty light, the anti shake really has a hard time with me, so I bought a few different tri-pods. The one huge downside to me is the shutter speed tends to be very slow..I need it to be much quicker so with my shaking the photo won’t be blury.. I love the image screen can rotate and move all around so I can get a better view. You will have to download Nikons Wireless Mobility Utility app in order for you to put the photos on your phone. We also bought a larger memory card and a “connector” that will download any of the photos from the camera to my computer.All in all I would buy this camera again…However to be honest I can already tell I will be wanting to upgrade in the near future. So I took the camera out for a test drive yesterday and took a few cool pics. I am experimenting with shadows..which is what the third pic is. Hope you like them.
