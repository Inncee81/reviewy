Great for stick handling but not the best for shooting

I love using these for stick handling and improving my forearm strength and I have taken shots with these along with regular pucks and I don’t want people to worry but they do chip a bit easier than regular pucks as they weigh more. Especially if you hit the post or boards often enough. I’ve had some tears on the lining part that is like the bread of a sandwich ,but on the top and bottom parts of the puck on the sides come off. But that’s just aesthetics and they have played the same.In general it’s best to use these for stick handling but it’s not a big deal for using them on shooting.
