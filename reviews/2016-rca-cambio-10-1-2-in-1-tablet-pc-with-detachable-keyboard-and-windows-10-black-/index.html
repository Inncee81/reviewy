won’t find better

This isn’t a Surface Pro folks. I will say though that for twice the money you won’t find better. Oh yea I wish had had more ram and storage space. But if you are looking for a great little tablet/laptop pc this is awesome. I installed the free version of Softmaker office because I didn’t like the mobile version of Microsoft Office. I now can create and edit all documents. I check email, surf the web, and stay in-touch with friends and family on Facebook. Right now I have the little 2-N-1 hooked up to a large monitor ( 42” ) via Mini HDMI to HDMI cable sitting in my bed righting this review on a wireless keyboard with a trackpad built in.Great choice for someone needing something to take on the road as well. Use your Cell Phone as a WIFI Hotspot and your good to go. Only install apps you really need and you should have enough space. For those extra files you have to have with you, either store them in the cloud or carry them with you on a flash drive.Hope this helps.

I think I’ll see if I have any return options and barring that I might open it up and see if the button is unaligned or something but as I’ve said, you can search the web and its a very common problem, but the devise itself is a decent budget tablet when on.
