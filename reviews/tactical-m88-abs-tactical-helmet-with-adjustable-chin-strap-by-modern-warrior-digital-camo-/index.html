Good idea, with a bit of a design flaw.

My 9 year old wanted military items for his birthday. This hat was a perfect choice. It fits nicely and would fit an adult as well. It has webbing inside that is adjustable. It looks very realistic and is great for dress up, halloween, or paintball. One part that was not a hit are the little metal tabs that are on elastic connected to the outer cover. They look like they are supposed to attach to the webbing inside, but won’t stretch that far. I didn’t notice until now, but you can actually see them hanging down in this photo. There are several of them hanging around the helmet and my son got really upset about them and said they were hurting him. I tried attaching them and couldn’t, so I just cut them off and the cover seems to stay in place just fine. I had to give it a 3 stars because of those. They could have made the elastic a little longer so they were functional or left them off entirely.
