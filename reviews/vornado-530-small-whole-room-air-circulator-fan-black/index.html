Product Specifications

  • Superior performance through deep-pitched blades that move air up to 65 feet
  • More powerful than an 18-inch commercial fan
  • 3 speed settings with adjustable tilt head for multi-directional airflow

  • Easy to clean, whisper quiet and backed by a 5-year limited warranty

    Product Description

Color:Black The Vornado 530 Compact Whole Room Air Circulator continues Vornado’s superior benchmark performance with a new edge definition design. With its unique signature grill and deep-pitched blade, the 530 moves air up to 65 feet while aerodynamically circulating all the air in a room. The 3-speed control allows for speed selection to meet any circulation need. The compact and convenient size makes the 530 perfect anywhere in the home or office. Use the 530 all year long for added comfort and energy savings in every season. Backed by Vornado’s industry-leading 5-year warranty. Model is made for 120 volts only.
