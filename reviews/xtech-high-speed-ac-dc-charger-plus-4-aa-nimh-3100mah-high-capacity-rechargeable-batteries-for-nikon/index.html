Nikon Coolpix L840 camera battery (x4) replacement or substitute?

Seem like quality, however just received. Comes with a car charger and an European outlet plug adapter. Worked with my Nikon Coolpix L840 camera, the sole purpose of my purchase. I did change the battery type to Ni-MH in the camera menu. It has the following options: LR6 Alkaline, Coolpix (Ni-MH), or FR6 Lithium to select from. It will only use Ni-MH 2 type, not the Ni-MH 1 type. You supposedly need to get a 4pk kind also. They need to be charged at the same time. Someone with better knowledge of batteries might be able to elaborate or debunk this [Nikon] claim. Very selective of the useable battery kinds it will accept. Needed around 2950-3100 mAH, or at least 2300 mAH or higher output. If they turn out to last, then would upgrade to 5 stars for review. $20 instead of the OEM $50 Nikon Coolpix brand does seem agreeable, and the OEM ones seem to be out of stock most everywhere [probable due to Christmas]. Shall see what the future holds. Do you get what you pay for? or is it a deal?
