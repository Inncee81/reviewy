Too bright???! Tinker Fix!!

I noticed some reviews remarked on how the light was still too bright on the lowest setting.The following is for tinkers or those who dare to live on the wild side and void warranties (it’s a $15 light and no harm will come to it.):To solve this take a 6-8 inch strip of aluminum foil, then fold it (long ways) the height of a AAA battery PLUS about 1/16th inch higher (aprox. 2mm). Roll that aluminum “battery” up into the rough shape of a AAA battery. Put the aluminum “battery” in one of the two slots and place two real batteries in the other slots. Still too bright? Make another aluminum “battery” and run the contraption on just one real battery. P.S. Also works on remote controls.
