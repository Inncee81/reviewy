    Product Description

The YI Smart Dash Camera combines the top features of all the dash cameras on the market and provides protection to you and your car with ultimate performance and affordability. Designed with Advanced Driver Assistance System ADAS, the camera analyzes data such as speed and the distance from the car in front and gives voice warnings in case you move off-lane or get too close to another vehicle. YI Smart Dash Camera supports 1920x1080p full HD at 60fps, 2304x1296p ultra high-definition at 30 fps, bright, clear and sharp images even at high speed

Great quality camera and very good value

Great quality camera and very good value: a must have for every car.It doesn’t have GPS, so don’t expect it to have location or speed data (which can be very helpful in the event of an incident), but I do believe that every vehicle should have one and this camera works great.The lens is wide angle enough to cover the entire front of my car from it’s viewing point attached to the windshield. It comes with a very long MicroUSB > USB cable to power the device and includes a 12v to USB adapter. It has G Shock detection for an accident, but I am fortunate to have not tested that.The ‘incident’ button commits about 15 seconds before and 15 seconds after the button press to a separate file. I find this to be inadequate, but the 32GB SD card I have in the device (not included) lets me record ~6-10 hours of video (conveniently chopped up in 5 minute files), so I just have to remember to pull the whole file off later.It has WiFi access point built in, so you can use the free Android app to connect to the camera, adjust settings and view/download videos. But, as with most WiFi enabled devices, when you attach to it’s network you will likely not get cellular data to your phone, so don’t expect to connect to it for very long.It has a lane-departure and forward-crash warning feature that attempts to perform some sort of analysis on the video it sees to look for cars and lane lines, but it’s absurdly useless, so I leave that disabled.READ MORE
