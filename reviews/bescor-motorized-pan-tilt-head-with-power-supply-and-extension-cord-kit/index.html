Product Specifications

  • Attaches between Tripod and Camera
  • Pans/Tilts Remotely at Adjustable Speeds
  • Supports Up to 6 lbs
  • 1⁄4”-20 Tripod Connection

  • Includes: Bescor MP-101, PS-260 AC Power Supply & Bescor RE-20

    Product Description

Overview The Bescor MP-101 Motorized Pan & Tilt Head attaches between a tripod and camera and enables panning and tilting the camera remotely. Two independent motors smoothly operate horizontal and vertical motion at adjustable speeds. The head supports payloads up to 6 lb. The mounting points are a 1⁄4 tripod mount thread underneath and a 1⁄4 camera mount stud above. An Auto Pan Mode allows for continuous panning motion, and a switch selector lets you assign end points at 45, 90, or 340. The maximum horizontal angle is 90 degrees, and the maximum vertical angle is 15 degrees. This head can be powered by four AA batteries (not included) It includes a Bescor PS-260 AC Power Supply for MP-101 Motorized Pan Head and a Bescor RE-20 extension affords 20’ (609.6cm) of additional separation from the MP-101 remote pan head to its remote control.
