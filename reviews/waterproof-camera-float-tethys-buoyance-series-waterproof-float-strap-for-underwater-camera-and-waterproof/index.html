Awesome, fun, and functional!

Honestly, I bought this for the color and was skeptical about buoyancy…I purchased it along with a heavy point and shoot (a half a pound camera) and thought surely it would over-weigh the floater….SURPRISE this thing keeps it afloat.Its wrist to camera length is on point, not too long, not too short.The stitching is great quality.The fabric dries in minutes.The only downside in my opinion is the size of it when not near water. It’s bulky and just too big vs normal point & shoots. But it is a floater after all and it does what it’s intended for very well.All in all, I am very happy with my purchase!
