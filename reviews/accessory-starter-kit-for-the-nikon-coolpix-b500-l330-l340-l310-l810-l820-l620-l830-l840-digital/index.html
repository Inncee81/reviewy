I bought the Nikon COOLPIX L830 awhile back and thought it would be nice to have a tripod, both for steadying my pictures and taking pictures of myself/groups. The other items in this set seemed like a nice bonus. Well, unfortunately the tripod broke after a few uses and I was never able to totally get it back together. I can sit the camera on the tripod and take pictures, but it doesn’t pan or rotate anymore. At this point I don’t think it’s worth it for me to invest in another, and it still sort of works.The mini tripod is adorable; I haven’t used it much but I carry it with me most of the time when I have my camera. The card reader has come in handy on numerous occasions, and the cleaning kit is nice too. I don’t use the camera bag much, as I prefer my own smaller bag, but it has come in handy when I want to carry other items with me.I bought this and had my doubts until I received it. Everything in it is great quality. The tripods, which I have used several times, are sturdy and easy to use. The case fits my camera perfectly and even has some extra room for the strap, batteries and a few SD cards. The screen protectors go on with no issues such as air bubbles. I haven’t had a chance to use the USB adapter yet, but it too seems to be good quality. I highly recomend this product over trying to piece together this kit. Worth way more than what they are asking!!

Good Budget-Buy

About the bag: I like the size and feel of the bag. When it first arrived, there was an issue with some of the stitching on the bag straps. Though there were some shipping complications, the seller amended the problem with good communication and a replacement part. The new strap is holding up nicely and the stitches look good.About the Tripod: I have the Amazon basics tripod and it is amazing. I’ll use this one as a backup tripod. It’s not super sturdy but is good if you’re filming on flat ground with little wind and good balance.. like in an office or room. The bag it comes with is nice and the attachment for the camera comes off with a click. It’s all right.About the Mini Tripod: Note, this is the first mini tripod I’ve ever had. It holds up the weight of my 2lbs camera very well and feels sturdy. I really like this thing for shallow table shots.About the Adapter: It doesn’t work on my computer at all. My memory card works fine everywhere else but in this thing. It doesn’t feel well made. If you’re wanting something like this, I wouldn’t depend on the one in this pack. I haven’t tried it any other computer but I have tried in all my USB outlets.About the Screen Protector & Cleaning stuff: It all works as expected. The only down side to the protector is that you’ll need to measure your screen exactly and cut to that measurement. This is expected with all screen protectors like this and I’m not docking them for it. Just keep it in mind. The spray, clothes, and protector all work as expected.Overall: It’s a good budget-buy if you’re buying a small (ish) camera. The seller communication really impressed me and got them an extra star just for that.
