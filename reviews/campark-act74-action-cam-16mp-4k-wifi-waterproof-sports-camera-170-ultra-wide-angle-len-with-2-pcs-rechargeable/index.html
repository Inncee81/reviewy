Good camera for the price

Good camera for the price, but much of the frame where the image looks more like blended paint splotches than crisp “4k” video. Sensor isn’t great, but you can at least see what is going on some spots more than others. It isn’t a deal breaker, but definitely something I would consider if you are looking for a high-quality camera for videos to share. I was looking or something that would have better quality, but this camera is definitely something I would recommend for those who just want an instant replay of their last ski jump or other tricks. The wifi connection, however, was seamless. Even while recording “1080” video at 60fps, there was almost no latency whatsoever. Overall, wifi works great, but I am not overly impressed with the video quality.

So far the waterproof case has kept the camera working in heavy thunderstorms. Wifi functionality is pretty useful as you can easily mount this on the exterior of a vehicle and control it from the interior. The menu feels more intuitive and easier to use than the GoPro Hero series. I’ll probably use this for everything from shooting sports to scuba diving in the near future.
