    Product Description

Description: The full motion mount fits 37” to 70” flat panel TVs with VESA mounting hole patterns within 200-600mm wide and 100-400mm high. This TV mount is constructed with heavy duty solid steel with powder-coated black finish providing security and stability. Adjustable tilt, swivel, rotation and extension place your TV in perfect viewing angles. The lock and release design secures the TV into place for additional security and offers convenient installation, Articulating arm design allows display to be folded parallel to wall to save space.

Please check whether the mount is suitable for your TV: First, please check your wall for wood studs, it can’t be used on concrete wall or drywall alone. Next, the weight of your TV should be less than 120lbs. Then,mounting holes distance on the back of TV must be less than 24” (600mm) horizontally and 16” (400mm) vertically. Your TV should be at least 37 inch. Otherwise, you will see the bracket. Finally, Please check possible blocked cable/input.
