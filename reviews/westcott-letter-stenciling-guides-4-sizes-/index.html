Product Specifications

  • Includes 4 stenciling guides
  • Contains A-Z, a-z, 0-9 and various punctuation symbols
  • 1⁄4”/6mm, 1⁄2”/1 cm in uppercase & lowercase letters
  • 1⁄8”/3 cm & 7⁄8”/21mm in uppercase letters & numbers

    Product Description

Size:1⁄4 to 1-1⁄8 in Westcott’s Letter Stenciling Guide is a great starting kit to your crafting needs. These plastic stencils can be used on most materials and are clear for easy maneuvering. The package includes one large, one medium and two small sized stencils in assorted colors. From the Manufacturer: Westcott Drafting & Drawing Tools are some of the finest products of their kind for students and professionals. Our commitment to meticulous quality and cutting edge innovation in measurement and art materials has earned an unparalleled reputation.
