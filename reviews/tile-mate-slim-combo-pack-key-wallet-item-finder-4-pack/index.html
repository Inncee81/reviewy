Tile Mate & Slim Combo Pack, Key/Wallet/Item Finder, 4-pack

by people ...

  • The Tile Combo pack includes 2 Tile Slim and 2 Tile Mate Bluetooth trackers - perfect for keeping track of your essential items like your keys, bag, wallet, and laptop.
  • Tile Mate is designed with a convenient hole to loop onto keychains or drop in a bag, Tile Slim is as thin as two credit cards and slides into your wallet or sticks to thin objects like laptops.
  • The easy-to-use, free Tile app remembers the last time and place it saw your Tile, so if you left it somewhere, you’ll always know where to look.
  • Use your smartphone to make your Tile ring when it’s nearby but out of sight. Can’t find your phone? Simply double press the button on your Tile to make your phone ring -even on silent!
  • If your Tile isn’t where you left it, the Tile community can anonymously help you and update the location of your item if another phone running the Tile app comes within range of your missing item.

Configuration:Pack only Never lose your keys, wallet, purse, laptop or phone again with the Tile Combo Pack —Bluetooth trackers designed for all your essentials.
