Quiet, Compact and Stylish

I love the looks of this mouse in Black/Red and it fits comfortably in my right hand. It takes 1 AA Rechargeable Battery ( Included ) and one of the features that I love about this mouse is that it will go to sleep after a period of non-use which in turn prolongs the battery life. The Nano receiver stores very nicely in the bottom of the mouse when you are traveling with it.I have been testing this mouse for the last week and I absolutely love it! If you have a need for a wireless mouse by all means check this one out and at the price, it won’t break the bank.

I had absolutely no issues with the wireless receiver and mouse, and it worked almost instantly. In fact, I didn’t even need to reboot after unplugging the old wireless receiver and plugging in this new one. This was a truly seamless, very easy process. I didn’t need to do anything with respect to configuration in order for the mouse to work as expected. The mouse did not skip and fit good in the hand, it is not too small and not too large and the materials seemed like a nice thick plastic.This is a right-handed mouse and has two buttons on the thumb side which are easy to access, a scroll wheel on the top that depresses and scrolls easily and efficiently, and the left click and right click buttons were as whisper quiet as described. Overall, the mouse felt very solid and had a nice look to it, I would recommend it.
