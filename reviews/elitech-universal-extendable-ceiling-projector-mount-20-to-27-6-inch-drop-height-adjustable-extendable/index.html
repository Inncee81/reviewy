Product Specifications

  • This projector mount fits to all projectors that can sit into the bottom plate. To purchase this mount in silver color, please go to ASIN B00OJ6AP1U
  • Standard Mount drop: 20” -27.6”, Drop length adjustable. The ceiling parts can be jointed directly with the projector tray parts without the central poles, this way the projector can be mounted much closer to the ceiling (please see the attached picture). It can be used on flat ceiling or vaulted ceiling.
  • The projector can be titled up or down 15 degree. Loading capacity: 33 lb. Can be used on flat ceiling or vaulted ceiling.
  • The dimension of the bottom tray is 15.2” L x (7.9” to 13.4”) W. The height of projector allowed: 4.7” - 9.4”

  • This projector ceiling mount’s drop length can be extended with our extension pole form 41” to 71”, which can be found by searching “B01167ZNYI”

    Product Description

This is a Brand New, solid, durable, all steel constructed projector mount for all kinds of projectors that can sit into the bottom tray of the mount.

It fits most projectors available in the market.

There is no need of any mounting screws on the projector. Just sit the projector on the tray in the up-right position, or upside down position, then tight the bracket to hold the projector.

After installation, the projector can be titled up to 15 degree in any direction.

This projector ceiling mount’s drop length can be extended with our extension pole form 41” to 71” or 71” to 130”. These extension poles are selling separately. The extension pole 41” to 71” can be found by searching “B01167ZNYI”; and the extension pole 71” to 130” can be found by searching “B01180LEXC”
