Product Specifications

  • Measures 31-Inches; 34-Inches when extended; weighs 3.5-Pounds
  • Measures 31 x 34 Inches when extended. Velocity of approximately 500 round mag
  • Power: AEG (battery and charger included) and Integrated rail system for accessories
  • Mock Red Dot Sight acts as the gravity Feed Magazine, and the Mock magazine houses the battery
  • This product is restricted from shipment into the state of New York

  • The battery must be charged before initial use for exactly 4 hours (no more/no less) to maximize the effectiveness and efficiency of the battery.

    Product Description

The Soft Air Firepower M4A1 electric airsoft rifle is one of the best starter AEGs on the market. The M4A1 features a 500 round mag and a velocity of 220 feet per second. The airsoft rifle has an integrated rail system and includes a Flashlight, Laser, Mock Red Dot Sight, and a Foregrip. The M4A1 comes with a battery and charger.
