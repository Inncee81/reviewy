Color:Black Infinite Compatibility It works with any type and brand of device such as TV, PC, CD player, iPod, Kindle Fire, MP3/MP4 etc. up to 30 feet working range.

High-Fidelity TaoTronics TT-BA06X is a High-Fidelity Bluetooth 4.0 music transmitter, NOT a Bluetooth receiver. You can use it to connect to the audio output of your non-Bluetooth devices such as TV, iPod, PC, then pair it with your Bluetooth stereo. So the audio would be transmitted to your stereo. Note that it cannot pair with your cell phones or tablets.

Two Audio Output Modes Turn your regular stereo audio devices into a Bluetooth-enabled, wireless streaming host with either the 3.5mm Audio Jack or USB Audio Output.

Easy Pairing It is easy pairing with Bluetooth stereo headset, headphones, earbuds, speakers and other Bluetooth stereo audio enabled system.
