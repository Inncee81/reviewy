I like everything about these earbuds! I tried 2 other brands to compare, and these are for sure the best of the 3. I purchased these to replace my apple ear pods, and I’m glad I did. They ear hooks are separate from the rubber tips, which is a great design. For me, I use the medium tips, with the small hooks. It is a good idea that they are seperate so that you can make those custom combinations. Being able to make the perfect fit is what allows for the decent sound it has. When I first tried them on out of the box, they didn’t sounds the best. So I tried different sizes and ended up with a good set up.For me, I was after some cheap Bluetooth earbuds, that had decent bass, and that’s exactly what they have! Compared to the other brands I tried, these have Superior sounding bass. This is most likely do to the fact that the fit the best. The fit is a big part of how they will sound, so for me, they fit the best, therefore sounding the best. When I hold the other 2 brands in place, they sound decent as well, but I’m not going to hold them the whole time I use them. So, having a pair that fits perfect, and you don’t have to force them into your ears to sounds good, that’s what you want. These were the more expensive ones that I tried, but to me it was worth the extra cost(less than 10 bucks) compared to the other 2. I haven’t had them very long, so I can’t talk about longevity. I also a don’t write very many reviews on amazon, but I liked these so much that I had to write a review, so that says something at least! Also, their customer service seems to be very good.They even sent me a follow up email to make sure I wasn’t having any issues setting them up, and assuring me that if I had any problems, I could reach out to them to make it right. I will be buying another pair for my wife to use as well.

The sound is great for the price, there is a very subtle hiss to them when nothing is playing, this fades completely away when audio is being played.
