like one or two seconds

I replaced a small frame pump on my carbon fiber bike with this set up combined with the Portland Design Works CO2 inflator. I tried to pump up my presta valve tires with the small frame pump and it took many pumps and never got to 100 pounds. These inflate to 100 lbs in seconds, like one or two seconds. I am now taking the frame pump off, which probably helps aerodymics and a little weight gone, and putting the inflator and a couple of catridges in my under seat bag just in case I need them. So far in 1500 miles of bike riding, I only had one flat and was close to home, but I always want to be prepared.
