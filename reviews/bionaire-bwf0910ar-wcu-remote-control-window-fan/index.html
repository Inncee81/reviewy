Product Reviews

An impressive design

The fan is not quite as powerful as I had hoped, but it is an impressive design and not too difficult to operate. Delivery was lightning fast. It is part of a thru-house ventilation system and so far [pre-summer] so good.

does the fan work good, yes it does

First of all, does the fan work good, yes it does. The low speed is very quiet which I like. But…. the lights on it are bright enough to light an airport runway so if you have it in the bedroom at night be prepared. Next, every time you turn it on or change the speed it sounds like it’s about to take off like a helicopter and then will adjust down, annoying to say the least. And last, if your spouse is sleeping and you want to change anything on the fan it has a beep loud enough to wake my daughter in the next room. Another annoying feature. So if you need it for daytime normal use you should be good to go, but if you want to use it at night you may want to consider something else.

design flaw

the bad thing about this is when you mount it onto your window, the hard plastic extension plate, the pullout collapsible extension plate, and also the small square opening for the electrical cord all have a crevice that bugs can come in through. i had to stick 5 pieces of cardboard into these spaces… 3mm opening on left and 3mm opening on right side of the the hard plastic extension plate. 3mm opening on left and 3mm opening on right side of the the pullout collapsible extension plate. and 2cm square opening for the electrical cord.the design should have made them to better fit into sliding windows without the need for extra custom modifications. This model would be even better if they made filter screen attachments for when the unit is off, so bugs cant come in.the worse design flaw is this is a dumb automatic. the thermostat only powers on or off and totally ignores power settings. they way it should be is if temperature is very hot, the fan should auto high power, and if temperature low at night, the fan should auto low power.
