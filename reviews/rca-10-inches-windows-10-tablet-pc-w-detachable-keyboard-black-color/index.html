    Product Description

Introducing the RCA Cambio 10.1-inch high-resolution Windows tablet with detachable keyboard. Powered by an Intel Intel® Quad Core Atom™ Processor Z3735E (2M Cache, up to 1.83 GHz) the RCA Cambio is ready to tackle any task. Preloaded with Windows 10, the RCA Cambio boasts a clean and easy-to-use user interface. Create powerful documents and slideshows with Microsoft Office mobile apps including Word mobile, PowerPoint mobile and Excel Mobile. Store your files on the ample built-in 32GB flash memory or use the microSD card slot which supports up to 64GB of additional storage. Use the Cambio’s Bluetooth capability to connect and pair with nearby compatible devices. Connect with the world and setup a videoconference using the Cambio’s front 1MP or rear 2MP cameras.

Good value for the price!

This “netbook” style windows 10 computer with a detachable and reversible keyboard is a very good value. Though not a big processor, it is fast enough for all the basics computer needs: word processing, spreadsheets, web surfing and basic games (though “RPG” and other heavy duty games probably would suffer some lag). You could do video editing on this machine but it would be a bit slow going - however, you probably didn’t buy it for that reason!Screen is decent at 10.1; it is as big as most windows and android tablets. Weight is comfortable (even with the keyboard attached). and it allows you to bring your work with you to the office without lugging a heavy notebook computer.It does not have a big battery capacity so don’t expect 9 hours from it, best would be around 3 to 5 hours depending on the tasks and energy saving configurations. There is only one speaker so stereo is out unless you use a headset (either wired, USB or Bluetooth). It has a plethora of connection: mini-HDMI, Full and Micro size USB, and Headset. It is unique in that it can be powered or charged with either the dedicated A/C adapter (included) or through the micro USB port.The other nice feature is the detachable keyboard. You can put it on in two configurations, regular “clam shell” to work like a small notebook or reversed to create a “tent” to view the screen or to use as a tablet stand. This is a useful feature and not found in similar priced 2-in-1’s. There are forward and back cameras though I have never liked them being place on the the landscape orientations left side middle as opposed to the top middle in landscape mode.And then there is the price! A fully implemented Windows 10 tablet with a detachable, functional keyboard under $130.00 - nothing else at this price range and quality!Yes, recommended with just two considerations - the battery is not nearly enough so keep a charging cord (either the A/C adapter or micro USB) on hand for long time use and don’t expect to listen to stereo without a headset of some kind. Beyond those minor concerns this is a solid hit for RCA in the tablet / 2-in-1 category!
