Product Reviews

Sonos, where have been all my life?!?!?!

I absolutely love my Sonos - so much in fact that I’ve bought several for each room in the house, including a Connect to play our current home theater system. Setup is a breeze, sound quality is excellent, and the music sources are vast.I had been researching wireless speakers, including others, like Denon HEOS. I even considered upgrading my current amp to take advantage of newer technology. Up to this point, my wireless speakers were Airplay devices, which are extremely flaky and with limited music sources. Playing music through iTunes and Airplay can also be frustrating. I experience numerous drops, not only in my Airplay speakers, but also from my Apple TV playing through my home theater.Sonos solved all of this. I’ve experienced no drops since day 1. My house is sizable - 2 story and a pretty large footprint. They are the same Wifi network (no boosters) and sound quality is excellent. I love that I can access my entire iTunes library, Apple Music and Spotify in one app. We tend to listen to a lot of streaming radio stations - I love that the Sonos app defaults to the radio channel on Spotify when searching for an artist.There are a few improvements I would love to see. Are you listening Sonos?First, if the power goes out, or the app freezes (it happens sometimes), the speaker grouping is lost. Why can’t the app be smart enough to remember the last grouping? It easily could.Second, during significant power outages (we had some during Harvey), the speakers can become confused and not reconnect to the network. I learned that rebooting my router solved the issue, but only after re-powering a few speakers to find they still not reconnect. The software should be smart enough to retry connections. All my other devices reconnected just fine. Why can’t Sonos?Last, the Sonos app will prompt for your password when it should remember you. This is only when adding additional speakers, or larger changes - not on standard daily use. But I feel that if I am on the same device, I should not be prompted again. Allow to the user to stay signed in.All in all, I HIGHLY recommend these I regret not finding them sooner.
