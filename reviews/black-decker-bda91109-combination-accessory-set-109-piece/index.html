    Product Description

Style:Accessory set The BLACK & DECKER BDA91109 109pc Combination Accessory Set contains the ideal sizes of components for frequent use projects and installation. It offers a variety of drill bits for the most common uses with wood, metal, plastic and masonry. Along with the drill bits, it also contains a variety of 2-Inch and 1-Inch screwdriving bits for household projects. This convenient kit also comes with a durable, portable hard case with extra storage.

Product Reviews

Works For Me

I’m going to start this review by saying I don’t know much about tools. All I know is I needed basic tools to do household work. This was cheap, the tools seem to be good quality, and I can do almost everything I need. The only thing missing was the hex tool that a lot of ikea stuff use. Bummer on that. I’ll attach images so you can see all of the tools that come in the kit.

If you have a new drill and need a good started kit

If you have a new drill and need a good started kit, this is it. It has drill bits galore, for all types of jobs and materials. The case it comes in is very convenient as well, and all of the bits stay in place even after a good banging around in my trunk driving around some bumpy back roads. A great price for the amount of bits you gets, plus on top of that you get a few other random little pieces that come in handy when you are trying to use your drill, or even replace some ones you may have lost or broken.

Great price for what you get!

Got this to go with my new Black + Decker LDX120C 20-Volt MAX Lithium-Ion Cordless Drill/Driver. I wanted something I could put my hands on when I needed general bits. I have lots of different size bits in “several places” around the garage and was tired of hunting them down. It fits nicely in the Black & Decker BDCMTSB Matrix Wide-Mouth Storage Bag I purchased for the drill and the bit case. As always Black + Decker is good quality.

Good in combination with drill; not “flimsy”

Good variety for the price. Was about 60% the cost of the drill unit–not sure if that speaks to the quality of this kit or the quality of the drill. Has plenty of options for boring into wood and others have reporting being able to drill through metal, which I don’t doubt. Some people have reported that certain bits (especially smaller ones) are prone to breakage & bending, but I can confidently say that was likely due to user error. I have bent & broken many expensive drill bits in my day and I have no doubt in this kit’s substance; I wouldn’t put these through a “stress test” or intentionally try to break bits, because you WILL break them, but if handled properly while in motion, I’m confident these bits won’t break/bend under pressure. My biggest complaint is the difficulty of putting the bits back into the plastic housings (where you may break them), but this is a good buy otherwise, both on price point and variety. I’m sure there are other kits out there with a better selection at a similar price, but if you’re buying this alongside the B&D drill motor, it’s a solid purchase.

Good for organization

Everything was accounted for. I bought this for better organization and to have all the bits for the drill that a homeowner could need around the house. One of the latches on the case was broke , not a big deal to me as it still stays close and it gets out in the toolbox anyway.

bits to go with my new drill

The case is not very big so doesn’t take up much space. The set has just about any bit that might be needed for making repairs or building things. My dad was a carpenter for over 40 and years and I feel he would have been proud of me for making this purchase.
