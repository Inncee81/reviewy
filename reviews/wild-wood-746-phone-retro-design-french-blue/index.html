A nice throwback.

I know not a lot of people use landline phones anymore, but I still find a use for them in my home and it allows me to keep a minimal-cost plan for my family’s cell phone lines. The sound is crystal-clear, and the long power cord allows the whole unit to be carried around the room while talking, just in case one needed to go to the desk and write something down. My only issue with this device is that the cord for the handset is hardwired into the handset itself and the base unit. This makes the handset impossible to replace, should it fail. A benefit of this, I suppose, is that it prevents wear and fatigue that a jacked handset would normally fall victim to. Overall, I say this is a very good buy, and an attractive piece that would be useful for anyone who still uses landline.
