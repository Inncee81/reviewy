Roomy and protective

I will begin with a small difference in the description. First I would call this a semi-hard shell rather than a hard shell case. This means it offers proper protection but the case itself is somewhat flexible. Again I am splitting hairs but the description indicates a handle (which I would not expect) and it simply is referring to the detachable carry strap.This does not detract from what the case is as I found it to be exactly what I needed. I would have liked to see the inside of the case before I purchased the item so I am including a picture of the inside for those considering the case in the future. The case arrived well-packaged and on time. I can’t verify for just how durable this case will be in the future but I can verify that it is a nice sturdy looking case.I like the large zipper. Inside you will find that the case is lined with a soft material that I wouldn’t call plush but it is definitely a nice lining. The mesh pouch sewn into the top of the case is bordered by an elastic edging so that it is easy to spread the pouch open to insert your cord or other accessories. There is a flap/divider that is sewn into the case (not removable) that will aid in protecting your GPS unit. On top of the divider there is a 3⁄4 inch elastic band that keeps the divider in place as well as holds the GPS unit in even if the case is accidentally opened upside down.For reference, when my 5-inch Garvin Nuvi is placed in the case, there is about 1⁄4 of an inch of space in length and approximately 1⁄2 inch of space relative to the width of the case. So it’s basically a perfect fit for my unit. I don’t ever keep my unit in my car and this will be great to transport back and forth from home to car as well as take on my travels.
