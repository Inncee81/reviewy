Cute and Fun Film

I usually order the plain white film but I thought this particular combo was cute and fun. My personal favorite was the airmail but they were all great. I buy all my regular white film in bulk for about $0.65 per picture. This set comes out to $0.91 per picture. It’s a little more pricey than the film I usually buy, but it’s still under $1 per picture. I don’t mind it since I have only gotten it once for fun. I will probably buy again. The only disadvantage is that you have to use the whole pack; you can’t take them out early and switch them. So once this film is in the camera, you have to use that film for the next 10 pictures. I suppose you could take it out early, but then the leftover pictures would be exposed to light and would be ruined. If that doesn’t bother you (it didn’t bother me), then have at it! I still love the cute borders.
