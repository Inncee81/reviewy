    Product Description

Fits 42-70” TVs

This mount fits most of 42-70” TVs sold today. It fits TVs with mounting holes as close as 8”x4” or as wide as 24”x16” (in TV terms - VESA 200x100mm to 600x400mm).

Low 1.5” profile Flush-Tilt design is only 1.5” from wall yet still tilts to improve viewing and reduce glare.

Heavy duty steel construction and automatic robot welding technology This mount is constructed with heavy duty steel materials and with durable powder coated finish, and 100% welded by automatic robot, this advanced welding technology makes the mount sturdy and ensures mounted TV stable and secure, supports TV weight up to 132 lbs.

Fits 16” or 24” studs plus concrete wall This mount fits for 16” or 24” studs plus concrete wall typical in US homes. Not for dry wall alone application. All installation hardware included.

Torpedo bubble and 6’ high speed HDMI cable It comes with Torpedo level and 6’ HDMI cable. HDMI cable supports 3D, Ethernet, 1440P and Audio Return conforming to latest HDMI 1.4 Standard.

Includes all necessary mounting hardware This mount ships with hardware for both wall and TV mounting.

RoHS certified This product is RoHS certified, RoHS is the European Union directive standard of Restriction of Hazardous Substance, aiming to protect human health and environment.

Prevents falling off from both sides of horizontal bar You can slide the TV assembly left and right over the horizontal bar of the wall plate but still can prevent the TV assembly from falling off from the horizontal bar because of the stop plates at both ends of the bar.

Product Reviews

Simple and Easy to Install

This wall mount is extremely strong and able to support my new Samsung 55” flat screen. I was able to screw the mount into two wall studs top and bottom, ensuring this is mounted to the wall safely and w/o any concern of it coming loose. The tilt option and the quick release pull downs are easy to use. The quick release is a great safety assurance that no one is able to accidently lift the bottom of the TV away from the wall. But should you need to do so, each release is quickly accessed by hand and because the TV is a 55” unit, it hides the release pull down ribbons very well. You’ll want to use a “real” level and not the 1” bubble level that comes with the wall mount.Installing it was very easy, all (and more) of the screws, washers, connectors came in the package, so no need to have to run out to the hardware store due to missing parts. Instructions were written very well and easy to follow.

Good quality, economical TV mount.

I used this to mount a Ultra-Slim Samsung 55” LED TV above a fireplace so I wanted some tilt while maintaining a low profile. TV mounted easily and even with just under 10 degree tilt I had no issues with connections and cables behind the TV.One of the arms that mounts to the TV did not want to latch onto the frame even when pulling down the release cord. I could get it to catch without the TV mounted, but once the TV was mounted I was no longer able to place pressure in the right location to get the catch far enough over the frame to mount. Does not affect appearance and as one of the two are latched and I don’t live in an earth quake area and too high for kids to reach it works fine, but why removed 1 star.

There are better mounts out there but these are very good at this price.

Although I prefer one piece mounts to one’s that have to be assembled such as these, these worked great to mount 3 flat panel TVs in my new home. Leveling them can be a bit of a chore due to the center connector of the 2 piece mount giving such a short span to reference. You have to be careful when assembling the 2 piece wall bracket to ensure the center support and each side piece are in the same “plane” when referenced from the top. Once that is done though the rest of install is a breeze. I also recommend if only one stud is being used to secure it in other holes using better dry wall anchors than the ones supplied. I got lucky on 2 of the 3 mounts and was able to get it secured to 2 studs. On the third I used large toggle style drywall anchors that engage a large area on the back of the drywall. All in all I am happy with the mounts.

Easy installation I must say. Mounted a 47 inch Samsung with ease. The most time consuming part was locating the wall studs. I much prefer a magnetic stud finder over an electronic.
