Incredible camera for the price!

The GoPro HERO 4 Black is a fantastic camera. I found this on Amazon from a third-party seller selling it for $200 brand new. It is a great camera and the image quality is amazing. From looking at the reviews the image quality above water and below water is very similar to the GoPro HERO 5 (and even the 6, yes the 6). Not much improvement to the naked eye, in my opinion. I understand the greater 4K support of 60FPS on the newer GoPro’s but most TVs and youtube videos won’t stream to many devices at that picture quality.This surf bundle has a lot of sticky tapes and a few mounts to attach to your surfboard​. I don’t surf but I do take underwater snorkeling videos and the price of this GoPro was too hard to pass up.
