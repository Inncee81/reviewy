    Product Description

Size:19-Inch Slim design monitor with contemporary style and round stand. Featuring a glossy black finish and simple stand, the monitor boasts Game Mode technology which allows users to enjoy smooth images, even during the fastest moving scenes. With a 178-degree viewing angle, this monitor offers the perfect view from any position.

Product Reviews

Great Monitor

Price can’t be beat. It’s hard to find a monitor that shows really good color saturation including deep blacks, no ghosting, and no bluish haze when viewed at an angle. This monitor does not exhibit any of those things. Images are sharp and clear, plus it’s response time is very good for the games I play.

Great looking picture; works with PS4

I was looking for a PC monitor that would double as a gaming monitor. This fits the bill nicely. I only used the HDMI input, but there is also a VGA input. This isn’t any issue for me since both my PC and PlayStation 4 use HDMI. There is only one HDMI input on the back of the monitor, but that is fine for my setup.Games did run very smooth and the screen is bright and vibrant.One thing I found funny with it was that they decided to put a bend in the plug. this may be fine if you are plugging into a wall outlet, but if you are using a power strip it’s a bit of a nuisance. I attached a photo to show the bent plug.

I like the screen

I like the screen. My only complaint is input is limited to vga or HDMI. I would love to have Display port or DVI on it too. Also it does not take a standard power cable. There is a power brick with 2 pin power cable. But for the price you can’t beat it. The colors are great. I would not use this as a primary monitor but it makes a killer secondary and third.
