Product Specifications

  • Pitching training aids for fastpitch softball and baseball.

    Product Description

The Xelerator Fastpitch Softball Pitching Trainer teaches correct muscle memory nearly 20 times faster than conventional pitching only training. The end mounted weight combined with the flexible rope handle automatically snaps the wrist through the pitching motion, resulting in increased speed. Forces the player into the correct pitching & throwing plane that leads to increased speed. Great for general arm warm up prior to practice or game.
