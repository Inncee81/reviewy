EXACTLY what I wanted

Man, I so wish I had this on my last trip! But it will be here for all of my future trips. Clamp is really good quality and the spring is pretty stiff so not at all worried about slippage. The gooseneck is actually a bit thicker than it appears in the photos but that’s ok because I was concerned that my Hero 5 would stay in place, now I have zero concerns. The adjustable end is really my only long term concern. It doesn’t seem to have the same build quality as the rest of the product has, but at the same time I have had no issues with it, but in the long run I am expecting if anything gives out from wear and tear, it will be this plastic part of the build. The cost was not outlandish so even if I get a few trips worth of usage I will be very pleased with the product.

Quality accessory at half of the price!

Perfect for Vive Lighthouse

Very impressed!

Not quite perfect, but impressive.

The arms are STRONG! Works well, but the clip jaws are too wide, and they don’t have a traction coating on the material behind the ends of the jaws, so if you grab onto a pipe, the smooth part holds it, and the ends of the jaws remain open.
