it is not as easy to carry around as some other flash drives I’ve …

While I have no doubt it will do the job of a flash drive, it is not as easy to carry around as some other flash drives I’ve had. It isn’t a case of attaching a split ring to it and then putting it on my key chain. The little bar that is meant for this is so poorly placed that it will be difficult to get anything onto it. I’ll be using Instamorph to attach a small ring where the attachment bar is and then placing a larger snap ring onto that so I can then put it on my key chain. Just another example of poor design and not considering that a human being might actually want to put it on a key chain. At least the frustration free packaging proved to be true.
