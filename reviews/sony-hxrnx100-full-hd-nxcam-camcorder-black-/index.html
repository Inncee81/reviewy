Sony HXRNX100 Full HD NXCAM Camcorder (Black)

by people ...

Product Specifications

  • 1.0”-type Exmor R CMOS sensor for a wide range of shooting conditions, High sensitivity and high resolution, Sony G Lens (optical 12x zoom) with Optical Steady Shot, 24x Clear Image Zoom, and 48x Digital Zoom
  • Active Steady Shot corrects camera sway that occurs while walking, ND filter control mechanism, Three lens ring for focus, zoom, and iris manual control, XAVC S recording, 59.94p recording at Full HD 1920 x 1080

  • Dual media slots for simultaneous/relay recording, and independent record control, Slow & Quick Motion, Picture Profile, 99 Camera Profiles, Multi Interface Shoe (MI Shoe) for flexible connection

    Product Description

The HXR-NX100 comes with Sony’s unique 20 mega pixel 1.0-type Exmor R back-illuminated CMOS sensor that delivers high resolution and fantastic low light performance, as well as offering more depth of field control as demanded by today’s diverse shooting requirements. Three independent manual lens rings provide excellent creative control. A fixed Sony G series lens achieves 12x optical zoom, which can be doubled to 24x with Clear Image Zoom making use of the 1.0 type sensor to still deliver a native resolution 1920x1080 image. You can also double the zoom again using a sophisticated Digital Extender for a maximum 48x zoom. There is also a ND Filter function, allowing you to choose four conventional mechanical ND filter positions that will enhance your ability to work in any shooting scenario.

Ready to get serious about video? This is a good upgrade from a consumer camcorder

Purchased from another seller with warranty for the same price, so be careful here. As to the camcorder, if you are looking for a logical next step up from a compact camcorder, this is worth thinking about. I’ll get this out of the way first: This is a very high quality HD camcorder. It is not a 4K camcorder. A 4K camera with this feature set, add another $1,000.So, without writing a novel, you get a 1” sensor which rewards you with great low light performance and superb depth of field control for those creative blurry backgrounds. You can run it auto-everything, or manually control any aspect of the camera (focus, iris, gain, etc..) manually, or control everything manually. You get a variety of input/output options… XLR for microphones, composite out, HDMI out, LANC control, etc… but no SDI. You have to go further up the food chain for that.This camcorder model is still fairly new and there are not a lot of reviews. I put nearly 3 months into researching camcorders in this category and this ended up being the one. The only solid negative I have heard is that the viewfinder (eyepiece in the rubber cup) is barely large enough to use. I don’t even use it in my configuration, but the viewfinder is smaller than most, by measurement.If you wanted to go beyond this, then your next stop would be something with interchangable lenses (this lens is permanently fixed), or something 4K.4 MONTH UPDATE: I have received some comments on my review and so I’m going to do a little update. I have about 100 hours of use on it (there is a readout for this). After some time in the field, I have to say that getting deep bokeh out of this camera is challenging because the lens is not that fast. I’m getting good results, but it just takes more effort than I would like. I looked around and I still don’t see a better HD camcorder in this price range, so still highly recommended. You will need class-10 memory cards, 64GB in order to use the better file formats, so be sure to budget for that.6 MONTH UPDATE: I had a funny little problem worth talking about. I was filming a person standing about 5 feet in front of a wall and accidently left auto zoom on. And, even though I was on a tripod and the subject was standing still, the auto zoom hunted back and forth between the subject and the wall. I did not realize auto zoom was on…. I never use it. A fix for this is probably the facial recognition feature that allows you to select a face to target the focus. This could be a limitation for people who want to use this camcorder in a dynamic setting.
