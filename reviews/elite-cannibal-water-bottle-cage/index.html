Product Specifications

  • Material: reinforced fiberglass

  • Recommended Use: cycling

    Product Description

Elite’s design for the Cannibal Water Bottle Cage is ideal for race situations, when quick and easy bottle access is critical. The side-entry cage allows the bottle to be inserted from the sides as well as the front, so you can stay focused on what’s ahead. The Cannibal is built with reinforced fiberglass that is durable and lightweight, with the cage weighing less than 40 grams.
