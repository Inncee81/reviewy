It’s just about perfect for using at the gym

I love this fan. I got it for taking to the gym. I easily overheat when running on the treadmill, and I need airflow on me when running. This fan is perfect. I clip it onto the display panel of the treadmill or one of the little compartments to hold your keys or whatever. It’s adjustable and perfect. I can adjust it to blow right on my face or push it down a little and it will blow on my neck or torso. And you can adjust the speed of the fan as well. The only thing that went wrong with it is that the battery cover wouldn’t easily come off when I tried to open it and it snapped off and broke, just as soon as I got it. I can use it without the battery cover, but would have preferred it if that hadn’t happened at all. Oh well. It also would be nice if there was some type of battery indicator to see how much charge is left. One time I took it to the gym, the battery died shortly after I’d started running and so it was useless that run. Now I have to be very careful to charge it before going to the gym because I can’t tell how much power it has or how long the battery will last between uses.
