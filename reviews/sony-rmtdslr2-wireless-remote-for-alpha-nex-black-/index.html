Product Reviews

Works as advertised

Works as advertised with my Sony a6300. Each button mimics what’s on the camera body. My only peeve is I wish Sony had their IR receiver in the back of their cameras as well, so that this remote would work when I’m behind the camera outdoors.

Works well.

I was originally on the fence about purchasing this as I thought i was a bit pricey for $30. But once I got it, I find it to be worth it. It’s designed specifically for Sony Cameras and it works well with my A6300. The range on this is great and I can use it behind the camera with no problem.I’m able to use all the buttons and they are very functional. The buttons for the video portion works well if you have the camera hooked up to your tv or monitor.

I bought this remote to use as I record videos …

I bought this remote to use as I record videos so I can zoom in and out as needed. It works very well with my A6300. Its a Sony remote so expect this to work flawless with your Sony camera. Also don’t forget to enable remote in your settings before it will work as I found this out later.

Works, with some caveats

The remote works greats, but there are two things to consider if you’re just looking for a remote trigger.First, some companies make third-party remotes that work just as well for triggering the shutter, for a lot less. If you don’t need the extra controls on the Sony remote, these may be a better option. For example: Opteka RC-3 Wireless Remote Control for Sony Alpha A33, A55, NEX-5, A230, A330, A380, A390, A450, A500, A550, A560, A580, A700, A850, A900 (RMT-DSLR1 Replacement)Second, at least on my NEX-6, there is an IR receiver on the front but none on the back. That makes the remote less useful as a remote trigger for use with a tripod. That’s the camera’s fault, of course, and not the remote’s, but worth considering if you’re looking for a remote trigger.

Limited function, but it works

The remote apparently works fine. The only thing I noticed is that with the A-33 DSLR, there are only a couple of buttons on the remote that work. I have not yet, however, tried it for video playback. I was hoping that the remote would also access the camera’s menu system but that did not work. Regardless, I bought it primarily for remote shutter release, and it works perfectly for that. The remote was apparently DOA on arrival, but I had a spare battery on hand and she’s working just fine now.
