Good pump for blowing up air beds and inflatable pools and such

This is a great pump and it does its job, however the job that you’re doing must be a small one. Because in the instructions it clearly states to not run the pump more than 10 minutes in a row. If you do it can cause the motor to burn out it says. Other than that it works great. However, I noticed there are two holes on the inflate tube that leads to what you’re inflating. So when I use it I cover those two holes with my fingers while I inflate the item. Doing that it works wonderful.

After my brother and I both nearly developed brain aneurysms …

After my brother and I both nearly developed brain aneurysms straining to blow up a giant air mattress because we couldn’t find any D-cells to power its included pump, I bought this thing. It’s cheap, if you use air mattresses for unexpected surges of guests rather than camping, it’s a lot more convenient than a pump which utilizes batteries whose only other use is in GIANT Mag Lites.

Air Pump = more pool floaty time

I can now blow up all my pool floaties in a fraction of the time it took me to blow up one side of a raft by mouth. This is my new best friend in the summer. It’s a no-brainer, but honestly, it took two hands and a foot holding the float in place to get it fully inflated. The time saved is worth the extra foot of effort.
