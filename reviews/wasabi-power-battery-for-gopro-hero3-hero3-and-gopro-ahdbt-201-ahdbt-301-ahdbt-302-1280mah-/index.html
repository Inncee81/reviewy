Product Specifications

  • Rechargeable lithium-polymer battery for GoPro AHDBT-302 and GoPro Hero3 and Hero3+
  • Rated at 3.7V and 1280mAh for longer battery life
  • Premium Japanese cells for longer battery life with no memory effect
  • Fully compatible with the GoPro Hero3 or Hero3+ camera and all GoPro accessories

  • Includes battery with 3-year manufacturer warranty

    Product Description

This Wasabi Power battery replaces the GoPro AHDBT-302 battery pack. This battery features 1280mAh which provides a longer run-time between charges. It is made with premium Japanese cells to provide maximum battery life and also comes with a 3-year manufacturer warranty. Additionally, the Wasabi Power battery is completely compatible with all GoPro original equipment including the charger and camera.

Compatible with the following GoPro models: Hero3 (White, Silver, Black) Hero3+ (Silver, Black)
