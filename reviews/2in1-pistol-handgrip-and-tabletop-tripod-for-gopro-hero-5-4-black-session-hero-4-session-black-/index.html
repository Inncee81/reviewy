CamKix 2-in-1 Hand Grip and Tabletop Tripod

The Hand Grip and Tabletop Tripod can be used in various ways with a wide range of compact action and photo cameras. Check the product pictures for examples or continue reading.

The pistol type hand grip offers you a firm grip to keep your GoPro or compact camera steady, resulting in better pictures. Also great for selfies!

The hand grip/tabletop tripod weighs only 89 grams (3.14 ounces) and is foldable. Its compact dimensions and low weight make it easy to take it with you, anywhere.

The hand grip is equipped with a GoPro tripod adapter, compatible with the frame/housing of your GoPro HERO5, Black, Session, HERO4, Session, Black, Silver, Hero+ LCD, 3+, 3, 2 and 1. The standard tripod connection also enables you to attach other compact photo or video cameras.
