I’m a believer so far

Up until now we have only stuck with Easton bats (Mako and FS3). Comparing these 2 bats with the LXT I can honestly say that this bat does seem to perform better. There seems to be a better pop off the bat as compared to the Easton bats. Granted with it being the off season I have only been able to compare her hits against soft toss batting practice but my daughter even admits it swings better (not sure if you can chalk it up to placebo effect). I will say if you are in the market to upgrade to know the length/weight to go with. I made the mistake of getting the 2015 LXT Plus in a 29⁄18 and we quickly found out the bat was small (lengthwise). I then tried calling the supplier (different one from the LXT Hyper) to see if there was anything we could work out and they advised because the bat was out of the wrapper and had been swung there was nothing they could do. Given that these bats aren’t chump change we have the 29⁄18 sitting. I am hoping our younger daughter will be able to use it when she gets older.
