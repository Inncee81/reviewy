Durable, a simple solution to a persistent problem of mildew

We’ve gone through a couple of insulated lunch bags, as one does with small kids, but this one is particularly great. Yes, I’m a dad and yes I pack the lunches and yes I love Totoro. So should you. But what’s really great is that the bag is just simple neoprene. Other bags have more complicated construction with different materials, lots of seams and areas where mildew develops (we live in the tropics so good insulation and mildew are constant issues here). And once mildew starts, it’s often hard to get rid of. And some bags just don’t handle being put through the washer very well. Neoprene is a very tough materials, it can easily handle runs through the wash when it gets dirty, and so far no problems with mildew since March. Of course, it’s black inside already so I’m not sure I would even be able to SEE mildew. But really I’m not concerned, this is what wetsuits are made from and they are quite hardy. It also insulates quite well, comparable to more traditional insulated bags. The shape is a little awkward as it’s not a rectangle, so some things may not be able to fit that you would otherwise be able to fit in similarly sized but square container. But overall we like the simple design and the durability of it. And Totoro.
