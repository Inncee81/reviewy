Super easy changeout system, still hurts when the discs die

First off: I LOVE the new changeout system. Very easy to do, and once you do it the first time, you wonder why on earth they hadn’t invented this before! So simple, so user friendly. Awesome advance, and well worth changing out bits to get this new attachment.The metal cutting blade in this pack did very well… initially. I was sawing off screw ends that went through some plywood on a chicken coop left and right. I probably went through 20 when my crazy sensitive ears detected a slight change in tone (instead of the usual higher pitch whirrr of the rotary tool, it had deepened slightly in the sound it was making). Within 2 screws of that, it broke apart and popped me just under the nose and across my arm. Now… anyone who has used a dremel for cutting metal knows this is inevitable. This happens. A good reminder to pay attention to the tone of the machine, because that’s the only clue you get. However, the thicker blade vs the old thin reddish ones may have helped. No cuts, just a small line bruise on my arm, and a few moments of holding my mouth and my eyes watering. LoL. No bruise on my face. Sure beats cuts!So, honestly.. I’m not real sure if it’s “2x’s longer lasting” or not. I think so, but it has been a while since I did that much metal cutting. But, they give out in the same fashion (ka-blewy!)Overall, this kit is worth it if you’re still on the old tiny-screw-head holders for your cutting disks. This makes life much easier!
