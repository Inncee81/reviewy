OEMTOOLS 24667 4V Max Lithium-Ion Cordless Screwdriver

by people ...

  • 1⁄4 in. Hex drive with auto lock
  • No-load speed: 220 RPM
  • Trigger activated LED work light

The OEMTOOLS® 4V max* Lithium-Ion cordless screwdriver is used for fastening and predrilling wood, plastic and light-gauge metal and other applications. The screwdriver uses a high-capacity, 4V Lithium-Ion slide battery for longer life and more power to get the job done. The trigger activated LED work light is perfect for jobs with minimal lighting. The battery resists self-discharge and will remain ready-to-use even after storage. The charger is designed with an auto shut-off feature to save energy and prevent over-charge.
