A versatile cordless 4.8V power drill from GOCHANGE

I own several power drills already at 12V and 20V but I need something more portable for lighter works around the house like assembling furniture and fix broken drawer rails. I’m glad that I gave GOCHANGE portable cordless drill a try.The package includes a “transformable” cordless drill, a charger, 45 bits, and a box. The 45 bits includes Philips bits, Hex bits, Torx bits, slot bits, HEX sockets, and 2 drill bits. I’m surprised that GOCHANGE even incldues a bit holder/extension in the package.The cordless drill has a built-in 1300mAh Lithium-Ion battery and charging time is around 4 hours. It’s 4.8V and has max torque of 3.7N.m. To give an idea of how powerful the drill is, I would say the power drill works pretty well at assembling IKEA furniture and also can drill through dry wall easily but would take a bit longer if you’re drilling through solid wood. The best part is that the drill is transformable. You can use the drill in a 90 degree angle for normal position or a 180 degree angle to fit in tight spaces. Please see my pictures for more details. There is also a battery indicator on the drill showing how much battery left.Overall I’m pretty satisfied with the drill as it’s pretty light and can fit in tight spaces. I’d recommend this GOCHANGE portable cordless drill to people who prefer to DIY stuffs around the house.Do remember to open the box with the clips on the upper side. Otherwise all the bits would fall out of box when open.I have received this product at a discounted price. My review is honest and unbiased solely based on my experience using the product.
