Gama Sonic Rechargeable 12-Inch Cooling Fan with AM/FM Radio and MP3 Input #GS-27R

by people ...

  • Rechargeable oscillating 12-inch fan with 10-inch blade offers indoor/outdoor cooling comfort
  • Use the built-in AM/FM radio or plug in a music player or smart phone via the auxiliary port for information and entertainment in any location
  • Choice of high and low speed settings
  • Includes rechargeable battery–AC adapter for charging is included
  • Runs up to 4 hours at low speed with a single charged battery

Size:12 inches The Gama Sonic GS-27R 12-inch rechargeable fan with AM/FM radio and auxiliary input port for music players and smart phones keeps you cool and entertained at the beach, by the pool, or anywhere you happen to be–indoors or outdoors. This two-speed portable oscillating fan with a 10-inch blade is made from a durable polycarbonate with a white finish. The Gama Sonic GS-27R is lightweight for easy portability with a rechargeable battery that powers the fan up to four hour. The included AC adapter charges or runs the fan via a standard electrical outlet. fan is16 inches tall, 12 inches wide and 6 inches deep.

Great for Living Abroad

I’m studying abroad in Haiti and I do not have electricity throughout the day. I’ve traveled to Haiti previously so I knew I had to get some type of rechargeable fan. I found this on Amazon to bring with me. When the electricity goes out, my fan is still running. On low setting, the fan runs for about 5 hours. On high, it’s about 2-3, I think. The radio is a plus - the sound quality is great and the speakers are impressive. I would recommend ANYONE going abroad to buy this.

A nice fan for tailgating.

It gets hot in Florida. Tailgating for a 3:30 football game is not always so pleasant. This fan does what it needs to do and the radio works pretty well also. I decided to buy the old version after reading some reviews and it seems like I made the right choice. We received it on a Friday and the next day we put it to the test. The battery lasted about 2 1⁄2 hours on high and the radio didn’t come in as clear as I thought it would. It worked but I wanted a little longer on the battery life so I ordered a second battery. Buy the GAMA SONIC battery ONLY. The battery recommended for this fan on the page will NOT fit. Next tailgate I told my husband NOT to remove the charging plug from the back and to throw the whole thing in the car. The radio worked great! The plug acts as an antenna and it was pretty loud for a small speaker. The fan lasted the whole tailgate on high with the radio on. I haven’t run the battery down all the way again yet. It puts out a pretty decent breeze and we have used it several times already. It also worked well when the grill was really smoking to blow the smoke away from us, much better. I can only hope the games get scheduled a little later in the day but we will still be using this fan because it gets HOT in Florida. Several people around us have since gotten this fan just from them watching us and asking about it. I also like the fact the in the h season it is a backup if we have no power and therefore no a/c.
