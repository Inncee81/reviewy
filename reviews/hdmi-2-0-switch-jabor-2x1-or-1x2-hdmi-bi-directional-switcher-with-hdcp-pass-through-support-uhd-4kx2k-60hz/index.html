    Product Description

HDMI Bi-Directional Switcher: Allowed to Connect two HDMI sources to one monitor, HDTV, or projector or reconfigure to connect toggle between two displays connected to one HDMI source like a PS4 or a TV box ( 2 displays can NOT work simultaneously)

Gold-Plated Ports: The 24K gold-plated ports resists corrosion and reduces tarnish.

Compatibility: Compatible with HDMI display sources like Laptops, PS3/4, Blu-ray, Fire TV, Apple TV, or cable boxes, and HDMI display inputs found on HDTVs, projectors, and computer monitors.

Product Features: -Support hot plug and play. -Easy to install and operate. -Main control: switch button control. -With 2-LED display working ports. -Video Amplifier Bandwidth 3.4Gbps/340MHz. -No power needed, MAX operation current:15mA. -Support UHD 4K@60Hz & 3D,full compliant with HDMI 1.4,1.3,1.2,1.1, 1.0. -2x1 mode: Easily switch between two HDMI video sources to one HDMI display. -1x2 mode: Easily switch single HDMI video source between two displays.
