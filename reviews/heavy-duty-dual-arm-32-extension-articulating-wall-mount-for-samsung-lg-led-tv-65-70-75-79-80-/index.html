Product Reviews

It’s actually pretty nice. I was surprised

It’s actually pretty nice. I was surprised. It would probably hold the 200lb rating if I had confidence in the studs that it’s screwed into. Though that’s not the mounts fault. If anything could be better it’s that the mount seems to lean the tv just slightly to the right or left after each time the mount is extended. You can tighten and adjust all you want but the leaning is still there. An quick lift/bump with your hand and it levels out…. Until the next time you extend it. Some folks would even notice the lean.

it makes the tv viewing angle very easy to adjust

I bolted this to two studs, shifted the bracket to the far left of the base that fits against the wall, hung my 180 lb self from it while fully extended out, then mounted an 85 pound tv to it with no problems. if you fully extend it out, you have to pull the arms slightly apart to push it back in, put otherwise, it makes the tv viewing angle very easy to adjust. It even allows for a tilt down feature if it is placed high like over the fireplace. Great product.

Really disappointed for the price I paid

2 secure bolts broke in half just installing the mount without the tv, imagine what would have happened if I actually had my tv on. What kind of material are you guys shipping?? Really disappointed for the price I paid!!! Not sure if hanging my tv with this mount is a good idea now.

Great Buy for 65” TV

Husband needed this for already existing built in wall unit that wouldn’t fit 65 inch tv. This worked out perfect!! Tv is pulled out flush with unit, looks amazing! Very sturdy!!!
