Nice Case

I’m using this case for a Panasonic FZ1000 and it fits, but it is a bit tight. Ideally, another 3⁄4 of an inch taller would provide a cleaner fit. If you want the smallest case that will fit the FZ1000 and extra batteries and filters, then this is it. It will fit with the camera strap on the camera. The only tight spot is at the top, so the viewfinder slightly raises the cover when zipped. It still zips okay, but just a little tight. Some may find a tight fit preferable, but I think it should be slightly taller for the FZ1000. The zipper is smooth, the fabric seems durable, and it looks good. The strap is of adequate quality. The stitching seems to be of good quality and the case will stand on its own with or without a camera inside.
