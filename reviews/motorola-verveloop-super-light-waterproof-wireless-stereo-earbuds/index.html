Good headphones, but misleading (poor) battery life.

Mixed feelings on these headphones. Solid fit on the earbuds, and I’m a fan of the build construction in general. They don’t easily tangle if you just leave them in your bag, for instance, and the weight/balance is sufficient for them to remain firmly in your ear (for general walking, not rubbing or exercising). On the other hand, the battery life (and accompanying software responsible for battery life notifications) is all over the place, and starkly different from the manufacturer’s advertised 9.5+ hours.Battery life is the most significant performance aspect for me, so I test the units rigorously before paying judgement. On the low end of the scale, I’ve gotten as little as 5.5 hours of playtime. On the higher end, as much as 6.5, leaving us with an average of 6 hours - a stark contrast with the advertised 9.5+ hours of playtime. So, the monitoring software will inform you that you have ‘more than 3 hours available’ (which is a great feature in principle), followed shortly by a ‘low battery’ warning, followed shortly by the device turning off. It’s hard to gauge my unit’s performance with others, since people aren’t measuring and talking about it.For myself, I’m still debating how to go about addressing this with the manufacturer.

This light is way too bright. I imagine it’s a bit distracting to others around me.
