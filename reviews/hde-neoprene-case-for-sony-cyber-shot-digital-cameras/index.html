    Product Description

Road trips can be quite the ordeal, both for you and for your equipment. Luckily, this black hard case is a great way to keep your compact digital cameras protected and looking like new whether you’re kicking up sand at the beach or rushing back to your hotel through a sudden rainstorm. The case will help prevent dust, dirt, scratches, and dents from ruining your camera, so you can keep capturing those precious memories.

Fits Sony Cyber-Shot DSC T70, T700, T77, T90, T900, TX1, W180, T99, TX9, and more!

Very Nice For The Price!

Obviously, you would not expect a case for this price to be very good, but for the price point, it is a great little case, as long as you understand that it is not intended to be the ultimate case for every situation. This case is not something meant to protect a camera against falls, water (it might absorb a drop or two of rain better than a bare camera, but it is definitely a porous material), and certainly not against crushing. It is very good at the two primary concerns I have about my camera, however: scratches and dust.I purchased this case for a Sony Cyber-Shot DSC-W530, based on the reviews here. I found that the case was just a little larger than I would have liked, but does fit the camera without enough room for it to slide around. I was expecting a snugger fit, but am happy with it overall.The material is a sponge-y cloth material, and is thicker than I thought it was going to be from just looking at it here on the webpage. The zipper teeth are made of a smooth plastic, so they should not accidentally scratch your camera when slipping it in and out of the case. The pulls for the zipper are made of a hard plastic, and I have my fingers crossed that they hold up for a long period of time. It did come with a carabiner clip, though I could not determine a way in which I would use it personally, and it went into my spare parts drawer. The zipper takes a little focus at times, as the case is fairly elastic, and pulling the zipper at certain angles prevents it from moving around the corners, but it is merely a physics(?) problem, not a problem with the product.I had to use a razor blade to shave a small burr (leftover from the manufacturing process) from one of the zipper pulls that I found annoying, but other than that the case seems to be built well and durable enough to last a while. At this price, if it doesn’t last forever, I would not regret the purchase, anyway.
