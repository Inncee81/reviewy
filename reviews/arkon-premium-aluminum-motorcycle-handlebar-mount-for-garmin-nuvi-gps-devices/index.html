Product Specifications

  • Mount fits on handlebars measuring up to 31.75mm or 1.25 inches in diameter
  • Made of aluminum and is easy to install
  • Designed for use with Garmin nuvi GPS devices with 17mm swivel ball pattern

  • 2 year limited warranty

    Product Description

Package Type:Standard Packaging Arkon’s MC2B premium aluminum motorcycle handlebar mount is compatible with Garmin nuvi 40, 50, 200, 2013, 24x5, and 25x5 Series devices, as well as Garmin nuvi 1450, 1450T, 1490T, 1200, 1250, 1260T, 1300, 1350, 1350T, 1370T, and 1390T models. This mount package includes only the Premium Motorcycle Mount with 17mm Swivel Ball. (The above Garmin nuvi devices should include a 17mm swivel ball-compatible holder; replacement holders sold separately.) Available in black or chrome (see Arkon Model MC2C) finishes, the mount is made of aluminum and features a minimal but solid design. The mount fits on handlebars measuring up to 31.75mm or 1.25 inches in diameter and is easy to install with the included hex key. (Three handlebar spacer/adapter rings of different widths are also included with the mount. Choose the appropriate size for optimum mounting to your handlebar.) To use the mount with small action cameras with a 1/4”-20 camera pattern, Arkon recommends Model MC2BCAM. Or, combine the MC2B with a water-resistant case for smartphones or GPS devices with Model MC2B5WPCS. For use with Arkon universal smartphone and tablet holders with the Dual-T configuration, Arkon recommends Model MC2BSBH.

solid as a chunk of aluminum.

I purchased this to fix a bike phone mount that was too small for my handlebars. I liked the holder (Bestrix Universal Phone Bike Mount Holder) and it did fit the standard 17mm swivel ball). There is no doubt this mount is well made and solid. The issue I had was that the shims (comes with 3) are all a hard plastic and didn’t allow for the “custom” fit I had hoped for. Had the shims been of rubber, it would have allowed more flexibility with odd sizes (most road handlebars are 31.8mm so neither the 30 or 40 really fit right). I ended up using tape to protect the bar and provide a more custom fit. Once installed, I think I could hang off this thing without it budging, it is that solid. I am concerned that the screws may not weather well and this is a tad heavier than I expected (not necessarily a bad thing unless your counting every gram on your bike), but worked well for my application.

Quality design / poor manufacturing

Overall, the design of this Garmin mount is very good. However, the size of the spherical head of the mount is ever so slightly too small. This results in the mounted GPS to swivel far too easily, almost drooping on its own. I expected a much firmer mount, where the GPS required a some bit of resistance to be moved.I will await a response from the reseller before deciding whether to return this mount to Amazon.
