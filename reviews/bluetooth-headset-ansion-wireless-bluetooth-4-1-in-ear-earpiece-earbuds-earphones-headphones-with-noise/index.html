Great headset

Fabulous little bluetooth headset indeed. I wanted a headset for when I’m driving in my van as didn’t want to risk getting pulled over by the police. The headset itself is nice and small and comes with charging cable, clip and spare ear bud cover. It fits nicely over the ear and is embossed with a nice pattern.Operating the headset is very easy indeed. To pair first of all you press anywhere on the embossed section to power on the device and set your phone to bluetooth discovery mode. Then once paired you can either listen to music through one ear or just use it to make or received phone calls. When a call comes in simply press the embossed section once to answer a call and the same to hang up. On the side are two volume control buttons to increase and decrease the volume. Also on the side is a micro usb port for charging the device.Sound quality is outstanding for the price you pay and it feels very good to wear. Would definitely recommend to anyone to get one of these especially if you don’t have a handsfree kit in your car.

Good Voice Quality

I’ve always thought that a phone headset should have the microphone as close to your mouth as possible, so I’ve favored ones with a boom mic in the past. But this one seems to have no trouble picking up my voice, and the people I talk with on the phone report that it sounds fine. It has a long-lasting battery. Do note that this headset comes with a short microUSB cable for charging the internal battery, but does NOT come with the charger itself.
