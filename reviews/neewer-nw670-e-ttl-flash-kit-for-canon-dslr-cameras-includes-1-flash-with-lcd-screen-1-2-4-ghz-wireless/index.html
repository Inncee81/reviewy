Product Reviews

Love this flash. Update a year later

I just got it. I am new to photography and have not used a speedlight or really any flash before.But I am in love. When you add the neewer 6x8 soft box, it’s a great pairing. I was able to take lots of photos of friends and family at a recent party and between this light and the extra soft box, I was able to get well lit photos that weren’t harsh. The added goodies including remote/triggers only sweetens the deal.I haven’t used a canon speed light before, but for 1/6th the price, I can’t imagine the Canon being THAT much better.I can’t speak to durability but so far so good.*A year later, I still have this flash. I was using it to take pictures last Thanksgiving and noticed a weird electrical burning smell. There was a dark spot on the front glass/plastic. I had been taking back to back high flash shots. So I would be careful firing off a ton of shots in quick succession. I had the built in diffuser down and the pop on one as well, so that might have added to the heat build up. The flash still works and I haven’t had an issue since. I don’t use this a lot, but when I do, I am glad I have it. Even outdoor shots really benefit from a nice flash. As the holidays approach it’ll be time to take plenty of family portraits between my side and my in-laws, I plan on buying a 2nd, and maybe 3rd flash w/ receivers to supplement my one flash. I might upgrade to a faster Neewer “speedlight.” I’d still highly recommend this flash for any DSLR owner who doesn’t want to spend a fortan on a flash.
