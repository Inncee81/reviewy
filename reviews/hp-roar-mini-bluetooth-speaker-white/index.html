Product Reviews

So much in a small package!

Connected very quickly with my old iPhone 5S that I’ve been using as my iPod and place where I follow the podcasts that I love. I also store music on it and things like that.This speaker I got at a ridiculously great price from a sale or something and I wasn’t expecting much to be quite honest. However, I was pleasantly surprised! This speaker plays the high highs and the lowest lows. It doesn’t have too much bass, but just enough that it makes a difference. It produces rich sound in a tiny package. I haven’t tried playing it yet without it plugged in (because I haven’t needed to), but I can’t wait to use it for the holidays at work.The only downside is that one is given very little instruction about pairing and things like that. If I didn’t have other mini bluetooth speakers in the past, I wouldn’t have known and would have had to look it up. It’s simple, but a little clear instruction makes a difference.All in all, I would ABSOLUTELY suggest this speaker to others and may buy another in the near future for my kid.

Works now, but took effort

I needed a portable bluetooth speaker and choose this, mainly because it was orange.I have to say it took me around 4 hours to get the speaker connected to my pc. Well, maybe 3 minutes to get it connected, but then 4 hours to get sound to come out. Turns out the pairing is fine and seamless. Getting the sound to come out of them is another thing completely.I searched high and low for answers but ran across a solution which described the problem in terms of an ACER pc. Just so happens, I have an Acer. So, I re-downloaded the latest bluetooth drivers from Acer (which were dated years ago) but that did the trick.Now I can take my speaker all over the house and outside and have my music or tv show playing. I really love it.Oh, and I am not sure any of the problems I had were HP’s fault. Just one of the glitches I guess.
