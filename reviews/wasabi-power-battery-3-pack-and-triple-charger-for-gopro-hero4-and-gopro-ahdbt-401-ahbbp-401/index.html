They just don’t fit, and are poorly made.

The Wasabi Batteries, work, yes. But if you want to avoid damaging the door for the battery, I would stay away. The batteries are not square, and increase from a low of 1.4260” all the way to 1.4545” at the high end. The batteries that come standard with the Gopro Hero4 are 1.4250 at the MAX (ribbon laying down) and it is consistent. I have to say that I am no doubt disappointed with the fit inside the battery compartment (trying to squeeze the door closed). Also the width on the original GoPro batteries is 1.2830” at the max, whereas the Wasabi Batteries are 1.2900” which also causes some concern for the frugal user who does not want to void his/her warranty. Despite this, they did get the depth correct at .4260”. Also, the two batteries that came with this kit were WIDELY different from EACHOTHER. The power is there, but not the quality or the accuracy to keep me easy minded about using them. I will probably return them after further testing.(Gopro battery is the odd one out in all pictures, it has the rounded bottom to it)It is unfortunate, because I had high hopes.

Great product and more affordable than original GoPro stuff

I bought this product to use with my GoPro Hero 4 Silver. In my opinion, the Wasabi brand is just as good as the original GoPro stuff. The batteries charge just as fast and last just as long as the original GoPro battery that came with my action camera. I like how the Wasabi charger allows you to charge two batteries at the same time, which is pretty convenient when you are pressed for time once in a while like I am. The charger here will also charge the original GoPro battery as well, which is a huge plus. Overall, I am 100% satisfied with my purchase here. If you are looking for chargers and batteries that are more affordable than the original GoPro ones but work just as good, then look no further. Buy these and you will love them!
