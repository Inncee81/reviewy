Great deal: Bucket and Baseballs

I purchased the Diamond 6-gallon ball bucket with 30 balls so my little leaguer could practice his pitching (and batting), and also so I could have a seat without having to drag my folding chairs too and from the baseball field. The balls that come inside the bucket are pretty decent and they do work perfectly for my needs. What sold me was the balls that come within the bucket, because I always find myself buying packs of baseballs over and over again as they seem to disappear each time he returns home from the baseball field. I figured this way after practice, I could ensure all the balls are accounted for and are placed the bucket (which is also my seat). You can’t find a better price. This deal is too good to pass up. I’ve actually searched for a cheaper price with other vendors but for some reason folks are only selling the bucket. Why would I pay $50+ just for a bucket? Anyway, I’m very happy with this purchase.
