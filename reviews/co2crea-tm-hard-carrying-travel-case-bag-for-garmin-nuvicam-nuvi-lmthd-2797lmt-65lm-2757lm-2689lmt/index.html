Interesting case. Does not fit Garmin 65LM with all accesories. Did not work for me. YMMV.

I bought recently a Garmin 65LM. I was looking for a case to enclose everything, so I can move the GPS to different cars in a single container. This unit had good reviews and the price was right. It arrived quickly and no issues.Unfortunately the complete GPS does not fit. First, the unit itself is very tight in the cover of the case. It barely fits the thickness of the Garmin. The protection appears insufficient. It does not feel protected enough. And second, the suction base does not fit. The original packaging of the 65LM is clever in letting the lip of the round suction cup appear next to the unit to minimize the box size. That is not the case of this carrying case, so the case needs to be deeper. I tried many ways and it would not fit. It is quite big and may work for other brand GPS units. Cute mesh zipper keeps the accessories separate.I ended returning the unit, and Amazon made it painless. Your unit may be different and may work well. In may case it did not. As final comment I find the top portion not offering enough protection to the electronics. I will continue my search.

Almost perfect!

Good case for the Garmin Nuvi 2789LM and the included accessories with some room to spare. The windshield mount can be stored in this case along with the navigation unit but it will be putting pressure up against the navigation unit when closed (no matter what orientation the mount is in). Because I leave that piece in my car this does not bother me much, but for people who take that piece in and out of their cars and want to put everything in this case, some might not feel comfortable with how much pressure is up against the navigation unit when sharing the case with the windshield mount, even with the foam divider wall in between them. Case cannot fit the sun shade visor clip-on accessory due to the fact the divider net that is used to keep the accessories on one side, zippers from the middle instead of around the sides and the case isn’t tall enough. Would be the perfect case if it was just 1⁄2” taller and the inside zipper was rerouted around the 3 sides instead of down the middle. Hopefully the manufacturer takes my comment into consideration.
