Sturdier than expected

I wasn’t sure about the quality of this device, especially given the price. But, it stayed put during my 4 hour car ride, though the windshield wasn’t too hot/cold. I had to configure the stand so it rested on my dash; otherwise, without that support, it shook the phone with each bump in the road. The flexible portion is a little difficult to bend, but I guess this helps it stay in shape. The cradle provides a button for quick release of the phone. I have a simple case on my iPhone 6 (no lifeproof case), and it fits well in the cradle.

Use with my iPhone 6, with charger and headphones

I use this for my iPhone 6. There is minimal bounce while driving and the mount does not sag with use. The little support foot on the bottom has the right spacing to accommodate the charging port/cord, but does have the potential to interfere with headphones (using for hands-free). I use the original Apple headphones, plug them and the charging cord in first, then put my phone in the mount with the cords going on either side of the foot at the bottom. The phone sits a tiny bit crooked doing this, but it is not distracting and is still held securely. The release is a little slow to respond, but overall, the product does it’s job decently. I’ve only had the mount come detached once on a very hot day.
