The Ti S6 is the world’s best selling tennis racquet ever. That can be said about only one racquet and this is it; and it says it all. It has gotten the best reviews from many tennis magazines. Product Reviews

Great value. Super racquet. Happy with this purchase!

45 yr old untrained casual tennis player but very athletic and have messed around with tennis my whole life, just never took it too seriously or played competitively. This is the only racket over $30 I’ve ever owned so it feels fantastic in my hand. Nice slightly tacky grip that won’t slip from sweat…feels light but also feels sturdy at the same time. Seems to have plenty of sweet spot and pretty forgiving from the balls I hit.Took it to the high school courts the day it arrived and served a ton of balls and hit off a backboard. My serves were considerably more powerful and accurate than with any racquet I’ve ever played with so I am extremely optimistic about that. Hitting off the backboard, my shots were very accurate…seemed to go right where I wanted the ball to go. I move very well around the court and I can tell this racquet will make me that much better. The cover is a little on the tight side. When inserting and removing the racquet there’s not much wiggle room and you have to be deliberate to unzip the zipper all the way and gently remove the racquet so as not to stress and rip the zipper and material around it. I bought a nicer bag anyways but the one it came with is just ok. Overall this seems to be a really nice racquet at a good price point. I recommend it.Would give it a 5 star rating but giving it 4 stars only because it’s a very basic looking racquet and the cover is very basic. Some nice colors on this raquet would look great although it’s more about the feel than the look.
