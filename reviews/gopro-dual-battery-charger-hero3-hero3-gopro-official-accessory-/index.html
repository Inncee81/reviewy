Functional, but minimal, battery charger

The charger works as promises: it charges the batteries. Unlike most digital camera battery chargers, that plug directly into a wall outlet, this charger is powered by a USB connection. It works fine but that’s one more cable and adapter to bring along; I wish the charger would connect directly to the wall.

Great Charger!

I’ve been looking for a while for a charger for the great GoPro system I have. This one fits the bill in all ways. I love the ability to charge more than one battery at a time.

Easy Charge!

This is such a quick way to recharge your GoPro batteries after an exciting day of adventures. All you have to do is remove the battery from your camera and put them in the slots. Then plug it into an outlet and the light will come on to let you know that it is charging up. Then a green light will come up to let you know that it is done charging. I usually just leave it overnight and take it out in the morning.It will charge your batteries about the same time your camera charges it, but you can do two at the time it takes for one. So you can do more action adventure scenes. This is from GoPro not an after market one, so it is fully compatible with your 3 and 4 series cameras. It has a nice rubber grip, so that it does not slip out of your hand, making it feel nice and solid.

So happy I purchased!

Love that I can charge up two batteries at once and know I am set for a day of diving and snorkeling. I charge both overnight, in the morning both lights are green, and I set out. Would highly recommend for anyone taking long day trips without the ability to re-charge throughout the day!When it comes to GoPro, I don’t mess with knock-offs or off-brand batteries and chargers. There is nothing worse than seeing or doing something awesome, only to realize your camera battery is dead. For me, this item drastically decreases the likelihood of this happening!

Charging two while using one, perfect!

On my GoPro adventures, this was the single best investment. The dual charger allows me to charge two batteries while I’m using another one. I never run out of GoPro battery! It’s amazing!!!Using this and a solar charger, I was able to record our vacation the entire time we were awake. My wife hated seeing the red flashing light above my brow, but I was not going to stop recording as long as I had battery to last. I figured out how to turn off the red flashing light, but didn’t turn it off because at that point I’d already taken all the slack for it. Might as well just leave it on. This way she didn’t win.Anyways, without this charger I wouldn’t have been able to record so much of our vacation.
