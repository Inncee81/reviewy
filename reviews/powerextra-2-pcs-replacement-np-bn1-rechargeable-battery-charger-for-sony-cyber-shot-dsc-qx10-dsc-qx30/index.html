Good deal on decent batteries.

Item is just as described. For the price, you can;’t go wrong. I don’t know if the batteries will last as long as the stock Sony battery, but to get 2 extra, plus the charger for less than $15, it really doesn’t matter. I LOVE the fact that the batteries come with a small plastic cover That is a great touch that I have never seen before. It is always nice to not have batteries lying around loose in a camera bag or case. Sony’s in camera charging is nice, but it is nice to be able to charge a battery while still being able to use the camera with another battery. I will give one warning…Ignore the hype/claim about a higher capacity than Sony batteries. That is not all that matters when it comes to battery life, and these batteries will definitely NOT give you more, or even the same, life as the Sony battery that came with your camera. If you get 60% consider yourself lucky. I knew going in than they would not last longer than the OEM battery, but I DID think they would last a little longer than they did. It’s a shame they feel the need to falsely advertise these as lasting longer than Sony batteries. They could be honest and it would still be a good deal. So, as long as you know going in that you are getting maybe 120% more battery life (combined between the two) plus the charger, you won’t be disappointed. (Unless I just happened to get a bad set, but I doubt it. And I am not going to ask fora refund because like I said, for the price, even with the shorter battery life, it is still a good deal.
