A Fantastic Option for Carrying Water with You!

I bought 3 of these for my kids to take on hiking field trips and they’re pretty awesome! My kids are completely in love with the vivid pictures on the outside and so are their friends. I fill the water bottles half full, freeze them, and then fill them the rest of the way before putting them in these holders and my kids have shown me that there is still ice in the bottle even after 9-10 hours! (Granted, they aren’t sitting in the sun all of those 9 hours, but that’s still pretty amazing!) The only reason I couldn’t give these holders 5 stars is due to the fact that the ONLY bottle of water I could find that would fit in them was Smart Water (the one liter size). We will buy our kids reusable bottles soon, but we haven’t yet and had to buy plastic ones. I took one of the holders to the grocery store with me and tried several brands. None of them would fit into the opening except the Smart Water- and I prefer water without so many additives. But, as I can still reuse the bottle once the water is gone, it all works out in the end and I will buy more of these in the future!

Love our glass water bottles

We have Camelbak and Life Factory bottles for our kids. I was reeeeeeeeeeeally hoping to be able to put the glass bottles, while still encased in the silicone sleeves for extra drop protection, into the neoprene sleeve. Love our glass water bottles, but the lack of a shoulder strap for carrying isn’t terrific. The kids LOVED the designs of these neoprene sleeves and are really excited about using them, so I have pulled the silicone sleeves off of the bottles and put the glass bottles alone into the neoprene sleeve…. and am keeping my fingers crossed. I probably could have forced the bottles in the silicone sleeves into neoprene, but it would be a pain in the neck to do that each time in order to wash the bottles of kid grime. If these were just a smidge bigger, they’d be perfect for us. If having the extra silicone layer doesn’t matter much to you, these little carrying cases are really cute :)
