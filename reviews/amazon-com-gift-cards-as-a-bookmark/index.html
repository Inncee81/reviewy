Very eco-friendly – a gift card that can be repurposed!

When I went browsing gift cards online, I was looking at the packaging to figure out what could be reused and what was basically a throw-away. The gift cards in small metal tins make a lovely presentation, and the tins can be reused if you’ve got something small to store or if you give a lot of gift cards. But even with the gift cards in metal tins, the gift card itself gets thrown away or recycled after you’ve redeemed it. However, this bookmark is a refreshing change from your average gift card, especially for people who like reading or have other uses for page markers. It comes in a cardboard sleeve, which is tucked inside a fitted clear plastic zip-close bag.
