This tripod base stand’s hybrid sheet metal and composite leg housing make it virtually unbreakable. An extremely stable stand, even on irregular surfaces, the MS7700B is an excellent choice for use in a variety of settings. The long foldable legs also make this stand ideal for the touring pro!

Product Reviews

Great mic stand! Does the job, but a few caveats to consider!

Good mic stand! Does the job and can be easily packed up for a gig. I mostly use this for home recording, but it is very nice to be able to use this and move it around. The adjustable height is also useful for various heights.I’m giving it 4 instead of 5 stars for two reasons. The first is that the three legs of the tripod are a bit wobbly, even when the screw is the tightest you can make it. It’s not too bad, but it’s noticeable especially if you accidentally knock into it when recording or if have an Acoustic Shield and a mic on top of it. With heavier objects, the legs become a bit questionable. The second is that the 3 legs lock into place. There becomes a few scuff marks on those legs the more you lock it into place and unlock it. The scuff marks were noticeable after the first lock/unlock.Other than those two things, the mic stand does its job! And at a pretty decent price too!

I will add to this review once I complete the setup since I do not yet have the mic or shield.
