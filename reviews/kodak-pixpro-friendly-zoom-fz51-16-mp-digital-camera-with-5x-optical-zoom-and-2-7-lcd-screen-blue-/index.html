easy to use

I bought this to replace a much more expensive Nikon Coolpix with tons of “features”. This camera is smaller, lighter, and takes better pictures with MUCH MUCH MUCH less fiddling. It is very easy to use while still getting good pictures and videos.Here is why it is so cheap: the screen is low resolution. So when you look at your photos on the tiny phone screen, they look pixelated and blurry. But the pictures are actually fine once you print them or look at them on your computer; it is just the inexpensive screen can’t display the full resolution.
