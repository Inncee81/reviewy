Product Specifications

  • Allows you to quickly change rod holder positions with a simple twist
  • You can use your favorite, Fully adjustable Scotty rod holder and adjust it to any vertical or horizontal position without having to drill permanent mounting holes

    Product Description

You can use your favorite, fully adjustable Scotty rod holder and adjust it to any vertical or horizontal position with the Gear Head Track Adapter, without having to drill permanent mounting holes. Designed to fit all kayak track systems, the adapter quickly slides into place and locks down post mounted Scotty rod holders and accessories. Change accessories with a simple twist.
