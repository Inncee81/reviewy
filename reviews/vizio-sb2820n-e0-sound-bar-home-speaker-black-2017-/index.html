A Good Choice for a Smaller Sound Bar

This sound bar was easily connected to our TV and it produces excellent audio. The bass and treble can be adjusted along with the usual volume control. The smaller size of this unit is just perfect for a smaller TV, but the quality of the sound does not suffer. There are controls on the top of the sound bar as well as those on the remote control included with the device.The fit and finish of the Vizio sound bar is quite good and the bar seems solid.
