Product Specifications

  • 16.4MP BSI-CMOS Sensor
  • Waterproof to 50ft/15m, freezeproof to 14 DegreeF/-10 DegreeC, shockproof to 5.8ft/1.75m, and dustproof
  • Fujinon 5x Optical Zoom (28-140mm)
  • Wi-Fi sharing and remote shooting

  • Interval Shooting Mode + Time Lapse Movie

    Product Description

Color:Black/Orange  |  Style:Base Compact and portable, yet rugged enough to handle even the most trying of conditions, the blue FinePix XP90 Digital Camera from Fujifilm is a point-and-shoot featuring water, shock, freeze, and dustproof construction. A 16.4MP 1⁄2.3” CMOS sensor produces high-resolution stills and full HD 1080p video, which is complemented by a 5x Fujinon zoom lens that covers a 28-140mm equivalent focal length range. Sensor-shift image stabilization minimizes the appearance of camera shake while features such as an Action Camera mode and a 320 fps Slow Motion mode provide additional creative options when recording video. A rear LCD monitor features an anti-reflective coating for greater visibility in a variety of lighting conditions, and built-in Wi-Fi connectivity permits one-touch transferring of imagery to linked mobile devices, as well as wireless remote controlled shooting from smartphones and tablets.

Product Reviews

Great waterproof camera at an affordable price

This camera is awesome. It takes great pictures, and is waterproof. The videos I took didn’t turn out bad either. Sure, the quality isn’t as good as my DSLR, but who takes a DSLR in the water? I also didn’t have any issues with the water coming into the battery door. It’s pretty simple, turn a quarter turn to lock it, a quarter turn to unlock it. You have to push the door closed before you do either of these things. I’m not sure how anyone would have any issue with this unless they were ham fisting it. 10⁄10, would buy again.

Works well for a quick point and shoot camera

The camera has worked better than I expected so far! I haven’t taken any underwater pics yet, but plan to this weekend. However, for everyday photos it has done better than I thought (based on some of the other reviews and it being a refurbished camera). No complaints so far!

Great camera, one minor design flaw IMHO

I’ve owned half a dozen Fuji shockproof cameras for work and they are great. My only complaint on this one is the shutter button is a little harder to press than previous versions so there’s a nuance to it. Not a deal breaker at all, it just sits pretty flat on the housing and it would have been nice to have a little more upward mount to make it easier to snap.

Perfect for what I needed.

Perfect point-and-shoot for soggy adventures on the river!

This camera was exactly what I was looking for in a point and shoot waterproof camera. I bought this for a rafting trip in Colorado and could not have been happier with how the pictures turned out. Sometimes I debate about having a camera that can accept AAA batteries instead of lithium batteries like this but, it held a good charge most of the week and when it finally ran down, I got a quick recharge from a friend’s power bank. I didnt try any underwater shots because the water was a bit muddy so no comment on underwater shot quality. Panoramic feature was awesome for some canyon shots too!

I like the pictures my cell phone takes just as much …

I bought a refurbished unit and saved about 20% by doing so. It has worked flawlessly and takes decent pictures. I bought it specifically to take scuba diving in Tahiti and it served its purpose. I like the pictures my cell phone takes just as much but I am not taking my cell phone anywhere near water, let alone in it. It is a nice addition to other cameras you might have - maybe just use this one at the beach and pool. The wifi photo transfer was easy and worked every time. I was glad I had extra batteries because the combination of taking pictures and shooting video along with transferring really uses up the battery. I definitely recommend this camera.
