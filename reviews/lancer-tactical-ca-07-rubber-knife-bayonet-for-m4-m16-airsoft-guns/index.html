Great product. Noticed a little up-and-down wobble, but nothing to be concerned about. Overall, it’s of solid construction and shouldn’t fail you unless you throw it directly at a wall. However, after reading other reviews on this product, I should note that I received the Lancer Tactical version, as posted, so those of you who receive different products might not find it so pleasant as I.
