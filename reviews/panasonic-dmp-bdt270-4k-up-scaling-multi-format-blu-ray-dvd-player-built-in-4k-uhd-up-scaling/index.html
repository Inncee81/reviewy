Product Specifications

Got a 4K TV? Then make the most of it by adding four times as much resolution to your Blu-rays with the BDT270’s 4K Upscaling system. Even without a 4kTV and only a standard TV (with HDMI inputs) you can take advantage of many modern conveniences this unit has to offer.Also enjoy internet, smartphone and multimedia content via its Smart Network Player.
