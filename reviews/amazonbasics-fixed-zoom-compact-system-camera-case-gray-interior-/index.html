Product Reviews

Simple and Straightforward

This product is well built and straightforward and is perfect if you’re looking for a compact camera case that can hold just your camera with some basic accessories. It is built for larger cameras that don’t take interchangeable lenses. It has room it to hold a microfiber cloth, but not much else. I got it for my Nikon COOLPIX P520 and it fits perfectly. If you’re looking for a case to fit with the Nikon COOLPIX P510 or P500 they will also fit. Feel free to ask any questions.

I like it

This is a great product and works perfectly for our smaller “mirro-less” Samsung DSLR and it’s small zoom lens. My true rating would probably be a 4.5 because, really, this product is just as described, does the job well, and was received on time. I have zero complaints. I just can’t seem to get myself to click the 5th start because I don’t “LOVE” it. I just like it a lot.The bag is light and appears well-made. All the zippers work smoothly and seem of high quality. The insides provide good protection for a smaller to mid-size camera and there is enough room left over in the side pockets and such for things like an extra battery, charger, memory cards and the like. The shoulder strap, while basic, is of good length and material. Really you can’t go wrong on this.

Really great bag, especially for the price

This fit our new Nikon Coolpix L340 camera. It has a divider that can be placed anywhere in the main compartment. It has velcro on it’s sides so it can be placed anywhere that works best. It has 2 compartments (Mesh net with elastic) inside the two outer compartments. There is an adjustable shoulder strap and a handle. It seems to be built very well and I anticipate it lasting for many years. It’s a sharp looking case too. It’s probably too much of a case for our needs but for the price, we couldn’t pass it up. Trust me, you won’t regret buying this

Quality at a fair price

A bit larger than I was looking for but definitely a quality product. Padding is excellent and seams are tough. I was looking for something to carry a little Nikon point and shoot with a few extras. This will hold a DSLR with regular lens and batteries plus one or two other small items. Not what I was looking for but quality nonetheless.

This is well worth the $$$

Very well made bag! It fits my Nikon Coolpix L340 perfectly. I am able to fit the user manual, USB cable and lense wipes all in the bag. The bag is very sturdy and will definitely hold up to everyday wear and tear, camping, birthday parties, weddings, etc.

Good bag for a Nikon Coolpix L830

This is a great little bag for my Nikon Coolpix L830. The camera fits very snugly with the lens pointed down. If you need it, when the camera is in the bag, there is a little room on top of the back of the camera. As others have said, the side pockets are very shallow and you’re not going to get much more than about 6 AA batteries inside them. All in all though, if you are looking for a compact bag for the non-SLR cameras out there, this should work great.

Its nothing fancy

I am a photographer who bought the Nikon P520 for my sister. Now as a Photographer I have a huge camera bag with all my stuff in it, so when I was looking at this case I was a little taken back. It is a great case, dont get me wrong. It is built nice, there are nice pockets to hold memory cards and other small stuff and the shoulder strap is nice. The only problem is its size. The camera fits nicely in the case but that is it. There is really not a lot of room for anything else. That maybe exactly what you need, it may not. That is why I gave it a 4⁄5.

Great Product At A Great Value!

This camera bag is perfect! I recently bought the GE x450 Power Pro Series camera and was looking for a small but durable bag to house my camera and accessories as I was traveling in New York. This bag is a prefect fit for my camera and holds plenty of extra batteries in the two outer zip pockets and cables in the inside pocket. There is also another zip pocket inside on the top, I use it to hold an extra memory card. It has a great amount of padding and I feel my camera is plenty protected, now granted I wouldn’t just go dropping my camera all willy-nilly even while in the case. You definitely won’t regret this purchase!
