CYMA MP5 review.

I am rating this aeg five stars for many reasons. The first has to be the price, but don’t let the price fool you, it is cheap and really good. The quality of this gun is more than excellent for the price, but good for airsoft in general. The plastic is textured and doesn’t feel flimsy at all. The stock has low wobble and multiple positions, but beware the stock position locking piece is sketchy, a little wobbly and sometimes doesn’t lock into place until you move the stock just right, this might be the locking piece or the stock is just very precise. The sling sucks and the sling mounts don’t look great but I have yet to break one. The iron sights are not adjustable, but are accurate. The selector switch is great with a nice click, and the trigger response is great but make sure you complete the shot or it could stop right before it actually shoots the round. The front grip houses the 7.2 small battery, but is secure and no wobble. The cocking arm is great although it does nothing, and the flash hider is horrible but you can take it off and the threaded barrel is orange so you can leave it just like that or add the suppressor. The stick mag can be touchy if you aren’t careful, the dual mag high cap is nice but it doesn’t like to shoot the last 40 or so pellets. Your scopes are NOT broke, it just requires some construction with pieces included in the small parts baggy. My red dot sucks though because it blinds me more than it helps me see. The internals aren’t that bad. The gearbox is plastic, but for being a plastic gearbox it works really good. Even better it has a hop-up adjustment on the right side of the gun, and if you adjust it correctly it will shoot beautifully. I would only recommend this for small games at most, no big or “pro” games. I hope this review helps you with your purchase decision of this gun.
