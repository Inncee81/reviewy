Product Specifications

  • Signature Vortex technology moves air up to 40 feet
  • 3 speed settings with remote and push-button controls
  • Oscillates and flips for multi-directional airflow
  • Quiet operation and energy efficient

  • Backed by a 5-year satisfaction guarantee

    Product Description

The Vornado Flippi V Compact Air Circulator powerfully circulates the air around you using Vornado’s signature Vortex technology to move air up to 40 feet. The Flippi V oscillates and flips to direct air where you need it and offers 3 speeds to provide a quiet breeze in high style. Other features include a remote control and push-button controls on the base. This modern personal air circulator will add sophistication to any home or office.

Office Best Friend

I got this personal fan for my office and, so far, have really enjoyed it. I started off buying the smaller Vornado V6, but found the air it pushed was laughable at best, so I returned it and got this guy. I am glad I did.If you are looking for a steady, gentle breeze from about three feet away (on the low setting), this is the model of the Flippi line to get. The low setting is practically silent (great for cubicle settings), the middle setting is louder yet still quiet, and the loud setting sounds like a plane taking off, so keep all this in mind.Also, a lot of people complain about the setting dial of the lower Flippi models, but this one is completely push-button! Even better, you can use the remote, though the beeping can be quite loud in an office setting. My only wish was to be able to get this model in a color other than black (though the black looks nice, I’m just the kind of person that likes color!).
