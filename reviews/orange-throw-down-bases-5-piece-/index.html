Thin and somewhat fragile, but they work

Bought these to help the grandchild practice, and they do the job, but they are extremely thin and require a bit of care. When it was time to put things away, he (being 5) grabbed the set and stuffed in unceremoniously down into his backpack. I took them out immediately, but the damage was done. There is now a bright crease line running down the middle of home plate, and it looks like it may snap in half if it is bent that direction again. They’ll work for what we needed, but I’m not sure I would recommend them to anyone for anything more than indoor games.
