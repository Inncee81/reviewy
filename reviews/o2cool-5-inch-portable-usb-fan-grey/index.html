Clicking sound - RESOLVED! I love this fan!

I just got the fan today and it is making a horrible clicking sound when turned on. Sounds like one of the fan blades is hitting the plastic somewhere, but I can’t tell for sure. No matter what I do, I can’t get the clicking to stop. I hope the one I got is just defective. I really like this fan. It’s a nice size and very powerful. The only problem is the clicking sound. I’ve sent an order into Amazon to have it replaced. Hopefully the next one works fine. I will update my review when that one comes in.Update: Ok, so my replacement came in the mail today and I am happy to say that this one does not have the clicking problem that the first one has. Since that is the case, this fan is now perfect. It is small and compact which makes it great for travel, and yet its a very powerful fan given its size. I really like that it can run on either batteries or usb. I also really like that it has a high or low setting. The fan is relatively quite compared to most fans so you don’t have to worry much about it disturbing your coworkers if you happen to use it as a desk fan. My purpose for buying this fan, however, is so I can take it with me when I travel abroad. I’m one of those people who can’t sleep with out a fan, and this fan here was the perfect solution!

Hot flashes be-gone!

The CFO referred to a few other retailers, and I found this seller to have the exact model I needed, and for only a few dollars more. This fan is awesome, it does have two speeds, which for a desk, or better yet, for a person experiencing a hot flash, this fan is your guy. It folds for travel, and uses three sources of energy; 4 AA batteries, USB adapter, or even the ol’ fashion outlet in the wall. This fan provides enough power to cool even the hottest hot flash.Be weary though of other look-alikes, even with the same 02Cool name, they are not the same model as this one.
