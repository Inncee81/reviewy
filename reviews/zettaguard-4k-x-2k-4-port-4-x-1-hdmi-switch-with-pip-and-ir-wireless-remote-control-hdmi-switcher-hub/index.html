    Product Description

Style:4 x 1 HDMI Switch Switch seamlessly between devices with the Zettaguard 4K x 2K 4 Port High-Speed 4 x 1 HDMI Switch with PIP (Picture in Picture) and IR Wireless Remote Control. Use the HDMI switch to connect your game consoles, laptop, desktop, and other HDMI-compatible devices to your high definition television. It supports the latest generation of HD resolutions, which includes 1080p, 3D, and 4K. This Zettaguard HDMI switcher comes with an infrared remote to let you wirelessly switch between devices in PIP (Picture in Picture) display while being in the comfort of your couch.

PIP (Picture in Picture)

Enjoy multiple displays of your devices on your TV screen with the PIP feature. This feature will allow you to watch your football game and, at the same time, check your emails on your laptop display all on the same TV screen! The PIP feature supports up to 3 sub-screen and 1 main-screen displays, allowing you to multitask and switch between main displays with a single click on your infrared remote.

Great Box with very minor downsides

I took a bit of frosted clear plastic and made a little light diffusing shield to put in front. Now I’m blinded but can still see the lights comfortably.Good product would buy again.
