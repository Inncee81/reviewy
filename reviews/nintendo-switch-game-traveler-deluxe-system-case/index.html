Product Specifications

  • Officially Licensed Nintendo Product
  • Deluxe System Case - Holds and protects complete Nintendo Switch system with a durable 1680D hard shell case for maximum protection and comfortable carrying handle
  • System Storage - Sectioned Compartments secure Screen with Joycons attached, Charging Cradle, AC adaptor with Power Cord, Joycon Grip or Switch Pro Controller
  • Mesh Pocket - Holds HDMI and USB cable, Game Card and Micro SD Cases

  • 2 Game Card Cases and 2 Micro SD Card Cases – Offering dust free protection for your cards

    Product Description

The Nintendo Switch Game Traveler Deluxe System Case, holds and protects the complete Nintendo Switch System. It has sectioned compartments to secure the screen with joycons attached, charging cradle, AC Adapter with power cord, and Joycon Grip or Switch Pro Controller. There is a mess pocket to hold HDMI & USB cable, as well as game card & micro SD cases. 2 Game Card Cases hold 4 game cards each. 2 Micro SD card cases which can easily fit in any section of the Game Card Case. It has a durable 1680D hard shell case for maximum protection and a comfortable carrying handle for easy portability.

==============================================================What’s in the box?————————-(1x) Deluxe System Travel Case(1x) Mesh Pouch(2x) Game Cartridge Dust Protector Case(2x) Micro SD Dust Protector Case———————————————–Design———–The way this hard shell case Is made it on par with most hard cases. What I really like about the design is that it fits the whole system nice and snug. Obviously, if you start throwing it around you have a possibility of activating a joy-con or pro controller so be mindful of this. It has a nice and comfortable rubber grip on the handle that makes it easy to carry and not slip from your grasp. This case resembles a lunch box/ mini suitcase and has red and white threading along the zipper line, which I thought was a great aesthetic touch. The hard shell is coated with a coarse mesh that reminds you of any ole’ hard case out there. The zipper has a rubber switch logo as this is an officially licensed Nintendo product.On the inside, you have shaped pockets that are specifically made for each item as you can guess. The front pocket, closest to you when you open it, is for the little beast itself the Nintendo Switch. Right behind that you have the pocket for the dock, and proceeding that is the pocket for either your joy-con grip or pro controller ( I use it for the pro controller which I highly recommend if you like a more natural controller-esque feel). Along side this middle section and holds all the aforementioned items nice and snug are two more pockets and these I presume are for the cables such as the HDMI cable and AC adapter.Additional items include a mesh pouch to hold your cables and two game cartridge cases to hold your physical games and two micro SD cartridges to hold the memory cards that are compatible with the Nintendo Switch.Note - this could be due to materials used or design flaw but do not smell the case. It stinks of burnt rubber. Maybe put an air freshener in it for the time being. This will obviously fade over time but is quite strong so I don’t recommend you sniff it for long periods of time. It’s a nice case, and it seems pretty sturdy

It’s a nice case, and it seems pretty sturdy. I think it would keep everything pretty safe and secure, but I guess I can’t confirm fully since I’m not really up to dropping my Switch in the case. It can fit most everything in it.That being said, I do have a couple complaints. The first is that the Switch is located on the bottom of the case. So, if you’re carrying the case and drop it, the first thing that will take the impact is the Switch. I wish they would have tried to put it in the middle of the case. The second is that the USB C adapter is a tight fit. You can get it in there, but I wish there were a little extra space for it.
