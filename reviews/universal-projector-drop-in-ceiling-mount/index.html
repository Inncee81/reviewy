Product Specifications

  • 2’ x 2’ Reinforced Steel Plate
  • Replaces a Standard Ceiling Tile
  • Supports Projectors Up To 30 lbs
  • Lightweight, Heat-Dissipating Surface

  • Multiple Mounting Points For Alignment

    Product Description

The Universal Projector Drop-In Ceiling Mount removes the hassle from projector mounting. This patented design replaces a standard 2’ × 2’ ceiling tile. Installation is easy and takes less than 1 hour! Featuring a reinforced steel plate and a white cast aluminum mounting head, this lightweight mount can support projectors up to 30 lbs. The mounting head is designed to dissipate heat for longer lamp life. Additionally, the plate includes cutouts and covers for 2 NEMA standard outlets for mounting additional equipment or electrical components.

Killer projector mount. Easy to set up. Comes with a safety cable to save your projector if your ceiling t-bar ever fails. It’s hard to see in the pictures, but it actually has five separate locations for mounting the projector, which translates to 14 projector orientations depending on how you rotate the 2’x2’ plate in your ceiling.It’s strong but not heavy and uses high quality parts to secure your projector. Not deal breakers,

I used it in our theater basement room and it was much easier than cutting a hole in the tile and attaching a mount to some other location.I only had two minor issues with the product and I don’t feel like these are deal breakers. 1. (One side note is my projector needed metric bolts and not standard as well. I have the Optima HD141X projector.) Not a huge issue as I figured with a universal mount there may be some adjustment. 2. The knock outs for power, the holes to attach a box too are a little off. Again not a deal breaker and I managed to make it work.The universal mount can be a little bit to deal with to make it fit and work on the projector, but with a little patients you should be able to get a good fit. I would much rather have a universal mount than one specific to the projector, then if I change projectors I don’t have to get another mount and go through the whole process again.

Good Construction, Light Weight

I purchased this to use in conjunction with an InFocus projector on a drop in ceiling dropped roughly 8 ft. It included all the required hardware for installation and came with simple instructions. The mount itself is relatively light and with the projector it weighs roughly 15-18lbs. I mounted it between two double tile lights, so the support was already in place and didn’t require the anchors. There is no sag in the ceiling at all and installation was simple. The only issue I have is that the arm sometimes slightly bends to one side; I’m sure I could just climb up and adjust the screws, but it’s 9’ in the air. I would recommend this to others looking to mount to a drop in ceiling.
