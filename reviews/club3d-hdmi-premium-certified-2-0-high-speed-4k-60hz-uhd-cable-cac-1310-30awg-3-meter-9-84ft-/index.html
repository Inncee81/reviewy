    Product Description

Size:3m/9.84ft The Club 3D HDMI 2.0 High Speed 4K UHD cable enables the connection of your HDMI 2.0 supported Gaming PC or Laptop to an (Ultra) High Definition Monitor or other HDMI 2.0 supported device. It fully supports the ‘HDMI High Speed with Ethernet’ specification, ensuring that video up to 4K UHD 3840x2160p at 60Hz is supported, as well as audio and data streams. The cable combines the unmatched signal quality and convenience of HDMI connectivity with the power and flexibility of home entertainment networking. It includes a dedicated data channel into the HDMI link, enabling high speed networking at up to 100 Mbps. It is the ideal solution for the ultimate future proof HDMI 4K UHD Gaming and Viewing experience
