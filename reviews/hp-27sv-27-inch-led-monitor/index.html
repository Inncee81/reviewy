Excellent monitor!

I purchased two to upgrade the size of my dual-monitor workspace from 24” screens to 27”. They are very thin screens, lightweight, and the bases are plenty sturdy. The top edge and both sides of the display are extremely thin. Sitting side-by-side, it makes for a nearly seamless combination.This monitor does have a matte screen finish. Now that I have it, I absolutely love it over a glossy finish. Glare and reflection is cut to an absolutely bare minimum.

i must say they all 3 go very good together being as cheap as they are

I Bought 3 of these monitors to replace my aging dual 19 inch HP monitor setup. i must say they all 3 go very good together being as cheap as they are. the picture quality is in my opinion decent out of the box without any adjustments though i imagine it could be better. it is a 1080p 27 inch screen so the clarity will be less then other smaller or higher resolution monitors.The displays were very easy to put together, all you have to do is slide the base on and use the little flip up hand screw on the bottom to tighten it and its good to go. The only con i can think of is they only come with VGA cables. i had to buy 3 mini display port to hdmi adapter cables to hook these up to my R9 295X2 GPU. that added about 10 dollars onto the price of each monitor. to my suprise they have built in speakers but they absolutely SUCK. so definitely plan on getting headphones or a speaker system to go with this.All in all not a bad monitor for the price. does alright for gaming or web browsing. and is great if you’re getting into stock trading or need to monitor a bunch of information at once.
