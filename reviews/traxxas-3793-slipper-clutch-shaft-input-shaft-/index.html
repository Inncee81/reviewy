Be careful putting the gear on..you’ll break the pin

This is one of those things you really don’t want to have to replace but eventually you will have to. One of ours broke after a year of abuse so I ordered this and the Traxxas 3195 Top Steel Drive Gear. The pin in the top is what broke and after trying to put the new one together I found out real quick why it broke. The pin is not a solid pin so it bends really easy if you don’t get the gear on just right the first time. I learned my lesson and ordered another one and it went together just fine. Just be careful because it break really easy.
