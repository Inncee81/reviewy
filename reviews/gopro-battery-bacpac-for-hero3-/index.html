GoPro Battery BacPac for Hero3+

by people ...

Product Specifications

  • Extended Battery Life: Seamlessly attaches to the back of your Gore to increase its battery life
  • USB Charging: Charge using most USB chargers. The Gore Auto or Wall charger or a computer
  • LCD Window: Indicates the camera’s battery level and charging status

  • LED Light: Displays the camera’s recording status

    Product Description

Size:one size  |  Color:Black The Battery BacPac seamlessly attaches to the back of your Gore to increase its battery life. It’s perfect for long-duration Time Lapse photo sessions, endurance motorsports racing or any application where you need extended battery life. Compatible with all HERO3+, HERO3 and HD HERO2 cameras.

Product Reviews

Performs as stated

This back pack battery certainly extends the useful time of the camera when I’m scuba diving with it and can’t recharge it. It allows me to do two full dives without a recharge or battery change even if I video all of the time.

A Mandatory accessory for all GoPro owners

It really does double, maybe triple the minimal battery time of the original battery. a must have item for serious GoPro users. The description doesn’t make it clear, but this item DOES include 4 different clear plastic backs for the camera case, depending on the size camera, and whether you want waterproof or open back.The original battery stays in.. this piggybacks on top. A great plus (for the open back) is you can connect charge cord without taking the camera out of the case, and the one connection charges both batteries.considering all that, a great bargain.

While I have not tested this for the full length …

While I have not tested this for the full length of time. I ran it on my Hero 3+ Silver, during a white water rafting trip. This was the longest single trip I have tested it on. I turned the Resolution to 920(I think) and had this and a fully charged regular battery in it. I did not use the WiFi or anything else though. I didn’t want to run out of memory on the card, or run the battery down. In the Hour and a half that it ran, it only used about 1⁄2 of the battery pack, per the guage on the back.Just what I was looking for, and I would have bought it sooner, but it was not out when I got the camera. I figure between the stock battery, and this backpack, I should be able to get between 4 and 5 hours at least of continous shooting, at least based on the same settings.

Does the job.

Very pleased with this battery backpac thus far. Lately I have been recording my flight lessons, and the Hero 3’s battery could never make it the full length of the lesson. I would get anywhere between 40 min to just over 1 hour on the internal battery alone at 1080⁄60. With the backpac I was able to get my last lesson in its entirety which lasted about 2 hours, and there was still juice to spare. Looking forward to getting 2 more for my other two Hero 3’s soon. Pass through charges like a charm, does add significant charge time however, to charge both the backpac and the camera, but it’s a very convenient feature. Highly recommend this product for GoPro users.
