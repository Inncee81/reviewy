The Lightest and Longest batteries for serious outdoor use

One set of these batteries is lighter than a set of standard batteries of the same size and lasts much longer. That makes the price easier to swallow, because it “sort of” evens out. I wouldn’t carry these on a standard weekend camping trip, or for anything around town, but for serious backcountry trips, these are a must have.One caution: These will go from reading “full charge” to “empty” very quickly. I use them in my Garmin GPS, and I rarely notice anything between a reading of “full charge” and “warning, out of power, shutting down”. That’s fine, but you do need to be aware of it, especially if you are using an electronic device that monitors the charge. “Always carry a spare set” is even more important with these batteries than with standard batteries.

Best batteries on the market

Best batteries on the market, light weight and they really do best no lithiums by about 3 times in length. I use them in a motion detector burglar alarm. At less that 2 a piece well worth not having to replace an awkwardly placed device for well over a year. They have just come out with some AA lithium rechargeables but pricey and with the charger needed they don’t appear by other reviewers to yet be worth the expense. Get these in an original package, not rewrapped.
