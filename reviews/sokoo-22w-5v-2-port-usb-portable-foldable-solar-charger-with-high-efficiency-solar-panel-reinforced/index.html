Cautions To protect your charging devices from overheating and stop charging, please place the phone under cool and well ventilated area, and connect the phone with the solar charger under sunlight via an USB cable.

Even though the solar charger has waterproof function, minimizing moisture exposure to protect electrical components is recommended.

This charger is not compatible with the iPod nano, iPod Classic, iPod Shuffle, HP TouchPad and Asus tablets.

iPhone/iPad is designed to active charging with a stronger current than that of Android. It pops up incompatible message when the current is not strong or strength of sunlight becomes weaker.Please use it in strong sunlight, unfold panels before insert USB, and re-insert USB to activate charging when sunlight becomes stronger again.

Steps: 1) Unfold the panels, 2) Face panels to the direction of strong sunlight, 3) THEN insert the USB.
