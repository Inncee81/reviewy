Welcome, Baby

If you have time to wait for this to arrive, you can get the exact same thing from AliExpress for a few bucks cheaper. I’d gotten mine from there, but needed a baby gift sooner than a month. I had liked the product so much for my kid that I knew it would be a great gift to greet a baby sister.This was BEYOND what I had expected. The item itself? Standard. The trimmings and presentation? Superior and then some. If you’re going to send a welcome item to baby via Amazon, this is the one to get.The swaddle isn’t a swaddle. It’s basically a cozy blanket that is hard to wiggle out of. There is very little constraining involved; your kid just will have a heck of a time trying to kick this blanket off. The hood is mostly for looks. I would suggest not using it or using it as a pillow to elevate baby’s head a little. The hood doesn’t come down far, but it also could possibly be a smothering hazard. The material is soft and washes easily from spit-up if you don’t leave it to dry too long.As a cozy blanket for 0-3 months while kiddo is laying in their stroller, this is a great item with awesome packaging. If you’re looking for this to last longer than early infancy, good luck. This is a welcome item only.
