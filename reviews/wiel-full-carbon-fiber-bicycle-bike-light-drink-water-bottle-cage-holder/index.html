Great Product - Highly Recommended

Bottle holders arrived at time promised and were packaged well. Mounted the pair on two Fat Tire bikes in minutes with the screws provided and they fit perfectly. Tried fitting water bottles and they were tight and seemed like they will hold through any conditions. Being of the Carbon Fiber material, they will not leave marks on my bottle(s) which along with the look, weight, and ease of use, is why I picked these holders. I would definitely recommend them and purchase again if needed in the future.

Great bottle cage

Great bottle cage. It is light, stylish (goes great with my red & black bike). It also does not mark up your bottle with alloy black marks! Which is why I purchased this carbon fiber cage. Got tired of how all the other alloy cages marred my water bottles with black scuff marks. This one doesn’t. And it holds snug.

Probably not good for horizontal mounting

I’ve both these water cages for both of my bikes. The first set works great on a standard road bike. I liked them well enough that I bought a second set for my recumbent. The second set constantly drops my water bottles. They are mounted nearly horizontally (about a 15% angle because it’s a recumbent bike). However, the description claims that these cages hold tight enough for a MTB which I thought would be good enough for the horizontal mounting on a recumbent. As it is now, the water bottles will always work their way out of these cages on my recumbent bike even on fairly smooth pavement. Any sort of moderate bump will cause a water bottle to immediately drop out of the cage.Are these not recommended for horizontal/near horizontal mounting? I don’t feel like this pair of cages is nearly as tight as the ones I mounted to my road bike.

Too fragile

These bottle cages are very light and they do look great. They are however very fragile. I broke the first one when my pant leg caught on it (I was commuting and had only rolled up my right pant leg). This is forgivable - I do not necessarily expect a cage to survive a pant leg catching on it, though it really did not seem to take much force. But, I noticed after a couple uses that my other cage was also cracked. There was no impact or anything that might have caused it. I would look for a different design since these are too fragile.
