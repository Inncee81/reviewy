Inexpensive Lightweight Case

The Case Logic 15.6-Inch Laptop Attache is a very lightweight laptop case that has decent storage and provides light protection. The case is well designed and made. There is plenty of room for a 15.6” laptop and its accessories. Padding is minimal which is why it is so lightweight (under 1 lb). The case will provides good protection from bumps and scratches, but minimal protection from major drops. This is fine with me as I was looking for lightweight, not maximum protection. The case has a zippered pocket on the outside for accessories, and an interior sleeve compartment for papers, etc. If you’re looking for a very lightweight inexpensive case, then this is a great case. If you’re looking for maximum protection, then you may want to look elsewhere.

Excellently Sized for my 15.6” Laptop. Room for Tablets, Phones, AND Chargers.

I ordered this for my slimline Toshiba 15.6” laptop. The laptop is an odd size, long and narrow, none of my other laptop sleeves would work properly. Even bags sized for my laptop were not wide enough to also allow me to store the charger for the laptop, my iPad, and my phone. So, I ordered this 17.3 sized bag, and I am able to fit everything into this one bag, PLUS all of the necessary chargers and cables.The bag is attractive, black material with red piping”, there is an outside zippered pouch with internal storage areas for pens, pencils, and enough room for my iPad and it’s cable and charger. On the other side, there is an outside zippered pouch which fits my iPhone with room to spare, there is certainly enough space for a much larger Android or other type of cell phone. The inner area is just the right size for my laptop, there is a bit of space at each end which makes it easy to insert and remove the laptop. There is also an inner storage zippered compartment, just the right size for the cables and the charger for the laptop. With all 3 items packed inside of this bag, it still does not bulge out, or make it difficult to remove whichever item you need.The padding of this sleeve with handles is thick enough to provide adequate protection from bumps, and from being packed inside of a carry-on bag. The padding is not thick enough to save your laptop from a fall from height. But, it is not marketed as such, for ultra protection buy a much more expensive hard-sided laptop carrying case. The handles are well-padded and extremely convenient. My past laptop sleeves had no handles, and also no room for cables, chargers, tablets, and phones.This is a lot of carrier bag for a low price, the quality is much higher than the price would seem to indicate. As mentioned, I DID order one size up, had I ordered the recommended size for my laptop, there would not have been enough space for the tablet, phone, and all of the chargers. I recommend this carrying sleeve/case as a good quality, low-priced, and convenient carrier for all of your various phone and computer items and accessories. The outer cover is attractive, tough, and does the job.
