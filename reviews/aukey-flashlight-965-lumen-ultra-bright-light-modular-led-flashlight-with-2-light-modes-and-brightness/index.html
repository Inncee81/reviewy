Super bright, well machined and lightweight

This is a really nice, well-built flashlight that is super bright. It uses, and comes with, two 18650 rechargeable batteries, which allow the LED to be driven to very bright levels. It has a side mounted switch that allows you to switch between four levels of brightness, the brightest is so bright that a brief flash in the eyes is blinding. It has a strobe mode as well, but I love that it is not part of the normal rotation of brightness modes (like almost every other similar light), rather, you must hold the button down for 4 seconds or so to get into strobe mode. The machining on this light is very smooth and tight. The parts screw together very smoothly. When I first unscrewed the rear cap to check on the batteries, the O-ring shown in the lower right of the picture fell out; unfortunately broken. So now my flashlight is no longer as water resistant as it should be, thus I deducted a star for that. Otherwise this light would be a very strong 5 stars.
