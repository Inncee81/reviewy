This is the one you want

Looks awesome! Works great! Was expecting a bit more speed with the i7 but it’s probably due to all the bloatware that big boxes load on their machines. Uninstalling that stuff would probably make a huge difference. One would be surprised the difference a ‘clean install’ would make to a computer. Love the accelerometer that parks the hard drive if dropped, although I hope I never need that feature. Resolution is only 720p, but under most circumstances, you wouldn’t notice the difference in 720p and 1080p. Only drawbacks I’ve noticed are that the battery is built in so it would need to be disassembled to be replaced. Same would be true if trying to upgrade/replace the RAM and hard drive as well. Overall, a great buy for the price and I don’t think you will be disappointed. There is better, but not at this price range.
