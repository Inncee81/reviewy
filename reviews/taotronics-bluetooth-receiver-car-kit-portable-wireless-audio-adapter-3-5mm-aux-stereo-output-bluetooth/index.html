Car, headphones, stereo input, etc.

A car kit is just the tip of the iceberg. This setup works well in headphones, earbuds, home stereo. I tested it on every wired speaker I could find…instant Bluetooth connectivity. The built in mic made Bluetooth calls a reality in my dinosaur of a truck. I do use a ground loop isolator in-line to eliminate feedback when the receiver needs to charge but it’s a cheap fix to avoid engine whine through the speakers. I can highly recommend this receiver if you want quick connectivity and a cheap update to your existing wired speakers.
