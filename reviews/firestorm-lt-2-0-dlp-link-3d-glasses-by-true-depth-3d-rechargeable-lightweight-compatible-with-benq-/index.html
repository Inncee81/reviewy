The BEST 3D glasses from all available for my BEN Q projector.

I have tried pretty much all available glasses for my BenQ projector and this one takes the top spot currently!They are VERY comfortable first of all but fit tightly without movement which is important to me.The power button also works as an inverse option in case your 3D movie is looking odd, push the button once and it will invert the 3D effect. Very cool feature!The charging port is the common USB you use for your cellphones…no more weird proprietary or rare port! Great change!Unlike the Estar 601 (my previous favorite after modifications) or the previous Firestorm edition (and the BenQ glasses for that matter), there are NO reflections on the glass from your face on this one. This is HUGE because those reflections cut on the contrast and make things look hazy.I had to modified the ESTARs before adding pieces of cloth all around to complete seal them from the outside.I did not detect any Ghosting on this one unlike the previous version so its a BIG improvement in that regards too.The only downside to me is not having a side “cover” like the previous gen and I wish the glass was a bit bigger because I sit 12’ from a 12’ wide screen so it almost didn’t fit ..but I don’t think such setup is common so I can understand the choices.I also noticed there is a slight color change from wearing the glasses…seems like its cutting on the red a bit more than the others but not a big problem to adjust that. Much easier than getting rid of reflections on the others.This is better than any other glasses right now including their other versions.My rating is 4.5 stars but I gave it 5 since there is no .5 option.Overall, no is not perfect but in its stock configuration this IS the best option on the market.
