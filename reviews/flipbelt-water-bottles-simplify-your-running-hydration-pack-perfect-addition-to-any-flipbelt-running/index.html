    Product Description

Staying hydrated while running, working out and being active is critical, but carrying a water bottle while running, walking, hiking and other activities is cumbersome. FlipBelt’s sleek, contoured water bottles fit effortlessly into all of FlipBelt’s fitness and running belts so you can carry your water hands-free.

Good purchase

I had two of the smaller bottles but needed a replacement cap and then came across the bigger bottle. So far has worked great. Fits across the belt well. May be a little harder to get in and out than the smaller bottle, but may just need to get used to it some more. Very happy with it overall though.

Comfortable water bottle to fit your spandex running belt

Fits in any running belt, not just the FlipBelt, as long as it has a pocket at least 8 inches long and about 4 inches wide. It is marked for up to 10 ounces or 300 ml of liquid. I like the curved profile so it fits comfortably around the waist. The spout is a flexible material so drinking is comfortable.

I’m glad I made this purchase

Giving this product 4 stars because it does what it’s supposed to do. It holds my water in my flipbelt when I run. It doesn’t leak. I only put water in it, no juice or mixes, because the neck is so small it would be hard to clean. I’m glad I made this purchase.

Appreciates not having to carry a water bottle

My daughter didn’t know she needed this until I bought it for her. She has a flipbelt but always carried a water bottle in her hand as she ran. She appreciates having her hand free now and says this is very comfortable to wear and is the perfect size for her.
