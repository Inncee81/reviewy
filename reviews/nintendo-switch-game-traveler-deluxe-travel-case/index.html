Product Specifications

  • Officially Licensed Nintendo Product
  • Deluxe Travel Case - Durable hard shell case holds and protects Nintendo Switch
  • Protect and Organize - Padded divider protects screen and zippered mesh pockets hold extra Game Cards
  • 2 Game Card Cases - Each red transparent game card case holds 4 Nintendo Switch Game Cards offering dust free protection

  • 2 Micro SD Card Cases - Each red transparent case holds 2 Micro SD Cards and fits in any section of the Game Card Case protecting your SD cards from dust

    Product Description

Color:Black The Nintendo Switch Game Traveler Deluxe Travel Case holds and protects the Nintendo Switch. It has a padded divider to protect the screen and a zippered mesh pocket to hold extra game cards. This Case also includes 2 red transparent game card cases, which hold 4 Nintendo Switch game cards. In addition this case includes 2 red transparent Micro SD card cases which will fit in any section of the game card cases. The Deluxe Travel Case has a durable 1680D hard shell for maximum protection and a comfortable carrying handle for easy portability.

Product Reviews

Easy Modification Tip

Love this case. There was a slight modification I made to it so that the game holders do not fall out when the Switch not in the case. I did this so I do not accidentally and unknowingly loose my investments sometime down the road when I have more games.I simply used a piece of elastic (use lighter to melt ends to keep from fraying) and a few dabs of super glue to keep the game holders in place. Super glue was applied to the 2 ends as well as the center with the elastic slightly stretched. It holds the game holders while case is upside down. I usually do not write reviews, but wanted to share this idea with the community in hopes that it helps someone!

Good case to hold the basics

Case is sturdy and seems durable. The zipper is kind of flimsy, so I’m sure that will be the first part to go. Holds the switch with attached joy cons easily, and you can fit a cable in the top. The flap has a small pocket that they list as extra cartridge storage, and that’s about all it can be used for, or maybe a wiping cloth and/or those straps for the cons. Underneath the console storage there are two plastic cases to hold 8 games total and 2 inserts that hold 2 micro SD cards in a game slot. There is also a small rectangular recess that I’m not sure what it’s for and can’t find any good use for. Maybe a small stylus.

Sturdy, but not spacious.

Case outer material is sturdy; it will definitely protect the Switch in case of a drop on concrete. The Switch is held in place by the flap which also protects the display from scratches.You can jam a USB-C cable inside but that’s about it. It is impossible to jam an iPad 12 watt charger in there.

A very stylish case that doesn’t make me feel bad that I’m carrying my switch around with me.

When I was ordering my switch, I knew I wanted to have a travel case for it to keep it safe on the go. Little did I know that there was a case that was this awesome. I absolutely LOVE this case. The design on the front is amazing and I really think it looks great. The feel of the case is just really good and I always seem to find myself running my fingers over it because of the texture of it is just really nice. On the inside, it has a little pouch for my car charger and two little game cases that go underneath the switch. When I started using it, I was surprised to learn that the switch actually fits with the joy-cons connected! I was very happy with this purchase.

Good case, could use some more security when it comes to the games and the games cases sliding around.

The case itself feels very secure in the protection of the console and games inside of it. However, the games holders that come inside are a bit of a nuisance, as they will easily slide around (when the system isn’t in its spot), and the games themselves are way too easy to pop out or slide around when opening the games holders. If there was a bit more security when putting the games cases into the overall traveler case, or at least some more security for the games themselves, then this would be a 5-star. Otherwise, the case if very nice, protects what it needs to protect, holds everything, and doesn’t take up an obscene amount of space.
