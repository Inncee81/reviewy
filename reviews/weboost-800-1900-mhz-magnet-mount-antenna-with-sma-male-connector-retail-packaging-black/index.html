    Product Description

The Wilson 311125 (formerly 301125) 50 Ohm 12-inch Magnet-Mount Antenna is a multi-band antenna that features up to 6.12 dB of gain. It is designed to be used with one of our amplifiers. The Wilson 311125 antenna is omni-directional which allows it to receive signal from all directions.

Application/Usage:

Designed to be used as an outside antenna in any vehicle with steel exterior including cars, pickup trucks, vans and SUV’s

Compatibility:

Works with 50 Ohm amplifier and accessories

Product Reviews

Works fine, strong magnet stays put

Used this in combination with a Wilson Mobile 4G booster on a month-long, 8,600-mile cross country van trip last summer. Never had a problem with it blowing off the roof (Ford van) even as 80 mph speeds out west. I can’t say if it’s any better than the smaller antenna that comes with the Wilson booster, but intuitively I feel it has to be, as it’s got much more height to it. Has plenty of cable length to allow you mount it in a convenient location.

It sort of helps

I attached this antenna directly (had to make sure it was SMA male) to my cellular network extender Samsung Network Extender SCS-2U01 Verizon Wireless SCS2U01 Signal Booster and ran the cable out the window and attached the antenna to my RV roof. I now see more bars further away but the jury is out on whether our devices are getting better signal- sometimes phone calls lose signal, or sound terrible, and we end up having to stand closer to the extender anyway.

Stays put @ 100 mph winds

This is mounted on the roof of a full sized Transit van near the front. Get the full force of the headwinds. Cruising @ 80 in a 20 mph headwind equates to staying in place with no apparent movement @ 100 mph. This is really a nice item.
