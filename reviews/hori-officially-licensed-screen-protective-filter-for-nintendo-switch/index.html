Product Reviews

I hate screen protectors

Let me start out by saying I absolutely detest screen protectors. I have never been able to put them on without bubbles or dust, and my hands are always too shakey to line things up properly. Since the screen of the switch contacts the dock as it slides in, I knew that I would have to put on a protector of some sort to avoid scratches. I ordered this protector, and dreaded putting it on.Hori’s application process is flawless, I have never had this easy of a time getting a professional result on a screen protector. The 4 step process let me anchor it down perfectly (even with my shakey hands), and get it stuck on without any air bubbles. Would not hesitate to buy any of their screen protector products again.

Great, easy to apply protector

Excellent cover. Is as easy to apply as requested. I couldn’t get the link for the video to work, so I’d recommend looking for it on youtube. Basically you remove the number one tab, use that to apply the screen, then pull the 2 off from back side while the pad is stuck on device with sticky pad from #1. Then, you pull 3 from the back as the 2 side has made things stick. Then, you smooth the bubbles and remove the front-facing 4 pad.Very nice pad overall.

Watch the installation vid, good protector

This has a unique application method that made it a bit confusing even with the instructions. I looked up the vid and it was easy after seeing it. The unique application method helps to reduce the amount of dust you grt under it when applying. I like the protector and havent had any issues with it peeling from heat. I typically play zelda for a few hrs at a time.

The best

Pre-ordered for day 1 protection for my Switch console and I am floored with how well this looks on the screen. It took me a bit to figure how to put it on based off directions, but once I got the steps it went on quick and easy. I had a can of compressed air to make sure all dust was off my screen before applying. Looks great and seems to be holding up well after several days and long play sessions.

Didn’t get any dust under it and air bubbles were all fixable.Would definitely recommend just for the applicator making it easy. No worries on positioning it right the first time.

Works good so far
