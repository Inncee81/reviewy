Great product- sparse packaging

Product arrives it it own package which is surprising given the value. Clearly no money wasted on pretty packaging such as that the Sonos Playbar comes in. Plain brown box. As for the unit it comes with a wall mounting plate but no hardware to mount to the wall. The unit itself was easy to set up and the learning function was easy and worked the first time using a Time Warner remote. Once I got it setup the sound is excellent as promised. But the sound level does not actually deliver any sound at all until you reach 30 on the sound scale. This is surprising and makes little sense. That being said the sound bar will push way beyond that level and it rocks. Sound sound output is not a problem.Highly recommended, sounds great understand it will improve after 10+ hours of use.

I really have enjoyed using this product

I really have enjoyed using this product. The sound quality is excellent for a soundbar, and was a good balance between wanting a minimal install, and having a full blown surround sound system. Mounting on the wall under my TV was easy. The wireless sub transmitter that comes with it is a great feature! I was able to to hook the transmitter up to my Gemini studio (non-consumer) sub using an adapter (Monoprice 104775 1.5-Feet Premier Series XLR Male to RCA Male 16AWG Cable). This leads me to believe it would work with almost any powered sub. I’m not in love with the menu system, but once it’s adjusted, there was no need to change anything again.
