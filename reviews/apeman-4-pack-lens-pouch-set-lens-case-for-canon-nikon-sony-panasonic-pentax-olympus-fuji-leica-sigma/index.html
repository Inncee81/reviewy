Product Reviews

Great Protection At A Very Reasonable Price

I bought these to protect my growing lens collection. The interior is nicely padded and soft without having any loose fibers that can get on your lens. The largest pouch was a bit a too large so I actually use it to hold a Panasonic camcorder. The beauty of these lens pouches is that they are nicely padded to protect against minor bumps and they can clip to the side of your camera back. I no longer need to carry a large bag to hold my DSLR and Camcorder since I can use the largest pouch for my camcorder and simply attach it to my smaller camera bag. The one downside is that the top drawstring closure does not allow for protection against light rain, sand, or dust. If the top was zippered or Velcro instead of drawstring it would allow for greater protection against the elements. Despite this, these are an excellent choice to protect your lens or gear at a very reasonable Price.

Great Lens Pouches

Nice construction, and all of my lenses without hoods fit in either the small (Nikkor 35 & 50 MM f/ 1.8, Nikkor 18-55mm vr, Tamron 90mm f/2.8 macro, Nikkor 55-200mm vr & Tokina 11-16 f/2.8); medium (Nikkor 18-140mm vr) or extra large pouches (Tamron 70-30mm di VC.) I don’t really need the large size for my current Nikon F mount lenses, but it holds large speedlight flashes. If the small size was ever to be offered in a 4 pack, I would definitely order.
