Great, except for defective 3.0 USB cable

This has worked well for the 10 months I’ve had it. Only one complaint: the USB 3.0 doesn’t work. It would connect/disconnect repeatedly and rapidly when plugged into the 3.0 port, but had no issues with the 2.0 USB. Transfer speed with 2.0 was only 41 MB/s, less than half of the 3.0, which really matters when you’re dealing with 4TB. I tried lots of fixes (power management of USB port, etc.), but finally just tried a different 3.0 USB cable and this fixed it.If it wasn’t for the USB cord issue, I’d be giving this 5 stars. Well constructed, this drive spends a lot of time with the US Postal Service, as I use it primarily to send and receive large data sets from colleagues. It works great for this.
