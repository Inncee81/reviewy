Great Product and easy to install

The Glass Pro is an excellent screen protector for the Samsung Tab Pro 12.2. When it comes to screen protectors, I have little experience with installation but the Glass Pro was really super easy. I took the advice of some of the more experienced installers and did the application in my bathroom after I had run a hot shower to make the room steamy. The steam cuts down on most of the lint flying in the air which essential to a successful screen protector application. After cleaning the screen of my Samsung Tab Pro with the provider lens wipes (one wet and one dry) I used the installation stickers included in the package to lift the Glass Pro from the package, peeled the backing off, lined up the screen protector, and starting at the bottom of the Samsung’s screen, I gently lowered the Glass Pro on the to the screen of the Samsung. It adhered like a charm. I wish I had taken a video of the actual application because it almost looks like the Glass Pro is suctioned onto the screen of the Samsung…it’s really pretty cool. The Glass Pro has rounded corners and the edges are just shy of full coverage. The one negative to the Glass Pro is that it does not come with specific instructions although instructions are noted on the box. But was pretty easy to figure out what needed to be done. Overall, I am totally satisfied with the Glass Pro.
