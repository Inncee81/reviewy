Love this little machine

Love this little machine! My first impression when I took it out of the box was, “how cute!” It is small, but is exactly what I needed. Have done dozens of loads in the past month catching up on all the laundry I had been hand washing. Ideal for trailer living. It does a phenomenal job of cleaning and spin drying. Much quieter than I would have expected. I did have to purchase an adapter for the kitchen faucet and a longer hose, but that is to be expected any time you have a portable appliance of this type, so why people complain about that, is beyond me. I found it helpful to elevate this little machine by using 4 cinder blocks, which was cheap and easy. Because it’s small, you have to be careful not to overload with too much or it will throw it off balance. A little common sense goes a long way. The price is better than any comparable machine and it shipped from California to New Hampshire in a weeks time. The controls are intuitive and have worked without issue. I like the pause feature so I can readjust the load if needed. It has a low, medium, or high water level settings and so far have only used the high setting. The rinse cycle rinses twice automatically. When I want only one additional rinse, I set for wash only, then when that’s finished, (it beeps when done) I set it on spin. There is a little lint trap in the washer that works fine. I have 2 Alaskan Malamutes so dog hair is a problem for me and the lint trap does as good a job as it can for what it is. This has been a very satisfactory purchase and I would purchase it again. If you have a space constraint, hate the laundromat and that expense, this little machine is worth it. For the price, it appears to be constructed well. Considering I’ve really been giving this little machine a heck of a workout, it’s performed admirably!READ MORE
