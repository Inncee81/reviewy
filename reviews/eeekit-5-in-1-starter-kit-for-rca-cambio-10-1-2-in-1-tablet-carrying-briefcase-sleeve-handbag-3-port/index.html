Not bad for the money

Not bad for the money. I hate to nit-pick, but the bag starts getting bulky when you include all the accessories especially with the mouse(more on this later) and the tablet inside. Another thing is that the zippers do not lock in place if you are using the handle. There is a flap that you fold through the handles and over which secures the top of the bag with Velcro, but the 2 zippers on the sides of the bag can easily slip open letting your tablet come tumbling down on the hard floor. I’m not sure why they designed it like that ? Due to this design I do not feel totally comfortable lugging my tablet around in this bag with the handle. Another thing is the slick ultra-thin mouse. Sure the mouse is ultra thin and looks sleek, but it is made of cheap plastic, the kind of plastic used in less than $5 toys. I once dropped it from about 2 feet and the mouse stopped working ! I had to do some skillful twisting to get it to work again. Other than that, everything else works and the extra accessories might and do come in handy.

Great case for your RCA 11 Maven Pro

After shopping around for a case for my daughters Maven Pro I ended up choosing the 5-in-1 starter kit for 2 reasons: 1. It was designed to fit the Maven Pro specifically and 2. None of the other cases had such a great deal! I’m very happy with it. It fits perfectly, and it came with everything advertised. The mouse is simple for even a 5year old to connect, but does not come with batteries (2AAA), it does come with a convenient storage pouch though. The HDMI cord is about 6ft and the USB port comes in really handy seeing how the tablet only has 1 USB plugin. It also came with a ball point pen/stylus. Overall I am very happy with the quality of everything in the kit and that everything came as advertised.
