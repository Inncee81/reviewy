The only thing that could be seen as a disadvantage for this particular style of fan is that you are unable to work on your phone while using the fan because the fan blades cover the home button and typing area. The fan blades are a foam material, so it is not painful if you get hit. It’s just more of a nuisance. That doesn’t matter to me, I will always choose a cool breeze over multitasking when sitting in Southern Hell (summer in Alabama).
