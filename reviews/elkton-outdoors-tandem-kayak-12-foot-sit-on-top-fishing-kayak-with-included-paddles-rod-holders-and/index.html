    Product Description

Built With Anglers In Mind! The Elkton Outdoors Tandem Sit On Top Kayak is the perfect fishing kayak for lakes, rivers and the ocean. When designing this tandem kayak we made sure to keep anglers in mind by optimizing this kayak for fishing.

The mounted adjustable rod holders allow for a versatile fishing experience for any water or weather conditions. The internal storage compartments are airtight to ensure your valuables are not damaged while on the water. While fishing we all know that leisure is part of the experience so we included premium kayak seats and cup holders so you can kick back and enjoy your day of fishing! The Center of the kayak features a platform that fits coolers and ice chests so you can keep your catch cool or bring your favorite beverage with you!
