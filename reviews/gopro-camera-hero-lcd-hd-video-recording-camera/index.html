My first GoPro Action Camera

This GoPro model fell short of 5 stars and I’ll tell you why…….Let me start by saying the last video camera I owned / purchased used an 8mm video cassette, so this was a huge leap in technology for me. I am not a GoPro expert, however before I purchased this GoPro HERO+ LCD I did some homework and visited the GoPro website and compared all the models they offered so I would know exactly what I was getting.I will say the pictures and video this little camera produces is impressive, but I am very disappointed that the camera itself cannot be removed from the housing. The GoPro website’s comparison chart indicated the battery was not removable, and I was ok with that, but said nothing about the housing. I assumed it was removable like the other models. Now I have to be extremely careful I don’t scratch or damage any part of the housing especially the lens. There’s no way to replace it. If I knew then what I know now I would have went for one of the HERO 4s. Live and learn.
