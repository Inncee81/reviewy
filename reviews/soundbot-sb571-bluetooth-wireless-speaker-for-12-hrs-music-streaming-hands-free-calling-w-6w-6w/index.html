Awesome little speaker

Awesome little speaker! My work vehicle does not have Bluetooth. I bought this so I can listen to music and so I can hear the maps instructions. If you are looking for a speaker to vibrate the windows in your car then this is not the speaker. For my use it is perfect. I listen to a lot of news radio and the voices are crisp and clear. The only thing I have noticed is that if I am using it solely for the directions from map there is a little bit of lag or delay. It will miss the first few words of the instructions. The first two I bought I gave as gifts and after my experience with them when I wanted one for for my work vehicle I did not hestitate to buy this speaker for myself. Pretty Good for the Price

For the price, it’s a nice little unit. It may not satisfy bass heads or people who are used to more expensive speakers, but if you keep your expectations reasonable, you won’t be disappointed.I see a lot of reviews complaining about a female voice. I think the company has fixed that issue on new ones, because I haven’t heard any voice from my unit. It always beeps when you turn it on and beeps a different tone when Bluetooth connection is established. The beep is not excessively loud or annoying to me.This product is also nice for listening to podcasts and audio books. In other words it’s good for both music and voice.Pros:- Reasonably priced- Sounds pretty good for the price (don’t expect Bose sound)- also good for audio books and podcast listening (no excessive boom added to voices)- long bluetooth range - I can take the unit upstairs or to the back porch while the phone is downstairs.- the volume buttons are separate from the back/forward buttons.- loud enough to fill the entire bedroomCons:- The bass is slightly lacking depending on the type of music, but this also contributes to the versatility of the unit being good for both music and voice.
