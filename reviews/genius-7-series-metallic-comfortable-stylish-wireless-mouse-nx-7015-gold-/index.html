Didn’t hold up

Initially I liked this mouse. I liked how you can stash the USB in a slot in the back which makes it nice for travel. I also liked the look of it with the textured silver surface. It was also comfortable. Unfortunatly for me the mouse started glicthing out pretty bad after 4 months of daily use and would no longer select text (i.e. cut and paste). It would work for a moment if I turned it off and on. I was able to confirm it was the mouse and not my OS by testing a different mouse.I work from home so I was using this thing 8-10 hours a day. For the price I paid I got a lot of use out of it thus the two stars and not one. Looking back it may have had some issues from the beginning, and I would turn it off and on from time to time to get it to function properly.I would surmise that my mouse was a lemon, but I replaced it with a different kind of mouse.

Stopped working in less than two months

Purchased on April 29, 2016. Quit working properly on June 21, 2016, just a few weeks after the return period. I’ve changed the battery but it acts like it’s skittish. The mouse moves itself whether or not my hand is on it or if it is resting with no hand on it. Sometimes it freezes and will not move at all. When I am directing it, it suddenly jumps, so for example I just saved a Microsoft Word doc as a webpage unintentionally because the cursor jumped to that selection in the drop-down menu.. So basically this is pretty but apparently a piece of junk, because now I need a new mouse.
