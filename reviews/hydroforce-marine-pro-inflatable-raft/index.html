    Product Description

Grab a friend and spend a summer day out on the water in this Best way Hydro-Force Marine Pro boat. Made of premium marine grade material that can withstand the most extreme nautical conditions including exposure to petroleum products and ultraviolet rays, this boat offers superior strength and durability. This boat also features a high-pressure inflatable floor, motor mount grommets, safety-grab rope, Omni-directional oarlocks, comfortable inflatable cushions and a storage bag. Includes one pair of 60” aluminum oars, a storage bag and a 14.5” air pump. Capacity is 2 adults and 1 child. Dimensions: 115” x 50” x 18”. Maximum weight capacity is 594 lbs.

Works great

Waited until I actually used the product before I decided to do a review . What I can say is that it met my expectations and then some .Because I wanted to have the ability to power this little boat I also ordered the Intex motor mount, which does fit perfectly . The boat only took 10 minutes to set up with the provided pump ( had set it up at home first to be sure of no leaks) . Added a piece of plywood with pipe insulation on the edges for a more solid floor . As set up , it worked flawlessly . Spent a few hours on the water fishing with it and had zero problems. I like that I can toss it and my boat motor in my small car with my fishing gear and head to any body of water without towing a trailer or needing an actual boat launch .For someone looking at purchasing this , I would have to say that adding the plywood floor is a good and cheap addition . The motor mount from Intex fits and does a good job . But I would not go bigger than a 3 hp two stroke for a gas engine . I am running a 2 hp and it goes along well without trying to bend the back of the boat down to much . Great boat for one person or possibly two if you don’t have to much gear .If this one ever gives up the ghost I will buy another . Would recommend it to anyone wanting a quick and easy boat that is easy to transport as well as set up / take down . Perfect for apartment dwellers or those not having space for a full time boat .
