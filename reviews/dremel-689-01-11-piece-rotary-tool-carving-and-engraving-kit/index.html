Product Reviews

Great accessory kit with a million uses!

This is a great little set. I use it in silversmithing to remove fire scale or to add texture or designs. With all of the attachments, I have found a ton of uses for this. Easy to change out the tips with my Dremel tool. Packaged in a hard plastic case which keeps tips organized and includes descriptions of what each tip can do. Great set!

Price is right

Nice assortment for finishing and engraving. Cut off the retail hanger. Instructions how to insert sectioners, but nothing about the tips included. Sectioners not that useful. Instead I trimmed out the bit holder tray to keep tips in place. Room enough to add small Dremel bits and parts later.

Not exactly as advertised

I can’t say I’ve used it yet, as I’ve just received it, but upon opening it, this product does not contain a collet (481) as it says. In place of it, it includes an additional grinding stone, 85602 (making 5 grinders in all instead of 4).

A very nice set

I love my Dremel and this carving set is a great addition to my Dremel arsenal. You’ve got all kinds of tips to smooth as well as carve and some very small carvers for detail work.

Good carving set.

Great price, way cheaper than at the hardware store. I have gotten some mild use out of these carving and etching tools and, so far, they’re working out great. If any of them break I’ll come back on and post an update. I have not tried any generic versions of these, so I have nothing to compare them to. Until then, these have been working just fine and cut well. As a note, I use them with a Black and Decker rotary tool.
