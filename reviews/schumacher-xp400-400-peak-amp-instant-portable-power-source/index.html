Reliable, portable, lightweight, powerful, awesome!

I purchased this on May 22, 2014. I have used it as a backup in my trunk. It fits perfectly inside the rim of my donuts on my 2003-2008 Pontiac Vibe. I have used it to jump two cars. I use the light every now and then too. I do keep it charged by taking it out every 2 months and topping it off. During the summer I keep this in my boat just incase my radio runs the marine battery dead while we are on the sandbar. In the spring I use it to raise and lower my boat motor trim so I don’t have to lug the battery out to move the boat without dragging the keel. I have even used the 12V power socket to charge a GPS before we went on vacation (long story). You really cannot go wrong with this unit. I paid $32 shipped in 2014 and I see the price is still around there so I will be buying this again if I ever need to.
