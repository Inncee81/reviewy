Short and to the point. Stop looking and buy.

As a new floridian, I wanted a kayak. Did my Amazon research to pick out the best. I had never been on a kayak. Chose the Ocean Kayak Frenzy based on reviews. A few days after they arrived, we took them out on our Lagoon. Paddled around for a bit, it was fun, and put them up.Cut to a few weeks later when hurricane Hermine came a knocking. She made landfall late September 1st early September 2. Water started entering the house and didn’t stop. We escaped out of the house on the kayaks with a dog each.Bottom line-If I can safely paddle in the pitch black thru a hurricane with a forty pound dog who is 100% sure the worst place to be is on a kayak, and not tip over and not get swept away my second time being on a kayak? Then I made a damn good choice in said kayak purchase.Thank you Ocean Kayak.

So happy with my choice

After much research & deliberation, I settled on the frenzy & I have no regrets. I needed something small enough that I could handle loading & unloading it from my car by myself, but also wanted a good sturdy sit on top.I was slightly concerned by some reviewers saying the seat is narrow & uncomfortable for larger folk during longer paddles. I’m a solid size 12-14 female & I think there’s plenty of room. It’s snug, but not the least bit tight or uncomfortable. I don’t think I would want any more room to wiggle around. I’m 5’5” & there’s plenty of room to stretch out. The storage areas in front & back can hold all kinds of stuff. The only bummer is my dog doesn’t really fit with me. She’s a large dog & would fit, but then I would be stuck in one spot & wouldn’t be very comfy.Another thing I was concerned about with choosing such a short kayak is how it would track. All the other ones I was considering were 10-12 ft, so this was my shortest option. I ultimately decided to prioritize ease of transportation over quality of ride on the water. However, I really don’t feel like I lost much. This thing tracks really well for being so short. I mostly paddle around lakes on day trips, so it’s not like I needed some kind of serious touring model. I’m also excited to try it out in the surf. Never tried that but it looks like fun.My only negative mention is the shipping time. To be fair, they do say shipping could take 1-2 months (it only took a month), so it’s not any surprise. It’s just a long time to wait.

Narrow seat but great value

This is my first kayak purchase and I was super excited to get the yellow Frenzy - all of the reviews I’ve read online were positive emphasizing stability, durability, light weight and great price (I agree with all of the above). Obviously I purchased this product online, so I was eager to see it in person. I rushed to unwrap it like a kid on Christmas morning, and when I went to sit in it, I was disappointed to feel how snug I fit in the seat. I am a plus size lady and my husband is a 6’2” XL guy and we made our final purchase decision based on weight capacity and width of the kayak (31”, wider than most). HOWEVER, the mold of the seat is a mere 18”, narrower than most! I am on a weight loss journey, so by summer’s end this will be a mute point for me but I think it is imperative for extra-large kayakers to beware.On a positive note, there is ample leg room, my husband’s long legs fit well and I am able to stretch out my legs completely (this will be great for lounging back in my yak and taking in the summer sun!) Over all I am satisfied with the Ocean Kayak - Frenzy, it is a quality product at an affordable price.
