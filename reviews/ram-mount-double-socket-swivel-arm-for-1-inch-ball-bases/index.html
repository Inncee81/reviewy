    Product Description

The RAP-B-200-2U consists of double 1” diameter open ball sockets with a 360 degree rotation point at the center and a connecting swivel joint that rotates forward and back 180 degrees. The rubber ball and socket at either end, and a central pivot point provides unsurpassed adjustability. The RAM ball sockets maximize ball coverage for optimal fit. Dimensions: Overall Length: 6.25” Socket-To-Socket, Length: 5.5”.

Works for my Application, with Modification

All of the Ram Mount devices I have previously purchased were aluminum, but this one is PLASTIC. The ball clamping arms at each end DO NOT have the normal spring loading as do all other arms I have purchased.The clamping arms at both ends are indexed at the middle connector, with a male eight sided configuration on the middle connector matching a female eight sided configuration, four on each side, of each arm. Slightly loosening the tightening knob of the arm does not allow turning a device attached to the arm. The tightening knob must be dramatically loosened in order to turn the arm, with the possibility of losing the device attached with a ball mount to that arm, like a video camera.My intent was to use this swivel arm to hold a video camera on a motorcycle handlebar with the capability of loosening the top knob just enough to turn the camera to either side, up to 90 degrees, but the octagonal indexing of the arm connector prevents that turning, without the possible loss of the camera at the ball connecting top of the arm.Turning the male, eight sided connector on a lathe to remove the corners of the eight sides into a perfect circle allowed the top arm to be turned with only a slight loosening of the knob, without any possibility of losing the attached device.

Very disappointing, not stable, not to RAM Mount standards

I love my RAM Mount with the all-metal, normal arm, which is highly stable and works great. This swivel arm is nowhere near the quality and performance of the normal arm. I wanted a longer arm with more bending options, so bought this product. It is not stable, not steady, shakes a lot, and despite appearing to be highly adjustable, it actually offers a limited number of rotational positions to choose from. This is made from plastic, not all-metal like the normal arms, and between the plastic construction and the the five joints/connection points in this design (yes, five from ball to ball), it just cannot hold a standard size phone stably at a 6” distance from the suction cup, particularly not for video recording while driving (such as with the Harry’s Lap Timer app.)
