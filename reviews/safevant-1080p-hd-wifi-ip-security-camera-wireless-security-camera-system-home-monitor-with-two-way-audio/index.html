To be more specific

  1. Plug in your camera. Set you network to 2.4ghz channel, and connect your mobile device to wifi.
  2. Download MIAC App by scanning the QR code on the box or Download directly from APP Store/ Play Store.
  3. Register your account and log in.
  4. Add the camera by scanning the QR code at bottom of the device or manually by entering its ID and Password.
  5. Connect to your IP camera by following on-screen instructions and start to play (Reset needle included in the package).

Importance

1) Safevant IP camera is a 2.4ghz supported camera. If you have a dual-band router, be sure to segregate your wireless channels and connect the camera to the 2.4ghz channel.

2) Micro SD card required (up to 64g) to record, NOT included in the package.
