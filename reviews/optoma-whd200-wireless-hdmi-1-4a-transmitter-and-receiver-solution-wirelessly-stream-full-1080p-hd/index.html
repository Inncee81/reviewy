    Product Description

“The Optoma WHD200 provides wireless streaming uncompressed Full HD 1080p content from your HDTV set-top box, gaming console, blu-ray/DVD player, or AV receiver up to 100 ft away* to your projector or TV. No complicated installation, no expensive cables to buy and no unsightly wires to hide. With zero latency and uncompressed HD resolution video with 5.1 channel digital audio, the Optoma WHD200 is ideal for gamers and Home Theater enthusiasts alike. Play your favorite game or watch your favorite movie or 3D content just as you would with a hard wired connection. The Optoma WHD200 allows the same image to be viewed on two separate displays, one wireless and one wired, and makes it easy to enjoy your entertainment in your favorite room in the house. (*Stated distance is based on optimum, line-of-sight conditions. Actual workable distance may vary depending location. Structures constructed of steel, concrete, wood or brick will decrease transmission distance.)”
