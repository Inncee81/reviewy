    Product Description

This is a Full Scale airsoft pistol that will work perfectly for the beginner player that is looking to have some real fun. Shooting at a velocity of around 200 feet per second, with a range of approximately 100 feet. This is great for shooting at your home due to the lower velocity which ensures accuracy without damaging windows or other objects. The spring action mechanism makes this easy to operate without the need for expensive batteries or gas.
