Baby loved it from the start

My daughter really enjoys this chair. She used to lean back when she was younger and loved sitting upright and looking around. It gave her a different perspective from lying down most of the day. Now that she’s 5 months old, she likes to grab the toys and try to put them in her mouth. The chair is very easy to wash as the fabric is held on with a few buttons. Takes no time to take off and put on again. I’ve washed it a few times and it dries fairly quickly as well. I didn’t put it in the dryer because I was afraid it might shrink, but it always dried within a day.
