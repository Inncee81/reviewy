No Slip, No Worry…Excellent Product Until it BROKE

2nd Attempt Amazon sent me a free replacement to try out and this one appears to be built a little better. I’d stll say have your GoPro tethered or at least a floaty backing while doing a water sportUPDATE Great concept but it broke and I almost lost my GoPro in the river!! I had this clip in my pocket to attach it to my lifejacket while paddle boarding a couple weeks ago. I pull the clip out with my GoPro attached and I notice the bolt came loose for no reason so the swivel wouldn’t work anymore. Luckily I had a tether to my GoPro as a back up because sure enough the clip fell apart into two pieces and I lost then “claw” in the river while catching my GoPro. I don’t understand how the bolt wasn’t secured down but this is a very dangerous risk to losing a GoPro.I bought this clip mount to use with my GoPro Hero 4 Silver attached to a backpack or sling bag when we go on hikes and camping trips. If you want a head mount just buy the GoPro head mount set-up because the camera is a bit too heavy to just clip onto any ball cap. This thing may be made in China so the quality control is spotty, but it works flawlessly and as described. The 360 degree rotary mechanism seems solid and has very positive clicks, probably about 20 clicks to go around one time. I mounted it to my left side backpack shoulder strap and it looks great plus you get a perfect angle view of everything in front of you. The camera tightens down just fine to the mount and the jaws are so stiff that you really can probably jog with this thing and it wouldn’t move a bit. Great purchase and a good item to have in your GoPro accessory arsenal.
