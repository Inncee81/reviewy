A Small But Powerful Camera

I bought this camera because I wanted something small, much smaller than my Nikon and all the lens. A camera that was portable that I could take to places and events that I wouldn’t be able to take my Nikon I’m happy to report that this camera passed the test. It has an awesome 40xs optical zoom which is what caught my attention. Now the camera is small and fits right into the palm of your hand. When using the zoom, it was a task just trying to get it focused because it was really hard to keep the camera straight. But I’m starting to get the hang of it and have taken some amazing photos with this camera using the zoom. The camera also took some great photos in low light as well. Of course I sharpened them up in Lightroom but to be honest I do that with all photos that I take. The only negative that I had is the battery life. I shot many photos during an afternoon with the grandkids at a theme park recently and the battery that I had charged before I went….died. I attribute that to the lens opening and the zoom….Be sure to buy an extra batter and you should be fine. This camera is for folks that just want to downsize from the big multi lens cameras and lighten up your load a bit.
