This product is well built, and perfectly fit on …

This product is well built, and perfectly fit on the back of my GPS model. Only problem…I had to push so stinking hard to get the round opening to fit over the ball on my GPS non-skid dash-pad holder that I broke the plastic arm that connects to the suction cup. (Very irritating) I would take all the blame, and give it 5-stars, but I don’t see why was it had to be so stinking hard to get it to just “snap” onto the standard-size GPS ball mount. This product still swivels, and seems to fit perfectly…so I don’t think I forced a smaller product onto a bigger ball. Now I just need to replace the part I mounted it to… Regardless: I would buy this product again - but I would just suggest others watch where they apply force to their mount.

Doesn’t properly fit Garmin nuvi 2597LMT provided mount

Connection is too tight on Garmin provided mount for Garmin nuvi 2597LMT (which is the friction mount with four little sandbags and the mount that slides into that). Have to force it onto the ball mount and then some of the metal ring starts bending out of the bracket. If you plan on never removing your bracket from the mount and never, ever shift the bracket in the slightest this product will work.Otherwise, avoid it and pay a little more for something better because with the metal twisting out that way its not going to last too long.

Functional, low cost replacement

Fit my Garmin Drive 50 perfectly, a great replacement for my cracked and broken original. Both the part where it mounts on the Garmin and the ball joint fit, the ball joint was a bit ‘tight’, but that also means it is less likely to come off after it is snapped on. Overall, I’m impressed with the fit for the low price.
