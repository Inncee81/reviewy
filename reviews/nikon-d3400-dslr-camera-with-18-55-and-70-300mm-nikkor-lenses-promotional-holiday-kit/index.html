    Product Description

The new Nikon D3400 makes it easier than ever to take vibrant, sharp pictures and videos and share them with people instantly. The introduction on smartphones changed the way most people take and share pictures and video. The convenience of having a camera phone with an internet connection meant that people could take a photo or video and instantly share it with friends and family via text messages or social networking sites. However, the trade-off for this convenience is a huge drop in image quality. Using a smartphone camera in low-light environments frequently produces blurry and grainy images while trying to capture an action shot is largely a game of chance thanks to the phone’s slower performance. The Nikon D3400 changes all this by giving you the best of both worlds: You get beautiful 24MP images and HD 1080p videos that can be wirelessly transferred to your phone or tablet for instant sharing. The D3400’s compact design means it’s easy to take anywhere and the swappable NIKKOR lens system allows users to tailor the camera to the shooting environment. This particular camera includes a versatile 18-55mm NIKKOR lens that’s great for shooting portraits as well as landscapes and a 70-300mm telephoto lens for shooting subjects from long distances.
