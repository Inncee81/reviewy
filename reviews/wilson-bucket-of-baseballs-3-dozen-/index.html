Perfect for Batting Practice with Little Leaguers

Couldn’t be happier with this purchase. The balls all came individually wrapped. The Wilson A1030s are the type of baseball my son’s little league uses, which is what caught my eye. The balls are classified as blemished balls, but I have a hard time determining where the blemishes are (mainly just printing smudges). I have used them with my 11 year old son for batting practice, as well as his 12U travel team when I throw BP. So far they are holding up fantastically (probably have thrown them about 30 times); and with the raised seems they are pretty easy to throw accurately. There is a huge difference in how these balls jump off the bat compared to the old balls I have been using for BP. And for the price I paid (~$102), I have yet to find anything that comes close to the value. Highly recommend this to anyone who wants baseballs to use for practice with little league baseball players.
