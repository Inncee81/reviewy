    Product Description

Color:Blue/Green Whether at home or on the go, these snacking essentials will help make your little one the happiest lil’ snacker and keep snacktime mess-free. Set includes 2 Snack Catchers and 2 Click Lock Weighted Flexi-Straw Cups. Munchkin’s best-selling Snack Catcher is a portable snack bowl with soft, flexible flaps that allows independent toddlers to self-feed with a bit more dexterity and a lot less mess. Each spill-proof Snack Catcher has two handles and a no-slip rubber bottom. With the Weighted Flexi-Straw Trainer Cup, your toddler can hold it like a bottle, but drink from a straw. This cup is perfect for transitioning from bottle to sippy cup, allowing your toddler to drink from any angle. Flip top lid and Click Lock functionality are also perfect for use on the go. Must haves for toddlers on the move.

My favorite cups

I love these cups and snack bowls. The cups are so easy for my daughter to use and the plastic on the bowls is soft enough for her to be able to shove her hand in and get the food, but hard enough to keep the food inside if it’s tipped over. They would be 5 stars, but these cups love to spew out water through the straw. It seems like whenever I put cold water in, a little while later I look over and the straw is shooting it out and there’s a big puddle sitting on the table. The only way I’ve found to solve this is to open the top a little to relieve the pressure after it’s started leaking. It’s annoying but not annoying enough to stop purchasing them.
