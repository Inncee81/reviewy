A good price for the variety of tool bits.

The quantity of tool bits you are getting for the price is a good deal. I have not used these bits yet, but they seem to be made of very good material, are well machined, and the bits are sharp to the touch. They come in a nice plastic container that you can continue to use and keep them organized and stored in. For the price, these are well worth trying out. I will be using these bits for light duty projects around my shop. They also have an 1⁄8” shaft that will fit my Dremel 4000.

Excellent value; gets the job done

This is a great set. I use it for hard woods and they do what they are intended to do. The case is tops and allows to use it as a free standing bit holder. No problems at all. Highly recommended.

Don’t waste your money.

You get what you pay for. I got these yesterday, and had a chance to use it in my Foredom rotary tool. I wasn’t even ten minutes into cutting into PVC pipe when the straight fluted bit bent. I had 1⁄4” exposed before the cutter head, so it wasn’t bottomed out (which would render the bit useless). Now I’m down a bit on a brand new set. I’ve moved onto the straight bit with the rounded top and it cuts so smooth it melts the plastic… I.e. It’s not cutting at all.I’m not sure if I got a bad batch, but right now I cannot recommend these even at a cheap price point.
