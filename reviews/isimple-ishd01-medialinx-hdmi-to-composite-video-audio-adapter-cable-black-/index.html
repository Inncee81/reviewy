    Product Description

Product Packaging:Standard Packaging Connect your devices easily with the Pac ISHD01 HDMI To Composite Video/Audio Adaptor Cable. This specialty cable helps deliver a clean and clear audio and video quality in almost any application. The HDMI to composite cable is designed to convert digital content to analog compositive video and stereo analog audio RCA in a vehicle, office or boat’s entertainment system. It works with HDCP encoded videos. It features a standard female HDMI input. The RCA adaptor cable is easy to install, with no programming necessary.

Product Reviews

Works great, would recommend.

The form factor is nice (not too bulky) and it works brilliantly. Converts movies being played on an iPhone through the apple HDMI-adapter, and pipes them into the composite inputs in the rear-seat entertainment center of my car. No separate power cord needed, and the resulting image is just fine, both in color and clarity. There are noticeable pixelation artifacts in my setup, but I think that is the result of a old low-res LCD screen that came in my 2008 car (before super-high resolution screens were common in everything) rather than a lack of quality in the converter. I’d recommend this to anyone that is stuck with RCA inputs.
