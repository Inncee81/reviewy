The is definitely a company that likes to work through all options so that no one wastes their money. As a result, it’s pretty hard not giving them a five star review.My instructions for install:1. Fully clean your screen with a wet wipe that is safe for glasses or computer screens (do not use included package. It has alcohol and will strip your Oleophobic coating), then make sure it is completely dry. Then, use a different cloth to remove any dust or residues.2. Use the spray included in the box to completely cover your phone screen.3. Use the spray on your fingers that you plan to use when you install the screen protector. If you run out, make more of the fluid by mixing 3⁄4 water with 1⁄4 dish detergent.4. Pull the white sheet off the screen protector and lay that onto your phone screen. Adjust if needed by keeping the screen wet. As long as the screen’s wet, the phone will not adhere to the screen protector.5. After aligning, use a squeegee (included in package) or a credit card to squeeze all the water and bubbles out of the main section.6. Use a clean, dry towel (make sure it doesn’t have lint) and dry as much as you can.7. Use your palms to hold down on both curved edges of the phone until it adheres.8. Squeeze and dry water as you’re holding the edges.9. Finish with a hair dryer on low heat to completely close the seals.
