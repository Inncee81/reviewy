Great, cheap mount - holds our 65” Vizio E Series fine

Can’t beat the price on this mount. Bought this to mount a Vizio E65-C3 - I was a little worried at first since this was a really cheap mount and there were some questionable reviews. All in all, it took about 15 mins to assemble the wall bracket, and we had the TV on the wall in 30 mins. Seems very sturdy - just make sure you hit the studs when you screw the bracket to the wall!On a side note - with this mount there was very little clearance between the TV and the wall, so we had to plug in all of the cables BEFORE hanging the TV on the mount. Your experience may differ depending on your TV and where your ports are - but for the Vizio E65-C3, we could not access the rear ports once the TV was on the wall.

Great wall mount for the price!

I searched a few other sites and compared multiple wall mounts. My criteria was low profile, low price, ability to hold a 50” LED TV, and tilt. My wife and I wanted to mount the TV above our fire place directly in front of our couch so there was no need for left or right movement of the TV itself.After comparing multiple wall mounts, I settled on this one, hoping for the best. For the price I figured it was worth a shot and I could not be happier with the purchase.Came well packaged, instructions were clear but not needed really. The picture on the front of the box is enough illustration to put this together correctly. Came with a level so placing it on the wall and marking out places to predrill was not difficult at all. The tilting motion is smooth yet stiff which is what I wanted. It took about 15 minutes to put together, half of that was the time it took to find the tools needed. 1⁄2” socket is needed for the lag bolts, phillips head screw driver for the screws and a 5⁄16” wrench for the little nuts on the frame were the tools to put it together and get it on the wall. I elected to not use the plastic anchors that come with the kit because I was able to mount it to studs. It comes with 6 lag bolts, 4 to go on the top rail and 2 for the bottom rail. I had to use washers on the back of my Samsung as the plastic washers that came with the kit were too big or the mounting screw for the back of the TV was not long enough, 1 of the 2. No big deal as it worked out fine, I had some spare washers from another project.Will buy another to mount TV in another room!

This is a very good value, especially if you need something for studs …

This is a very good value, especially if you need something for studs more than 16” on center. There are instructions for mounting the wall plate, but there are not instructions for assembling the plate itself. It was not difficult, you just need to lay everything out in front of you and take your time with it. If I can provide a tip for anyone, put in the bolt (the one that is notched for the square hole, that does not have a philips head) in first, with a washer and tighten. The other two can then go on and use a wrench to hold the bolt and tighten with a screw driver. I tried to put these on first initially, and then did not have room to tighten that center bolt. Once everything is assembled, this mount is really sturdy. I have an older 47” Insignia LCD tv hanging on it, bolted into 2 studs and it is not going anywhere. You’ll also need a really long Philips screw driver to tighten the bolts on the bottom to secure the TV on the mount. For the price, do not skip over this mount. No need to pay 2 or 3 times the price, this is what you are looking for if you stumbled on this listing.
