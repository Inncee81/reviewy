    Product Description

CamelBak Podium 24 oz revolutionized the sport bottle by offering an innovative design that simplifies high performance hydration. The patented spill-proof Jet Valve allows the user to hydrate immediately without having to open or close a drinking interface and the bottle’s streamlined ergonomic design offers excellent squeezability without compromising bottle grip. The large aperture of the drinking nozzle generates an exceptional water flow rate and is easy to remove for thorough cleaning. The Podium also has a lockout dial with easy to read graphics for leak-proof transport.

It looks like a quality bottle and not cheaply designed/built. Pretty neat valve on the top and what you want water… you’re gunna get water… fast! I was expecting the bottle to be hard plastic - it’s not. It’s squishy. Not a bad thing, just thought i’d mention it incase you thought the same.

GREAT Sports Bottle!

Great water bottle. I love the quality and also the color scheme! I got the red and black one. I have had mine for nearly a year and it still works fine. However, I did remove the rubber nozzle tip because it was annoying to keep clean but even without the nozzle it works just fine. If you have trouble with a smell then I recommend cleaning soaking it in vinegar and water solution. I once tried dishwasher soap but it left a soapy taste for a few days.

No odd plastic taste, reliable, great for indoor spin workouts

This water bottle is great. I’ve had it for a year and use it mainly for indoor spin workouts. The mouth is wide enough to put ice in, although remember this isn’t the insulated version so the bottle will sweat. You have to squeeze to get water out, which means that if you turn it upside down it doesn’t spill.I only put water/ice in mine and have never had a problem with mold in the bite piece, which is surprising because I don’t take good care of it. I often leave water in and forget to wash the bottle until a few days later, or right before my next workout. The water doesn’t smell/taste weird, which has been a problem with every other water bottle I’ve ever had.As other reviewers have mentioned, it makes a quiet but weird hissing/screaming noise sometimes if you are using ice, and the ice melts creating suction on the inside. If you “burp” it by squeezing once it goes away. I never notice while I’m working out because of other noise, but if you’re doing yoga or something quiet I can see where it might be annoying.

Bicycle Bottle That Doesn’t Leak

After realizing that all of our bicycle bottles were really old, I suspected that they must be made of BPA. So I went on a hunt for a replacement that was free of toxic plastics. I didn’t come across many choices. After asking other purchasers if this was good as a bicycling bottle and they confirmed it was, I purchased one. This bottle is superior to my previous bicycle bottles as it doesn’t leak. You have to squeeze for the water to come out of the top, even when the nozzle is turned to open. I like that it doesn’t leak easily, but I don’t like have to squeeze it into my mouth when I want to drink water. But when I weighed the two: leaking vs having to squeeze, I found that I prefer that it doesn’t leak. I recommend this product to others.
