    Product Description

Color:Black CamKix Camera and Accessory Case for GoPro HERO4 and HERO5 Session Cameras

Keep your GoPro camera and accessories organized, dust-free and protected inside this case. Grab & go when you’re ready to shoot some spine-chilling action. Store it, when you’re not.

This case is designed specifically for the GoPro Hero4 Session camera with Standard or Low Profile Housing. Tailor made, fits perfectly. The Elastic Mesh Pocket and extra compartments are ideal to store flat mounts, quick release buckles, thumb screws, USB cable, memory cards, etc.

The High Quality EVA Interior not only fits your GoPro Hero4 Session and GoPro Smart Remote seamlessly, the shock-absorbing padding also provides protection to your camera and other equipment stored inside the case. If you remove the separate interior for the GoPro Smart Remote, space for extra accessories is created.

The outside of this case is made from durable nylon mesh. It’s tough and will not break, like hard plastic sometimes does. It’s also lightweight and provides grip, compared to smooth surfaces that can slip through your fingers.

Use the carabiner to conveniently attach the Carry Case to the belt loop of your pants, a larger bag or a backpack.

Contents:

  • 1x Case (6.7 x 4.7 x 2.8 inch/17 x 12 x 7 cm)

  • 1x High Quality EVA Inlay

  • 1x Carabiner

  • 1x Microfiber Cleaning Cloth
