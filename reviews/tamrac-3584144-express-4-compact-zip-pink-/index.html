    Product Description

The Tamrac 3584 Express Case 4 is an ultra suede case in a vertical, ultra-thin design that holds and protects an ultra-compact digital camera. It features protective closed-cell foam padding and a zippered top closure for security. Elastic sides provide a perfect fit to most ultra thin digital cameras. It has a front pocket for an accessory. A belt loop and removable shoulder strap provide convenient carrying options.

Product Reviews

pretty dusty rose

Fits my Canon G9x perfectly, plenty of room for spare battery in the front pocket. Exactly what I wanted to protect the camera in a purse or tote, plus the belt loop on back fits securely over the strap of my DSLR bag when I travel. As noted by others, IRL the color isn’t Pepto-pink, but a subtle, pretty dusty rose.

Nice case for non-ultra slim compact cameras

I was looking for a compact case to hold my Panasonic Lumix ZS15 and all the cases in fun colors I was hoping for (something more feminine than the standard black, generic looking cases) were all too narrow. This one fits it perfectly. I was hoping there might be extra room for other things, but there isn’t. That’s okay though since it does what I wanting it to do most. The clips on the side are a little clunky, difficult to undo & the way to me and I would prefer something different, but it works. Overall a nice case and it seems sturdy & well made.

Great fit

Got this for my Canon GX9, fits great, just the right size for camera and two batteries.

Great for the Canon G9X Mark II

Perfect fit for the Canon Powershot G9X Mark II. Well made and secure feeling and has both a neck strap and a belt loop to slide over a belt.

Refined and Stylish

The pink suede on the camera case is a dusty rose color and not a bright pink. It’s a very nice color and I’m glad it’s not bright or neon. The screen color doesn’t do it justice, because the color is a bit deeper than what it looks like here. The case fits my Nikon COOLPIX S6200 16 MP Digital Camera with 10x Optical Zoom NIKKOR ED Glass Lens and HD 720p Video (Blue) perfectly. Because the case is small enough, I can wear it under a raincoat or jacket without adding bulk. That means I’m more likely to carry my camera around for those unexpected photo-ops.The front flap is secured by velcro and lifts to expose a pocket that has elastic mesh on both sides. The pocket is big enough for a spare battery or memory chip, or to hold a credit card or subway pass card. The interior is one large pocket for the camera.I gave the bag only four stars because the strap could really use a billet, one of those loops that holds the extra length of a strap in place. I’ve shortened the strap and the part that’s doubled tends to get caught on things. I may end up making a holder for it. It’s not a huge problem, and I’d give the item 4.5 stars if I could.

Cute Little Case!

Selected this Tamrac camera case for my new Samsung TL320 Digital Camera. It is pretty in pink and a perfect fit. The Express 4 case has extra storage for an SD card or battery and a shoulder strap. There is a loop on the back to lace through a belt or another bag’s strap. Tamrac makes a good case as I already own a larger Tamrac case for my DSLR. Be sure to check out Tamrac’s Website as they have a chart that helps you fit the right case to your particular camera. This was purchased via Beach Camera and it arrived quickly and in new condition.
