Product Specifications

  • WSS2-W1

    Product Description

These SANUS speaker stands are designed to work with both the SONOS PLAY:1 and PLAY:3 speakers in any orientation. They position the speakers at the speaker manufacturer’s recommended angle and securely support the speaker for optimum bass response. The column and base have wire channels to make the cables disappear. This product is sold as a pair (2 stands - Speakers not included).

Overall I think this is a decent product, but there are some problems

When I opened the box, I could see that one of the posts was damaged. It does not impact functionality, but esthetically it is pretty displeasing because it faces out and is highly visible. I plan to reach out to the seller to see if they can send me a replacement post without having to send the whole unit back.The collars that are meant to disguise the connection points at the top where it meets the underside of the speaker and at the bottom where it meets the base do not seat properly so there is a noticeable gap. Not necessarily a big deal for the bottom, but the top is at eye level when sitting so it’s quite visible.It is sturdy, due in part to a large and heavy base. The bottom weighting is necessary should you want to mount a Play 3 or Play 5 to prevent it from being top-heavy, but is overkill if you are just using them for Play 1s.But the most disappointing thing is that the top mount does not rotate. It is in a fixed position due to a lip that the mount sits on and the base attaches to. It prevents you from being able to orient the direction of the speaker, which for surround sound is critical. In most applications that could be solved by just positioning the whole unit, but when placing these behind a sofa which is close to a wall, you have no option but to put the flat end of the base against the wall which means you can either point it straight ahead, or at about a 70 degree angle; neither position is ideal.I’ll add that the inclusion carpet spikes is a nice touch to ensure the stands stay in place. I use the stands on a hardwood floor surface, so the included rubber footing work well to prevent the base from making contact with the floor (which would otherwise scratch it) and keep the unit from slipping. However, unlike the spikes, here is no height adjustment for the rubber mounts so if you have an uneven floor, expect that you will have to get creative with your own solution, otherwise they will tilt to one side.

Be careful with assembly instructions

The quality of the materials is good, but I only offer this caution. Take time to be sure you are installing the right plastic caps on each pole. In the packaging my double (set) packet, the covers for the top and bottom of each stand are not marked as to which goes on which end, and they are packaged in a way that I used both on one stand, and one should have been on the other stand, so I was stuck with 2 with the opening on one stand and 2 with no opening on the other which looks bad. And, they are almost impossible to get on and almost impossible to get off. Just pay attention to what goes where, and you will be fine. Great, excellent service from the company when I pointed this out! I would recommend.

Great stands for SONOS PLAY 1 speakers

They’re very sturdy and the SONOS PLAY 1 speakers fit nicely on them. They’re easily moved (we bring the set outside for entertaining purposes), which was important to us. Really dresses up a surround sound SONOS setup.

There are dedicated brackets for Play 1 and both vertical and horizontal Play 3 mounting. The box also includes both rubber feet and spikes for carpeted rooms. I have a set of white stands with white speakers which look great together. Speaker is about 4’ high on the stand which is right at ear level while seated watching TV.
