Product Reviews

Not for Phantom or flying, read on!

I got this protective lens for my GoPro 3 black, after reading the reviews decided to order it to protect my lens from damage and scratches.My GP3 is primarily use on my Phantom 2 with a H3-3D gimbal.The bad:The first thing I noticed right away was that my shots from pics and video are more obscure even on good lighting conditions, probably about two steps down, the biggest and more annoying issue is when shooting wide pics or video, you will get an “black halo” on the right side from the protective lens (see picture). One more issue that I never had before was “jello” on the video, on the same day of flying under same conditions (snow day) I removed the protective lens and the the “jello” effect was gone!On 4K the “jello effect” is far worst.Also, the lens need to be attached after you secure the GoPro3 to the gimbal or is impossible to secure it with the bracket, not a big issue but is there.The Good:On very sunny days the two steps down are also on the positive side, the protective lens work more like a polarized filter.Final words:The protective lens may work in a different application, but for flying on you Phantom is not a good choice.
