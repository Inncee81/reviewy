Product Reviews

What good is it if you can’t install it?

Whether or not the product works as intended (which to be fair, it appears to be) is almost irrelevant due to the absolutely atrocious design flaw surrounding installation. The VR Cover website gives a nice looking video that makes installation look like a breeze, but it also makes it look like the Vive has no velcro whatsoever holding the facemask in place beneath the VR Cover. They expect you to push little tabs of fabric between the velcro of the facemask and the headset, but doing this without getting things stuck to the rest of the velcro is either an arcane science or a rarified form of performance art. And the really fun part is that you have to do this every time you wash it, and I expect to be washing it far more regularly than I would a less absorbent covering.What this really needs is to have washable elastic straps that you place on the facemask before installing it onto the Vive. As it is, it’s just a piece of fabric that never seems like it’s fitting properly.
