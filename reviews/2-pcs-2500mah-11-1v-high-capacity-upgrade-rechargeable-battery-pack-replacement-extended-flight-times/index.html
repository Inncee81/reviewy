Ok batteries or the price but not great…

Ok Batteries for the price. I bought these batteries hoping that they would increase my flight time and there extend the fun of flying my Bebop, but they don’t perform much better than the stock 1200mAh batteries. I am in the process of documenting a more accurate explanation by recording the flight times of these batteries. I will update this review after I have documented more flights. As other reviewers have pointed out, these batteries fit onto the quad, even though mine took some breaking in to fit properly (they were too tight). They both feel the same as the original batteries, but as I said last just about as long, which is around 7 - 10 minutes depending on how you’re flying. I’m giving these batteries 3 stars because they shipped fast and work with my drone, but they definitely don’t perform better than the stock 1200mAh batteries that came with my quad. If you want a cheaper battery than stock I’d buy these, but if you want extended flight times, fine another brand/model. I hope this helps someone else.

Decent batteries, but do not significantly increase flying time

I knew that although these are rated as over twice the capacity as the original batteries, that I was not going to double flying time. However, I was a bit disappointed to find that I have only gained a minute or two at best. The original batteries provided 6-7 minutes of flying time, and these provide 7-8 minutes. They do feel almost exactly the same as the original batteries and are no worse, so I am not really unhappy with them, just disappointed.I don’t know for sure, but I have read some comments that sound as thought the drone itself is configured to read battery power in such a way that it isn’t really able to take full advantage of higher capacity batteries. I don’t know if that is true or not, but it seems that it may be.Two of these batteries are cheaper than buying two original batteries, so I wouldn’t say not to buy them, just don’t expect them to significantly increase your flying time.

Unremarkable

These batteries arrived on time, which is why I gave 3 stars instead of 2. The did hold a charge, and did fit into the Bebop, but they did NOT add any flight time at all. In fact, they performed about the same as the stock 1200mAh batteries.Others have gotten better results, but mine have not been stellar by any stretch. Also, they would not charge on my 3-bank charger. I have since purchased and received two 1700mAh batteries that out-performed these and work fine in the charger.

Don’t spend the extra money for these batteries

I don’t see the extended flight time advertised on these batteries. They are fine as a second set but they don’t last any longer than the originals that came with my quad copter. I also had issues getting them to fit into the chargers. Plus shipping from china took a lot longer than most items I buy off Amazon.
