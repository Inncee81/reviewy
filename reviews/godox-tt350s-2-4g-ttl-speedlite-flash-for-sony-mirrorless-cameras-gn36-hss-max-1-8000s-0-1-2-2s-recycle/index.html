Excellent Flash to work with Godox Flash Ecosystem.

I might be one of a few photographers that are using Fujifilm Camera to shoot weddings. My current Flash set up including 6 Godox TT600, 2 Godox V850, a couple of Godox studio light and several Godox 433 MHZ and 2.4 GHZ flash trigger.I also have a Sony A6000 with Zeiss 16-70 with a few prime lenses for my occasional video work. This flash is obviously for my Sony A6000. The reason I purchase this is because the compatibility of the rest of my Godox Flash Eco system.Here are my initial observations:1. The packaging materials seemed to be in pretty good in quality, the owner’s manual is bilingual in both English and Chinese. Since I can read both, I do not see any discrepancies in the translation of the manual. So far they explained almost everything I needed to know.2. The plastic material on the flash seemed okay, just feel a bit weaker than Godox 600TT3. The size of the flash is only about half of the TT600 flash, light weight with back lit LCD to display all the functions of the flash, and all the buttons seemed to be made of high quality gears/springs.4. The function/layout is a bit different than TT600 on turning on the radio, I had to dig into the manual to find out how to turn on the radio. Once it is turned on, all the layout of changing channels and groups seemed to be logical.5. As an on camera flash, the TTL works great!, For the initial half an hour, it has not underexpose or overexpose my photos.6. I tried the HHS, it seemed to work great as well.7. The flash is not very powerful as TT600, but since it is very small and fits Sony A6000 beautifully, I will just turn up the ISO to compensate the shortcoming of power.8. TT350 works great with TT600, the only drawback that it might have, should be the ability to use TTL function remotely with TT600. When I use I am not sure if it is because TT600 is just a manual flash that won’t work when TT350 is commanding the slave flash as TTL. When I turn the slave channel to act as manual flash, it works as promised.9. I will purchase an additional TT350S to or TT600S to test the slave TTL. Will update my finding soon.I really hope Godox can make this flash dedicated for Fujifilm Cameras, this would be what I need the most at this moment.I am going to use Sony A6000 with this flash, for my next wedding shoot, will also update my review using it for an event.
