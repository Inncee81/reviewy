 LOW GLOW VS NO GLOW TRAIL CAMERASThis camera is a no-glow, or BLACK FLASH, camera. A no-glow camera is nearly undetectable by animals or humans (you can see a faint glow if you are very close). No-glow cameras generally make good security cameras for this reason. The trade off is that night pictures are a bit more “grainy”, i.e. lower quality night pictures compared to low-glow, or red-flash, cameras.TRIGGER TIME:o 0.6 to 0.8 second trigger time for picturesPROS:+ Detection range of 60-65 feet is good+ Picture trigger time+ Day pictures and videos are good+ Video with audio+ I like the clip style strap to attach to a tree compared to buckle straps for securing to a tree, because it’s quick and easyCONS:- Slow picture recovery time, or time between pictures/videos of approximately 60 seconds.
