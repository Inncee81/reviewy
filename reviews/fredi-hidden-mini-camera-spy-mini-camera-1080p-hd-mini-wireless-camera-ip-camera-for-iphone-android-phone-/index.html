5 Stars for image quality, ease of use, and features for the price.

First impression of the box was “wow. First impression of the contents inside was “wow”. The product is coated with silky rubbery material. It seems well made. Connection with the camera using the cellphone app is flawless and easy. The app is somewhat intuitive. However, I had to test out each buttons in the app to figure out what each does. I’m still a little confuse on some of the buttons but otherwise it does what I need it to. When connected to the cellphone via wifi, all snapshots and videos are saved in your cellphone. When not connected via wifi, the snapshots and videos are saved locally to the SD card inside the device. The videos saved in the cellphone is nice but of course not really true 1080P. It cannot compete with GoPro quality videos or snapshots. I then viewed the SD card in my cellphone and it’s basically the same image quality. I did that in case the image quality gets downgraded to a lower resolution if it’s done through Wifi but it doesn’t. Again, image quality of both videos and snapshots are the same either in the SD card remotely or when it’s saved via wifi into your cellphone. The video goes into black and white mode when the night mode is activated. It seems to work with objects in front of the camera but I haven’t tested it yet inside a big room. I will primarily use it for hiking, using it as a small camera to take snapshots, and probably add as another surveillance camera for my dogs. IThe only improvement I recommend for the actual device is a button to switch the wifi on/off and also another button to switch the night mode on/off. As for the video/snapshot image quality and features in relation to price then I’ll give it 5 stars. Definitely worth the money.
