Product Specifications

  • Custom Case for UDI 818A Helicopter + Parts
  • Custom cut out foam to fit 818A: 6 slots for batteries, slot for battery, transmitter, proepller & charger
  • Classy & Stylish black exterior
  • UDI 818A comes in 2 different version chargers: This case fits the skinny/rectangle version. However, the square/block version would fit by cutting (scissor) part of the propeller slot

  • Includes Carrying Case only, quadcopter or any of the parts are sold separately

    Product Description

Description

Protect and carry your UDI 818A quad copter with this sleek, stylish and classy carrying case. The inner foam is custom cut to fit the 818A quad copter, slots for 6 batteries, transmitter, propeller, and charger/tools. Exterior is a nice and classy black finish with silver border.

Please Note

UDI 818A comes in 2 different version chargers. This case is custom cut for the the skinny/rectangle version. However, the square/block version would fit by cutting (scissor) part of the propeller slot. Please see photo for more detail.

Only the case is for sale in this listing; The quad copter and parts are for display purposes only.

Need to Modify Foam for Standard 818A Features

I like the way the case looks, how light it is with all the drone and accessories, how thin it is and the foam cushioning inside. The only problems are that the UDI 818A with mounted landing gear does not fit without modification by cutting slots in the foam thus allowing them to fit and then the copter body and motors fit into the slots already cut-out. Then it is a snug fit and works fine. The newest U818A comes with a VR headset and bonus battery. The headset cannot fit inside the case along with all the other accessories and elements. To fit the controller into the provided foam space, the smartphone holder for FPV needs to be unscrewed from the 2.4ghz controller and then slots must be carved for the phone holder brackets so that the controller fits into the area provided. I also had to carve a new slot for the bonus 2800 mAh backup battery and that worked well in spare space. The two included batteries and the extra three I bought do not fit into the very small slots provided in the foam without struggling and so I just stacked all of them into one of the larger slots and stuffed the cords and chargers into the smaller slots. It works, but I was disappointed that all of those things were not taken into account. I expected the preformed foam to be an exact match since it was advertised as being for the UDI 818A. It still works and basically I am satisfied, but you don’t expect to be required to cut-up such a nice looking foam with a new product.
