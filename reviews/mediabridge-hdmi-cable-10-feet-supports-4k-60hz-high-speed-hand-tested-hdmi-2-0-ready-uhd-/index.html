I have a new 4K TV and dvd player. I was told by several “experts” that better and/or more expensive HDMI cables would not make a difference. Not so! The right HDMI cables(HDMI 2.2 compliant) make a big, noticeable difference, at least with 4K. I purchased a set of Fusion 4K cables (nearly 5 times the price) and these Mediabridge cables. Honestly, they both appear to do the same outstanding job. I switched them from TV to dvd player and back and cannot tell the difference. But switching back to my “old” HDMI cables makes a very noticeable difference. The picture is still good, but without the clarity and total focus using the better cables. Which of the two would I buy again? Most likely the Mediabridge and save myself some money. The 4K cables are beautiful, but only time will tell if there is a difference in degradation over time. I love both right now!
