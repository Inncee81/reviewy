THough it works in either of the flossers they sell…I have seen no advantage to these whitening tips. I bought them both together with an order for the regular ones. I may think they are a bit stronger as they seem to hold up a day or two use. I’ve got braces as an older person having never had braces and freaked about how to dental floss and keep my gums and teeth healthy during the brace period. So led me to try these out. They are great for cleaning the gums and between the teeth gum area. I love the whole battery flosser for that. BUT I can’t see the more expensive of these white tips verses the blue ones. And I see no whitening effect at all. I have my doubts about it at all frankly as in really is there anything to it? No scent no notice of any detergent effect or taste release from these compared to the blue tips. Nothing at all. And no difference in atual performance of one over the other. So I’ll just buy the blue tips from now on. And safe yourself some money and buy the blue system to begin with the flosser as you can then purchase these tips if you really want to. I do wish I could pick a color as I hate blue but they are selected for you…pink or greenish or blue. Right! It works so no problem. So these are ditched only because there is no evidence as to whitening effectiveness at all. Really none! Overall however the little battery flosser and it’s blue tips get a big 5 stars for coming to my aid and effective in cleaning between my gums right past the braces. They do not clean the upper teeth…I do a faux sort of half measure with floss still there. These also do not fit well in my farthest back molars and back teeth but it’s still as good as or better than the water pik I also use. But more portable. Really 5 stars for portablity for the whole system.
