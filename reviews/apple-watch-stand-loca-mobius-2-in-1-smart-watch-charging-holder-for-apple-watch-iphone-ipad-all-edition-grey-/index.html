Great Product - Especially Useful for Phone on It’s Side

This is exactly what I was looking for. I use it to watch Netflix on before bed so that the light of the TV doesn’t bother my husband. I needed something that allowed me to rest my phone on it’s side but where the base wouldn’t cover the screen at all. I also needed something where the angle wasn’t tipped too far back so I could see the screen of the phone while lying on my side in bed, and it’s perfect. As I want my phone on its side anyway, the fact that there is nowhere for the phone charger to directly connect with the stand is a non-issue for me (as it can be plugged in while on it’s side). In fact, having the phone be able to directly plug into the bottom would prevent the phone from being able to lie on it’s side, so this is actually better for me. The slot for the watch cable fits perfectly and I have no troubles charging my watch by simply letting it rest on top. The stand is light and plastic, but it doesn’t slide around on my glass night table, and it’s great quality for the price. I also appreciate how little room it takes up since I don’t have much space to work with.
