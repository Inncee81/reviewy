Yuneec Q500 Typhoon UAV Pilot Simulator Wi-Fi USB Stick For PC Software Dongle

by people ...

  • Simulator for Yuneec Aircraft

Overview

Start your flight experience with the Yuneec flight simulator, users can experience real flight characteristics even when sitting in front of the computer. The flight simulator with the Simu-connector is compatible with all transmitters supported by Yuneec protocol, and provides users with a realistic flight experience in a safe, simulated environment. The flight simulator can be controlled without any wires within 10m, and features virtual telemetry data displayed on the interface.

Specs

Compatible with PC Only Dimensions: 2.99 x 0.91 x 0.31 in (76x23x8mm) Weight: 0.25+0.004oz (7+0.1g) Range Frequency Range: 2405 kHz - 2480 kHz Compatible Devices: ST10, ST10+, ST24 Transmit Power: 4.5dbm Working Current: 40mA

MUST HAVE FOR TYPHOON H OWNERS!

I purchased this to be used with my Typhoon H as I was informed that it was now compatible with the remote that comes with the Typhoon H. Yuneec has also updated the software so that you can fly a simulated Typhoon H with this simulator. I have to say, I was VERY impressed with how realistic and accurate the simulator was! With the Typhoon H remote you can control all of the basic flight functions of the H, including the landing gear. You can even rotate and pan the camera that is on the simulator. And just like the real thing, you can adjust the speed of the H by the joystick as well as the speed dial.TAKING OFF NOTE!One thing that I did have to learn is, in order to take flight, you have to make sure the speed dial is NOT at the slowest setting. This means you will need to push the lever that is on the right side in the front up slightly. You will hear a “beep” when you did this that indicates you are able to take off.Please note that the simulated camera does NOT broadcast any images! The simulator is not designed for that. It’s purpose is to provide flight experience and training.There are also 3 Challenges in the simulator that grade your performance and allow you to keep track of your progress. My first attempt at Challenge #1 yielded me a 19%, however, after several tries I reached a high of 89%. Because I am an over achiever, I’m pushing for an A!With this simulator you will DEFINITELY improve your control of your drone. I HIGHLY recommend that anyone who owns a Yuneec drone purchase this!

Great way for a beginner to learn to fly his/her new drone. A must have in my opinion.

This really should come with the Q500 drone but glad it’s available. I am a newbie flying drones. I had a cheapie that got away from me so wanted to get one that had a bit of more control so bought the Yuneec Q500+. Flew it twice but felt a bit clumsy with it so put it back in the box and ordered this. I didn’t find it hard at all to get connected to my controller. The software was a bit touchy on the computer, but my computer isn’t exactly fast or new. Once I got it working, I found it really did feel like flying the drone. I have always defaulted to the “headless” mode or what ever they call it so never really had any experience flying normally. This gave me the opportunity to practice that and I found it flies better that way. The software also has some activities to help you learn control, one is a flying box that you are supposed to keep the drone inside the box. It’s harder than it looks. Once I took the real drone out for a real flight, I felt a lot more confident and in control. I highly recommend you buy this along with your drone and use this first if you have not had much flying experience.
