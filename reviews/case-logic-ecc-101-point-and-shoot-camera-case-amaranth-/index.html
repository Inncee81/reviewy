It is very form fitting on the camera so you have just enough room for the camera, an extra battery and an extra memory card. Still gave it 4 stars because it’s not advertised as a purse, it’s advertised as a camera case and it does that. It’s small size is an advantage when you’re tucking it in a larger travel bag.
