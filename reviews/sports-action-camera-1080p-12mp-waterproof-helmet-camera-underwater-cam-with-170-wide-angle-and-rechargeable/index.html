Wonderful camera for the price

 Wow, do I love this camera. I have been using a GoPro Hero for a while now but one of the main complaints I have always had with it is there is no viewer screen to see what’s going on. While it isn’t a huge viewer screen, it is plenty big to see what is going on. You can certainly make sure what mode it is in from the rear side. Sure, the GoPro has a little LCD on the front to indicate things, but when riding I can’t see what’s going on like is it still running or did the battery die.The programming on this is great and it is so easy to use. It comes with so many parts, a lot more than my previous action camera as well as an extra lock part and back door. There is everything from a wipe, helmet mounts and a mount you can use for your belt. It comes with 1 battery that seems to last for a long time. It was charged up a bit when it arrived.The pictures I am adding to the review are from the camera itself and were created by this camera. I took the video in 1080P. It does make the file big. Like near 1 MB per 10 minutes. But that is not too bad and it will be a while to fill up 32 GB. I like that is also has motion capture ability as well.I had already a 32 GB card that I used in my GoPro. I just popped that in there formatted it and it is using it fine. The only downside is it doesn’t have Wi-Fi so all transfers have to be wired. I did not test the waterproof ability of the case. One thing I do like about the case compared to my other camera is the lock mechanism. It has a slide thing that has to be moved before you can open it to keep it from accidentally popping open. The camera is also a very tight fit in the case.it does not come with any software for editing. There is also plenty of apps out there though to do video editing as well. What else can I say but I am really happy with the camera.
