    Product Description

The Kettler table tennis net measures 70” in length and is made from high quality nylon mesh, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor use. Slide the netting into your existing posts for quick and easy installation. This replacement net does not include posts and fits all Kettler table tennis tables. Made in Germany and is covered by a 1 year manufacturer warranty for residential use. Does not include tension adjuster tabs.

I also found that trying to push the retainer connector back into the hole to allow for easy folding of the main net is next to impossible due to tension on the supports when assembled with the net.
