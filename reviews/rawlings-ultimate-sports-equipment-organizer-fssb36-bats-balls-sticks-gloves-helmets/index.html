Holds more than you think & great quality

I really like this organizer overall. It holds a lot of sports equipment & is very nice quality, especially for the price. The only downside is that the top right holder for equipment does not work well for anything larger like a soccer ball/basketball. We have kids soft bouncy balls & hard bouncy balls (like for dodgeball) in the lower holder so that they are easily accessible for our kids. We placed the nicer soccer balls & basketballs in the top holder. The way that the zipper is attached there is not enough space in the opening to remove the balls quickly. You have to unzip the top of the holder & lift out. Is it a dealbreaker? No. It is annoying thoough & not something that my Kids could do o their own as they cannot reach the top.
