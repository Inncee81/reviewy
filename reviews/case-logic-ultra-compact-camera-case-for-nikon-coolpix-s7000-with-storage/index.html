Product Reviews

Quality Case, Perfect Size

I bought a Nikon Coolpix S7000 and needed a case for it. Most cases seemed to be too big or have too many pockets. I wanted a good but compact case that would fit in my pants pocket while out on vacation. This case delivered! Besides being the perfect size, the case looks good and seems to be well made. I can put my belt through the non-intrusive rear strap, which is an added bonus, plus there is a carabiner if you need to clip the case to something. I recommend this case if you need something low profile for your new S7000.

Great Small Case

I recommend this case to anyone looking for a small, compact, case with a little bit of protection for your camera. The Nikon S7000 fits in this case perfectly and there seems to be a decent amount of padding to protect my camera from minor bumps and short distance drops. The front pocket is quite small, but can easily fit an extra SD card or battery. This case is perfect to take hiking and for traveling. Because it’s so compact, there is no room for the battery charger in this case.

perfect little bag for our new camera

perfect little bag for our new camera. I also like the belt-loop on the back side so I can attach it to my belt which insures I will never forget to put the camera back in my pocket… or for that matter, have to worry about cramming that camera in my pocket along with all the other junk I carry.

Compact design works well

Compact, well made and well designed, this case offers two ways to attach to a person. It has a belt loop for slipping onto a belt, plus an aluminum ring which can be clipped to a belt, a belt loop, or a backpack ring.

Great bag for my small point and shoot

Great bag for my small point and shoot. Fits snugly and feels like it has sufficient padding to protect the camera. Also, the design is nice and inconspicuous.

It is big enough to fit my Nikon Coolpix S7000 …

It is big enough to fit my Nikon Coolpix S7000 in the main compartment with a small zippered area on the front to store the battery and memory chip when not in use–that is all I wanted it to hold. It has a belt loop and a small carabiner.

Limited zipper opening is a big negative.

The zipper length is too short which means the case opening is limited – which means that it’s relatively difficult inserting and removing the camera.I cannot recommend this case for the Nikon CoolPix S7000.
