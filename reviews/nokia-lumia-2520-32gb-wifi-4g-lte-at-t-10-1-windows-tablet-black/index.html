Experience PC productivity with the mobility and fun of a tablet with the Nokia Lumia 2520. The Lumia 2520 features 4G LTE connectivity and a quad-core processor for fast email, browsing, and gaming. True mobility means being able to use your tablet wherever you choose to go. The Nokia Lumia 2520 has a 10.1-inch Full HD display with Nokia ClearBlack technology for amazing outdoor readability so you can use it anywhere under the sun. Enjoy stunning video playback at 1080p, powerful gaming, fast email, and easy document viewing from virtually anywhere. Create and edit Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and OneNote files on the go. Sync your email and calendars with Outlook. And with 32GB of internal memory (expandable up to 64GB via the microSD card slot) and SkyDrive, you can easily save your documents and pick up where you left off. The Lumia 2520 comes with both a 6.7MP rear-facing camera with ZEISS optics and low-light capability, and a 2MP HD front-facing camera for great video calls. With the Windows Store, you have access to over 100,000 apps including popular apps like Twitter, Facebook, Skype, Netflix and Hulu Plus. You can also pin websites to your Start screen and use them like apps. And with Windows RT 8.1, you can display two apps side by side to use them at the same time and view real-time app info on Live Tiles. With the powerful 8,000mAh battery, the Lumia 2520 delivers up to 11 hours of continuous use. PLEASE NOTE: AT&T 4G LTE connectivity requires an AT&T data plan (not included). However, you can still connect online with a valid WiFi internet connection via the built-in WLAN 802.11 a/b/g/n
