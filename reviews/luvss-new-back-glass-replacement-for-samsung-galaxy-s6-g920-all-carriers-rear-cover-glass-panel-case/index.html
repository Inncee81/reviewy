Quick and easy

Excellent fit and replacement for my S6 that had a completely shattered rear glass. The pre-installed adhesive made switching the rear glass a breeze. I was concerned with removing the broken rear glass myself, but after heating the edges and pulling up on it with a suction cup I was able to get the edge of a plastic pry tool under it. I found it much easier to simply pull the pry tool around the edge to release the old adhesive. It took a little bit of pressure to do so, but not being concerned about the old glass made the removal easy. Also, I thought I would be fighting the little glass shards, but the S6 rear glass has a pretty tough thin plastic that the glass is attached to and it kept most of the shards in place. After cleaning off all of the old adhesive, putting this new glass in was simple. Couldn’t be happier.
