Not oleophobic by my definition.I have used glass screen protectors on my tablets and phones whenever possible for years, going back to my galaxy s4, note 10, notepro 12.2, and note 4 (note 4 eventually gave up and went back to a traditional film, as the note 4 has a slightly curved screen which prohibits edge-to-edge glass coverage). Even my huawei smartwatch has a glass screen protector. Sadly the manufacturer of glass protectors that I have used before (and liked) does not yet provide a glass protector for the tabpro s.Item arrived reasonably packaged, and included a protective film on the side of the protector which adheres to the screen, there was no protective film on the ‘user side’ but since this is supposed to be 9h glass, it really shouldn’t need it. the whole glass was in a white foam envelope inside of the cardboard box and two screenwipes were included. (see photos).Before I peeled off the backing and started to prep my install environment, I placed the screen protector for a ‘dry fit’ and immediately noticed two problems:1: the cutouts for the camera and light sensor are a little off center when compared with the center placement of the screen.2: (This is the big one) the user side is seriously prone to picking up fingerprints. At first I thought this might only be on the protective film covering the adhesive side, but close analysis shows that it is definitely on the user side. I wiped my thumb on the gorilla glass for comparison and it was far less noticeable. This is a deal-breaker in my book and I will be returning the product. The product is described as having an oleophobic surface, but comparing to any of the 4 other glass screen protectors in my houseAdditionally you may notice in the pictures that the screen, even when it’s clear of fingerprints, still adds a visible layer that would reduce contrast over the factory screen. Granted, this may be due to the protective film covering the adhesive side, and the adhesive not making direct contact with the glass, but with my mind decided to return the product, I wasn’t going to peel back the film to see.Considering that one of my major deciding factors in replacing my notepro 12.2 with the tabpro s was the oled screen, I really don’t want to reduce the spectacular image this screen produces.Pros: Seems like actual glass, feels like glass, packaged well and delivered quicklyCons: fingerprint madness! Camera cutouts don’t line up perfectly. possible reduction in contrast over factory screen.
