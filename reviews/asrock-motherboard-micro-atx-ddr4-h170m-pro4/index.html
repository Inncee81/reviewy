Solid Motherboard!

Overall this is a solid motherboard. It supports LGA 1151 Skylake Intel processors and has four PCIE and four DDR4 RAM slots. Its BIOS is very helpful, it detects your processor and RAM and other hardware and lets you control things like fan speed inside the case. It has plenty of USB on the side you you can plug lots into it.I have just a couple of minor complaints about it. First of all it is important to know that the bottom PCIE slot is very close to all othe the lower ports (i.e. for USB, HDAUDIO, etc) and if you were to install something large in this slot like a graphics card, the card would obstruct these other ports. When I installed the processor, I had to push really hard on the CPU socket’s retention arm to get the processor to clamp down into place, so hard that I kept thinking I was about to break it.But don’t take these criticisms to harshly - overall I am still very pleased with this motherboard and I would definitely recommend it to a friend.

Does exactly what I need

ASRock does a good job at keeping their boards up-to-date with the latest tech. Their only crutch is their ability to self-update their BIOS from Internet. It doesn’t bother me too much considering you can just use a flash drive.Speed is great, aesthetically pleasing, does exactly what any normal computer user or gamer would want to do. If you want to overclock, go with a better version of this board.
