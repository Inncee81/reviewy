Outstanding piece of gear!

Purchased this mount after reading lots of reviews. Used to mount a flat LED Samsung 40” TV to our living room wall. It it very heavy duty and easy to install. Looks good too. This also allows me to upgrade to a bigger or curved TV later and still use the same mount.Helpful hints:1. Many reviews talked about the TV falling forward and not staying perfectly vertical. There are tension bolts and hex nuts on the mount. The two bigger 1⁄2” nuts are for the tilt feature. I put a put small turns on those nuts and it tightened right up and the TV stays vertical.2. There are wire guides in the plastic sleeves that fit over the frame that I didn’t see mentioned in the reviews or write-up that help clean up the wires.3. The kit comes with plastic spacers to fit between the TV brackets and the TV. I didn’t realize till after I mounted it that it would provide more room for the RCA jack connections on my TV had I used them. Either way, it still works well.4. The kit only comes with 1 90 degree HDMI connector. Luckily I only needed one but plan ahead.5. The mini magnetic level that comes with the kit is awesome!I didn’t call customer service but their number is right in the kit and they are willing to help you solve your issues.Ordering a second one to mount another TV.

Works and looks great but 2 issues….

Setup was straightforward, would have given higher score except:(1) The bolts, washers and spacers provided to attach support bars to back of TV didn’t work as planned. I have a 2yr old Samsung 55”, it used the M8 bolts (various lengths to choose from) and no combination of the supplied bolts, spacers, or washers fastened the support bars securely. It was either the bolt was too short, couldn’t thread into back of TV or too long and I was left with play in spacer and where as I couldn’t tighten them down securely. I ended up using 2 extra washers I had in my workshop, along with the long spacer. No huge deal but I’m glad I had the spare hardware.(2) the tight space between the rear of TV and bracket leaves little room for any connections that have to be rear facing. Both my optical and ethernet cables fit, but the cables had to be forced to a sharp 90° to clear where the bracket is. I’ll add pictures of both problems. Again the bracket looks and works great, the wire tuck covers are a great touch.
