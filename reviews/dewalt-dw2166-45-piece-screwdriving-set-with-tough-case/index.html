Product Reviews

I love the 5 star screw driver bits but I hate the case!

This set of drill bits and case is for me a love and a hate relationship. I own several sets of these as I have multiple DeWalt tools that I use all of the time. The bits in the kit and the other accessories are good quality and overall this kit is a great buy. I rate the bits as a 5 star item and they are well worth the price of this kit. Getting all of these bits and accessories for this price is really a good deal.The plastic case that the bits come in is touted as an industrial-strength case that is strong and durable. While the outside of the case is nice and it is a strong case, the mounting of the bits into the case is nothing but a pain. It motivated me to make this video. Ultimately you will have most of the bits and accessories in the case and not snapped into their storage positons. I cannot ever see any construction worker or contractor ever taking the time and having the patience to mess with the poor mounting design inside of this case.I rate the bits as a 5 star item and the case a 3 star item. Combined the rating is a 4 star. My little video shows how the inside mounts work or don’t work. DeWalt is a great company and I have invested a lot of money into their 18 VDC power tools and accessories. I just wish they would make a better case design. I don’t have time to play with the bits when I have a lot of work to do.

A lot of the same size.

A lot of the same size bits. I thought I was getting a bunch of screwdriver bits of different sizes and that’s not the case. Each row is one different size bit but all the same size so not the variety i was looking for.
