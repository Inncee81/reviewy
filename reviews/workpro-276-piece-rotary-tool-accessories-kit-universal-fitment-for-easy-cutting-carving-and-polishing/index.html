Good

Compared to the dremel 180 pieces this is ideal. The quality is not high but overall satisfactory. A problem I noticed right away is the sanding bands do not stay on by themselves. Of course I noticed this with dremel too…

GREAT BUY!!

Very good product. Worked well and I can’t think of any basic job this set and the dremel tool itself couldn’t get done. For the price all the tools come organized and tidy in a case. No need to buy any other brand or accessories they are in this case. On top of that it came quick and intact.

This is a great bundle with enough pieces for all your needs

This is a great bundle with enough pieces for all your needs. I mainly bought this kit for the cutting wheels but it’s nice to have the added drill bits and grinding components. The only thing really “missing” if you will that I would recommend purchasing to complement this kit is a 1⁄8” shank Carbide Rotary Burr set of your choice as those will chew through any type of metal MUCH easier when grinding.

Awesome!

This is more that I could have hoped for. This was a Christmas gift for someone and it was perfect. It was packaged great and well protected. It was just as described. All parts were in order and set was complete.

Fine for light work. Not great for heavy duty work.

Here’s the deal…for the price, this set is awesome. But I burned through ALL of the “diamond tip” bits trying to fix some tile grout around my bathtub. I went to the hardware store, picked up a brand name bit and it never even started wearing out. So, I would say that if you have a heavy duty job to do…buy the real thing. But for as many pieces as you get in this kit, it can certainly be worth it for light projects.

Works Fine

These tools work good for the price but they are definitely built at a lower quality than Dremmel, hence the lower price for a larger set of adapters. They definitely get the job done but wear a lot faster than the Dremmel made products. Good product for a good price.

Buyer Beware. You get what you pay for

This set is pretty good. The majority of the pieces are pretty solid. The cutting wheels and sand paper fall apart within 5 minutes. The rubber wheel that the sand paper sits on became lopsided after the second use. I’m not trying to sand diamonds, just 5 ply to fill a mortise pocket. I used 3 pieces of sand paper and ultimately had to hand sand the 5 ply because the wheel was messed up. I still use quite a few of the bits, but as of now close to 100 pieces of the 276 are useless.

Good set with lots of bits

Good set with lots of bits. Downside is the case is poorly constructed and was broke on arrival. The bits work yet I would not recommend for daily harsh uses.

Not dremel quality. Some of it works. Some if it is dangerous to spin up fast. I wont buy this again.
