Product Reviews

Camera case for my Kodak

I bought this case at the same time I bought my new Kodak M580 digital compact camera. The case fits the camera like a glove and provides good protection for the camera. The case has little storage, but it’s enough for a couple of storage cards for extra capacity. There’s a built in belt-clip on the case that works great. I purposely tried to knock the case from my belt to see how well the clip works and it held the camera and case firmly. Good product, great price!

Great Case

Great Product & Great Price!

I purchased two of these cases and they fit my Kodak Easy Share and Kodak point and shoot perfectly. Only reason I did not give this product 5 stars was because the inside “pocket” is really not a pocket at all. I attempted to store my spare battery but decided against it since the pocket did not allow for a secure way to store the battery; the battery fell out of the case, several times, when I opened it to retrieve the camera. Thus, I’m not quite sure what the “pocket” can be used for. The case is sturdy and protects the camera nicely.

Excellent, sturdy case

After checking for size compatibility, I bought this case for my Fujifilm T200 digital camera, and it fit snugly. The case itself is very sturdy because of its hard shell, and though I have yet to use it for any sort of picture-taking excursion, I know this case will hold up well. The pocket on the inside of the case is of a good size for spare batteries and SD cards, but there’s no closure for it (no velcro or zipper, etc.), which isn’t a problem as long as you don’t open the case upside-down.

Great little case for slim cameras!

TheEasyShare Sport C123 is a really tight fit, which made me glad I got the package deal that included a case with room for extra batteries and memory chips.This hard case does not have room for much more than a couple memory chips in addition to a slim camera. It is the perfect size for the EasyShare M590 which is only about 1⁄2” thick, and a great value for the money!
