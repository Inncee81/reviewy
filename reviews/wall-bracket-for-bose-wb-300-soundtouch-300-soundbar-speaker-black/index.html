Good way to mount your Soundbar 300 and get it …

Good way to mount your Soundbar 300 and get it off the table. Brackets are sturdy and easy to mount. I’m told they are identical to the brackets sold by the OEM (Bose), and they appear to be. There is very little space for cords behind the bracket. The Soundbar has “cutouts” (notches) where the cords connect that allows just enough space for the cords. Before purchasing, I posted a question asking if there was enough space for the cords. This seller answered as follows - “Please don’t worry, you will find there are enough space to place cords when you install”. I was able to cut holes in the wall and install the cords, but again, very little space. Five stars if the brackets were manufactured to hold the Soundbar 1⁄2” away from the wall for a little more room to work when installing cords.
