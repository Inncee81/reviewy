surprised and happy

I received this sound bar as a bundle with a new television that i recently purchased. I did not use this for my new tv because I have an existing surround system. I did however pair it up with the tv that I was replacing (only moving it to another room). The sound is very good for a sound bar. I’ve had others with squashed high hopes and really never planned on owning another. The sub woofer pairs very easily, and the wireless part is a definite plus (still needs to be plugged in to an outlet, has about a 5-6 foot cord).The sound bar itself sounds very clear and the volume is pretty amazing. The sub woofer sounds good, but won’t blow your socks off. If your intent is to use the sound bar alone, don’t bother buying it. There’s practically no bass what so ever coming out of the sound bar alone. Paired with the sub woofer though is a different story.All in all it’s a very good combo. If i ever had a need for another I wouldn’t hesitate to purchase this again.

Adds robust sound to 55” HDTV

I purchased a LG LA6900 55” smart HDTV about a year ago and have never been happy with the sound. I knew this was a shortcoming when I bought it, and otherwise, I like the TV. This sound bar makes all the difference in sound quality. It has the option of using either optical or HDMI connection. When I first started shopping, a customer service rep at the well-known blue big box store told me optical was the best connection; that HDMI splits the signal. Sounded good at the time. However, after researching the connection options, I learned that the consistent recommendation is to use the HDMI ARC connection on the TV if available. Some articles said there was little noticeable difference between optical and HDMI, but still said HDMI was preferable. I downloaded the Owner’s Manual, and it describes the optical connection first, HDMI ARC next, without offering a preference. I used the HDMI connection and didn’t test optical. However, I’m glad to have the optical option because I only have 3 HDMI connections on the TV and I may need all of them in the future.Setup is super easy, even though the instructions that come with the unit seem less than robust at first glance. The instructions are mostly pictorial, but are clear and cover various connection and setup options. The TV has a setting for an external sound bar that includes auto AV sync. I plugged in power to the wireless sub and it was immediately synced in. My TV is on a stand that has 3 open glass shelves. I put the sound bar on the middle shelf and the sub beside the stand. Wall-mount brackets are also included. The sound bar has several sound effect settings, but the only one I use is “cinema.” This setting produces rich, room-filling sound while preserving dialog clarity. The sound bar has lots of options for connecting other devices, which I haven’t explored. I bought it to add sound quality and clarity to my 55” HDTV, and it more than satisfies that reason for purchase.
