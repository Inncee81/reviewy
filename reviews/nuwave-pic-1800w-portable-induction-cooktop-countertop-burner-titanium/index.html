… this product on our very first RV trip and love it. It works as advertised and is very …

Used this product on our very first RV trip and love it. It works as advertised and is very fast. I bought cookware specifically made for induction or gas cooking because the magnet test on my cookware from home was not very reliable. This PIC Titanium model was really nice because it gave me a larger heating area than some of the older models. I also love the ability to change temperature in smaller increments. I normally cook on a gas range so the induction cooking took a little getting used to. Things heat so quickly it is easy to burn or overcook food if you are not careful. Though the cooking surface stays cools where there is no pan, there is some radiant heat around the bottom of the pan itself so be careful if touching the cooktop surface near the pan. Love the feature to take the pan off the cooking surface for a few moments to add ingredients, then resume cooking as soon as the pan touches the surface again. Highly recommend this product!

Great little alternative to a gas stove

I purchased this unit as an alternative to the crappy electric stove in our house and am very impressed. I had bought a couple of cheaper units from other manufacturers before and had been pleased with induction cooking, but the cheap ones always broke within a few months. The NuWave seems to be of much higher quality and provides quick, even and very controllable heat. It also doesn’t have the really annoying alarm that indicates your pan isn’t on the burner that all the cheaper units regrettably come with. I’ve only had it now for a month so time will tell on the reliability but at least for now it has become my absolute go to cooking device for the house. Highly recommended.
