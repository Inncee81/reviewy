Product Reviews

Not what they say they are

These previous posts with pictures are fraudulent - I know my optics well and these are definitely not 60X60 Binoculars - You need to understand that the first number 60 is the magnification which mean the object should be 60 times closer than what you see with the naked eye. These binoculars optics do not provide 60 magnification.

Does not work as advertised

I got these for my husband for his birthday, but I ended up returning them because there does not appear to be any “night vision.” I understand that these are not true night vision binoculars, but the description states, “…this is LLL night vision, not see at dark environment like day but can make you see clearly when at light night and glimmer conditions…” I tested them, both in a darkish, but not completely dark, room and later outside at twilight. I used a normal pair of binoculars as well for comparison. When I could not see any difference in low light vision between this pair and the regular pair of binoculars, I broke the secret and told my husband what I’d gotten him - at this point, I wasn’t sure if I was using them correctly, and did not want to be unfair to the Zoostliss company. I am not very technically skilled, but my husband knows how to use such gadgets. He tried it out on a moonlit night with ambient light from neighboring houses - certainly not pitch dark. Not only could he not see “clearly,” he could not see anything at all… unfortunately, because of this experience, I can not recommend this product.
