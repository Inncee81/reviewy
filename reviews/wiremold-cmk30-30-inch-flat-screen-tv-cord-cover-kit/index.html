Great, simple, easy, but missing some parts?

This wiremold is a terrific option if you don’t want to deal with trying to get your cables inside the wall or hiring an electrician to install and electrical outlet behind your TV (note: electrical code does not allow you to run the TVs powercord behind the drywall). I’m a professional AV integrator and I’ve used this product in the past when running cable in the wall wasn’t an option. It installs very simply. After mounting the TV you can just put it under the TV and use a level to make sure its perfectly straight, use a pencil to mark out the screw hole positions, then install some drywall anchors (or screws if you are over a wood stud), screw the bottom piece of the wiremold down, put the cables in position, then snap the cover piece into place. Takes about 10 minutes. Also, the wiremold is paintable and since the edges are rounded, once you paint it it does a better job of blending in than other wiremold that is more rectangular in shape. The reason I docked one star is because even though in the picture it shows the wiremold coming with drywall anchors, my unit did not come with any. Luckily I have a ton of those things lying around, but if you don’t you might need to make a run to the hardware store.
