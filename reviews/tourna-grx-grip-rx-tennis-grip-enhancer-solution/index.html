Helps the grip

I constantly have problems with my hands becoming too sweaty when I play and this stuff does a good job drying off my hands and keeping them dry for a decent period of time. If I am getting very sweaty, it still lasts about a set. Otherwise it’ll last between 1.5-3 hours.I don’t like how messy this product can be, it basically coats your hands with chalk after it dries which can get on your clothes, sunglasses, etc. but I would rather have that than wet hands while I play.All in all I would recommend this product and have shared it with many people in my play groups who also have enjoyed its benefits.

Have not had a problem with the nozzle clogging as another reviewer noted, but I’m careful to immediately close the top when I use it.
