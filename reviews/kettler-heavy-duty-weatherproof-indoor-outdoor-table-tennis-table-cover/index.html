    Product Description

Protect and extend the life of your Kettler Table Tennis Table with Kettler Heavy-Duty Outdoor Table Tennis Cover. Kettler’s cover is UV Resistant with specially engineered weatherproof backing to provide a barrier against rain, snow, dirt, sun, mould and mildew. It includes features like tough reinforced corners, built-in air vents on the top of the cover and easy Velcro side openings. It’s built to last and is covered by a two-year warranty for residential use.

Product Reviews

Perfect for outdoor storage of outdoor tables!

I purchased this cover for my Kettler outdoor table because I cannot keep my garage clean to the point I can store a table tennis table consistently.Initial Thoughts:Initial appearances were good, and I could tell the cover was of very high quality - it just has that feel of quality. The thickness of the material makes it very easy to slide onto the table without worrying about snags and tears. There is a vent seam near the top that is covered by several inches of material to prevent water from entering the vent.Installation:I am a 5’11 man, and I find that installation is very easy and straight forward. You simply open the velcro seam and start sliding the cover across the table. You make a few fit adjustments and then close the velcro seam. My wife is 5’4, and she can install the cover with ease as well. The only thing I will knock here is that the velcro cover does not have quite enough velcro. A few more inches on each part would give it a much tighter seal on that side.Removal:Removing the cover is as simple as the installation - just release the velcro seam and pull the cover off from the other side. The cover is thick and sturdy enough that it comes off cleanly wihtout snags.Does it work?I have had this cover on my table since the day I bought it in June 2014. I keep my table up against my garage door, with the vent side towards the garage door. It is exposed to direct sunlight from the East in the morning. It has seen 90+ degree direct sunlight days, many severe thunderstorms, wind, pollen, and now freezing weather. It protects my table 100% from all weather, and the cover has not blown off or even been dislodged in the slightest. There is no discoloration or embrittlement of the cover.Bottom Line:This cover is an absolute MUST if you have to store your table outside. Considering the sales price of my table, this cover is about as sound of an investment as one can make. The quality is every bit as good as the table it protects (also a Kettler product). There simply is no comparison between a product as this and some generic product you would find at the local Wal-Mart. The only flaw I can point out is the velcro closure should be more stout. I did not take off a star for it because it is just my opinion that it needs more. It has proven to be more than effective through 5 months of constant service and never coming apart or letting water in.READ MORE

Sturdy, good quality material, but needs more velcro on seam

I can only give this 4 stars because the seam down the edge needs a little more Velcro. The gaps between the 1 inch Velcro tabs are too far apart and the cover blows open in high wind and lets sideways blowing rain in through the seam. I bought more industrial strength Velcro tabs at my home center and added them to the edge and fixed the problem myself. But it was a trip to the store and labor to make up for the products lack of a few more Velcro tabs to make this product excellent. Still the best one on the market for the price that I could find, so I would recommend it, just find a good quality Velcro to order at the same time if you live in a windy, wet area like I do.
