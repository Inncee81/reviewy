    Product Description

The revolutionary training aid players, parents and coaches are using to give themselves an edge. Over 90% of the game is now played with topspin because it is the only way to control the ball with pace. Mastering it unlocks an exponentially better level of tennis. The TopspinPro combines both the kinaesthetic and visual elements essential to accelerated learning: 1. it lets you feel what it’s like to brush the ball, not hit it, and 2. the mesh screen is a visual guide that ensures the player keeps his racket face angle constant through the contact zone.

The player’s objective is to make the ball spin without touching the screen. The angle of the screen reflects the angle of the racket face of pro’s when they make contact with the ball. By swinging with the racket face parallel to the screen, players will execute the correct stroke.

A couple of minutes a day helps lock in the essential ‘new’ muscle memory when changing a stroke habit.
