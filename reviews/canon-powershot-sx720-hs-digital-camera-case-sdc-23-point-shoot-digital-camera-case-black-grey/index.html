Great case for cannon SX720!

Excellent case for my canon SX720. Fits great. Padded. Love the material. Nice strap. Love that it has two zippers because I like to open and close from the middle. The only thing it’s missing to make it the perfect case is a smaller zipper pouch on front for charger, extra battery, cleaning cloth, sdcard. It has a small elastic space inside for SD card. This will be my go to case for around home and daily life taking pictures of my kids etc, but I am now going to purchase a seperate case that has a smaller zipper pouch for the extra accessories I would need when I go on vacation or for longer outings when I may need additional battery or charger. Only drawback to that other case I’m buying is it only has one zipper so I can’t open and shut it the way I like. I don’t think they make a perfect case, but I really like this one and it earns 5 stars. I bought it because it was highly recommended for my camera and I knew it didn’t have separate storage pouch so I can’t take off for that. Highly recommend if you don’t care about second smaller pouch.

Perfect for the Canon SX730

Perfect case for the Canon SX730… Tried three others–were either way too big or too small even though the listed measurements should’ve worked… Initially did not order this one because it is so, well, … unattractive… But you know–it’s perfect… fits the camera exactly… enough room to tuck in a battery or a spare card (although put ‘em in a baggie in the bottom–the ‘net’ pocket doesn’t close and is too shallow to keep things from falling out). Enough room to slip the camera in and out easily. Good zippers. Thick padding but not bulky. Strap is nice and sturdy… camera seems well protected… took off the label and it’s only slightly more appealing.. but you know… it’s perfect… and I looked at way too many ‘attractive’ cases that didn’t work. Job done! Thank you Amazon.
