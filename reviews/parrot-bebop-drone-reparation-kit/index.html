Hey folks, my order came with all 4 cages …

Hey folks, my order came with all 4 cages…NOT TWO! So, if they are SEALED and packaged from Bebop that way and you only receive two, then you got ripped off. Bear in mind, that these cages do not come with the e-clip retainer, so be sure NOT to lose your original e-clips. I nearly lost a few of mine. Also, the motor screws are separate. Go ahead and order you a screw set for your Bebop Drone in case you lose a few. My drone basically had 3 motor screws missing, so I replaced them with that other kit I bought.Now when I started replacing my motor cages, I noticed the factory did not have a lot of glue keeping them secure and separated. One of my factory cage magnets was touching another one, and it caused tremendous vibration on the drone. These new motor cages came with plenty of glue in between the magnets. I give this a 5 star, as it is EXACTLY what I needed.
