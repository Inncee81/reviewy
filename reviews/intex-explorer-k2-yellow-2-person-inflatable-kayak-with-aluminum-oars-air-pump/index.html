Product Reviews

This kayak is pretty great for the price

This kayak is pretty great for the price. I took it to West Virginia and did class 3 rapids with it.. hit rocks, hit huge rapids, went airborne. This kayak was more durable than my hardshell kayaks that I took on the same trip. The paddles are not that great, simply because they aren’t long enough. if you are planning on short distances they should work fine, but if you are trying to do a 10 mile stretch of river you should get some better and longer paddles. The pump is plastic and appears frail but has stood up to all the use I have given it. There is also a water drain plugin the back which is pretty great as well, especially if you fill with water from rapids. If you have a tall person and a shorter person the seating is fine. If you have two tall people in the kayak you may get a bit cramped. Overall I was super impressed and will be taking it down more class 3’s in next summer.
