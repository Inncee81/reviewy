First things first - I bought this kit pretty much exclusively because it includes a USB charger

First things first - I bought this kit pretty much exclusively because it includes a USB charger, and a Micro USB cable WITH a switchI ran a stress test on the Raspberry Pi 3, and the included power supply can’t even keep up without any peripherals. There is NO way this is an actual 2.5 amp supply, but I don’t have a USB power meter to see what the actual power draw is.I don’t have any stability issues, but if I stress the RPI, the red power LED blinks intermittently which indicates a power delivery issue. I have zero problems with my Samsung 2 Amp rated power supply, even with a USB Mouse/Keyboard, Digital-to-Analog converter, and I even plugged in a USB fan to draw as much power as I could.The power supply is garbage - but the case is standard (meaning not great but more than adequate), and the heatsinks actually have good adhesive on them.All-in-all, I think it is a good value but the power supply is really sub-par. If the distributer found a better quality power supply - I would wager that these would FLY off the shelf.As it stands I do recommend that you either buy this kit and substitute it with a good quality USB charger, or just peice your own kit. I threw the included USB power supply in the trash, but the cable, heatsink, are all worthwhile.
