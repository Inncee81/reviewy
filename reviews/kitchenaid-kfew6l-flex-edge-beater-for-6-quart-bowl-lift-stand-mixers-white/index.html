Product Specifications

  • Mix, beat, and whip up your favourite treats with this flex edge beater; features a flexible sweeper that is made from silicone and is used to insure that no batter goes unturned
  • Coated metal construction with silicone flex edge, top rack dishwasher safe
  • For use with 6 quart wide mouth bowl lift stand mixers

  • Fits models KD2661, KL26M, KP26M1, KP26M8, KP26N9, KV25G, KV25M

    Product Description

Reduce mixing time and the need to scrape ingredients down the side of the bowl when you’re mixing with this beater, which features a flexible edge that thoroughly scrapes ingredients into the bowl. Fits models KD2661, KL26M, KP26M1, KP26M8, KP26N9, KV25G, KV25M.
