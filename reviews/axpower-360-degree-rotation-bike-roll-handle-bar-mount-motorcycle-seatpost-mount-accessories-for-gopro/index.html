Cheap, easy to use mount that is great for cycling on the beach!

Tested with my GoPro Hero 5 Black and it worked well with Image Stabilization on. The video footage was a little shaky on bumpy roads, but for paved bike paths, the footage wasn’t nauseating and it worked well. Without Image Stabilization however, the vibration from the handlebars and bumpiness from dirt paths made the footage unusable and I opted to use my gimbal instead.The mount was easy to install and the ball-rotating base made it simple to adjust angles on the fly. I rode tandem with my wife at the beach and we got some great ‘selfie’ footage along with some scenic shots of the ocean as we rode along the coast.I have only used it twice so far but will update again as I use it more. One thing to note - I have noticed other users complaining the neck of the rotating part of the mount broke off. If you don’t completely loosen the locking mechanism that tightens the mount before you adjust it, you are straining the ‘neck’ of the mount, shortening it’s lifespan. Despite traveling on a dirt path, riding several miles on a paved bikeway, and supporting the heavier GP5, I have not had any issues thus far.
