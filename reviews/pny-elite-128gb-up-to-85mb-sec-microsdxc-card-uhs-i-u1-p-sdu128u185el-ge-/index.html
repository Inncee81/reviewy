More counterfeit flash memory from Amazon

Another counterfeit item from Amazon? I have a 50% failure rate on getting items that are obvious fakes. This card was sealed in the cardboard packaging, but cutting it open shows the plastic sleeve holding the card is sealed with a piece of scotch tape. The card itself barely gets 1⁄2 the rated rated speed on USB 3.0 ports with the right adapter. Other flash memory works correctly at rated speeds.Be careful and check your memory, commingled inventory means that Amazon can have fakes.

Excellent read speeds, write speeds ~28MB/s in the 128GB version.

I tested the 128gb capacity version of the PNY Elite microSDXC card. It provided read speeds above 90MB/s in benchmark tests in several USB 3.0 card readers. Sequential write speed was about 28MB/s. The card is rated UHS Speed Class 1 (U1) so it will maintain at least 10MB/s continuous write for video recording. Did not last long at all

I’ve factory reset my tablet via fastboot thinking that the tablet may be blamed but again no luck. I’m amazed that this product locked up in less than two months. Once the sd card was inserted into my tablet, I did not remove it once. Very disappointed.
