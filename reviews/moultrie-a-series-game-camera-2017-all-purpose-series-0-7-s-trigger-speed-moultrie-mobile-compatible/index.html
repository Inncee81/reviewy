Great wildlife/security camera

Great camera. We have 3 of these around the house after someone stole a propane tank from our property. I check them every few days now because the wildlife is amazing! I like the moon phase added to the camera. Had a full moon puma show up at 3:33 (Santa Cruz, CA area)! We also have a regular grey fox, and now I know it is him, and not my cat and another cat having a turf war leaving presents on my door mat and the driveway. Great cameras. Easy to use and set up. I wish that they didn’t have to be so far back to get the frame needed, but I think I’m just used to a nice pancake wide angle lens on my handheld camera. It took a couple mounting adjustments to get it in the right place but happy with the quality. You will see the red IR lights come on if you’re outside looking at it, at night when it is triggered. You tend to not see it unless you are looking right at it or near it. Animals don’t mind it seems, but if being stealthy from people is your goal you might want a “no-glow” version. Sometimes I get shots with nothing in the frame, and I think the animal has passed through already (a couple occasions of a tail or leg barely in the frame), so sometimes wish the reaction time was a little faster, but could probably fix with changing where the camera is mounted. I plan on getting 2-3 more to study the wildlife around the property.
