A great rocker for your child to grow into. What Is The Fisher-Price Infant-to-Toddler Rocker? The Fisher-Price Infant-to-Toddler Rocker in this sweet bunny print is just right for growing babies. It’s a reclining infant seat with a fold-out kickstand that makes it great for feeding. Adjust the seat upright so baby’s movements create a rocking motion, while stimulating toys and music are there for baby to enjoy. For naptime, you can remove the toy bar and switch on calming vibrations. And as your child grows, this product becomes a toddler rocking chair for children up to 40 lbs. The seat pad is machine washable and dryer safe.

Features: Use from infant to toddler - up to 40 lbs (18 kg) Fold-out kickstand & reclining seat for feeding or napping Bat-at toys & musical butterfly stimulate baby’s senses Deep cradle seat with calming vibrations Toybar easily removes for toddlers to sit & play Removable, machine washable pad

The toybar features 3 fun, spinning toys. The Rocker Grows With Baby! With easy conversion from a stationary seat to a toddler rocker, the Fisher-Price Infant-to-Toddler Rocker is perfect for growing babies! The rocker features a removable machine washable pad, a secure, three-point restraint, a lightweight toybar and more.

The Fisher-Price Infant-to-Toddler Rocker features bat-at toys and a musical butterfly to stimulate baby’s senses and curiosity. The deep cradle seat features calming vibrations to comfort and sooth younger babies, providing a sense of security and happiness.

A calming seat offers soothing vibrations. The Fun Never Ends! The toybar features 3 fun, spinning toys (a butterfly with pull-down musical heart, and two colorful flip paddle flowers) and easily removes for toddlers to sit and play.

The fold-out kickstand and reclining seat make it ideal for feeding or napping.
