    Product Description

Take some time to cool off with this remote controlled reversible twin window fan. The convenient fan is equipped with independently controlled motors, three whisper-quiet speeds, full-function remote, 2 removable covers, and 2 snap-on feet. The fabric cover attaches to the front of the fan when it is not in use to keep out hot and humid air. The bug screen attaches to the back of the fan to keep pests out. Expander panels allow for a safe, custom fit. The cool feature has both fans working to bring fresh new air to your room; exhaust function removes hot sticky air from your room; circulate function will have one fan pull hot sticky air out while the other fan brings cool fresh air in. Fan measures 23 1/2” W by 12” H and fits windows measuring 23 1⁄2” to 37” wide. For even more customization, use included removable snap-on feet to put the fan on a table or the floor.

Product Reviews

Works well, remote is simple to use, and the fans reverse without having to turn the fan around

The fan actually is a reversible fan: the blades change direction without having to move the fan, itself, and you can either set it to both fans intaking, exhausting, or ‘circulate’ (where the left fan intakes and the right fan exhausts). There isn’t a button for each setting: you cycle through the 3 options, which is very easy and fast. The fan speed option works the same to change from low to medium to high (and then back to low if you press the button again). You literally only have to press the selection button once or twice to get from the fan speed you’re on to the one you want; it’s fast and easy.The previous fan I had didn’t have a remote control, so this one having one was a pleasant upgrade. It uses 2 AA batteries and is simple and intuitive: it only has 3 buttons: power, fan speed, and fan direction. You push the speed or direction button once or twice to get to the selection you want; there aren’t separate buttons for each selection, which is why it’s so simple to use.The fans chirps/beeps when a selection is changed, which was a little startling at first, but then it was no big deal. The selection also has an LED under each selection on the fan itself (green for speed and red for direction) so you can look at the fan and tell that it’s currently on medium speed and exhaust, for example.It moves air pretty well, so I have no complaints there, either.***ADDITIONAL INFORMATION:It’s been a month, and the fan is still working great. It’s cooling a second floor room that’s about 11x11 feet, and you can feel in intake breeze at the doorway opposite where the fan is. I’ve had it running pretty much continuously since I got it, and have changed from intake to exhaust to circulate on a regular basis, and it still works like it did when I first got it.I would again recommend this fan: it does what it says it will do, and the remote control is especially useful since I don’t have to walk over to the fan to change anything.
