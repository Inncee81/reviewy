Jump-N-Carry

The “Carry” part of Jump-N-Carry is slightly misleading because this beast is heavy! I am a big guy standing 6’4” at 240 pounds and carrying this thing around is a workout. I leave it in my car’s trunk and I have used this so many times since December 2009 when I bought it that I have lost count.I originally purchased this for emergency use in case my battery dies or a tire goes flat. For that purpose, I have used it many times, both to jump-start my car when the battery died and to fill my car and motorcycle tires with air when they start to get low. Just having it available for these tasks makes it completely worth what I had paid for it. However, by far the most number of times I have used this is to help a stranger in need.Whenever I drive by and see someone staring dumbly at their car with the hood up, I stop and ask if they need any help. More often than not, they just need a jump-start. Out comes my Jump-N-Carry and within just a few seconds, their vehicle is running again. It is as simple as placing it near their vehicle, putting the red cable on their positive connector, black cable on their negative connector and telling them to start the vehicle. Every time, the vehicle had started instantly–no hesitation. I once had to use the jump-starter three times in a single day without needing to charge it. I am very impressed with its performance.There are smaller and lighter ones out there, but if you need something you can depend on, this beast of a jump-starter will not disappoint you.
