Compact Camera Case

This is a very nice hard-shell case for a compact digital camera. (It will only fit a small point-and-shoot, not a DSLR.)First of all, it’s a lovely shade of purple! Very bright and cheerful-looking. It has a double zipper, which is a little annoying, because it takes longer to unzip both sides, but the zipper itself has large pulls, which are easy to use. It includes a narrow strap, which seems a bit too long for a wrist-strap, and a bit too short for a neck-strap. Not sure how you’re supposed to use it, honestly. I certainly wouldn’t want to wear this case around my neck—it’s hard as a rock!Be aware that this is a very small case. My camera is a brand-new Samsung WB35F, which is the smallest digital camera I’ve ever owned. Yet, there must be smaller cameras out there, because it just barely fits in the case. I have to work the zipper to get it closed.All in all, I’m pleased with this case. I wanted something to protect my new camera, and I wanted it to be purple—this fits the bill perfectly! Four stars and recommended for anyone who has a compact digital camera that needs protecting.
