Disappointing

My husband likes this replacement brush, but I do not. The brush has different lengths in several areas. It actually hurt my gums. I had to cut it down to make it completely level. It is also a very large brush. My original brush that came with my Sonicare system was small and round. I prefer the original shape.

Great price for replacement brushes!

The price is so low compared to the replacement heads purchased at a retail store! The only negative is that they are longer than the originals and have longer edges on the top and bottom. These edges are good at getting at the outside edges of molars, but take some getting used to because of their shape. I do like that I am getting a good, clean brushing as my teeth feel like I’ve just had them cleaned at the dentist.

Four Stars

We love our sonicare and will only use replacement heads from Sonicare. So far its been good.

Good enough for a toothbrush

I’ve used a couple of these already and have no complaints. They seem like they should work without any problem and they cost quite a bit less than most other places. First one I used seemed to become dislodged from my Soicare after just about a month of use but I haven’t had the same problem since.

Prefer these over some of the newer, enhanced versions

These are the “standard” head for the Sonicare elite. I tried a few of the newer versions that claim to do great things but they all felt much rougher and I didn’t like them. Sticking with these from now on.

Long, stray bristle strands

Maybe I got a bad batch? These have stray bristle strands that were not cut and are longer than the rest. You wouldn’t think one or two longer strands would be an issue, but I could feel it when I brushed. Otherwise they’re okay.
