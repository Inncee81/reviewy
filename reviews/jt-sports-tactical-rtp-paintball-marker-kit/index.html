Product Reviews

This is a great buy, for the price

This is a great buy, for the price. It comes with all that you need to start to play, but you will need some more equipment before you go to the actual paintball parks. The gun is fairly accurate, but don’t expect to be sniping from across the map. The aluminum body makes it durable and yet, still somewhat light. The barrel is able to be removed, in case of a jam or paint is stuck inside, and the cleaning cord is good enough to be able to clear blockages. The mask is nothing special, but it does what is intended.What it doesn’t come with that you NEED, is a refillable tank. The one that comes with it is pretty much useless. It will last one round, maximum, and it cannot be refilled. What you will need is a compressed air tank with at least 3000 psi, or a CO2 tank, but the compressed air tanks are better for the gun and perform better in the cold then the CO2. The paintballs that come with the gun are pretty much only for practice, since you will definitely need far more for a game.

Quality gun for extremely low cost.

I got this gun because it was a cheaper alternative than going into the store, and it exceeded my expectations. I have heard it is slightly heavier, but it was the perfect weight for me. It was light enough to were I could hold it with one hand and shoot straight ahead, but also heavy enough to keep a good grip on it while running. My friend bought a Tippman rifle for $180 at a Scheels sporting goods store and his gun was about 2-3 pounds lighter than mine, but he paid way more than me. The mask is a nice fit, the padding is a bit scratchy, but you don’t feel it when you get it wet. The only bad thing I could say was that my 90 oz C02 tank did not work. It shot like two shots and then the C02 would not fill up the gun anymore. It is no biggie since you will have to buy another tank since the one they send you IS NOT REFILLABLE (in case you did not know). All and all, great gun, great accuracy, and you look super cool and intimidating to the first time players.
