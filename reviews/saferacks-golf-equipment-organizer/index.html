The little hooks they provide to hang towels / gloves are awesome for drying them out

This organizer is exactly what I was hoping for. It fits two sets of clubs, no problem. Multiple shelves to store shoes, balls, towels make organizing a breeze. The little hooks they provide to hang towels / gloves are awesome for drying them out, or for easy access. It looks great, moves around easily on the wheels and I would recommend to anyone.The only complaint I have is with this ‘style’ of storage unit itself. I don’t like the plastic ‘sleeves’ that hold the shelves in place. No matter how well you have them aligned they will move when you attempt to secure the shelf.
