Solid For Everyday Use

This is a really solid point and shoot video camera. Like most people, I usually use my phone for video and photos. But sometimes, that just doesn’t cut it. Sure the sensors in my phone might be pretty good, but sometimes I want to save my battery or use my phone while doing other things. This camera is a nice price point and allows me to do some of those things.When you open it up, you get a camera and a USB cable. There is no hot shoe for an external shotgun mic, so you will be stuck with the microphone built into the camera.The video itself is pretty darn good. Shoots at 1080p and that is just about what I would expect. The screen is touch screen and makes going through the menus a breeze. Then the buttons are all self explanatory, and there is even a flash for outdoor night shoots. The screen rotates so you can make it a ‘vlog cam’ or a ‘selfie cam’ if you desire, or if you are shooting videos of unboxings.Like I said the resolution is pretty good. The thing is the contrast and a few exposure settings are a bit wonky, so do mess around with those a bit to get them set up properly. But after about 10 minutes I got everything set just how I like it so it was not an issue.

Works flawlessly

I wanted to buy this kind of product to record my two son’s daily life! The first impressions when opening the LCD / viewfinder is that camcorder looks well made. The camera is light to hold at weighing only 320g measuring approx 13.5cm x 7.5cm x 7cm. The camcorder contained in a small pouch for protection. Also inside the box with a USB cable, AV cable, HDMI to HDMI cable, separate battery, small remote control, lens cleaning cloth, mini CD containing PC software for operating the camcorder and an English user manual.The front of the camcorder has the wide angle lens surrounded by a nice bezel. Camera has nice strap on the right side for comfortable handling. Screen cab be flipped 180 degree to face it front. This is very useful while shooting selfies or self videos. This includes a 5 megapixel full HD CMOS image sensor with 1920x1080 resolution. The specification is stated as 24 megapixel image sensor and from testing the image quality this seems very nice indeed. Every settings are very easy.The quality of videos and pictures are pretty decent given the price. The only thing to remember is you have to buy the SD card at the same time! To start with, this camcorder is very portable and lightweight. It was easy to set up everything as batteries came pre-charged.I don’t think this would fit those who are looking for high-end professional shooting! For this price you can’t go wrong.
