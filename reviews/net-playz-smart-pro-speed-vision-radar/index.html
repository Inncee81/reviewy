Couldn’t get past the poor radar performance

Returned. Regardless of angles and distances placed per the instructions, it only picked up readings periodically. When it did, the readings were a bit random. I am assuming I may have just had a defect. I bought this one because a friend of mine has the version without the video recording features. His seems to work reliably and accurately. I sat this one next to his during a bullpen session and found the one I bought providing infrequent and inconsistent readings. Never did get to use the camera or wifi features. Couldn’t get past the poor radar performance. The product concept on this one is good. They may have gone a little cheap on the design, component selections or possibly the manufacturing process/supplier. Bummer.

Not ideal for evaluating a baseball/softball bat swing

I really, really wanted this to work. I bought it to videotape softball batting and clock the speeds the ball is coming off the bat. When placed in a low position in front of the batter (ideal for videotaping), the radar only measured the speed of the ball coming from the pitcher from behind going over it. When positioned behind the batter, we got readings that were a little better but still not that consistent (but the camera view was useless). When placed on the side of the batter slightly in front (decent video angle), it failed to record more than half of the hits. I think this would work great for pitching or behind a soccer goal, but not for evaluating a bat swing.
