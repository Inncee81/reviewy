All have worked great. The selfie stick is kinda (meh) as the …

I spent over an hour deciding which accessory kit I should purchase for my new (to me) Hero 4 Session. My one requirement was that the kit have the float/bobber handle. I chose this one in the end because of the case to hold everything. And while it does fit everything, they are in there kind of messy. That aside, this kit had all the accessories I need as a casual GoPro user and more. Besides the the floating attachment, I’ve used the selfie stick with tripod mount, the helmet attachment, the suction cup, the clamp, and the head mount. All have worked great. The selfie stick is kinda (meh) as the weight of the camera causes it to spin around, especially when extended all the way. I can tell the plastic mounts on the items in this kit is a little cheaper quality than the actual GoPro accessories, but nothing has broken or caused issues. That’s what you get when you pay less, so it was expected. I would highly recommend this kit to a new, casual GoPro user so you can start using your GoPro as many ways as you can! You can always upgrade to nicer quality attachments if you need to. But these were more than sufficient for me!
