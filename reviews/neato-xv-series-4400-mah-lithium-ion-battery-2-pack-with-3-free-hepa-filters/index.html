Replacement Lithium 4400mAh Batteries for Neato XV Series

I have previously purchased a set of lithium replacement batteries for my Neato XV Signature that were identical in every way to these with the minor exception of having a different name on the label. All of the 4400mAh batteries currently available for the XV series here on Amazon were produced by the same company; the only difference is a dollar or two in the price. I wrote an extensive review of my original ones so I won’t repeat myself here, but suffice to say that I love them and they work perfectly in my Neato while managing to fix every issue I had, including navigation issues, vision issues, and short lifespan between charges.Regardless, it goes without saying that the weak spot in the Neato XV series robotic vacuums is the stock batteries. The original nickel batteries had poor capacity and even worse voltage regulation - all now in the past with these replacements. The price for the XV series bots is now going low enough (thanks to more current models being introduced) that if you want to buy a great robot and prevent many issues that have plagued these vacuums right from the start (or want to breathe new life into your older unit) you certainly can’t go wrong with lithium. I purchased a factory refurbished XV-21 for my sister recently and before I even gave it to her I installed these batteries and it works absolutely like new. Since you’re going to have to replace the stock nickel batteries every 6-12 months anyways, why not just go ahead and spend about twice the amount and get a battery that will last FOUR TIMES longer and prevent (or cure) many problems from the get-go.These lithium batteries truly make the Neato XV-series bots live up to their potential. Strongly recommended.
