Product Reviews

This is a great racquet for new players

This is a great racquet for new players. It’s a good weight and size, pretty forgiving and holds up to tough games. It did start to lose paint on the rims and on the strings pretty quickly, but for the price that’s expected. The only weird thing to notice is that at the bottom of the face the strings don’t go into the rim as normal, they go around it, which means the face isn’t actually flat at the bottom, it’s angled. So if you hit a shot inside of the sweet spot it could angle strangely.
