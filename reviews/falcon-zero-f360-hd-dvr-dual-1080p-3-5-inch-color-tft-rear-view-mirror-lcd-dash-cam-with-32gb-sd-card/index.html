Great Dash Cam, Looks Like it’s Part of the Car from the Factory. So Far, So Good!

I have had the Falcon Zero F360+ for about a week now and I am impressed and completely satisfied with it. I was a bit worried with near-perfect reviews and I also noticed that many of the reviews were not for this product, but for a USB power adapter from the same company which made me wonder if these were legit to start with. I have had several dash cams and this one is the one that I am keeping.I was concerned that it would not fit my Mercedes E550C mirror because of the mirror’s thickness (contains HomeLink buttons and has the factory camera behind the mirror for the adaptive cruise control and emergency braking system). The Falcon Zero actually fit right over the mirror with no problem and looks stock to most people that are in the car. I wired the system behind the trim and molding and it only took about 20 minutes and no wires are visible.I like that you can set the video display to shut off after a number of seconds so that it does not distract you while driving but can bring it up at any time with the press of a button. This dashcam can take HD Video as well as still pictures and has instructions that are easy to follow to personalize the camera. I also like that you can use one or both of the cameras depending on what you are using it for.The only “complaint” that I have is that the rear-facing camera has to look through the entire car before looking out the back window so the rear traffic video is not a wide as the front. I knew this when I bought it though and didn’t want to have to wire a camera to sit in the back window deck so this works as good as it can without doing that. The cameras can be easily turned without turning the rear view mirror if you need to video out either the driver or passenger mirror.This would be the perfect option for an Uber driver or cab service, I bought because I seem to be a magnet for road rage and a dash cam has saved me on more than one occasion (kids trying to race me, doing burnouts, etc and when I was pulled over as being a participant I simply played video back and showed that I in fact was not participating). I find it numerous that when angry people see you have a camera they de-escalate quickly.I purchased this on a lightning deal from Black Friday but I think that it’s worth every penny at the regular price. I will update in the future if I have any problems to report.
