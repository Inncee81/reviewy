Cheap Cheap Cheap

Very Cheaply made. Should cost $10 or $15 at most. The front flap is like construction paper and it does not fit right. The projector sticks up out of the top of the bag by an inch or so. I returned it (my first return to Amazon ever).

Pretty good, but not the best quality or design for an OEM product

This is fine, and does the job, but it’s kind of cheap, and not quite large enough. That is, the flap should really be about 2-3 inches longer so it can securely cover what’s inside the case when you have all the cables and remote inside.

A pricey totebag

This bag is flimsy and cheap, similar to the swag you get for free at trade shows. While it does fit the projector like a glove, it offers minimal protective padding. The accessory pocket can barely contain the cables and remote and a single strip of velcro strains to hold it all together.The projector itself is a good buy, but for five bucks more they could have included such a minimal case.

Deplorable Quality

Horrible, Horrible, Horrible! The quality of this bag is deplorable. The flap and front pocket material feels thinner than single-ply toilet paper. While the Epson VS345 fits snugly in the case, NO accessories (power cord, computer cable, or remote) will fit. I am embarrassed that Epson put their name on such a poor quality bag. I initiated my return within about 30 minutes of receiving this product.

Not worth the money! After you put the projector …

Not worth the money! After you put the projector in there is very little room for all the cord and connections.Should have just come with the projector. I think it’s Epson’s way of making extra bucks.
