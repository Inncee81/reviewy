Really great refurbished monitor from a reputable company.

I wanted a second monitor primarily for keeping my chat clients open while gaming on the other screen, and this served its purpose very well. After updating my graphic card drivers, the native resolution of the monitor was adjusted to 1366 x 768.The monitor appears to work like brand-new for a refurbished product, and there is no noticeable decrease in quality from the monitor I use for gaming. I cannot say much for things like display lag, but for regular, everyday computing, there shouldn’t be any problems.The monitor also comes with a VGA cord and stand. The stand is not height or angle adjustable (it “snaps” into place), but it’s VESA mount compatible.Overall, I really recommend this for people looking for a monitor without the need for any fancy adjustments or low display lag. I had and have high expectations for Acer and I trust that this monitor will keep working for me in the long run.

Great reliable monitor

I bought this monitor for school in order to have a duel screen with my macbook. This is a great and reliable monitor and it is very easy to adjust the picture size and quality. The previous reviews regarding the adjustability of the stand is over exaggerated. You can just hold the top of the monitor with one hand and the bottom with the other other hand to tilt it down. It’s not too difficult to handle the monitor. The size is perfect as well as I use it to watch movies and tv shows in my room. I don’t have a tv in my room and it’s a good inexpensive replacement for a new tv. The only negative comment I have is that whenever I unplug the chord between my macbook and the monitor to disable the duel screen capability, the colors are off. All the colors seem to go towards a gray color. I have to restart my computer to go back to the original colors. This is why I only give it 4 stars. Besides that, the color (when working properly) is great, size isn’t too big and is great on a desk. This is a great buy.
