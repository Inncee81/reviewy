    Product Description

Heavy duty anti-tip steel cable straps from VIVO. STAND-SK01 is especially useful for flat screens within arm’s reach or in areas of high traffic and is an essential tool in preventing tip-overs causing accidents and injuries. The dual steel cables work with any flat panel TV up to 65”. The kit attaches your TV directly to wood stud walls, concrete walls, brick walls, or the entertainment centers underneath the TV, and the cables are adjustable using the tensioning clamp. All of this is done with the provided mounting kit which includes all necessary hardware and screws required for assembly.

Nice, simple design

Nice, simple design. Easy enough to install on my wooden media stand using a power screwdriver. The cable seems VERY thin, but it’s to keep something from tipping and is not holding up the TV so I suppose it’s enough, and the cable is coated to keep it from marring your TV or stand. Main complaint is that the included hex key is really cheap - rounded off while tightening the little grub screws that anchor the cable.

Good kit, but weak screws

I just moved a large TV to a bedroom, and put it on a dresser. This TV is a little older, and it’s fairly front heavy with an enormous glass bezel around the screen.I got this kit to secure the tv to the dresser, so I know it won’t tip. For that, it works well. There was plenty of cable for my 46 inch TV. I imagine the cable could probably handle 60 inches of TV.The one problem I had was when I was attaching the anchors to the dresser, one of the screws sheeted off. So I had to reposition and try again. For something that needs to hold up weight, strong screws is a must.But still, with the new, better, screws in place, this gives a nice secure fit for the TV.
