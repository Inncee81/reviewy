Wilson Electronics AC/DC 6 V Power Supply for Wilson 801201, 801230, and 801245 Series Boosters

by people ...

  • For In-building Wireless Smart Technology Amps
  • Converts 120v Ac To 6v Dc
  • Plugs Into Standard Wall Outlet
  • 5.9ft Cable

This Wilson Electronic power supply for use of Wilson 801201, 801230, and 801245 is used for in-building wireless smart technology amps for charging up the products. This Wilson Electronic power supply converts 120v Ac To 6v Dc. This power supply plugs into a standard wall outlet for easy use while it has an 1.8-M cable attached to it.
