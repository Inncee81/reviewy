    Product Description

This camera case is for those occasions when you are not willing to be burdened by extra lenses and much of other stuff. When you just feel inspired by a perfect day, or walking your dog, heading for a family outing, or… the list of such moments is limitless. They seldom require any spare lens, your camera flash will be sufficient for proper lighting and even a spare battery is just an extra load. Well, keeep the battery. There is always a chance of getting extra creative and I would hate if my advice ruined your session. But quite often all the rest is really a cargo. Size of this case is neither too bulky, nor too snug - just right for most DSLR cameras with attached zoom lens. If you prefer being absolutely sure that your camera fits, please check the dimensions below: Camera W x H: 5.1”x 4.1” Camera length with attached lens: 6.5” That comfortably suits such popular models like Canon Rebel T5, T6i, D70 or Nikon D3300, D5500 or D90 (all with their kit lens). We can add to this list Panasonic GH4, Sony A7 IIK and even… but we have to stop here. Few more steps and we will be lost in the universe of digits and letters. The bag allows multiple ways of carrying it around. If you wear it sling style or on shoulder you will enjoy comfort of meshed and cushioned shoulder pad. You can also fix the bag on your belt for even freer movements. After all, you can just grab it on top handle. Its hard to imagine that someone can use this option for shooting, but it definitely is useful when you only need to move the bag for a few yards. In all the above approaches, the most protruding part of the bag is its bottom. That is why it is reinforced and extra padded in this bag. The whole case is reasonably padded on all 360 degrees, but we put special efforts to protect lens.
