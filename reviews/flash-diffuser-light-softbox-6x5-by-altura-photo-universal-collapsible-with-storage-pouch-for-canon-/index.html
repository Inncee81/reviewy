Works but needs a bit of attention when shooting

Overall, the build quality and performance of this softbox is good. Works especially well with a flash mounted to rotating flash frame. Why then the 4 star rating? As noted by other reviewers, it does have a tendency to want to slump and tilt forward. I solved this issue by cutting a piece of 1” wide very thin aluminum bar stock and creating a support bracket. I insert this into the softbox, it has a bend and 3” of extra flat surface that rides the top of the flash head, and then cinching down the velcro collar holds all in place and the softbox nicely supported in an upright position.You can also keep an eye of the box and pop it back up periodically.
