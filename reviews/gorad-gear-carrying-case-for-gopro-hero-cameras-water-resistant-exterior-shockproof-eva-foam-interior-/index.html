I wrote the seller that I returned my GoPro 4 to replace with the newly launched GoPro 5 Black and the seller was gracious enought and more than willing to refund me but I decided to keep the case because it’s a really nice protective case

When I bought this case the GoPro 5 was not yet released and had intended to use it for the GoPro 4 Silver. I wrote the seller that I returned my GoPro 4 to replace with the newly launched GoPro 5 Black and the seller was gracious enough and more than willing to refund me but I decided to keep the case because it’s a really nice protective case, well-built, sturdy and aesthetically good looking and I can use it for other gadgets. To my surprise, it worked really well with the GoPro 5 especially so with the Super Suit (pls look at the pic) which I always use not only for water-proofing (not being confident about the 5 being waterproof by itself, having read many instances/horror stories that water had seeped in when submerged in water) but to protect the camera lens as well and I use the Super Suit all the time whether in or out of water when shooting. Shown in the photo here is the other case that I bought, a bigger, cheaper one, but as can be seen, the foam is not of good quality (soft & flimsy) while the GoRad is firm and much better (as they say, you get what you pay for). Also shown in the photo are the GoPro 5 wall-outlet Supercharger, the GoPro 5 dual charger, GoPro car cigarette lighter charger (a little tall but still ok), extra battery and various mounts including the hand-wrist mount. The zippered mesh top pocket is useful for keeping charging cables and other small accessories. Overall the GoRad case is not perfect but it’s a really good buy that it had become my goto case for the GoPro Hero 5 Black kit, and will recommend it for other GoPro 5 owners who use the Super Suit as well.
