Product Specifications

  • AT&T 3G MicroCell Needs GPS Signal to Activate & Operate.
  • Powerful GPS Antenna to Boost Incoming GPS Signal.
  • 25-Feet Cable Allows User to Place GPS Antenna Near A Window to Receive GPS Signal.
  • High Performance GPS Antenna with Narrow Band Filter for Pure Signal

  • Acquires and Locks onto GPS Signal in Marginal Areas

    Product Description

AT&T Cisco 3G MicroCell Wireless Network Extender requires GPS signal to activate and operate. However, MicroCell may not be able to acquire and lock onto GPS signal in the middle of a house or building. Gilsson High Performance GPS Antenna will allow one to place GPS antenna near a window to receive and boost incoming GPS signal for use with AT&T 3G MicroCell Wireless Network Extender.* Please note - The Microcell model# DPH 154 DOES NOT have an external GPS antenna port, therefore the antenna cannot be used.

On the phone with AT&T for hours and hours, they couldn’t figure out why, after 2 years of successfully using the MicroCell tower, it suddenly could not find the GPS signal. Ordered this antenna, arrived two days later, plugged it into the tower, placed the antenna next to the window (inside). Our location is low, almost in the creek bottom, lots and lots of trees. I was UNABLE to get even a one-bar phone signal without the MicroCell. It took about three or four hours for the antenna to capture the GPS. Now I have five bars and it’s working perfectly!
