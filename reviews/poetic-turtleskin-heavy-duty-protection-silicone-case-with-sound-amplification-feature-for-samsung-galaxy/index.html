Product Reviews

Great design and easy to apply. But…

I like the simple design and application of this case. However I’m concerned about how easy it is for it to come loose while holding it, especially along the long edge (see photo). I’ll be testing this in a shop environment and I foresee lots of dust and other nasties getting in there and wreaking havoc. Not to mention it just doesn’t feel “solid” when you hold it like this. I’m tempted to try and apply some non-permanent adhesive, like a double-sided gel-type, to make it more snug. Will update if I find something that works well. Would love hear suggestions from other users or the manufacturer on this.

The case can be easily removed by pulling at the sides

It fits my Samsung Galaxy Tab A 10.1 perfectly. It is not a snug fit though, and is a bit loose but not very loose. The case can be easily removed by pulling at the sides. The sides are protected with the thick design on the sides. I suggest buying a screen protector for your tablet to protect screen. I am happy with this purchase.

A very good product at a reasonable price.

I bought my son a new Galaxy and needed a case ASAP. This case is bright so it makes it easy to find the device. Most importantly, it has already saved me a great deal of money by protecting it from several drops. The thick rubber case is very protective. My 7 year old is hard on electronics and this is a very good product at a reasonable price.

Beefy!

I try to not buy critical items unless I can assess them with my own two hands, so I was a little leery ordering a case for my Tab A without knowing how durable it was. Well, this case arrived today and after putting the screen protector on (another review) and placing the Tab A in the case, well, I’m very impressed.Make no mistake - this is a beefy chunk of durable silicone rubber. The screen-side bezel is a raised lip about 3⁄16” meaning if it fell face-first it would land on this bezel and protect the screen. The corners - with the “crush zone” cutouts - is genius. All buttons and port holes line up perfect. Surprisingly, it seems to have added some weight to the overall unit; now when I hold it it has heft and is easier to hold and carry.My biggest concern when I bought the Tab A is that it’s thin and slippery - this case gives some traction and something to hold, and now the Tab A won’t slide around.Thanks Poetic, This is exactly the case I wanted. Well done!EDIT 12/1/16 - still very happy with the case; as another reviewer mentioned it would be nice if there were ‘stiffeners’ along the screen-side edges to prevent the case from pulling away when lifting the tablet from the side. (NOTE Poetic - if you’re reading this - get in touch if you’re interested in what I mean by ‘stiffeners’). Still a great product, will recommend all day long.

I think this was a good choice for me

Tablet’s new, not ready for prime time yet, but I knew when I bought it I’d want some kind of rugged case that wasn’t a book cover type. I think this was a good choice for me. I like the vented corners, and the high profile of the edges, even though they make typing somewhat clumsy, particularly at the bottom edge. But a well-protected tablet face trumps the occasional peskiness of a keying error in my book, anyday. Also, I appreciate the case’s not being overly snug. I can remove it without cutting-off my fingers’ circulation, yet it’s tight enough not to gap along the side edges. I also like being able to view the entire screen without obstruction. I can’t comment on the speaker placement yet, but it seems like an intuitive move.My only negative criticism would be the weight of the case itself, and the material it’s made of. It makes my feather-light tablet feel sort of heavy, and ìt’s not very lint/dust/ grime-removal friendly. Stuff wants to stick to it more than I’d prefer.The fact that the design of the back helps to keep the tablet from over-heating is valuable enough in and of itself to cancel out a couple of picky negatives.On the whole I think I made a very good purchase. I’m going to enjoy using it.

I justwish that Poetic made one specifically for the Tab A 10.1 with S Pen. If they did, I wouldn’t hesitate to purchase it.
