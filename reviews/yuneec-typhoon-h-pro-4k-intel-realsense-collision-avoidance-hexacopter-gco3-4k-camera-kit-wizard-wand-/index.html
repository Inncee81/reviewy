Good Machine

Pros: The Yuneec Typhoon H so far is pretty good. I like the ST16 controller, and the machine itself flies and handles well.The first few experiences are a little harrowing, but I think that’s mostly the extreme fear of crashing such an expensive bird - Even being somewhat experienced flying drones doesn’t seem to alleviate that.The firmware updates are a little fussy, but after an evening of fiddling and message board searching it appears to have been handled.The built in No fly Zone programming is asinine, but after several rounds with customer service, a signed liability release form and some more fiddling got that fixed.Cons:The packaging from Ritz was awful. The factory typhoon packaging was well done and secure in it’s carrying case, but it was just thrown in a box with all the bundled stuff from Ritz - just a mess. The tape job wasn’t terribly secure and one of the 32gb sd cards was missing. Not sure if it wasn’t included originally, or if it fell out of one of the gaping holes in the box during transit.
