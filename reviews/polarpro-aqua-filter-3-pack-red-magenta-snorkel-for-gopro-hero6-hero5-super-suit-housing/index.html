    Product Description

Style:Hero5 Super Suit 3-Pack The PolarPro Aqua Filter 3-Pack for the GoPro Hero5 SuperSuit housing provides you with the 3 necessary filters to capture vibrant underwater colors. Simply press on one of the filters and let the proprietary color formula do all the color correcting for you, no need to do any post-process editing. The Red filter is perfect for diving in tropical and blue waters from 15 to 75 feet. The Magenta can be used to capture stunning underwater colors in green water from 15 to 75 feet. Finally the Snorkel Filter can be used for shallow waters from 2 to 20 feet. You are covered for all underwater filming with the PolarPro Aqua filter 3-pack. Included with each filter set is a case to protect and keep your filters easily accessible. Pick up the Aqua 3-Pack before your next dive and capture vibrant underwater colors.

Product Reviews

Awesome Red Filter!

I bought this red filter for my GoPro Hero 4 Silver after reading all the reviews. I didn’t encounter any of the negative issues that others have wrote about. This lense filter fits very tightly to the camera case. I like that Polar Pro included the small synthetic piece of tether. I would suggest that you do what I did and use a lighter to singe the ends of the tether cord so they won’t unravel. So the tether wouldn’t float into the shot, I tied the tether to the rear underside mounting point of my telescoping pole and made sure any extra slack in the tether’s length was also secured out of the camera’s lense view.These screen shots were taken from the videos that I shot while completing my PADI Open Waters SCUBA Diving Course off Isla Mujeres, Mexico. The diving depth was about 35 ft. This filter was awesome in bringing out all the spectacular colors that I couldn’t even see due to the normal light transition through the clear diving mask I was wearing.In summary, this is a great red lense filter for about half the price of the other brands, including the obvious other brand! I definitely recommend this if your planning on do any diving with your GoPro in the Caribbean waters.

Worked Beautifully

I bought this in advance of a dive trip spring 2014. One giant stride into the water and the red filter popped out of the frame and was gone. I contacted the seller [Update Note: PolarPro was not the Amazon seller when I purchased this.] They refused to replace it since I was now months after the purchase. No chance to see if it improved my videos or not since it came apart on entry into the water. I am very disappointed in this product.Update January 2015Bottom Line: This filter saved me hours in post production doing color correction.Jeff, from PolarPro, and his co-worker Austin reached out to me and sent a replacement just in time for my dive trip last month. It worked wonderfully. No lens popping issues was a huge help ;-)Longer Version: This filter corrected my GoPro Hero 3+ video beautifully. I used it in the Caribbean between 30’ and 70’. On previous dives, I spent hours correcting video to make it look as beautiful as I remembered it. The problem with using post production to correct for blue tint at depth is that it introduces loads of color distortion. To get the subject reasonably color corrected often resulted in super saturation and weird color shifts elsewhere in frame. This filter not only saved hours but dramatically improved the color and consistency of my videos.I also used it with my Canon PowerShot SX260HS while taking a tour in a glass bottomed boat. It is not designed to do this. I held it in place and the Canon lens fit reasonably well within the PolarPro red filter frame. Even though we were only in about 7’ of water, the color correction delivered significantly better photos (and video) than my wife’s Nikon DSLR. While she is a much better photographer than I am, her lack of color correction produced photos looking like half-tone pale blue works of art. The coral, turtles, and fish were all various hues of the same color. My only complaint is that I developed some fine line scratches with my unapproved use of this with my point and shoot. While a glass lens might have minimized these scratches, I imagine it would also have driven up the price.Between the filter performance for diving and PolarPro’s customer service, I updated my review to 5 stars. Thanks Jeff and Austin!
