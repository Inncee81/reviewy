When I realized I could attach an adjustable neck to a second adjustable neck to double the flexible length, I purchased two more. Really sturdy.The clamp is also fantastic and is able to clip on to almost anything. Sometimes you really need to use your muscles to open it up all the way to clamp onto a larger pipe or something, but its not really an issue.5 Stars all the way!!!

Flawed Product

While the clamp and the goose neck are of high quality and are rather useful, you’ll soon run in to the same problem that many people on the forums are having - the mount at the base of the gooseneck: the locking clips are relatively thin and will break within a short period of ownership.GoPro wants you to send them pictures of the broken locking clip along with receipt, a description of what you were doing when it broke and then…you still aren’t going to get a replacement. The goose neck base is slightly different than the one used on other accessories so you aren’t going to be able to swap out parts. Buy it, use it, when it breaks, reach for the duct tape.

Great Mount, for Stagnant Mounts

Really do like this mount: but it isn’t as versatile as you may imagine. It’s fantastic for mounting in places where the object you are mounting on will not move, shake, or vibrate; otherwise - beware.I’ve used this mount for quad riding, roll bar mounting on vehicles, and bicycling: but with mediocre results. The mount offers so many possibilities, but has very little stability. The gooseneck transmits and amplifies mounting point vibrations, which is no good for video quality. Taking off the gooseneck and using the clamp alone is does improve transmitted vibration, but obviously limits your views. The clamp does offer pretty firm mounting, and the faux zip tie insert allows for very secure mounting around round objects smaller than the jaw opening.Also, be cautious when looking elsewhere: the first few goosenecks released were released with 7 “gooseneck balls” and later versions had 8.As with most of GoPro’s mounts, they are better than any of the knockoffs I’ve seen, but at least double the price.

Holds Firmly and easy to remove

We mount our GoPro to the roll bar of our Caterham, and I got tired of having to unscrew the roll bar mount to remove the camera every time we left the vehicle unattended. I thought this, which has a quick release mount at at both ends of the neck, would be a better alternative. I learned fairly quickly that removing the camera from a quick release mount isn’t quick at all if you use the anti-vibration tab, so removing just the camera, leaving the mount in place is not a quick alternative. However, it’s easy enough to take it all with you. You can use this item with just the jaws or if you need it higher, you can leave the neck on. For us, mounting the camera directly to the jaws is perfect. The jaws hold firmly where you put it, but a firm squeeze and it all goes with us. This is a much quicker solution to the roll bar mount that screws on.
