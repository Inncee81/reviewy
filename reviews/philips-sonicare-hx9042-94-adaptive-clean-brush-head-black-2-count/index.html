The Best Brushheads

These Sonicare brushheads are a revelation. So it almost turns your sonicare into more of a hybrid of a Sonicare and a Oral-B (the ones where the bristles actually move back and forth). The cleaning effect is, in my personal experience, the best of both worlds. I just can’t believe these are so hard to find – none of my local pharmacies have these Adaptive Clean brushheads!
