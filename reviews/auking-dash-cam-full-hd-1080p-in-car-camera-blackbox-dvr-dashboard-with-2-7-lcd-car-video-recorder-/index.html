    Product Description

Main Features:

Automatic recording : The cam will turn on and start recording automatically when the engine starts, and turn off shortly after the engine off.

Collision Detection: Built-in G-sensor,the cam automatically saving and locking the video recording data in case of collisions. The sensitivity of the G-sensor can be set to off/low/mid/high levels.

Loop Recording: This feature enables users to optimize their data by having the newest video overwrite the oldest video in sequence with 3, 5, or 10 minute intervals. Locked files will not be overwritten.

Motion Detection: When the cam sensed to the moving objects ,it will automatically start recording.When the cam does not sensed to moving object in about 5 seconds ,it will stop recording automatically.This function make the cam protect your car when parking.
