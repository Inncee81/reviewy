Excellent product

This cap exceeded my expectations. I was looking for a cap which would prevent “racing stripes” on my forehead from a new, well ventilated helmet. This cap does the job very well, without interfering with the good ventilation of this new helmet. It provides no insulation which would allow heat to be trapped against the skin and once wet with sweat it provides some evaporative cooling whenever one is moving. This evaporative property also keeps sweat from running down into my eyes. It is large enough to cover my head (L to XL hat size). It is easily cleaned (wet under faucet, drop of dish soap, squeeze a bit, then rinse) and dries quickly when hanging.
