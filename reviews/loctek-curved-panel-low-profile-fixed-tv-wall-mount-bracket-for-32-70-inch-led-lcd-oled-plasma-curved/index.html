Doesn’t work well with Sony 930d display

I ordered this mount because I was going to get a Samsung 65ku7500 (curved display) however before the tv shipped I changed my mind and went with a Sony. I didn’t see a reason to return the mount because it said that it would work with a flat display fine. Well, it works but I have a few problems: 1. I can’t reach the HDMI ports 2-4 without taking the tv down (this might have been solved if I go a mount that pulls off the wall a bit. And it’s a little bit Sony’s fault in how they setup the plugs) 2. It was a bear to line up the hangers when putting the tv onto the wall bracket. The hangers pivot , which I assume helps a curved tv, but it added difficulty with our display. We shoved cardboard into the brackets to keep them from moving while we placed the tv. 3. The strings for locking the tv in place are super long. I kinda shoved them up so that I don’t see them. Not sure if I can easily shorten them and keep it it good shape.Overall if I have to take the tv down for any reason I might be putting a different tv mount up at the same time. Good price but doesn’t work with the Sony display as well.
