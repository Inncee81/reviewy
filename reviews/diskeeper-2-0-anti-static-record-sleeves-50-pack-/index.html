    Product Description

Our most popular audiophile inner record sleeve has been made even better and we guarantee you’ll love them. Made from 2.0 mil anti-static high-density polyethylene, our Diskeeper 2.0 is not only affordable but offers our best combination of quality and cost-effective way to protect your LPs. Each sleeve is precision die-cut at 12-1⁄16 x 12-1⁄16 inches and tested with 200 gram audiophile vinyl as well as older, thinner LPs. Manufactured by an audio accessory company in Taiwan exclusively for Sleeve City, each sleeve is perfectly consistent in size and quality.

Great inner sleeves, highly recommended.

These are great inner sleeves. The MoFi inner sleeves are a little better, a little more expensive and clear on one side only. I use these from Sleeve City for most of my albums because they seem to help with static, are clear on both sides and are thin yet tough. I put my albums in these after cleaning. If the old paper sleeve has pictures, words or whatever it goes in empty next to the other sleeve w/album - this keeps the paper fibers off of the vinyl. I highly recommend these if you care about your vinyl and sound quality.
