Good panel, works well, some design issues

Nicely made, quality panel. Flexible to some extent- it can be muted to a slightly curved surface or suspended easily from the grommets. The output cables are entirely too heavy for this to be considered a portable panel, the wire gage alone makes them inflexible and difficult to deal with. Also, the wiring connection is on the back of the panel for some reason, making mounting difficult and reducing flexibility. If the connection was smaller and located on the front or tabbed on an edge, mounting would be much easier and more practical. Be advised, this panel is a laminated polymer and when it heats up in the sunshine, it will EXPAND. If you have glued or screwed it to a flat or curved surface or frame, expect it to buckle or sag when it heats up. Some flexibility in mounting is required.
