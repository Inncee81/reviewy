Product Reviews

Two Strikes…

First off… these are pretty high quality for the $20 price tag. The housings are sturdy aluminum and the glass is apparently defect free.That said, they have two strikes against them that are enough to keep me from using the CPL and variable ND filters ever again on my Typhoon H. The first thing I noticed was that the edges of the outer bezel could be seen in photos and video while using each of these filters (see attached picture) - the UV filter was okay.The second issue was the weight of the CPL and ND filters. Sometimes the gimbal would have issues trying to accommodate the extra weight. During flight the camera would sometimes move several degrees from horizontal and not return, forcing me to land and restart the system to reset the gimbal. Once again… no issues at all with the UV filter.I didnt feel that a lower rating was fair since I dont know if they may function differently on other CGO models… plus other reviewers seem to have had luck with theirs!In the meantime I went with a Polar Pro 3 piece set and am very happy with it so far.Happy flying!
