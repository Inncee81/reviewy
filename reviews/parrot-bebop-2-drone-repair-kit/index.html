Parrot Bebop 2 Drone, Repair Kit

by people ...

  • Repair Kit designed for bebop 2
  • Includes brushless motor gimbals
  • Comes with “circlip” attachment system
  • Easy Removal and assembly
  • A mistake-proof system helps identify clockwise and anti-clockwise cages

Parrot offers a repair kit that takes into consideration the Flight experience of Drone pilots and includes brushless Cages which are easy to dismantle and available as separate parts. This kit contains two motor Cages (clockwise and anti-clockwise) and their two related “circlip” attachment systems designed for the Parrot bebop 2.
