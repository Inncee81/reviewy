Product Reviews

Strong and secure

This wall mount replaced my SU-WL810 that was model specific too. In fact, I used the top two bolt holes and drilled two new bottom holes to mount the bracket. The old and new bolts are exactly the same: length, thread, size but with a different lag bolt head requiring an adjustable wrench rather than a screw driver. It’s a good bracket to hold an 84 lb. tv.

Great mount for XBR-65X930E

Extremely heavy duty mount. I guess it has to be if it holds the 75X940E, I have the 65X930E and it seems a little overkill, but it works great. When pushed all the way in, the screen surface of the TV is only 2.5 inches from the wall, but you can pull it out about 12 inches from the wall to get access to all the cable connections; very handy. It is definitely more expensive than some of the generic mounts available, but I’m very happy with this purchase.
