Product Reviews

Superior protection for compact cameras

Please take a look at images I uploaded to the product page on this well-made case that offers superior protection against bumps and drops. This case will accommodate cameras measuring (L)3 5⁄8 by (H)2 1⁄2 by (D)1 1⁄8 inches in dimensions. The best design - full length zipper opening with elastic hinge supports (so that the two halves won’t open too wide when you unzip the case).The camera should be inserted into the case with its LCD screen facing the plush fabric surface half. The front of the camera will then face the other half of the case that has the super-tough EVA shell to protect against crush and drop.I STRONGLY suggest that you tether the wrist strap of the camera to the ringlet on the case, so that they are always together. When taking pictures, while holding the camera with your right hand, you should hold the case with your left hand. If someone accidentally bumps into you and causes you to drop the camera, it won’t fall to the ground because the case is tied to the camera. I had saved my camera from destruction numerous times this way.I like this case very much because it offers great protection without the bulk of other cases. If your camera is the same or smaller than the aforementioned dimensions, this is an excellent case to consider.

Perfect for the Canon Power Shot A2400 Digital Camera

I got this for my daughter (age 11) to hold Canon Power Shot digital camera she received for Christmas, in a similar pretty blue color. It is sturdy but compact, easy to carry, and has an extra compartment to hold other small items such as an SD card or even cash. The cord can be used in any of 3 ways: lanyard, belt loop, or wrist strap. My daughter likes to wear it around her neck. It’s also small enough to stuff in a pocket or purse, making it easy to keep with you at all times. The soft interior will keep your camera scratch-free while the semi-molded exterior will protect it from bumps and dings. We’re very happy with this case. Great color!
