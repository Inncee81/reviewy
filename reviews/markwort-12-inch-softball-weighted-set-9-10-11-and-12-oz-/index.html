Great for improving throwing distance

Quality constructed balls. I start off throwing a normal 12 inch softball and then go to the 9 ounce, then on up 1 by 1 to the 12 ounce. When you try to throw a regular softball after finishing with the 12 ounce ball it feels like a ping pong ball. You have to be really alert with the heavier balls as they’ll do some damage if you don’t catch it. I’ve noticed a good deal of improvement in the distance I’m able to throw. Very pleased with the purchase and the quality of the product.
