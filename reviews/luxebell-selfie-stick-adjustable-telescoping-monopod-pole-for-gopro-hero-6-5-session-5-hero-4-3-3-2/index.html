Product Reviews

I love my selfie stick

I love my selfie stick. It worked great when I have used it. The selfie stick is 12.2 inches when it is at its shortest length. When it is at its longest, it is 40.5 inches. This is a good product. You can also use the Sony action camera with this selfie stick. I used this selfie stick a ton when i was on vacation. This is a great product and I would definitely recommend it.

Quality at the price it should be

Was apprehensive about this product as the price range is so big for a simple adjustable stick.I was happy when I opened it and telescoped it all the way out. Works by twisting it open and then back closed in counter clockwise/clockwise turn of the tubs. Extends 3 times and it can be locked at any point of the 3 extension stages. Very solid.The camera stays in place and there is very little flex even when load is put on the stick (dragging it through water when filming underwater fishing in a moving boat)Great price. Don’t pay 5x as much for the same product.

… as it’s a little big (long) for me to easily carry around + I have the smaller handle that …

Only used it once so far as it’s a little big (long) for me to easily carry around + I have the smaller handle that floats that I can slip into my side pocket with camera attached. When I get a tripod I do anticipate it coming in very handy as the bottom is threaded so you could purchase a smaller tripod but still get the height you need by using this as an extension. It seems to be built quite well and will probably last a very long time. The size factor for me prevents the 5th star but I still recommend it highly if you need a selfie stick for your go-pro.

Well Made and Works Great for Galaxy and Go Pro

This selfie stick is very high quality aluminum and works great for both my Galaxy S5 (with thin case on) and my Go Pro. I was looking for one that fit both but wasn’t sure if the camera clamp mechanism would hold the larger phone based on the description. It does.This unit doesn’t have a wireless remote but a) I already have one, and b) it’s easier just to put the camera on a 5 sec delay photo delay anyway.From what I can see, this manufacturer invested in the quality of the core product and materials vs investing in features which aren’t needed.This is the best made selfie stick I’ve seen.
