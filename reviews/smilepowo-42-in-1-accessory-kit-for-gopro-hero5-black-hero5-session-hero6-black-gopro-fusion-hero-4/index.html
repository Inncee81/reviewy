Good set, lacks a manual

I have had a GoPro for about 6 months now, it’s my very first one and I am not a professional by any means. I ordered three different accessory kits at the same time, all with a similar list of items included, one had a couple of screws more but was priced higher and another one had about 20 things less and cost 25 bucks instead of 30. Im thinking this one might be the best deal, its well priced (29.99) and you get everything you need.All of that said, there is one major flaw in all of them, especially if you are a beginner like me: absolutely no instructions whatsoever, not in the case, not in the listing. Some of the items are self-explanatory (tripod, selfie stick, chest band, suction cup etc) but there are SO MANY various screws that I have no clue what to do with, I might actually Youtube a tutorial.All of the items seem to be universal when it comes to GoPro’s so they will fit any type that you may have. Everything is wrapped separately in little zip lock baggies.The case measures: 9” x 13” x 3”This accessory kit contains 42 items (the case fits all of them but once you take them out, its kind of hard to put everything back in and be able to zip it up):1 x Head Belt Strap Mount1 x Chest Belt Strap Mount1 x Wrist Strap Mount1 x Wrist Strap for Remote Control1 x 360 Degree Rotating Backpack Clip Mount1 x Helmet Strap Mount1 x Bike Handlebar Holder Mount2 x Bike Handlebar Holder Mount Adapter1 x Car Suction Cup Mount2 x Car Suction Cup Adapter1 x Extendable Handle Monopod2 x Handle Monopod Adapter1 x Floating Handle Grip1 x Floating Block2 x Surface Quick Release Buckle2 x Long Thumbscrew2 x Flat Mount2 x Arc Mount5 x Adhesive Pad1 x Insurance Tether Strap12 x Anti-fog Insert1 x Large Carrying Case1 x Tripod1 x Phone ClampLike I said above, there are other sets on the market that contain more items (49 in 1), but they are usually 3-10 bucks more expensive, the quality is exactly the same or worse and all you get on top of all thats in this set is more fog inserts and various screws, most of which you may not even need.Overall I am satisfied with the amount of accessories, the way they are wrapped and the quality, however I do urge the seller / manufacturer to add a good, well written instruction manual with suggestions for use.
