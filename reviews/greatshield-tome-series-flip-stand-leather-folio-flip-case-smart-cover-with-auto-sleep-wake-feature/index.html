Product Specifications

  • Protective leather case made for the Google Nexus 10 touch screen computer tablet
  • Automatically put your tablet to sleep by simply closing the cover to help preserve battery life
  • Flip cover has the capability to transform the case into a horizontal stand
  • Cut outs are custom made for the Google Nexus 10 providing full access to all of its features

  • Made with premium synthetic leather with a soft micro suede interior to provide nice protection for your device

    Product Description

GreatShield’s TOME Series slim leather case perfectly fits your Google Nexus 10 tablet. It is made of premium synthetic leather, lightweight and durable. The soft micro suede provides maximum protection for your tablet’s screen. Specifically designed cutouts allow you to easily access all ports and buttons of your Google Nexus 10. The built in magnet ensures a secure closure. This case also features a smart cover function to automatically wake and put your Google Nexus 10 to sleep. This case features a slim fit design. The front cover can be easily folded back for comfortable typing and video viewing positions.
