    Product Description

The 6 ft. Lifetime Wave Kayak is specifically designed for kids ages 5 and up, or up to 130 pounds. The wide stance provides a stable paddling platform to keep your child safely in the kayak. Its slopped back end and swim-up step allows the rider to easily re-enter the kayak from the water. With scupper holes that drian the cockpit, multiple footrest positions for different size riders and reverse chine for enhanced stability, the Wave is perfect for kids recreational kayaking. Includes a black double-sided paddle.

Product Reviews

Six year old loves it!

We live at a lake, so I purchased this for my son’s 6th birthday. He was so excited to have “a kayak of his own” that he slept it in for a week in his bedroom. (The instructions do say not to sit in it when it’s not in the water because it can deform it, but we figured it’d be fine). He’s taken it out twice so far. The first time we attached a long rope to the handle so that he couldn’t potentially float away (it was a very windy day). The second time he asked to have the rope off and even practiced paddling on his knees, standing up on it (like a paddle board), tipping over, swimming around and climbing up the back. His grandmother was amazed how quickly he was able to steer with his paddle. He’s proud of it and loves to pull it out of the water. He can’t carry it yet: 18 pounds is too heavy and it’s too bulky. He loves the independence of it, considering many other water toys (paddle boards, paddle boats, row boats, etc.) are too big for him. We purchased a paddle leash because, while there are notches in the kayak to sit the paddle, he knocked it into the water a few times while playing about. One piece of advice about pricing: check frequently. We got the green one for under a hundred dollars. The price on the green and blues ones fluctuate every couple of weeks or so.
