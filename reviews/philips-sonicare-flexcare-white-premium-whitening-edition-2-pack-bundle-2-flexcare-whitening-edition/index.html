Awesome upgraded toothbrush

I brought this toothbrush to upgrade from an older model of the same brand. I loved that toothbrush (just a basic sonic care model) and I really love this toothbrush.Thankfully I was used to a sonic care toothbrush, it is normal to have some gum sensitivity and even a little bleeding when going from Manuel brushing to an electric toothbrush, because this toothbrush is powerful. You really get that fresh from the dentist feel after brushing with this toothbrush. This set came with two brush handles, two brush diamond clean brush heads, a charger, a charger with brush sanitizer and two travel cases. A huge package deal! I could not be happier (unless it was free or something which it was not).There are several settings for the toothbrush: clean, white, massage, go clean, and max clean. I usually go for the max clean which is a 3 minute cycle but there are shorter cycles if you are in a hurry. Not that 3 minutes is that much time. The brush vibrates differently for the different cycles. I have really noticed a difference in my visits to the dentist and overall tooth and gum health since I began using a sonic care toothbrush. The toothbrush holds the charge for quite a while before you have to charge it again and then I usually just charge it overnight and it is ready to go in the morning.I love the new sanitizer feature on this model. You just pop the brush head into the sanitizer, hit the button and 10 minutes later it is germ free. Yay. The travel cases are great too. I always carry my sonicare when I travel. The case is great and keeps everything clean. The toothbrush and head fit into spaces that are specifically designed for them and they stay put. I mean there really is nothing negative to say about this toothbrush. Get it. Clean your teeth. You will love it!
