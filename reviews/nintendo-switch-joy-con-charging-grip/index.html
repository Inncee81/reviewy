includes a usb-a to usb-c cable, works great

Charging grip includes a usb-a to usb-c cable. You can actually use the cable included to charge the switch too provided you have a usb-a port that can provide the power needed. Charging grip charges the joycon with the included cable as expected. No issues so far.

The version I bought at the store works fine, almost exact same shape as the normal Joy-Con …

I had to buy this in person at a physical retail store, because when I tried to buy it new on Amazon from Amazon directly, Amazon sent me an obviously opened and used package, where the actual Charging grip was replaced with the stock, non-charging grip.The version I bought at the store works fine, almost exact same shape as the normal Joy-Con grip that comes with the Switch, just with a USB C port and yellow LED indicator to show that the controllers are charging on the top edge. It has metal rails, and has semi-transparent plastic to differentiate it from the standard grip. All in all, a solid build that really should have been part of the basic Joy-Con grip.No integrated battery to charge the Joy-Cons on its own; it’s basically a pricey, grip-shaped USB adapter for your Joy-Cons, nothing more.Comes with a Nintendo-branded USB-A to USB-C charging cable of decent length, 6 ft.
