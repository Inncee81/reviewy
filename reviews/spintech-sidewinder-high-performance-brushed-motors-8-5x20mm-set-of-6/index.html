Product Specifications

  • Fast 8.5x20mm brushed motor set (Hubsan X4 H107C or H107D)
  • 15700kv for more thrust and power. Optimized for 65mm propellors. 1s (3.7v) voltage.
  • Clockwise (red & blue) and counterclockwise (black & white) versions
  • 80mm wire length. Now has micro JST plugs

  • Set of 6 motors for hex builds or spares

    Product Description

Spintech Sidewinders are some of the highest performance brushed motors available for your micro RC! Get more thrust and faster speeds with these 8.5x20mm motors! We have tested our motors against regular Hubsan motors and ours generate 15g more thrust. See photos for details. Please break in motors at medium throttle for first 3 flights. And always allow motors to cool between flights.
