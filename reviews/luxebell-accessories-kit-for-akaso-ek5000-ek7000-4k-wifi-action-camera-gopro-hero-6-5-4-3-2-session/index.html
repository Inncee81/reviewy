Great beginner kit

I’m new the whole GoPro movement but as a freelance photography I wanted to jump on this bandwagon and see what sort of cool and interesting pictures the GoPro could add to my portfolio. As others have stated, the list of addons to the GoPro is very long and can be very pricy. Not knowing exactly how I wanted to use my new GoPro 4 HERO, I saw this kit and thought it would allow me a chance to my hands on some great addons that will be be helpful and because of the price, wouldn’t bust my wallet. Everything in the kit works great with the OEM products provided by GoPro and the case fits everything nice and neat including the selfie stick but is a little tight with the chest harness. I’m sure you can probably find a way to pack everything in there if needed but I find it to fit my needs perfectly. I recommend this kit for anyone like me just joining the GoPro movement and is undecided on what addons to get.
