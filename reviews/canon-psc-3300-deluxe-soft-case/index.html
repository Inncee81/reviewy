      Product Description

The Canon Deluxe Soft Camera Case PSC-3300 is ideal for protecting your point-and-shoot camera. Keep your Canon PowerShot Digital Camera in like-new condition while traveling or exploring with this soft camera case. This Canon Camera Accessory is compatible with the SX series of digital cameras from Canon, including the following Canon Digital Camera models: SX130 IS, SX120 IS, SX110 IS, and SX100 IS.

Product Reviews

Fits our Canon SX700 Very Well

I ordered this case for my wife’s new Canon SX700 camera, based upon several reviews that it fit the camera very well. It was all true. The camera fits in well, attaches to the belt, and is vertical when on your belt so it is less likely to catch on your car seat or other places when wearing.

While I was glad to see Amazon had Canon’s official case in stock

While I was glad to see Amazon had Canon’s official case in stock, the positive comments pretty much end there. Despite being the recommended case for my Canon PowerShot SX710 HS, the fit is terrible: the case is too big, and the boxy look isn’t all stylish. Even more unfortunate, though, are the materials used: while this case’s predecessor, the PSC-3200, was made entirely of a supple, high-quality leather, this one is mostly made of ballistic nylon. The flap and back of the case are made of leather so cheap that I almost wish the whole case were nylon.If you’ve never seen the excellent case this one replaces, you may be satisfied, but to me it just reeks of cut corners.

Excellent for casual use

I purchased this for a Canon PowerShot SX730 Digital Camera, and it fits snugly but safely. The magnetic clip is nice, but tends to pop open with rough use. The camera never once came out, but it worried me. This case should not to be used in high-pickpocket areas, as it is just too easy to open on your belt, but for outdoor hiking this is perfect. No extra room at all for spare sd cards or battery, so don’t expect to just squeeze them in. Nevertheless, this a really nice case. I felt that my camera was well-protected from bumps while in it.

Excellent Case, Protective and Light, Fits Canon PS SX700 HS Perfectly

Very minimalist case, with just enough space to fit my Canon SX700 to make it in and out of the case with no effort. Quality is outstanding, with a letter upper flap and high quality mesh thick nylon-like fabric on the rest, which makes it very light, contrary to my last Canon case made all of leather and it was on the heavy side. Fabric seems very resistant, difficult to pierce or rip. The flap also has a hole on top to get the wristband trough, although at least on the SX700 it can easily go via the open spaces on the side. The closing mechanism uses a strong magnet button that won’t allow the camera to get out of the case no matter how hard you shake it or flip it. Metal made Canon logo gives it a very nice, professional touch, and the case has a band on the back to hang it to a beltOn the inside, it is well cushioned and supportive, all around the interior, including the flap. It doesn’t matter in which direction you put the camera in as the protection is the same all around. If you have a Canon SX700 or a camera with the same dimensions, this is the perfect case

Works great for Canon SX710 camera!

Fits my Canon SX710 perfectly. Case seems well made–it is not nearly as “soft” as I had thought it would be. The back and cover is a hard vinyl/leather-like material while the front, pouch type area is a thick, stiff mesh/webbing type material. It does have a loop for a belt on the back. The inside of the case is lightly padded and soft. The snap is a magnetic closure. There is a space between the cover and the camera “pouch” meaning that you would not be able to put other small items into the case, as they would fall out through the top, but it holds the camera securely, which is all I really care about. Amazon’s price is way cheaper than you would find on the manufacturer’s website.
