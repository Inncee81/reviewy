Good deal for price

For only $30 this is a good deal. It’s a simple basic stereo. It doesn’t have a CD player, but it has a place for an aux, USB and an SD card. The aux was what my kids had been wanting anyway. It also comes with a remote (we’ll see how long my family keeps up with that). It’s fairly small, so most people will need a kit to make it compatable, but it’s still a good deal. It has wiring for front and rear speakers. We had planned to use it in our Tahoe, but since it has third row seating(and a third set of speakers), it wouldn’t work (although, someone more mechanical may be able to make it work for a third set of speakers). We ended up using it in our son’s Escape and it worked just fine.

Considering the low cost: works well… however…

…needless to say, it didn’t fit ‘effortlessly’ in my ‘03 Nissan Sentra like it stated it would. There were no screw holes corresponding to the mounting brackets… I had to use some creativity and hard wire to ‘tie’ the unit on to the brackets. However, after attaching the aftermarket wire harness & plugging it all in… it works fine. The low cost of this receiver can be felt when pushing the buttons… not as ‘high tech’ and smooth a feel as with other higher end electronics. Having said that, it sounds very good! The effects: loud/on/off, bass, treble, balance etc. are easy to adjust. (I have no use for the BlueTooth feature, but I will be using the USB/mp3 slot.) Again, for the price and reasonable quality, I recommend.
