Great for my Kam Snap Press!

I bought this not for use with a tennis racket but for the handle of my snap press from kamsnaps.com. The hard metal of the press was bruising my hand everytime I was using it but now not only is is hot pink but it’s nice and squishy and doesn’t leave horrible bruises on my palms. The only negative was that I had to use the entire package of wrap and it probably could use a little more just for some extra padding, it didn’t cover as much/well as I had hoped it would. Overall it’s great though. Some reviews complained about it being really sticky, maybe that’s only when you get it wet with sweat (I don’t sweat when I put snaps on things, lol), but I don’t find it to be too sticky/tacky (just enough to hold itself in place), it’s not attracting a bunch of dirt/dust/cat hair or anything.

Best “Tacky” Overgrip

In the ongoing battle of preference between drier grips like tournagrip and tacky ones like Gamma, I decided to try Wilson pro thanks to Federer, my favorite player. As a fan of tackyness, this overgrip is fantastic. However, it isn’t so tacky that it is sticky (which I love). It is soft and tacky at the same time and seems to just float in your hand. Sometimes you forget your racket is there. Moisture absorption is good, but then again I don’t sweat much. I recommend getting a 12 pack because you will go through these for several reasons. If you are increasing the size of your grip you can use multiple overgrips. Also if you play serious tennis you will wear them out. And also dirt appears on them quickly, so if you get grossed out then you’ll change them a lot. I won’t even be trying another overgrip. Just get this one, stop worrying about your gear, and go worry about your tennis!
