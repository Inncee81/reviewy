new go-to ski sock

These were great last season. Slightly thinner and tighter than I’m used to, but they performed well and were never itchy, which is fantastic. I had cold toes one day (out of many), but I don’t know if there’s a sock anywhere that can prevent that. I ended up ordering additional pairs for myself and my family and everyone was happy. We liked them better than our SmartWool ski socks which we used last season. These worked better with our ski boots and no one got blisters or rashes.

Great Ski sock

Went skiing with the family at Christmas and got us each a pair as a gift. Everybody loved them. They are soft and comfortable and work great with a ski boot. I did go up a size for my daughter because she has a thicker calf and I had read that it’s pretty snug on the calf. It was a good choice. They are snug on the calf. We have skied a lot over the years and tried different socks and nothing special. They also look pretty cool! I got everyone a different color. Very nice when you’re doing everyone’s laundry together. Would definitely recommend and buy again. Washed well too.

Great socks!

Got these for a friend and she loves them! She is an above-average skilled skier and we live in the Rocky Mts, so we ski at 9,000- 11,000 feet generally. She tried them on with her new boots (indoors only so far) and loves the fit and the style.

Not very warm

These fit well and looked great, but they just weren’t that warm. I used them with snow boots in Iceland and my toes were numb when I wore these out at night (late March, temperatures in the mid 30’s).

Tight around the calf

I have several pairs of Euro socks and these, by far, fit the tightest. I ordered the large and they are super tight around the calf to the point that it becomes painful after a few hours. I am of average build so I don’t know who these would be comfortable on if you plan on wearing them pulled up all the way. Also, they are made of a different material than my other Euro socks which makes them thinner and not nearly as warm. I gave two stars only because I love the color!

the fit and padding at shin is terrific! These are lightweight so wear silk sock liners

If your toe rubs inside front of boot, this sock is too thin. However, the fit and padding at shin is terrific! These are lightweight so wear silk sock liners!
