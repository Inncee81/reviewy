I’m very pleased.Oh, and this camera works with all my old Nikkor/Nippon/Nikon non-ai lenses. The camera doesn’t have the ability to program in my non-ai lenses like some of the more expensive Nikons, but the lenses work great. You just need to really know what you’re doing as the camera can’t adjust ISO, Aperature and shutter speed automatically with the non-ai lenses. However, the ability to switch from viewfinder to screen preview helps dial in the settings easily.
