Product Description DataComm Electronics’ Easy Mount Cable Organizer Kit is used to install power and hide unwanted audio video cables in the wall behind your wall mounted TV. This kit is the ideal solution for converting an unfinished basement or spare room into a high end home theater or media room and will being a clean, professional look to your in-home installation.

From the Manufacturer DataComm Electronics’ Easy Mount Cable Organizer Kit (50-3338-WH-KIT)

Exposed Cables ? Create the Sleek Look of an Expensive, Custom Flat Panel TV Installation If you already have AC power connected to your wall mounted flat panel TV, how will you hide and organize the HDM and A/V cables connecting the TV and home theater components? Solve this common problem of exposed audio video cables behind your flat panel TV, your amplifier or other audio/video devices using the Easy Mount Cable Organizer Kit. The patented design allows for easy A/V cable management. Cluttered A/V cables can be hidden behind the wall at the location of the flat panel TV. The wall plate can be installed upright or upside down.

Problem Solved. Designer to be Installed Without Low Voltage Mounting Plates The new Easy Mount Cable Organizer plates are designed to be installed without “Low Voltage Mounting Plates” or “Old Work Mud Rings”. The innovative mounting wings are molded into the Easy Mount wall plate and fasten tightly against the back of the drywall. You now only have to install the cable plate and do not have to worry about installing the mounting brackets.
