Product Reviews

A Good Way To Transfer Photos And Videos From An iPhone To A Computer. Frees Up Valuable iPhone Memory.

I seem to frequently seem to run out of memory on my iPhone. The iPhone (or iPad) is great for taking HD video and high resolution pictures. However, the higher quality video and pictures comes with a price. The file sizes are larger, and eventually my iPhone memory will fill up. Since Apple memory is not expandable, I must then delete some files, or, use a lightning cable to transfer them to my PC, or, transfer them to iCloud or iTunes, to free up phone or tablet memory. However, free iCloud memory fills up rapidly, and then you must pay monthly fees if you want more iCloud memory.I think that this RAVPower iPhone Ligntning USB 3.0 Flash Drive (RP-IM013) is a good solution to this problem. It is a 64 GB flash drive (USB 3.0) with a USB connector on one end of the memory device, with an Apple Lightning Connector on the end of a 2.5 inch flexible cable attached to the memory device.. As long as your Apple device uses a Lightning Cable for charging, you can plug this RAVPower Flash drive into your iPhone (or iPad), and transfer your videos, pictures, and music to the Flash Drive. You can then access these pictures and videos from your Apple device, whenever you plug it into your phone or tablet. Or, you can later plug the USB Flash Drive into a USB port on your PC or laptop, to transfer them onto your PC or laptop hard drive.Since a typical 16 GB iPhone only has about 10 GB of free memory (rest is used by the iOS operating system), all of your apps, pictures, and videos must fit into this 10 GB of space. Thus, after your typical collection of apps, you only have a few GB of space to hold all of your photos, videos, and text messages. This RAVPower Flash drive will give you a new 64 GB of space to transfer your photos and videos to. This is a lot of memory space, and you can access any of these photos or videos by merely plugging the USB Flash Drive into your Apple device, without taking up valuable space on your iPhone or iPad.A nice feature of this Flash Drive is that it can be used as a charging cable to charge your Apple iPhone or iPad. Simply plug the USB 3.0 connector on this RAVPower Flash Drive into your computer USB port (or a USB charger, or USB battery pack). Then plug the Lightning connector into your Phone or iPad to charge your Apple device.This RAVPower Flash Drive is compact and well made. It has a metal body with a standard USB connector attached to one end, and a 2.5 inch flexible rubber insulated cable attached to the other end, which terminates with an Apple Lightning connector at the end of the cable. It is lightweight and small, and can easily be carried in your pocket.An Apple applicaton (app) automatically comes up on your iPhone or iPad, the first time the Lightning connector is plugged into your Apple device, asking to install a iPlugmate app (published by HooToo). Simply touch Install on your screen to install the app. This iPlugmate app is used to control transfers of all files to and from your Apple device and this RAVPower Flash Drive. Operation is simple through the app. You will need iOS 8 or later to install this application on your iPhone or iPad. To transfer files to and from your PC and the Flash drive, no additional software is needed. You simply drag and drop files using your PC File Explorer, just as you normally transfer files between folders and devices.Although this device does not come with a paper manual, it does have PDF user manual files on the Flash Drive, in multiple languages. Just use your PC file explorer to find, and open the file that is in your language. Adobe Acrobat Reader (or any PDF reader) can used to read the manuals. You will probably find that you do not need the user manual, as operation of this device is simple, but it does give a good explanation of the use of the iPlugmate app.The side of the body of the flash drive has a built-in magnet. This allows the Lightning connector to be folded back to the drive for a more compact package to carry. However, I liked the magnet to stick the flash drive to my computer case, so that I always know where it is.I used this RAVPower Flash Drive with an iPhone 5S, iPhone 7, and an iPad Air 2. I really liked that this Flash Drive has an Apple Lightning connector on the end of a flexible 2.5 inch cable. This is important. It allows you to plug the Lightning connector into your iPhone or iPad, even if you have a case on your phone or tablet. Many of thet other Lightning connector USB flash drives that I have seen have a flush Lightning connector, which will probably not plug into your phone or tablet, without removing the case. Although this is not really a problem, some cases are more difficult than others to remove and replace, and it is inconvenient. This RAVPower flash drive worked with all of the cases that I used (even my thick Otter Box Commuter case), without removing the case, including all of my thicker cases. I now have plenty of memory space on my iPhone, and still have my photos and videos readily available to use whenever I want. The Flash Drive is nicely packaged in a small very sturdy black cardboard box, with a high density foam insert which is cut out to hold the Flash Drive securely.

Great for adding phone storage capacity.

I can now plug into a power pack with the same device I use for extra storage for my iPhone.I have used the drive with an iPhone 6 and an iPad Air 2 and haven’t had any issues with either.This review is for the 32 GB version of this product.
