Great mood lighting

Good to know: If you have two or more of these, any of the remotes will control all of the light strings. This could be good thing or a bad thing for you. The remote is powerful enough to go through two walls in my house and turn the lights on and off in the other room when I don’t intend them to. I’m deducting 1 star from my rating, because they should make it clear in the product description that you cannot independently control the strings if you have multiples.Other than that issue, I love the product. I tangled one loosely up into a ball to make a hanging lamp, and it looks awesome! I stuck the remote control to the wall to use as a light switch. I love that it is dimmable for mood lighting. It produces a nice glow, not a really warm yellow, but neutral, well-balanced light. And unlike old-school string lights, it actually looks good turned off, because the wire is thin copper wire with, I presume, some very thin transparent coating.I used my Kill A Watt to measure power consumption, and on full brightness, it uses 4 watts of power, and proportionally less at lower brightness levels.Some negative reviews say that it is difficult to unravel. It took me about a minute to careful straighten it out. Not a big deal - just be patient and slow and you won’t have trouble.
