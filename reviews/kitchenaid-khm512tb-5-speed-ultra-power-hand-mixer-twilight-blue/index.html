Five Stars

Great mixer, top rated by Cook’s Illustrated.

Not too loud

I really liked the color of this hand-mixer. I wanted to buy a mixer to match my KitchenAid stand mixer and this was close enough for me. Works extremely well and is not overly loud- I mean it is a mixer so it’s going to be noisy but it’s not too loud that you can’t hear your music or have a conversation while you mix. Thumbs up from me! The only reason I didn’t give it 5 stars is the fact that it is entirely plastic. I know that KitchenAid has hand-mixers that are made of metal and I thought this was one of those. I just really like the comfort of the metal ones, I feel like that would last longer. We shall see.UPDATE:I still really like this hand mixer but I’ve realized that it doesn’t have as much power as I’d initially thought. It’s slightly annoying because it slows down my progress. Still a good mixer but I wish it was more powerful or, at least, have the more powerful mixers available in a wider range of colors. Still a decent mixer but kind of slow going.
