Cleaning and care:

  • To clean your yoga mat the best practice is to use a clean damp (only water) towel and using slight pressure, lightly clean from the top down.

  • Allow your mat to dry completely before rolling back up.

  • Don’t using a washing machine or dryer to clean your mat.

-Please keep your yoga mat away from heat sources, and avoid sharp item touching your yoga mat’s surface.
