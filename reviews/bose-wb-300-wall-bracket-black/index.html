Product Specifications

  • Designed for the sound touch 300 sound bar to bring music, movies and TV to life
  • Premium quality steel bracket provides easy wall mounting
  • Mounts flush to wall’s edge for optimal space efficiency

  • All necessary hardware and instructions are included

    Product Description

Who says your speakers can’t look as stunning as they sound-without having to sacrifice valuable room space? designed as an optional accessory for the Sound Touch 300 sound bar, this high-quality steel bracket provides unobtrusive wall mounting, so you can enjoy home entertainment that looks as stunning as it sounds. The brackets are designed to mount flush to the wall so your speakers won’t be more than 0.4in away from the wall’s edge. It mounts to all types of surfaces including masonry, wood and dry wall.

Great but contradictory

The brackets work great but the do not conform to the instructions of the Soundtouch 300. The soundtouch 300 specifically states do not place or mount the bar with less then 1⁄2 inch clearance from the rear to any opposing surface, and these brackets do not provide that. but with a small plywood spacer between the wall and the bracket that can be acheived.

I plan to paint 2 bars black to match bose brackets. Nice sleek look because soundbar sits flush with TV front w/ 1 1⁄2 space in between.
