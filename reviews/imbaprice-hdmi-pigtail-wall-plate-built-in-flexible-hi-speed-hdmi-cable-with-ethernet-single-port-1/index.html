Product Specifications

  • Includes dual HDMI pigtails on the back side
  • Works with up to two separate HDMI cables
  • Non-circuited, passive connector works with all versions of HDMI
  • Front and back female connectors no adaptors are needed

  • Insert can be removed from wall plate and used in multi-gang applications

    Product Description

Size:1 HDMI Port (White) The iMBAPrice HDMI Pigtail Wallplate is a reliable high-definition audio/video compontent manufactured to cater to your HDMI wall connecting needs. This HDMI wall plate comes in two types – One HDMI Port or Two HDMI Port Wallplate. Included with every HDMI wallplate purchase is the pigtail that makes installation easier and efficent. Our HDMI port is a non-circuited passive connector that works with all versions of HDMI. Our product works great for connecting your HDMI device to DVD players, Blu-Ray players, HDTV, and other HDMI Products.
