Product Specifications

  • Adjustable shoulder and sternal straps
  • Lateral zipper for easy access

  • PVC mesh net

    Product Description

The Mares Cruise Backpack Mesh Deluxe is your all-in-one dive travel bag. This bag can be packed for travel and then taken directly to the dive site, whether from the beach or boat. Durable PVC, nylon, and polyester construction ensure durability and longevity. The PVC net allows for water drainage from the dive site and an easy access lateral zipper means that you can easily access anything in your bag. Additionally, there is a drawstring top for access. An additional padded outer pocket is great for regulator and computer storage. The backpack straps are padded and adjustable for the perfect fit. Plus, two rubber handles allow for additional carriage as a duffel bag for quick transportation. A great feature with the deluxe mesh bag is that it is not entirely mesh, so when carrying wet gear, you have a barrier to keep you dry. The Mares Deluxe Mesh Backpack is a versatile and durable dive bag that every traveler should own.

Comfortable way to carry a lot of gear!

Bought this for a recent scuba/snorkeling trip, along with another Mares Cruise Duffle (which I am returning as we didn’t use it). This was far superior to the duffle. The mesh is the rubbery mesh which tends to be more durable than the fabric only mesh. Additionally, it holds a TON! We could easily put the following in the bag:Inside the big mesh section, which has a nice long zipper so you can load either from the side or from the top:-2 sets of fins-2 sets of boots (both with harder bottoms, so they’re a little bigger)-2 BDCs-2 regulators-PLENTY of space for additional stuff, like towels, clothes, etc.Inside the inner zipper pocket (zips at the top and is really long (the entire length of the bag):-2 snorkels (I liked this pocket so the snorkels stayed somewhat protected from all the other big gearInside the outer pouch:-2 pairs of goggles in their plastic casesAfter previously carrying one set of gear in a duffle and carrying 2 sets in this, the backpack style is SO MUCH more comfortable to carry! You don’t even really notice it’s on your back. I love this bag! It would be great for the beach or any other activity that you want the ability for the contents to air out. There are drain holes on the bottom of the main compartment as well as on the bottom of the outside pouch. It also rolls up to a fairly compact size. My only wish is that there were an additional smaller pocket to put keys or a wallet. If the goggles weren’t in the boxes, I wouldn’t want to put my keys in the same pouch to avoid scratching the goggles, so it would be nice to have a place to put a few other small things, but this is definitely not a deal breaker for me.

Nice Bag!
