MUCH SMALLER THAN APPEARS

This bag is way smaller than it appears in the photo. I’m 5’8” and it fits more like a small purse, the model they used here must be fairly petite. The arm hole is only comfortable if nothing is in the bag. I can fit my Liforme mat inside and my small block (tightly), but a bottle of water in the outside pocket is too tight…and then trying to wear the bag is extremely uncomfortable. Will be returning. :(

Good quality, nice looking, but strap not long enough

The strap is about 3 inches too short, which makes it very awkward to carry this bag. A little more length in the strap would make over the shoulder more comfortable and natural. I thought it may be because I am a larger dude (height and weight), but my girlfriend tried it and while it carried on her shoulder better than mine, she found it to be less than ideal, too.

Spacious & Holds Larger Mats With Ease

I was looking for a yoga bag to hold my yoga mat, water bottle, and towel. I have a 5mm mat that’s a little wider than most standard mats, and was happy to find that it has no problem fitting in this bag with plenty of room for my other items. Most others that I tried with my mat were too short and didn’t have any carrying space.I do find that this is awkward to carry with a larger mat in it.It’s feels like the mat is going to tumble out or that the whole thing is going to fall off my shoulder, but neither has ever happened.It gets the job done and does exactly what I wanted it to do, but the “awkward” factor keeps me from giving it a 5 star review.

Not comfortable and expensive return shipping

I wish I could return the item without having to pay $7 in shipping (that’s the cheapest option). It’s very uncomfortable because the strap kind of twists. Also if I want the pocket to be on the outside with the mat behind me, I have to wear it on my left arm. Otherwise the pocket is on the inside and isn’t easily accessible. My mat feels kinda loose and flimsy and doesn’t feel secure.Otherwise, it’s cute and the actual fabric feels like it may last a while, but I doubt I will use it too much since I wish I could return it for the price I paid for it. If it gets free prime shipping it should get free returns too.
