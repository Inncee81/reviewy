Really ruggedly made, waterproof, range scale, and built in heading readout! Clear glass what else can you want?

I have bought Feyachi products before, rifle scopes, and other shooting equipment so I knew they had good quality stuff, so I needed a good pair of hunting binos, and was willing to give these a try. WOW was I shocked by the quality of these things. Talk about tough, rugged and having some nice features I did not expect.First the rubberized coating is really nice. Every seam is sealed and watertight. The binos are supposed to be waterproof, I have not tested that feature out yet and will update when I do. What I really found nice and did not think I would like it that much was the built in compass that shows heading when looking through the objective! Makes getting your bearing really nice. These are boxed and advertised as Marine Binos but serve really well for hunting and other outdoor activities. The neck strap is wide enough to be comfortable, its not some narrow piece but wide enough not to rub into your neck. Both the eye objectives and large objectives have rubberized covers that attach to the neck strap so you will not loose them.When looking through the objective you can see the compass heading reading, and that even can have a light on it if you want. In addition, there is a gradient scale in the lens that yu can see that will allow you to judge distances pretty darn accurately, I practiced with it a bit and after about 10 minutes could judge distances pretty good, as verified by my LASER Rangefinder. Clear glass is vital in a good pair of binos and these had that.I have to say I am really impressed with the quality and features of these binos! More than I was expecting. I will be using these in the field and will post an update after a few trips to let you know if I still like them. But for now these are great and I highly recommend them!

Decent basic military binoculars

I purchased these on a whim, to get a set of fixed magnification military binoculars. So far these have been working great. The individual focus oculars have enough range on them for me to get them into focus even without my eye glasses, despite my aging eyes and strong eye Rx. Compass bearing is large and easy to read. The ranging/size scale is easy to use once one pays a bit of attention to the instructions on how to use it. The lens covers go back on easily enough with a very positive seal. Overall a decent product that should stand me a lot of good use at the range. I am quite satisfied with my purchase.
