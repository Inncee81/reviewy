Emarth Travel Telescope with Tripod & Finder Scope

I bought this Emarth Telescope as a gift for my nephew and niece. It’s light and compact in size so it works pretty well with toddlers, preschool, and older kids. This telescope does require some assembly before it can be used and it’s best to be done by adults because kids might touch and make the lens parts dirty. I think the design is pretty clever as there is a fold-able carrying bag included in the package and everything can be fit inside the bag when not used.The telescope comes with a viewfinder scope alone with the main objective lens. Two eye-pieces are included and one is 25mm while the other is 6mm (25mm offers 14.4x and 6mm offers 60x). There is a 3x Barlow lens which can further increase the magnification when needed. The viewfinder scope should be calibrated first with a closer object - align the viewfinder scope by tightening the adjustment screws around the mount. One the viewfinder scope is calibrated and the same object are observed through the viewfinder scope and main lens, we can proceed to observe other subjects, such as moon. A compact tripod is included in the package to enhance stability on most surfaces and the pan handle helps to lock the telescope in certain angle.** Pay attention to kids when they’re using the telescope so they won’t look directly into the sun as this might damage their eyes.
