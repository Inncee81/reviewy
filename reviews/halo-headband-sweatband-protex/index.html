Terrific Skull Cap Made In USA But Does Run Small

I have a few Halo Skull Caps and am pleased with the quality - As a person who is incredibly heat intolerant and sweats outside during the dog days of summer like a monsoon this particular cap made sense on multiple levels.* SPF factor of 50* Micro Mesh breathes and allows for evaporation and natural cooling* Sweat Diverter is patented by Halo and keeps perspiration out of your eyesI’m not quite sure if I really like the rubber sweat diverter, I do prefer the Halo Protex Skull cap because it’s larger and has ties which I like. These caps run very, very tight. But they’re so light and airy you’re used to the ultra snug fit in no time. As for the “sweat diverter” it might take some getting used to, but the rubber is extremely soft - however it still leaves a red mark on your forehead which takes a bit of time to disappear.Perfect for under a helmet, a bike ride, or hanging out on a hot day these really do perform as promised. They’re made in the USA and the finishing and stitch quality are superb. They may cost more than others but those issues stated above make them worthwhile for me to pay a little more.The only complaint I have is once again the small size. I’m a head shaver and this model is snug as in second skin snug, I’m not sure how guys with hair would be able to wear these in complete comfort. That said due to their popularity it appears to be no problem whatsoever.Halo products are made from their Patented “DRYLINE” materials which in addition toantimicrobial and odor elimination properties is made of 63% Nylon 23% Polyester and 14% Lycra.Remember to only hand wash or machine wash on cool settings and never use the dryer, always air dry.

Great For Mountain Biking

First off the beanie cap model is adequate but i really enjoy the self tie in back as i can adjust the fit how i want and fact that i do ton of mountain biking and the knot helps keep it from riding down my forehead in front. As for singapore i do eventually get sweat in eyes/face but not a fault to the headband.
