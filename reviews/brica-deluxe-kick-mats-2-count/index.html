Simplicity makes this product great.

Pros: It has a very simple design. It’s just a simple black mat behind your front seats. Very little in the way of fancy designs, and very little in the way of pockets, which can become really cluttery. And if you have dogs or children, those pockets become points of interest for them to grasp on. We’ve had products with the pockets before, and it never works out the way you would like as a storage space. It will become cluttery.Cons: For some people, not having pockets is a con. But for neat freaks, and people with kids, no pockets is a blessing. Another con is that, it is a universal fit, therefore, if you have a larger seat, it may not cover the edges. So be sure to measure it out first. Our seats are a bit large for it, however, we like the low profile look.

Great kick mat, Great coverage!

This kick mat is well made, great material, looks and feels durable. Fits well to the seat back, great coverage. I’m pleased with it.My only complaint (but not enough to take away a star) is the position of the lower strap. I was able to tuck the lower strap down into seat crevice so it’s pretty inconspicuous. The mat is attached to the second row captains chair of our minivan, so when we fold up and slide the seat forward to get our kids in the 3rd row seating, it can work its way up a little. I thought it would slide up higher through this process, but it has actually stayed down fairly well without me having to keep tucking it down. I think this is due to the size of the mat, with the length of it going down further to the base of the seat. If it was shorter in length, I imagine it wouldn’t be able to stay tucked into the seat crevice (which would annoy me greatly).So overall, I highly recommend this product.
