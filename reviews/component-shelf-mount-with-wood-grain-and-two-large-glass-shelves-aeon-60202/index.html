    Product Description

Component Shelf Mount with beautiful wood grain finish and smoked glass - Sleek and stylish.

This black wood grain dual DVD component wall mount shelf and bracket holds your entertainment audio / video component to the wall. Perfect to compliment your wall mounted TV. Gain back valuable floor space, eliminate wires and cables, make your entertainment center into a work of art rather than an eye-sore. Unlike other component shelves, this dual component shelf has over sized glass shelves to fit even the largest of components . Built from a beautiful black wood grain finish and extra heavy duty glass shelves to compliment your TV. The large channel behind the shelves can hide and manage many power and av cables. The extra heavy duty shelves are safety tempered and safe for even children- rated to hold 35 pounds per shelf.

Can be used for any of the following audio video components: -AV receiver- UP to 14.5” deep -DVD Player -BluRay Player -Cable / Satellite Box -PS3, Playstation -XBox -Wii -All other Audio Video Components
