Product Specifications

  • Control your TV and set-top box with app control
  • Turn on/off, change volume, or switch channels of your TV using Alexa or the smartphone app

  • Create your own soft remote - save your favorite channels in the smartphone app by naming channels reachable from a TV or set-top box. Use the predefined list of channel names, or specify your own, then enter the channel number in the app for each. Your configured channels appear as one-touch buttons in a list that sends the saved number to the TV or set-top box.
