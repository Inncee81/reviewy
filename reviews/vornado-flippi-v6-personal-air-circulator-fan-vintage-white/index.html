Product Reviews

Love the design, functionality left a lot to be desired, maybe defective?

I have a Vornado V8, which I really love. So, when I found the need for a more compact, smaller fan, this V6 model seemed like a no-brainer. I also liked the fact that this model was offered in all kinds of nifty colors. So I ordered it in the Blue Bliss, which turned out to be a perfect mach for my bedspread.I was a little surprised at just how small this model was, I expected it to be a little larger. I love the design of these Flippi fans, they are very sleek, attractive and I like the fact that they can be swiveled vertically. I loved the size, I loved the color and I loved how quiet it was on “low.” Unfortunately, that’s where the love ended. On low I found that the air flow was almost imperceptible. If I was more than a couple feet away, I felt nothing. I had purchased it to keep on a nightstand to blow across the bed. When I tried it on “high”, I was surprised at how noisy it was, with a loud rushing sound and noisy rattling. There was no way we could sleep with this running, so it’s going back. I’m hoping ours was just defective, but based on the large amount of used ones being offered in each color at a discount by Amazon Warehouse, I’m suspecting that it may be a design flaw in these V6 models. My much larger V8 moves a lot of air and does not rattle like this one.All in all, although I loved the design and features of this model fan, the functionality for our purpose was severely lacking. I would highly recommend checking out the larger V8 model if you are looking for effective air flow. If you want something very quiet. and will have it sitting on a desk blowing right into your face, then this one on set on “low” may be for you. Also, perhaps others won’t have the same noisy rattle on “high” that ours did.I rely heavily on customer reviews when I go to purchase an item, and I know others do, as well. I hope you have found the information I have provided to be helpful.

Good for cooling electronics

These fans are small and don’t move a whole lot of air but I bought three of them to keep electronic equipment cool and for that, they are great. I don’t think it would be very useful to actually cool a room but these are small enough to put in cabinets or under desks where you have a bunch of electronics just to keep the air circulating and dissipate heat. For that, they are quite nice. I think if you want a little air movement or even some white noise, these are great. But if you’re looking for something to keep you cool in a hot room, they make a larger version just like this & that would be a better choice.
