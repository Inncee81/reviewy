Installation 1. Clean the screen lightly with the wet cleaning wipe and dry cleaning wipe. 2. Peel off the backing film of the tempered glass from one end, position the screen protector over the screen, then peel off the entire backing and smooth down the protector. 3. Press the middle of the screen protector, then it will absorb the screen automatically. 4. Lightly wipe the air bubbles toward the edges with a card or your hand.
