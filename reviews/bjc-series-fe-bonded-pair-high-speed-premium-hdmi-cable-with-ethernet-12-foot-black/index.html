    Product Description

Series-FE Premium HDMI Cable, 12 foot: Certified to 18 Gbps for full-bandwidth 4K performance

HDMI version 2.0 increased the bandwidth of HDMI to a maximum of 18 Gbps, to allow high-framerate 4K video. Conventional high-speed testing, however, tests cables only out to 10.2 Gbps; HDMI Licensing’s new Premium HDMI Cable certification tests all the way to the 18 Gbps limit, and Blue Jeans Cable was among the first cable manufacturers to meet this exacting standard.

As a Premium HDMI Cable, this cable comes with the official Premium HDMI label – beware of imitators who call their cables “premium” but whose products do not bear the scannable, validated Premium sticker.

Cable Stock made in the USA

Our HDMI cable is the only mass-production HDMI cable made of American-made cable stock (cutting and connectorization is done for us overseas), produced to our design by Belden in Indiana and Kentucky. This cable incorporates Belden’s unique bonded-pair technology for extremely low skew and return loss.

Supports EVERY feature of HDMI

You’ll see a lot of feature lists with cables; but the Premium HDMI Cable certification covers everything – if a feature, resolution, framerate or protocol of any type is permitted under the HDMI spec, it is supported by this cable. This includes:

  • Deep Color (10 bit, 12 bit, or 16 bit, or as they are sometimes referred to, 30 bit, 36 bit or 48 bit);

  • xvYCC and other colorspaces;

  • 4:4:4 color and all other color encodings and samplings;

  • all sound formats including DTS-HD and TrueHD Dolby;

-all resolutions including 4k x 2k, 3D, 1080p, etc.

-Audio Return Channel;

-All framerates (e.g., 4k/60fps);

  • Ethernet over HDMI.
