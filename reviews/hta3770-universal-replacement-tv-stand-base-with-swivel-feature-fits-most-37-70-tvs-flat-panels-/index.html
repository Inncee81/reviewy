Product Reviews

Fantastic stand, easy to assemble

I was really dreading putting this together as we have always used the stands that come with the TV. We just purchased a 65” Vizio M series and the included feet are too wide for our entertainment center. Also the display was too low for our soundbar to sit in front of it. This stand solved both problems. It was super easy to put together by myself. The directions are diagrams only sort of like IKEA if you have ever put their furniture together. All of the parts are very clearly labeled and all of the tools needed are included. The base is very good looking and swivels easily. The TV does not tilt forward as others have mentioned, even on the highest setting, and I had no issues getting it level. I didn’t even check to make sure it was level until it was completely assembled. I’m not sure if put together correctly how it wouldn’t be.

Excellent value, very easy to assemble except wrong bolt size

Overall this product is excellent … it looks nice, is sturdy and is well priced compared to other options. Unless you can find something significantly cheaper, I would not try to save $5 or $10 on an alternative. I would concur with everything said by Ray Diggins in his review on 9/9/2016. A few things to add:* I too could not use the included bolts to attach my TV to the stand. They only provide two sizes of bolts and if you have purchased wall mounts or other similar products, they sometimes include a dozen different sizes. Be prepared to go to the hardware store and work your way through finding a bolt that fits unless you happen to have them already. Do take note that they include square washers which I found to be essential for holding the bolts to the frame – the holes on the frame are bigger than any bolt head I could find so this was a vital piece the manufacturer included* The base of the stand is made of tempered glass. I had expected plastic so this was a minor surprise. It’s attractive so its not a bad surprise. The post from the base to the frame is made of plastic.* Despite the bolt problem, I give them a solid five stars because they had possibly the easiest to follow instructions I’ve ever seen for this type of assembly. I’ve attached a picture but kudos to the manufacturer for actually labelling all of the parts instead of forcing you to guess which one is which. This saved a ton of time.* The only confusing part of the instructions is there are two rounded triangular brackets that attach to the upper pole to hold the frame. The angle they show on the instructions makes it easy to misunderstand where they go. Also, be sure to note the direction the base is pointed in the instructions to be sure you put those triangular brackets facing the right way (they face towards the front of the stand).I am very pleased with this purchase. For reference, I use this with a 42” Phillips LCD TV 4000 series. Many other reviews report using this successfully with much larger TVs and I can see why.
