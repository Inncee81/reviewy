Awesome Lens Hood.

The Neewer Lens Hood works great. It fits my Canon zoom EF 75-300 mm and my EFS 18-55 mm lenses. It was very easy to snap on and remove. I found out that is does in fact does help prevent glare and ghost spots when taking pictures and you do see a deeper picture. Although I’m new to photography, I do believe this would be great for the photographer in your live. It’s small design fits easy into my camera backpack. If you don’t have a large case that has individual pockets for storing, all you have to do is turn the lens hood around over the camera lens for easier storage. I think for the price it’s a great deal.
