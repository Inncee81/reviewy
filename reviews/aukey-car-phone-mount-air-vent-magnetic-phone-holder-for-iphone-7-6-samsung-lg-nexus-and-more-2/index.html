Product Specifications

Fast Setup Easily install the mount in seconds. Put the one of or both of the included metal discs between your phone and phone case or adhere directly to the back of your device.  

Driving Necessity Faster, easier phone access, more accessible GPS and music controls – This magnetic phone mount duo puts your phone at your fingertips without the hassle of expanding cradles, flimsy docks, or unreliable suction cups.
