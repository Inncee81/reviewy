Great glove!

Great glove. I purchased this for my 8 year old. I noticed that the glove he was using was really too small and there was no picket for the ball to land in. My husband has never played baseball and purchased that smaller glove. After my brother came to one of my son’s baseball games he said we should get my son an 11 or 12 inch glove and it would make all the difference in his fielding. My brother has coached his 14 year old son’s team since t ball, so I listened to him. Right out of the box my son took it outside with his bounce board and was able to catch 90% of the rebounded balls. He was pretty excited and loves this glove. The glove itself is soft, and will require minimal break in time. It has a great, deep pocket and the inside is comfy on the hand.We have a game tomorrow so we will see how it goes on the field!
