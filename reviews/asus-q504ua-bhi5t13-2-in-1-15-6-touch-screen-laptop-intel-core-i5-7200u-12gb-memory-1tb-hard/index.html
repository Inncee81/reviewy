Not worth the trouble

I’ve had this computer for less than 6 months and it frequently spools up to 100% CPU use even when not using very many programs (usually internet with maybe 5 tabs open, none streaming voice/video; 1-2 MS Office programs; and iTunes). Contacted Asus and their only solutions were to send it in for repair or install a driver they provided. However, when I started to install the driver it was older than whatever version was already on the laptop, so I didn’t install. Since this is my work laptop and I don’t have another computer available, sending it off for 3+ weeks for repair is not an option. Will probably look for a new laptop very soon.
