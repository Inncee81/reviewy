I create portable stereos for Different people. Some are the beach and some for off grid living. One of my issues is compact size. This unit blew me away with the size. The deck its self is normal standard size but the depth is nearly one third the size. One thing to Note is this is a very light weight unit. It can do most of the standard powering. It can handle bass and subs very well on its won but it doesn’t have the strength to go all out.This would make a great starter deck or a fast replacement for an old car that needed an upgrade. For this cost you cant beat it. This latest project i call stereo in a box. Its a chest with mounted speakers, solar panels, car deck, solar charger with outputs for external speakers aka subs. There is more to it than that but i needed a stereo.The quality of audio on these even hooked up to 2 flat screen TV speakers sounds wonderful. Perfect bass i tested each speaker line. One thing to note there is no custom EQ and the presets are not that great the classical and Rock seem sot be the only usable settings rest not so much if at all.The Bluetooth setting linked to my phone within seconds and it remembered my phone so i didn’t have to connect each time i turned the car on. The aux port was a bit stiff plugin in but i have had head units that needed a break in. The display is bright and clear. the key buttons light up and the configuration for the presets are easy to set with a simple press and hold.The wires are as basic and simple as they come. Some times its confusing what color is what they mark it with numbers and show pictures as to what position each number is. What i like is they grouped them front left front right rear left rear right so its really easy to know which is which if your new to this. This deck also has very minimal battery drain. i had it playing off a small 12 volt battery for roughly 6 hours and barely scratched the bank.I’m not a car guy so i cant really tell you much more than this. Its basic its well made and very lightweight. If you want something simple to install and replace and old unit with a nice look this is for you. All in all i like it and this is going in my list of company’s to buy from for my next builds. If anything changes i will let you know till than have a great day.
