Nice SMB for the price

With the low price of this smb everyone should get one. The material is tough and the mouthpiece works fine. It rolls up compact enough for it to not be in the way during a dive. It’s hard to justify the more expensive brand name SMBs when there are tough and functional markers at this price

It isn’t one to use frequently

I am not a fan of this marker. It is a little too narrow. It is only blow up. When trying to use the air it just doesn’t work well. Once blown up it bends way too much because it is so narrow. So the slightest wave or wind does make it hard to work with. Since it only blows up on surface I can not use it during my safety stop to set myself up ahead of time.
