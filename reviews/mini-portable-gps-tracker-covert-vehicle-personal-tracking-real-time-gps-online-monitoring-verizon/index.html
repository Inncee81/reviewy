    Product Description

As of this moment, there’s only one personal level GPS tracker that’s capable of functioning on America’s Most Reliable Network. This brand-new GPS tracking device uses the CDMA network to transmit data, the largest wireless telecommunications service in the United States, keeping up to 1 year of historical data. The new design of the BlackHawk 2.0 allows less hassle with even more productivity. There’s no SIM card that can be damaged or remove to render your device useless, while the new shape and plugin layout improves weather resistance. Supporting geo-fences, power-on and power-off reports, low power alarms, SOS alarm, special alarms based on internal 3-axis accelerometer, and much more. The improved internal li-polymer battery allows up to two weeks of battery life with constant 1 minute updates and can be recharged with any regular Mini USB charger. This device is only available from Datablaze. We are currently in development of accessories for this device.
