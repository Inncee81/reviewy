There’s no way this is chafe free.

I have ridden, and still ride, PI shorts for the last five years. This is the first product that I’ve rated so low and simply cannot recommend all because of the chamois. It’s like foam core padding that irritates my skin to the touch.I washed them once and then proceeded on a regular 35 miles ride. In the beginning they really stayed in place. The pourous chammy really gripped my skin. As long as everything stayed in place it was good. Once I started getting out of the saddle things got much worse. If anything shifted around I’d get an immediate discomfort from the coarse material. Ultimately I took a shortcut home and went at a slower pace. I cannot fathom how this product was deemed acceptable.I’d seriously recommend not buying or using this product. Their previous chammois were great and this is a complete 180.

These bib shorts were somewhat small to begin with (both compared to their own sizing chart as well as to other brands). The chamois’s stitching does not extend over the edge of the chamois and as such tends to move around and roll up creating a seam. I wouldn’t recommend these and will not be purchasing PI shorts again.
