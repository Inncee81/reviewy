    Product Description

Box fits the Bushnell Trophy Cam HD Aggressor Model 119774c and 119776c The box is manufactured with All Steel Construction to provide exact camera fit. This allows camera to be taken in and out and still point back to the same spot. (Unlike Competitors Shoe Box Design). This box is powder coated with A Camo Break-up paint applied. The CAMLOCKbox security box helps to protect your camera from natures elements as well as stops animals from chewing on your trail camera. This is for box only. Camera and Cable are not included.
