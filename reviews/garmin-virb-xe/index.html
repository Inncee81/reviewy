Product Specifications

  • G-METRIX-Built-in GPS and external sensors with connectivity to Garmin devices to capture performance data like speed, elevation, heart rate and G-force
  • ADVANCED VIDEO - Capture thrilling footage with high definition 1080p resolution at 60 frames-per-second, or capture up to 240 frames-per-second slow motion
  • BURST CAPABILITY - The VIRB X and VIRB XE offer a burst shooting mode that will capture several photographs in quick succession so that you don’t miss the action.
  • WATERPROOF - Waterproof to 50 meters without a case, allowing control of cleaner and clearer video and audio. Weather proof contacts allow for rugged charging and powering

  • BLUETOOTH AUDIO - VIRB X and VIRB XE can connect directly to wireless Bluetooth headsets or microphones to capture audio in high definition

    Product Description

Style:With Image Stabilization (1080p / 60fps)  |  Product Packaging:Standard Packaging Garmin VIRB XE performance action camera shoots high definition footage at 1080p/60fps. Truly built for rough conditions, VIRB XE provides gyro based imagestabilization and stunning clear 12MP photos at up to 30fps. The rugged compact camera fits every activity and all conditions with a waterproof rating of 50meters, with no case needed. Use the VIRB App on your smart phone for real-time viewing while recording, remote control and instant share functions. G-Metrixin the camera and from compatible Garmin devices and sensors create true excitement by adding animated gauges and graphs to your video with action data likespeed, g-force and other performance data.

Keeping it at 5 stars since battery issues seem to be a problem for a lot of cameras and the end product with this thing is still great.ORIGINAL REVIEW:I am very impressed with this camera. I have taken it out a couple of times already and have not had any difficulty getting a GPS lock, exactly what I would expect from one of the leaders in GPS equipment. The video it shot in 1080p at 30fps was sharp and putting the gauges on the video was super easy using the free software. I came from a contour camera to this and the difference is night and day. Didn’t require that I format my SD card and just uploaded my video with intuitive editing software. Very nice and a massive change from contour, which hit or miss would even recognize the camera! The only con so far is that the little quick start manual is pretty useless. It takes a little bit of toying around with it to figure out all the tricks this compact thing can perform. Looking to putting it through the tests on the mountain bike and hopefully in the water soon. Unlike the other reviewer, I was able to find all the mounts I needed for this quickly on Amazon and Garmin’s website. Will update as I get a bit more experience with it!

By far the best camera on the market

This is by far the best POV camera I have ever gotten. I started using POV for skiing and sailing 10 years ago before the GoPro was even a thing. Recently my GPS enabled camera broke and I was looking around for a better version and was refusing to get a GoPro. When I came across the Garmin Virb I was skeptical at first because it is not exactly their field. Fear not, this self contained camera does everything and more. Having a built in waterproof and shock resistant case makes me feel safe using it in the worst of conditions. The one button on and record is great for knowing exactly when it is recording without the necessity of looking for a red light. The GPS is very accurate and is quick to connect with the satellite connections. The software that comes with the camera matches the great quality of the camera itself. The desktop editing software makes it easy to import telemetry from the camera and other Garmin devices, making for quick editing and posting of your favorite clips. The app is also great for seeing both live footage and recorded footage. Great for showing your friends on a lift ride up the previous run you just did. Battery life is surprisingly good for constantly running a GPS, plus I bought some external batteries that are easy to swap in mid run. It is convenient that Garmin chose to stick with the universal mounting system that GoPro made because when you finally replace your GoPro with the Garmin Virb you can still keep all the same mounts.

Great images and other data

Great video images. I love the GPS/location capture aspect and other G-metric data that can be captured from the vehicle. Camera is rugged and has good battery life. While I also got the power cable, I haven’t needed to use it when doing various video runs. Virb software is user friendly and works very well for editing the video clips and applying a wide range of G-metric data in numerous formats. I prefer this to my GoPro Hero 4 when recording vehicle operations.
