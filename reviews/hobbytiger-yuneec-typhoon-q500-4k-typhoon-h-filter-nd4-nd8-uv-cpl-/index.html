These lens filters are designed for Yuneec Typhoon Q500 4K and Typhoon H. They can be used for Typhoon Q500 4K CGO3 camera and Typhoon H CGO3+ Camera. CPL filter reduces light reflections which increases color contrast and saturation of the photos. ND filters are must-haves under high light conditions, since they diminish the amount of light that goes into the sensor, leading to wider apertures and longer exposure time; avoid overexposed photos caused by bright sunlight to have better color contrast and richer color. ND8 filter films in brighter sunlight situation than ND4 filter does. UV filter prevents UV light from entering the lens, reducing blurring.
