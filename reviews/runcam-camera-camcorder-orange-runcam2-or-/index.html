Great little camera

Great picture quality, I have the GoPro Hero 3 and this one and neither one have a great interface however this is easy to use. I was really worried that I would have to mount it upside down and then rotate every one of my quad videos in post however if you connect it to the wifi app and rotate it it will stay rotated outside of the app which is great for being able to mount it button side down and still access them easily. One of the downfalls is that it does not come with a gopro mount which I thought it did. I mounted it to a rubber mechanical gimbal via velcro and it has already popped off three times during a rough landing and has survived all of them. The other drawback is that while it does have a mini lens hood it does not have an optional glass filter so the lens is braving all of the elements on it’s own. As far as the wifi connection it doesn’t have much range but no wifi does, however when in that range it works great. I think the GoPro 3 does have a slightly better image quality but they are both comparable and great and if you only owned one of them you would be happy either way although I get the impression this one can take a bit more of a hit caseless than the GoPro. It is slim, lightweight and works great-I thought I would use it for FPV however I have since bought board cams so I never had the opportunity to test out the latency but there are tons of youtube videos out there-I use mine for on board recording and sending my big 450mm up for aerials although I am much more into 250’s racers at the moments so it occasionally goes along for the ride on them. As a side note I did dump an xk x350 on it’s back at incredibly high speed (for those unfamiliar that is a very powerful inverted flyer) with the runcam attached and it is still going strong.

Very impressed with Runcam

The good:- Full HD, rivals GoPro!- Weight and size- Comes with a matching mount that can be zippy-tied to your frame / gimbal- Comes with many cables, including a composite video out (piping mine straight to a 600mw VTX!)The bad:- If it’s unplugged from a USB, even after “eject”ing, the cam locks up and the battery removed to get it back- If running solely on external power (no battery, as an FPV cam), will lose the video files when power is cut if it isn’t first turned off using the power button- WiFi range sucks. Do yourself a favor and get a 5.8GHz transmitter/receiver to use this as an FPV cam

Works well for my miniquad, I needed to adjust the focus

Better form factor than a gopro or Yi for miniquads. Bitrate is good, but firmware is not config customizable. Only complaint I have is the lens is not focused for distance (the intended use of this cam) , but this seems to be common among most Chinese manufactured action cams. Be ready to take out 6 small screws, take a razor blade to the adhesive around the lens screw and take some sample video/photos with the lens twisted to different locations. A small mark with a sharpie helps keep track of the best focus location while testing. I turned my lens about 20 degrees clockwise to get good distance focus. Color saturation is high, but I like the results. All these cams are pretty good at this point.
