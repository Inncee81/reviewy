Different size gear/prop attachment, but a perfect replacement.

4 stars only because the replacement gears are a different size. Otherwise, I’ll be keeping a few of these handy, as they are very handy. Funny enough, these are smaller, so you shouldn’t have a problem using props that fit either gear… in theory. I’ll have to see. I’m a much more aggressive flyer, so I’ll find out soon enough. The new motor pods are doubly nice, as they get banged up quickly. If they came with the rubber feet on the bottom, that would be perfect; but I’m not complaining.Since the props are made quite well, they will swap out with your stock ones no problem. I know it seems backwards that cheap toys will have varying quality in part suppliers, but there are plenty of crappy blades out their. I’ll likely be buying this particular pack until I can’t find it any more. Perfect price and value all in one.
