You really can fit it all in!

This is a great case for protection of your 5” GPS. It fits both the Garmin Nuvi 55 and the Magellan 5045. It holds all the cords and plugs including the suction cup for the windshield mount. For placement of the windshield mount in the case, put the suction cup flat end between the cardboard divider and the hard side of the case. The ball of the suction cup mount will rest between the felt covered hard cardboard divider and the fishnet pocket. If necessary, put a slit in the fishnet netting so the other half of the suction cup will fit in there without resistance. The fit is snug but nothing sloshes around and everything is protected. You can zip the case shut.
