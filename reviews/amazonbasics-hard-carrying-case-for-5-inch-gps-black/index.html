Disappointed in the Quality

Very disappointed in the quality of this fabric coated cardboard like case. It is not hard at all and does not offer much protection for my Garmin nuvi 55LMT GPS. The good part is that both cords fit into the case with the GPS unit, suction cup mount does not (didn’t expect it to), but the case is very cheaply made. I could’ve bought a similar one from Hong Kong for half the price, but would have it to wait a month for it. It’s no big deal and I’m going to keep it, but I just wanted warn others who would want something more substantial. Prior to the purchase, I was looking at the Caseling Universal Electronics/Accessories Hard Travel Carrying Case, which is a bit more expensive, but looks to be better made. I would’ve bought it if I knew in advance of the poor quality the Amazon Basics Case was.

Perfect home for my Garmin 1450LMT. <3

This case is everything I hoped it would be. I’ve had my GPS for a few months now, but put off buying it a case since they were all really pricy. $19 for a SOFT case? No thanks. This AmazonBasics case has been out of stock for a long time, but I waited for it to become available since they were charging $10 for a hard case. It came back into stock for $8 and change! When I first ordered it, all I was hoping for was a case that could comfortably hold my Garmin 1450LMT. It holds my GPS in its little pocket and has room for the charger as well. Note: don’t expect a hard plastic, completely crush-proof case. It does have a little give when smushed together. Unless you’re lugging it around at the bottom of your tool box, you should be good to go. Well done, AmazonBasics. Well done. <3

Does what it needs to do

I purchased this AmazonBasic GPS case when I ordered a new GPS unit. When I first got the case I didn’t like it - I thought it was much too big. The reason for that, the case I was using for my previous GPS unit was sized to fit just the display unit. This new case is made so that you can also store the power cord in it. With my previous GPS unit it didn’t matter what power cord I used with it, so I just kept cords in the different vehicles. But the new unit I purchased, it has a traffic alert function and the power cord that came with the unit is the antenna for that. So I now need to take that cord with the display when I transfer to a different car. This new case does a good job of holding both. There is a pocket for the display unit and plenty of room for the coiled cable - even with it’s large power plug. I like that the sides of the case is made of a stiff material. I believe this AmazonBasic case was a good deal for the price I paid.
