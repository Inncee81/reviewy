Big enough to protect the tablet yet small enough to allow full use of the screen

I purchased the Cambio case for my granddaughter. She needed a case that protected her tablet from damage if she dropped it. The case is designed to be used as a stand for the tablet or as a holder for both the tablet and keyboard. The keyboard can be attached to the tablet while it is in the case. It comes with a bonus stylus; there is a handy place to attach it to the case. She has full access to the screen and side buttons. This has a magnetic closure making it very easy for a child to open and shut. The front of the case opens to slide the tablet in. It is held closed with Velcro so things don’t slip out of place. This is a very nice looking case; the pink color is deep and rich looking. She is very happy with it.
