Product Specifications

  • Fits 51”-70” TV’s
  • Holds Up To 125lbs

    Product Description

The Sanus 51”-70” Tilting Flat Panel Mount gives you the best view from the floor or couch. Fingertip tilt allows for easy TV adjustments, and the mount tilts the TV back for easy cable access. The TV can slide from side-to-side for perfect positioning on the wall, and a safety tab provides a safe and easy connection you can hear.

Best TV mount I’ve owned to date! Very happy with Sanus.

Credentials: DIYer who’s mounted about 10 TVs now.My first Sanus mount. WELL worth the extra money. Build quality is superb with exception attention to detail. The back bracket itself is sexy, with a clever way to conceal the [wall] mounting hardware. Post hanging leveling is fantastic.For larger TVs that require 6”+ of horizontal off-centering (due to the stud placement and desired TV placement), having a unibody wall bracket is critical to ensuring a level platform for hanging the TV. I’ve had various, cheaper mounts that require assembly (multiple pieces) and the result is always a not perfectly flat (or level) base for hanging a TV.Minor complaints:- For larger TVs (say 70” and higher) it is difficult to access the tilt tension adjustment knobs and leveling screws (depending on TV mounting pattern and position).- Outlet plate clearance: if the wall plate is immediately above an outlet and the desired tv placement dictates one of the hangers to be directly over the place, the hander WILL come in contact with the plate. This causes a slight yaw on the TV, also complicating leveling.
