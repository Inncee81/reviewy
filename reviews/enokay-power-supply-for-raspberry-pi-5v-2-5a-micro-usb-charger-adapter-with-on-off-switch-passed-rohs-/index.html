Defective cable

The unit that I received did not allow enough current draw for my Raspberry Pi 3 to even think about starting to boot. No LED activity on the board at all. I thought for a while that my brand new Pi was defective after I had used this cord to try charging another device, and could connect/disconnect that with the switch on this cable. It turns out that that device drew a much lower current, so I found another cheap microUSB cable, and the Pi booted up just fine. At least the power supply seems to work just fine. Caveat emptor.
