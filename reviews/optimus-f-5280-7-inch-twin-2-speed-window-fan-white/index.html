    Product Description

Optimus 7 inch twin window contains a 2-speed rain resistant motor for efficient air delivery. Fan is manually reversible to pull cool air in or push stale air out and both sides contain speed control. Fan includes built-in slide screens to close in extra space on either side of the fan. Includes a carrying handle for easy transport and installation, plus feet for free standing operation. Fan comes fully assembled and ready to use, no tools required for installation. Safety grill to protect fingers from fan blades.

Fan is a bit noisy, and the air output is low. However, on low breeze days, it does pull some cool air into a 10’ x 14’ x 8’H room. Unfortunately, that air doesn’t travel very far from the window; therefore, I need a second small fan to circulate the air about the room. My setup is: One Optimus at the top of the window (exhaust) , another at bottom (Intake) with a small circular fan in front of it.
