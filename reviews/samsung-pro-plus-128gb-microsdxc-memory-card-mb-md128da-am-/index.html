Fast, durable, and all around fantastic

I purchased this card for my Galaxy S7 Edge. My phone quickly ran out of space with my 16GB SD card so I needed more capacity. The write speeds on this card are fantastic. I noticed a speed improvement over my old SD card which was a plus. I’d recommend checking your devices to see if they can utilize the full 90 MB/s. A lot of times the bottleneck lies in the device and paying extra for this SD card isn’t worth it. The durability of this card is also great and gives me piece of mind. If money is no issue than I would recommend this card hands down. If you are looking for a cheaper option with the same amount of space than you can get the Samsung Evo or a different card for cheaper and still work just fine.Writing in-depth reviews to help other shoppers has become a hobby of mine.
