Revo VRS-LANC Wired Remote Control for Camcorders with LANC Terminal

  • Limited 1-Year Manufacture Warranty

The Revo VRS-LANC Wired Remote Control for Camcorders with LANC Terminal allows you to control zoom, start/stop recording, take photos, and power on/off compatible camcorders from up to 5’ away. It connects via a LANC connector and features an integrated clip for attaching to your tripod’s pan handle for quick and convenient access while shooting. The VRS-LANC is compact, lightweight, and easily stores in your camera bag (sold separately).

Four-Button Remote Control The wired remote lets you start and stop video, snap photos, zoom in and out, and turn your camcorder on and off.

Two-Step Zoom When used with camcorders that support the function, the VRS-LANC can control zoom at two speeds. Press the zoom button halfway to zoom slowly, and press it down all the way for fast zooms.

LANC Connector The VRS-LANC offers compatibility with most camcorders that have a LANC terminal.

Built-In Clip The built-in clip attaches the remote to you tripod’s pan handle. The oversized design provides extra leverage when opening the clip, and interior nonslip rubber pads keeps the remote securely in place.

Power and Recording Indicator LEDs Separate LEDS indicate when your camcorder is turned on and recording. The power LED glows green when your camcorder turns on, and the recording LED glows red when shooting video.

No Batteries Required The VRS-LANC is powered by your camcorder, without the need for separate batteries.

Connector: LANC

Cable Length: 5’ (1.5 m)

Dimensions: 1.6 x 1.1 x 0.8” (4.0 x 2.7 x 2.0 cm)

Weight: 1.7 oz (47 g)
