    Product Description

Introducing amFilm Premium Anti-Glare/Anti-Fingerprint (Matte) Screen Protectors for your Fire 7 2015.

amFilm Premium Anti-Glare/Anti-Fingerprint Protectors are made from superior quality 3-layer PET film, and specifically designed to fit your Fire 7 2015 device. The Anti-Glare top coating not only reduce the glare but also reduce the fingerprints from daily use. In addition it effectively protects the screen from daily scratches, dust and scrapes. With a self-adhering surface, the screen protectors are designed for easy application and zero-to-minimal bubbles during application. A squeeze card is also included to remove the occasional bubble easily.

Works with: Fire 7 2015

Contains: 2xScreen Protector , Micro-Fiber Cleaning Cloth, Plastic Squeeze Card, Hinge/Dust Removal Stickers. Color: Anti-Glare/Anti-Fingerprint Application: Each of the screen protectors contains 3 layers of PET film. The first and last films are mask films, where the stickers are affixed. The actual screen protector for your device is the film in the middle. Please use the stickers to remove the mask films (NOT the stickers themselves).
