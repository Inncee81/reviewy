Really nice tool kit for a Parrot Bebop 3.0, but lacks a case.

I really like this tool kit for my Parrot Bebop 3.0. All of the pieces are well made and work perfectly with my Bebop. The only reason I’m not giving this item 4 stars is that it doesn’t come with any kind of case to hold the tools, other than the foam pocket/ tray that holds the tools. If this tool kit came with some kind of a holder/box for the foam pocket/tray it would be an even more outstanding item for anyone.
