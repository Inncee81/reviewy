Product Reviews

Just what I needed

I’ve been buying these same grips for years and they did not disappoint this time either. Great grip and perfect pre-cut length. Plus the white color helps to know visibly when it’s time to change your grip tape. I think too often people continue to play on grips that need to be replaced, simply because their grip tape is so dark that they don’t see how bad it is. Would recommend.

… I didn’t put an overgrip on it because I liked how much the original felt

So when I first got my racquet I didn’t put an overgrip on it because I liked how much the original felt. After the wear and tear, I had to eventually and luckily this overgrip feels even better. It also last a pretty long time.

Easy choice, no contest.

Simply said, these grips are the best ones I’ve used. I like them better than those overly-advertised light blue brand, because they didn’t last as long as these do. Plus, I like the white color and the fact it comes with 3 of them in a single pack, so it’s convenient to carry around extras in my tennis bag to use if needed. I still haven’t had to change the first one and it’s been months, and I play heavily, so they are definitely high quality. I tend to find Wilson brand usually makes the best grips as well as rackets.

Good, But Only “Over”grip

I didn’t realize an overgrip goes over a regular grip. I pulled off the old grips and used the overgrip, which is pretty thin. Otherwise, it is good quality and I would buy these again with replacement regular grips to use correctly.

Doesn’t last long

Color was bright neon pink, which wasn’t terrible but quite different from the photo. The real problem I had with this overgrip is that, after about 2 months of playing every weekend the grip started ripping like a sponge. The neon pink also becomes a lot uglier than other colors when it gets dirty. Would not buy again

Wilson Pro Overgrip is the best I’ve tried.

Comparing the Wilson Pro overgrip to the Head Super Comp, I much prefer this Wilson. It has a nice tacky grip so the racket is less likely to spin and shift in your hands. This is the one I’ll be buying from now on.

Good Grip

This is very pleasing to hold, it very soft but has a really good grip. Make sure your hands are clean when using this, it can get dirty but it’s fine, there’s three in the package. These last long.

Good product and good price

Delivered as expected; protects handle effectively with good grip. I don’t play any better (sadly), but at least I am more confident. :)
