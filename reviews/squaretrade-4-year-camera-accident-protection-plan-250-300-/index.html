I am disappointed in my Square Trade experience.

I have several square trade plans based on the interview of others. Until recently I never actually had to use one. I purchased a camera last fall, and used it on one vacation. Over the holidays, the shutter button began to malfunction. Square Trade directs you to the manufacturer first to use their warranty and if you do, they will cover the repaired product or a replacement. The trouble is, you may not receive from the manufacturer what you want, which is what happened to me. They are replacing my camera with another model that has features I rejected when I originally chose my purchase. Though Square Trade will resume the warranty, I have something I do not desire. AFTER I incurred the cost to send the camera back, and after several conversations with Square Trade, they told me if I said the camera was damaged accidentally in the first place, they would have refunded my money and I could have purchased that same model and then a new plan. Since I was honest in admitting that it appeared to be a defect (I did not drop or mishandle the item) they put me through the harder repair process. I am very disappointed and advise anyone who does use them to ask very carefully about this potential problem. Not everyone purchases the latest models of technology; sometimes an older model has better features. Finally, I will say their “call you back” program is excellent as long as you are not waiting for the “claims” people. Customer service is quick and prompt, but claims is a whole separate area and they are not fast or easy to deal with.
