Product Specifications

  • Adjustable height shelves
  • Each shelf measures 14 in. wide x 10 in. deep
  • The tempered glass shelves support up to 17 pounds (8kg) each
  • Ideal for cable boxes, DVD players, speakers, and game consoles

  • Cable management system hides wires from view

    Product Description

Size:3 Tier This three-shelf component wall mount is ideal for placement of cable/satellite boxes, set-top receivers, DVD players, game consoles, and/or other AV equipment without the need for bulky furniture. Its riser columns (the part of the shelf that mounts against the wall) feature cable-management ridges to help hide the wiring in your system. The riser is crafted so that the shelves can be mounted at almost any height. The shelves measure approximately 14” wide, 10” deep, and extend out 11-1⁄4” from the wall at their deepest points when installed. The shelves are 3⁄16” thick glass, tempered for extra strength, and secure safely to the sturdy aluminum riser columns. This sleek, modern looking cantilever design allows for use in smaller spaces or in A/V closets. Simple assembly using included hardware set. Certifications: RoHS, CE & ISO-9001. Complete mount shelf package includes component shelves, installation instructions, and hardware.
