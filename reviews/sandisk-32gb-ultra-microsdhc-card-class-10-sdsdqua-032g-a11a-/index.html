Works on my Samsung Galaxy S4

This card has word flawlessly so far on my device. I’ve had it for over a month, and there have been no issues.My device: Samsung Galaxy S4Model: SGH-M919Android OS version: 4.3 Jelly BeanCarrier: T-MobileI read that there were problems with early batches of this memory card, so I called SanDisk ahead of time and asked if they could confirm whether this listing is from one of the bad batches. They said they could not determine that without the serial number, but told me that if I purchase this memory card on Amazon they would replace it free of charge if it was from one of the defective batches. Of course, I didn’t want to risk storing a bunch of data on a bad memory card only to find out the hard way (with lost/corrupted data) that it was from a bad batch, so SanDisk said I could call them up and give them the serial number and they could tell me if it was from a bad batch. When it came in the mail, I called SanDisk, gave them the serial number, and they confirmed it wasn’t from a known bad batch.If you want to play it safe like I did and call them up with the serial number, just be warned that the serial number printed on the back of the card is in VERY small print. I consider myself to have very good eyesight and it still took me a minute to figure out what it said while trying to hold it a just the right angle to catch the light. So if you’ve got less than perfect eyesight, you might need a magnifying glass if you want to read the serial number.
