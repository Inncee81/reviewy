Problem Solver for GoPro battery charging

Perhaps you may use you GoPro Camera to charge your battery? I did so myself until one day my camera would no longer turn on or charge the battery. I did some research online to see if this was some kind of defect or to find a way to troubleshoot the problem. I found a video on YouTube that identified precisely what the cause was and how to fix it. It appears that when the battery voltage drops to 2.85V, the battery won’t charge using the camera. The solution was to attach bare wires directly from an charging adapter to bring to voltage back to 2.87V which would allow for charging with the GoPro camera. I suggest buying a charger such as this one, it solved all my battery and charging issues. Works just as expected!
