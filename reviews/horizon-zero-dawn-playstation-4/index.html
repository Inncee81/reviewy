This Game is Consuming MY LIFE!!!!

When you think about a game all day at work or when you lay down to go to sleep it makes me compelled to write something in the review thread about it. I’ve never wrote a review about a game until now, yet I base my purchases largely off of the Amazon reviews/comments, etc. I want to start of saying how I am also a very narrow minded gamer, I admit it. Superior open world gaming with an intriguing storyline.

Omigosh, what a great game!! Great open world environment with plenty of options on game play. At first, I wasn’t sure I’d be crazy about the fact that Aloy’s (our brave female protagonist here) main weapon, and the one she must prove very proficient at, is one or more bows using arrows with variant payloads (fire, shock, etc.), but as the player progresses, you get more and more comfortable using the bow(s), and it is soon like your hand, it just feels natural using it. There are player choices in reacting to NPC’s, and you can progress in your own way to some extent, though there are main quests that need to accomplished if the player is to progress in earning skill points so you can improve Aloy’s traits in the built-in skill tree. The deadly machines become more and more challenging to kill, and the mystery surrounding Aloy’s origin and background, which is slowly developed through the complicated but intriguing storyline makes the player want to know more. I’m about 28% through the game now, and won’t put it away until I’ve completed it. Good for hours and hours of outstanding gameplay; highly recommended. Hope they will later offer some add-on DLC’s to keep a good thing going.
