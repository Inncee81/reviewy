The plastic part on the wrist mount broke, maybe due to over tightening thought. The suction cup mount holds very tight to windows and car hoods. The elastic parts on the head and chest mount seem to be a very good quality. The mounts remained very tight and sturdy throughout the use. I use the tripod mount probably the most. and seems to be working fine. I was surprised to see what it was able to grab hold of and mount to. The basis handle bar mount holds firm with no issues. The handlebar mount that swivels hold pretty good, but the locking screws seems to be cheaper. It seems like you could over tighten and strip the threads on the plastic if not carful. The floating handle is just a basic handle. Seems to be very sturdy and will keep you from losing your camera while in the water.As far as the case goes. When I received the kit all the mounts were in individually wrapped in plastic bags. They all fit in the hard case. Once I started unwrapping all the pieces I was started to wonder if I would ever get them all back in the case but don’t sweat, they will all fit. The case isn’t a true “hard case” it has a little flexibility to it, but still sturdy. The cutouts in the foam seem to be pretty generic shapes. Other than the locking screws nothing really sat in firmly. This isn’t a big deal because all of this stuff is somewhat rugged. This is additional room in the hard case for your camera to fit in as well.
