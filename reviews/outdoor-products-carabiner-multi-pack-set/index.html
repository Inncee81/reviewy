Great for ready access to kids’ little this and that

These carabiners are not for mountaineering, but for those of us urbanites with little children who can appreciate the ease of hooking up kids’ this and that to which you want to have ready access. We use these carabiners to hang a water bottle to a backpack, a camera to the pants, and a little bag to a bigger bag, etc. They save us from the hassles of having to dig into a bag to find what is needed right away. We especially like the smaller size ones that come in this package.
