    Product Description

The Supernova Dry snorkel has a unique Splash Guard at the end which greatly reduces the entrance of water during use on the surface. The special Valve on top seals the tube as you dive, almost totally preventing the entrance of water when the snorkel is submerged. The end part has a corrugated piece that is smooth on the inner side and keeps the snorkel away from the mouth during use when scuba diving. A special water collection well, with a large elliptic valve, allows quick and easy expulsion of water. The replaceable angled mouthpiece and corrugated tube section are both made from Hypoallergenic Silicone adding comfort and function. A quick release snorkel keeper and ergonomic contoured shape make this one great snorkel.

This one did not work for me

I have been a certified diver since 1980 and been snorkeling even longer than that. This snorkel has the same problem that is suffered by many “dry” snorkels. Simply put, the airflow is limited, at best. For some reason, the modern designers like to make the snorkels look more aerodynamic or ergonomic or whatever. So, instead of making the tubes round, some designers make them oblong; like this one. As long as the air intake volume remains the same, the shape of the tube should not be an issue. But by making the tube oblong instead of round it makes the tube too wide to be comfortable; nor would it fit most masks ergonomically. So as a result, they shrink the size of the oblong shape, thereby reducing the volume of air that can be taken in by the snorkel. In this particular model, that air intake is further limited where the flexible silicone meets the hard clear plastic. For some reason, the designer reduced the open width of the hole between that connection so that even less air gets in. Comically enough the reduced size of the hole combined with the flexibility of the silicone tube sometimes results in a whistling sound as you breathe. It had been my hope that this would be a good replacement snorkel. Unfortunately, I must keep looking.
