Durable Pitching Rubber

Bought this pitching rubber for my daughter. She prefers pitching off the rubber instead of flat dirt. She gets the real push off she needs. After reviewing a few other models I decided to go with this one because of the spikes mounting location. They mount on the sides instead of from the bottom. Go with this version because the bottom mount spikes end up coming through the pitching rubber after a few uses. The four included spikes are solid and should be able to dig into most dirt and grass easily. The pitching rubber itself is heavy and durable rubber no signs of wear yet. Looks solid. Price was right and doing the job it was meant for.
