Great projector stand if it worked like it supposed to.

I used this Projector stand a few days after I received it and I love how portable it is. However, I have two issues with this stand. One, I found that after I put my projector on the stand two of the legs would not hold the weight after I clamp them down to keep them at the position I need. This is disappointing since I do not have a large projector and because I had a hard time keep the table adjusted to the height I needed because it was outside on an uneven landscape. I kept playing with the legs and finally they stayed well enough to use for night as long as the stand was not bumped. Second, the stand comes with a very nice case to put the stand, projector, and accessories inside, however, there doesn’t seem to be a carry strap for it making it a little awkward to carry the case around (that is more of a minor issue though). I have another movie night this weekend and I plan to play with the stand more to see if I can fix the legs. If all goes well and I can fix the legs issue I will change my rating, if not I’m sending it back. But for now I can only give it 3 stars.
