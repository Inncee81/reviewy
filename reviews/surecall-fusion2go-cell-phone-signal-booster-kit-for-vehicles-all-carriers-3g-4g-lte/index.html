    Product Description

The Fusion2Go vehicle cell phone signal booster by SureCall increases cellular signal strength for voice, text and 4G LTE data for all North American cell carriers, including AT&T, Verizon, Sprint and T-Mobile. The Fusion2Go delivers unrivaled performance with cutting-edge technology, reducing dropped and missed calls and extending battery life for multiple simultaneous users on all cellular networks. Cellular reception weakens due to topography, building materials, structural design and distance from the cell tower. The Fusion2Go captures this outside cellular signal, boosts it and re-broadcasts that signal inside the vehicle to your cellular device. The Fusion2Go booster is bi-directional meaning signals are amplified as they travel back and forth from your device to the cell tower ensuring that you stay connected. Dropped calls are history, as the Fusion2Go provides clear and consistent coverage for all cellular devices. With an industry leading 3-year warranty, SureCall proudly stands behind their products and emphasizes the importance of a great user experience.

Excellent built unit and great signal improvement.

I was apprehensive about getting this unit after trying the Weboost 4GX that for some reason just did not work for me and ended up returning. The SureCall Fusion2Go did a fantastic job on my recent trip to the Southwest. For much of the areas we camped, there was limited signal but once we turned on the Fusion2Go, we immediately was able to use the much improved signal. For the most part, I got a 20-40 db improvement as long as I had some kind of signal. The areas we visited included, Vermillion Cliffs (White Pocket), Glen Canyon Recreation area (Alstom Point), Grand Canyon North Rim. All with limited signal availability from ATT.I highly recommend getting one if you travel to remote locations with limited signal strength.
