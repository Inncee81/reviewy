Product Reviews

This Pioneer is a pleasant sounding speaker that plays loud and clean

I actually purchased the Klipsch Reference R-25C Center Channel first, thinking since it was double the price and had bigger drivers, that it would be a better sounding speaker than this Pioneer. I was wrong. I ordered the entire klipsch reference set and was not happy with the harsh sound that came out of the center channel and bookshelf speakers. I thought maybe it was my amp so I purchased this Pioneer SP-C22 and tried it out. The sound was so much more pleasant coming out of this Pioneer. And I was shocked and surprised to hear that the lower tones, like male vocals, sounded so much deeper and clearer. One of the main reason for purchasing the Klipsch speakers as opposed to the Pioneers was that I thought the larger 5.5” driver would better produce the lower frequencies found in the deeper male voices. I was wrong again. This Pioneer plays loud and clean and sounds much better to me than the Klipsch.
