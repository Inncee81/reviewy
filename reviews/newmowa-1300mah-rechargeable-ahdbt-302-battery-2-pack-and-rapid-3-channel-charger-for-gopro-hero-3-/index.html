Works Well, Charges Fast!

These are actually very good quality batteries for the price. I was able to record for over an hour and a half at high settings on my GoPro Hero 3+ with several starts and stops which typically drain batteries faster. The Newoma batteries work just as well as the stock GoPro batteries at a much lower cost, and the included charger works very well.I would definitely recommend these to anyone who uses their GoPro frequently, or for long periods of time throughout the day.These are a great value!

The seem to last as long as the OEM batteries …

The seem to last as long as the OEM batteries. The charger will use either a micro USB or the trapezoid shaped connector. If you are charging three batteries at once I suggest that you use a 2.1 amp charger. Otherwise it may take forever and a day to charge three at once. I was able to use the 2.1 amp car charger I have with my phone’s charging cable with no problem. The cord stayed snug in the charger driving down bumpy roads.

Definitely a good purchase.

I bought this to replace an inexpensive 2 battery charger that shorted out from a static discharge, while on a trip of course. It was waiting for me when I got home. I placed all 3 of my batteries into this charger, and it worked right away. The fit is snug, so you don’t have to worry about the battery not being fully seated to get its charge. After the batteries are charged up, it turns off, and no current appears to be flowing. Really far better than I expected for as little as the cost is.

This kit makes it really nice because you can charge everything at once and be …

These batteries last longer than the original Hero 3 batteries (1300mAh vs 1050mAh), but not too much longer. This kit makes it really nice because you can charge everything at once and be set for a solid day of adventure. The USB charging is nice because I could just plug it into my iPhone wall charger and not have to carry around an entire extra plug.
