Functional Enough

The only negative review doesn’t tell you a darn thing. I recently got these, and they do the job just fine. There are no instructions though, its just a bag of these fabric covers, and several adhesive strips about the size of your pinky nail. You remove the adhesive and guess were on the rift foam part would be best to hold the cloth in place. There are roughly 6 of these things to glue on. The outward facing side is like a velcro surface that grips the sanitary fabric and holds it while you wear the rift. The good news is, that it certainly does what is intended - creates a barrier to soak up oil, sweat, etc. Especially good for the sweating issue when wearing this. The down side is that you forever have these velcro things on your headset so if you ever play without the pad now you got these things poking unpleasantly into your skin unless you decide to rip them out - at which point you better figure out a way to buy more of the stick tab things.
