Product Reviews

Great for surface run cables

Works great really helps make the new wall-mount flat screen look great, even without painting blends in pretty good on my light colored wall, don’t even really notice it which is really what you want. Very good option for through the wall anyway, and easier if you need to change things around as well.One thing that would be a bit of an improvement is if they had holes down the center so you could wall screw right into the stud where you bolted your set into so it is perfectly centered, easily fixed with a small drill bit though so no big deal.I just have a few dry wall screws holding the channel full of wires and it is very secure, the material is very tough, and have no need for the supplied anchors at this time anyway, and yes there is A LOT of room for all your cables.

A decent product, but probably oversized for most people’s needs.

The cord cover does what it is supposed to do. That being said, it is very wide, you could likely fit 10 plus wires in side of the cover. If you only have a couple off wires, I would look for another product. It is deep enough to hold cable wires, power cords, HDMI wires and so on. The wall anchors that come with the cord cover are absolutely huge, and I have no idea why you would need such a large diameter plastic anchor. Since they aren’t holding much up, I decided not to use them.All things considered, this is a good product, but it may not be the best option for someone who is just running a couple of wires. Additionally, if you consider later repair, it may make sense to buy small plastic anchors separately, or just not use them.

Nice, easy way to hide cords between mounted TV and cabinet.

The cord cover is 4 feet long. I only needed about 2 1⁄2 feet to cover the cords between my mounted TV and the cabinet underneath it that holds all the other entertainment components. It was easy to cut down to the size I needed. Easy to install with the included screws and anchors. I used 2 coats of paint to match the wall and it blends in nicely. I am using it to cover 1 HDMI cable, 1 power cable, 1 coaxial cable, and a cable for the Wii system.

Wish it had come with Command Strips

My wife and I got this because we have a young one who just started to walk and now he can’t pull on the cords around our television. Works like a charm at hiding cords

Pros: 1) Great price with easy installation; 2) I painted it to match my wall, and it looks great; 3) it does exactly as advertised.Cons: 1) I had to cut down the cover because some of the power cords to my devices (digital antenna, smart Blu-Ray player) were not long enough to be held down to the top of the baseboards (at the bottom of the wall). By cutting six inches off the bottom of the cover, I was able to keep the two devices in place.Summary: Make sure the power cords to the devices connected to your TV are long enough to prevent having to place them elsewhere or else you will have to cut the cover down to provide some slack to the cords.
