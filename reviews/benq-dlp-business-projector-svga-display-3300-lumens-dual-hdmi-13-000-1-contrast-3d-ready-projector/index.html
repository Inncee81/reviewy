I’m a fairly cheap guy, but I can’t stand low quality things. It makes my life miserable sometimes, but this projector leaves me incredibly satisfied.I have been wanting a home cinema projector for a long time but was unwilling to drop a grand+ for a good one or suffer with a cheap one. Finally I feel like the price and quality equation has become acceptable. (Even when factoring in replacement bulb cost)I was able to produce a 108 inch screen that was very acceptable in a semi bright room and exquisite in a dark room. The image was crisp and the lines clean, I saw no bleeding of light and was easily able to pick up even random stray hairs on screen. I can’t say native resolution is perfect 1080p, but it’s got to be close.I would now rather watch a movie at home than in the theater. Also I have used this with my PS4 and experienced no lagging or issues of any kind. The gameplay was perfect. (Though be warned, it can be quite disorienting when the entire wall in front of you becomes chaotic)The only negative I can possibly say about this projector is the built in speakers on it are absolutely pathetic, but I don’t know anyone who would want to use built in mono speakers for a home cinema, so I didn’t even take a star off for this. Many projectors don’t even have built in speakers.I have not used the 3D function. So I can’t say how good that is, but I assume if it’s up to the standard of the normal 2D imagine, It’s probably pretty good too.Easily a 5 star purchase for me.
