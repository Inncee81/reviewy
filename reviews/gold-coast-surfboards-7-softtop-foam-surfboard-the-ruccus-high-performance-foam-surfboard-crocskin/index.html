Product Specifications

  • WHY THE 7’ RUCCUS? - At 84” long x 22” wide x 2.85” thick, we designed and tested this shape until we had the perfect 7’ foamie for incredibly fun surfing with a Soft-Top Surfboard. We focus on pure performance in the Ruccus Soft-Top Surfboard with our unique shape and added multiple features to make it incredibly durable compared to your standard soft-top surfboard. A perfect soft-top surfboard for epic foamie fun board surfing sessions that will make all levels of surfing love the 7’ Ruccus.
  • SURFBOARD PURCHASE INCLUDES - The purchase of one 7’ Verve Soft-Top Surfboard will include the Surfboard, the 7’ Rishu Surfboard Leash, and a complete Soft-Top Surfboard Screw-In Fin Set (3 Thruster Plastic Screw In Fins, 5 Fins Screws, and 1 Leash Plug Screw). The fins are easily attached with a coin and the leash comes with a leash string to attach your leash to the leash plug on your surfboard.
  • HIGH PERFORMANCE FEATURES - The 7’ Ruccus Soft-Top Surfboard is a 7’ long, 22” wide surfboard with a slim pinched in squash tail with rubber bumper (for sharper turns and safe vertical storage), Compressed Croc-Skin Textured IXPE Foam Deck (No Wax needed), Hand Tapered Rails, Beginner Friendly Nose Rocker to prevent nose diving, Slight Tail Rocker for added release in your turns, AND our Bottom Deck Features a Slick HDPE surface with a full Double Concave shaved from the nose to the tail.
  • DURABILITY - Our 7’ Ruccus Soft-Top Surfboard is made through a Mold and Vacuum Sealed strengthening process, NOT cheap heat laminated. The 7’ Ruccus has a Double Concave Bottom Deck with a Cross-Hatch Impact Threading - helps absorb direct impacts and disperse them evenly. The EPS Foam Closed Cell Core Will NOT take on water & has 3 Stringers (2 Full Length Hand Resined Wooden Stringers + 1 2⁄3 Length Fiberglass Center Rod ). PVC Lined Fin Holes. IXPE Compressed Croc Skin Textured Foam Deck.

  • WHO IS THIS SURFBOARD BEST FOR? - The 7’ Ruccus can surf riders up to 220lbs & is great for kids to learn with. You choose the 7’ Ruccus because it is very buoyant and paddles easy and with our tapered rails and high performance shape, the 7’ Ruccus can turn and gain speed fast, making it very versatile. The 7’ Ruccus is a favorite for parents getting their kids a beginner surfboard and also ideal for more experienced surfers who use the 7’ Ruccus to turn small waves into huge playgrounds.
