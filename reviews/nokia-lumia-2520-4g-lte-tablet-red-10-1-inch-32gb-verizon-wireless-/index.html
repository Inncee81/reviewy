amazing

The perfect tablet experience.

Got mine from the Microsoft Store. It’s better than I could have imagined. I can’t go back to using my Galaxy Tab or the iPad work gave me. Such a smooth, intuitive interface with addictively elegant swipe gestures. Multitasking is flawless, I keep TuneIn Radio on in the background while perusing my People Hub (social media feeds) on one side of the screen with the web browser open on the other side. Having full Microsoft Office on board allows me to leave my laptop at home.Nokia made the right choice going with Windows RT 8.1 over the full Windows 8.1. RT is built specifically for tablets (unlike iOS and Android) and allows for a more fluid experience and much better battery life than if it ran full Windows. Speaking of battery life, this thing goes from 0% charged to 100% in a little over an hour. That’s unheard of. Battery life is stellar and like its smartphone brethren, this Lumia is stunningly gorgeous - a much more arresting color and design than any tablet on the market - and built to last. The app store has more than everything I need and the browser has Flash on board! You don’t realize what a gigantic advantage that is over the competition until you experience it.I really couldn’t ask for a more complete tablet experience. It makes me wonder how people can even stand the primitiveness of iPads.
