Product Reviews

This is the flash system my Sony A6000 was waiting for.

I waited quite a while to get an external flash for my Sony A6000. I wanted something that would work with the TTL exposure system of the Sony, would have full features (such as high speed sync), and would not overpower the small size of the camera itself. This flash provides all of that, plus the built in 2.4G wireless feature allows it to do full TTL off camera flash and even TTL with multiple flash units. The only thing it does not do is act as a commander flash to do TTL off camera flash with other Sony compatible units. However, that feature is not really needed because of the built in 2.4G capability, which achieves the same TTL ability and actually is more flexible. I bought two of these flashes and the X1T-S wireless trigger. I tested all of the features and everything works exactly as advertised. I’ve used the units for over 5 months and all works well. Besides the TTL capability, the manual control and optical slave capability makes these very flexible. The flashes, the transmitter, and even the cases and diffuser all have a high quality feel and appearance, nothing looks cheap even though the price is quite reasonable. The manual is quite clear. Two features perhaps not well documented that I found a present surprise: 1) If I set the camera shutter speed to a high shutter speed (say 1⁄1000 sec) the flash automatically switches into HSS mode. 2) If I set the camera to high speed continuous shooting (say 11 fps) the flash automatically switches to strobe mode and flashes in time with the shutter. This is one smart system - as smart as my camera, which is saying quite a lot. And, if you need more flashes, Godox now offers an entire range of flashes up to studio units, and they will all talk to this flash and can be used together. I recommend them highly.
