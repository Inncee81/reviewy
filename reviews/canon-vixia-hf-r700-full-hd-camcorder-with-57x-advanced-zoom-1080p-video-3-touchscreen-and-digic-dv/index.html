Canon Vixia HF R700

Very nice product. Did a lot of searching before deciding on this one. Maintains a good focus when shooting at a distance. Does average for white balance inside. You can calibrate and make it do well, but the white balance is so close, I saw no need to do the special calibration. One of the requirements for me was the hdmi output. Sometimes we tie 2 event arenas together. The sound is easy to cross over. Now the video running through hdmi/sdi adapters goes about 100 feet to 2 70” monitors.
