Product Reviews

I see why these are the most popular cages on the market.

These Elite bottle cages are with out a doubt the most popular bottle cages on the market and having purchased quite a few of them myself. I can see why they are so popular. They are very light weight, but also durable. They hold your water bottles tight enough where if you are riding over bumpy terrain the water bottle wont pop out of them, but they arent so tight that you cant easily grab a water bottle and put it bike while you are riding. These Elite cages also come in different styles and a multitude of different colors to match just about any bike. So they are great for those weight weenies out there that want something light but still looks great.

Just as light as a carbon cage

I had these same cages for 4 years prior, and these are just as good. They are more snug, and honestly the design is great. The weight vs some carbon fiber cages cannot be beat.
