Fits great, protects your investment!

This is a really nice silicone cover for the Sony Walkman MP3 Player. My job is dirty and this cover does a really good job of keeping the dust and grime at bay. It has indents for the button locations so you know what you are pressing, and holes for certain controls, like the hold switch, charger port and earbud jack. I read some complaints that it didn’t fit well - mine fits great. Also that it wasn’t clear - well, it’s silicone, so it’s not going to be transparent. The picture clearly shows it is translucent. On a silver Walkman, like mine, it makes it look more whitish, but if you had a colored one, like red maybe, it would allow the color to show through more muted. My only gripe would be that it is not as “grabby” as I would like. The silicone cover on my old iPod had a slightly less smooth texture, which made it easier to hold and manipulate, I feel, than this case. But that’s really minor. I would recommend this cover to anyone with a Sony Walkman.
