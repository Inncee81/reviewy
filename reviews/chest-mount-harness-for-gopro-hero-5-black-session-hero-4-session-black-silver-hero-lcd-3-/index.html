The CamKix Chest Mount allows you to shoot completely hands free. Perfect for use during action sports such as cycling, climbing, skiing, snowboarding, surfing, kayaking, go carting, equestrian sports and much more.

Quality, Low Price Harness

I bought this to film a nerf war that my college campus does. I bought this harness because it was cheaper and highly reviewed. I’m glad I did. It arrived quickly and safely and is very easy to use. It’s comfortable and can easily be worn by people of many sizes or over bulky clothing such as winter jackets. It didn’t slide around or ride up and gave me some great footage.I haven’t put it through the ringer just yet so I’m not sure how it would hold up to, say, a BMX fall or something but it seems sturdy from my use.My only complaint is specific and very minor – I’d love if the mounting bracket stuck out a bit more. Being so close to the chest piece means you get the widest angle possible, but it also means you’re going to get a low angle if you’re using the battery pack or LCD screen attachments with the GoPro. I had to remove my detachable screen because it was forcing the camera to point too far downward.That’s a minor gripe, though, and I’m happy with the harness. I’d recommend it to anyone who is looking for a good, comfortable harness that won’t blow their budget.
