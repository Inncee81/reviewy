Product Specifications

  • Recreational all-weather table tennis table with 6mm aluminum plastic composite surface and 30mm x 30mm rust-resistant, powder-coated undercarriage
  • Two-piece table quickly folds up for playback mode and compact storage; Adjustable leveling feet to conform to any terrain
  • Comes with double anti-tilting devices and locking casters on 3in wheels for addtional safety, stability, and mobility
  • Quick 15 minute assembly time; Includes easy-to-install matching weatherproof net set. UV resistant. Stain resistant

  • Assembled: 108in x 60in x 30in; Stored: 62in x 22in x 67in; Weight: 166 pounds. Table Top material-synthetic laminate

    Product Description

The JOOLA Outdoor Table Tennis Table is a high-quality table tennis table that can be used inside or outside, designed specifically to withstand outdoor use all year round, in all weather conditions and terrains. Featuring a 6mm aluminum plastic composite surface, resistant to warping and chipping. The JOOLA Outdoor Table offers a consistent bounce even after years of extended outdoor play, making this a great family investment. No maintenance is required since each half is mounted on a 30mm x 30mm rust-resistant, powder-coated steel undercarriage. Adjustable height levelers on each leg help balance the table on uneven ground. Double anti-tilting devices and durable 3-inch locking casters make it safe and easy to transport. Enjoy playing with friends or training solo by folding up one half of the table in the playback position. The JOOLA Outdoor comes with an easy-to-install weatherproof net set with adjustable posts to ensure a level playing surface and is the perfect addition to your deck, backyard, or community playground.  Start playing like a champion with equipment developed for champions! JOOLA is a proud sponsor of the US National Team.
