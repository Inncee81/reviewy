They originally sold these seats for males only, calling them prostate safe…even than I bought one for my wife…as it makes perfect sense as there is a space or hollow down the whole seat so there is no rubbing on skin which can eventually lead to bleeding. To me this is the only Bike seat that should ever been made…it is the best on the market!!! My only hope is when I need another one down the road that I will be able to find one.

Huge improvement in riding comfort

I’m a woman, 5.5ft, 135lbs. I ride my bike daily approx 10 miles as a commuter. A standard saddle was causing me significant discomfort so I tried this after the positive reviews. I’ve found it very durable after a couple months of use-no significant wear or fraying in the fabric. It reduces the pressure centrally and has enough cushion to allow a bit of give with the left to right motions, but it’s far from cushy. I think additional padding or bike shorts would still be a good option for many.
