Product Reviews

Cute and comfy, but require some help from mommy

These Firik Kids Soft Fleece Bunny Adjustable Headband Headphones are super cute for any little girl. My daughter loved them instantly, though I do have some positives and negatives about them. One of the issues we have are the fact that they are a bit hard for my 4 year old to put on herself. She cant really get the velcro put on by herself and the earbuds adjusted to sit over her ears at the same time. I help her, but she seems to do better when she leaves the velcro connected and she can just slip them off her head and back on when she wants them. They are wired so you have to make sure your child knows that they cant get them wrapped up or pull on the plug, but the wires sit nicely on the front of her face and haven’t given her much issue. They definitely are made for kids. I tried them on and they don’t fit my adult head at all. They are adjustable though, and you can move the ear pieces inside the headband to where your child needs them to be. Also, you can remove the earbuds entirely so that you can throw the headband in the wash which I think is nice. They have an okay sound, not amazing but good enough for her, and they also have a volume adjustment button halfway down the wires so your child can easily turn things up and down. All in all a cute set, if you are looking for something cute and warm, and would definitely help keep a lil one warm in the winter while they play on any device.P.S.My daughter accidentally lefts hers on the floor and the puppy chewed it up. Dont make the same mistake!~Thanx~
