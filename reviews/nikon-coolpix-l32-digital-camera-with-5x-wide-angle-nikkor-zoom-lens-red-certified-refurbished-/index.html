Decent low cost camera

Very decent little camera. Takes great pictures when you can get your subject to be still, the camera does not take good pictures at all if there is the slightest bit of motion, which isn’t the greatest when you are trying to take pictures of kids. The camera is very compact and great to carry around in a purse or pocket. The fact that it used two AA batteries is a definite plus. Camera has a lot of great shooting and editing options built in for photographing different scenes and altering your pictures before you load them on to a computer.Pros: compact, great photos of still subjects, lots of features, decent battery life, good low light photosCons: terrible photos if your subject moves at all, doesn’t come with cord to transfer photos to computer

Meh

Regular alkaline batteries worked, but it was so slow it became nearly worthless with them. With lithium batteries it would “recover” much faster after each picture. The focus was ok, but I expected better.
