Product Reviews

The OTA is very good, but not the package.

As a starter telescope with the supplied tripod, diagonal & eyepieces this scope deserves a 2 to 3 star rating. You would be better off to spend $60 more for the blue colored infinity model (209004) that comes with a much better tripod & eyepieces.The optical telescope assembly (OTA) is the same for both models. It is very good and that the reason I bought this package. I felt for $99 the telescope alone was worth it to me. I already have a tripod, diagonal and many excellent eyepieces.I got very respectable views of Saturn and the Andromeda galaxy the first time I use the OTA and this was in very light polluted sky’s. This telescope has a very short focal length of only 400mm, so it produces a wide field of view (FOV). That is why I bought it, so i could look at star formations like the Pleiades (seven sisters). There are better starter scopes for high magnification, but many astronomical curiosities require a wide field at low magnification to see the entire object. I just like scanning around the sky with the 4.0 degree FOV this OTA offers.
