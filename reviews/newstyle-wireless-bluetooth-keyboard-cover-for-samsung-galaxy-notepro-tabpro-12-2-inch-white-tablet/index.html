Product Reviews

I wanted to love this case…. but

I wanted to love this case and for the most part it’s pretty good. Nicely textured but thick - while this may be good for tablet protection its very bulky. I purchased white and it did come as white, not off-white/ivory, etc.. Openings and cutouts work fine and it’s easy to get the S pen out. While the description doesn’t say it’s a sleep case I didn’t notice this absence from the description - that’s on me. It’s disappointing that this case doesn’t put the tablet to sleep when you close it. I have to turn it off and back on - can’t just fold up and go. I was a bit concerned about the tab in front but it hasn’t been an issue for typing. Keyboard sync’d right up with the bluetooth and is easy to type on. I do like that I can turn the keyboard power off when not in use - I use it mainly for taking class notes. Tablet is well supported and doesn’t slip around when you’re typing. However, when needed to use in portrait position for those apps that don’t refuse to swing into landscape, the charger is right at the bottom making it cumbersome - I end up having to unplug it. Just an annoyance. And the magnetic tab barely holds closed - I would never trust it to secure the case. It’s extremely weak. It does fit into my iPearl neoprene pouch with the case and keyboard and the power cords (one for the tablet and one for the keyboard) which will be great when traveling - having everything in one place.

It’s clean and smart looking and adds some protection to the tablet

Fits my Tab Pro 12 very well. It’s clean and smart looking and adds some protection to the tablet. All the cut outs make it very easy to reach all the various buttons for sound, power, etc. Keyboard works great with the Bluetooth and I like that you can remove the keyboard (magnetized) when not using it. Shipped on time and the price was right!

Nice Keyboard, lousy support

Keyboard and tab protection are fine. The design is seriously flawed on holding the screen at your preferred angle. Like others we added velcro strips to give two positions that don’t slip every time you touch the screen or move the keyboard. Newstyle should issue pre-cut velcro to all their customers until correcting the design.
