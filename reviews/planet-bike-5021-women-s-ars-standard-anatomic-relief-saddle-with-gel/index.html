Product Reviews

Medium distance rider - LOVE this seat.

I really want to respond to some of the reviews I saw prior to buying the product. To be clear: I love this seat! I ride 9 miles round-trip daily to work and do weekend rides of 20-30 miles at a time.I chose this seat because the reviews for the men’s seat were extraordinary! The women’s were mixed. I’d like to address just a couple things in this review:1) I wear a size 10 and am 147 lbs. I didn’t experience any issues with the seat rubbing. I have the nose pointed slightly down, so I really have no issues with the nose touching me at all.2) Some reviewers noted that this seat wasn’t particularly “cushy” or “gel”-ly. It’s not supposed to be cushy! That’s what beach cruiser seats are for! The gel in this seat absorbs energy so that instead of the bike seat pushing up on you with the same amount of force that you exert on the seat by sitting on it (sorry for my high school physics language), you actually don’t really feel much. Basically, on a 25 mile ride, I don’t think about the seat. I don’t have to wriggle around and stand up while riding to give my butt a break like I did on my old seat! Getting on and off of it after a 12 mile stretch doesn’t cause me any soreness! It doesn’t feel cushy like a nice armchair, but it also doesn’t feel like much at all. Just a seat!It’s lightweight for such a supportive seat (plus!) and it’s contoured exactly right for a woman’s body. I give my highest recommendation to this seat - if you want anything better, you CAN find it, but it will cost at least $10 more.

Nice shape, decent price for the quality.

I bought this bike seat to replace the old one that originally came with my bike. My old seat was perfectly fine for a year, then started feeling uncomfortable. After first trying out this new seat, it was hard to get used to and felt uncomfortable for my first few rides, yet still felt better than the old seat. Once I got used to the different shape of this seat, it was easy to forget about it. I no longer thought about the bike seat at all, and was able to focus more on my bike ride. I wouldn’t say this bike seat is extremely comfortable, but it isn’t designed to be in the first place. I really like the shape of this bike seat, how light it is, and the fact that you can adjust the angle of the nose. I have mine angled slightly downward and I think that helps a lot when riding up and down hills. Buy it and try it out!
