The No Leaks Guarantee is just what I want.I ordered one of these [24 pieces of size AA]. My batteries arrived packaged in a black BOX. Not the pretty cardboard back with a clear plastic molded cover as shown in the picture.I called Energizer. They advised me that ONLY product that boldly states “Energizer® No Leaks. Guaranteed. Or we will replace your device.” on the package is covered on the no leak guarantee.They are familiar with the rectangular boxes. They told me that these are only for OEM use and similar, not for resale. They emphasized over and over, that these are NOT guaranteed against leakage. They showed me how they tell the difference by numbers stamped on the battery label.I placed another order, I received the correct packaging as shown in this listing.Look for the printing that assures you are covered by the no leak guarantee.
