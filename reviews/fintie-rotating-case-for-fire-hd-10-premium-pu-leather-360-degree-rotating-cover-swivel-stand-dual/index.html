Fintie Fire HD 10 2015 Case - Premium PU…Pro and Cons

I purchased this item towards the end of November, and have had it in use since that time. I am writing this review as a ‘heads-up’ to those of you who are considering the purchase of a protective cover for your Fire HD 10.Pro:It does, indeed, protect the Fire HD 10.Cons:1) The easel function is not well thought out; the tablet slips down both in the portrait and the landscape position, more so in the portrait position.2) The elastic band (which is used to hold the cover securely closed) tends to press against the on/off button with unpredictable results.3) The magnetic switch actuator does its job only too well; not only does it turn the device off when the cover is closed, but it also shuts the device off when the cover is folded back for “book reading”All-in-all, not a good investment.I plan on discarding this case, and will be looking for a better solution. My advice, skip this item and save your money…

The cover has a loop for a stylus that I doubt I will ever use but seems to function just fine and does no interfere with the use of the Kindle. Nicely made and cute!

This case is cute! It was a little more than I wanted to spend on a case, but it is well made. I love that it looks like a Composition Book and it gets a giggle from people when they realize what it really is. This case is a little thicker than my previous case so my new, slim, Fire HD 10.1” isn’t that slim anymore. But, it’s just the design of the case. The stand on the one side adds some width and the other side of the cover has to be thicker to create the grooves that the Kindle sits in so it stands up. I really like that the stand rotates, so I can stand the Kindle horizontally or vertically.
