Product Specifications

  • Dual Cutting Blades offer a balance of maneuverability and cutting capacity making it ideal for mid-sized areas
  • Automatic battery switchover pulls power from the second battery once the first is depleted to eliminate downtime
  • 2-in-1 feature offer versatile use with mulching and rear bagging capabilities

  • Easy Store, Fold-Down Handle makes storing and cleaning the mower easy

    Product Description

Powerful, Easy Starting and Hassle-Free the Poulan Pro 40-Volt Lithium-Ion rechargeable Mower delivers Superior Performance without the use of cords, gas or oil.  Mower includes two 40-Volt Lithium-Ion batteries with automatic switchover for extended run time. The 20 in. cutting deck and a dual blade design delivers superior mulching, bagging and cutting performance. With instant push button starting, this powerful mower gives the performance of gas without the mess and fumes. The Fold Down Handle makes this mower easy to clean and store when not in use. Environmentally friendly and quiet, this mower is the perfect solution for small yards and close neighbors. The Poulan Pro 40-Volt Lithium-Ion family of outdoor power equipment includes the following products, all powered by an interchangeable 40-Volt Lithium-Ion Battery and Charger: Axial Blower, Chainsaw, Hedge Trimer, Lawn Mower, Pole Saw and String Trimmer. Backed by Poulan Pro 4-Year Warranty.

Very solid machine and worth the extra money!

Have used this for about 2 months. Yard is maybe a 1⁄4 acre at most. Really solid mower here. Dual battery setup is top notch. I can get about 1.5 cuts out of a full charge(depending on grass height and thickness). Feels really durable, easy blade height adjustment, it’s louder than other electric mowers but not louder than a gas engine. Also batteries charge very quickly-maybe 30-40 min each. Only complaint is seems to miss some blades of grass(maybe the blades need to be sharpened) but very minor issue. I would definitely recommend and buy again!
