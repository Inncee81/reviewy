Great for recording video not so much for taking photos.

 The digital hardware components that make up the Activeon CX Action camera all combine to create one of the best wide angle 1080P videos I have ever seen. The lens pulls in a full scene that is almost beyond my own vision. The digital processor and recording to the micro SDHC card deliver flawless videos that seem to never miss a moment. The best part is that videos are encoded as MOV files that take up very little file system space and have a single channel audio track at 48 kHz.Sadly, the same cannot be said for the pictures taken with the Activeon CX Action camera system. Initially, over 50 photos were taken with the camera while exploring our forest land. As expected the camera lens was able to generate some great wide angle photos that were impossible to take with my Apple iPhone 6. We did notice that the corners of the photos were not always in full focus but this was acceptable given that it provided so much more detail. The problems started on our second outdoor expedition to the Louisiana Longleaf Pine forest. We took many more photos and as always would turn off the camera when done. Once we downloaded the photos to a laptop and looked at them, all the photos had their bottom frames cut off. No image utility was able to correct the photos and after several emails to Activeon technical support there was no replies.The wireless mode of the camera does work and with about a 75ft range in the thick Longleaf pine forest, we were able to snap photos remotely while having the camera view find on my Apple iPhone 6. My complaint is that the wireless mode does not offer the user the ability to manually take photos from the camera itself nor does it offer the ability to see the current view on its screen.The camera will run for at least two days on a full charge with moderate use. In other words, I used the camera to review videos and left it on until it timed out on several occasions. The camera will take about 6 hours to recharge to a full charge once fully discharged.In conclusion, even though the photos taken with this action camera no longer save correctly, the videos are of such high quality that we continue to use it. The rugged plastic case is one of the best features with complete waterproof capabilities for your outdoor adventures. Due to the corrupt pictures and unresponsive customer service, I have given this product 3 stars.
