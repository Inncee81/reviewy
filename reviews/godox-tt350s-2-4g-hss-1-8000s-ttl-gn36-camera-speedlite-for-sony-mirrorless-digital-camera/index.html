Great compact flash

This is exactly what I was looking for. A compact flash with HSS that can be triggered remotely and reasonably priced! Works very well with the Sony A7II and X1TS trigger. A compact kit for traveling that still allows for some off camera options. The Godox system is working out very nicely for me (AD360, TT685S, TT350S, X1TS)

Nice little flash for the RX-10

I’ve got the larger flash as well but wanted something smaller for everyday situations and travel. This is definitely smaller yet has enough power for most many situations. Balances nicely on top of the diminutive (compared to my D810) Sony. For me, it would be nice if it had a socket for an external battery but I understand that is a “nice to have” that wouldn’t really match the use case for this item.

The build seems good and looks professional

I bought this for my Sony a6500. My lens, the Sony E 18-105mm, casts a shadow when using the onboard flash from about 18-30mm, so I needed a fix for this. I also wanted TTL functionality to work with my a6500. I received the TT350s flash today, slapped a couple of batteries in it, and took a few shots. It worked perfectly. The build seems good and looks professional, and it’s only a 3rd of the price of a Sony comparable flash. I took a picture of a white wall (below) to show how it prevented the shadow effect while using the Sony 18-105mm at 18mm focal length.

Another winner from Godox

This is my third Godox flash and while this is puts out a bit less power, it is perfect for the Sony a6300. Yes it works with all the Sony a6000s, a6300 and a6500, as well as the A7 and new A9 ranges. The best part is the ability to use the X1T transmitter which sends 2.4 ghz radio waves to trigger the off camera flashes. It’s a great product at a reasonable price!
