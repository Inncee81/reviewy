Product Specifications

  • 100W Giosolar Semi Flexible Monocrystalline Solar Panel ideal for caravans and campers, motorhomes, canal and marine boats, sheds, workshops, gardens and greenhouses
  • The modules can withstand high wind-pressure, snow loads and extreme temperatures.
  • High reliability with guaranteed +/-3% output power tolerance.
  • Can be curved to a maximum 30 degree arc and mounted on an RV, boat, cabin, tent, car, trucks, trailers or any other irregular surface

  • 25-year transferable power output warranty: 5-year with 95% efficiency rate, 10-year with 90% efficiency rate, 25-year with 80% efficiency rate

    Product Description

Size:100W Flexible Giosolar solar modules are constructed with high efficiency Monocrystalline solar cells and produce higher output per module than others in it class. This industrial grade module is an industry standard among various industry professionals. This 100W Giosolar Semi Flexible Monocrystalline Solar Panel is ideal for uneven roofs, this panel will flex to the shape of the roof making it the ideal choice for curved roofs on boats and caravans for 12V battery charging.

Solar Panel Specifications:

  • Maximum Power: 100W
  • Optimum Operating Voltage (Vmp): 19 V
  • Open-Circuit Voltage (Voc): 23 V
  • Optimum Operating Current (Imp): 5.8 A
  • Short-Circuit Current (Isc): 6.1A
  • Dimensions: 1120*540*2.5mm / 44 X 21.25 X 0.1 In
  • Weight: 5.5 lbs (2.5KG)
  • 100mm of special cable with MC4 connectors attached
