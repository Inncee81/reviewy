Product Reviews

I am very pleased with these gel guards

I am very pleased with these gel guards.They have a good fit, not to tight yet not loose. They are very smooth to your touch and in this past week of use I haven’t felt any issues with them slipping from my grasp in any way.That takes me to the next point I haven’t had any difficulty with pressing any of the buttons as I’ve seen other reviews mention that they’ve had to cut out areas around the buttons.Visually the colors are nice and vibrant, it doesn’t have any discoloration or faded out hue and to some one with grey joy-cons like myself I feel it adds a nice touch to the switch. They don’t have any bad odors either.All in all I like them a lot as they works both for protection and as a cosmetic upgrade.

Good Accessory To Have For The Joycons To Make Long Gametime More Comfortable.

When you have medium to big hands prolonged gaming with the joycons can get a little uncomfortable as the joycons put pressure in the middle of your hands which make keeping them in your grip a little tedious as you want to open them up a little and give your hands a rest. These grips really help out with that situation as they make holding the joycons more comfortable when playing a game for a few hours without the discomfort you had before. The silicon rubberized grip fits snugly onto the joycon without any fear of tearing or moving about when gaming the thumbstick covers are decent to but I’m a kontrolfreek thumbsgrip fan and prefer theirs over most others though again that’s not to say these thumgrips are bad far from it.This is another accessory for the Switch I feel is definitely worth getting along with the protector case and screen protector to insure your Switch has a long life without any hassles.

Good, comfortable product for the money

Pros:- If you can get them around $8 or less they are great- Much more comfortable for larger hands on the switch with the contoured back- Thumb grips are very comfortable, much prefer them to the default sticks- Can detach and attach the joycons without having to remove the silicone grips- Easy to mod to your preferenceCons:- New out of package, they make pressing some face buttons harder than normal. May require light modding.- Slight plastic/silicone smell brand new out of package, will probably go away with time.Overall these are great, assuming you can get them around $8. And I say that because they do require some slight cutting if you want to make them really nice. After playing a circuit in Fast RMX, I immediately found it hard to feel if you were pressing the phase button (X) while holding down accelerate (A). I decided to cut away both the cross segments between the YXBA buttons and the UDLR cross buttons. Upon doing so and seeing that it was an immediate improvement, I cut additional paths out the the home and screenshot buttons. Everything is accessible now and they work great, took maybe a minute to fix. You could probably cut out additional paths to + and - buttons as well but since those don’t get pressed quite as much I didn’t see that necessary.So again really nice inexpensive product if you feel comfortable enough with an exacto to make them perfect.
