Killer deal on a LOT of really useful accessories

I have a #GoPro 4 Silver and a GoPro 2HD & all these goodies fit both cameras very well. I’m pretty sure this kit has everything I’ll ever need for my cameras, including a case that has a foam insert with a cutout to fit the camera in. I especially like the wrist cuff thing that has the 360º swivel mount on it because, as we all know, life isn’t all right angles. Sometimes you need to mount your camera at a weird angle & this thing does it!The chesty rig is pretty snug on me, but I’m a girl with…ahem…well, I’ll just leave it at that. ;) The head mount is nice and has grippy stuff on the strap so it stays put. The selfie stick is stiff, so the camera doesn’t feel like it’ll just twirl over. I like the helmet mount arm for fun selfie footage. The lens cover fits my GoPro 4 Silver perfectly, but only fits the cameras with the square lens housing (3 & up). All the clips and mounts fit together snugly and securely & I have no worries about them coming undone or causing shaky footage. I’m always leery about mounting a $400-$500 camera on ANY suction cup, so I use a long, steel fishing leader as a safety tether just in case the suction fails for some reason. It hasn’t happened, but better safe than sorry!I can’t find any quality issues with any of the pieces and there are no shoddy molding marks or seams & they don’t feel brittle. The fabric pieces, like the straps and the case, are all nicely made and finished; no stray strings or fraying. The adhesive pads are 3M VHB, which stands for “very high bond”, so be sure you want to stick them wherever it is that you’re sticking them!Overall, this is a killer deal on a LOT of really useful, easy to use accessories. I got a deal on this kit with a coupon code for evaluation, but It’s totally worth the asking price and then some.
