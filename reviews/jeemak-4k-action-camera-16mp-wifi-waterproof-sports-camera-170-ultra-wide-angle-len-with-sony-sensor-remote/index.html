Great camera, but SDXC cards over 32GB cannot be read from external card reader

The camera is a good upgrade to my SJ6000. I have it mounted to my helmet when I ride on the motorcycle. Videos are clear and the driving mode is key. They driving mode basically starts recording as soon as it turns on. Pretty helpful if I forget to turn it on before I get on my bike. Remote is really cool too. I can snap photos with a click of the button on the remote.Only issue I have with this camera is that it kinda supports 64GB SD cards. What I mean by that is that it can use 64GB cards to store the videos, but it formats the 64GB SD card as FAT32. Well FAT32 can only go up to 32GB so when I try to put the 64GB SDXC card into a USB 3.0 SDXC card reader attached to my laptop it won’t read it. The laptop complains that the card is not formatted. So the only way to read the data on the card is by connecting the camera itself to the laptop but because the USB interface on the camera is USB 2.0 transfer rate of 4K quality video takes much longer. Using a 32GB card works fine.Other than that, the camera is great for the price.
