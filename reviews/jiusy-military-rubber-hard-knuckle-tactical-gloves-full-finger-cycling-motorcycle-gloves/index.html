Decent Gloves

Fine quality, but you can definitely get a nicer pair for about $10-$15 more. Might want to get a pair of Mechanix gloves if you plan on getting any gloves for shooting. But for the price you pay here, it’s what you expect. Slightly bigger than I expected (I ordered medium size), but nothing feels exceptionally cheap about these gloves. Not the most dexterity or finger flexibility, but they are rather thick gloves. The goal is protection, not movement with these.
