Product Specifications

  • Small Universal TV stand for many applications. Affordable, adjustable TV stand for most makes and models, easily works as a Samsung TV stand, Vizio TV stand, Panasonic TV stand, Sony TV stand, and many more. This TV swiveling TV stand creates a modern look for your audio video equipment. The black 8mm thick tempered safety glass base enhances the look and overall design.
  • Adjustable! Swivels left or right so achieving the ideal viewing angle has never been easier. Also height adjustable so your TV can be adjusted to your application or allow room for a soundbar or component below. This way the IR sensor on your TV will never be blocked and your remote control will always function as it should.
  • Replace your TV stand. This TV stand adds a unique ability to swivel where others cannot. Now your TV can be directed up to 45 degrees in each direction towards the viewing area by pivoting the unit. No need to twist the base with it. This feature is usually available only on articulating TV mounts.
  • This TV stand measures 18.75 inches tall from bottom to top of column, not including TV height. The base is 17.75” wide and 12” deep. Max TV weight is 80 pounds. This unit weighs in at just 15 pounds.

  • Will it fit? This TV Stand will fit most TVs from 23 to 50 inches in size and weighing up to 60 pounds. To make sure this mount will fit insure your TV’s VESA bolt pattern falls between these two sizes : 100x100mm up to 400x400mm.

    Product Description

Works great as a replacement TV stand for Samsung TV stand, Vizio TV stand, Sony TV stand and many others. All while making the most of functionality. FUNCTIONAL TV STAND This TV stand mounts the TV high enough to have a Sound Bar or video game sensor in front and not block the view or the infrared sensor on the TV. Most TV stands have the TV too low to do this.

Good Value, but check your set first

Very solid and surprisingly heavy. It looks great, but may be a challenge for some to assemble. It is not hard, but steps need to be followed.It was used on my mothers 39” TV. The bolt kit comes with T8 and T6 screws for the VESA mount. Her TV uses T4 screws. A trip to the local store solved the issue. Be aware of the mounting bolt size on the back of your TV.

Good quality. Assembly Is fairly easy.

Seems like good quality. Many posted here how difficult it was to assemble. Seemed pretty straight forward to me and had my TV mounted in half hour or so. Nice swivel feature. Fit my 40 inch perfect.

The drawings were good, but sometimes a little hard to perceive orientation

When I first opened the box and looked at the assembly directions, it seemed a bit overwhelming. However, all the parts and tools were segregated into separate plastic envelopes, and were well-labeled. The drawings were good, but sometimes a little hard to perceive orientation. Assembling it took about an hour for me because I’m methodical. (The second step of assembly was problematic because the screws wouldn’t go in all the way until I got out some pliers). When it was completed and I attached a 46” Samsung LED TV, it seemed stable and secure, but as a couple others have mentioned, the tv tilted downwards slightly instead of being perpendicular, because the vertical support shaft tilts forward little. (Instructions warned against over-tightening it.) All-in-all, I think this stand is a great value for the price compared to many others I researched, especially because it also swivels.

I love that wide glass base and with rubber feet.
