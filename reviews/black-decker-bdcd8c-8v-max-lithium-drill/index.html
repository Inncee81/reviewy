Black & Decker BDCD8C 8V MAX Lithium Drill

by people ...

  • 8V integrated battery, with USB charger
  • Mid-handle design for balance and control
  • 3⁄8” keyless chuck for quick and easy bit changes
  • Includes double-ended bit

The BLACK+DECKER BDCD8C 8V MAX Lithium Drill is ideal for DIY projects! This drill features a mid-handle design for balance and control and operates at up to 400 rpm. The 3⁄8” keyless chuck allows for quick and easy bit changes so not to hold up your project. It offers an integrated battery with a micro USB charger that can also be used for charging other household items. Includes: (1) BDCD8C 8V MAX Drill, (1) Micro USB Charger, (1) Double-Ended Bit.

Great little drill for jobs around the house.

This little drill is great for simple jobs around the house. It’s small and lightweight, weighing only about a pound. It uses a micro USB charger, which is included, and also comes with double-ended bit, so you can use it right out of the box.It isn’t extremely powerful, but that’s to be expected. It does, however, have enough power for simple drilling and screwing tasks. The first thing I did with it was drill a hole in a solid oak table leg with a 1⁄8 drill bit, and it performed just fine. Keep in mind, it doesn’t have variable speed, so you need to be careful when drilling. I also used it to remove and replace molding from around a door and it performed perfectly. The 8V battery holds more than enough power to allow for everyday tasks and it charges fairly quickly.I highly recommend this little drill to anyone looking for an inexpensive, yet dependable and quality power drill for around the house. For the price, you can’t do a much better.
