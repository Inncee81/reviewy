Product Specifications

  • Auto Trim Hull which keeps the bow down when paddling solo from stern seat

  • Maximum load range is 450 to 500 pounds

    Product Description

The Old Town Twin Heron Kayak is great for paddling with a partner, your dog, or all the camping gear you need for an extended stay in the wilderness. Old Town engineered a hull that won’t pop up when you’re paddling this boat alone, so you can comfortably paddle from the stern seat without feeling like you’re riding a wheelie across the lake. The front seat can be easily removed to make room for gear when you’re headed out on a multi-day trip or to make room for Fido. The boat includes two adjustable Comfort Flex seats for full-day comfort on the water. The easy-to-adjust foot brace system keeps your feet secure and comfortable, and it’s fitted with position indicators so you can set both sides to identical lengths. Getting the boat to and from the water is easy with a partner thanks to carrying handles at the bow and stern. The boat also features paddle holders and cup holders, so you can enjoy the beverage of your choice out on the lake without dropping your paddle after a few too many.
