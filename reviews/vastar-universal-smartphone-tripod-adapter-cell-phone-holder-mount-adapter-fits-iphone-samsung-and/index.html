The Ultrapod is great because it can be strapped to a post or branch to elevate the perspective, but the lumpy digital camera was sometimes difficult to position next to the post/branch. Not only that, but my phone takes better pics than my old camera, and it’s easier to carry.The tilt feature of this tripod adapter is unnecessary if you have a tripod that already has a ball-and-socket swivel on it. So you can just take the clamping piece with you (since it has a threaded hole in it), and leave the base part at home. Perfect, the least amount of stuff to carry with you to get good selfies that aren’t all taken by a 1-meter selfie stick: half of this mount, one Ultrapod, and one smartphone.
