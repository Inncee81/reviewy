  • Official Raspberry Pi Zero Case with 3 Interchangeable Lids

    Product Description

NOTE: The Raspberry Pi Zero W (Wireless) does not come pre-installed in the case.

This exclusive kit from CanaKit includes the ultra-small and ultra-slim Raspberry Pi Zero W (Wireless) and all essential accessories. The Raspberry Pi Zero W (Wireless) is the smallest form factor Raspberry Pi on the market and now incorporates WiFi and Bluetooth connectivity on board. It is 40% faster than the original Raspberry Pi but measures only 65mm long by 30mm wide and 5mm deep. The Raspberry Pi Zero W supports mini connectors to save on space and the 40 pin GPIO is unpopulated provding the flexibility to use only the connections your project requires.
