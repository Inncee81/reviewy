Introduction: The booster kit includes an booster, an indoor antenna and an outdoor antenna, the outdoor antenna can pick up good mobile signal from outside of your house, and send the signal through coaxial cable to the booster, the booster can amplify the signal, then the amplified signal is sent to the indoor antenna, the indoor antenna can transmit the signal into your house, so you can enjoy clearer phone call or faster mobile date inside your house.

Preparing to Buy a Booster: 1. Check your frequency, because different Phone Providers operate on different frequencies. Usually, 850MHz and 1900MHz are associated with voice and 3G data; while 700MHz and 1700MHz are associated with 4G data. For more detail, refer to wirelessadvisor.com. 2. Make sure that you can make calls outside your house, at roof or wherever you plan to install the Outdoor Antenna. If there is no signal outside your house, the booster will not work for you.

Great Value and Excellent Design

I live in a coastal area with almost no cell phone coverage. Maybe one bar outside in the yard. I bought this a week ago, and it works well. In my opinion, This is a good “value-priced” unit. First, I considered a $1,000 unit which would give better coverage of perhaps 4,500 square feet in my large home, but I didn’t want to spend the $$, and have an elaborate technical installation. A local firm gave me a bid for $ 2,100 for their unit which included installation. I bought this instead. This unit is absolutely “plug and play” and puts 3 -4 bars inside the my home covering a 750 Sq foot area. I unboxed it, laid the Yagi antenna in the attic on a cardboard box, extended the cables 30 feet away to our home office, and turned it on. That’s it. Works on Verizon text and voice with 3G. Now..a few points to consider. At this price, you get an excellent design, but you notice the coax cable is the less expensive RG 58, not more expensive “low loss” coax. Also, the coax length from the repeater to the indoor unit is rather limited. Perhaps 10 feet. So this unit has a great price, excellent design, plug and play operation, and in my opinion, scaled for smaller homes. It represents a value “break-through” at $ 100.
