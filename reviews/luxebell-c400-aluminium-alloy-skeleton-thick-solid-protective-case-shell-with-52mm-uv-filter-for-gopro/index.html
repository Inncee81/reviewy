Must be a new model, because NO VIGNETTING!

Ok, so I read all the reviews saying that using the filter in superwide mode will cause vignetting. Well, I received the Luxebell C200 housing, took both videos and photos in superwide mode with the included filter, and it was perfect. NO VIGNETTING AT ALL!!! So, I was pleasantly surprised and happy about that. As for the body itself, it is great. The fit and finish are flawless. My GoPro Hero 4 Silver fits snugly inside and the screw on the side holds the GoPro nice and tight. The best thing about this aluminum body, which makes this one better than the rest, is the slide on back. This protects the built-in screen on the Silver, and gives the whole thing a nice finished look. Unlike the others, where the back screen sits flush with the body, not protecting it. All in all, I am very happy with my purchase, and would recommend this to any GoPro user looking for an aluminum housing.
