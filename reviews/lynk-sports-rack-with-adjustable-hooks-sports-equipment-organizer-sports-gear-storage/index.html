Product Reviews

Perfect for sports organization but lacking in quality

I was sick of having all our sports gear sprinkled throughout the garage. I looked at a lot of different options for balls, rackets and bats and didn’t find anything that seemed to work and kept coming back to this. I finally decided to make the purchase as there weren’t any other options that hit the mark for the organization. When it arrived I was disappointed with the quality of the pieces. The plastic is very cheap and flimsy. Lucky for me I have a very handy husband who was able to get it together and hung. We put basketballs, soccer balls, footballs and a couple of baseballs on the ball racks. The hooks on the side held the baseball bat just fine and we hung life vests on the hooks. This will work great until a kid grabs onto it a little too hard and then it will be done for. If the manufacturer made a durable version of this product it would be 5 stars and I would recommend it to every soccer mom I know.I’d give it 5 stars for organization and 1 star for quality giving it a solid 3 stars.

Pretty good for the mone y.

Overall, not bad for a $25 (or so) rack. Assembly was easy. The only issue I had were the 4 bat hooks that hang on the side (2 on each side). They attach with a built in plastic push clip - one broke. So I cut the rest of the clip off, filed it flat, and used a 5⁄64 bit to drill a hole in the middle of where the push clip was. Reattached with a small 3⁄4” long screw (#6 size maybe? They were leftovers in my misc screws bin) and #8 washer. My repair felt sturdier than the factory clips, so I ripped the other 3 off and did the same with them.

Easy to assemble but be careful putting on the side …

Easy to assemble but be careful putting on the side racket hooks. The first four all broke off before the rack was even mounted. Lynk sports was very responsive and sent me replacements which are working well. Suggest putting these on last after mounting the rest of the rack to the wall. Functionally, the rack works really well at organizing all our small sports equipment.. I’m so glad I’m not chasing the basket balls down the driveway anymore.

It holds a lot of stuff and doesn’t take up too much space. The only reason I didn’t give it 5 stars was because I had a hard time assembling it, and I wish the bottom rack was large enough to hold more larger sized balls (more basketballs) but I can use the lower rack for my son’s basketball shoes instead. So to sum it up: great product for the price, is really sturdy if put together properly, holds a lot of stuff, and keeps my house free of basketballs being tripping hazards.
