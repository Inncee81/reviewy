Great for the price

I took these on a trip to Winter Park, CO. They stayed dry for the entire trip and provided decent insulation. It was rather warm this year with temps between 15-30 degrees F. My hands did get a little cold riding the lift up to the top, but once I started skiing they warmed up. For the price I don’t have any complaints. Dexterity wasn’t great, but I didn’t have any problems hanging onto my poles. For temps under 15 I would recommend something else with a little more insulation.

Ordered XL for long fingers. Warm and nice features.

My hands are normal size, but I guess my fingers are longer than average. If I fit a glove for width (L), then I normally having fabric “webbing” between my fingers because that part is too short. I really didn’t want that situation with these during winter sports, so I ordered the XL. The result: the fingers fit perfectly, and the width was fine (not too big), but the downside is that I have a lot of overlap between my jacket sleeve and the glove.It’s awkward to put on, but once on they are great.They are comfortable, warm, and flexible. I really like the squeegee for goggles on the back of the right thumb (they should show that in the photos!). Funny thing: it has pockets for chemical hand warmers but they labeled them as “key pockets”… maybe they copied a design and didn’t realize.
