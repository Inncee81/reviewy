Just received this Hard Case for out Epson 2045 and …

Just received this Hard Case for out Epson 2045 and had to do some serious customization to it. I found that it wouldn’t fit perfectly until you cut and removed the foam on the bottom of the case. There are three pieces of foam the bottom is approx 1in thick then the middle is about 3 to 4inches the one on the top of the case with all the dimples is glued to the top of the case. So, what I ended up doing is cutting out the bottom part of the first layer to match the middle layer so the top layer didn’t put to much pressure on the top of the projector and mess up the controls. I also trimmed the dimples on the top layer to give the back of the projector more room and so they again wouldn’t mess up the controls.So in summary this case WORKS, I wish it was about 1 1⁄2 to 2inches deeper, removing foam (protection) from the bottom of the case isn’t idle but with the soft feet on the projector I hope will help protect it. I don’t plan on throwing the case off a building or at a wall so I’m sure it will work. I’m keeping the foam I’ve removed to see if I can use it to help protect the bottom of the projector a little more.If you buy this case then expect to customize it, take your time and just don’t hack away at it.Jason
