Fintie, a quality product within your reach!

This Tempered Glass Anti-scratch Screen protector is designed for the Amazon Fire HD 10 (Fire 10.1” HD Display 5th Generation - 2015 release) only. It will not fit for any other Kindle Fire tablets.

Fintie Premium Tempered Glass Screen Protector for Fire HD 10 Tablet Fintie premium glass screen protectors are designed to protect the screen of your devices against every day use, scratches, dust and fingerprint smudges. Manufactured from a high quality chemically treated glass, the screen protectors are lightweight, thin but most importantly, extremely protective. A specially coated adhesive means the screen protector is perfectly fitted to your tablet screen’s display, ensuring that the clarity and sensitivity of your screen are not reduced.

Anti-Shatter: By undergoing more than 4 hours of high temperature tempered treatment, this screen protector’s compression strength reach as high as 125MPa, 5 times higher than regular glass.

Ultra Thin: Add no thickness to device’s astonishingly slim and light design.

No Bubbles or Residue: The oleophobic coating largely reduces smudges and fingerprint. You keep your screen clean by a simple wipe.

Repeatable Installation: If there is spot, bubble, or section that doesn’t adhere, peel it off and clear the spot, reposition it and then it will stick to the screen again automatically.
