Better than expected for Kodi.

Needed a 2nd Pi for Kodi to drive another display. Cloned an existing Pi v2 microSD, changed the hostname, and booted. The v3 Pi handles hidef 1080i mpeg2 broadcast TV without the paid mpeg decoder, which is required on the v2 Pi. Of course, the h.264 hidef video is well supported by both v2 and v3 Pis.The v3 did drop the wired network connection for an unknown reason. Don’t know why. Hasn’t happened again and nothing else on the network has shown any issues.Took about 5 min to stick the heat sinks to the chips and snap the board and case together. Haven’t figured out why tiny screws are included.Using an Android device as a remote - BubbleUPnP is the least hassle, free, controller that also works with our network Plex server.Don’t know anything about wifi or bluetooth performance. Everything is wired here.
