Great toaster for the price

PROS:- The toaster is extremely light, which is great for me, as I don’t have a lot of space in my kitchen and I often need to store it away.- The crumb tray makes removing crumbs from the toaster super easy.- Thanks to its design, this toaster looks much more expensive than it actually is.CONS:- There is no way to tell if the bagel and frozen features are on or off as there is no feedback when you push the buttons.- The plastic at the top heats up quite a bit, pay attention!- You can easily remove crumbs, thanks to the tray but, if you have cheese or larger pieces of breads stuck inside, you are out of luck.- The toasting is not always uniform. This is not a big deal for me, as I like my bread lightly toasted.

Plastic isn’t only for fast food containers & garbage bags

Well built and near perfect operation. I know that plastic is better than metal in many manufacturing applications. There are many uses for plastics other than fast food containers and garbage bags (same use)This is our first wide slot toaster and it will replace a toaster oven that was only used the heat/toast length wise sliced baguettes for our steak sandwiches.This toaster very good control over toast darkness and toasts the same on both sides, the power cord is long enough for our kitchen and it doesn’t have that ugly all stainless steel look.

Good, inexpensive toaster

I am generally not a toast eater. I bought this as I’m having some house guests, and want to be able to serve a full breakfast, which, of course includes toast. I have used it twice….trial runs….and it produced a hot, medium toasted product on the #3 setting. It will live in it’s box in the pantry most of the time, but I’m happy to have it for use on occasions.

Fantastic value at a low price

I bought this for the house I just moved into, since we didn’t have a toaster already. If you aren’t specifically looking for fancy features or higher capacity, this is the perfect choice. It does what it needs to do, and after some experimentation with the dial, I can get my morning bagels toasted just like I’m used to with this thing.

Did not last long

It’s a toaster. I didn’t expect much, but, now it is October, 2017 and it is not working well. The level of toasting that I get at a setting is variable now. I mean, if it’s on medium, sometimes that’s fine, sometimes it’s not enough, sometimes it is burned. I suppose it is planned obsolescence. But I ordered a different brand.
