Product description Intex Composite Boat Motor Mount Kit for Inflatable Boats - 68624E

Amazon.com Kick back and relax while cruising down the river on an Intex inflatable boat with this motor mount kit. The kit lets you attach up to a 3.5-horsepower motor to the rear of your Intex boat, so you can rest your paddling arms and float in comfort. The mount–which is made of heavy duty composite with aluminum support arms – attaches and detaches easily, so you don’t have to spend a half hour fussing with the components each time you want to use the motor. Plus, the mount disassembles quickly when you return back home for easy storage in your garage or closet.

The motor mount kit is compatible with the following Intex inflatable boats:

Challenger 2, 3 & 4 Seahawk 2, 3 & 4 Excursion 2, 3, 4 & 5 Mariner 400 Seahawk II

This mount works great for my Intex Excursion 5 Boat Set - Latest Model. It was a little confusing at first to get set up, but the directions seemed OK. I put a small electric trolling motor on the mount and I was set. Although the mounts will probably take a moderately sized motor, the boat itself cannot handle more than 30 - 40 pounds of thrust. You can feel the back of the boat start to deform and the prop starts to move under the boat. That is not to say that you CANNOT use something larger, just realize that the boat will buckle a bit in the back and you are pushing it…I have used this mount a couple dozen times in the past year without issues.
