POP, DROP, HEAT AND EAT THEN REPEAT: 1. Pop up the popcorn popper and remove the lid. 2. Drop your choice of kernels and seasoning or drop of oil. Gently drop lid back into bowl. 3. Heat for 2-4 minutes* depending on your microwave power. 4. Eat as a healthy snack any time of day! Not for use with convection microwave ovens.
