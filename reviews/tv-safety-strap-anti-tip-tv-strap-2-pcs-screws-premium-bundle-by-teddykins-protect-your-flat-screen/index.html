If you have toddlers at home, I would make sure to measure the distance from your TV screw holes, to the place you want to secure it, and make sure it’s no more than 30-36” because you’ll bottom out the strap rendering it useless (Or potentially dangerous for a small child) Other than that, it seemed very sturdy, and would theoretically work just fine for anything except a big TV)
