Garmin Nuvi 2589LMT

Fits a Garmin Nuvi 2589LMT like a glove. I know the description doesn’t mention the 2589 but the size between the models mentioned and the 2589 are comparable so I took a chance and went with it. It is extremely secure … read: hard as hell to get the GPS in and out.My only criticism is that the power button on the back of the GPS is covered, and that may just be the difference between the 2589 and the models this unit says it will fit. I plan on just drilling out a hole in the back of the cradle to accommodate the difference that way I don’t have to try and remove the GPS from the cradle every time I want to turn it on or off. This is a slight inconvenience, not enough IMO to deduct any points.
