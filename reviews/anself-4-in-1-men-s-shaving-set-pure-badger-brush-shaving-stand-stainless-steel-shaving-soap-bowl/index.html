Quality Kit for an excellent price

This shaving kit is awesome! I don’t know why no one has reviewed it.First off the finish on everything is nice and shiny and has that polished chrome look.The faux ivory is very nice looking and gives the kit a quality feel and look.The shaving bowl is also very nice and is a double walled design so you can put hot shaving cream in it and still hold it comfortably.The brush does need a good rinsing upon arrival.
