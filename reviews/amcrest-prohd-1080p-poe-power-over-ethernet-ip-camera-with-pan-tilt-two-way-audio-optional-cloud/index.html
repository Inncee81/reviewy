This Amcrest ProHD is the current hot setup in inexpensive Pan & Tilt cameras!

This Amcrest ProHD is finally a finished, updated, and easy to setup and use mini PTZ camera.Setup is easy after you install the Amcrest IP camera tool to find the camera on the network. I changed the IP address from the one it got via DHCP. One delight for me was that the firmware on the camera is actually newer than the latest that’s available on the Amcrest web site.I was able to add the Amcrest ProHD to Blue Iris with little trouble. Hint: Add as Generic IP network camera, Supply the IP address and select Discover. Blue Iris will find the capabilities and configure to match. Easy peasy.The 1080P video is very good quality and has an acceptably fast response over Ethernet. The field of view is wider, the colors are accurate and the detail is acceptably sharp - MUCH better than the previous products. Best of all the Pan and Tilt movement is at least twice as fast as the first two generations of Foscam cameras, which I have. The Zoom function is digital zoom, which you could do in the viewer, but it seems useful.I’m going to install mine outdoors in a weatherproof enclosure, but don’t tell anybody.Disclaimer: I paid my own hard earned dollars for this product at full price and GOT NOTHING for my honest review.
