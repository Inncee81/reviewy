Just What I Needed For My Nook!

First, I am not compensated in any way to write this review. That’s important, because, if you are like me, you are wondering how many of these reviews actually come from people who have used the product objectively, and are offering their candid opinion. So, jokes about still using a Nook aside, I have to say that I went from hating my tablet, to loving it. I’m a real estate agent, so I have a serious need for mobile computing, and until I came upon this mouse, my fat fingers were making using my Nook a complete nightmare. But once I had this mouse up and running, my tablet became my new ‘go-to’ computing device, even over my laptop. Starting with my first impressions when I opened the packaging, I felt like the mouse was not as small as I had hoped, but it is still smaller than the bluetooth mouse I use for my laptop. The feel is admittedly cheap and plastic-y. Pairing the mouse to my Nook was not easy…I followed the instructions, but they were not the clearest. Ultimately, I got frustrated and just Google-ed the pairing instructions. I advise you to download the bluetooth pairing app from Google Play, as you will save yourself a ton of frustration. Once the mouse was paired and connected, I was instantly over-joyed that I made this purchase. My Nook is so much more capable, and efficient, without all the multiple finger taps and incorrect selections. Who knew that I could work just as quickly on my cheap Nook as I can on my laptop. And I love that the bluetooth mouse works perfectly while the bluetooth keyboard is paired and connected as well. The battery life has surprised me, as well…I expected a fairly short battery life, but I’ve yet to replace the batteries, and I think it’s been about a month. Also, I love that you can adjust the speed of the pointer…I don’t know if that is unique to the mouse, or if that’s a ‘Nook thing’, but it really helps as the speed of the mouse right out of the package is a little fast, and hard to get used to. The only things I’d like to see changed are the feel of the product, and the ease of connecting it to the computing device. For the price, I am super thrilled with this product, though.
