Product Reviews

Solid Sound, Easy to Setup and Sync.

Very easy to sync with my Samsung TV and no issues with TV losing connection as stated in other reviews. Decent sound for a sound bar. I’m not looking for an audiophile experience. I just wanted a better experience than the speakers on the TV. Sound is clear at all levels and seems to have a good range of highs and lows. Subwoofer isn’t the strongest, but works fine for what I wanted. Spend $1000 and get Dolby ATMOS if you are looking for top of the line sound. Get this if you want something that is a major improvement over the TV speakers with solid sound. It definitely improves the experience of my Samsung 8500 TV.

Great Soundbar If You Can Get It For The Right Price

The soundbar was super easy to connect to my Samsung smart TV. I’m not sure if it’s like this for non-Samsung TVs. All I had to do was turn on my TV and then switch the source on the soundbar to TV and the connection established. Connecting the wireless sub-woofer to the soundbar was also extremely easy. Overall it probably took 5 minutes to connect everything. I love the fact that the sub-woofer is wireless. I have it hidden behind our couch by the corner of our living room. You can also easily pair this with other devices such as your smart phone by switching the source to BT which I use quite often. You do have the option for HDMI as well if your TV doesn’t support any sort of wireless connection. The sound to me is really good. I have yet to crank it up to it’s full potential. Usually at 20 is where I stop with the volume and it’s plenty loud to me. At night time I put it to a 10 and I can still here the sound perfectly and you can hardly hear it in the bedroom behind the TV with the door closed. For sound control it’s pretty simplistic, which is a good thing for me since I’m no audiophile. You have bass, treble, and woofer. There are preset sound effects such as standard, music, movie, clear, sports, and night mode. You can turn the surround sound on or off. It has the capabilities to connect to other Samsung Radiant speakers that are sold separately to enhance your listening pleasure. The speakers can auto detect and turn itself on once your TV is on but it takes about 30 - 40 seconds which is pretty long. The soundbar will also auto shut off when the TV is turned off. This happens instantly. You can use the TV remote to control the volume on the soundbar. Even with some of the negative, the cherry on top is that I got it for $277 on Amazon over the Thanksgiving week which is why the 5 stars. I don’t know if I would pay the standard price of $600 - $700 for it though.
