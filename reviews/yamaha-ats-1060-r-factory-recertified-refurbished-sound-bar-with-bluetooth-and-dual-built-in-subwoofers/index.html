Favorite Sound Bar, Little Much Bass

Pros - This sound bar was a huge step up for my small apartment entertainment system. Plenty of power/volume with full sound because of the built-in subs. The unit is very aesthetically pleasing. Easy to read LED interface. I really like the built-in subs, which avoid extra cabling or wireless paring to a separate speaker.Cons - The low end is REALLY powerful; almost too much. I am glad the subs have a separate level control. Mine is always set very low. A little goes a long way in this case. The speakers are rather directional, which makes the unit better when wall-mounted rather than freestanding.
