Product Specifications

  • 8-inch saw bar cuts branches up to 6 Inch in diameter
  • 2-stroke 25cc engine
  • 12-foot total reach
  • Surefire starting with Spring Assist

  • Pro Link Attachment Capable

    Product Description

The PP258TP Pole Pruner is perfect for light maintenance of tree limbs 6 Inch in diameter and smaller. The PP258TP features an 8-inch saw bar with a 12 foot total reach with the use of the 3 foot extension. A 2 stroke 25cc engine with Surefire starting provides quick starting and plenty of power for your tree maintenance. This item is attachment capable with all ProLink attachments. 2 year warranty.
