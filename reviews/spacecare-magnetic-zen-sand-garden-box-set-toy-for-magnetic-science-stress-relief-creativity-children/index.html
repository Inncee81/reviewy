    Product Description

THE MAGIC OF ZEN GARDEN Zen gardens are a relaxing escape right on your desk. Drawing little circles in the sand. Placing the rocks. Leveling the sand and starting again. Except one day you came in and your little rake was gone. That makes it challenging to be zen.

This magnetic sank set combines natural magnetic sand together with 2 magnets and three 3 real metal & wood tools. Use this sand to have fun with and learn about magnets and magnetism.

MORE MAGIC WITH MAGNETISM Give your zen garden an upgrade with SPACECARE Magnetic Zen Garden. This set uses magnets to let you build stereoscopic patterns.

Similar to a zen garden where you can rake your sand around and keep calm by however that works, the set is a box filled with iron powder that has 4 magnets that intereact with iron powder to make unique and mesmerizing designs in the iron powder. And when you’re ready to start over, just take out the magnets and shake it like an Etch a Sketch, and your ephemeral designs will disappear into a blank canvas of iron powder.
