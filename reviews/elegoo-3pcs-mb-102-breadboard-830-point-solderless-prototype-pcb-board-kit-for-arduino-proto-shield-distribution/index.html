Can’t argue the price for a breadboard. It is what it is.

It’s a breadboard what much can I say, but they don’t stay locked together very well (stated in other reviews). I locked all three and used mounting tape to keep them on a home made project board, but they bend a little and there is a slight gap between the boards. I personally don’t care since I just needed something that is cheap and worked and this was it.

Great

Purchased six of these to attach together for large breadboard. Packaging is great. Quality is great. Colors prints and lines are sharp. Numbers and letters are correctly positioned. No warps and all are a cool white (vs warm white) color. The positive and ground power rails are continuous (i.e. no break points).

Loose holes

A lot of loose holes which is not a problem…just use another…but a bother none the less. I would have much rather had single board made to exact specs.

I like the overall quality, but there is no back plate

I like the overall quality, but there is no back plate. It has the sticker on the back, but without the plate there is nothing to prevent the internal contacts from coming off.I suppose the idea was to only stick the breadboard into another big board, or something.

Great, but not all that flat

Nice set of breadboards, albeit a bit on the bendy end of things. Not sure why it appears to be difficult to make perfectly flat boards of this kind. Good enough for my needs, but be warned if you are expecting to have them sit flat on the table.
