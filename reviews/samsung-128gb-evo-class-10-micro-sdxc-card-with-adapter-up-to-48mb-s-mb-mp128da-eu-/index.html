With the release of the 200GB cards, the 128GB has become a much more affordable option/upgrade. So for value to dollar, I find this to be a good deal. Shooting video and photos are a secondary use for me, and one I mainly assign to the phone’s internal memory than move over to the SD card for storage as that memory is faster.
