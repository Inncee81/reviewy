The original battery in my MacBook Pro swelled up and caused some operational issues. Now that we have the good out of the way, on to the not so good. The battery does work and I am happy to have my PowerBook back in operational condition however, the charge depletes very quickly. In a standby or sleep mode, the battery does well with holding g the charge but wake things up and watch it go. When you need to plug in and charge the battery back up, the battery heats up and the upper left hand corner of the PowerBook gets very warm to the touch. Neither of these two issues are a deal breaker and I knew going in that the quality would not be the normal Apple standards I am use to. This replacement battery is a decent and reliable option for when your original battery gives up the ghost.
