Very cool… just know there are a few caveats…

VERY COOL! One caveat… if you read the detail on the instructions included with the Wizard, this has FAR LESS range then the ST10/10+ transmitter. We’re talking a few hundred feet at best… they don’t tell you that on anything you’ll read on the net or their site. I was disappointed to read that… but… with that in mind, its still very cool. lastly you will likely need a separate device to control most camera functions as the Wizard only controls tilt.A COOL feature of this (not available yet on the ST10/10+) is it will adjust the drones altitude if you walk up hill. if you walk/drive up hill with the ST10/10+ it doesn’t adjust altitude so you could accidently be flying lower then you intended and hit a tree or the ground. using the Wizard, this is automatically accounted for… so a nice feature and one I hope they program into the ground station.
