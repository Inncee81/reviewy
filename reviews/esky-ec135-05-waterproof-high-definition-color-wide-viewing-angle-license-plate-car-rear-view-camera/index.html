Esky EC135-05 Rearview Cameras

Reversing accidents, some know it as Bye-Bye Syndrome, but did you know that it costs lives every year? Research shows that even using a rearview mirror, you could still easily hit objects, animals or even little ones that are too small to be seen through your rear windshield.

Thankfully there is an easy solution. Rearview cameras like Esky EC135-05 can be mounted anywhere on the reverse side of your car. Free from obstructions (like say those of a trailer or the boxes in your trunk) they provide a full view of everything behind your car, in darkness or in daylight.

At Esky, we are especially proud of the EC135-05, our latest rearview camera that offers a wide, 135° viewfinder that’ll have you sure the coast is clear the next time you put your car into reverse. The EC135-05 comes fitted with 7 infrared LED lights, that assist the camera when lighting conditions would be inadequate for most regular cameras.

What’s more 420 TV lines of high resolution deliver a seamless and detailed image. Meaning you rest assured that when Esky gives you the all clear, the way really has been cleared for you to reverse.

Easy Installation

1.Mount the camera to your car’s license plate area, back window, or any other suitable place 2.Adjust the lens of the camera for full rearview clarity. 3.Connect power wire (red and black) to your vehicle DC 12V power system. 4.Connect the video output (yellow wire) of the camera to the display in your vehicle.
