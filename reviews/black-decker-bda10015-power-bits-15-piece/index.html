Compact useful selection without too many needless bits to puff up the size of the set

Pros: Low price. Bit set isn’t bloated with too many bits you don’t need. Doesn’t include those annoying little magnetic-only bits that always pop out of the holder an get lost. Doesn’t come in overly large container with wasted space. The bits and holder will fit in the wasted space of bit sets you probably already have.Cons: more bits than holder will accommodate. Bits don’t last as long as other better bits. But at the price, with so little useless extra bits, it’s a fair tradeoff.
