    Product Description

Important Tips: a) When replacing the motor, please make sure you connect the right motor and right wire, otherwise, the motor will be burnt out. b) The motor will heat up and be broken if you continuously to fly with many batteries. Please take a 5 minutes rest to let motors cool down before you change another battery to fly. c) Please dont fly the drone when the environment temperature is too low, otherwise the temperature difference will stop the motor to work.
