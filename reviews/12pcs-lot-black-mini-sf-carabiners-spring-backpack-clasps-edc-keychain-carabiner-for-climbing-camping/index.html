These are great and actually better quality than I expected

These are great and actually better quality than I expected. …very nice metal finish with no sharp or jagged edges.There are 12 pieces, more than other seller as same price.The eyehole can accommodate 7-strand paracord. However, the clip part is actually a little too small to hook the same piece of 7-strand cord and close the latch. It’s doable, but it takes effort and it’s definitely not quick release. Forget hooking anything larger than that. It does work great in that regard when hooking 275 (5 strand) paracord. So I’d say they’re really made for use with 5 strand cord or smaller. Luckily that’s exactly what I had in mind when I bought them.They came quickly (prime) in excellent condition and as described, so I have no reason not to give these 5 stars.

then you have come across one of the better ones around

If you are looking for a small carabineer to organize items, then you have come across one of the better ones around. Now these are not built for anything heavy duty for heavy lifting. They are prefect for keys simply because they are of a small design which in most cases you will not need a key ring but you can still use one. I did have a couple lock box keys that had a smaller hole but ten seconds on my drill press solved that issue. SO look at your keys and you will note a uniform size of the holes for the most part and my set of 6CM carabineers would fit through those key holes. Please do not limit the use of these guys to just keys for I have one on my vacuum water bottle. I also use a couple to secure my tool kit to my bicycle and they work better than the larger ones I had in place (pulled in tighter for a better fit or more secure binding). The best part I get to retrieve some of my more expensive units from light duty because my girlfriend has ideas. My neighbor has a small dog and has problems with the leash spring clip. I used one of these as a replacement for the original leash or collar clip. It works great for her and has helped to overcome one of the arthritic challenges in her life. Now I would not recommend for a larger dog that has high energy levels. This is a great deal for small projects or applications and I will give a five star just because of the light duty design.
