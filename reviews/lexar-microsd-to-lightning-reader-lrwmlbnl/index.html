Product Specifications

  • Easily offload and transfer content to or from your iPhone or iPad with a micro SD card
  • Great for anyone capturing photos or video with a sports camcorder or aerial camera
  • Small, portable design with a Lightning connector
  • Works with use of free file management app (App Store)

  • Automatically and securely sync files

    Product Description

Size:microSD To Lightning Reader Capturing life in action? With the Lexar micro SD Reader, you can easily offload content from your sports camcorder or aerial camera to your iPhone or iPad. This makes room for more, and allows you to view and playback photos and video—wherever you are. This small, portable reader features a Lightning connector, providing quick file transfer on the go. It works with use of a free file management app from the App Store, and automatically and securely syncs files on the go.
