Fell apart until I fixed them.

The first bottle I used seemed to work well at first. Then the sprayer assembly came off the bottle body and I had the liquid I was spraying on me. The second one worked great. The third one was a repeat of the first. That is when I decided to closely examine the product and found that during assembly two gaskets had been installed on the tubes of the sprayer assemblies of the two bottles that had come apart when used. The extra gasket prevented the sprayer assembly from screwing onto the bottle body properly. Therefore, I cut one gasket off each and now all three screw together well, do not come apart unexpectedly, and so far work great.
