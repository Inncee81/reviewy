    Product Description

Product Packaging:Standard Packaging The RAM-B-149Z-UN7U consists of a zinc coated u-bolt base, double socket arm and universal X-Grip cradle. Included in this package is hardware that will accommodate rails from 0.50” to 1.25” in diameter. The base includes black cover caps to be placed over the exposed ends of the u-bolt. The mount is designed with a RAM 1” diameter patented rubber ball and socket system that has adjustment points at both ends of the double socket arm; this allowing for almost infinite adjustment and perfect viewing angles. The included composite rail adapter can narrow the rail to a minimum width of 0.5”. The included X-Grip cradle has a clean and clever four leg design that sports great holding power without hiding your phone behind foam pads and plastic. The spring loaded X-Grip cradle expands and contracts, allowing for a perfect custom fit of your cell phone. The perfect complement to the modern sleek interior of today’s vehicles, the RAM X-Grip is the cell phone cradle evolved.

The X-Grip includes a device tether recommended for outdoor applications. The cradle is compatible with handheld devices that will fit the dimensions listed below. It is important to verify the dimensions of your handheld device with a case/sleeve/skin when determining the overall size.

Cradle Dimensions: Minimum Width = 1.875” (Minimum Height = 4.25”) Maximum Width = 3.25” (Minimum Height = 2.25”) Depth = 0.875”

Only real issue that I have found with this product would be that the clamp area does not have some sort of maybe like a rubber or foam protection as to not scratch the paint on the handle bar. A solution that I used was duct tape. Yes, you read correct, duct tape. Took a couple small strips and placed them where the clamp would touch the handlebars. Works so far and has not caused any paint issues. Maybe some tape residue but that is easily cleanable. Other than that, it is a very good product.
