Product Reviews

… for my new camera (Panasonic Lumix DMC-ZS50S) and am glad I did

I took a chance on the large case for my new camera (Panasonic Lumix DMC-ZS50S) and am glad I did. The camera fit perfectly, with room to carry an extra battery, small microfiber cloth, and SD card in the separate pocket. It’s nicely padded, well made, and the price is excellent.

Fits Canon PowerShot G16

I was looking for a camera case for my Canon PowerShot G16, which is a relatively large point-and-shoot. This case in Large is a good size for the camera itself. Once I put on the thick neck strap that came with the camera, it became a tight fit, but I could still zip it all the way. The separate compartment is good for storing extra battery and/or memory card.

Works well with a Canon S120

Perfect for my Canon S120, two extra batteries and an extra sim card. It’s not a tight fit and has a little extra room which I like: 1⁄4” on top and 1⁄2” on length. Another recommended “compact” camera case was so light I had trouble closing the zipper and had no room for any accessories. The S120 has exceptionally poor battery life in video mode and you need someplace to put extra batteries or you’re in trouble.It also has a belt loop in the back which you can’t see in the vendor picture. The neck strap seems superfluous as the case is so small. It lives in a man’s purse :-) with my wallet, tablet, knife, etc.

Perfect size for my Canon Gx9

Perfect size for my Canon Gx9. I wanted a case that I could put the camera/battery charger & batteries in. The Case Logic I had ordered before was nice but the battery charger for this camera is fairly large and that case was too small. Price is great, well made and very happy with my purchase. No need to spend more money on a case. I have a slim case for the camera only that will fit in my purse.

Very nice case for the price!!!

This is such a nice case, it works wonderful, feels very durable, and of course works perfect for my Fiio X3II + accessories!

The first one I bought the loop on the case …

The first one I bought the loop on the case that holds the strap broke after a couple of uses. They were kind enough to send me a replacement but that one broke in the same spot after 1 use. Will not purchase again.

Canon G7X Mark II Fits Perfectly!!

Fits my Canon G7X Mark II perfectly with a little room to spare. The extra compartment fits extra batteries and SD cards too. The only thing I had to mod was the carrying loop. When I have the case on my belt, it sat up too high and my shirt looked funny pulled down over it. As you can see in the photo, I just used a paracord bracelet to lower the case a bit so my shirt looked normal. Other than that, I’m happy :-)

Good for a small point and shoot

It’s a little smaller than expected, but it works great. My Nikon Coolpix s9900 fit in it well with no room to spare in the main compartment. The small front pocket fits a couple of batteries and some extra sd cards, but not much more.I took it to Japan for a 3 week vacation and it was the perfect size. It allowed me to take my Nikon on my belt without really bothering me while walking around.I would consider this a Small case not a Medium.
