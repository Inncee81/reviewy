Worth every cent

I demo-ed 5 saddles from the LBS, all women specific and in the $90-130 price range. My A** wasn’t impressed and let me tell you why. I am a small woman, 5’ 3” and 106 pounds and pretty lean in that oh-so-sensitive area. I expect most cyclists are. The Selle Italia Diva Gel Flow has excellent flex on the down stroke in the middle area of the saddle so when your leg is at full extension, the saddle is not pushing back against you and creating pressure. Because of this, I never really feel the saddle beneath me at all, I am at one with it, so to speak, because it moves with me. Whether I am in the dips or up on the bar, this saddle is crazy comfortable.I tend to go on long rides, and spend up to 6 hours at a time on my bike so I really feel like I can give a fair review. I have logged about 200 miles on this particular saddle and LOVE IT. The quality is excellent, and the white leather cleans up very easily with Maybelline Eye Make Up Remover. Try this Saddle, you will not be dissapointed.
