Great addition to my Dremel 395

Dremel 4486 MultiPro Keyless ChuckI always found collets to be a PITA, and now consider this keyless chuck to be a must-have companion to my Dremel rotary tool. It’s so useful that I don’t understand why Dremel doesn’t include it with every rotary tool.This keyless chuck is easy to install and makes bit changes very fast and very easy. As others have noted, the chuck will secure shaft sizes from the incredibly tiny up to 1⁄8”, which covers the vast majority of available accessories (and 100% of the accessories that I own).Install it by pressing the shaft brake and screwing the chuck onto the shaft. Continue pressing the shaft brake and use the wrench included with your rotary tool to tighten the chuck onto the shaft.To change bits, press the shaft brake and unscrew the chuck by hand (turning to the left) until it opens large enough to accommodate your bit. Insert your bit, hand tighten the chuck to secure your bit (turning to the right) and let go of the shaft brake. It only takes a couple of seconds. Brilliant!
