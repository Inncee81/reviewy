Good QPR, Works, Assembly is Reasonable…

Some of the threaded holes were badly done, but a drift or thread chaser will help, as will patience.There are pages of golf club stands on the net, some elegant/fancy, some pieces of junk. This one works, and on a fire sale is a very good QPR unit.
