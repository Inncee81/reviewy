Product Reviews

I love this little keyboard

I love this little keyboard. When it arrived, I was a little skeptical because the “e” key clicked weird. I jiggled it a little and that fixed it. This keyboard is exactly the width of IPad Air/Air2/Pro 9.7. It also attaches magnetically to the Ipad if you have a Smart Cover installed. Just place it on the top of the Smart Cover and it holds pretty well. The back is aluminum, the top is plastic. The keys have a good feel to them and have a backlight you can turn off or on (also has 3 levels of key brightness!). Additionally, the top “function key” row has different functions for IOS- brightness, home screen, search, safari, play/pause/forward/back, and volume control. There is also a “Lock” button that turns your ipad off. This keyboard was designed to work with computers with bluetooth as well as android tablets, though I haven’t tried it with one. I got the gold one because my air 2 is gold and it matches great. Overall, I would highly recommend this keyboard to anyone with an ipad who wants a slim, lightweight, portable keyboard that’s easy to type on!
