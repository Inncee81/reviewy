Good stuff, wrong fit.

These items were one of the suggested items that Amazon stated that people order with a Nikon D5300. None of the items in the kit that attach to the lens of the camera fit. I would just suggest that if you buy this to make sure it’s compatiable with your lens or camera before spending the $70+ dollars on it. Other than that the items came in nice condition. Cannot give a rating on whether it is good or not because it does not fit the camera.

Don’t buy for Nikon D5300 55mm

The kit is cool however, If you have a Nikon D5300 55mm don’t bother buying cause the lenses don’t fit as they are for a 52mm camera lens. Make sure you look on the lens of the camera and match up whether or not you have a 55mm. It’s very important!! It’ll save you so much time. Especially on returning and shipping.

Very nice for the price!

I got everything listed in the kit. The mini tripod is pretty pointless, as it couldn’t even hold my camera body without a lens, let alone with it on! The macro filter attachment works great. The wide angle has some distortion around the edges, but nothing a crop couldn’t fix. The bag is very nice, and holds my camera, 2 lenses and all accessories. I was pleasantly surprised at the quality of the tripod. I thought I was going to get another cheap, flimsy one like i already had. This one was much sturdier, and has a built in level, which is nice. I have yet to try the shutter remote, however, I don’t have much faith in it working. But, with everything you get in this bundle for the price, I’m not taking the loss too hard lol. I was also happy to receive 2 Sandisk brand memory cards, and a USB memory card reader, which works perfectly. I’d say overall, this was a good deal and most of the things are useful, and as advertised. My photo below was taken using the neutral density filter that came with the kit.

Very Unstable Tripods

Nice kit but both tripods are extremely unstable. I will not use them with the Nikon 3300 for fear that they will topple over and damage the camera.

So far so good, I am still learning how to use everything

This is a bundle for beginners. It is great for just that!!

Outgrew my bag quickly, but the card, lenses and the remote are amazing! the tripod is good as long as their is no wind and a great solid ground. This is a bundle for beginners. It is great for just that!!
