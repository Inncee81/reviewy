Good device for the price

Good device for the price. I like that there are no contracts with the company and you can cancel your service at any time. Customer service is a little iffy but they got my questions answered. The battery on the device does not last as long as you’d hope. If my device has very little movement through the day I can get a week out of the charge. If there’s a lot of driving around, like on weekends, the battery dies after 2 or 3 days. The charging port and charger could be better. I feel they are flimsy and can break very easily if not carefully plugged in. The charging port cover is not very secure and sometimes falls open. The power button SUCKS!! You push it and have no idea if the device is off or on, you push it again holding it down to try to get the device off/on and still nothing. The instructions for the power button are not clear at all on how to power this device off/on. So, you end up just pushing the power button all different ways until you see lights flash and you know it’s on. That is very annoying. The web site to monitor the devices is pretty good but setting up geofences can be a hassle. Getting text notifications doesn’t work, only getting g email notices for geofences. The mobile app is very limited, all you can do is monitor the devices current location and the “breadcrumbs”. The time stamps on the breadcrumbs aren’t always accurate. There’s no live tracking on the mobile app.
