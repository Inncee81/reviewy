Product Reviews

Stable, perfect weight, water tight, comes with all your accessories, easy to use - great value

This is my first Kayak purchase so take my review with a grain of salt as I don’t have a ton of other product experience. All that said this thing feels like the perfect weight / balance mix. It’s just light enough for me (5’11” / 182Lbs) to mount it on top of my FJ Cruiser without any help. On the water it feels stable. I’ve been out twice now, both times fishing on a relatively calm lake… the first time was with a buddy who’s about my same size. Our combined weight was about 360-370Lbs and it felt like the Kayak could have shouldered another 200Lbs no problem. The Pole mounts are easily removable and can adjust to your preference. I didn’t find them getting in the way and we were managing 2 poles, a net & both paddle sets. The paddles conveniently & easily snap apart and can be mounted in slots right behind where you’re sitting. This is great regardless of whether you’re fishing (and catch something / need to put the paddle down) or if you’re in another situation where you need to free up your hands. There’s a nice sized open aired compartment in the back that comes with overhead netting. I had a small tackle box back there along with some other equipment, no problems.There are two water tight compartments that’ll hold your phone, wallet, keys and a snack or two…there’s one of these in front of each seat and it seems to be of fairly high quality.For now, I’m in love! Very happy with the purchase and see this guy being put to use for many years ahead. BTW, shipping delivered on time & was a joy to work with (they set up a time with you to take delivery)
