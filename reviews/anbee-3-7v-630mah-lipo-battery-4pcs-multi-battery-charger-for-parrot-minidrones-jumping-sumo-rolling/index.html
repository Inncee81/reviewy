Awesome with a few minor flaws

These batteries so far work great. Though they have a higher mAh rating than the OEM Parrot batteries, I don’t notice that much more flight time maybe 10% more; which is fine as they are much cheaper. Quality overall seems fine, with only a complaints.First, the batteries are a bit tight going into the charger; they fit fine in my drones however. Second, the charger’s lights are red whether or not a battery is inserted or charging. The tight fit issue I mentioned makes you question whether you’ve completed the circuit or not. It would have been better the LED for the unused slots were off or constant red; then red or flashing red, for charging; then green for full charge.I don’t mind not receiving a micro USB cable as they are ubiquitous and one comes with the drone already. Also, I would’ve loved it it if it were 5 slots, that way you can charge the four Anbee batteries along with the one from Parrot you get with the drone.

Better capacity than OEM battery

These batteries have been great so far. They’ve been through 4 flights each, and seem to last a little longer than the original battery that came with the drone. The charger is handy for keeping them organized during outings, too. I put it in a coat pocket and just pop a fresh battery out as needed and put the dead one in the other pocket.I would definitely recommend this product.
