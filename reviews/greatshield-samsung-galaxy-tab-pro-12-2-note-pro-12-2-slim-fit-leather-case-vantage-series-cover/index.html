Great case with big glaring flaw.

Overall design is nice. Cut outs are good. Capture is good. Like the fit & finish. The lip secures the lid when closed and gives it a little tilt when folded under (but read below while you’ll never use it that way). Doesn’t feel cheap in any way.The bad. The magnet is too strong. When the Lid is folded behind it turns the screen off and the tablet is unusable. Have to fold the Lid back one section which is a bit annoying. Love the case, but this could be a deal breaker. They need to address the design flaw. Not sure if I should give the case 2 or 3 stars yet.Update. It’s going back. Having to fold the back cover in half so the tablet stays awake is just annoying. Shame because the lip at the top would give it the perfect tilt to kill any glare from the lights above. I just don’t want to have to fiddle with the back all the time when it should just be able to sit flat. Shame. It is a very well made case, and a step above the typical plastic case with glued on flip cover. I just can’t live with the flaw. If you can, it’s a great case. Tried to see if there was a way to disable the auto screen off, but haven’t found anything as of yet. I’ll revisit if I can find a way.Back to the Verizon case for now. I can’t stand the tent mode on it as a stand, but it’ll do for now.
