Much more comfortable than the regular brush head

I have lupus and sjogren’s and my gums are very fragile. My son (12 years old) has some sensory integration issues that make him hate brushing his teeth. We are both using these brush heads in our e series sonicares, and both of us like them much better than the heads we were using.I was using a regular brush head and dealing with a sink that looked like a crime scene every morning and every night, because the toothbrush was shredding my dry gums. My son was using the compact brush head, which he complained “feels like a jackhammer.” I asked him how this one is, and he said, “like a soft jackhammer. Better.“The brush cleans as well as the others. Doesn’t hurt my sensitive son. Doesn’t tear my sensitive gums. I’m keeping these on subscribe and save.

Defective right out of the box!

We have never gotten a defective replacement brush until we ordered this one. One of the brushes seems to work just fine, but the other just barely vibrates. I will not be buying any more of these from Amazon. I really am disappointed.
