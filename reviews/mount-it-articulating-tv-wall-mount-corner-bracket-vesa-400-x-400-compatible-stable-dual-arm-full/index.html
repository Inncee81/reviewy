    Product Description

Mount-It! Articulating TV Wall Mount

Mount-It! MI-4461 is a wall mount that will safely install your TV on the wall. It has full motion capabilities to adjust the position of your TV just right. 

This bracket is a durable, low profile, full-motion articulating TV wall mount that offers extreme versatility with a maximum extension of 13.5” from the wall. It is suitable for Smart TVs such as LCD, Plasma, LED or OLED displays that range from 20 to 50 inches. The MI-4461 dual arm design is constructed with a high-strength steel for its durability to ensure the arms will not sag. This design also allows for the ability to swivel 180 degrees and tilt up to 12 degrees with ease.

This mount offers a sleek, high-tech look, adjustable mounting brackets, a free cable and the necessary hardware with instructions - making this mount user friendly. 

Features:  Fits most of 20-50” TVs  Collapses to 2.7” low profile, Extends 13.5” from the wall.  Tilt, Swivel and Extend/Collapse function  Easy tilting angle adjustment  Heavy duty steel construction and high-tech welding technology  Free HDMI CABLE  Includes all necessary mounting hardware 

Specifications:  TV Size Range: 20” - 50” (some even up to 55” depending on VESA bolting pattern and the TV weight)  Max TV weight: 115 lbs  Mounting Pattern: Universal and up to VESA 600 x 400. Fits VESA 400X400, 400X300, 400X200, 300X300, 300X200, 200X200, 200X100, 200X150, 100X100, 75X75mm  Tilt: up to 12 degrees down or 2 degrees up and any angle in between. Swivel: 90 degrees to left or to right  Extension from the wall: between 2.7” to 13.5”
