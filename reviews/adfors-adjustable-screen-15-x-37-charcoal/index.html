Great for the price, these are large and seat fairly well with my old wood framed windows. Landlord is “frugal” and won’t insert actual screens into my existing windows. This gave me an inexpensive option to allow for fresh air. The frame isn’t very sturdy but it holds up in the window. Beware of Amazon and their poor packaging, I had one arrive bent. These would be better packaged if they were sandwiched between some sturdy cardboard.

Great screen at the best price

These are great and at a much better price than I could find locally. I did not find them flimsy and they keep my big cat safely inside (and he often lays down against it). It does have a very small space at the top when installed where I suppose tiny bugs could get in. I am in the Pacific NW and I haven’t had any issues. However if I did, I think putting a strip of sticky-backed foam insulation on the bottom of the window would fill that space and eliminate the problem.

Does the job but flimsy construction

You pay what you get for with these. While they are cheap in price and are certainly made of screen, the frame is flimsy and torques when handling. Don’t get me wrong, they sit in a window and keep bugs out so it does the job just as well as any other, that said, if you’re looking for a bit more heft would suggest the Frost King brand which are a little brighter, in terms of seeing through the screen, and less flimsy of a frame.

These screens do the trick!

I read some of the less favorable comments about people not being pleased with these screens. However, I have to say, they work perfectly for our situation. We are using them in our garage windows (the garage is 60 years old, windows with no screens) where we wanted added ventilation. They fit the windows without any issue. It’s a nice snug fit, both horizontally and vertically. No gaps, no screen warping ~ nothing. We ordered this smaller sized screen for the smallest window and have just now placed an order for two of the medium sized screens!Yes, there are more rugged, more expensive screens out there ~ but for us, these suit the purpose!
