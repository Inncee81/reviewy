Shelure fishing coiled lanyard takes great convenience to fishermen, free their hands by connecting it with their equipments. This small lightweight gadget one direction ended with metal carabiner clasp is attached with your clothes or other parts at your side, the other ended with circle ring connects with easy-losing stuff, such as fishing pliers, which ensure you will never lose your expensive fishing tools when face urgency situation. Besides, this spring rope with great elasticity could stretch to the length your need, and handle your equipments portably. In a way, this great coiled tether is well-suited for anglers and other outdoor enthusiasts.
