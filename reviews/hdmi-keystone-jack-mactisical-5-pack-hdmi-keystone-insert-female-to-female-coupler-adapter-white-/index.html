    Product Description

Color:HDMI

Keystone Jack Plug & Patch This MACTIS HDMI keystone jack is designed to fit any standard keystone wall plate. It features a gold-plated female connector on each end, provide a simple and secure way to connect HDMI cables into a wall plate. This keystone has no effect on the signal and therefore supports all High Speed HDMI features, including 3D, 4K, Audio Return Channel and Ethernet features of your HDMI cables.

Features: Easy snap-in retaining clip ensure a secure and corrosion free connection RoHS compliant and Field replaceable. Pigtail. Perfect for Conference Room or A/V Theater Wall Plate

Specification: Connector 1: HDMI A Female Connector 2: HDMI A Female Color: White

Package including: 5x HDMI Keystone Jack Inserts(wall plates are not included)

Installation Tips: - HDMI Keystone Jack Insert is vertically oriented - Hook the bottom lip into the cut-out while holding the keystone insert at a slight angle - Rotate the insert upward to snap the retaining clip on the top into the cut-out until it snaps into place - Connect an HDMI 90 or 270 degree angle adapter to route cables in tight spaces - Test all the cabling with the keystone insert before installing permanently in the wall
