First Impressions Of A Keen Critic–One Of Satisfaction!

I ordered my Mosiso Plastic Hard Case in Wine Red on Sunday June 25th, and it was delivered on Saturday July 1st. I’d just picked this item up today July 3rd, so I’ve only had it for a few hours now. At first glance, the case appeared to be a plastic cheap looking object wrapped in a simple plastic film, within blue and clear plastic packaging. I inspected the object before opening it for scratches or cracks, seeing as the vessel it came in was so simple–I’d expected a defected item. I immediately tore through the unsightly plastic in a hurry, as I was curious to see if my product would some how start to look like my expectation of it–and yes, yes it did!The case is a smooth, matte finish and the color is just as it appears online–with a tinge more dusty brick to it, not as much of a dark-magenta-cranberry hue (if your eyes see anything like mine!). Although I’m in love with the matte aspect of the casing, and the color is beautiful, I do wish I could see the Apple symbol through the case (outside of the dim orange-looking symbol that appears when lit)–but that’s just asking for too much at this point, it’s a MATTE case for crying out loud! This is a total steal if you ask me. For $14 you get a clean, perfectly fitting case, a matching keyboard cover, and a SCREEN PROTECTOR. I’ve had my Macbook Pro 13 for two and a half years and this is the first screen protector I’ve had! And it looks great! I’ve also always had a problem with getting my keys dirty–so the keyboard cover is a VERY welcome addition as well (I attempted to clean off my keyboard before putting on the new accessories so you may notice some water spots in the attached photos). Although some of the key lettering isn’t PERFECTLY aligned, the fit works out great–and I’m getting used to the new feel of typing with it. No complaints here–I’m just a tough critic!For the price, the accessories, and initial product quality–I give this product an A+. I look forward to spending more time with this product to see how durable it is, as that is the most important aspect of buying a case for your laptop (aside from the pretty colors, of course). I’ve had a Speck case for the last two years, the bottom cracked a year in, I’d mysteriously lost one of the little foot buttons, and it collected a lot of dirt within the casing–that was a $50 product. I hope to get at least a few good miles out of this one. For now, my first impressions are that of a very satisfied customer, and I’d definitely buy again just to build up a collection of attractive cases.
