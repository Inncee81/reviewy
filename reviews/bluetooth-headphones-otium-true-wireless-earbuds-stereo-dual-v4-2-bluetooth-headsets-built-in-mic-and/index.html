About Two using mode of Otuim T2 Mini Bluetooth Headphones 

  1. Dual Ear Mode:

Press left and right mini earbuds together about 2 second, two indicators light turn blue first.Then flash alternately with red and blue light, that means the earbuds are pairing together. Open the bluetooth of your phone or other devices, to search “Otium T2” and pair it. 

（Note: Only the only the main headphone (Left) can transfer voice signal when you use dual ear mode for hands-free calling. ）

  1. Stereo Mode:

Press one of the earbuds about 2 second, open the bluetooth of your phone or other devices, to search “Otium T2” and pair it, then use one earbuds to listening music or handsfree calling.

Same pairing method with the first earbud, you could open the second earbud to pair with another phone.

（Just notice don’t open them at the same time if you want to use one of them independently.）
