Great quality, love the color

No chemical smells! Yay! Great quality, love the color.The only thing I don’t like about the blocks is that they are 9x6x4. I thought this was standard size when I ordered (& the same size as what I use in Iyengar yoga). However, I later discovered that the blocks we use in yoga class are actually 9x6x3. They are smaller & easier to use, turn & grip with my small hands. Unfortunately, I wasn’t able to find the smaller 9x6x3 size made by Reehut on amazon. So I ordered from a different seller.

My New Go To Yoga Blocks

Great blocks, especially for the price.Usually when you get something foam/plastic at this price point the product reeks of chemicals. I was pleasantly surprised to find that wasn’t the case with these. Even putting my very sensitive nose right next to it it hardly had a smell at all. The density isn’t so hard that it hurts your hands, but also dense enough that it has no give to it and just has the support you need. I’ve used blocks before that were painfully hard and these hit the balance perfectly.The only downside is that it has the familiar suede-like finish that takes on scratch marks very easily. This isn’t a problem for me at all, but I know some people like theirs to remain looking pristine. So you might want to keep that in mind if that’s a consideration for you.All in all I think this is a great product and would recommend it.

I love these yoga blocks and I think the quality is …

Overall, I love these yoga blocks and I think the quality is mostly fantastic. My only frustration with them is that I wish they were a bit stronger. When I’m lying on my back, balancing a block at its highest level to stretch my sacrum, it bends a bit underneath the full weight of my body (118 lbs.), which interrupts the posture and leads to readjustments that shouldn’t be necessary. For beginner yogis who are using these as a balancing aide– I give these 5 stars!
