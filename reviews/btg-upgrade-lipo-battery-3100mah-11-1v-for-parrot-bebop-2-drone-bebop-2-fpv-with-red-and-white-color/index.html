Good charge, questionable construction

The battery works well, but the construction is flimsy. As you can see in the photo, the little “feet” that help keep the battery seated to the done break off. This isn’t a deal-breaker, as the battery will still stay attached without them. However, if they break off in the drone body, (which happened to me) it’s a huge pain to get them out, and will prevent other batteries from fitting on the drone.Aside from this the batteries work well.
