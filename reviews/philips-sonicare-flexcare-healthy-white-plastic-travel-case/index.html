Good travel case for your sonicare even the HX6211 series

UPDATE (10/17/2015): After my purchase of this case, my wife purchased it as well. It seems like there are two different versions of this case, one fits the HX6211 the other doesn’t. The one that fits is when I bought it from “Genuine OEM Parts” and the only Philips logo is on the tab. My wife’s purchase which was from “Square Imports LLC” has the Philips logo on the top of the case, this does not fit. I will post up pics of the two cases. Hope this helps clarify why my case fit and others did not with the HX6211 brush.—————————————It fits the HX6211 series sonicare. It’s not a perfect fit but almost perfect. The bottom of the sonicare is a tiny bit wider than the “cradle” of the case that it sits on. But it’s almost negligible. It still closes perfectly. The only reason I didn’t give it 5 stars is the that the clasp of the case isn’t the best. It feels weak and doesn’t give me the assurance that it will keep the case closed while traveling in a bag. It feels like it’s really easy to open. Only time will tell regarding that as it was okay for my first trip with it in my carry-on bag.

Fits Sonicare EasyClean

I have the Sonicare EasyClean HX6511/50, but the description says it fits the Flexcare and Healthy White. I called Sonicare, and they said each of these models is the same length. My EasyClean will fit, although it moves around a little bit. The latch on the case is just OK. Overall, I’m pleased with the case although wish they made one specifically for the EasyClean that was available for purchase separately (not bundled with the toothbrush).

Works for Oral B Pro 1000

Got this as a carrying case for my husband who has an Oral B Pro 1000 toothbrush. It doesn’t fit perfectly in the slot as the Oral B is a little longer than the Sonicare, but it does fit and will snap closed securely. I think all the Oral B toothbrushes are the same size.

Must have.

Bought this as a replacement. Over time, the plastic latch on my old case started to weaken and would no longer stay closed. It got misplaced somehow, which quickly taught me a lesson about not packing your electric toothbrush away in a case when traveling—the little metal piece that you snap the brush head onto got knocked around in my bag and then became loose, causing the head to swivel around too much when brushing. Oy! If you don’t have a case, get one.

Did not fit my series 2

Recieved today, however despite multiple reviews this case does not fit Philips Sonicare 2 Series Plaque Control Sonic Electric Rechargeable Toothbrush, Coral,HX6211/28. Picture is with the brush inside (lid does not come close to closing).
