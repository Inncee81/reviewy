I purchased this case for my new YI 4K Action Cam because I didn’t want to scratch/ruin my action cam early in its life. This is a nice case from Yi and I really liked that it comes with a protective lens. I’ve taken pictures with and without this protective lens, and both were great! The case is a pretty snugged fit. I had to push my camera in a bit harder than I wanted to (but not too hard so my camera is still in perfect condition). I wish it had a small slot for a wrist string so that I could hang the camera off my wrist as an additional security (don’t want to drop my phone). The one issue I have with my case, however, is that I am unable to charge my camera while it is in the case. There is an opening for the charger, but after pulling out the cover where the microUSB goes into the camera, I am unable to plug the cable into the camera due to the thickness of the cable/case. So every time I need to charge my camera, I need to take it out of the case which can be a bit of a hassle because I have to push it in and out of the case. I hope the case will stay in its good condition though! Below is a picture I took with the protective lens on.
