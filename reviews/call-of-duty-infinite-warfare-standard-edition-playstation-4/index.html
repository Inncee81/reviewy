A Decent Venture into Sci-fi for COD

I know it’s not a popular opinion, but this campaign is the best one I’ve honestly played in the Call of Duty franchise. As a hardcore Sci-fi fan I was reminded of elements of the Expanse novels from James Corey. I found the companions in the main story line to be better developed than several past protagonists in the franchise and the gun play and weaponry to be absolutely delightful. The dog fighting in particular is actually quite well done, which I did not expect. If you are a sci fi fan this game has is in spades.
