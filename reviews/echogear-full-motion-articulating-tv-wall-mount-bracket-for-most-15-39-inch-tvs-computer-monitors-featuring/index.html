very good mount with very good instructions

Not much bad to say about this mount. Comes with an abundance of hardware to make adjustments and fit just about any monitor or TV imaginable. The true ‘deal maker’ is the instruction packet. Actual words with complete sentences to accompany the clear pictures. The parts are separated into separate, well-labeled packets. There are very clear mounting suggestions and warnings.My only minor gripe is that the monitor tilt articulation requires an allen wrench. There is a knob on one side (right side) which implies one should be able to tilt-and-tighten fairly easily. Alas, the weight of even a 17” LCD is too much if the screw on the other side isn’t 100% tight. And, when it is tight, the tilt won’t happen whether the knob is loos or not. So, the knob on the right seems pointless. The screw that seems to control the tilt requires an Allen wrench. Although one is supplied that fits the other articulating joints, this one is smaller. That’s said, it’s a minor inconvenience. I used one of my own and I’m all set up. Maybe 15 minutes in total.
