    Product Description

Color:White/Black Crystal Clear Day Video & Night Vision

The HelloBaby HB24  is a wireless video baby monitor that enables you to always keep an eye on your baby. It provides a crystal clear image without grainy or pixelated texures.   Rest assured knowing you can see your baby day and night, including overnight, with automatic night vision. Infrared LEDs automatically adjust to the level of light in the room and transmit a clear black and white image in low-light and darkened room conditions.   Portable Handhold Monitor & Two-way Talk

The parent unit monitor has 950mAh rechargeable battery lasting for 7-8 hours with screen-on, range up to 960 feet, allowing parents to move freely throughout the house and keep baby in view.    You could talk back to your baby to comfort him/her via talk button when he/she is crying.   Sound Activated LEDs Indicator & Screen Off, Audio Only Mode

A row of LEDs on parent unit allows you to see the sound of your baby voice alerting you if he/she is crying out when you have the volume turned down. The function provides assistance for poor hearing person.   Audio only mode turn the video baby monitor to audio baby monitor if you don’t want to disturb by the video. It is power saving mode, battery consumption reduce -50%. You could turn on the display when you want to check your baby.   Temperature Monitoring & Lullabies Playing

The child unit is equipped with a room temperature sensor which is reported temperature on parent unit display, let you know your baby is comfortable and safe.   Lull your baby to sleep with one of 8 polyphonic lullabies or comfort him/her when he/she wakes up.
