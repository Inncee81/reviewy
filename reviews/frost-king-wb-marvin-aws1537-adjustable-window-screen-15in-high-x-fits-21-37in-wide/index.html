Ahhhhhhh fresh air!

We recently rented a house in NC that has no screens in any of the windows. Seriously…. 17 windows in the house and not a single screen. How do you have a house in NC without screens in the windows??? Well anyway,we purchased several of these screens to use now that the weather has cooled off and the a/c has been turned off. They do the job… allow us to get some air into the house and keeping most of the bugs out. The screens just sit in the window and for us, we have some small gaps between the window and screen that have allowed a few of those nasty stink bugs in… but if they’ve let 2-3 in I’m sure they’ve kept 20-30 out, so that’s not a bad result in my book.
