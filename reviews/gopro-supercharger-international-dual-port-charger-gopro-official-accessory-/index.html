Product Specifications

  • The compact design of Supercharger lets you take it everywhere
  • The 27.5W Supercharger USB-C port charges fast-charge compatible GoPro devices up to 20 to 70% faster than a conventional charger.
  • Supercharger can power your GoPro while recording for extended uses like time lapse capture
  • Supercharger comes with AC outlet adapters for US, UK, EU and AU style wall sockets.

  • Supports normal charging for all other GoPro devices and works with most devices that can be charged via USB.

    Product Description

The fastest way to charge your GoPro, the 27.5W Supercharger (International Dual-Port Charger) lets you charge two GoPro devices simultaneously-anywhere in the world. The USB-C port offers 20 to 70% faster charging on fast-charge compatible devices compared to using a conventional charger. You can also continuously power your GoPro during longer captures. Includes interchangeable AC plugs for US, UK, EU and AU style wall sockets.
