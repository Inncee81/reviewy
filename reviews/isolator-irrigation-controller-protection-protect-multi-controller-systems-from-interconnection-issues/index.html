(FROM MANUFACTURERS WEBSITE) If you have multiple controllers on your system, you need ISOLATOR. When two or more controllers need to operate the same pump or master valve, they become interconnected. When this happens, feedback, phasing and polarity differences can plague your system. These can cause false start times, blown fuses and even blown controllers! ISOLATOR is the easiest way to solve these problems and protect your controllers from each other. ISOLATOR provides a safe, effective method of operating a multi-controller while protecting it against future problems
