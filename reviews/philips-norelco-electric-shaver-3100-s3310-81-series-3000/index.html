Product Reviews

No need to twist my arm

After almost forty years with a razor and cream, I bought this Norelco recently to simplify left-handed shaving with a broken right arm. Part of the reason I resisted electric shavers for so long is that the one time I tried one, I had a horrible experience, like scraping my face with broken glass. I was emboldened to order this one after experimenting with my son’s electric shaver, which seemed to work fine and caused no discomfort. I am happy enough with this purchase that I expect to continue using it at least as often as not after my arm is healed. With practice it shaves almost as close and quickly as a blade, without the mess or the need to coordinate shaving and showering to ensure a moist face. The heads flex in a few more directions than my son’s low end Norelco, making shaving just a tad more convenient still than with his. I made sure when buying it that it would work directly from the power cord in a pinch; however, unlike some of my older battery powered devices, the “battery low” indicator has so far prevented the unpleasant surprise of a dead battery.The closest thing to a negative that I can find with this gadget is that cleaning it is not quite as convenient as the advertising might make you think. Yes, you can pop the head open and rinse it, but you still need to take the thing apart every few days and clean the individual pieces. Otherwise, those gunked-up innards will make shaving less effective and be no sight for the faint of heart.On the whole, however, I’m quite satisfied with this device. You wouldn’t need to twist (or break) my arm to get me to buy it again.

Quality razor with great performance.

I bought this shaver to replace a similar style 10 yr old Phillips shaver. I had a great experience with the previous one (durability, closeness of shave, skin-friendliness) so was hoping for a repeat performance with some improvements.The Phillips 3100 serves as almost exact replacement, exhibiting minimal skin chafing, a relatively close shave, and long battery charge (still haven’t had to recharge). The only con is the shave isn’t any closer than my old shaver and slightly noticeably longer than a straight razor.
