Product Reviews

Overpriced

Way overpriced for what it is. As others have noted item is not as pictured. Has only 1 GPU/riser support rail not two as shown. I added a second one in order to mount my risers in place. One of the screws on the bottom was not installed far enough and scratched my wooden floor when unit was moved (empty). Bottom shelf is NOT pre-drilled as shown in image. Bottom shelf is supposed to support motherboard and power supply but is only 1⁄4” plywood. Will flex and warp unless you add reinforcement underneath. It is fairly compact but because of this the frame will not fit 2 power supplied unless you mount the PSUs exhausting to one side, in that case part of the PSU exhaust will be blocked by corner posts. ATX power supplies will not fit vertically. Came with some rather large wood screws to mount motherboard and GPUs. I’d recommend using smaller screws and remember to pre-drill when mounting GPUs or the wood will split!

Well build open air case

First of all, this case is well build and solid. I paid way too much for it tho and see the price came down and it is now prime so no shipping costs, which I also paid. Besides that, it has pre-drilled holes for the motherboard and includes all the standoffs and screws needed. Also has pre-drilled holes for zip ties and correct hole locations for both an SSD and the power supply. This case is much cheaper than quite a few of the other cases available. I chose not to build my own and go this route to save time. It can stack but you will want to install a bracket on the legs so the case on top is more stable. I plan on buying another one very soon for a 2nd miner. I am not going to stack but rather place in a white bread type rack for better air flow.

Holds 7 card great.

This rig is awesome. The only problem is when it’s loaded with two power supplies and a motherboard, it starts to sag. It holds the cards great. I’m using the one I bought as a model to build my own. The materials are cheap and I’m putting a piece in between the base pieces to reinforce the load from the additional power supplies.My setup has 7 Geforce GTX 1070 cards in it.
