Like a hot knife through butter.

This metal grinding wheel is a bit on the expensive side, but they’re one of the best accessories you can buy for a rotary tool. I’ve used them on metals ranging from brass to steel. The metal just disappears effortlessly. So far the grinding wheel shows no wear, so I expect it to last a long time. Overall, I’m very happy with it.Be aware that it’s only 1.5” in diameter, so if you need to cut through more than half an inch, you’ll want something bigger.

GREAT FOR SMALL JOBS

These are great for small jobs. I have been using the cutting wheels to do my grinding so it was nice that they came out with these for smoothing things out and not wearing out in just a few minutes. Quality product, fast shipping, and fair pricing. Perfect sale if you ask me. Thank you Amazon.com.
