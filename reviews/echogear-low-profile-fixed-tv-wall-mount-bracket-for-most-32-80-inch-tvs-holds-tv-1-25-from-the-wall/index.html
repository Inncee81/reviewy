FANTASTIC!!! If you are in the market for a frame bracket to hang your flat screen TV, then look no further! I was very impressed how well thought out this wall mount is. I chose the one that mounts the TV flat onto the wall, with NO tilt, NO articulating arm, NOTHING. Just flat against the wall. ECHOGEAR does offer other models of wall mounts that provide these capabilities.Everything that you will need to mount your TV onto a wood frame wall with 2X4, or any 2X framing is included with the one I ordered. If you are going to mount this onto a concrete wall, or a masonry wall, then you will need to furnish the anchors yourself. I am not sure if ECHOGEAR offers them with anchors instead for a wood frame wall.The wall mount allows you to anchor onto a 16” or 24” on center wall framing. The wall mount consists of 2 main sections: the square frame that goes on the wall, and the 2 vertical pieces. The two vertical pieces you see, that have the straps on the bottom, are what bolt onto the back of your TV. This kit comes with all the different bolt sizes that most TV’s have on them. My TV is a 65” Samsung LED TV. The kit also has some spacers that will allow you to place between the TV and the two vertical frame pieces that allow you to fur out or move the TV a little bit away from the wall. In addition, these spacers provide small clearance for the cords that connect to your TV. I did purchase a 6 piece HDMI kit that has different 90 degree elbows to turn down the cable, instead of coming straight out the back of the TV where this would introduce a clearance problem with the video cable would be interfering with the wall. Turning down the cable with a quick 90 degree connector solved this clearance problem for me. The rectangular frame piece is what bolts to the wall. The horizontal slots are what allow you to adjust the frame horizontally on your wall. Prior to any hole drilling or entry into the wall, I used a stud finder and a level to mark out the center of the studs and where the four holes will locate vertically and horizontally on the wall. The kit comes with four (4) 5⁄16” x 2 1⁄4” wood lag bolts and flat washers which I used after drilling a 3⁄16” pilot hole in the center of the wood studs. I then drove them into the wall with a 1⁄2” impact gun.Once the square section frame is fastened to the wall, and the 2 vertical pieces are fastened to your TV, you can hang TV. The straps are level to pull down the spring loaded clips that lock your TV onto the square frame.I was unsure about purchasing this frame and read the previous reviews with almost all 5 star ratings. I am very glad and happy that I purchased this wall mount kit. I think you will as well.
