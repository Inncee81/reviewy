Solution to the Bluetooth issue

I bought this Soundbar because it fits very well with my LG TV. Nevertheless there is this strange problem that the Bluetooth module of the tv is always running and searching for near devices. I connected my tv via optical cable and this is the only function that I want to use. However if your neighbour is funny and wants to listen to music on your soundbar, he can just connect and interrupt your current audio. Since there is no way to disable Bluetooth in general or make some settings for it I disconnected the whole Bluetooth module.Here is the solution for the Bluetooth issue if you don’t want to use Bluetooth at all:- Disconnect your soundbar from power and audio sources.- Turn it around and remove all screws that you can find.- There should be 26 screws + 2 more screws at the HDMI connectors.- Remove the black cover to reveal all electronics.- Find the Bluetooth module and remove the screw.- Remove the cable carefully which connects the Bluetooth module to the rest.- Put the cover back and add all screws.- Enjoy!I also uploaded some photos to make it easier to identfy the Bluetooth module.Be careful and have fun!
