Motors are easy to install

Fast delivery. Motors are easy to install. Small screwdriver and a soldering iron are the only tools required. The 4 pack includes 2 clockwise and 2 counterclockwise rotating motors. I am still very happy with my drone purchase. I only had to replace a motor because I pulled it out while trying to remove a propeller without using the propeller removal tool. Otherwise no repair would have been necessary. Great drone, great company.

Motors get hot with multiple batteries

I had a motor go bad, I got these to replace it.It might be helpful to know that if you buy extra batteries in order to fly the drone for a few charges in a row, you risk burning up the motors. The drone needs to cool down. I realized this after 2 motors went bad, but now I give it about 10 minutes between flights and no more issues with motors.
