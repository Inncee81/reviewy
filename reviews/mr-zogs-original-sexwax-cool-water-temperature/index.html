    Product Description

Mr. Zogs Original Sex Wax is an iconic American brand of surf and hockey wax known throughout the world for its consistent quality. Zogs Original brand goes on smoother than Quick Humps, making it ideal as a base. Available in four water temperatures; Cold, Cool, Warm, and Tropical, it can be used for both base and top coats. Each water temperature has 4 different scents available, Coconut, Strawberry, Grape, and Pineapple, OR, choose the mixed scent to receive one of the four different scents chosen for you.
