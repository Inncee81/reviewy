The only cons I have are it is super heavy, the cross bar frame is in four pieces and has to be assembled from four pieces screwed together with four screws and it is extremely heavy and the bottom plate is made of glass which looks nice but having the entire thing supported by two screws through the glass seems iffy for long term life. Much better then you think it will be.

This stand is being seriously undersold. The product description does not do it justice. I purchased it to raise my new TV enough to allow my center channel speaker to fit under it. I was pleasantly surprised when it arrived at my door (late but that is Amazon’s fault) by the weight of the box. When I unboxed it, I found all the metal parts are made of very heavy gauge steel. Nothing was bent in shipping. In fact, I don’t think you can bend it. The base pedestal is solid and very thick tempered glass. So far this has been completely scratch proof. The stand does come flat packed and requires assembly however the step by step instructions were very clear and included good drawings of all the parts and assembly. The hardware was cleanly and individually packed and included all the tools required. The only negative thing I can comment on is a couple small scratches to the paint on the bottom of the glass base. These were easily touched up with a Sharpe but better packaging might have prevented this. If I could make any changes it would be to add a better and more detailed measured drawing to the description. This product completely deserves its 5 Stars.
