The Seattle Sports Bilge Pump makes it easy to keep your kayak dry. Seattle Sports Paddlers Bilge Pump Keep your kayak’s bilge dry with the Paddlers Bilge Pump from Seattle Sports. Designed for ease of use, the pump features a comfortable, easy-grip rubber overmolded handle and a high-visibility neon yellow stock for extra safety. Although not required or included, a 1-inch internal diameter hose can be added to the output valve to route water away from your boat.

Perfect for kayaks and small boats

I used this today to pump out several kayaks and was amazed at how much water it pumped on every stroke! This is the best kayak pump I have ever owned. The flotation collar and the bright yellow body keep it from sinking if it happens to fall off the boat.One note of caution; The information states that a one-inch ID hose can be attached to extend the discharge. If you want to add this, get a one and one-eighth inch ID bilge hose, as you will find that the one inch hose will NOT FIT!Otherwise, an excellent pump!!

Great Bilge Bump

Worked well for it’s intended function of removing water from my kayak. I never had issues with it, even with muddy water, I have not used it in salt water, but I assume it would need to be maintained if you use it in salt water. It floats just in case you drop it, and it shoots the water far enough that no hose is needed to clear the edge of the kayak, although it would be very easy to attach a flexible tube purchased from a hardware store to it.It worked well for the year I had it, until the time I sold it with my kayak.

good product. Just set the expectation that the bilge …

I finally got to use the bilge pump during a safety and rescue class. My kayak was filled 3⁄4 way with water and it took a while (more than 5 minutes) to empty the boat - even at the claimed 8 gpm. I also noticed once the water is below bottom blue plastic end cap, air will start to get siphoned in through that seam. Just set the expectation that the bilge rate may not be as fast as you imagine it to be.
