Radio interference with this hard wire kit.

I’ve been having FM radio reception problems with my car and I thought it was due to a poor design of the car or cheap radio parts… I have an appointment to bring the car in at the dealership to be looked at so I disconnected the camera and all the wiring so that everything was back to factory condition. That action alone caused the FM reception to clear up completely and I had no more problem.Reconnecting the wiring instantly introduces static to the FM radio. It’s actually kind of funny. Plug the camera in: static. Unplug the camera: no static, repeat. I left this hard wire kit disconnected and used my Rexing camera with the cigarette plug instead and didn’t have much problem. A spot or two here and there would have some static that seemed to go away if I unplugged the cigarette plug but for the most part it wasn’t an issue.I have this hard wire kit in another car as well that has poor radio reception, now I’m interested in seeing if it’s the cause there as well.This kid needs to be re-manufactured with some kind of shielding or something to prevent interference. I’ll be looking for a different hard wire kit ASAP!

This is a must-have..

Absolutely love this kit..Before I ordered it, I was a bit apprehensive, as I had read reviews that the connector was the wrong way around. Well, it wasn’t in the kit that I received. It seems that listened to some criticism.. The picture on Amazon shows 2 blank wires at the end.. However, in my case, the red wire ended with a molded-on connector WITH a mini fuse. I had also ordered Add-a-fuse Tap kit, but didn’t need it at all). The black wire (ground) had a spade connector (U-shaped connector) attached to it. They could not have made it easier to hook it up. After testing it first and seeing that everything worked (finding an empty fuse socket that only goes hot when the car is started), I hid it in the head liner and A-pillar down to the driver’s side dashboard where my fuse box is (2016 VW Golf). The wire was way too long, so I rolled it up and used a zip tie to the excess cable in the dashboard. All very neat and tidy. This is one sweet setup, and it seems that Rexing has listened to some of the criticisms that customers had..Good job!

Didn’t work, had internal short.

Wouldn’t power my Rexing V1P and after fighting with it for about an hour, I finally figured out why. Hooked up a multimeter and saw 12v+ coming from both ground and vcc(power) pins on the mini-usb connector. So something was shorted out internally AND bypassing the step-down regulator (What’s supposed to convert it to 5v output). I’m just lucky it didn’t damage my camera.I’d strongly advise testing power from this before hooking it up to your camera. I’m just going to order the brand I used to hook up my other Rexing cam in my other car as that one worked fine and at least looks serviceable if I have to fix a short it.
