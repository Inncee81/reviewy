Great, but smaller/rounder than the original.

These toothbrush heads are MUCH more durable than the knockoff third party options. They fit the toothbrush well, and last a reasonably long time. The only thing to be aware of is that the heads are smaller than the ones that come with the toothrbush - the original heads are a longer oval head, while this is a smaller, rounder head. In our case this is fine because the kids are small so the smaller head is actually easier for them to use, but it is a difference to be aware of.

Same Great Replacement Heads; Different Packaging

My 7 year old daughter has been using the Sonicare toothbrush since she was 4 years old, and she loves it! My 4 year old son just received his Sonicare toothbrush a few months ago, and he’s loving his too. I buy these replacement heads specifically for ages 4-7 because they are the perfect size for their little mouths. Unfortunately, Sonicare has changed their packaging, so now, instead of getting two different colored replacement heads, you get two heads of the same light blue color. It can be confusing for parents who have two or more kids, who either share a toothbrush (and just switch the heads) or who have identical Sonicares. Fortunately, my daughter has the older Sonicare kids toothbrush, which is red, while my son has the newer one, which is a teal blue color, so they can easily tell their toothbrushes apart even though they both have the same colored head.The length of the brush head from base to top is about 2 3⁄4 inches long and the head itself is just over .5 inches.Once when I ran out of this size replacement heads I used the size up, (the one for ages 7-10) and immediately noticed the difference. That size head is significantly longer (closer to the length of the replacement heads for adults) and is much harder for little ones to control. As a matter of fact, my daughter did such a poor job of using the toothbrush with the longer head that my husband and I had to brush her teeth for her. So, even though my daughter is now seven, I still buy this smaller size replace head for her, and I always make sure to have one on hand for whenever needed. I buy them through Amazon’s subscribe and save so I never run out.I would highly recommend getting this size replacement brush head for any little ones who uses the Sonicare toothbrush. FYI these replacement heads also fit, and work with, the adult toothbrush. So, you could just buy the heads for your little one and use with your own Sonicare…..the kids’ toothbrush is about the same length as the adult one, (the actual toothbrush not the head) only a bit chunkier so kids should be able to manipulate the adult Sonicare as easily as they would the kids.

Excellent quality

I have bought the generic replacement heads previously and they just don’t compare in quality to these Sonicare replacement heads. The generic brand only provided a month or so before they needed replaced, where as these lasted the 3-4 months as intended. I frequently use generic brand items, but this is not an item I will use generic on because the generics require too frequent of replacements..
