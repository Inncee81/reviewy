Product Specifications

  • Input: 100~240V, 50/60Hz
  • Output: 9V, 1A
  • 2-flat-pin plug
  • Connector size: 5.5 x 2.1mm

  • Cable Length: 100cm

    Product Description

High quality switching ‘wall wart’ AC to DC 9V 1000mA wall power supply manufactured specifically for Electronics. These are switch mode power supplies which mean the output is regulated to 9V and the capable output current is much higher (1000mA!).These will power most projects that don’t require more than 1000mA of current. Dimensions: 2.91 in x 1.57 in x 1.06 in (7.40 cm x 4.00 cm x 2.70 cm) Weight: 2.54 oz (72 g),Works with most devices that use a 9V adaptor and less than 1A of power.
