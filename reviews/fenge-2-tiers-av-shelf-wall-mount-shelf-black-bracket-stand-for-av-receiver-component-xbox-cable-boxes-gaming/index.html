Shelves are nice, but the lag screws are poor quality.

This wall mount rack is quite nice, except for the lag screws that hold it into the studd. The quality of the screws is so bad that with no effort I stripped the screw heads out before they even got close to going all the way into the 3⁄16” pilot hole I drilled. Also, I have no idea if this was the correct size pilot hole because the instructions did not say what size to use! I ended up having to go to Lowes and buy new stainless steel screws to install this. Not a big deal, just really annoying.Update: I have attempted to place an orignal XBox One on the bottom shelf (weight wise it should not be a problem) but it does not fit. These shelves are tiny; they look very nice but they are not entirely functional. I am going to attempt to find a light weight platform that is larger than the shelf itself and place that on top to place the Xbox One on. I will also tie the platform into place because the Xbox One comes farther out from the wall than the shelf does and I don’t want it to fall forward if the weight distribution isn’t perfect.

Low Quality… Even at this price

The concept behind these shelves is great. The pictures show a nice, clean, and spacious space for your electronics. Unfortunately, once you start plugging cables in, it will push almost every device off the front edge. The cable management tabs are a bit confusing and don’t really provide any practical use.I ended up returning this item due to some issues that may have been specific to my package so keep that in mind as you continue reading. The package that I received appeared to have been scuffed up and the glass got the worst of it since the black film on the underside had been rubbed off in several spots. After predrilling holes to put up the support, the screws quickly stripped out and I was only able to recover one of them when I was repackaging the item (the other screw had to be drilled out). The clamping mechanism that holds the shelves in place were also ineffective and after tightening all screws, the shelves continued to rock and would not stay level.The seller was quick to authorize the return and processed the refund the day I shipped it back.
