Product Specifications

  • Slide pack battery provides easy installation and removal of the battery

    Product Description

The Black & Decker GCO1200C 12v Cordless Drill with Over molds is ideal for many drilling and driving applications. This drill features a variable-speed design that delivers up to 750 RPM for work in a variety of materials. The 24 position clutch prevents screws from stripping and the keyless chuck makes bit changing quick and simple. The soft grips provide additional comfort while using on projects. It has a slide pack battery that provides easy installation and removal of the battery. Includes: (1) 12V Battery, (1) Charger and (1) Double Ended Bit.

I bought this drill after my old 18v B&D drill finally gave out on me after about 12-13 years of intermittent use for the odd household and hobby tasks I required of it. This new 12v B&D drill is not made nearly as well. It arrived with something bent in the head, this caused a quite noticeable wobble in the drill bit. It seemed unsafe to me and definitely not the quality I was expecting out of B&D so I returned it. I give this little drill 3 stars because it is affordable well balanced and has excellent power for its size. If you want to give it a chance, I recommend buying from a vendor that ships with Amazon so you have 30 days to return it for a full refund if you get a bad one like mine. As always Amazon’s customer service was excellent.
