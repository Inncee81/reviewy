Product Specifications

    Product Description

Product Description This easy-to-spot flotation wraps around any HERO Session camera to keep it afloat. It’s specially designed to protect your camera, while also giving you easy access to the shutter button. Floaty is perfect for worry-free shooting in and around the water while surfing, snorkeling, wakeboarding and more. Includes an adhesive anchor and camera tether for added security in extreme conditions.

Set Contains: • Floaty (for HERO Session cameras) • Long Thumb Screw • Tether • Adhesive Anchor
