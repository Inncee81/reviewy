It offers some good scratch protection while its on the switch and your …

Bought this crystal cover to help protect our new nintendo switch from scratches. It is very simple to place the case on the main switch and just a tad trickier to place on the joycons but still not hard. It offers some good scratch protection while its on the switch and your using it in handheld mode while traveling or such. You will have to take the cover off the main part of the switch to place it in the home docking station as it’s just to snug to be able to leave it on and dock it. You can leave the covers on the joycons if using them separated but if you want to place them in the joycon controller grip you will have to take them off. So this is good product to place on the nintendo switch if your gonna be taking it on the go and using it as a handheld device for awhile but i wouldn’t put it on just to play around the house because of the taking it off and putting it back on. So you can’t go wrong for the money to help protect your nintendo switch on the go.

Good protection

You have to protect your new Nintendo Switch when traveling. Yes I have a carrying case but when I take it out there’s always a chance it can drop, get scratched up, or dirty. The cut outs all line up perfectly and the whole back is protected well. It’s three pieces so it’s easy to pop on and off. Good thing too because you cannot keep the back case on while it’s docked. It feels pretty good when you’re holding it and doesn’t hinder my play time at all. No protection to the front so you’ll have to find a screen protector for sure.

BOI Stickers

I love this cover. I bought this for one reason, so I could put my Binding of Isaac stickers on my Switch without the fear of damaging the unit. The cover works perfectly and not just for my sticker project. It fits perfectly to the Switch’s housing and doesn’t feel loose at all. It’s also super easy to put on and take off. The covers for the joycons are also amazing and fit wonderfully in my hands. The only issue I have is that the joycon covers do not allow the joycons to be removed easily when docked into the console. The release buttons are not accessible with the hands. My super simple work around for this is to use a spare 3DS stylus to push the release button. Surprisingly it works perfectly and I don’t feel the stylus would scratch the release buttons at all. Would buy definitely buy again, which I might so I could display other stickers I have, or maybe even a decorative skin without the worry of damaging the switch.

This looks and feels amazing. There are little “Nubs” on the bottom of …

This looks and feels amazing. There are little “Nubs” on the bottom of the Joy-cons that mirror the bumps that cover the Zl and ZR buttons, this really makes gripping the controller much more comfortable when in either attached to the switch or when using the joy-cons individually. I use these on my neon color joy-cons to keep them protected, and I like how it almost makes the buttons flush with the case. Also, The buttons on the back of the joy con are still (semi) easily reached so you can still detach them with little difficulty.NOTE: the switch will NOT fit in the dock with this case, the joy cons can keep them on and be docked, but the switch itself will not fit. I’m just planning on keeping it for when I will be traveling or not using the dock for a long period of time. It snaps on and off very easily so this should not be an issue.
