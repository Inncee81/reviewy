Great for Lumia 2520

If you have a 2520, you know how smudgy and fingerprinty the screen gets. I was very satisfied with these screen protectors. They are very clear, no loss in sensitivity, and don’t pick up fingerprints like crazy. Application was relatively easy, (no need for liquid sprays, etc.), the only tricky part was trying to get dust out from under the screen (Scotch tape is your friend here, just some tips for you all). I didn’t use the application card to remove bubbles since I’ve read it can scratch or damage the screen protector if pressed too hard. I just used my fingers and hands to press the bubbles to the edge of the screen, and that worked OK for me. The screen protector has been on for about 2 weeks, and honestly you can’t really tell that it’s there! (unless you look at the camera holes, etc.). They feel sturdy and very protected. Unfortunately, last night I dropped my power brick on the screen, and it must’ve fallen on a corner or something, but I noticed a small dent on the screen protector, which protected the glass screen behind it. So scratch-proof maybe, not dent-proof! Anyway, I still like these screen protectors, and will go with Illumishield when looking for replacements.

Stops the fingerprints

The screen protector went on easily and bubble free, as it states. But, more importantly, it stops the collection of fingerprints on my screen that was driving me nuts. I scraped a bit hard in places, and it left a visible mark. Not noticeable except when angled in light, but just warning you.
