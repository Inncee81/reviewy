Product Reviews

3 days of use and one of them broke already.

I thought these would be good to use on a line for my dog when we went camping… They were only OK.The threads on the locking part were really rough cut and some of them were VERY difficult to screw lock and unlock.Also, one of them broke and lost the spring on day 3 of usage. Fortunately it didn’t break in such a way to render the D ring completely useless… but it did still break.

Great for the price.

For the price, these are great. I used them to secure things to my kayak while I am out fishing. They see a lot of time in saltwater and after several fishing trips still feel and perform like new. I wouldn’t use them for supporting a massive load but for a light load that needs a lockable quick release, these are great. Don’t trust them with your life and you’ll be happy, the manufacturer makes that pretty clear.

Exactly what I needed

Purchased these for travelling to help me clip all the random things to my pack (like my Camelbak All Clear Purifier Bottle) and even though they aren’t meant for heavy duty stuff, they excelled at everything I did use them for.

But these carabiners are pretty rough. I can hear metal crunching on the …

OK, I am somewhat forgiving when it comes to metal. But these carabiners are pretty rough. I can hear metal crunching on the threads, and one of them feels like the threads are just too short, I expect it to become a slide lock instead of a screw lock. They’ll work for attaching some non-essential gear to my pack, but the stuff I need will be attached with something I can trust more.
