Product Reviews

Excellent accessory kit!

This is a great accessory kit that covers a wide range of mount options. I haven’t tested the suction cup mount besides just sticking it to the table to see if it worked, so I can’t speak to how well it holds up in actual use, but all the other mounts work very well. The floating mount is great for keeping your camera floating and visible in water; I used it while kayaking during a recent camping trip and it made handling the camera quite easy. I couldn’t figure out what the light blue strap (that they are apparently calling a “J-hook”??) was supposed to connect to (definitely nowhere on their own AKASO EK7000 4K WIFI Sports Action Camera Ultra HD Waterproof DV Camcorder 12MP 170 Degree Wide Angle) but as they advertise as being compatible with other cameras, perhaps it works with some other brand.My only complaint is that the mount joints don’t seem to tighten up quite enough – they will still rotate if bounced or moved with too much acceleration, which is common when using the camera “actively”.

The yellow floaty handle is cheaply made and the suction …

The yellow floaty handle is cheaply made and the suction cup holder was able to hold the camera in an upright position for about 60 miles of road before it was removed. I would give it a higher rating except for its cheapness/flimsy floaty handle.

Good product especially for the price

Good accessory set. It is definitely comparable to other name brands but at a much lower price. I would buy it again.

I was disappointed with the design of connections

I was hoping to get an attachment for my motorcycle helmet but instead it was for my head. The chest straps the connection doesn’t allow for proper movement of camera. I was disappointed with the design of connections. There are hardly any place on the motorcycle to attach the suction cups and be secure that will not fall off. Good materials but poor design for camera adjustments.

LOTS FOR THE PRICE

Have to figure out what all the pieces go to before I can comment any further. Good price for all the extras.
