Roundup 181538 Lawn and Garden Sprayer Repair Kit with O-Rings, Gaskets, and Nozzle

by people ...

Product Specifications

  • Fits all handheld Roundup brand and Ortho brand compression sprayers
  • All necessary seals and gaskets in one kit: two sets of gaskets and O-rings for the pump along with one O-ring for the wand

  • Poly adjustable nozzle (enables stream to cone patterns)

    Product Description

This Roundup 181538 Sprayer Repair Kit has all the required seals and gaskets to help your Roundup brand or Ortho brand handheld sprayer work like new again. Kit includes two sets of gaskets and O-rings for the pump and an extra O-ring for the sprayer wand. Poly adjustable nozzle enables stream to cone patterns giving you control of your project.
