Not useful for cutting through thin metal.

Got to cut thin sheet steel in my car. Tools used to “hold” the cutting discs did not seem to fit into half the discs then the screws stripped after half a dozen uses. Ended up going out and purchasing drimal cutters set. Works well with the tool but overall caused more difficulty then it was worth. Use of other bits has worked better.

Almost

This is an OK set for light to medium duty use. The tool itself really got hot when using the cutting disc to cut some sheet stainless steel. The tool appeared to ‘bog’ down quite a bit without having much pressure applied. Again the rating will depend on your use, for me it is OK, but if you want heavy duty - then Dremel is the only way to go.

For home owner use

For the person who only uses this sparingly, This item would be good for them. You can’t beat the price with all the accessories. If you will be using this daily, or at least once a week, I would suggest the Dremel Brand. This is lower quality, and the speed dial did not work right on mine. Had to send it back

Great tool for the money

A very good dremmel clone. Minor quality issues such as the label sticker was half off when I received it, will not stay on. Minor issue aside, it works great without the high dremmel name price.

I can understand some of the other reviews’ concerns, but we are nice to our tools and try to never over-use them. I’ve got tools 20+ years in use and this feels like it’s in the league of good quality home-use type of tools.We buy industrial grade tools when the use is daily or extreme.
