Got replacement screen for a iPhone 6S. Works perfectly. Touch ID works, but required performing a complete factory reset and restore from backup.A couple of tricks: Be sure to transfer the plastic parts from old screen. That is, the round/square clear camera shroud, the small clear square part, AND the small white plastic sticky square under the proximity sensor. Also, the lasso band on the speaker cover requires brute force to string around the display chip. Screw speaker cover and lasso on first and then stretch lasso around the chip. That’s how I did it anyway. Bottom line, this screen works perfectly, and phone is fully functional. Do your homework before attempting this. Be careful and good luck.
