Perfect fit for Sony a6300 w/ zoom lens and hood!

I always choose Case Logic when looking to protect my gear. This case is compact and fairly sturdy. My Sony a6300 fits fine with an 18-200mm zoom lens and hood. There’s also enough space for an extra batteries (2), a charger, usb cables, and possibly more. This case also features a blue weather cover for inclement situations. The case offers versatile protection without the bulk.For the price, you cannot beat it.

Great!

Shipping was incredibly fast and for the price it’s great! It is a little bigger than I expected and it even comes with a rain cover. I originally wanted to get a clip for my camera for easy access but I didn’t want it dangling on me and hitting anything so I decided to get this case. It’s pretty well cushioned and holds my Nikon d3300 and the 50mm well. Although there’s a lot more room for a longer lens. My only complaint is that I wish it could be tightened on the back when I strap it on my belt or backpack buckles.

Compact DSLR Case

Perfect case for my my DSLR camera. Nice and compact, but still has enough room for my Nikon D5300 with an attached 18-55mm lens, plus accessories (sd card, battery charger, cables).
