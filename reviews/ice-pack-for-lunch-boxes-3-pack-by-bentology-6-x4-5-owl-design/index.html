They’re adorable, and they last forever!

Who knew I would get so excited about ice packs? First of all, they’re adorable. Which doesn’t really matter in the long run because no one sees them but me, but it makes me happy. However, what is actually important is that they work. I use these in my lunchbox that I bring to work because the fridge is too small for everyone. I keep my lunchbox in a locker. I take these (one or two at a time) out of the freezer between 7:00-7:30am, and depending on the day, I don’t eat my lunch until 1:00-3:00pm. The ice packs have always still been frozen or at least half frozen by the time I eat my lunch. I was honestly shocked the first time I used them. They have never thawed out completely and they keep my lunch fresh. I’ve been using them for about 2 months. One day I was so busy at work I didn’t have time to eat so I took my lunch back home with me. The ice packs were still cold by 7:00pm (12 hours out of the freezer!).
