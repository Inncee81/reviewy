    Product Description

Men’s Montana Collection Triple Pocket Dopp Kit has internal metal frame, which allows for a larger top opening detail while protecting internal goods from travel damage. Bottom pocket with full zipper allows for almost 360° access. Features a wrist handle for easy carrying and a side zipper pocket for easy access to small items. Internal compartments help keep items organized and guards against internal spills and shifting. Features soft, stain and moisture resistant rustic leatherette finish (PVC) in deep brown. Durable PVC material resists staining and moisture and helps contain internal spills so that items packed along side your toiletries do not become soiled. 11”Lx6”Wx5”H.
