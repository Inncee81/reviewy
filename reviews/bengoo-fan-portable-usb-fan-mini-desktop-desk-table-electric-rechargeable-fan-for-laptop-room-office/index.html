The ULTIMATE review on this fan.. 3.5 STARS

I ordered this fan after using the similar “O2COOL” USB fan sold on here at work. That fan was nice as it had two speed settings and was direction adjustable due to it’s folding “kickstand” design….. BUT, I never just buy something without doing research first and after looking at all theoptions in the $15 range, I decided on this one because it has THREE speed settings and comes with the rechargeable battery making it completely portable…Here’s my FULL REVIEW of this product:PROS:(1) the THREE fan speeds are all surprisingly powerful and overall, this unit delivers a much better air stream than the O2COOL unit.(2) One 18650 rechargeable battery INCLUDED making this unit totally portable and wireless if needed.(3) ULTRA QUIET OPERATION(4) USB cord is 3 Feet Long(5) There’s a cheesy little LED flashlight on the side that turns on after you cycle through all 3 fan speeds. Kinda weird, but it’s a small bonus.(6) Looks cool.CONS:(1) THEY LIED about the BLUE LED LIGHT that supposedly illuminates the fan blade area!!! THERE IS NO LIGHT IN THERE AT ALL!!(2) While it seems to operate great, it FEELS cheap. Super light plastic that I don’t think would survive a fall.(3) The bottom of the fan has NO GRIP, so while it’s blowing, it slowly crawls away from you and you have to keep replacing it or somehow give it traction on your own. This is a careless mistake.(4) Unlike the O2COOL unit, this item has no “kickstand” or height adjustment. So, if you’re using it at your desk, your rib cage will be plenty cool and your face will be jealous. You either have to place it atop something higher up, OR tilt it against something on your desk to make it point higher.For the price, this is still a great value and I will be keeping it. I am disappointed with the LIE about the fan blade light. I know it’s silly, but i was really looking forward to see that in action. I am going to add a thin strip of grip tape to the bottom so this thing stops dancing around my desk AND I’m working on a DIY solution for the fact that this thing only points one way.The manufacturer really should read these issues and make the corrections. This item has the potential to sell in big number this summer and these issues are SIMPLE and cheap to correct in mass production.NOTE: Although I wish I was, I am NOT a paid or reimbursed reviewer.
