    Product Description

 

A good TV wall mount is as important as your flat-screen TVs!

Not only does it secure the TV firmly in place but a wall mount will also enhance your viewing experience by holding the TV at the right viewing angle.

At the same time, a wall mount gives your TV more flexibility, allowing you to shift your position throughout the room without losing the optimal viewing position.

The Basics Hardware TV Wall Mount is designed to fit most 26 to 55-inch LED, LCD, OLED or Plasma flat screen TVs or monitors. It does so while maintaining an 66 lbs maximum loading capacity and a full-motion swivel feature.

This wall mount also comes equipped with the mounting hardware you require along with a 6 ft HDMI cable and a 6-inch 3-Axis magnetic bubble level to aid you during the installation process.

Specs:

The TV mount fits VESA - mounting hole pattern: 200x200mm / 200x100 / 100x100 (8”x8” / 8”x4” / 4”x4”).

Comes with 4 removable mount adapters that can extends the VESA to 600x400 / 400x300 / 400x200 /  300x300 / 300x200

Color: Black
