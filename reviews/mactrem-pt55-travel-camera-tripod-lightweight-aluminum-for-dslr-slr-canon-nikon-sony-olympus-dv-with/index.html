This tripod is awesome, its portable

I’ve had a few tripod and so far this is my favorite. It actually exceeded my expectation, this is a well made and it’s a good quality product for a very affordable price.I just got surprised, how lightweight it, very small and it dosen’t take much space and very stable too…- Hexagonal center column really keeps the center post steady regardless of if it’s raised or not.- All three legs are padded (you’d be surprised how many higher brands aren’t)- The pan head is very fluid/smooth and bubble levels are everywhere.- The video/still panning dial/lock is also a very nice feature, allowing you to really lock down the vertical tilt while still being able to smoothly pan horizontally when shooting video. This virtually eliminates the problem of nose droop from the weight of the camera lens, which was a problem with ALL of the other tripods I tried. And then when you need to you can just flip the knob and be able to lock down both pan and tilt securely (like for still photography).- The quick-release plate is hand-tightened (requires no tools) and the entire tripod head is easily removable.Lastly it is very easy to carry everywhere cuz it has a carrying bag. Love it! I’m very pleased with my purchased.If they provided a phone holder, it would been a good addition too, but never mind, i had one already and used it as most of my photos are with mobile… :)
