Great for the price but was not designed for cyclists

This does keep your water cooler longer than a bottle that is not insulated. Before these I was using just the clear sports Lake Arrowhead water bottle that you buy at Costco. I use two of these on my road bike. I immediately took off the black strap on the top as there is no need to have these when using with a bike. After the black strap is removed there is a gap between the lid and the bottle. This gap tends to get dirt in it after a ride. There is also engraved lettering on the top black part where you drink and that tends to get dirty as well. This can be washed in the dishwasher. Overall I am happy with these, especially due to the good price as compared to similar products.
