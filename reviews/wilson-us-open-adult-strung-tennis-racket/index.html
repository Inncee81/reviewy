Turned into part maraca in the first 30 minutes of play.

Within 30 minutes of play I had part tennis racquet and part maraca. My guess is internal pieces of the grommet broke off on hard hits and now rattle around the hollow portion of the beam. Each hit was met with a discordant shaka-shaka noise that affected me more than my opponent.I would argue this plays like a $22 racquet and is suitable for beginners or light(er) hitters.
