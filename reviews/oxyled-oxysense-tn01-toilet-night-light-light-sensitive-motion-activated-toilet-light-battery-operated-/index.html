    Product Description

OxyLED TN01 Colorful Toilet Night Light, Light Sensitive & Motion-activated Toilet Seat Light

OxyLED - Natural Life, Natural Lights  The OxyLED toilet night light will transform any toilet into a nightlight in seconds, making your life colorful all day. No more missing your target or stumbling around in the dark. It is motion-activated and light-sensitive light that illuminates at darkness when someone get close to its sensing range(5m, 120 degrees) and lasts about 35s after he stepping away. One button press, you can choose from 7 different single colors or 12 colors Carousel mode. 

Specifications:  - Material: ABS - Sensor: motion sensor, light sensor  - Light Source: LED  - Voltage: 4.5V  - Power: 0.1W  - Light Color: 12-color - Power Supply: 3x AAA Batteries (Not Included) - Sensing Range: 5m, 120 degrees - Dimensions: 80*63.8*20.5mm 

How to Set Working Mode:  1. Set single color or Carousel mode It’ll enter carousel mode automatically after battery installation.If you want to fix a single color, short press the button to shift in order red – green – yellow – blue – purple – aqua – white.Under single white color mode, short press the button until white light flashes twice. Wait a few seconds, it would start Carousel modes again. 

  1. Select brightness level Under single light color mode, long press the button until it flashes once. Short press it to adjust brightness(total 5 brightness levels). Wait a few seconds until it flashes once, then it will lock this brightness level.

Worked well for 2 months, then pooped out…(pun intended).

I initially bought this because it was the cheapest option as it was on sale. For about 2 months, these were great. I had a wide array of colors to choose from, the motion sensor worked well, and it just did what it was intended for. I was also willing to let the fact that it did not stay on long enough to finish taking care of business 50% of the time since everything else about it was so great. After two months,for whatever reason, I noticed that the original color option of white light that I had chosen for both was unavailable to me. No idea why, but that and several other colors disappeared from the choices when I tried to run through them and change it. This was another thing that I decided to overlook as long as some of the colors still worked and that my toilet bowl was illuminated. Not too long after, I noticed that the light was fading in both units so I figured that I needed to change the batteries. I did so with brand new batteries and initially this fixed my issue. Maybe two days later they were dead again and now I can’t get them to turn on no matter what batteries I put in or what I do. Started off promising with some quirks, but quickly became useless for unknown reasons. I’m disappointed that this occurred after the one month mark because now I will be unable to return them and get a replacement. As of now this is a zero star product for me, but I kept two stars on for how pleased I was initially.

Great solution for nighttime trips

Everyone laughs when I tell them about my new multi-color toilet night light, but it’s a great improvement for the person who has to visit the bathroom during the night. It solves the problem of a nightlight always shining too brightly out the bathroom door and it puts the light right where you need it. It does take a little getting used to, sitting down on a glowing toilet bowl but that’s where you learn to lower the brightness of the light! The controls are simple to use. It helps this has both a motion and light sensor - in the bathroom with a window the light goes on only at night, even on really cloudy days. The other bathroom does not have a window and the light will go on anytime there is motion in the hallway outside the bathroom – then I am glad that the motion-activated light is on a timer and shortly switches off automatically. I have cleaned the bathrooms a couple times since I got them and they are not an issue since only the thinnest and smallest part of the light is inside the toilet bowl, the rest sits outside the bowl. I have 2 very different style toilets: one with a standard thickness bowl rim and another with an oval shape and a much thicker rim - this light easily adapted to both rims.
