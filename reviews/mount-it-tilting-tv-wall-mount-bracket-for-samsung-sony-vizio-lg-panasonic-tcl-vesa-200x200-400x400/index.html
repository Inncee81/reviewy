Great Tilt Mount Installed In About 2-3 Hours (revised)

Revision clarification: Comment applies only to the larger version of this wall mount (i.e. for 42”-70” TV version). This is a partial repeat of a comment I made under questions for this item. I just bought and installed this mount for a 42” LG TV. My wall application was for a sheet rock surface with wood framing wall joists 16” on center. It is an excellent wall mount and all the hardware is more than appropriate in strength and stability for mounting a TV on the wall. The only difficulty lies in locating your wall mount plate (i.e. assessing exactly where you want the TV be located on the wall, finding wall joists, and if applicable assuring wall cable and electrical connections are covered by the TV). The wall mount plate is 36” in length. This means that, for wall joists spaced every 16” typically, the wall plate holes one uses to bolt into the wall joists easily span a 32” length between say a first and third wall joist. This would have your TV centered over the middle (second) wall joist. Alternatively, attach bolts to the wall spanning consecutive wall joists, 16” apart, with the TV centered between two wall joists. Assuming you centered bolt locations correctly on your wall joists, either approach will have 4 lag bolts of over 2” in length screwed into wall joists to bear the weight of your TV. This would easily be strong enough, and allows great flexibility in positioning the TV location. HOWEVER, when attaching your wall mount plate, be aware that for a 40”-42” TV you must center your TV exactly on the wall mount or the wall mount plate will likely be visible; most 40”-42” TV’s are only slightly greater than 36” wide! Not an issue for the larger flat screen TV’s, I suspect. There were some review comments made about adequate wall mounting bolt length and strength. I found all bolts to be more than adequate for length and strength.
