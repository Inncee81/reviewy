    Product Description

With this VeloChampion double bottle cage mount you are able to mount up to two water bottle cages from the rear of your saddle. This offers quick and easy access to water bottles during racing conditions with aerodynamic benefits. Ideal for road cycle racing or triathlon competitions where both water and sports nutrition are required during intense workouts. Equally suitable for everyday commuting to keep water/nutrition or a tool bottle in a handy easy accessible location. Product Reviews

Well made, easily adjustable, very secure once mounted, overall very happy with my purchase.

After looking at other similar products (i.e. Profile Design), I settled on this on for a couple of reasons. It is all alloy, no plastic parts to break off. It accepts a regular bottle cage. It mounts easily and adapts to almost any desired height or angle. It is very secure, once mounted does not move or come loose, and being entirely metal, looks to be very durable. I have had it on my road bike and ridden about 800 miles so far without any issues at all. Overall, I am very happy with my purchase.

Doesn’t fit on beefier carbon rails.

I have the SQlab 612 Race Saddle, and since the rails on it have a slightly thicker diameter, the securing clamps of this cage mount don’t fit. The only way to get it to work is to remove one side of the clamping mechanism. Insofar the fastener is then driving itself directly into the carbon and increases the risk of the rails rupturing.VeloChampion have a design that is on the verge of being perfect. It simply needs a little more space for the clamps to allow for the slightly larger diameter of the carbon rails. FIVE stars if that were the case. Six stars if they had a optional mount that allowed a single, center-mounted cage for shorter rides. :D
