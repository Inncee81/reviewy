Nice Mini Quadcopter

Yipa 3D flip Mini RC Drone with LED Flashlight came nicely packed without any damage. This is the smallest quadcopter and it looks really tiny. This quadcopter is very stable, precise and it flies really well and it flips nicely, Its got nice bright LEDs on the each motor mount. It comes with a little transmitter, USB charging cable and 4 pieces of spare props. It has got built in 100 milliamp power battery and it takes about 15 minutes to charge and get about 5 minutes flight out of it.Before using this we need to sync the quadcopter with the transmitter by moving the left throttle to upwards and then backward, once synced the LED on the quadcopter will become solid. And to flip, you need to press the right button and do and flip and itwill beep to let you know that is waiting to flip and once you give the input it will flip and it will turn the flip mode back.It has got nice tight flips and it is really good stable flips. It has got three different rates from the left stick

Not a bad toy for the price and its fun just need a little getting used to get acquainted with the controls.
