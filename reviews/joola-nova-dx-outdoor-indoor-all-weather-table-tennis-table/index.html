Product Specifications

  • Recreational all-weather table tennis table with 6mm aluminum plastic composite surface and 30mm x 30mm rust-resistant, powder-coated undercarriage
  • Two-piece table folds up for playback mode and compact storage; Adjustable feet to level the table during gameplay
  • Comes with double anti-tilting devices and locking casters for safe and easy transport
  • Includes easy-to-assemble matching weatherproof net set

  • Assembled: 108in x 60in x 30in; Folded: 62in x 22in x 67in; 166 pounds

    Product Description

The JOOLA Nova DX Table Tennis Table is a high-quality table tennis table that can be used inside or outside, designed specifically to withstand outdoor use all year round, in all weather conditions and terrains. Featuring a 6mm aluminum plastic composite surface, resistant to warping and chipping. The JOOLA Nova DX Table offers a consistent bounce even after years of extended outdoor play, making this a great family investment. No maintenance is required since each half is mounted on a 30mm x 30mm rust-resistant, powder-coated steel undercarriage. Adjustable height levelers on each leg help balance the table on uneven ground. Double anti-tilting devices and durable 3-inch locking casters make it safe and easy to transport. Enjoy playing with friends or training solo by folding up one half of the table in the playback position. The JOOLA Nova DX Table comes with an easy-to-install weatherproof net set with adjustable posts to ensure a level playing surface and is the perfect addition to your deck, backyard, or community playground. Start playing like a champion with equipment developed for champions! JOOLA is a proud sponsor of USA Table Tennis. Very nice table

I have had this table for about a month. It seems to be very sturdy and well made. The surface is quite interesting…its like a solid piece of 1⁄4” resin. It seems like it will be very long lasting and has a nice look to it. The net is of quality design and keeps its tension. It is a 2 piece table, so it can be a bit difficult to get perfectly level if playing on an uneven surface. It folds up and down seemlessly, but is not like a normal recreational table that stays in one piece. When the tables are folded up the net generally needs to be taken down as well because it attaches to both sides of the table. The two table pieces lock when folded up and roll nicely. All of this won’t matter if you plan to leave it in one place, but if planning to move it a lot it could be more work than you want.Assembly was a breeze, but the table pieces were quite heavy so you will need at least one other person to help, my wife was able to be my helper, so it doesn’t have to be a “muscle man”…although my wife is pretty ripped ;).I’m not too particular about my surfaces, but like I said the surface seems even in terms of bounce and the net is very nice…not sure what else you would want in a table!Enjoy!

Great table for the price! Very easy setup.

The JOOLA Nova Outdoor Table Tennis Table is a great value (mind you I purchased it from Amazon at 40% off MSRP). I was looking at purchasing an outdoor table for my family to play in our carport during the warmer/nicer months with the option to move it into our basement when it got too cold. Looking over the reviews of other tables (Stiga Coronado for one) I decided to go with the Nova due to the ease of assembly and storage. The shipping was fast and the delivery company worked well with us to make sure we were home for delivery. Assembling the table was a piece of cake. All you have to do is attach the “T” sections of the legs that have the casters and all that consisted of was using the included plates and two bolts per “foot” to bolt them together. Everything else was assembled. This is my first high quality table tennis table purchase so I don’t have experience with other’s build quality, but the Nova is very solid. The table top is solid and flat even though it’s only 6mm surface. The casters are high quality, sturdy, all metal (exept for the rubber wheel surface), and all four will independently lock. I don’t feel that I have to be gentle when moving the table. The other legs are solid and have adjustable feet. The only issue that I saw were that the two different sections stood at different heights in the middle so I had to use the net bracket to even them out to play. Speaking of the net, I read quite a few people complain about the quality of the net, but I haven’t had a lot of issues with it and it works just fine. If the net is a HUGE deal to you, you’ll probably want to invest in a higher quality one though.Overall, I have been very happy with this purchase. I have 5 children under the age of 12 and we play on the table AT LEAST 2 hours a day between everyone and it has held up VERY well. (I was even inspired to get a JOOLA catch-net/Ipong combo to use for working out). NOTE: Due to getting this table at the start of winter I have yet to use it outside.
