Almost perfect!

This is a nice little multi-purpose laser level that’s missing a few small touches that would make it fantastic.- No visible horizontal bubble tube when holding the level vertically, flat on the wall with the ruler-side to the surface. You can turn the entire thing on its side and switch the laser projection to the cross to get a vertical laser line and horizontal bubble level, however, but the unit doesn’t lie flat when doing so.- Soft (round) edges on the bottom / ruler part make it harder to use against softer items you want to get a level on, for instance a taped template for drilling, which may be only cardboard or paper thick. A normal square edge would “catch” much better on smaller items and be more useful.- No instructions for how to change the laser projection setting - you slide the laser projector lens up and down! Remember that!Other than that, a great little tool.
