All boards came as advertised. They do have a sort of wax protecting the copper that you’ll need to clean up before trying to etch it. I’m not sure how thick the Cooper layer is but it hasn’t been an issue for me so far on peeling or lifting from the board.

priced right

size and squareness isnt perfect but they work and are priced right. I prep them with steel wool, bath in 5% hcl and distilled water. Green scrubber pad while in hcl. I then rinse with distilled, rise with ipa dry and apply uv resist. using a bath of distilled water helps get resist on with out bubbles
