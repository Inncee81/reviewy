A great addition to your saddle bag

 Hey guys, back with another review, this time I am looking at the Co2 Inflator from Vibrelli. I think this is the perfect mix of size, ease of use and control. Fits great in my bag and it is really easy to fill your tire and still keep a hand on your tire to check the pressure. If you are out on the trail or the road and you get a flat you want the right tools to get back up and spinning quickly. This item should be in your saddle bag.

The valve was precise- it was easy to put just a tiny amount of CO2 in …

I had to use the inflator yesterday and it performed as promised. The valve was precise- it was easy to put just a tiny amount of CO2 in to inflate the naked tube to find the puncture. The cartridge sleeve did a good job of keeping the frozen cartridge from freezing my fingers. The tire I was patching was bigger than one cartridge could fill after using part of the load to find the puncture but changing the cartridge was easy. Two cartridges, the inflator and the patch kit make a small package which is easy to carry. All around, a great system. I would, however, recommend carrying a small bottle of soapy water to aid in finding the puncture.
