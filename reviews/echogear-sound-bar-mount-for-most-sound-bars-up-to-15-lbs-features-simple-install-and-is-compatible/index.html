Not Quite There, So A Little Disappointed. ECHOGEAR I Know You Can Do Better.

As others have alluded to, using this mount for a Sonos Playbar is not without challenges. Yes there is an issue with the size of the provided fasteners, but I was able to make it work. The two issues/concerns that I have with the mount is that even though it seems well made, it does not have the strength to hold the play bar next to the bottom of the TV. I easily fixed this with a couple of command strips, but that should not be necessary or if it is then the strips should be included. The other issue that I ran into was that in order to use the correct spacer for my 65” Samsung TV, the provided fasteners were not long enough. Once again I was able to correct this with a quick trip to Lowes Home Improvement, but there should be a greater variety of fastener lengths included in the packaging. To say that I was a little disappointed would be an understatement as I was expecting the same great engineering and quality as the TV mounts, but the sound bar mount missed the mark especially since you can buy cheaper sound bar mounts and would have achieve the same results. I recommend that ECHOGEAR reevaluates their design and packaging and if they make the proper changes then without a doubt I believe that they will have a product that is on the same level as their TV mounts.
