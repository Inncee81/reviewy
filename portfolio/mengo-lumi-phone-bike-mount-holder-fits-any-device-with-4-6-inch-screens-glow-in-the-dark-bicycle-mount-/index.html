Dared to try it on iPhone 6+

All of the iPhone bike mount products I was considering were disappointingly clumsy, until I came across the Mengo Lumi. Near perfect simplicity in design. Under a minute to install, no tools required, plenty secure, no bouncing, easy to move. I took a chance as I have an iPhone 6 plus with a low profile case. The silicone material is stretchy and strong and is holding my bigger phone just fine. Rather than the bottom cross-band interfering with the top of the home button as shown in the pictures, mine skims the bottom edge of the home button. No problems accessing any controls except for maybe the silencing switch, which is no big deal. If they come out with a version for iPhone plusses, I will upgrade, but for now I’m very satisfied.

Personally I would rather it just be a solid color and not glow, but it serves as a safety mechanism so that others can see you at night so it’s fair.Worth the money.
